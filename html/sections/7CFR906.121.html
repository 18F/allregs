CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 906  /  Sec. 906.121 Reestablishment of districts.

The three districts of the production area specified in Sec. 906.20 Districts are reestablished as a single district comprising the entire production area. [34 FR 6651, Apr. 18, 1969] Sec. 906.122 [Reserved]
