CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 672  /  Sec. 672.320 What timeframes must be devoted to education and

YouthBuild grantees must structure programs so that participants in the program are offered:

(a) Eligible education activities, as specified in Sec. 672.310(a), during at least 50 percent of the time during which they participate in the program; and

(b) Eligible workforce investment activities, as specified in Sec. 672.310(b), during at least 40 percent of the time during which they participate in the program. Grantees must provide the eligible workforce investment activities described in Sec. 672.310(b)(1) as part of their program of eligible workforce investment activities.

(c) The remaining 10 percent of the time of participation can be used for the activities described in paragraphs (a) and (b) of this section and/or for leadership development and community service activities.
