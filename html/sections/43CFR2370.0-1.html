CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 2370  /  Sec. 2370.0-1 Purpose.

The regulations of this part 2370 apply to lands and interests in lands withdrawn or reserved from the public domain, except lands reserved or dedicated for national forest or national park purposes, which are no longer needed by the agency for which the lands are withdrawn or reserved. [35 FR 9558, June 13, 1970]
