CFR /  Title 31  /  Part 10  /  Sec. 10.52 Violations subject to sanction.

(a) A practitioner may be sanctioned under Sec. 10.50 if the practitioner--

(1) Willfully violates any of the regulations (other than Sec. 10.33) contained in this part; or

(2) Recklessly or through gross incompetence (within the meaning of Sec. 10.51(a)(13)) violates Sec. Sec. 10.34, 10.35, 10.36 or 10.37.

(b) Effective/applicability date. This section is applicable to conduct occurring on or after September 26, 2007. [T.D. 9359, 72 FR 54551, Sept. 26, 2007]
