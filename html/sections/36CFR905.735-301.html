CFR /  Title 36  /  Part 905  /  Sec. 905.735-301 General standards of conduct.

(a) Special Government employees of the Corporation shall adhere to applicable regulations adopted under Sec. 904.735-102, except 5 CFR 735.203(b). In addition, the standards of conduct set forth in Sec. Sec. 905.735-201, 905.735-204, and 905.735-205 shall apply to special Government employees.

(b) Special Government employees of the Corporation may teach, lecture, or write consistent with the provisions of 5 CFR 735.203(c).

(c) Pursuant to 5 CFR 735.305(b), the provisions concerning gifts, entertainment, and favors set forth in Sec. 905.735-202 are hereby made applicable to special Government employees.
