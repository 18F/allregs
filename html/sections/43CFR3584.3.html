CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 3584  /  Sec. 3584.3 Other applicable regulations.

Subject to regulations in this subpart, the regulations in parts 3500, 3510, 3520, 3530, 3540, 3550 and 3560 of this title shall govern the leasing of all leasable and hardrock minerals within the area. [51 FR 15213, Apr. 22, 1986; 51 FR 25205, July 11, 1986]
