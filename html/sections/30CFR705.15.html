CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 705  /  Sec. 705.15 Where to file.

The head of the State Regulatory Authority shall file his or her statement with the Director. All other employees and members of advisory boards and commissions representing multiple interests, as provided in Sec. 705.11, shall file their statements with the head of the State Regulatory Authority or such other official as may be designated by State law or regulation. [51 FR 37122, Oct. 17, 1986]
