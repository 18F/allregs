CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 90  /  Sec. 90.1311 License term.

The license term is ten years, except as set forth in Sec. 90.1307, beginning on the date of the initial authorization (non-exclusive nationwide license) grant. Registering fixed and base stations will not change the overall renewal period of the license. [80 FR 36222, June 23, 2015]
