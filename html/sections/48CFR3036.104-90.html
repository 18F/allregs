CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 3036  /  Sec. 3036.104-90 Authority for one-step turn-key design-build

The Head of the Contracting Activity (HCA) of the U.S. Coast Guard may use one-step turn-key selection procedures to enter into fixed-price design-build contracts in accordance with 14 U.S.C. 677. [73 FR 24883, May 6, 2008] Subpart 3036.2--Special Aspects of Contracting for Construction
