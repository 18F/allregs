CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 179  /  Sec. 179.200-3 Type.

Tank built under these specifications must be circular in cross section, with formed heads designed convex outward. When specified in Sec. 179.201-1, the tank must have at least one manway or one expansion dome with manway, and such other external projections as are prescribed herein. When the tank is divided into compartments, each compartment must be treated as a separate tank. [Amdt. 179-10, 36 FR 21348, Nov. 6, 1971]
