CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 112  /  Sec. 112.35-7 Activating means.

The activating means must be in the navigating bridge or in a location where the means can be controlled by the chief engineer. [CGD 74-125A, 47 FR 15267, Apr. 8, 1982, as amended by CGD 94-108, 61 FR 28287, June 4, 1996]
