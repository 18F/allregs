CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 17  /  Sec. 17.278 Confidentiality of records.

Confidentiality of records will be maintained in accordance with 38 CFR 1.460 through 1.582. (Authority: 5 U.S.C. 552, 552a; 38 U.S.C. 501, 1781, 5701, 7332)
