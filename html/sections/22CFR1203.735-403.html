CFR /  Title 22  /  Part 1203  /  Sec. 1203.735-403 Employee's complaint on filing requirement.

Each employee shall have the opportunity for review through agency grievance procedure of the employee's complaint that the employee's position has been improperly included within Sec. 1203.735-401 as one requiring the submission of a statement of employment and financial interests. Employees are reminded that they may obtain counseling pursuant to Sec. 1203.735-103 prior to filing a complaint.
