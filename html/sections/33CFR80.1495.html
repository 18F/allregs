CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 80  /  Sec. 80.1495 U.S. Pacific Island Possessions.

The 72 COLREGS shall apply on the bays, harbors, lagoons, and waters surrounding the U.S. Pacific Island Possessions of American Samoa, Baker, Howland, Jarvis, Johnson, Palmyra, Swains and Wake Islands. [CGD 94-011, 63 FR 5731, Feb. 4, 1998]
