CFR /  Title 32  /  Part 169a  /  Sec. 169a.1 Purpose.

This part:

(a) Reissues DoD Instruction 4100.33 \1\ to update policy, procedures, and responsibilities required by DoD Directive 4100.15 \2\ and OMB Circular A-76 \3\ for use by the Department of Defense (DoD) to determine whether needed commercial activities (CAs) should be accomplished by DoD personnel or by contract with a commercial source.---------------------------------------------------------------------------

\1\ Copies may be obtained, at cost, from the National Technical Information Service, 5285 Port Royal Road, Springfield, VA 22161

\2\ See footnote 1 to Sec. 169a.1(a).

\3\ Copies may be obtained if needed, from the Office of Management and Budget, Executive Office Building, Washington, DC 20503.---------------------------------------------------------------------------

(b) Cancels DoD 4100.33-H,\4\ ``DoD In-House vs. Contract Commercial and Industrial Activities Cost Comparison Handbook.''---------------------------------------------------------------------------

\4\ See footnote 1 to Sec. 169a.1(a).---------------------------------------------------------------------------
