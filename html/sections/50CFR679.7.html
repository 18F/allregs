(a) Groundfish of the GOA and BSAI--(1) Federal Fisheries Permit (FFP). (i) Fish for groundfish in the BSAI or GOA with a vessel of the United States that does not have on board a legible copy of a valid FFP issued under Sec. 679.4.

(2) Conduct any fishing contrary to notification of inseason action, closure, or adjustment issued under Sec. 679.20, Sec. 679.21, Sec. 679.22, Sec. 679.25.
