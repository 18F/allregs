CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 1126  /  Sec. 1126.75 Plant location adjustments for producer milk and

For purposes of making payments for producer milk and nonpool milk, a plant location adjustment shall be determined by subtracting the Class I price specified in Sec. 1126.51 from the Class I price at the plant's location. The difference, plus or minus as the case may be, shall be used to adjust the payments required pursuant to Sec. Sec. 1126.73 and 1000.76.
