CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 301  /  Sec. 301.48-6 Movement of live Japanese beetles.

Regulations requiring a permit for and otherwise governing the movement of live Japanese beetles in interstate or foreign commerce are contained in the Federal Plant Pest Regulations in part 330 of this chapter. Applications for permits for the movement of the pest may be made to the Administrator. [44 FR 24035, Apr. 24, 1979, as amended at 61 FR 32641, June 25, 1996]
