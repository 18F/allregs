CFR /  Title 16  /  Part 1615  /  Sec. 1615.2 Scope and application.

(a) This Standard provides a test method to determine the flammability of items as defined in Sec. 1615.1(d).

(b) All items as defined in Sec. 1615.1(d) are subject to requirements of this standard.

(c) The flammability standards for clothing textiles and vinyl plastic film, parts 1610 and 1611 of this chapter, are superseded by this part insofar as they apply to items defined in Sec. 1615.1(d). [40 FR 59903, Dec. 30, 1975, as amended at 64 FR 34533, June 28, 1999]
