CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 235  /  Sec. 235.205 Deed in lieu of foreclosure.

All of the provisions of Sec. 203.357 of this chapter relating to the acceptance of a deed in lieu of foreclosure shall apply to mortgages insured under this part, except that where a family unit in condominium is involved, the deed in lieu of foreclosure may be accepted only if the mortgagee establishes to the satisfaction of the Secretary that there are no unpaid assessments owed to the association or cooperative of owners.
