CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 1000  /  Sec. 1000.142 How does a recipient determine the ``useful life'' housing must remain affordable as required in sections 205(a)(2)and 209 of NAHASDA?

To the extent required in the IHP, each recipient shall describe its determination of the useful life of the assisted housing units in its developments in accordance with the local conditions of the Indian area of the recipient. By approving the plan, HUD determines the useful life in accordance with section 205(a)(2) of NAHASDA and for purposes of section 209. [77 FR 71525, Dec. 3, 2012]
