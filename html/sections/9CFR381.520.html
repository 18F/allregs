CFR /  Title 9  /  Part 381  /  Sec. 381.520 Deselection of ineligible establishments.

(a) The Administrator will deselect a selected establishment that becomes ineligible to participate in a cooperative interstate shipment program for any reason listed under Sec. 381.513(c) of this subpart.

(b) An establishment that has been deselected must transition to become an official establishment as provided in Sec. 381.521 of this subpart.
