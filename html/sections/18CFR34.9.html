CFR /  Title 18  /  Part 34  /  Sec. 34.9 Reports.

The applicant must file reports under Sec. 131.43 and Sec. 131.50 of this chapter no later than 30 days after the sale or placement of long-term debt or equity securities or the entry into guarantees or assumptions of liabilities pursuant to authority granted under this part. [Order 575, 60 FR 4853, Jan. 25, 1995. Redesignated by Order 737, 75 FR 43403, July 26, 2010]
