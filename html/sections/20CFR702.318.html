CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 702  /  Sec. 702.318 The record; what constitutes; nontransferability of the

For the purpose of any further proceedings under the Act, the formal record of proceedings shall consist of the hearing record made before the administrative law judge (see Sec. 702.344). When transferring the case for hearing pursuant to Sec. 702.317, the district director shall not transfer the administrative file under any circumstances.
