CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 120  /  Sec. 120.378 Ungrounded systems.

Each ungrounded system must be provided with a suitably sensitive ground detection system, located at the respective switchboard, that provides continuous indication of circuit status to ground with a provision to momentarily remove the indicating device from the reference ground. [CGD 85-080, 62 FR 51352, Sept. 30, 1997]
