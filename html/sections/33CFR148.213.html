CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 148  /  Sec. 148.213 How do I withdraw my application?

The applicant may withdraw an application at any time before the proceeding is terminated by delivering or mailing notice of withdrawal to the Commandant (CG-5P) for docketing. [USCG-1998-3884, 71 FR 57651, Sept. 29, 2006, as amended by USCG-2013- 0397, 78 FR 39176, July 1, 2013]
