CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 154  /  Sec. 154.103 Applicability.

(a) In general. The requirements of this part apply to health insurance issuers offering health insurance coverage in the individual market and small group market.

(b) Exceptions. The requirements of this part do not apply to grandfathered health plan coverage as defined in 45 CFR Sec. 147.140, or to excepted benefits as described in section 2791(c) of the PHS Act.
