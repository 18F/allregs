CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 2421  /  Sec. 2421.19 Eligibility period.

Eligibility period means the payroll period during which an employee must be in an employment status with an agency or activity in order to be eligible to vote in a representation election under part 2422 of this subchapter. [60 FR 67291, Dec. 29, 1995]
