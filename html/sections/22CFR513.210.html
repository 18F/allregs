CFR /  Title 22  /  Part 513  /  Sec. 513.210 Voluntary exclusion.

Persons who accept voluntary exclusions under Sec. 513.315 are excluded in accordance with the terms of their settlements. Broadcasting Board of Governors shall, and participants may, contact the original action agency to ascertain the extent of the exclusion.
