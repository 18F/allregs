CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 556  /  Sec. 556.52 Apramycin.

A tolerance of 0.1 part per million is established for parent apramycin (marker residue) in kidney (target tissue) of swine. The acceptable daily intake (ADI) for total residues of apramycin is 25 micrograms per kilogram of body weight per day. [62 FR 40933, July 31, 1997]
