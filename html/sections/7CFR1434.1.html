CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 1434  /  Sec. 1434.1 Applicability.

(a) This part specifies the terms and conditions of Commodity Credit Corporation (CCC) nonrecourse marketing assistance loan (MAL) and loan deficiency payment (LDP) Programs for honey. MAL gains and LDPs for honey are limited by the payment limitation and adjusted gross income provisions specified in part 1400 of this chapter.

(b) Producers must comply with all provisions of this part and part 1421 of this chapter. [74 FR 15656, Apr. 7, 2009, as amended at 80 FR 139, Jan. 2, 2015]
