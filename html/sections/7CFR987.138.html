CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 987  /  Sec. 987.138 Handlers of record.

Prior to handling dates, each person shall file CDAC Form No. 18 with the Committee at the times, and containing the information, prescribed in Sec. 987.38. [43 FR 28437, June 30, 1978]
