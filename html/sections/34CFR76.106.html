CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 76  /  Sec. 76.106 State documents are public information.

A State shall make the following documents available for public inspection:

(a) All State plans and related official materials.

(b) All approved subgrant applications.

(c) All documents that the Secretary transmits to the State regarding a program. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1221e-3 and 3474)
