CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 111  /  Sec. 111.105-29 Combustible liquid cargo carriers.

(a) Each vessel that carries combustible liquid cargo with a closed- cup flashpoint of 60 degrees C (140 degrees F) or higher must have:

(1) Only intrinsically safe electric systems in cargo tanks; and

(2) No storage battery in any cargo handling room.

(b) If a submerged cargo pump motor is in a cargo tank, it must meet the requirements of Sec. 111.105-31(d).

(c) Where the cargo is heated to within 15 [deg]C of its flashpoint, the cargo pumproom must meet the requirements of Sec. 111.105-31(f) and the weather locations must meet Sec. 111.105-31(l). [CGD 74-125A, 47 FR 15236, Apr. 8, 1982, as amended by CGD 94-108, 61 FR 28285, June 4, 1996; 61 FR 36787, July 12, 1996; 61 FR 39695, July 30, 1996]
