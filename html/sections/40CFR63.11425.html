CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 63  /  Sec. 63.11425 What General Provisions apply to this subpart?

(a) The provisions in 40 CFR part 63, subpart A, that are applicable to this subpart are specified in Table 1 to this subpart.

(b) For existing sources, the initial notification required by Sec. 63.9(b) must be submitted not later than November 13, 2007.

(c) For existing sources, the initial notification of compliance required by Sec. 63.9(h) must be submitted not later than March 13, 2009. [72 FR 38913, July 16, 2007, as amended at 73 FR 15929, Mar. 26, 2008]
