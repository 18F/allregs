CFR /  Title 29  /  Part 4022  /  Sec. 4022.92 What definitions do I need to know for these rules?

You need to know three definitions from Sec. 4001.2 of this chapter (PBGC, person, and plan) and the following definitions:

``We'' means the PBGC.

``You'' means the person to whom we may owe benefits at the time of death.
