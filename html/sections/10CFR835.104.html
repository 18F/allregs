CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 835  /  Sec. 835.104 Written procedures.

Written procedures shall be developed and implemented as necessary to ensure compliance with this part, commensurate with the radiological hazards created by the activity and consistent with the education, training, and skills of the individuals exposed to those hazards. [63 FR 59682, Nov. 4, 1998]
