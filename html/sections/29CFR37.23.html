CFR /  Title 29  /  Part 37  /  Sec. 37.23 Who must designate an Equal Opportunity Officer?

Every recipient must designate an Equal Opportunity Officer (``EO Officer''), except small recipients and service providers, as defined in Sec. 37.4. The responsibilities of small recipients and service providers are described in Sec. Sec. 37.27 and 37.28.
