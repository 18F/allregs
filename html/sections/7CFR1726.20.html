CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 1726  /  Sec. 1726.20 Standards and specifications.

All materials, equipment, and construction must meet the minimum requirements of all applicable RUS standards and specifications. (See part 1728 of this chapter, Electric Standards and Specifications for Materials and Construction, which is applicable regardless of the source of funding.) [69 FR 7109, Feb. 13, 2004]
