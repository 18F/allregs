CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 970  /  970.0371-9 Contract clause.

The contracting officer shall insert the clause at 970.5203-3, Contractor's Organization, in all management and operating contracts. The approval authority of the Secretary of Energy required in paragraph (c) may not be delegated. In paragraph (a) the words ``and managerial personnel (see 970.5245-1(j))'' may be inserted after ``(see 952.215-70)''. [65 FR 81009, Dec. 22, 2000, as amended at 74 FR 36370, July 22, 2009]
