CFR /  Title 39  /  Part 965  /  Sec. 965.6 Comments by parties.

Within 10 days of receipt of the other party's initial submission under Sec. 965.5, each party may file with the Recorder an additional statement or rebuttal argument setting forth in detail its disagreements, if any, with its opponent's initial submission. Such rebuttal may include any additional documents relevant to the dispute. [76 FR 15219, Mar. 21, 2011]
