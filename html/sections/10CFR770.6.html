CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 770  /  Sec. 770.6 May interested persons and entities request that realdevelopment?

Any person or entity may request that specific real property be made available for transfer for economic development pursuant to procedures in Sec. 770.7. A person or entity must submit such a request in writing to the Field Office Manager who is responsible for the real property.
