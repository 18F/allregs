CFR /  Title 37  /  Part 353  /  Sec. 353.1 When granted.

A motion for rehearing may be filed by any participant in the relevant proceeding. The Copyright Royalty Judges may grant rehearing upon a showing that any aspect of the determination may be erroneous. [71 FR 53330, Sept. 11, 2006]
