CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 58  /  Sec. 58.10-10 Diesel engine installations.

(a) The requirements of Sec. 58.10-5 (a), (c), and (d) shall apply to diesel engine installations.

(b) A diesel engine air intake on a mobile offshore drilling unit must not be in a classified location. \1\

(c) A diesel engine exhaust on a mobile offshore drilling unit must not discharge into a classified location. \1\---------------------------------------------------------------------------

\1\ Sections 108.171 to 108.175 of this chapter define classified locations for mobile offshore drilling units. [CGFR 68-82, 33 FR 18878, Dec. 18, 1968, as amended by CGD 73-251, 43 FR 56801, Dec. 4, 1978; CGD 95-028, 62 FR 51202, Sept. 30, 1997]
