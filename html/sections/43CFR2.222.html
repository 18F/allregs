CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 2  /  Sec. 2.222 Records subject to Privacy Act.

The Privacy Act applies to all ``records,'' as that term is defined in Sec. 2.46(e), which the Department maintains in a ``system of records,'' as that term is defined in Sec. 2.46(f).
