CFR /  Title 25  /  Part 700  /  Sec. 700.713 Tenure of grazing permits.

(a) All active regular grazing permits shall be for five years and shall be automatically reissued for another five-year period provided the permittee is not in violation of Sec. 700.711 or Sec. 700.715 or Sec. 700.719 or Sec. 700.723 or Sec. 700.725 of the regulations. Permits will initially be issued with an ending date of October 31 of the fifth year following the date of initial issuance.

(b) Amendments to these regulations extending or limiting the tenure of grazing permits are applicable and become a condition of all previously granted permits.
