CFR /  Title 36  /  Part 905  /  Sec. 905.735-503 Statements of employment and financial interests by

All special Government employees shall submit a statement of employment and financial interest prior to beginning employment or service with the Corporation. Each statement shall be submitted to the Ethics Counselor, Office of the General Counsel, Pennsylvania Avenue Development Corporation, 1331 Pennsylvania Avenue, NW., Suite 1220 North, Washington, D.C. 20004, except that the statements of Board Members appointed from private life shall be filed with the U.S. Civil Service Commission. [43 FR 60902, Dec. 29, 1978, as amended at 50 FR 45824, Nov. 4, 1985]
