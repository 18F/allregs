CFR /  Title 16  /  Part 1109  /  Sec. 1109.11 Component part testing for paint.

(a) Generally. The Commission will permit certification of a consumer product, or a component part of a consumer product, as being in compliance with the lead paint limit of part 1303 of this chapter or the content limits for paint on toys of section 4.3 of ASTM F 963-11 or any successor standard of section 4.3 of ASTM F 963-11 accepted by the Commission if, for each paint used on the product, the requirements in Sec. 1109.5 and paragraph (b) of this section are met.
