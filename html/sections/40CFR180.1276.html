CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 180  /  Sec. 180.1276 Tobacco mild green mosaic tobamovirus strain U2;

An exemption from the requirement of a tolerance is established for residues of Tobacco mild green mosaic tobamovirus strain U2 in or on all commodities of crop groups 17 and 18 when applied as a post-emergent herbicide and used in accordance with label directions and good agricultural practices. [79 FR 75756, Dec. 19, 2014]
