CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 1205  /  Sec. 1205.505 Communication.

All reports, requests, applications for reimbursements, and communications in connection with the Cotton Research and Promotion Order shall be addressed as follows: Cotton Board, Post Office Box 2121, Memphis, Tennessee, 38101-2121. [57 FR 29186, July 1, 1992]
