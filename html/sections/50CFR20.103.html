CFR /  Title 50  /  Part 20  /  Sec. 20.103 Seasons, limits, and shooting hours for mourning and

This section provides for the annual hunting of certain doves and pigeons in the 48 contiguous United States. The mourning dove hunting regulations are arranged by the Eastern, Central, and Western Management Units. [44 FR 7147, Feb. 6, 1979]

Editorial Note: For Federal Register citations affecting annual regulatory schedules for this section, see the List of CFR Sections Affected, which appears in the Finding Aids section of the printed volume and at www.fdsys.gov.
