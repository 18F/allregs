CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 1846  /  1846.670-4 Multiple shipments.

(a) If the ``shipped to,'' ``marked for,'' ``shipped from,'' ``CQA,'' and ``acceptance'' data are the same for more than one shipment made on the same day under the same contract in a single car, truck, or other vehicle, one MIRR shall be prepared to cover all such shipments.

(b) If the volume of the shipments precludes the use of a single car, truck, or other vehicle, a separate MIRR shall be provided for each vehicle. [62 FR 14024, Mar. 25, 1997, as amended at 69 FR 44610, July 27, 2004]
