CFR /  Title 14  /  Part 435  /  Sec. 435.8 Human space flight.

An applicant for a license to conduct a reentry with flight crew or a space flight participant on board the vehicle must demonstrate compliance with Sec. Sec. 460.5, 460.7, 460.11, 460.13, 460.15, 460.17, 460.51 and 460.53 of this subchapter. [Doc. No. FAA-2005-23449, 71 FR 75632, Dec. 15, 2006]
