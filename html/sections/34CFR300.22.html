CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 300  /  Sec. 300.22 Individualized education program.

Individualized education program or IEP means a written statement for a child with a disability that is developed, reviewed, and revised in accordance with Secs. 300.320 through 300.324. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1401(14))
