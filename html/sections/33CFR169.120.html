CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 169  /  Sec. 169.120 When is the southeastern reporting system in effect?

The mandatory ship reporting system in the southeastern United States operates during the period beginning on November 15 each year through April 16 of the following year. [USCG-1999-5525, 66 FR 58070, Nov. 20, 2001]
