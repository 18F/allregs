CFR /  Title 29  /  Part 1471  /  Sec. 1471.440 What method do I use to communicate those requirements

To communicate the requirement you must include a term or condition in the transaction requiring the participants' compliance with subpart C of this part and requiring them to include a similar term or condition in lower-tier covered transactions. [68 FR 66604, Nov. 26, 2003]
