CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 435  /  Sec. 435.903 Adherence of local agencies to State plan requirements.

The agency must--

(a) Have methods to keep itself currently informed of the adherence of local agencies to the State plan provisions and the agency's procedures for determining eligibility; and

(b) Take corrective action to ensure their adherence. [44 FR 17937, Mar. 23, 1979. Redesignated at 59 FR 48809, Sept. 23, 1994]
