CFR /  Title 12  /  Part 7  /  Sec. 7.1005 Credit decisions at other than banking offices of

A national bank and its operating subsidiary may make a credit decision regarding a loan application at a site other than the main office or a branch office of the bank without violating 12 U.S.C. 36 and 12 U.S.C. 81, provided that ``money'' is not deemed to be ``lent'' at those other sites within the meaning of Sec. 7.1003.
