CFR /  Title 25  /  Part 514  /  Sec. 514.8 Where should fees, quarterly statements, and other

The statements, remittances, and communications about fees shall be transmitted to the Commission at the following address: NIGC Attn: Comptroller, C/O Department of the Interior, 1849 C Street NW., Mail Stop 1621, Washington, DC 20240. Checks should be made payable to the National Indian Gaming Commission (do not remit cash). [77 FR 5181, Feb. 2, 2012, as amended at 80 FR 31994, June 5, 2015]
