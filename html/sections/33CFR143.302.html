CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 143  /  Sec. 143.302 Hazardous location requirements on foreign vessels engaged

Each vessel that is documented under the laws of a foreign nation and is constructed after April 2, 2018 must comply with the requirements of 46 CFR subpart 111.108 prior to engaging in OCS activities. [USCG-2012-0850, 80 FR 16990, Mar. 31, 2015]
