CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 5  /  Sec. 5.406 Responsible party, ``stop-buzzer,'' and notification

(a) Medical testing licensees must identify a single point of contact responsible for all experiments conducted under the license and must also identify a ``stop buzzer'' point of contact for all experiments, consistent with subpart E, Secs. 5.307 and 5.308.

(b) Medical testing licensees must meet the notification and safety of the public requirements of subpart E, Secs. 5.309 and 5.311.

Effective Date Note: At 78 FR 25162, Apr. 29, 2013, Secs. 5.406 was added. This section contains information collection and recordkeeping requirements and will not become effective until approval has been given by the Office of Management and Budget.
