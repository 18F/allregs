CFR /  Title 26  /  Part 54  /  Sec. 54.6091-1 Place for filing excise tax returns under section

Effective for any Form 8928 that is due on or after January 1, 2010, the return required by Sec. 54.6011-2 must be filed at the place specified in the forms and instructions provided by the Internal Revenue Service. [T.D. 9457, 74 FR 46000, Sept. 8, 2009]
