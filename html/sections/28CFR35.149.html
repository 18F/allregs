CFR /  Title 28  /  Part 35  /  Sec. 35.149 Discrimination prohibited.

Except as otherwise provided in Sec. 35.150, no qualified individual with a disability shall, because a public entity's facilities are inaccessible to or unusable by individuals with disabilities, be excluded from participation in, or be denied the benefits of the services, programs, or activities of a public entity, or be subjected to discrimination by any public entity.
