CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 404  /  Sec. 404.424 Total amount of deductions where more than one deduction

If more than one of the deduction events specified in Secs. 404.415, 404.417, and 404.421 occurred in any 1 month, each of which would occasion a deduction equal to the benefit for such month, only an amount equal to such benefit is deducted.
