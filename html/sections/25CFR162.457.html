CFR /  Title 25  /  Part 162  /  Sec. 162.457 May a lessee mortgage a business lease?

(a) A lessee may mortgage a business lease by meeting the consent requirements in Sec. 162.458 and obtaining our approval of the leasehold mortgage under Sec. Sec. 162.459 and 162.460.

(b) Refer to Sec. 162.449(c) for information on what happens if a sale or foreclosure under an approved mortgage of the leasehold interest occurs.
