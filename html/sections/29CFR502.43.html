CFR /  Title 29  /  Part 502  /  Sec. 502.43 Responsibility of the Office of Administrative Law Judges.

Upon receipt of the ARB's Notice pursuant to Sec. 501.42 of these regulations, the Office of ALJ shall promptly forward a copy of the complete hearing record to the ARB.
