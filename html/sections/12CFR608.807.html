CFR /  Title 12  /  Part 608  /  Sec. 608.807 Right to inspect and copy records.

The debtor may inspect and copy the FCA records related to the claim. The debtor shall give the FCA reasonable advance notice that it intends to inspect and copy the records involved. The debtor shall pay copying costs unless they are waived by the FCA. Copying costs shall be assessed pursuant to Secs. 602.11 and 602.12 of this chapter. [59 FR 13187, Mar. 21, 1994, as amended at 71 FR 54900, Sept. 20, 2006]
