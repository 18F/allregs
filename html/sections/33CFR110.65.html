CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 110  /  Sec. 110.65 Indian River Bay, Del.

Beginning at a point bearing 174[deg], 300 feet, from a point on the southerly edge of the project channel 5,500 feet westerly from the State highway bridge across Indian River Inlet; thence 174[deg], 600 feet; thence 264[deg], 800 feet; thence 354[deg], 600 feet; and thence 84[deg], 800 feet, to the point of beginning.
