CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 403  /  Sec. 403.101 How must funds be used under the Programs for

In administering programs receiving funds reserved under Sec. 403.180(b)(5) for criminal offenders, each State corrections educational agency designated under Sec. 403.100(a) shall--

(a) Give special consideration to providing--

(1) Services to offenders who are completing their sentences and preparing for release; and

(2) Grants for the establishment of vocational education programs in correctional institutions that do not have such programs;

(b) Provide vocational education programs for women who are incarcerated;

(c) Improve equipment; and

(d) In cooperation with eligible recipients, administer and coordinate vocational education services to offenders before and after their release. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 2336(b))
