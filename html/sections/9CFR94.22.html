CFR /  Title 9  /  Part 94  /  Sec. 94.22 Meat or dressed carcasses of hunter-harvested bovines.

The meat or dressed carcass (eviscerated and the head is removed) is derived from a wild bovine that has been legally harvested in the wild, as verified by proof such as a hunting license, tag, or the equivalent that the hunter must show to the authorized inspector. (Approved by the Office of Management and Budget under control number 0579-0393) [78 FR 72999, Dec. 4, 2013]
