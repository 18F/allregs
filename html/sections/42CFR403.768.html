CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 403  /  Sec. 403.768 Excluded services.

In addition to items and services excluded in Sec. 409.49 of this chapter, items and services are also excluded if they are provided by:

(a) A HHA that is not a RNHCI.

(b) A supplier who is not providing RNHCI designated items under arrangement with a RNHCI.

(c) A nurse who is not providing RNHCI home nursing services under arrangement with a RNHCI. [69 FR 66419, Nov. 15, 2004]
