CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 956  /  Sec. 956.20 Establishment and membership.

(a) The Walla Walla Sweet Onion Marketing Committee, consisting of ten members, is hereby established. The committee shall consist of six producer members, three handler members, and one public member. Each member shall have an alternate who shall have the same qualifications as the member.

(b) A producer shall have three years of experience in producing onions in order to qualify for committee membership. At the time of selection, no more than two producer members may be affiliated with the same handler. [60 FR 27626, May 24, 1995, as amended at 64 FR 4933, Feb. 1, 1999]
