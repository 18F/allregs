CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 707  /  Sec. 707.72 Termination of reporting requirements.

(a) The reporting requirements of subpart D of this part are terminated for certain specific chemical substances and mixtures as set forth in this paragraph.

(1) When data required under part 766 of this chapter have been submitted to EPA for a specific chemical substance produced by a specific process, and the data show no positive test result as defined in Sec. 766.3 of this chapter, reporting is no longer required by persons who export or intend to export that substance produced by that process.
