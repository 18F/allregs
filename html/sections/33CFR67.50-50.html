CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 67  /  Sec. 67.50-50 Seventeenth Coast Guard District.

(a) Description. See Sec. 3.85-1 of this chapter.

(b) Line of demarcation. There is no line of demarcation prescribed for this District. When required it will be determined in accordance with Sec. 67.01-20. [CGFR 68-95, 33 FR 15285, Oct. 15, 1968]
