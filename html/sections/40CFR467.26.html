CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 467  /  Sec. 467.26 Pretreatment standards for new sources.

Except as provided in Sec. 403.7, any new source subject to this subpart which introduces pollutants into a publicly owned treatment works must comply with 40 CFR part 403 and achieve the following pretreatment standards for new sources. The mass of process wastewater pollutants from the core and ancillary operations introduced into a POTW shall not exceed the values set forth below:
