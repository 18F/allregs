CFR /  Title 12  /  Part 7  /  Sec. 7.2018 Lost stock certificates.

If a national bank does not provide for replacing lost, stolen, or destroyed stock certificates in its articles of association or bylaws, the bank may adopt procedures in accordance with Sec. 7.2000.
