CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 932  /  Sec. 932.53 Inspection and certification.

(a) Each handler shall have the olives such handler handles inspected and certified as for conformance with all applicable requirements pursuant to Sec. Sec. 932.51 and 932.52 with respect to such handling. Inspection and certification for conformance with the requirements of Sec. 932.51 shall be by the Federal or Federal-State Inspection Service, including certification as to size, and inspection for conformance with the requirements of Sec. 932.52 shall be by the Processed Products Branch, USDA, except that the disposition of olives, other than as canned ripe olives, in accordance with the requirements of Sec. 932.51(a)(3) may be under the supervision of any of such inspection services. A copy of each certification by the said inspection services, pursuant to the provisions of this section, shall be furnished to the committee.

(b) The committee may enter into an agreement with either or both of said inspection services with respect to the costs of the inspection required by this section and may collect from handlers their respective pro rata share of such costs. [30 FR 12629, Oct. 2, 1965, as amended at 47 FR 32910, July 30, 1982]
