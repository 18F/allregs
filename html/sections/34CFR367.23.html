CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 367  /  Sec. 367.23 What additional factor does the Secretary consider?

In addition to the criteria in Sec. 367.22, the Secretary considers the geographic distribution of projects in making an award. (Authority: 29 U.S.C. 711(c) and 796k(b)(1) and (i)(1))
