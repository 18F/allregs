CFR /  Title 19  /  Part 122  /  Sec. 122.29 Arrival fee and overtime services.

Private aircraft may be subject to the payment of an arrival fee for services provided as set forth in Sec. 24.22 of this chapter. For the procedures to be followed in requesting overtime services in connection with the arrival of private aircraft, see Sec. 24.16 of this chapter. [T.D. 93-85, 58 FR 54286, Oct. 21, 1993]
