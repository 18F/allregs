CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 585  /  Sec. 585.825 When must I assess my facilities?

(a) You must perform an assessment of the structure, when needed, based on the platform assessment initiators listed in sections 17.2.1-17.2.5 of API RP 2A-WSD, Recommended Practice for Planning, Designing and Constructing Fixed Offshore Platforms--Working Stress Design (as incorporated by reference in Sec. 585.115).

(b) You must initiate mitigation actions for structures that do not pass the assessment process of API RP 2A-WSD.

(c) You must perform other assessments as required by BOEM. Sec. Sec. 585.826-585.829 [Reserved]
