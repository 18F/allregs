CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 103  /  Sec. 103.35 Is there a time limit on when I can file?

Yes, you must notify us within one year that you are a victim of discrimination. If you indicate that there is more than one act of discrimination, or that the discrimination is continuing, we must receive your information within one year of the last incident of discrimination. [64 FR 18540, Apr. 14, 1999]
