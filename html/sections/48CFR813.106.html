CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 813  /  813.106 Soliciting competition, evaluation of quotations or offers,

(a) Contracting officers may use other than competitive procedures to enter into a contract with a SDVOSB or VOSB when the amount exceeds the micro-purchase threshold up to $5 million.

(b) Requirements exceeding $25,000 must be synopsized in accordance with FAR Part 5. [74 FR 64630, Dec. 8, 2009]
