CFR /  Title 16  /  Part 1205  /  Sec. 1205.2 Effective date.

This standard applies to all rotary walk behind power lawn mowers manufactured after June 30, 1982, except Sec. 1205.6 Warning labels, applies to rotary and reel-type walk-behind power lawn mowers manufactured after December 31, 1979. [44 FR 10024, Feb. 15, 1979, as amended 45 FR 86417, Dec. 31, 1980]
