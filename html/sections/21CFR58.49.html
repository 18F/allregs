CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 58  /  Sec. 58.49 Laboratory operation areas.

Separate laboratory space shall be provided, as needed, for the performance of the routine and specialized procedures required by nonclinical laboratory studies. [52 FR 33780, Sept. 4, 1987]
