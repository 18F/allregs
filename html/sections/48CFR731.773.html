CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 731  /  731.773 Independent research and development costs.

Pending establishment of Government-wide principles in Attachment B of OMB Circular A-122, USAID will apply the cost principles at (48 CFR) FAR 31.205-18 for independent research and development costs. [49 FR 13250, Apr. 3, 1984, as amended at 79 FR 74988, Dec. 16, 2014]
