CFR /  Title 16  /  Part 453  /  Sec. 453.7 Comprehension of disclosures.

To prevent the unfair or deceptive acts or practices specified in Sec. Sec. 453.2 through 453.5, funeral providers must make all disclosures required by those sections in a clear and conspicuous manner. Providers shall not include in the casket, outer burial container, and general price lists, required by Sec. Sec. 453.2(b)(2)-(4), any statement or information that alters or contradicts the information required by this part to be included in those lists.
