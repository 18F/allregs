CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 1039  /  Sec. 1039.660 What special provisions apply to Independent

Under Sec. 1039.801, certain engines are considered to be new engines when they are imported into the United States, even if they have previously been used outside the country. Independent Commercial Importers may use the provisions of 40 CFR part 89, subpart G, and 40 CFR 89.906(b) to receive a certificate of conformity for engines meeting all the requirements of this part 1039.
