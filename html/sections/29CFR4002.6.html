CFR /  Title 29  /  Part 4002  /  Sec. 4002.6 Place of meetings; use of conference call

Meetings of the Board of Directors shall be held at the principal office of the Corporation unless otherwise determined by the Board of Directors or the Chair. Any Member may participate in a meeting of the Board of Directors through the use of conference call telephone or similar communications equipment, by means of which all persons participating in the meeting can speak to and hear each other. Any Board Member so participating in a meeting shall be deemed present for all purposes. Actions taken by the Board of Directors at meetings conducted through the use of such equipment, including the votes of each Member, shall be recorded in the usual manner in the minutes of the meetings of the Board of Directors.
