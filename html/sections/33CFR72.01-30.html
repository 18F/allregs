CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 72  /  Sec. 72.01-30 Temporary deficiencies.

Temporary deficiencies in aids to navigation are not published in Notices to Mariners when it is known that the defects will be corrected promptly. [CGFR 52-15, 18 FR 13, Jan. 1, 1953] Sec. 72.01-35 [Reserved]
