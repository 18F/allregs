CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 82  /  Sec. 82.50 General.

A batch of a straight color listed in this subpart may be certified, in accordance with the provisions of the regulations in this part, for use in food, drugs, and cosmetics, if such batch conforms to the requirements of Sec. 82.5 and to the specifications in this subpart set forth for such color.
