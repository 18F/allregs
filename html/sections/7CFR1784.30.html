CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 1784  /  Sec. 1784.30 Subsequent grants.

Subsequent grants will be processed in accordance with the requirements set forth in this part. The initial and subsequent grants made to complete a previously approved project must comply with the maximum grant requirements set forth inSec. 1784.8(f) of this part.
