CFR /  Title 16  /  Part 437  /  Sec. 437.10 Severability.

The provisions of this part are separate and severable from one another. If any provision is stayed or determined to be invalid, the remaining provisions shall continue in effect. [GRAPHIC] [TIFF OMITTED] TR08DE11.000 [GRAPHIC] [TIFF OMITTED] TR08DE11.001
