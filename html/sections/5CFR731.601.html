CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 731  /  Sec. 731.601 Savings provision.

No provision of the regulations in this part is to be applied in such a way as to affect any administrative proceeding pending on June 16, 2008. An administrative proceeding is deemed to be pending from the date of the agency or OPM ``notice of proposed action'' described in Sec. Sec. 731.302 and 731.402.
