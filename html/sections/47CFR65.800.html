CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 65  /  Sec. 65.800 Rate base.

The rate base shall consist of the interstate portion of the accounts listed in Sec. 65.820 that has been invested in plant used and useful in the efficient provision of interstate telecommunications services regulated by this Commission, minus any deducted items computed in accordance with Sec. 65.830.
