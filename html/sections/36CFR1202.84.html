CFR /  Title 36  /  Part 1202  /  Sec. 1202.84 Can I seek judicial review?

Yes, within 2 years of receipt of a NARA final determination as provided in Sec. 1202.54 or Sec. 1202.80, you may seek judicial review of that determination. You may file a civil action in the Federal District Court:

(a) In which you reside or have a principal place of business;

(b) In which the NARA records are located; or

(c) In the District of Columbia.
