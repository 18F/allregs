CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 201  /  Sec. 201.38 Importations.

Prior to release into the commerce of the United States, imported seed and screenings shall be inspected as provided in Sec. Sec. 361.4 of this title. [5 FR 32, Jan. 4, 1940, as amended at 62 FR 48459, Sept. 16, 1997]
