CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 985  /  Sec. 985.233 Salable quantities and allotment percentages--2014-2015

The salable quantity and allotment percentage for each class of spearmint oil during the marketing year beginning on June 1, 2014, shall be as follows:

(a) Class 1 (Scotch) oil--a salable quantity of 1,984,423 pounds and an allotment percentage of 95 percent.

(b) Class 3 (Native) oil--a salable quantity of 1,351,704 pounds and an allotment percentage of 57 percent. [79 FR 26364, May 8, 2014, as amended at 79 FR 64661, Oct. 31, 2014; 80 FR 3147, Jan. 22, 2015; 80 FR 16552, Mar. 30, 2015]
