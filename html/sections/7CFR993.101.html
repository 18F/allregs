CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 993  /  Sec. 993.101 Order.

Order means Marketing Agreement No. 110, as amended, and Order No. 993, as amended (Sec. Sec. 993.1 through 993.97), regulating the handling of dried prunes produced in California, or as they may be further amended hereafter.
