CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 14  /  Sec. 14.505 Submissions.

All submissions will set forth the question of law on which the opinion is desired, together with a complete and accurate summary of relevant facts. Files, correspondence, and other original papers will not be submitted unless pertinent portions thereof cannot practicably be summarized or copies made and attached as exhibits. [42 FR 41411, Aug. 17, 1977]
