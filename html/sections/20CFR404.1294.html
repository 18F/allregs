CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 404  /  Sec. 404.1294 Notification to State after reconsideration--

(a) The State will be notified in writing of the reconsidered determination on the assessment, allowance, or disallowance, and the basis for the determination.

(b) If the State does not agree with the reconsidered determination, it has 90 days from the date of notice of the reconsidered determination to request the Commissioner to review that determination. The rules on what the request should contain and the time for filing the request are the same as in Secs. 404.1292 and 404.1293.
