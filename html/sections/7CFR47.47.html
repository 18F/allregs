CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 47  /  Sec. 47.47 Additional definitions.

The following definitions, which are in addition to those in Sec. 47.2 (a) through (h), shall be applicable to proceedings under Sec. Sec. 47.47 through 47.49.

(a) Chief means the Chief of the PACA Branch, or any officer or employee to whom authority has heretofore lawfully been delegated or to whom authority may hereafter lawfully be delegated by the Chief, to act in such capacity.

(b) PACA Branch means that PACA Branch of the Fruit and Vegetable Programs.

(c) Petition for review means the document filed requesting review by an Administrative Law Judge of the Chief's determination. [61 FR 11504, Mar. 21, 1996]
