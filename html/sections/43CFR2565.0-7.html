CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 2565  /  Sec. 2565.0-7 Cross reference.

Townsites in Alaska may be reserved by the President and sold as provided for in sections 2380 and 2381 of the Revised Statutes; 43 U.S.C. 711, 712. The regulations governing these townsites are contained in Sec. Sec. 2760.0-3 and 2761.3.
