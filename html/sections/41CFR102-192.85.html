CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 102-192  /  Sec. 102-192.85 Who must report to GSA annually?

Large agencies, as defined in Sec. 102-192.35, must provide an annual Mail Management Report to GSA. If your agency is a cabinet level or independent agency, the agency mail manager must compile all offices or components and submit one report for the department or agency as a whole, for example, the U.S. Department of Defense or the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services.
