CFR /  Title 15  /  Part 720  /  Sec. 720.4 Effect of denial.

Any person denied export privileges pursuant to this part shall be considered a ``person denied export privileges'' for purposes of the Export Administration Regulations (EAR) (15 CFR parts 730 through 774). Orders denying export privileges pursuant to Parts 764 and 766 of the EAR are published in the Federal Register when they are issued and are legally controlling documents in accordance with their terms. BIS maintains unofficial compilations of persons denied export privileges on its Web site. [71 FR 24929, Apr. 27, 2006, as amended at 73 FR 78183, Dec. 22, 2008]
