CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 1  /  Sec. 1.91 Delegations to the Federal Transit Administrator.

The Secretary delegates to the Federal Transit Administrator the authority vested in him to carry out the following:

(a) Chapter 53 of title 49, United States Code.

(b) Sections 3 and 9 through 15 of the National Capital Transportation Assistance Act of 1969, as amended (DC Code, Sec. 9-1101.01 et seq.).

(c) Sections of title 23, United States Code, that involve public transportation projects, including those provisions that pertain to environmental reviews and use of historic resources for public transportation projects.

(d) Section 303 of title 49, United States Code, as it involves public transportation projects.

(e) The following sections of SAFETEA-LU:

(1) [49 U.S.C. 5308 note], 3046 [49 U.S.C. 5338 note], 3048, 3049 [5 U.S.C. 7905 note], and 3050; and

(2) Sections 6009(b) [23 U.S.C. 138 note] and (c), and 6010, as they relate to public transit projects.

(f) Section 601 of Title VI of the Passenger Rail Investment and Improvement Act of 2008 (Pub. L. 110-432, Div. B).
