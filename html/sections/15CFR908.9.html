CFR /  Title 15  /  Part 908  /  Sec. 908.9 Retention of records.

Records required under Sec. 908.8 shall be retained and available for inspection by the Administrator or his designated representatives for 3 years after completion of the activity to which they relate. Such records shall be required to be produced for inspection only at the place where normally kept. The Administrator shall have the right to make copies of such records, if he or she deems necessary. [52 FR 4896, Feb. 18, 1987]
