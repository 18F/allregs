CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 235  /  235.006-70 Manufacturing Technology Program.

In accordance with 10 U.S.C. 2521(d), for acquisitions under the Manufacturing Technology Program--

(a) Award all contracts using competitive procedures; and

(b) Include in all solicitations an evaluation factor that addresses the extent to which offerors propose to share in the cost of the project (see FAR 15.304). [65 FR 2058, Jan. 13, 2000, as amended at 69 FR 65092, Nov. 10, 2004]
