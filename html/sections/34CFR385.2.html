CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 385  /  Sec. 385.2 Who is eligible for assistance under these programs?

States and public or nonprofit agencies and organizations, including Indian tribes and institutions of higher education, are eligible for assistance under the Rehabilitation Training program. (Authority: Secs. 7(19) and 302 of the Act; 29 U.S.C. 706(19) and 774) [59 FR 8345, Feb. 18, 1994]
