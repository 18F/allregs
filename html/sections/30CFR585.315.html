CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 585  /  Sec. 585.315 What deposits are required for a competitive ROW

(a) You must make a deposit, as required in Sec. 585.501(a), regardless of whether the auction is a sealed-bid, oral, electronic, or other auction format. BOEM will specify in the sale notice the official to whom you must submit the payment, the time by which the official must receive the payment, and the forms of acceptable payment.

(b) If your high bid is rejected, we will provide a written statement of reasons.

(c) For all rejected bids, we will refund, without interest, any money deposited with your bid.
