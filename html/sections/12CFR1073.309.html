CFR /  Title 12  /  Part 1073  /  Sec. 1073.309 Interest, penalties, and administrative costs.

Where the Bureau is the creditor agency, it shall assess interest, penalties, and administrative costs pursuant to the procedures set forth in Sec. 1073.206 and in accordance with 31 U.S.C. 3717 and 31 CFR parts 900 through 904.
