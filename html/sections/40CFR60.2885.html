CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 60  /  Sec. 60.2885 Does this subpart apply to my incineration unit?

Yes, if your incineration unit meets all the requirements specified in paragraphs (a) through (c) of this section.

(a) Your incineration unit is a new incineration unit as defined in Sec. 60.2886.

(b) Your incineration unit is an OSWI unit as defined in Sec. 60.2977 or an air curtain incinerator subject to this subpart as described in Sec. 60.2888(b). Other solid waste incineration units are very small municipal waste combustion units and institutional waste incineration units as defined in Sec. 60.2977.

(c) Your incineration unit is not excluded under Sec. 60.2887.
