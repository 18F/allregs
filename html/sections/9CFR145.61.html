CFR /  Title 9  /  Part 145  /  Sec. 145.61 Definitions.

Except where the context otherwise requires, for the purposes of this subpart the following terms shall be construed, respectively, to mean:

Chicks. Newly hatched ostriches, emus, rheas, or cassowaries.

Ostrich. Birds of the species Struthio camelus, including all subspecies and subspecies hybrids. [63 FR 40010, July 27, 1998, as amended at 65 FR 8019, Feb. 17, 2000]
