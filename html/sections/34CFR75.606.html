CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 75  /  Sec. 75.606 Completing the construction.

(a) A grantee shall complete its construction within a reasonable time.

(b) The grantee shall complete the construction in accordance with the application and approved drawings and specifications. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1221e-3 and 3474)
