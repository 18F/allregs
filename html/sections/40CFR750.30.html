CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 750  /  Sec. 750.30 Applicability.

Sections 750.30-750.41 apply to all rulemakings under authority of section 6(e)(3)(B) of the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA), 15 U.S.C. 2605(e)(3)(B) with respect to petitions for PCB processing and distribution in commerce exemptions filed pursuant to Sec. 750.31(a) of this part.
