CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 1  /  Sec. 1.552 General provisions.

(a) Additional information. The following Internet link will provide access to VA's information that is electronically available under the FOIA: http://www.foia.va.gov/.

(b) Public Liaisons. VA has made available FOIA Public Liaisons to assist in the resolution of disputes between the agency and the requester. Contact information for VA's FOIA Public Liaisons can be found on VA's FOIA home page. See Sec. 1.552(a) for the pertinent Internet address.

(c) FOIA Annual Report. Under 5 U.S.C. 552(e), VA is required to prepare an annual report regarding its FOIA activities. The report includes information about FOIA requests and appeals. Copies of VA's annual FOIA report may be obtained from VA's Chief FOIA Officer or by visiting VA's FOIA Web site. See Sec. 1.552(a) for the pertinent Internet address. [76 FR 51893, Aug. 19, 2011]
