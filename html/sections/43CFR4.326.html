CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 4  /  Sec. 4.326 What happens to the record after disposition?

(a) After the Board makes a decision other than a remand, it must forward to the designated LTRO:

(1) The record filed with the Board under Sec. 4.324(d) or (f); and

(2) All documents added during the appeal proceedings, including any transcripts and the Board's decision.

(b) The LTRO must conform the duplicate record retained under Sec. 4.324(b) to the original sent under paragraph (a) of this section and forward the duplicate record to the agency concerned. [73 FR 67288, Nov. 13, 2008]
