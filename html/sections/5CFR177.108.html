CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 177  /  Sec. 177.108 Referral to Department of Justice.

When Department of Justice approval or consultation is required, or the advice of the Department of Justice is otherwise to be requested, under Sec. 177.107, the written referral or request will be transmitted to the Department of Justice by the General Counsel of OPM or his or her designee.
