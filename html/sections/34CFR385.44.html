CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 385  /  Sec. 385.44 What requirement applies to the training of individuals

Any grantee or contractor who provides training under any of the programs in 34 CFR parts 386 through 390 and 396 shall give due regard to the training of individuals with disabilities as part of its effort to increase the number of qualified personnel available to provide rehabilitation services. (Authority: Sec. 302(a)(1) of the Act; 29 U.S.C. 774(a)(1)) [59 FR 8347, Feb. 18, 1994]
