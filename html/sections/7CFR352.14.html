CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 352  /  Sec. 352.14 Costs.

All costs incident to the inspection, handling, safeguarding, or other disposal of prohibited or restricted products or articles under the provisons in this part shall be borne by the owner. Services of the inspector during regularly assigned hours of duty at the usual places of duty shall be furnished without cost to the person requesting the services, unless a user fee is payable under Sec. 354.3 of this chapter. [56 FR 14844, Apr. 12, 1991]
