CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 385  /  Sec. 385.306 What are the consequences of furnishing misleading registration process?

A carrier that furnishes false or misleading information, or conceals material information in connection with the registration process, is subject to the following actions:

(a) Revocation of registration.

(b) Assessment of the civil and/or criminal penalties prescribed in 49 U.S.C. 521 and 49 U.S.C. chapter 149. [73 FR 76488, Dec. 16, 2008]
