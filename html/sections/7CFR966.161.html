CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 966  /  Sec. 966.161 Reapportionment of Committee Membership.

Pursuant to Sec. 966.25, industry membership on the Florida Tomato Committee shall be reapportioned as follows:

(a) District 1--two members and their alternates.

(b) District 2--two members and their alternates.

(c) District 3--four members and their alternates.

(d) District 4--four members and their alternates. [50 FR 3738, Jan. 28, 1985, as amended at 60 FR 5560, Jan. 30, 1995]
