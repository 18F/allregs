CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 222  /  Sec. 222.155 When and where is an administrative hearing held?

Administrative hearings under this subpart are held at the offices of the Department in Washington, DC, at a time fixed by the ALJ, unless the ALJ selects another place based upon the convenience of the parties. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 7711(a))
