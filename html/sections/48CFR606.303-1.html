CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 606  /  606.303-1 Requirements.

Justifications for contract actions prescribed in FAR 6.303-1(d) shall be forwarded by the contracting officer to A/OPE for transmittal to the Office of the United States Trade Representative. [53 FR 26165, July 11, 1988, as amended at 59 FR 66755, Dec. 28, 1994]
