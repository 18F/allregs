CFR /  Title 16  /  Part 18  /  Sec. 18.6 Plants collected from the wild state.

It is an unfair or deceptive act or practice to sell, offer for sale, or distribute industry products collected from the wild state without disclosing that they were collected from the wild state; provided, however, that plants propagated in nurseries from plants lawfully collected from the wild state may be designated as ``nursery-propagated.'' [Guide 6] [59 FR 64549, Dec. 14, 1994]
