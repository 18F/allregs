CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 21  /  Sec. 21.6320 Authorization of services under Chapter 31 rules.

(a) General. Sections 21.320 through 21.334 of this part are not applicable to a veteran pursuing a vocational training program except as specified in paragraph (b) of this section. (Authority: 38 U.S.C. 1524(b)(2))

(b) Applicable rule. Section 21.326 of this part pertaining to the beginning and ending dates of a period of employment services is applicable to veterans under this temporary program. (Authority: 38 U.S.C. 1524(b)(2))
