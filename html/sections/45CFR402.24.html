CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 402  /  Sec. 402.24 Withholding.

After notice and opportunity for a hearing, the Secretary may withhold payment of funds to any State which is not using its allotment in accordance with the Act, these regulations, 45 CFR part 74 (for grants awarded in FY 1988) or 45 CFR part 92 (for grants awarded in FY 1989 and succeeding fiscal years), and terms of the grant award. [56 FR 19808, Apr. 30, 1991]
