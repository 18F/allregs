CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 75  /  Sec. 75.14 When application may be withdrawn.

An application may be withdrawn at any time before the requested service is rendered. The applicant will remain responsible for payment of expenses incurred in connection therewith as provided in Sec. 75.44.
