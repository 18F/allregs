CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 17  /  Sec. 17.54 Rated lamp voltage.

To insure the necessary lumen output by obstruction lights, the rated voltage of incandescent lamps used shall correspond to be within 3 percent higher than the voltage across the lamp socket during the normal hours of operation. [42 FR 54826, Oct. 11, 1977]
