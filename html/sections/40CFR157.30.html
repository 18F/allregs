CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 157  /  Sec. 157.30 Voluntary use of child-resistant packaging.

A registrant whose product is not required to be in child-resistant packaging may distribute or sell his pesticide product in child-resistant packaging. If he does so, that packaging must meet the standards for child-resistant packaging stated in Sec. 157.32. The registrant must certify to this effect in accordance with Sec. 157.34, and must retain the records required by Sec. 157.36.
