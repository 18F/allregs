CFR /  Title 32  /  Part 884  /  Sec. 884.16 Reporting requests for assistance and action.

The commander or designee promptly reports each request for assistance and intended action by message. Send reports to HQ AFLSA/JAJM, which submits required reports, through channels, to USD/P&R;, HQ AFLSA/JAJM conducts all communications with requesters.
