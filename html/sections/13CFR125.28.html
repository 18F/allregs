CFR /  Title 13  /  Part 125  /  Sec. 125.28 What are the procedures for appealing an SDVO status

The protested concern, the protester, or the contracting officer may file an appeal of an SDVO status protest determination with OHA in accordance with part 134 of this chapter. [76 FR 5685, Feb. 2, 2011]
