CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 69  /  Sec. 69.408 All other customer services expenses in Account 6620.

All other customer services expenses shall be apportioned among the Interexchange category, the billing and collection category and all access elements based on the combined expenses in Sec. Sec. 69.404 through 69.407. [52 FR 37313, Oct. 6, 1987, as amended at 54 FR 3456, Jan. 24, 1989]
