CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 838  /  Sec. 838.931 Court orders that provide temporary awards of former

A provision in a court order that temporarily awards a former spouse survivor annuity satisfies the requirements of Sec. 838.804(b)(2), but the temporary award becomes permanent on the date on which OPM is barred from honoring a modification of the court order (the date of retirement or death, or, in the case of a post-retirement divorce, the date of the initial court order), as provided in sections 8341(h)(4) and 8445(d) of title 5, United States Code. [57 FR 33574, July 29, 1992, as amended at 58 FR 43493, Aug. 17, 1993]
