CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 89  /  Sec. 89.21 Purpose.

Inland Navigation Rules 9(a)(ii), 14(d), and 15(b) apply to the Great Lakes, and along with 24(i), apply on the ``Western Rivers'' as defined in Rule 3(1), and to additional specifically designated waters. The purpose of this Subpart is to specify those additional waters upon which Inland Navigation Rules 9(a)(ii), 14(d), 15(b), and 24(i) apply. [CGD 85-081, 51 FR 4592, Feb. 6, 1986]
