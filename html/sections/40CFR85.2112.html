CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 85  /  Sec. 85.2112 Applicability.

The provisions of Secs. 85.2112 through 85.2122 apply to emission related automotive aftermarket parts which are to be installed in or on 1968 and later model year light-duty vehicles and light-duty trucks. [54 FR 32588, Aug. 8, 1989]
