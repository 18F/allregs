CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 493  /  Sec. 493.1234 Standard: Communications.

The laboratory must have a system in place to identify and document problems that occur as a result of a breakdown in communication between the laboratory and an authorized person who orders or receives test results. [68 FR 3703, Jan. 24, 2003; 68 FR 50724, Aug. 22, 2003]
