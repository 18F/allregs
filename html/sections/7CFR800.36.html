CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 800  /  Sec. 800.36 Certificates of registration.

The Service shall furnish the applicant with an original and three copies of the registration certificate. The registration shall be effective on the issue date shown on the certificate. Each certificate of registration is issued on the condition that the registrant will comply with all provisions of the Act, regulations, and instructions. The Service shall charge a fee, in accordance with Sec. 800.71, for each additional copy of a certificate of registration requested by a registrant. [48 FR 44456, Sept. 29, 1983]
