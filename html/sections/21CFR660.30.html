CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 660  /  Sec. 660.30 Reagent Red Blood Cells.

(a) Proper name and definition. The proper name of the product shall be Reagent Red Blood Cells, which shall consist of a preparation of human red blood cells used to detect or identify human blood-group antibodies.

(b) Source. Reagent Red Blood Cells shall be prepared from human peripheral blood meeting the criteria of Sec. Sec. 660.31 and 660.32 of this chapter, or from umbilical cord cells which shall be collected and prepared according to the manufacturer's biologics license application. [52 FR 37450, Oct. 7, 1987, as amended at 64 FR 56454, Oct. 20, 1999]
