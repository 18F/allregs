CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 105-68  /  Sec. 105-68.440 What method do I use to communicate those

To communicate the requirement, you must include a term or condition in the transaction requiring the participants' compliance with subpart C of this part and requiring them to include a similar term or condition in lower-tier covered transactions. [68 FR 66627, Nov. 26, 2003]
