CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 27  /  Sec. 27.12 Eligibility.

(a) Except as provided in paragraph (b) and in Sec. Sec. 27.604, 27.1201, and 27.1202, any entity other than those precluded by section 310 of the Communications Act of 1934, as amended, 47 U.S.C. 310, is eligible to hold a license under this part.

(b) A person described in 47 U.S.C. 1404(c) is ineligible to hold a license that is required by 47 U.S.C. Chapter 13 (Middle Class Tax Relief and Job Creation Act of 2012 (Pub. L. 112-96, 125 Stat. 156 (2012)) to be assigned by a system of competitive bidding under Sec. 309(j) of the Communications Act, 47 U.S.C. 309(j). [78 FR 50254, Aug. 16, 2013]
