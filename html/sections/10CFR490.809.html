CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 490  /  Sec. 490.809 Violations.

If a State or covered person that received a waiver under this subpart fails to comply with the petroleum motor fuel reduction or reporting requirements of this subpart, DOE will revoke the waiver and may impose on the State or covered person a penalty under subpart G of this part. A State or covered person whose waiver has been revoked by DOE is precluded from requesting an exemption under Sec. 490.204 or Sec. 490.307 from the vehicle acquisition mandate for the model year of the revoked waiver. [79 FR 15907, Mar. 21, 2014]
