CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 709  /  709.403 Definitions.

Debarring official in USAID is the Assistant Administrator, Bureau for Management, or designee as delegated in Agency policy found in ADS 103--Delegations of Authority.

Suspending official in USAID is the Assistant Administrator, Bureau for Management, or designee as delegated in Agency policy found in ADS 103--Delegations of Authority. [79 FR 74992, Dec. 16, 2014; 80 FR 12935, Mar. 12, 2015]
