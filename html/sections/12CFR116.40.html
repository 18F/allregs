CFR /  Title 12  /  Part 116  /  Sec. 116.40 Where do I file my application?

(a) OCC Office. (1) You must file the original application and the number of copies indicated on the applicable form to the attention of the Director for Licensing at the appropriate OCC licensing office listed in paragraph (a)(2) of this section or with the OCC licensing office at OCC headquarters. If the form does not indicate the number of copies you must file or if the OCC has not prescribed a form for your application, you must file the original application and two copies.

If the form does not indicate the number of copies you must file or if the OCC has not prescribed a form for your application, you must file the original application and two copies.

(2) The addresses of appropriate OCC licensing offices and the states covered by each office are listed in 12 CFR 4.5.

(b) Additional filings with OCC headquarters. (1) In addition to filing in the appropriate OCC licensing office, if your application involves a significant issue of law or policy or if an applicable regulation or form directs you to file with OCC headquarters, you must also file copies of your application at the OCC licensing office at headquarters. You must file the number of copies indicated on the applicable form. If the form does not indicate the number of copies you must file or if the OCC has not prescribed a form for your application, you must file three copies.
