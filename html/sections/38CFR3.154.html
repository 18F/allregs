CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 3  /  Sec. 3.154 Injury due to hospital treatment, etc.

Claimants must file a complete claim on the appropriate application form prescribed by the Secretary when applying for benefits under 38 U.S.C. 1151 and 38 CFR 3.361. See Sec. Sec. 3.151, 3.160(a), and 3.400(i) concerning effective dates of awards; see Sec. 3.155(b) regarding intent to file the appropriate application form. (Authority: 38 U.S.C. 501 and 1151.)

Cross Reference: Effective Dates. See Sec. 3.400(i). Disability or death due to hospitalization, etc. See Sec. Sec. 3.358, 3.361 and 3.800. [79 FR 57695, Sept. 25, 2014]
