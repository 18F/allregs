CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 1519  /  1519.705-70 Synopsis of contracts containing Pub. L. 95-507

The synopsis of contract award, where applicable, shall include a statement identifying the contract as one containing Pub. L. 95-507 subcontracting plans and goals. [49 FR 8855, Mar. 8, 1984; 49 FR 24734, June 15, 1984]
