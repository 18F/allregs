CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 1355  /  Sec. 1355.31 Elements of the child and family services review system.

Scope. Sections 1355.32 through 1355.37 of this part apply to reviews of child and family services programs under subparts 1 and 2 of title IV-B of the Act, and reviews of foster care and adoption assistance programs under title IV-E of the Act. [77 FR 926, Jan. 6, 2012]
