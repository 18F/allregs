CFR /  Title 25  /  Part 573  /  Sec. 573.5 When does an enforcement action become final agency action?

An enforcement action shall become final agency action and a final order of the Commission when:

(a) A respondent fails to appeal the enforcement action as provided for in subchapter H of this chapter and does not enter into a settlement agreement resolving the matter in its entirety; or

(b) A respondent enters into a settlement agreement resolving the matter in its entirety at any time after the issuance of the enforcement action. [77 FR 47519, Aug. 9, 2012, as amended at 78 FR 4324, Jan. 22, 2013]
