CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 400  /  Sec. 400.410 Penalties.

Unauthorized disclosure of SSN's or EIN's by any person may subject that person, and the person soliciting the unauthorized disclosure, to civil or criminal sanctions imposed under various Federal statutes, including 26 U.S.C. 7613, 5 U.S.C. 552a, and 42 U.S.C. 408. [57 FR 46297, Oct. 8, 1992. Redesignated at 62 FR 28608, May 27, 1997]
