CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 308  /  Sec. 308.600 Records retention requirement.

The records specified in Sec. Sec. 308.8, 308.517, and 308.548 of this part shall be retained until a release is granted by MARAD, at which time MARAD will take custody of the records. (Authority: 46 U.S.C. sections 53902, 53910; 49 CFR 1.93)
