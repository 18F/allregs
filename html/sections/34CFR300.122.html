CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 300  /  Sec. 300.122 Evaluation.

Children with disabilities must be evaluated in accordance with Secs. 300.300 through 300.311 of subpart D of this part. (Approved by the Office of Management and Budget under control number 1820-0030) (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1412(a)(7))
