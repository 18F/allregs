CFR /  Title 31  /  Part 598  /  Sec. 598.304 Foreign Narcotics Kingpin Designation Act.

The term Foreign Narcotics Kingpin Designation Act means the Foreign Narcotics Kingpin Designation Act, Pub. L. 106-120, title 8, 113 Stat. 1606, 1626-1636 (codified at 21 U.S.C. 1901-1908, 8 U.S.C. 1182(a)(2)(C)).
