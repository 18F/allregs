CFR /  Title 2  /  Part 200  /  Sec. 200.309 Period of performance.

A non-Federal entity may charge to the Federal award only allowable costs incurred during the period of performance (except as described in Sec. 200.461 Publication and printing costs) and any costs incurred before the Federal awarding agency or pass-through entity made the Federal award that were authorized by the Federal awarding agency or pass-through entity. [78 FR 78608, Dec. 26, 2013, as amended at 79 FR 75884, Dec. 19, 2014]
