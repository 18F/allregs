CFR /  Title 2  /  Part 25  /  Sec. 25.210 Authority to modify agency application forms or formats.

To implement the policies in Sec. Sec. 25.200 and 25.205, an agency may add a unique entity identifier field to application forms or formats previously approved by OMB, without having to obtain further approval to add the field. [75 FR 55673, Sept. 14, 2010, as amended at 79 FR 75879, Dec. 19, 2014]
