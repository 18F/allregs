CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 52  /  Sec. 52.32 Sanctions following findings of SIP inadequacy.

For purposes of the SIP revisions required by Sec. 51.120, EPA may make a finding under section 179(a) (1)-(4) of the Clean Air Act, 42 U.S.C. 7509(a) (1)-(4), starting the sanctions process set forth in section 179(a) of the Clean Air Act. Any such finding will be deemed a finding under Sec. 52.31(c) and sanctions will be imposed in accordance with the order of sanctions and the terms for such sanctions established in Sec. 52.31. [60 FR 4737, Jan. 24, 1995]
