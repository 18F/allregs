CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 97  /  Sec. 97.17 Papers of completed application to be retained.

The papers submitted with a completed application shall be retained by the Office except as provided in Sec. 97.23(c). After issuance of a certificate of protection the Office will furnish copies of the application and related papers to any person upon payment of the specified fee.
