CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 917  /  Sec. 917.103 Terms.

All other terms used in this subpart shall have the same meaning as when used in the marketing agreement and order. [18 FR 712, Feb. 4, 1953. Redesignated at 26 FR 12751, Dec. 30, 1961]

Effective Date Note: At 76 FR 66605, Oct. 27, 2011, Sec. 917.103 was suspended indefinitely, effective Oct. 28, 2011.
