CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 225  /  225.772-5 Solicitation provision.

Use the provision at 252.225-7049, Prohibition on Acquisition of Commercial Satellite Services from Certain Foreign Entities-- Representations, in solicitations for the acquisition of commercial satellite services. If the solicitation includes the provision at FAR 52.204-7, do not separately list the provision 252.225-7049 in the solicitation. [79 FR 45664, Aug. 5, 2014]
