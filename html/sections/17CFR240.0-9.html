CFR /  Title 17  /  Part 240  /  Sec. 240.0-9 Payment of fees.

All payment of fees shall be made by wire transfer, or by certified check, bank cashier's check, United States postal money order, or bank money order payable to the Securities and Exchange Commission, omitting the name or title of any official of the Commission. Payment of filing fees required by this section shall be made in accordance with the directions set forth in Sec. 202.3a of this chapter. [72 FR 6014, Feb. 1, 2008]
