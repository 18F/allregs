CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 2620  /  Sec. 2620.1 General statement.

This part is issued in accordance with, and subject to, the regulations of the Secretary of Agriculture Sec. 1.1 through Sec. 1.23 (and appendix A of subpart A of part 1) of this title, implementing the Freedom of Information Act, 5 U.S.C. 552, and governs the availability of records of the Office of Inspector General (OIG) to the public upon request.
