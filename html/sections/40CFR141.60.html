CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 141  /  Sec. 141.60 Effective dates.

(a) The effective dates for Sec. 141.61 are as follows:

(1) The effective date for paragraphs (a)(1) through (a)(8) of Sec. 141.61 is January 9, 1989.

(2) The effective date for paragraphs (a)(9) through (a)(18) and (c)(1) through (c)(18) of Sec. 141.61 is July 30, 1992.

(3) The effective date for paragraphs (a)(19) through (a)(21), (c)(19) through (c)(25), and (c)(27) through (c)(33) of Sec. 141.61 is January 17, 1994. The effective date of Sec. 141.61(c)(26) is August 17, 1992.

(b) The effective dates for Sec. 141.62 are as follows:

(1) The effective date of paragraph (b)(1) of Sec. 141.62 is October 2, 1987.

(2) The effective date for paragraphs (b)(2) and (b)(4) through (b)(10) of Sec. 141.62 is July 30, 1992.

(3) The effective date for paragraphs (b)(11) through (b)(15) of Sec. 141.62 is January 17, 1994.

(4) The effective date for Sec. 141.62(b)(16) is January 23, 2006. [56 FR 3593, Jan. 30, 1991, as amended at 57 FR 31846, July 17, 1992; 59 FR 34324, July 1, 1994; 66FR 7063, Jan. 22, 2001]
