CFR /  Title 37  /  Part 352  /  Sec. 352.1 How made.

Except for decisions authorized by law to be made by a single Copyright Royalty Judge, determinations in a proceeding will be made by a majority of the Copyright Royalty Judges. The opinion or opinions of the majority and any dissenting opinion will be included in the determination. Each determination will be transmitted to the Register of Copyrights to enable review for consistency with the Copyright Act on the day it is issued. [70 FR 30905, May 31, 2005, as amended at 71 FR 53330, Sept. 11, 2006]
