CFR /  Title 22  /  Part 1101  /  Sec. 1101.15 Judicial review.

After having exhausted all administrative remedies set forth in Sec. 1101.7(g)(3) or Sec. 1101.14, a requester may bring a civil action against the Section, in a United States District Court of proper venue, within two years of the final administrative decision which the requester seeks to challenge.
