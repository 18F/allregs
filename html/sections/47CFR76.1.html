CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 76  /  Sec. 76.1 Purpose.

The rules and regulations set forth in this part provide for the certification of cable television systems and for their operation in conformity with standards for carriage of television broadcast signals, program exclusivity, cablecasting, access channels, and related matters. The rules and regulations in this part also describe broadcast carriage requirements for cable operators and satellite carriers. [37 FR 3278, Feb. 12, 1972, as amended at 70 FR 21670, Apr. 27, 2005]
