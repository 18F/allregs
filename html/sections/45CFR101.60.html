CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 101  /  Sec. 101.60 General provisions.

(a) HHS may take specific official actions to implement the provisions of this subpart.

(b) These official actions include, but are limited to, Rating Authorizations, Directives, and Memoranda of Understanding (See Sec. 101.20.)
