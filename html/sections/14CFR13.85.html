CFR /  Title 14  /  Part 13  /  Sec. 13.85 Filing, service and computation of time.

Filing and service of documents under this subpart shall be accomplished in accordance with Sec. 13.43 except service of orders of immediate compliance under Sec. 13.81(b); and the periods of time specified in this subpart shall be computed in accordance with Sec. 13.44.
