CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 70  /  Sec. 70.72 Fees for appeal grading or review of a grader's decision.

The costs of an appeal grading, or review of a grader's decision, shall be borne by the appellant on a fee basis at rates determined based on the formulas in Sec. 70.71. If the appeal grading, or review of a grader's decision discloses that a material error was made in the original determination, no fee or expenses will be charged. [79 FR 67324, Nov. 13, 2014]
