CFR /  Title 28  /  Part 42  /  Sec. 42.212 Preliminary hearing.

(a) Prior to the suspension of funds under Sec. 42.210(a), but within the 90-day period after notification under Sec. 42.208, the State government or unit of local government may request an expedited preliminary hearing on the record in accordance with 5 U.S.C. 554 in order to determine whether it is likely that the State government or unit of local government would, at a full hearing under Sec. 42.213, prevail on the merits on the issue of the alleged noncompliance.

(b) The preliminary hearing shall be initiated within 30 days of request. The ALJ shall make his finding within 15 days after the conclusion of the preliminary hearing.
