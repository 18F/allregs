CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 408  /  Sec. 408.3 Definitions.

As used in this part, unless the context indicates otherwise--

Enrollee means an individual who is enrolled in the SMI program under Medicare Part B.

Taxable year means the 12-month period (calendar or fiscal year) for which the individual files his or her income tax return.
