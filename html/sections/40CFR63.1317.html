CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 63  /  Sec. 63.1317 PET and polystyrene affected sources--monitoring provisions.

Continuous process vents using a control or recovery device to comply with Sec. 63.1316 shall comply with the applicable monitoring provisions specified for continuous process vents in Sec. 63.1315(a), except that references to group determinations (i.e., total resource effectiveness) do not apply and owners or operators are not required to comply with Sec. 63.113.[65 FR 38111, June 19, 2000]
