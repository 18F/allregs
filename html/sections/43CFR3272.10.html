CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 3272  /  Sec. 3272.10 What must I submit to BLM in my utilization plan?

Submit to BLM an application describing:

(a) The proposed facilities as required by Sec. 3272.11; and

(b) The anticipated environmental impacts and how you propose to mitigate those impacts, as required by Sec. 3272.12.
