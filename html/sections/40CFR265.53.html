CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 265  /  Sec. 265.53 Copies of contingency plan.

A copy of the contingency plan and all revisions to the plan must be:

(a) Maintained at the facility; and

(b) Submitted to all local police departments, fire departments, hospitals, and State and local emergency response teams that may be called upon to provide emergency services. [45 FR 33232, May 19, 1980, as amended at 50 FR 4514, Jan. 31, 1985]
