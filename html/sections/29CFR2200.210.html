CFR /  Title 29  /  Part 2200  /  Sec. 2200.210 Review of Judge's decision.

Any party may petition for Commission review of the Judge's decision as provided in Sec. 2200.91. After the issuance of the Judge's written decision or order, the parties may pursue the case following the rules in subpart F.
