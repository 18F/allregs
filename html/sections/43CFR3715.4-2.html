CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 3715  /  Sec. 3715.4-2 What if I do not notify BLM of my existing occupancy?

If you do not provide the written notice required in Sec. 3715.4, you will be subject to the enforcement actions of Sec. 3715.7-1, the civil remedies of Sec. 3715.7-2, and the criminal penalties of Sec. 3715.8.
