CFR /  Title 14  /  Part 27  /  Sec. 27.399 Dual control system.

Each dual primary flight control system must be designed to withstand the loads that result when pilot forces of 0.75 times those obtained under Sec. 27.395 are applied--

(a) In opposition; and

(b) In the same direction.
