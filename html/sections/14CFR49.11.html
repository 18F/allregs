CFR /  Title 14  /  Part 49  /  Sec. 49.11 FAA Aircraft Registry.

To be eligible for recording, a conveyance must be mailed to the FAA Aircraft Registry, Department of Transportation, Post Office Box 25504, Oklahoma City, Oklahoma 73125-0504, or delivered to the Registry at 6425 S. Denning Ave., Oklahoma City, Oklahoma 73169. [Amdt. 49-10, 70 FR 246, Jan. 3, 2005]
