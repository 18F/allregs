CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 117  /  Sec. 117.443 Du Large Bayou.

The draw of the Terrebonne Parish bridge, mile 23.2, near Theriot, shall open on signal; except that, from 9 p.m. to 5 a.m., the draw shall open on signal if at least 12 hours notice is given. [CGD 08-84-07, 50 FR 24195, June 10, 1985]
