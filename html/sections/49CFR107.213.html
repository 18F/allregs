CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 107  /  Sec. 107.213 Judicial review.

A party to a proceeding under Sec. 107.203(a) may seek review of a determination of the Chief Counsel by filing a petition, within 60 days after the determination becomes final, in the United States Court of Appeals for the District of Columbia or in the Court of Appeals for the United States for the circuit in which the person resides or has its principal place of business. [71 FR 30068, May 25, 2006]
