CFR /  Title 27  /  Part 28  /  Sec. 28.151 General.

Specially denatured spirits may, under this part, be withdrawn from the bonded premises of a distilled spirits plant, free of tax, for:

(a) Exportation; or

(b) Transfer to and deposit in a foreign-trade zone for exportation or for storage pending exportation. All such withdrawals shall be made under a consent of surety on the proprietor's operations or unit bond, as prescribed in Sec. 28.58(c).
