CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 252  /  252.203-7003 Agency Office of the Inspector General.

As prescribed in 203.1004(a), use the following clause:

Agency Office of the Inspector General (DEC 2012)

The agency office of the Inspector General referenced in paragraphs (c) and (d) of FAR clause 52.203-13, Contractor Code of Business Ethics and Conduct, is the DoD Office of Inspector General at the following address: Department of Defense Office of Inspector General, Investigative Policy and Oversight, Contractor Disclosure Program, 4800 Mark Center Drive, Suite 11H25, Alexandria, VA 22350-1500. Toll Free Telephone: 866-429-8011.

(End of clause) [77 FR 76937, Dec. 31, 2012]
