CFR /  Title 25  /  Part 1000  /  Sec. 1000.306 Must the Secretary always reassume a program,

Yes, the Secretary must reassume a program within 60 days of a finding of imminent jeopardy, unless the Secretary's designated representative determines that the Tribe/Consortium is able to mitigate the conditions.
