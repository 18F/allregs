CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 2401  /  2401.106 OMB approval under the Paperwork Reduction Act.

The Paperwork Reduction Act of 1980 (44 U.S.C. 3501-3520) requires that Federal agencies obtain approval from the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) before collecting information from 10 or more persons. HUD has received approval from OMB to collect information under the provisions of its Acquisition Regulation. The OMB Approval Number is 2535-0091. [50 FR 46575, Nov. 8, 1985. Redesignated at 61 FR 19470, May 1, 1996]
