CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 1944  /  Sec. 1944.69 Agency point of contact.

Grantees must submit packages to the appropriate Agency office serving the designated county and/or colonias. Packages for Single Family Housing loans/grants are submitted to the appropriate County Office. All other packages are submitted to the appropriate District Office. The applicable forms required to develop a package can be obtained in any District or County Office. Packagers should coordinate their packaging activity with the appropriate District and County Offices. [58 FR 58643, Nov. 3, 1993, as amended at 61 FR 39851, July 31, 1996]
