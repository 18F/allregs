CFR /  Title 14  /  Part 61  /  Sec. 61.117 Private pilot privileges and limitations: Second in

Except as provided in Sec. 61.113 of this part, no private pilot may, for compensation or hire, act as second in command of an aircraft that is type certificated for more than one pilot, nor may that pilot act as second in command of such an aircraft that is carrying passengers or property for compensation or hire.
