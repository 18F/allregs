CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 180  /  Sec. 180.1246 Yeast Extract Hydrolysate from Saccharomyces cerevisiae:

This regulation establishes an exemption from the requirement of a tolerance for residues of the biochemical pesticide Yeast Extract Hydrolysate from Saccharomyces cerevisiae on all food commodities when applied/used for the management of plant diseases. [69 FR 9958, Mar. 3, 2004]
