CFR /  Title 27  /  Part 19  /  Sec. 19.577 Documents that are not records.

The term ``records'' as used in this subpart does not include qualifying documents required under subpart D of this part, or bonds required under subpart F of this part. Approved active formulas, plant registrations and similar records are permanent in nature and must be maintained in a permanent file. (26 U.S.C. 5207)
