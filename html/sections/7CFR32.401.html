CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 32  /  Sec. 32.401 Cost of samples for grease mohair grades.

(a) Complete set. $22 each, delivered to any destination with the United States and $25 each, delivered to any destination outside the United States.

(b) Individual sample. $2.50 each, delivered to any destination within the United States, and $3 each, delivered to any destination outside the United States.
