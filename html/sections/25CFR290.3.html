CFR /  Title 25  /  Part 290  /  Sec. 290.3 Information collection.

The information collection requirements contained in Sec. Sec. 290.12, 290.17, 290.24 and 290.26 have been approved by the OMB under the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995, 44 U.S.C. 3507(d), and assigned clearance number 1076-0152.
