CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 154  /  Sec. 154.540 Discharge removal.

Each facility to which this part applies must have a means to safely remove discharged oil or hazardous material, within one hour of completion of the transfer, from the containment required by Sec. 154.530 of this part without discharging the oil or hazardous material into the water. [CGD 93-056, 61 FR 41460, Aug. 8, 1996]
