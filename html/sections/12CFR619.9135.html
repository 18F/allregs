CFR /  Title 12  /  Part 619  /  Sec. 619.9135 Direct lender.

The term direct lender refers to Farm Credit banks and associations (production credit associations, agricultural credit associations, and Federal land credit associations) authorized to lend to eligible borrowers identified in Sec. 613.3000. [55 FR 24889, June 19, 1990]
