CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 640  /  Sec. 640.94 Labeling.

In addition to the labeling requirements of Sec. Sec. 610.60, 610.61, and 610.62 of this chapter, the container and package labels shall contain the following information:

(a) The osmotic equivalent in terms of plasma, and the sodium concentration in terms of a value or a range in milliequivalents per liter.

(b) The cautionary statement placed in a prominent position on the label, ``Do Not Use if Turbid. Do Not Begin Administration More than 4 Hours After the Container Has Been Entered.'' [42 FR 27583, May 31, 1977, as amended at 49 FR 2244, Jan. 19, 1984; 64 FR 26286, May 14, 1999]
