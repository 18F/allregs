CFR /  Title 12  /  Part 561  /  Sec. 561.42 Savings account.

The term savings account means any withdrawable account, except a demand account as defined in Sec. 561.16 of this chapter, a tax and loan account, a note account, a United States Treasury general account, or a United States Treasury time deposit-open account. [54 FR 49545, Nov. 30, 1989, as amended at 62 FR 54765, Oct. 22, 1997]
