CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 792  /  Sec. 792.103 Coverage.

This part applies to all positions in Executive agencies as defined in section 105 of title 5 of the United States Code, and to those positions in the legislative and judicial branch of the Federal Government which are in the competitive service. [49 FR 27921, July 9, 1984]
