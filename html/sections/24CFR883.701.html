CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 883  /  Sec. 883.701 Cross-reference.

All of the provisions of part 880, subpart F, of this chapter apply to projects assisted under this part, subject to the requirements of Sec. 883.105. For purposes of this subpart G, all references in part 880, subpart F, of this chapter to ``contract administrator'' shall be construed to refer to ``Agency''. [61 FR 13593, Mar. 27, 1996]
