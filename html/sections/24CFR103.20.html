CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 103  /  Sec. 103.20 Can someone help me with filing a claim?

HUD's Office of Fair Housing and Equal Opportunity can help you in filing a claim, if you contact them directly. You, or anyone who acts for you, may also ask any HUD office or an organization, individual, or attorney to help you. [64 FR 18540, Apr. 14, 1999]
