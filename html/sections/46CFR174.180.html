CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 174  /  Sec. 174.180 Applicability.

This subpart applies to OSVs except liftboats inspected under subchapter L of this chapter and OSVs of at least 6,000 GT ITC (500 GRT if GT ITC is not assigned) as defined in Sec. 125.160 of this chapter. [USCG-2012-0208, 79 FR 48939, Aug. 18, 2014]
