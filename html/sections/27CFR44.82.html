CFR /  Title 27  /  Part 44  /  Sec. 44.82 Application for permit.

Every person, before commencing business as an export warehouse proprietor, must apply on TTB Form 2093 (5200.3) and obtain the permit provided for in Sec. 44.93. All documents required under this part to be furnished with such application shall be made a part thereof. (72 Stat. 1421; 26 U.S.C. 5712) [25 FR 4716, May 28, 1960. Redesignated at 40 FR 16835, Apr. 15, 1975, as amended by T.D. ATF-480, 67 FR 30801, May 8, 2002]
