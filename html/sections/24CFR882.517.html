CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 882  /  Sec. 882.517 HUD review of contract compliance.

HUD will review program operations at such intervals as it deems necessary to ensure that the Owner and the PHA are in full compliance with the terms and conditions of the Contract and the ACC. Equal Opportunity review may be conducted with the scheduled HUD review or at any time deemed appropriate by HUD. [43 FR 61246, Dec. 29, 1978. Redesignated at 63 FR 23854, Apr. 30, 1998]
