CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 87  /  Sec. 87.447 Supplemental eligibility.

An applicant for an operational fixed station must certify that:

(a) The applicant is the licensee of an aeronautical land station in the aeronautical mobile service; and

(b) Common carrier facilities are not available to satisfy the aeronautical station's requirements. [53 FR 28940, Aug. 1, 1988, as amended at 63 FR 68958, Dec. 14, 1998]
