CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 26  /  Sec. 26.1202 Definitions.

The definitions in Sec. 26.1102 shall be applicable to this subpart as well. In addition, the definitions at 45 CFR 46.202(a) through (f) and at 45 CFR 46.202(h) are applicable to this subpart. In addition, a child is a person who has not attained the age of 18 years.
