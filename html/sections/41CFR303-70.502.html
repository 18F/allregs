CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 303-70  /  Sec. 303-70.502 Must we continue payment of relocation expenses for an new official station within CONUS, but the family was in transit to the new official station or had not begun its en route travel?

Yes, if the immediate family chooses to continue the relocation, you must continue payment of relocation expenses for the immediate family, provided the immediate family was included on the employee's relocation travel orders. (See Sec. 303-70.503.)
