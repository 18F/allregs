CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 920  /  Sec. 920.23 Selection.

From the nominations made pursuant to Sec. 920.22, or from other qualified persons, the Secretary shall select the 12 members of the committee and an alternate for each such member, with the exception of the public member and alternate member, who shall be selected by the Secretary in his discretion.
