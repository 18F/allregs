CFR /  Title 9  /  Part 91  /  Sec. 91.30 Defective fittings.

If previously used fittings aboard an ocean vessel are employed, any portion thereof found by the inspector to be worn, decayed, unsound, or otherwise defective shall be replaced. [42 FR 28990, June 7, 1977. Redesignated at 45 FR 86413, Dec. 31, 1980]
