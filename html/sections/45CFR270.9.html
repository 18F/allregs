CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 270  /  Sec. 270.9 How will we redistribute funds if that becomes necessary?

(a) If we cannot distribute the funds as specified in Sec. 270.8, we will reallocate any undistributed funds among the measures listed in Sec. 270.4.

(b) If we still cannot distribute funds within the bonus year, they will remain available for distribution in the next bonus year, to the extent authorized by law.
