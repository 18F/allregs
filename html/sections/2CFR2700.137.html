CFR /  Title 2  /  Part 2700  /  Sec. 2700.137 Who in the Small Business Administration may covered transaction?

The Director of the Office of Credit Risk Management may grant an exception permitting an excluded person to participate in a particular covered transaction under SBA's financial assistance programs. For all other Agency programs, the Associate General Counsel for Procurement Law may grant such an exception. [72 FR 39728, July 20, 2007, as amended at 73 FR 43348, July 25, 2008]
