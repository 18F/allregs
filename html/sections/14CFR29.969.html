CFR /  Title 14  /  Part 29  /  Sec. 29.969 Fuel tank expansion space.

Each fuel tank or each group of fuel tanks with interconnected vent systems must have an expansion space of not less than 2 percent of the combined tank capacity. It must be impossible to fill the fuel tank expansion space inadvertently with the rotorcraft in the normal ground attitude. [Amdt. 29-26, 53 FR 34217, Sept. 2, 1988]
