CFR /  Title 17  /  Part 209  /  Sec. 209.0-1 Availability of forms.

(a) This part identifies and describes the forms for use under the Securities and Exchange Commission's Rules of Practice, part 201 of this chapter.

(b) Any person may obtain a copy of any form prescribed for use in this part by written request to the Securities and Exchange Commission, 100 F Street, NE., Washington, D.C. 20549. Any person may inspect the forms at this address and at the Commission's regional offices. (See Sec. 200.11 of this chapter for the addresses of the SEC regional offices.) [60 FR 32823, June 23, 1995, as amended at 73 FR 32227, June 5, 2008]
