CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 930  /  Sec. 930.256 Free and restricted percentages for the 2014-15 crop year.

The percentages for tart cherries handled by handlers during the crop year beginning on July 1, 2014, which shall be free and restricted, respectively, are designated as follows: Free percentage, 80 percent and restricted percentage, 20 percent. [80 FR 30923, June 1, 2015]
