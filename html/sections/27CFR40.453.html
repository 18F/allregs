CFR /  Title 27  /  Part 40  /  Sec. 40.453 Use of the United States.

A manufacturer of cigarette papers and tubes may remove cigarette papers and tubes covered under bond, without payment of tax, for use of the United States. Such removal shall be in accordance with the provisions of part 45 of this chapter. (72 Stat. 1418; 26 U.S.C. 5704) [26 FR 8174, Aug. 31, 1961.
