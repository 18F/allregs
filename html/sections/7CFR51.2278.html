CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 51  /  Sec. 51.2278 U.S. Commercial.

``U.S. Commercial'' consists of portions of walnut kernels which meet the requirements of U.S. No. 1 grade, except for increased tolerances. (See Sec. 51.2280.)

(a) Color of walnuts in this grade shall be not darker than ``amber'' classification, and color need not be specified. However, color may be specified in connection with the grade in terms of one of the color classifications. (See Sec. Sec. 51.2276, 51.2281 and 51.2282.)

(b) Size shall be specified in connection with this grade in terms of one of the size classifications. (See Sec. Sec. 51.2284 and 51.2285.)
