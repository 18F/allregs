CFR /  Title 12  /  Part 747  /  Sec. 747.305 Effectiveness of suspension or removal until completion

Any notice of suspension or prohibition issued under Sec. 747.303 and any order of removal or prohibition issued under Sec. 747.304 will be effective upon service on the concerned party and will remain effective and outstanding until the completion of any hearing or appeal authorized under section 206(i) of the Act (12 U.S.C. 1786(i)) and this subpart, unless such notice of suspension or order of removal is terminated by the NCUA Board. [56 FR 37767, Aug. 8, 1991; 57 FR 523, Jan. 7, 1992]
