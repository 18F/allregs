CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 160  /  Sec. 160.5 Standards for naval stores.

In addition to the standards of identity for spirits of turpentine and rosin and the grade designations for rosin specified in the act, certain standards for naval stores have been promulgated by the Administrator pursuant to the act as indicated in Sec. 160.301 et seq.
