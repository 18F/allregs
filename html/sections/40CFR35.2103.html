CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 35  /  Sec. 35.2103 Priority determination.

The project shall be entitled to priority in accordance with Sec. 35.2015, and the award of grant assistance for the project shall not jeopardize the funding of any project of higher priority under the approved priority system.
