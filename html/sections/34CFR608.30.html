CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 608  /  Sec. 608.30 What is the procedure for approving and disapproving

The Secretary--

(a) Approves any application that satisfies the requirements of Sec. 608.10 and Sec. 608.20; and

(b) Does not disapprove any application, or any modification of an application, without affording the applicant reasonable notice and opportunity for a hearing. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1063a)
