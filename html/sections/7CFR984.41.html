CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 984  /  Sec. 984.41 Vacancy.

Any vacancy occasioned by the removal, resignation, disqualification, or death of any member of alternate, or any need to select a successor through failure of any person selected as a member or alternate to qualify, shall be recognized by the Board causing a nomination to be made by the appropriate group and certifying to the Secretary a new nominee within 60 calendar days. [39 FR 35330, Oct. 1, 1974, as amended at 41 FR 31543, July 29, 1976]
