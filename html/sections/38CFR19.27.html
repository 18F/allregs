CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 19  /  Sec. 19.27 Adequacy of Notice of Disagreement questioned within the

If, after following the procedures set forth in 38 CFR 19.26, there remains within the agency of original jurisdiction a conflict of opinion or a question pertaining to a claim regarding whether a written communication expresses an intent to appeal or as to which denied claims a claimant wants to appeal, the procedures for an administrative appeal, as set forth in 38 CFR 19.50-19.53, must be followed. (Authority: 38 U.S.C. 501, 7105, 7106) [71 FR 56872, Sept. 28, 2006]
