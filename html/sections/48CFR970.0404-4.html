CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 970  /  970.0404-4 Solicitation provision and contract clauses.

(a) The contracting officer shall insert the clause at 970.5204-1, Counterintelligence, into all management and operating contracts and other contracts for the management of DOE-owned facilities which include the security and classification/declassification clauses.

(b) The contracting officer shall refer to 904.404 and 904.7103 for the prescription of solicitation provisions and contract clauses relating to safeguarding classified information and foreign ownership, control, or influence over contractors. [65 FR 81009, Dec. 22, 2000, as amended at 74 FR 36371, July 22, 2009]
