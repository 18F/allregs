CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 932  /  932.602 Responsibilities.

The Department of Energy (DOE) contracting officer has primary responsibility for determining the amount of contract debt and notifying the cognizant finance office of such debt due the Government. The servicing DOE finance office making payments under the contract has primary responsibility for debt collection. [49 FR 12011, Mar. 28, 1984, as amended at 74 FR 36367, July 22, 2009. Redesignated and amended at 75 FR 29458, May 26, 2010]
