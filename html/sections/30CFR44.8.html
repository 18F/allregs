CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 44  /  Sec. 44.8 Ex parte communication.

There shall be no ex parte communication with respect to the merits of any case not concluded between the Assistant Secretary or the administrative law judge, including any employee or agent of the Assistant Secretary or of the administrative law judge, and any of the parties, intervenors, representatives, or other interested parties. [55 FR 53440, Dec. 28, 1990]
