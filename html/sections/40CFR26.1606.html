CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 26  /  Sec. 26.1606 Human Studies Review Board review of proposed

In commenting on proposals for new research submitted to it by EPA, the Human Studies Review Board must consider the scientific merits and ethical aspects of the proposed research, including all elements required in Sec. 26.1603(b) and (c) and any additional conditions recommended pursuant to Sec. 26.1603(d). [78 FR 10545, Feb. 14, 2013]
