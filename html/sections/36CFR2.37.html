CFR /  Title 36  /  Part 2  /  Sec. 2.37 Noncommercial soliciting.

Soliciting or demanding gifts, money, goods or services is prohibited, except pursuant to the terms and conditions of a permit that has been issued under Sec. 2.50, Sec. 2.51 or Sec. 2.52.
