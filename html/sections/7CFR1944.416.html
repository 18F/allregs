CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 1944  /  Sec. 1944.416 Grant closing.

The grant is closed on the date the Agreement is executed as defined in Sec. 1944.403(a) by the applicant and the Government. Funds may not be advanced prior to the signing of the Agreement. The District Director or Assistant District Director are authorized to execute the Agreement for FmHA or its successor agency under Public Law 103-354. Person(s) authorized by resolution may sign for the applicant.
