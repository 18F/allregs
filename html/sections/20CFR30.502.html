CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 30  /  Sec. 30.502 When is entitlement for survivors determined for purposes

Entitlement to any lump-sum payment for survivors under EEOICPA, other than for ``covered'' children under Part E, will be determined as of the time OWCP makes such a payment. As noted in Sec. 30.500(a)(2) of these regulations, a child of a deceased Part E employee will only qualify as a ``covered'' child of that individual if he or she satisfied one of the additional statutory criteria for a ``covered'' child as of the date of the deceased Part E employee's death.
