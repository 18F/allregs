CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 663  /  Sec. 663.550 How is eligible provider information developed and

(a) The designated State agency must maintain a list of all eligible training programs and providers in the State (the ``State list'').

(b) The State list is a compilation of the eligible programs and providers identified or retained by local areas and that have not been removed under Sec. Sec. 663.535(g) and 663.565.

(c) The State list must be accompanied by the performance and cost information contained in the local lists as required by Sec. 663.535(e). (WIA sec. 122(e)(4)(A).)
