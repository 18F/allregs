CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 266  /  Sec. 266.220 What does a storage and treatment conditional

The storage and treatment conditional exemption exempts your low-level mixed waste from the regulatory definition of hazardous waste in 40 CFR 261.3 if your waste meets the eligibility criteria in Sec. 266.225 and you meet the conditions in Sec. 266.230.
