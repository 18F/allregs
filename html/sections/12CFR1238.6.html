CFR /  Title 12  /  Part 1238  /  Sec. 1238.6 Post-assessment actions by regulated entities.

Each regulated entity shall take the results of the stress test conducted under Sec. 1238.3 into account in making changes, as appropriate, to the regulated entity's capital structure (including the level and composition of capital); its exposures, concentrations, and risk positions; any plans for recovery and resolution; and to improve overall risk management. If a regulated entity is under FHFA conservatorship, any post-assessment actions shall require prior FHFA approval.
