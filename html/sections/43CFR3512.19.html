CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 3512  /  Sec. 3512.19 Must I notify BLM if I intend to transfer an overriding

Yes. Although we do not approve these transfers, you must file all overriding royalty interest transfers with the BLM within 90 days from the date of execution. Include the transferee's statement of qualifications required in subpart 3502 and the filing fee for transfer of overriding royalty found in the fee schedule in Sec. 3000.12 of this chapter. [64 FR 53536, Oct. 1, 1999, as amended at 72 FR 50888, Sept. 5, 2007]
