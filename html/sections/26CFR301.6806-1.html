CFR /  Title 26  /  Part 301  /  Sec. 301.6806-1 Posting occupational tax stamps.

For provisions relating to the posting of specific stamps used with respect to a particular tax, other than a special tax under subchapter B of chapter 35, subchapter B of chapter 36, or subtitle E, see the regulations relating to such tax. For penalties for failure to post occupational tax stamps, see section 7273. [T.D. 7188, 37 FR 12795, June 29, 1972]
