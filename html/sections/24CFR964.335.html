CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 964  /  Sec. 964.335 Grant agreement.

(a) General. HUD will enter into a grant agreement with the recipients of a Family Investment Centers grant which defines the legal framework for the relationship between HUD and a HA.

(b) Term of grant agreement. A grant will be for a term of three to five years depending upon the tasks undertaken, as defined under this subpart.
