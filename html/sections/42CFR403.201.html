CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 403  /  Sec. 403.201 State regulation of insurance policies.

(a) The provisions of this subpart do not affect the right of a State to regulate policies marketed in that State.

(b) Approval of a policy under the voluntary certification program, as provided for in Sec. 403.235(b), does not authorize the insuring organization to market a policy that does not conform to applicable State laws and regulations.
