CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 63  /  Sec. 63.9301 What are my options for meeting the emission limits?

You may use either a continuous parameter monitoring system (CPMS) or a continuous emission monitoring system (CEMS) to demonstrate compliance with the emission limitations. Continuous monitoring systems must meet the requirements in Sec. 63.9306 (CPMS) and Sec. 63.9307 (CEMS).
