CFR /  Title 15  /  Part 20  /  Sec. 20.16 Hearings, decisions, post-termination proceedings.

Certain DOC procedural provisions applicable to Title VI of the Civil Rights Act of 1964 apply to DOC enforcement of these regulations. They are found in 15 CFR Part 8, Sec. 8.12 and Sec. 8.13.
