CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 13  /  Sec. 13.115 Chemical testing requirements.

Each applicant for an original tankerman endorsement must provide evidence of having passed a chemical test for dangerous drugs or of qualifying for an exemption from testing in Sec. 16.220 of this chapter as specified in Sec. 10.225(b)(5) of this subchapter. [USCG-2004-17914, 78 FR 77988, Dec. 24, 2013]
