CFR /  Title 19  /  Part 212  /  Sec. 212.20 Filing and service of documents.

Any application for an award or other pleading or document related to an application shall be filed and served on all parties to the adversary adjudication in the same manner as other pleadings in the adversary adjudication, except as provided in Sec. 212.11(b) for confidential financial information.
