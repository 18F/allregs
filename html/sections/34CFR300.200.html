CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 300  /  Sec. 300.200 Condition of assistance.

An LEA is eligible for assistance under Part B of the Act for a fiscal year if the agency submits a plan that provides assurances to the SEA that the LEA meets each of the conditions in Secs. 300.201 through 300.213. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1413(a))
