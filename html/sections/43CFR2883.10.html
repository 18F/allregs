CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 2883  /  Sec. 2883.10 Who may hold a grant or TUP?

To hold a grant or TUP under these regulations, you must be:

(a)

(1) A United States citizen, an association of such citizens, or a corporation, partnership, association, or similar business entity organized under the laws of the United States, or of any state therein; or

(2) A state or local government; and

(b) Financially and technically able to construct, operate, maintain, and terminate the proposed facilities.
