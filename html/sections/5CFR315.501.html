CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 315  /  Sec. 315.501 Transfer.

Subject to part 335 of this chapter, an agency may appoint by transfer to a competitive service position, without a break in service of a single workday, a current career or career-conditional employee of another agency. [60 FR 53504, Oct. 16, 1995]
