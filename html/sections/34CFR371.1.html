CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 371  /  Sec. 371.1 What is the Vocational Rehabilitation Services Program

This program is designed to provide vocational rehabilitation services to American Indians with disabilities who reside on Federal or State reservations, consistent with their individual strengths, resources, priorities, concerns, abilities, capabilities, and informed choice, so that they may prepare for and engage in gainful employment. (Authority: Secs. 100(a)(2) and 130(a) of the Act; 29 U.S.C. 720(a)(2) and 750(a)) [60 FR 58137, Nov. 24, 1995]
