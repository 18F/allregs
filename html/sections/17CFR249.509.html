CFR /  Title 17  /  Part 249  /  Sec. 249.509 Form 9-M, consent to service of process by a partnership

This form shall be filed pursuant to Rule 15b1-5 (Sec. 240.15b1-5 of this chapter) by each partnership nonresident broker-dealer registered or applying for registration pursuant to section 15 of the Act. [33 FR 18995, Dec. 20, 1968]

Editorial Note: For Federal Register citations affecting Form 9-M, see the List of CFR Sections Affected, which appears in the Finding Aids section of the printed volume and at www.fdsys.gov.
