CFR /  Title 16  /  Part 1  /  Sec. 1.19 Modification of a rule by the Commission at the time of

In the event that a reviewing court determines under section 18(e)(2) of the Federal Trade Commission Act, to allow further submissions and presentations on the rule, the Commission may modify or set aside its rule or make a new rule by reason of the additional submissions and presentations. Such modified or new rule shall then be filed with the court together with an appropriate Statement of Basis and Purpose and the return of such submissions and presentations. [40 FR 33966, Aug. 13, 1975, as amended at 50 FR 53304, Dec. 31, 1985]
