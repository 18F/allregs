CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 32  /  Sec. 32.105 Does this part apply to me?

(a) Portions of this part apply to you if you are either--

(1) A recipient of an assistance award from the Department of Transportation; or

(2) A(n) DOT awarding official. (See definitions of award and recipient in Sec. Sec. 32.605 and 32.660, respectively.)
