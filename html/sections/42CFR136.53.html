CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 136  /  Sec. 136.53 General rule.

Federal funds may not be used to pay for or otherwise provide for abortions in the programs described in Sec. 136.51, except under the circumstances described in Sec. 136.54. [64 FR 58322, Oct. 28, 1999. Redesignated and amended at 67 FR 35342, May 17, 2002]
