CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 484  /  Sec. 484.11 Condition of participation: Release of patient

The HHA and agent acting on behalf of the HHA in accordance with a written contract must ensure the confidentiality of all patient identifiable information contained in the clinical record, including OASIS data, and may not release patient identifiable OASIS information to the public. [64 FR 3763, Jan. 25, 1999]
