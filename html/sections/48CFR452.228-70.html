CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 452  /  452.228-70 Alternative Forms of Security.

As prescribed in 428.204-2, insert the following provision:

Alternative Forms of Security (NOV 1996)

If furnished as security, money orders, drafts, cashiers checks, or certified checks shall be drawn payable to: ------*.

(End of provision)

* Contracting Officer shall insert the name of the USDA contracting activity.
