CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 502  /  Sec. 502.12 Applicability of Federal Rules of Civil Procedure.

In proceedings under this part, for situations which are not covered by a specific Commission rule, the Federal Rules of Civil Procedure will be followed to the extent that they are consistent with sound administrative practice. [Rule 12.] [58 FR 27210, May 7, 1993, as amended at 64 FR 7807, Feb. 17, 1999]
