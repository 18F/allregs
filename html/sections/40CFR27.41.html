CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 27  /  Sec. 27.41 Stay pending appeal.

(a) An initial decision is stayed automatically pending disposition of a motion for reconsideration or of an appeal to the Environmental Appeals Board.

(b) No administrative stay is available following a final decision of the Environmental Appeals Board. [57 FR 5327, Feb. 13, 1992]
