CFR /  Title 50  /  Part 660  /  Sec. 660.351 Recreational fishery--definitions.

These definitions are specific to the recreational fisheries covered in this subpart. General groundfish definitions are defined at Sec. 660.11, subpart C.

Bag limit means the number of fish available to an angler.

Boat limit means the number of fish available to for a vessel or boat.

Hook limit means a limit on the number of hooks on any given fishing line.
