CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 427  /  Sec. 427.1 What is the Bilingual Vocational Training Program?

The Bilingual Vocational Training Program provides financial assistance for bilingual vocational education and training for limited English proficient out-of-school youth and adults, to prepare these individuals for jobs in recognized occupations and new and emerging occupations. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 2441(a))
