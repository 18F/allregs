CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 802  /  Sec. 802.220 Party not represented by an attorney; informal procedure.

A party to an appeal who is not represented by an attorney shall comply with the procedural requirements contained in this part, except as otherwise specifically provided in Sec. 802.211(e). In its discretion, the Board may prescribe additional informal procedures to be followed by such party.
