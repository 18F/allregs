CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 101-25  /  Sec. 101-25.404-1 Limitation.

Nothwithstanding the provisions in Sec. 101-25.404, agencies shall limit acquisition of new office furniture to essential requirements as provided in Sec. 101-25.104. Replacement of correspondence filing cabinets will be governed by the provisions of Sec. 101-26.308. [61 FR 14978, Apr. 4, 1996]
