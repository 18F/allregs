CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 207  /  207.471 Funding requirements.

(a) Fund leases in accordance with DoD Financial Management Regulation (FMR) 7000.14-R, Volume 2A, Chapter 1.

(b) DoD leases are either capital leases or operating leases. See FMR 7000.14-R, Volume 4, Chapter 6, section 060206.

(c) Use procurement funds for capital leases, as these are essentially installment purchases of property. [64 FR 31732, June 14, 1999, as amended at 66 FR 55121, Nov. 1, 2001; 71 FR 53045, Sept. 8, 2006; 76 FR 76319, Dec. 7, 2011]
