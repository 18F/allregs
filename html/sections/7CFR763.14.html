CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 763  /  Sec. 763.14 Appraisals.

(a) Standard guarantee plan. For the standard guarantee plan, the value of real estate to be purchased will be established by an appraisal obtained at Agency expense and completed as specified in Sec. 761.7 of this chapter. An appraisal is required prior to, or as a condition of, approval of the guarantee.

(b) Prompt payment guarantee plan. The Agency may, at its option and expense, obtain an appraisal to determine value of real estate to be purchased under the Prompt Payment Guarantee plan.
