CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 1316  /  Sec. 1316.41 Scope of subpart D.

Procedures in any administrative hearing held under the Act are governed generally by the rule making and/or adjudication procedures set forth in the Administrative Procedure Act (5 U.S.C. 551-559) and specifically by the procedures set forth in this subpart, except where more specific regulations (set forth in Sec. Sec. 1301.51-1301.57, Sec. Sec. 1303.31-1303.37, Sec. Sec. 1308.41-1308.51, Sec. Sec. 1311.51-1311.53, Sec. Sec. 1312.41-1312.47, Sec. Sec. 1313.51-1313.57, or Sec. Sec. 1315.50-1315.62) apply. [73 FR 73556, Dec. 3, 2008]
