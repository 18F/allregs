CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 603  /  Sec. 603.405 Announcement format.

If the contracting officer, in consultation with the program official, decides that a TIA is among the types of instruments that may be awarded, the additional elements described in Sec. Sec. 603.410 through 603.420 should be included in the announcement.
