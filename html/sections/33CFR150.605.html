CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 150  /  Sec. 150.605 What are the procedures for reporting a possible workplace

Any person may notify the Sector Commander, or the MSU Commander, with COTP and OCMI authority verbally or in writing of:

(a) A possible violation of a regulation in this part; or

(b) A hazardous or unsafe working condition on any deepwater port. [USCG-1998-3884, 71 FR 57651, Sept. 29, 2006, as amended by USCG-2013-0397, 78 FR 39183, July 1, 2013]
