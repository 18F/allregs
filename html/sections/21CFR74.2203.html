CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 74  /  Sec. 74.2203 FD&C; Green No. 3.

(a) Identity and specifications. The color additive FD&C; Green No. 3 shall conform in identity and specifications to the requirements of Sec. 74.203(a)(1) and (b).

(b) Uses and restrictions. The color additive FD&C; Green No. 3 may be safely used for coloring cosmetics generally in amounts consistent with current good manufacturing practice.

(c) Labeling. The label of the color additive shall conform to the requirements of Sec. 70.25 of this chapter.

(d) Certification. All batches of FD&C; Green No. 3 shall be certified in accordance with regulations in part 80 of this chapter. [47 FR 52144, Nov. 19, 1982]
