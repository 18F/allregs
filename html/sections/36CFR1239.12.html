CFR /  Title 36  /  Part 1239  /  Sec. 1239.12 Whom may agencies contact to request program assistance?

Agencies in the Washington, DC, area desiring information or assistance related to any of the areas covered by subchapter B may contact the National Archives and Records Administration, Life Cycle Management Division (NWML), 8601 Adelphi Rd., College Park, MD 20740-6001, phone number 301-837-1738. Agency field organizations may contact the appropriate NARA Regional Administrator regarding records management assistance, including for records in or scheduled for transfer to the records center or the archival operations within the region.
