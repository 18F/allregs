CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 111  /  Sec. 111.79-13 Different voltages and power types.

If receptacle outlets on a vessel are supplied by different voltages (e.g., 110 volts and 220 volts) or by different types of power (e.g., AC and DC), each receptacle outlet must preclude the plugging of a portable device into a receptacle outlet of an incompatible voltage or type of power. [CGD 94-108, 61 FR 28283, June 4, 1996]
