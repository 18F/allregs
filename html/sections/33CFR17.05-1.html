CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 17  /  Sec. 17.05-1 Gifts.

The gifts or bequests may be in money or negotiable instrument form. If in the form of a money order, check, etc., it should be made payable to the Treasurer of the United States. [CGFR 53-18, 18 FR 3171, June 3, 1953]
