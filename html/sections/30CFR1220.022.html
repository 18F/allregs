CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 1220  /  Sec. 1220.022 Calculation of net profit share payment.

The net profit share payment shall be calculated by multiplying the net profit share base calculated in accordance with Sec. 1220.021 by the net profit share rate. The net profit share payment shall be paid to the United States in accordance with Sec. 1220.031. [45 FR 36800, May 30, 1980, as amended at 75 FR 61087, Oct. 4, 2010]
