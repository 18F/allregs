CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 75  /  Sec. 75.409 Special considerations.

In addition to the basic considerations regarding the allowability of costs highlighted in this subpart, certain sections in this subpart describe special considerations and requirements applicable to states, local governments, Indian tribes, and IHEs. In addition, certain provisions among the items of cost in this subpart are only applicable to certain types of non-Federal entities, as specified in the following sections:

(a) Direct and Indirect (F&A;) Costs (Sec. Sec. 75.412 through 75.415);

(b) Special Considerations for States, Local Governments and Indian Tribes (Sec. Sec. 75.416 and 75.417); and

(c) Special Considerations for Institutions of Higher Education (Sec. Sec. 75.418 and 75.419).
