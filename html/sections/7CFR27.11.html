CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 27  /  Sec. 27.11 Area Director, Marketing Services Office; responsibility.

Subject to this subpart and the instructions of the Director, the Area Director of each Marketing Services Office shall be responsible for the proper performance of the duties imposed on such office and on the persons connected therewith. [48 FR 49210, Oct. 25, 1983]
