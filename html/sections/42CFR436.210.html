CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 436  /  Sec. 436.210 Individuals who meet the income and resource

The agency may provide Medicaid to any group or groups of individuals specified under Sec. 436.201(a)(1), (a)(2), (a)(3), (a)(5), and (a)(6) who are not mandatory categorically needy and who meet the income and resource requirements of the appropriate cash assistance program for their status (that is, OAA, AFDC, AB, APTD, or AABD). [58 FR 4935, Jan. 19, 1993]
