CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 577  /  Sec. 577.1 Scope.

This part sets forth requirements for manufacturer notification to owners, dealers, and distributors of motor vehicles and items of replacement equipment about a defect that relates to motor vehicle safety or a noncompliance with a Federal motor vehicle safety standard. [69 FR 34959, June 23, 2004]
