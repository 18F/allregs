CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 3475  /  Sec. 3475.1 Lease form.

Leases shall be issued on a standard form approved by the Director. The authorized officer may modify those provisions of the standard form which are not required by statute or regulations and may add such additional stipulations and conditions as he/she deems appropriate. [47 FR 33151, July 30, 1982]
