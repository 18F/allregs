CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 384  /  Sec. 384.219 Third serious traffic violation.

The State must disqualify from operating a CMV for a period of not less than 120 days each person who, in a three-year period, is convicted, as defined in Sec. 383.5 of this subchapter, in any State(s) or jurisdiction(s), of three serious traffic violations as specified in Table 2 to Sec. 383.51. This disqualification period must be in addition to any other previous period of disqualification. [67 FR 49762, July 31, 2002]
