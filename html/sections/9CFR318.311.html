CFR /  Title 9  /  Part 318  /  Sec. 318.311 Recall procedure.

Establishments shall prepare and maintain a current procedure for the recall of all canned product covered by this subpart. Upon request, the recall procedure shall be made available to Program employees for review. (Approved by the Office of Management and Budget under control number 0583-0015)
