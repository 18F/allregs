CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 1  /  Sec. 1.43 Requests for stay; cross-reference.

General rules relating to requests for stay of any order or decision are set forth in Secs. 1.41, 1.44(e), 1.45 (d) and (e), and 1.298(a). See also Secs. 1.102, 1.106(n), and 1.115(h).
