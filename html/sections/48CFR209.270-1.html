CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 209  /  209.270-1 Scope.

This section--

(a) Implements--

(1) Section 802 of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2004 (Pub. L. 108-136); and

(2) Section 130 of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2007 (Pub. L. 109-364); and

(b) Prescribes policy and procedures for qualification requirements in the procurement of aviation and ship critical safety items and the modification, repair, and overhaul of those items. [73 FR 1827, Jan. 10, 2008]
