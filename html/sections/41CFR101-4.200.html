CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 101-4  /  Sec. 101-4.200 Application.

Except as provided in Sec. Sec. 101-4.205 through 101-4.235(a), these Title IX regulations apply to every recipient and to each education program or activity operated by such recipient that receives Federal financial assistance.
