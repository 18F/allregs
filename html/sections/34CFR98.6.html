CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 98  /  Sec. 98.6 Reports.

The Secretary may require the recipient to submit reports containing information necessary to resolve complaints under section 439 of the Act and the regulations in this part. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1221e-3(a)(1), 1232h)
