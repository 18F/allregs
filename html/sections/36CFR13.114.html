CFR /  Title 36  /  Part 13  /  Sec. 13.114 Appeal procedures.

The procedures set forth in Sec. 13.55(b) govern appeals of a permit denial, a denial of a permit renewal, a permit revocation and a superintendent's final decision on a permit revocation issued pursuant to Sec. 13.112(a).
