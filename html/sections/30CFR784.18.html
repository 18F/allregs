CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 784  /  Sec. 784.18 Relocation or use of public roads.

Each application shall describe, with appropriate maps and cross sections, the measures to be used to ensure that the interests of the public and landowners affected are protected if, under Sec. 761.14 of this chapter, the applicant seeks to have the regulatory authority approve--

(a) Conducting the proposed surface coal mining operations within 100 feet of the right-of-way line of any public road, except where mine access or haul roads join that right-of-way; or

(b) Relocating a public road. [44 FR 15366, Mar. 13, 1979, as amended at 64 FR 70838, Dec. 17, 1999]
