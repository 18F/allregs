CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 922  /  Sec. 922.13 Handle.

Handle or ship means to sell, consign, deliver, or transport apricots within the production area or between the production area and any point outside thereof: Provided, That the term ``handle'' shall not include the transportation within the production area of apricots from the orchard where grown to a packing facility located within such area for preparation for market. [27 FR 5188, June 2, 1962]
