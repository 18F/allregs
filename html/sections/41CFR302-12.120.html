CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 302-12  /  Sec. 302-12.120 Under a homesale program, may we direct the relocation his/her residence?

No, under a homesale program, you may not direct the relocation services company to pay an employee more than the fair market value (as determined by the residence appraisal process) of his/her home. [FTR Amdt. 2011-01, 76 FR 18344, Apr. 1, 2011]
