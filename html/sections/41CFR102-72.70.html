CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 102-72  /  Sec. 102-72.70 What are Executive agencies' responsibilities under representative authority) from GSA?

When an Executive agency does not exercise the delegation of authority mentioned in Sec. 102-72.30(b) to lease general purpose space itself, it may be delegated, upon request, lease management authority to manage the administration of one or more lease contracts awarded by GSA.
