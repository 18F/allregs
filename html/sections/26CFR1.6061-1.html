CFR /  Title 26  /  Part 1  /  Sec. 1.6061-1 Signing of returns and other documents by individuals.

(a)(5) or (b) of Sec. 1.6012-1 to make such return. Other returns, statements, or documents required under the provisions of subtitle A or F of the Code or of the regulations thereunder to be made by any person with respect to any tax imposed by subtitle A of the Code shall be signed in accordance with any regulations contained in this chapter, or any instructions, issued with respect to such returns, statements, or other documents.
