CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 385  /  Sec. 385.421 Under what circumstances will a safety permit be subject

(a) * * *

(1) A motor carrier fails to submit a renewal application (Form MCSA-1) in accordance with the schedule set forth in part 390, subpart E, of this subchapter.

(2) A motor carrier provides any false or misleading information on its application form (Form MCSA-1) or as part of updated information it is providing on Form MCSA-1 (see Sec. 385.405(d)).
