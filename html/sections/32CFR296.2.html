CFR /  Title 32  /  Part 296  /  Sec. 296.2 Definitions.

The terms used in this part, with the exception of the following, are defined in 32 CFR part 286:

(a) Freedom of Information Act appellate authority. The Chief of Staff, NRO.

(b) Initial denial authority. The Chief, Information Access & Release Center NRO.
