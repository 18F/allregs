CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 3011  /  Sec. 3011.2 Public inspection and copying.

(a) Background. 5 U.S.C. 552(a)(2) requires each agency to maintain and make available for public inspection and copying certain kinds of records.

(b) Procedure. To gain access to OFM records that are available for public inspection, contact the Freedom of Information Act Officer by writing to the address shown in Sec. 3011.4(b) of this title.
