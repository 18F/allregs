CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 1040  /  Sec. 1040.89-1 General responsibilities.

Each DOE recipient has primary responsibility to ensure that its programs or activities are in compliance with the Act and these regulations. A recipient also has responsibility to maintain records, provide information, and afford access to its records to DOE, to the extent required to determine whether it is in compliance with the Act and these regulations. [50 FR 8089, Feb. 27, 1985, as amended at 68 FR 51348, Aug. 26, 2003]
