CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 235  /  235.001 Definitions.

``Research and development'' means those efforts described by the Research, Development, Test, and Evaluation (RDT&E;) budget activity definitions found in the DoD Financial Management Regulation (DoD 7000.14-R), Volume 2B, Chapter 5. [65 FR 32040, May 22, 2000]
