CFR /  Title 14  /  Part 13  /  Sec. 13.31 Applicability.

This subpart applies to proceedings in which a hearing has been requested in accordance with Sec. Sec. 13.19(c)(5), 13.20(c), 13.20(d), 13.75(a)(2), 13.75(b), or 13.81(e). [Amdt. 13-18, 53 FR 34655, Sept. 7, 1988]
