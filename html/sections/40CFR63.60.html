CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 63  /  Sec. 63.60 Deletion of caprolactam from the list of hazardous air

The substance caprolactam (CAS number 105602) is deleted from the list of hazardous air pollutants established by 42 U.S.C. 7412(b)(1). [61 FR 30823, June 18, 1996]
