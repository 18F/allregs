CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 69  /  Sec. 69.156 Marketing expenses.

Effective July 1, 2000, the marketing expenses formerly allocated to the common line and traffic sensitive baskets, and the switched services within the trunking basket pursuant to Sec. 32.6610 of this chapter and Sec. 69.403 will now be recovered in the CMT basket created pursuant to Sec. 61.42(d)(1) of this chapter. These marketing expenses will be recovered through the elements outlined in Sec. Sec. 69.152, 69.153 and 69.154. [65 FR 38703, June 21, 2000]
