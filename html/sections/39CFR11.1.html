CFR /  Title 39  /  Part 11  /  Sec. 11.1 Establishment.

The Board of Governors may create such advisory boards as it may deem appropriate and may appoint persons to serve thereon or may delegate such latter authority to the Postmaster General. [73 FR 78983, Dec. 24, 2008]
