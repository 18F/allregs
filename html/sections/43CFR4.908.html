CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 4  /  Sec. 4.908 What is the administrative record for my appeal if it

If your appeal is deemed decided under Sec. 4.906, the record for your appeal consists of:

(a) The record established in an appeal before the ONRR Director;

(b) Any additional correspondence or submissions to the ONRR Director;

(c) The ONRR Director's decision in an appeal;

(d) Any pleadings or submissions to the IBLA; and

(e) Any IBLA orders and decisions. [64 FR 26259, May 13, 1999, as amended at 79 FR 62051, Oct. 16, 2014]
