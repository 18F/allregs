CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 520  /  Sec. 520.863 Ethylisobutrazine.

(a) Specifications. Each tablet contains either 10 milligrams or 50 milligrams of ethylisobutrazine hydrochloride.

(b) Sponsor. See No. 000061 in Sec. 510.600(c) of this chapter.

(c) Conditions of use in dogs--(1) Amount. Administer orally 2 to 5 milligrams per pound of body weight once daily.

(2) Indications for use. As a tranquilizer.

(3) Limitations. Federal law restricts this drug to use by or on the order of a licensed veterinarian. [40 FR 13838, Mar. 27, 1975, as amended at 46 FR 48642, Oct. 2, 1981; 61 FR 8873, Mar. 6, 1996; 62 FR 61624, Nov. 19, 1997; 79 FR 28821, May 20, 2014]
