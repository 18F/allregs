CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 76  /  Sec. 76.677 Continuation of a bypass.

The Secretary continues a bypass until the Secretary determines that the grantee or subgrantee will meet the requirements for providing services to private school children. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1221e-3, 2727(b)(3)(D), 2972(f), and 3474) [54 FR 21776, May 19, 1989]
