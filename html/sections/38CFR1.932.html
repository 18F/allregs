CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 1  /  Sec. 1.932 Enforcement policy.

VA may compromise statutory penalties, forfeitures, or claims established as an aid to enforcement and to compel compliance, if VA's enforcement policy in terms of deterrence and securing compliance, present and future, will be adequately served by VA's acceptance of the sum to be agreed upon. (Authority: 31 U.S.C. 3711; 38 U.S.C. 501, 3720) [69 FR 62198, Oct. 25, 2004]
