CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 1823  /  1823.570-2 Contract clause.

The contracting officer shall insert the clause at 1852.223-74, ``Drug- and Alcohol-Free Workforce,'' in all solicitations and contracts containing the clause at 1852.246-70, ``Mission Critical Space Systems Personnel Reliability Program,'' and in other solicitations and contracts exceeding $5 million in which work is performed by an employee in a sensitive position. However, the contracting officer shall not insert the clause at 1852.223-74 in solicitations and contracts for commercial items (see FAR parts 2 and 12). [61 FR 55757, Oct. 29, 1996. Redesignated at 69 FR 21765, Apr. 22, 2004]
