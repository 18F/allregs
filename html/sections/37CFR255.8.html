CFR /  Title 37  /  Part 255  /  Sec. 255.8 Public performances of sound recordings and musical works.

Nothing in this part annuls or limits the exclusive right to publicly perform a sound recording or the musical work embodied therein, including by means of a digital transmission, under 17 U.S.C. 106(4) and 106(6). [64 FR 6223, Feb. 9, 1999]
