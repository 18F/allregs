CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 350  /  Sec. 350.3 Who is eligible for an award?

The following entities are eligible for an award under this program:

(a) States.

(b) Public or private agencies, including for-profit agencies.

(c) Public or private organizations, including for-profit organizations.

(d) Institutions of higher education.

(e) Indian tribes and tribal organizations. (Authority: Sec. 204(a); 29 U.S.C. 762(a))
