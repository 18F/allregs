CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 98  /  Sec. 98.25-65 Filling density.

(a) The filling density, or the percent ratio of the liquefied gas that may be loaded in the tank to the weight of the water the tank will hold at 60 [deg]F., shall not exceed 56 percent for unlagged tanks and 58 percent for lagged or refrigerated tanks.
