CFR /  Title 23  /  Part 810  /  Sec. 810.302 Eligible projects.

(a) Eligible projects are those defined as nonhighway public mass transit projects in Sec. 810.4 of this part subject to the limitations in paragraph (b) of this section.

(b) All projects under this subpart for the construction, reconstruction, or improvement of fixed rail facilities shall be located within the urban boundaries established under 23 U.S.C. 101(a).
