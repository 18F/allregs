CFR /  Title 22  /  Part 120  /  Sec. 120.7 Significant military equipment.

(a) Significant military equipment means articles for which special export controls are warranted because of their capacity for substantial military utility or capability.

(b) Significant military equipment includes:

(1) Items in Sec. 121.1 of this subchapter which are preceded by an asterisk; and

(2) All classified articles enumerated in Sec. 121.1 of this subchapter. [58 FR 39283, July 22, 1993, as amended at 62 FR 67275, Dec. 24, 1997]
