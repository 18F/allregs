CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 15  /  Sec. 15.64 Parties.

The term party shall include an applicant or recipient with respect to whom the agency has issued a notice of hearing or opportunity to request a hearing in accordance with subpart A of this part and Sec. 15.81. The agency shall be deemed a party to all proceedings.
