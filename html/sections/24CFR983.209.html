CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 983  /  Sec. 983.209 Owner responsibilities.

The owner is responsible for performing all of the owner responsibilities under the Agreement and the HAP contract. 24 CFR 982.452 (Owner responsibilities) applies. [70 FR 59913, Oct. 13, 2005. Redesignated at 79 FR 36168, June 25, 2014]
