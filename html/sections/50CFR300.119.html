CFR /  Title 50  /  Part 300  /  Sec. 300.119 Penalties.

Any person or harvesting vessel found to be in violation of the Act, this subpart, or any permit issued under this subpart will be subject to the civil and criminal penalty provisions and forfeiture provisions prescribed in the Act, 15 CFR part 904, and other applicable laws. [61 FR 35550, July 5, 1996. Redesignated at 72 FR 48510, Aug. 23, 2007]
