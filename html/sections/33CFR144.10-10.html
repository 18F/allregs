CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 144  /  Sec. 144.10-10 Other lifesaving equipment.

Any lifesaving equipment on an unmanned platform that is not required in Sec. 144.10-1 must meet the standards contained in Subpart 144.01 of this part. [CGD 73-177R, 40 FR 8176, Feb. 26, 1975]
