CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 732  /  Sec. 732.14 Resubmission of State programs.

If, by a final decision, the program is disapproved, the State may submit another proposed State program to the Director at any time. Resubmitted State programs must meet the requirements of Sec. 731.14 and will be acted upon pursuant to Sec. Sec. 732.11-732.16. [47 FR 26366, June 17, 1982]
