CFR /  Title 36  /  Part 2  /  Sec. 2.11 Picnicking.

Picnicking is allowed, except in designated areas closed in accordance with Sec. 1.5. The superintendent may establish conditions for picnicking in areas where picnicking is allowed. Picnicking in violation of established conditions is prohibited.
