CFR /  Title 25  /  Part 293  /  Sec. 293.9 Where should a compact or amendment be submitted for review

Submit compacts and amendments to the Director, Office of Indian Gaming, U.S. Department of the Interior, 1849 C Street, NW., Mail Stop 3657, Main Interior Building, Washington, DC 20240. If this address changes, a notice with the new address will be published in the Federal Register within 5 business days.
