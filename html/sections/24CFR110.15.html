CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 110  /  Sec. 110.15 Location of posters.

All fair housing posters shall be prominently displayed so as to be readily apparent to all persons seeking housing accommodations or seeking to engage in residential real estate-related transactions or brokerage services as contemplated by sections 804 through 806 of the Act. [54 FR 3311, Jan. 23, 1989]
