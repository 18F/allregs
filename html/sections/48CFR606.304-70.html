CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 606  /  606.304-70 Acquisitions by overseas posts.

The Departmental Advocate for Competition is the approval authority for the purposes of FAR 6.304(a)(3). This authority is not redelegable. Any such justification must be transmitted through the Principal Officer at the overseas post. [59 FR 66756, Dec. 28, 1994, as amended at 80 FR 6918, Feb. 9, 2015]
