CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 603  /  Sec. 603.21 Publication of list.

Periodically the Secretary will publish a list in the Federal Register of the State agencies which he determines to be reliable authorities as to the quality of public postsecondary vocational education in their respective States. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1087-1(b))
