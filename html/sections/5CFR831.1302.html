CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 831  /  Sec. 831.1302 Scope.

This subpart covers the collection of debts due the Civil Service Retirement and Disability Fund, with the exception of the collection of court-imposed judgments, amounts referred to the Department of Justice because of fraud, and amounts collected from back pay awards in accordance with Sec. 550.805(e)(2) of this chapter.
