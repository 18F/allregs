CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 970  /  970.2570 Contract clauses.

Contracting officers shall insert the clauses at 48 CFR 52.225-1, Buy American Act--Supplies, and 48 CFR 52.225-9, Buy American Act--Construction Materials, in management and operating contracts. The clause at 48 CFR 52.225-1 shall be modified in paragraph (d) by substituting the word ``use'' for the word ``deliver.'' [75 FR 68219, Nov. 5, 2010]
