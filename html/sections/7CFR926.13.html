CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 926  /  Sec. 926.13 Processor.

Processor means any person who receives or acquires fresh or frozen cranberries or cranberries in the form of concentrate from handlers, producer-handlers, importers, brokers or other processors and uses such cranberries or concentrate, with or without other ingredients, in the production of a product for market.

Effective Date Note: At 71 FR 78046, Dec. 28, 2006, Sec. 926.13 was suspended indefinitely, effective Dec. 29, 2006.
