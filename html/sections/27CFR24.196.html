CFR /  Title 27  /  Part 24  /  Sec. 24.196 Formula required.

Before producing any special natural wine, the proprietor shall receive approval of the formula by which it is to be made as provided by Sec. 24.80. Any change in a formula will be approved in advance as provided by Sec. 24.81. (Sec. 201, Pub. L. 85-859, 72 Stat. 1386, as amended (26 U.S.C. 5386)) (Approved by the Office of Management and Budget under control number 1512-0059)
