CFR /  Title 12  /  Part 241  /  Sec. 241.1 Authority and purpose.

(a) Authority. This part is issued by the Board pursuant to section 618 of the Dodd-Frank Wall Street Reform and Consumer Protection Act (12 U.S.C. 1850a).

(b) Purpose. This part establishes the procedures by which a securities holding company may elect to register to be supervised by the Board.
