CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 1204  /  Sec. 1204.6 May I appeal if ONRR denies my request for prepayment

If ONRR denies your request for prepayment relief under subpart B of this part or other relief under Sec. 1204.203, you may appeal under 30 CFR part 1290. Subpart B--Prepayment of Royalty [Reserved]
