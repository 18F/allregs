CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 189  /  Sec. 189.60-1 Application.

The provisions of this subpart shall apply to all oceanographic research vessels on an international voyage. (See Sec. 188.05-10 of this subchapter.) [CGD 95-012, 60 FR 48052, Sept. 18, 1995; 60 FR 50120, Sept. 28, 1995]
