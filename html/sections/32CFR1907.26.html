CFR /  Title 32  /  Part 1907  /  Sec. 1907.26 Prohibition on adverse action.

Agency correspondence to the challenger shall include a notice that CIA will take no adverse action or retribution against the challenger for bringing the classification challenge in good faith. [76 FR 59032, Sept. 23, 2011]
