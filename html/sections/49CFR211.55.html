CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 211  /  Sec. 211.55 Special approvals.

Requests for special approval pertaining to safety not otherwise provided for in this chapter, must be submitted in accordance with Sec. 211.7; specifying the action requested. These requests shall be considered by the Railroad Safety Board and appropriate action shall be taken not later than 9 months after receipt. When a decision is issued, the requestor and other interested parties are notified or a notice is published in the Federal Register. [41 FR 54181, Dec. 13, 1976, as amended at 74 FR 25171, May 27, 2009]
