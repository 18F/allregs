CFR /  Title 14  /  Part 29  /  Sec. 29.161 Trim control.

The trim control--

(a) Must trim any steady longitudinal, lateral, and collective control forces to zero in level flight at any appropriate speed; and

(b) May not introduce any undesirable discontinuities in control force gradients. [Doc. No. 5084, 29 FR 16150, Dec. 3, 1964, as amended by Amdt. 29-24, 49 FR 44436, Nov. 6, 1984]
