CFR /  Title 15  /  Part 904  /  Sec. 904.202 Filing of documents.

(a) Pleadings, papers, and other documents in the proceeding must be filed in conformance with Sec. 904.3 directly with the Judge, with copies served on the ALJ Docketing Center and all other parties.

(b) Unless otherwise ordered by the Judge, discovery requests and answers will be served on the opposing party and need not be filed with the Judge. Sec. 904.203 [Reserved]
