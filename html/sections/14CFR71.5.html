CFR /  Title 14  /  Part 71  /  Sec. 71.5 Reporting points.

The reporting points listed in subpart H of FAA Order 7400.9Y (incorporated by reference, see Sec. 71.1) consist of geographic locations at which the position of an aircraft must be reported in accordance with part 91 of this chapter. [Doc. No. 29334, 73 FR 54495, Sept. 22, 2008, as amended by Amdt. 71-40, 73 FR 60940, Oct. 15, 2008; Amdt. 71-41, 74 FR 46490, Sept. 10, 2009; Amdt. 71-42, 75 FR 55268, Sept. 10, 2010; Amdt. 71-43, 76 FR 53329, Aug. 26, 2011; Amdt. 71-44, 77 FR 50908, Aug. 23, 2012; Amdt. 71-45, 78 FR 52848, Aug. 27, 2013; Amdt. 71-46, 79 FR 51888, Sept. 2, 2014]

Effective Date Note: By Amdt. 71-46, 79 FR 51888, Sept. 2, 2014, Sec. 71.5 was amended by removing the words ``FAA Order 7400.9X'' and adding, in their place, the words ``FAA Order 7400.9Y'', effective Sept. 15, 2014 through Sept. 15, 2015.
