CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 2301  /  Sec. 2301.102 Application.

This part (Sec. Sec. 2301.101-2301.170) applies to all programs or activities conducted by the agency, except for programs or activities conducted outside the United States that do not involve individuals with handicaps in the United States.
