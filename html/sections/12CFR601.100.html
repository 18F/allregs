CFR /  Title 12  /  Part 601  /  Sec. 601.100 Cross-references to employee ethical conduct standards

Board members, officers, and other employees of the Farm Credit Administration are subject to the Standards of Ethical Conduct for Employees of the Executive Branch at 5 CFR part 2635, the Farm Credit Administration regulation at 5 CFR part 4101, which supplements the Executive Branch-wide Standards, and the executive branch- wide financial disclosure regulations at 5 CFR part 2634. [60 FR 30782, June 12, 1995]
