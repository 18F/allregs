CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 35  /  Sec. 35.1220 Ongoing lead-based paint maintenance activities.

Notwithstanding the designation of the PHA, grantee, participating jurisdiction, or Indian Housing Block Grant (IHBG) recipient as the designated party for this subpart, the owner shall incorporate ongoing lead-based paint maintenance activities into regular building operations in accordance with Sec. 35.1355(a). [69 FR 34273, June 21, 2004]
