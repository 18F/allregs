CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 8  /  8.608 Protection of classified and sensitive information.

Agencies shall not enter into any contract with FPI that allows an inmate worker access to any--

(a) Classified data;

(b) Geographic data regarding the location of--

(1) Surface and subsurface infrastructure providing communications or water or electrical power distribution;

(2) Pipelines for the distribution of natural gas, bulk petroleum products, or other commodities; or

(3) Other utilities; or

(c) Personal or financial information about any individual private citizen, including information relating to such person's real property however described, without the prior consent of the individual. Subpart 8.7_Acquisition From Nonprofit Agencies Employing People Who Are
