CFR /  Title 13  /  Part 121  /  Sec. 121.904 When does SBA determine the size status of a business

For compliance with programs of other agencies, SBA will base its size determination on the size of the concern as of the date set forth in the request of the other agency. [67 FR 13716, Mar. 26, 2002] Procedures for Size Protests and Requests for Formal Size Determinations
