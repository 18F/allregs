CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 404  /  Sec. 404.310 When am I entitled to old-age benefits?

We will find you entitled to old-age benefits if you meet the following three conditions:

(a) You are at least 62 years old;

(b) You have enough social security earnings to be fully insured as defined in Secs. 404.110 through 404.115; and

(c) You apply; or you are entitled to disability benefits up to the month you attain full retirement age (as defined in Sec. 404.409). When you attain full retirement age, your disability benefits automatically become old-age benefits. [68 FR 4702, Jan. 30, 2003]
