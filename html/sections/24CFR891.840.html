CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 891  /  Sec. 891.840 Site and neighborhood standards.

For section 202 or 811 mixed-finance developments, the site and neighborhood standards described at Sec. 891.125 and Sec. 891.320 apply to the entire mixed-finance development.
