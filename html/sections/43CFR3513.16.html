CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 3513  /  Sec. 3513.16 Do I have to pay a fee when I apply for a waiver, or minimum production?

Yes. BLM will charge you a processing fee on a case-by-case basis, as described in Sec. 3000.11 of this chapter. [70 FR 58878, Oct. 7, 2005]
