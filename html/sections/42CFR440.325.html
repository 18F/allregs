CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 440  /  Sec. 440.325 State plan requirements: Coverage and benefits.

Subject to requirements in Sec. Sec. 440.345 and 440.365, States may elect to provide any of the following types of health benefits coverage:

(a) Benchmark coverage in accordance with Sec. 440.330.

(b) Benchmark-equivalent coverage in accordance with Sec. 440.335.
