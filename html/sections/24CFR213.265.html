CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 213  /  Sec. 213.265 Modifications and consolidations.

Where a mortgage covering an investor sponsored project is modified and consolidated with the mortgage of a purchasing nonprofit cooperative housing corporation or trust, it shall be deemed to be paid in full as of the date of such modification and consolidation. [37 FR 8662, Apr. 29, 1972]
