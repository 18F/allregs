CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 553  /  Sec. 553.704 How will BOEM publish the offshore facility limit

BOEM will publish the inflation-adjusted limit of liability, and any statutory amendments to that limit of liability in the Federal Register, as amendments to Sec. 553.702. Updates to the limit of liability under this section are effective on the 90th day after publication in the Federal Register of the amendments to Sec. 553.702, unless otherwise specified by statute (in the event of a statutory amendment to the limit of liability), or in the Federal Register rule amending Sec. 553.702.
