CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 31  /  31.000 Scope of part.

This part contains cost principles and procedures for (a) the pricing of contracts, subcontracts, and modifications to contracts and subcontracts whenever cost analysis is performed (see 15.404-1(c)) and (b) the determination, negotiation, or allowance of costs when required by a contract clause. [48 FR 42301, Sept. 19, 1983, as amended at 62 FR 51271, Sept. 30, 1997]
