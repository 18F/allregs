CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 236  /  Sec. 236.426 Interlocking rules and instructions applicable to traffic

The rules and instructions prescribed in Secs. 236.327 and 236.328, Sec. 236.330 to Sec. 236.334, inclusive, and Sec. 236.342 shall apply to traffic control systems.
