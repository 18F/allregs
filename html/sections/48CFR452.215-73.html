CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 452  /  452.215-73 Post Award Conference.

As prescribed in 415.570, insert a clause substantially as follows:

Post Award Conference (NOV 1996)

A post award conference with the successful offeror is required. It will be scheduled within ------* days after the date of contract award. The conference will be held at: ------*.

(End of clause)

* Contracting officer to insert number of days and location. [61 FR 53646, Oct. 15, 1996, as amended at 64 FR 52675, Sept. 30, 1999]
