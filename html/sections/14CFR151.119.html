CFR /  Title 14  /  Part 151  /  Sec. 151.119 Advance planning proposals: Procedures; funding.

The funding information required by Sec. 151.23, except the last sentence, also is required in connection with an advance planning proposal. The sponsor's share of estimated proposal costs may not consist of or include the value of donated labor, materials, or equipment.
