CFR /  Title 14  /  Part 25  /  Sec. 25.1717 Circuit protective devices: EWIS.

Electrical wires and cables must be designed and installed so they are compatible with the circuit protection devices required by Sec. 25.1357, so that a fire or smoke hazard cannot be created under temporary or continuous fault conditions.
