CFR /  Title 50  /  Part 216  /  Sec. 216.32 Scope.

The regulations of this subpart apply to:

(a) All marine mammals and marine mammal parts taken or born in captivity after December 20, 1972; and

(b) All marine mammals and marine mammal parts that are listed as threatened or endangered under the ESA. [61 FR 21935, May 10, 1996]
