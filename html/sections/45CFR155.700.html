CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 155  /  Sec. 155.700 Standards for the establishment of a SHOP.

(a) General requirement. An Exchange must provide for the establishment of a SHOP that meets the requirements of this subpart and is designed to assist qualified employers and facilitate the enrollment of qualified employees into qualified health plans.

(b) Definition. For the purposes of this subpart:

Group participation rate means the minimum percentage of all eligible individuals or employees of an employer that must be enrolled.

SHOP application filer means an applicant, an authorized representative, an agent or broker of the employer, or an employer filing for its employees where not prohibited by other law. [77 FR 18464, Mar. 27, 2012, as amended at 78 FR 54141, Aug. 30, 2013; 80 FR 10868, Feb. 27, 2015]
