CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 71  /  Sec. 71.10 Pacific zone.

The fifth zone, the Pacific standard time zone, includes that part of the continental United States that is west of the boundary line between the mountain and Pacific standard time zones described in Sec. 71.9, but does not include any part of the State of Alaska. (Act of March 19, 1918, as amended by the Uniform Time Act of 1966 and Pub. L. 97-449, 15 U.S.C. 260-264; 49 CFR 1.59(a)). [Amdt. 71-19, 48 FR 43281, Sept. 22, 1983]
