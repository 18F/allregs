CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 455  /  Sec. 455.518 Applicability to the territories.

The aforementioned provisions in Sec. 455.500 through Sec. 455.516 of this subpart are applicable to Guam, Puerto Rico, U.S. Virgin Islands, American Samoa, and the Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands. (Authority: Catalog of Federal Domestic Assistance Program No. 93.778, Medical Assistance Program)
