CFR /  Title 29  /  Part 37  /  Sec. 37.65 What is the Director's authority to monitor the activities

(a) The Director may periodically review the adequacy of the Methods of Administration established by a Governor, as well as the adequacy of the Governor's performance under the Methods of Administration, to determine compliance with the requirements of Sec. Sec. 37.50 through 37.55. The Director may review the Methods of Administration during a compliance review under Sec. Sec. 37.62 and 37.63, or at another time.

(b) Nothing in this subpart limits or precludes the Director from monitoring directly any WIA Title I recipient or from investigating any matter necessary to determine a recipient's compliance with the nondiscrimination and equal opportunity provisions of WIA or this part.
