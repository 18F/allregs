CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 63  /  Sec. 63.7 License not required for certain preliminary activities.

The requirement for a license set forth in Sec. 63.3(a) is not applicable to the extent that DOE receives and possesses source, special nuclear, and byproduct material at a geologic repository at the Yucca Mountain site:

(a) For purposes of site characterization; or

(b) For use, during site characterization or construction, as components of radiographic, radiation monitoring, or similar equipment or instrumentation.
