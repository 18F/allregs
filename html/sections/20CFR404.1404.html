CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 404  /  Sec. 404.1404 Effective date of coverage of railroad services

Coverage under the act of services performed after 1936 by an individual in the railroad industry is effective as follows:

(a) and (b) of Sec. 404.1402 insofar as they relate to survivor monthly benefits are effective for months after December 1946 and insofar as they relate to lump-sum death payments are effective with respect to deaths after 1946;

(b) The provisions of paragraph (a) of Sec. 404.1402 insofar as they relate to old-age insurance benefits or monthly benefits of dependents of old-age insurance beneficiaries are effective November 1, 1951; insofar as they relate to disability insurance benefits are effective for months after June 1957; and insofar as they relate to monthly benefits for dependents of disability insurance beneficiaries are effective for months after August 1958;

(c) of Sec. 404.1402 are effective for benefits for months after June 1955; and

(d) of Sec. 404.1402 are effective November 1, 1951. [25 FR 5182, June 10, 1960]
