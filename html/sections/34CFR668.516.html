CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 668  /  Sec. 668.516 Fewer-than-ten-borrowers determinations.

We calculate an official program cohort default rate regardless of the number of borrowers included in the applicable cohort or cohorts. However, an institution may not disclose an official program cohort default rate under Sec. 668.412(a)(12) or otherwise, if the number of borrowers in the applicable cohorts is fewer than ten. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1001, 1002, 1088)
