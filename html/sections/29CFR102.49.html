CFR /  Title 29  /  Part 102  /  Sec. 102.49 Modification or setting aside of order of Board before record

Within the limitations of the provisions of section 10(c) of the Act, and Sec. 102.48, until a transcript of the record in a case shall have been filed in a court, within the meaning of section 10 of the Act, the Board may at any time upon reasonable notice modify or set aside, in whole or in part, any findings of fact, conclusions of law, or order made or issued by it. Thereafter, the Board may proceed pursuant to Sec. 102.50, insofar as applicable.
