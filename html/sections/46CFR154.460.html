CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 154  /  Sec. 154.460 Design criteria.

At static angles of heel up through 30[deg], a secondary barrier must

(a) If a complete secondary barrier is required in Sec. 154.459, hold all of the liquid cargo in the cargo tank for at least 15 days under the dynamic loads in Sec. 154.409(e);

(b) If a partial secondary barrier is permitted in Sec. 154.459, hold any leakage of liquid cargo corresponding to the extent of failure under Sec. 154.448(a) after initial detection or primary barrier leak for at least 15 days under the dynamic loads in Sec. 154.409(e);

(c) If the primary barrier fails, prevent the temperature of the vessel's structure from falling below the minimum allowable service temperature of the steel; and

(d) Be designed so that a cargo tank failure does not cause a failure in the secondary barrier.
