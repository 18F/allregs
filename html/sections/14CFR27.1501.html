CFR /  Title 14  /  Part 27  /  Sec. 27.1501 General.

(a) Each operating limitation specified in Sec. Sec. 27.1503 through 27.1525 and other limitations and information necessary for safe operation must be established.

(b) The operating limitations and other information necessary for safe operation must be made available to the crewmembers as prescribed in Sec. Sec. 27.1541 through 27.1589. (Secs. 313(a), 601, 603, 604, and 605 of the Federal Aviation Act of 1958 (49 U.S.C. 1354(a), 1421, 1423, 1424, and 1425); and sec. 6(c) of the Dept. of Transportation Act (49 U.S.C. 1655(c))) [Amdt. 27-14, 43 FR 2325, Jan. 16, 1978]
