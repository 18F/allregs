CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 301-10  /  Sec. 301-10.161 What class of train accommodations must I use?

You must use coach-class accommodations for all train travel, except when your agency authorizes other than coach-class service. [FTR Amdt. 2009-06, 74 FR 55148, Oct. 27, 2009]
