CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 75  /  Sec. 75.1107-11 Extinguishing agents; requirements on mining equipment

On mining equipment no more than 32 inches high, the quantity of extinguishing agent required under the provisions of Sec. Sec. 75.1107- 7, 75.1107-9, and 75.1107-10 may be reduced by one-fourth if space limitations on the equipment require such reduction. [37 FR 15303, July 29, 1972]
