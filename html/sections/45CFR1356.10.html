CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 1356  /  Sec. 1356.10 Scope.

This part applies to title IV-E agency programs for foster care maintenance payments, adoption assistance payments, related foster care and adoption administrative and training expenditures, and the independent living services program under title IV-E of the Act. [77 FR 946, Jan. 6, 2012]
