CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 1601  /  Sec. 1601.40 Lifecycle Funds.

The Executive Director will establish TSP Lifecycle Funds, which are target date asset allocation portfolios. The TSP Lifecycle Funds will invest solely in the funds established by the TSP pursuant to 5 U.S.C. 8438. [70 FR 32208, June 1, 2005]
