CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 51  /  Sec. 51.913 How do the section 182(f) NOX exemption provisions apply

(a) A person may petition the Administrator for an exemption from NOX obligations under section 182(f) for any area designated nonattainment for the 8-hour ozone NAAQS and for any area in a section 184 ozone transport region.

(b) The petition must contain adequate documentation that the criteria in section 182(f) are met.

(c) A section 182(f) NOX exemption granted for the 1-hour ozone standard does not relieve the area from any NOX obligations under section 182(f) for the 8-hour ozone standard. [70 FR 71701, Nov. 29, 2005]
