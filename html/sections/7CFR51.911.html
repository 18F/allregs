CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 51  /  Sec. 51.911 Container.

Container as used in these standards shall, for the purposes of determining maturity and other factors of grade of grapes in packages containing 5 pounds or less, mean the master container in which the individual packages are packed for shipment. [52 FR 22437, June 12, 1987. Redesignated at 56 FR 15802, Apr. 18, 1991]
