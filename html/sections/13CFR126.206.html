CFR /  Title 13  /  Part 126  /  Sec. 126.206 May non-manufacturers be certified as qualified HUBZone

Non-manufacturers (referred to in the HUBZone Act of 1997 as ``regular dealers'') may be certified as qualified HUBZone SBCs if they meet all of the requirements set forth in Sec. 126.200. For purposes of this part, a ``non-manufacturer'' is defined in Sec. 121.406(b)(1)(i) and (ii) of this title. [66 FR 4645, Jan. 18, 2001]
