CFR /  Title 29  /  Part 102  /  Sec. 102.74 Complaint and formal proceedings.

If it appears to the regional director that the charge has merit, formal proceedings in respect thereto shall be instituted in accordance with the procedures described in Sec. Sec. 102.15 to 102.51, inclusive, insofar as they are applicable, and insofar as they are not inconsistent with the provisions of this subpart. If it appears to the regional director that issuance of a complaint is not warranted, he shall decline to issue a complaint, and the provisions of Sec. 102.19, including the provisions for appeal to the general counsel, shall be applicable unless an election has been directed under Sec. Sec. 102.77 and 102.78, in which event the provisions of Sec. 102.81 shall be applicable.
