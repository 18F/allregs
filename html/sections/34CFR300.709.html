CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 300  /  Sec. 300.709 Public participation.

In fulfilling the requirements of Sec. 300.708 the Secretary of the Interior must provide for public participation consistent with Sec. 300.165. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1411(h))
