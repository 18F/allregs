CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 225  /  225.770 Prohibition on acquisition of United States Munitions List

This section implements section 1211 of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2006 (Pub. L. 109-163) and section 1243 of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2012 (Pub. L. 112-81). See PGI 225.770 for additional information relating to this statute, the terms used in this section, and the United States Munitions List. [71 FR 53046, Sept. 8, 2006, as amended at 77 FR 30366, May 22, 2012]
