CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 503  /  Sec. 503.16 Frequency of monitoring.

(a) Sewage sludge. (1) The frequency of monitoring for the pollutants listed in Table 1, Table 2, Table 3 and Table 4 of Sec. 503.13; the pathogen density requirements in Sec. 503.32(a) and Sec. 503.32(b)(2); and the vector attraction reduction requirements in Sec. 503.33 (b)(1) through (b)(4) and Sec. 503.33 (b)(7) through (b)(8) shall be the frequency in Table 1 of Sec. 503.16.

(2) After the sewage sludge has been monitored for two years at the frequency in Table 1 of Sec. 503.16, the permitting authority may reduce the frequency of monitoring for pollutant concentrations and for the pathogen density requirements in Sec. 503.32(a)(5)(ii) and (a)(5)(iii).

(b) Domestic septage. If either the pathogen requirements in Sec. 503.32(c)(2) or the vector attraction reduction requirements in Sec. 503.33(b)(12) are met when domestic septage is applied to agricultural land, forest, or a reclamation site, each container of domestic septage applied to the land shall be monitored for compliance with those requirements.
