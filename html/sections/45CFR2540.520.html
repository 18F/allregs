CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 2540  /  Sec. 2540.520 What are the consequences for unauthorized use of the

Any person who uses the national service insignia without authorization may be subject to legal action for trademark infringement, enjoined from continued use, and, for certain types of unauthorized uses, other civil or criminal penalties may apply.
