CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 117  /  Sec. 117.551 Chester River.

The draw of the S213 Bridge, mile 26.8, at Chestertown, shall open on signal if at least six hours notice is given. [USCG-2009-0796, 75 FR 10174, Mar. 5, 2010]
