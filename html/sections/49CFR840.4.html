CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 840  /  Sec. 840.4 Information to be given in notification.

The notice required by Sec. 840.3 shall include the following information:

(a) Name and title of person reporting.

(b) Name of railroad.

(c) Location of accident (relate to nearest city).

(d) Time and date of accident.

(e) Description of accident.

(f) Casualties:

(1) Fatalities.

(2) Injuries.

(g) Property damage (estimate).

(h) Name and telephone number of person from whom additional information may be obtained. [41 FR 13925, Apr. 1, 1976]
