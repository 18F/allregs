CFR /  Title 13  /  Part 134  /  Sec. 134.225 The record.

(a) Contents. The record of a case before OHA will consist of all pleadings, motions, and other non-evidentiary submissions, all admitted evidence, all orders and decisions, and any transcripts of proceedings in the case.

(b) Closure. The Judge will set the date upon which the pre-decisional record of the case will be closed, and after which no additional evidence or argument will be accepted. [61 FR 2683, Jan. 29, 1996, as amended at 75 FR 47442, Aug. 6, 2010]
