CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 152  /  Sec. 152.404 Fee amounts.

The fee prescribed by the following table must be submitted with each application for registration, amended registration or experimental use permit. Fees will be adjusted annually in accordance with Sec. 152.410. The Agency may waive or refund fees in accordance with Sec. 152.412.
