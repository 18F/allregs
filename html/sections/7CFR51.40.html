CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 51  /  Sec. 51.40 Traveling and other expenses.

Costs including travel incurred by the Agricultural Marketing Service in providing inspection service or appeal inspections may be charged to the applicant, including any Government agency. These charges shall be included with the fee for inspection on the bill furnished the applicant. [56 FR 55800, Oct. 30, 1991]
