CFR /  Title 12  /  Part 336  /  Sec. 336.6 Verification of compliance.

The FDIC's Division of Administration shall order appropriate investigations as authorized by 12 U.S.C. 1819 and 1822 on newly appointed employees, either prior to or following appointment, to verify compliance with the minimum standards listed under Sec. 336.4(a) (1) through (4).
