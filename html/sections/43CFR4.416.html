CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 4  /  Sec. 4.416 Appeals of wildfire management decisions.

The Board must decide appeals from decisions under Sec. 4190.1 and Sec. 5003.1(b) of this title within 60 days after all pleadings have been filed, and within 180 days after the appeal was filed. [68 FR 33803, June 5, 2003]
