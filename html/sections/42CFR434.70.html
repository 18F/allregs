CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 434  /  Sec. 434.70 Conditions for Federal Financial Participation (FFP).

(a) Basic requirements. FFP is available only for periods during which the contract--

(1) Meets the requirements of this part;

(2) Meets the applicable requirements of 45 CFR part 74; and

(3) Is in effect.

(b) Basis for withholding. CMS may withhold FFP for any period during which the State fails to meet the State plan requirements of this part. [67 FR 41095, June 14, 2002]
