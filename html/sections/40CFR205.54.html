CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 205  /  Sec. 205.54 Test procedures.

The procedures described in this and subsequent sections will be the test program to determine the conformity of vehicles with the standards set forth in Sec. 205.52 for the purposes of Selective Enforcement Auditing and Testing by the Administrator. [47 FR 57714, Dec. 28, 1982]
