CFR /  Title 15  /  Part 2008  /  Sec. 2008.19 Classification Review Committee.

The Classification Review Committee shall be chaired by the Special Trade Representative. The Committee shall decide appeals from denials of declassification requests submitted pursuant to section 3-5 of Executive Order 12065. The Committee shall consist of Special Representative, two Deputies and the General Counsel.
