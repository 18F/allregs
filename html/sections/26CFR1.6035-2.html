CFR /  Title 26  /  Part 1  /  Sec. 1.6035-2 Returns of U.S. officers and directors of foreign September 4, 1982.

For rules relating to information returns required to be filed by officers and directors of foreign personal holding companies for taxable years beginning before September 4, 1982, see section 6035(a) (as in effect before the enactment of the Tax Equity and Fiscal Responsibility Act of 1982) and 26 CFR 1.6035-1 (Revised as of April 1, 1981). [T.D. 8028, 50 FR 23409, June 4, 1985]
