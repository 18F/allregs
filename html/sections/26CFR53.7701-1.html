CFR /  Title 26  /  Part 53  /  Sec. 53.7701-1 Tax return preparer.

(a) In general. For the definition of a tax return preparer, see Sec. 301.7701-15 of this chapter.

(b) Effective/applicability date. This section is applicable to returns and claims for refund filed, and advice provided, after December 31, 2008. [T.D. 9436, 73 FR 78458, Dec. 22, 2008]
