CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 180  /  Sec. 180.1075 Colletotrichum gloeosporioides f. sp. aeschynomene;

An exemption from the requirement of a tolerance is established for residues of the mycoherbicide Colletotrichum gloeosporioides f. sp. aeschynomene in or on the following raw agricultural commodities:

Commodity Aspirated grain fractionsRice, grainSoybean, forageSoybean, haySoybean, seed [47 FR 25742, June 15, 1982, as amended at 74 FR 26534, June 3, 2009]
