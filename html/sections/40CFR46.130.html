CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 46  /  Sec. 46.130 Debarment and suspension.

EPA will not award you a fellowship if you are debarred, suspended or otherwise excluded from participation in federal programs. Names of individuals who are excluded or disqualified are located in the Excluded Parties List System maintained by the General Services Administration and currently located at http://www.epls.gov. [68 FR 16710, Apr. 4, 2003, as amended at 72 FR 2427, Jan. 19, 2007]
