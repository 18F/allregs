CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 800  /  Sec. 800.26 Delegation to the Chief, Public Inquiries Branch.

The Board delegates to the Chief, Public Inquiries Branch, the authority to determine, initially, the withholding of a board record from inspection or copying, pursuant to part 801 of this chapter. [63 FR 71606, Dec. 29, 1998]
