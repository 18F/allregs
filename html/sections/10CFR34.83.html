CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 34  /  Sec. 34.83 Records of personnel monitoring procedures.

Each licensee shall maintain the following exposure records specified in Sec. 34.47:

(a) Direct reading dosimeter readings and yearly operability checks required by Sec. 34.47 (b) and (c) for 3 years after the record is made.

(b) Records of alarm ratemeter calibrations for 3 years after the record is made.

(c) Personnel dosimeter results received from the accredited NVLAP processor until the Commission terminates the license.

(d) Records of estimates of exposures as a result of: off-scale personal direct reading dosimeters, or lost or damaged personnel dosimeters until the Commission terminates the license. [62 FR 28963, May 28, 1997, as amended at 65 FR 63752, Oct. 24, 2000]
