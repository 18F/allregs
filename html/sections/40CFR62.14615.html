CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 62  /  Sec. 62.14615 How do I renew my lapsed operator qualification?

You must renew a lapsed operator qualification by one of the two methods specified in paragraphs (a) and (b) of this section.

(a) For a lapse of less than 3 years, you must complete a standard annual refresher course described in Sec. 62.14610.

(b) For a lapse of 3 years or more, you must repeat the initial qualification requirements in Sec. 62.14605(a).
