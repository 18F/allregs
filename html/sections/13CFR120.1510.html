CFR /  Title 13  /  Part 120  /  Sec. 120.1510 Other Regulated SBLCs.

Other Regulated SBLCs are exempt from Secs. 120.465, 120.1050(b), 120.1400(d), 120.1500(c), and 120.1600(b). This exemption is not intended to preclude SBA from seeking any other remedy authorized by law or equity. [73 FR 75521, Dec. 11, 2008]
