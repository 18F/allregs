CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 2400  /  Sec. 2400.63 Excluded graduate study.

James Madison Fellowships do not provide support for study toward doctoral degrees, for the degree of master of arts in public affairs or public administration. The Foundation may at its discretion, upon request of the Fellow, provide tuition only assistance toward teacher certification. [69 FR 11815, Mar. 12, 2004]
