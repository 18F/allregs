CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 97  /  Sec. 97.800 Publication of public variety descriptions.

Voluntary submissions of varietal descriptions of ``public varieties'' on forms obtainable from the Office will be accepted for publication in the Official Journal. Such publication shall not constitute recognition that the variety is, in fact, distinct, uniform, and stable. [58 FR 42435, Aug. 9, 1993, as amended at 60 FR 17190, Apr. 4, 1995]
