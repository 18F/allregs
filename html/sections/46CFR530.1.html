CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 530  /  Sec. 530.1 Purpose.

The purpose of this part is to facilitate the filing of service contracts and the publication of certain essential terms of those service contracts as required by section 8(c) of the Shipping Act of 1984 (``the Act'') (46 U.S.C. 40502). This part enables the Commission to review service contracts to ensure that these contracts and the parties to them comport with the requirements of the Act. This part also implements electronic filing provisions for service contracts to facilitate compliance and minimize the filing burdens on the oceanborne commerce of the United States. [64 FR 11206, Mar. 8, 1999, as amended at 74 FR 50723, Oct. 1, 2009]
