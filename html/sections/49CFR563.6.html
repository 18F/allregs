CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 563  /  Sec. 563.6 Requirements for vehicles.

Each vehicle equipped with an EDR must meet the requirements specified in Sec. 563.7 for data elements, Sec. 563.8 for data format, Sec. 563.9 for data capture, Sec. 563.10 for crash test performance and survivability, and Sec. 563.11 for information in owner's manual.
