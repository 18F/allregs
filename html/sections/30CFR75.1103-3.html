CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 75  /  Sec. 75.1103-3 Automatic fire sensor and warning device systems; minimum

Automatic fire sensor and warning device systems installed in belt haulageways of underground coal mines shall be assembled from components which meet the minimum requirements set forth in Sec. Sec. 75.1103-4 through 75.1103-7 unless otherwise approved by the Secretary. [37 FR 16545, Aug. 16, 1972]
