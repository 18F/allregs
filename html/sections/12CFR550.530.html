CFR /  Title 12  /  Part 550  /  Sec. 550.530 How do I surrender fiduciary powers?

If you want to surrender your fiduciary powers, you must file a certified copy of a resolution of your board of directors evidencing that intent. You must file the resolution with the appropriate Regional Office at the address listed in Sec. 516.40(a) of this chapter. [62 FR 66703, Dec. 30, 1997, as amended at 66 FR 13006, Mar. 2, 2001]
