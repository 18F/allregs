CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 152  /  Sec. 152.55 Where to send applications and correspondence.

Applications and correspondence relating to registration should be sent to the Office of Pesticide Programs' Document Processing Desk at the appropriate address as set forth in 40 CFR 150.17(a) or (b). [71 FR 35545, June 21, 2006] Subpart D [Reserved]
