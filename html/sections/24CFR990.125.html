CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 990  /  Sec. 990.125 Eligible units.

A PHA is eligible to receive operating subsidy for public housing units under an ACC for:

(a) Occupied dwelling units as defined in Sec. 990.140;

(b) A dwelling unit with an approved vacancy (as defined in Sec. 990.145); and

(c) A limited number of vacancies (as defined in Sec. 990.150).
