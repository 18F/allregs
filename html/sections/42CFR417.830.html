CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 417  /  Sec. 417.830 Scope of regulations on beneficiary appeals.

Sections 417.832 through 417.840 establish procedures for the presentation and resolution of organization determinations, reconsiderations, hearings, Departmental Appeals Board review, court reviews, and finality of decisions that are applicable to Medicare enrollees of an HCPP. [59 FR 59943, Nov. 21, 1994, as amended at 61 FR 32348, June 24, 1996]
