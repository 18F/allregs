CFR /  Title 15  /  Part 995  /  Sec. 995.16 Term of certification.

The term of certification is five years from the date of issuance. At the end of the certification term, the process must be re-certified by the distributor or value added distributor submitting a request for certification as described in Sec. 995.10. It may also be revoked prior to the end of the term under the conditions described in Sec. 995.15.
