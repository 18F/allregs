CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 106  /  Sec. 106.11 Application.

Except as provided in this subpart, this part 106 applies to every recipient and to the education program or activity operated by such recipient which receives Federal financial assistance. (Authority: Secs. 901, 902, Education Amendments of 1972, 86 Stat. 373, 374; 20 U.S.C. 1681, 1682) [45 FR 86298, Dec. 30, 1980, as amended at 65 FR 68056, Nov. 13, 2000]
