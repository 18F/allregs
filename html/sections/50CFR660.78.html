CFR /  Title 50  /  Part 660  /  Sec. 660.78 EFH Conservation Areas off the Coast of Oregon.

Fishing activity that is prohibited or permitted within the EEZ in a particular area designated as a groundfish EFH Conservation Area is detailed at Sec. Sec. 660.11; Sec. Sec. 660.112 and 660.130; Sec. Sec. 660.212 and 660.230; Sec. Sec. 660.312 and 660.330; and Sec. Sec. 660.360.
