CFR /  Title 32  /  Part 734  /  Sec. 734.5 Administrative procedures.

The designated officials specified in Sec. 734.3, shall, in consultation with the Judge Advocate General and Commander, Navy Accounting and Finance Center or the Commandant of the Marine Corps (FD), as appropriate, establish procedures for effectively executing their assigned responsibilities. Implementing procedures shall conform with 42 U.S.C., 659, as amended, the MCO 5800.16A, Marine Corps Manual for Legal Administration (LEGADMINMAN), the Navy Comptroller Manual, and the Federal Personnel Manual. [44 FR 42193, July 19, 1979, as amended at 65 FR 62619, Oct. 19, 2000]
