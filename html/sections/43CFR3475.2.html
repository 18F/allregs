CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 3475  /  Sec. 3475.2 Duration of leases.

Leases shall be issued for a period of 20 years and so long thereafter as the condition of continued operation is met. If the condition of continued operation is not met the lease shall be cancelled as provided in Sec. 3452.2 of this title. [44 FR 42643, July 19, 1979. Redesignated at 47 FR 33151, July 30, 1982]
