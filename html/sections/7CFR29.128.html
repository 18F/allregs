CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 29  /  Sec. 29.128 For certificates.

A charge may be made, in the discretion of the Director, for copies of certificates other than those required to be distributed in Sec. 29.67, and for the issuance of a duplicate certificate in accordance with Sec. 29.108.
