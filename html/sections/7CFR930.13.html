CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 930  /  Sec. 930.13 Primary inventory reserve.

Primary inventory reserve means that portion of handled cherries that are placed into handlers' inventories in accordance with any restricted percentage established pursuant to Sec. 930.50 or Sec. 930.51.
