CFR /  Title 27  /  Part 70  /  Sec. 70.415 Offers in compromise.

Procedure in the case of offers in compromise of liabilities under 26 U.S.C. chapter 51 and of penalties for violation of the Federal Alcohol Administration Act, is set forth in Sec. Sec. 70.482 through 70.484. [T.D. ATF-301, 55 FR 47654, Nov. 14, 1990]
