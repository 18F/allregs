CFR /  Title 31  /  Part 549  /  Sec. 549.407 Payments from blocked accounts to satisfy obligations

Pursuant to Sec. 549.201, no debits may be made to a blocked account to pay obligations to U.S. persons or other persons, except as authorized by or pursuant to this part.
