CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 52  /  Sec. 52.1893 Control strategy: Lead (Pb).

(a) Ohio's 2008 lead emissions inventory for the Bellefontaine area as, as submitted on October 29, 2013, satisfying the emission inventory requirements of section 172(c)(3) of the Clean Air Act for the Bellefontaine area.

(b) Approval--the 2008 lead maintenance plan for the Bellefontaine, Ohio nonattainment area has been approved as submitted on October 29, 2013. [79 FR 43660, July 28, 2014]
