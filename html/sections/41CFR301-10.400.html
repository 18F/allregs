CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 301-10  /  Sec. 301-10.400 What types of special conveyances may my agency authorize

Your agency may authorize/approve use of:

(a) Taxicabs as specified in Sec. Sec. 301-10.420 through 301-10.421 of this chapter;

(b) Commercial rental automobiles as specified in Sec. Sec. 301-10.450 through 301-10.453 of this chapter; or

(c) Any other special conveyance when determined to be advantageous to the Government.
