CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 81  /  Sec. 81.17 Certificate of Alternative Compliance: Termination.

The Certificate of Alternative Compliance terminates if the information supplied under Sec. 81.5(a) or the Certificate issued under Sec. 81.9 is no longer applicable to the vessel. [CGD 77-136, 47 FR 13800, Apr. 1, 1982]
