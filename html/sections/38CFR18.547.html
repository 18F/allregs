CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 18  /  Sec. 18.547 Hearings, decisions, post-termination proceedings.

Certain VA procedural provisions applicable to Title VI of the Civil Rights Act of 1964 apply to VA enforcement of these regulations. They are found at Sec. Sec. 18.9 through 18.11 and part 18b of this title. (Authority: 42 U.S.C. 6101-1607)
