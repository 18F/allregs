CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 75  /  Sec. 75.622 Definition of ``project materials.''

As used in Sec. Sec. 75.620-75.621, ``project materials'' means a copyrightable work developed with funds from a grant of the Department. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1221e-3 and 3474) [57 FR 30339, July 8, 1992]
