CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 884  /  Sec. 884.1700 Hysteroscopic insufflator.

(a) Identification. A hysteroscopic insufflator is a device designed to distend the uterus by filling the uterine cavity with a liquid or gas to facilitate viewing with a hysteroscope.

(b) Classification.

(1) Class II (performance standards).

(2) are exempt from the premarket notification procedures in subpart E of part 807 of this chapter, subject to the limitations in Sec. 884.9. [45 FR 12684-12720, Feb. 26, 1980, as amended at 61 FR 1124, Jan. 16, 1996; 66 FR 38808, July 25, 2001]
