CFR /  Title 12  /  Part 390  /  Sec. 390.264 Real estate lending standards; purpose and scope.

This section, and Sec. 390.265, issued pursuant to section 18(o) of the Federal Deposit Insurance Act, (12 U.S.C. 1828(o)), prescribe standards for real estate lending to be used by State savings associations and all their includable subsidiaries, as defined in Sec. 390.461, over which the State savings associations exercise control, in adopting internal real estate lending policies.
