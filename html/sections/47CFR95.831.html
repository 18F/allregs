CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 95  /  Sec. 95.831 Service requirements.

Subject to the initial construction requirements of Sec. 95.833 of this subpart, each 218-219 MHz Service system license must demonstrate that it provides substantial service within the service area. Substantial service is defined as a service that is sound, favorable, and substantially above a level of service which might minimally warrant renewal. [64 FR 59662, Nov. 3, 1999]
