CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 4  /  Sec. 4.1301 Who may file.

Any individual served a notice of proposed individual civil penalty assessment may file a petition for review with the Hearings Division, Office of Hearings and Appeals, U.S. Department of the Interior, 801 North Quincy Street, Arlington, VA 22203. Phone: 703-235-3800. [43 FR 34386, Aug. 3, 1978, as amended at 67 FR 4368, Jan. 30, 2002]
