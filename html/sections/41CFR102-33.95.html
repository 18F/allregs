CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 102-33  /  Sec. 102-33.95 What is the process for budgeting to acquire Commercial

Except for leases and capital leases, for which you must have specific Congressional authorization as required by 31 U.S.C. 1343, you may budget to fund your CAS out of your agency's operating budget. Also see Sec. Sec. 102-33.70 and 102-33.75.
