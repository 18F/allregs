CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 68  /  Sec. 68.300 Labeling requirements.

(a) Terminal equipment approved as set out in this part must be labeled in accordance with the requirements published by the Administrative Council for Terminal Attachments and with requirements of this part for hearing aid compatibility and volume control.

(b)(5) of this section. Telephones used with public mobile services or private radio services, and secure telephones, as defined by Sec. 68.3, are exempt from this requirement. [62 FR 61664, Nov. 19, 1997, as amended at 64 FR 3048, Jan. 20, 1999; 66 FR 7585, Jan. 24, 2001]
