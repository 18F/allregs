CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 118  /  Sec. 118.95 Lights on structures not part of a bridge or approach structure.

Lights on sheer booms, isolated piers, obstructions, and other structures not part of a bridge or approach structure must meet the requirements for aids to navigation in Subpart 66.01 of Part 66 of this chapter. [CGD 84-022, 51 FR 16313, May 2, 1986]
