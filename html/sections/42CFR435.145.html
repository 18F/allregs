CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 435  /  Sec. 435.145 Children for whom adoption assistance or foster care

The agency must provide Medicaid to children for whom adoption assistance or foster care maintenance payments are made under title IV-E of the Act. [47 FR 28665, July 1, 1982. Redesignated at 55 FR 48607, Nov. 21, 1990. Redesignated at 58 FR 48614, Sept. 17, 1993]
