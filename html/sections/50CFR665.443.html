CFR /  Title 50  /  Part 665  /  Sec. 665.443 Prohibitions.

In addition to the general prohibitions specified in Sec. 600.725 of this chapter and Sec. 665.15, it is unlawful for any person in Crustacean Permit Area 5 to fish for, take, or retain deepwater shrimp without a permit issued under Sec. 665.442.
