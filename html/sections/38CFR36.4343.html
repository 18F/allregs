CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 36  /  Sec. 36.4343 Restriction on designated fee appraisers.

(a) A designated fee appraiser shall not make an appraisal, excepting of alterations, improvements, or repairs to real property entailing a cost of not more than $3,500, if such appraiser is an officer, director, trustee, employer, or employee of the lender, contractor, or vendor.

(b) An appraisal made by a designated fee appraiser shall be subject to review and adjustment by the Secretary. The amount determined to be proper upon any such review or adjustment shall constitute the ``reasonable value'' for the purpose of determining the eligibility of the related loan. (Authority 38 U.S.C. 3703(c)(1), 3731)
