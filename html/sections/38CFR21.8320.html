CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 21  /  Sec. 21.8320 Authorization of services.

The provisions of Sec. 21.326, pertaining to the commencement and termination dates of a period of employment services, apply to children under this subpart in a manner comparable to that provided for veterans under the 38 U.S.C. chapter 31 program. References in that section to an individualized employment assistance plan or IEAP are considered as referring to the child's individualized written plan of vocational rehabilitation under this subpart. (Authority: 38 U.S.C. 1804(c), 1814)
