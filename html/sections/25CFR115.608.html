CFR /  Title 25  /  Part 115  /  Sec. 115.608 If you request a hearing to challenge BIA's decision

BIA will conduct a hearing within ten (10) working days from its receipt of a written request from you or your guardian, as applicable, for a hearing to challenge the decision to restrict your IIM account.
