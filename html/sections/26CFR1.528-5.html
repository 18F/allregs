CFR /  Title 26  /  Part 1  /  Sec. 1.528-5 Source of income test.

An organization cannot qualify as a homeowners association under section 528 for a taxable year unless 60 percent or more of its gross income for such taxable year is exempt function income as defined in Sec. 1.528-9. The determiniation of whether an organization meets the provisions of this section shall be made after the close of the organization's taxable year. [T.D. 7692, 45 FR 26322, Apr. 18, 1980]
