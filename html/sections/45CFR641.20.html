CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 641  /  Sec. 641.20 Notification of the availability of environmental documents

The Environmental Officer, Office of Polar Programs, shall make Environmental Action Memoranda, environmental documents and final data obtained under Sec. 641.21, available to the public upon request. However, notice of such availability need not be given, except as specifically provided in this part. [57 FR 40339, Sept. 3, 1992, as amended at 59 FR 37438, July 22, 1994]
