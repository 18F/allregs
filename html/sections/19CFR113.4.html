CFR /  Title 19  /  Part 113  /  Sec. 113.4 Bonds and carnets.

(a) Bonds. All bonds required to be given under the Customs laws or regulations shall be known as Customs bonds.

(b) Carnets. A carnet is an international customs document which serves simultaneously as a customs entry document and as a customs bond. Therefore, carnets, provided for in part 114 of this chapter, are ordinarily acceptable without posting further security under the Customs laws or regulations requiring bonds.
