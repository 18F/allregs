CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 112  /  Sec. 112.43-1 Switches.

An emergency lighting system must not have a switch, except:

(a) In a distribution panel;

(b) As required in Sec. 112.43-7; or

(c) In a circuit that serves a hazardous space such as a paint room or cargo handling room if the switch is located outside of the hazardous location. [CGD 74-125A, 47 FR 15267, Apr. 8, 1982, as amended by CGD 94-108, 61 FR 28287, June 4, 1996]
