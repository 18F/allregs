CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 362  /  Sec. 362.409 Conversion to the competitive service.

(a) A Fellow must complete the Program within the time limits prescribed in Sec. 362.404 of this part, including any agency-approved extension. At the conclusion of that period, the Fellow may be converted, as provided in paragraph (b) of this section.

(b) An agency may convert, without a break in service, an ERB-certified Fellow to a competitive service term or permanent appointment.
