CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 520  /  Sec. 520.302 Carnidazole tablets.

(a) Specifications. Each tablet contains 10 milligrams of carnidazole.

(b) Sponsor. See 053923 in Sec. 510.600(c) of this chapter.

(c) Conditions of use--

(1) Amount. Adult pigeons: 1 tablet (10 milligrams); newly weaned pigeons: \1/2\ tablet (5 milligrams).

(2) Indications for use. For treating trichomoniasis (canker) in ornamental and homing pigeons.

(3) Limitations. Not for use in pigeons intended for human food. Consult your veterinarian for assistance in the diagnosis, treatment, and control of parasitism or when severely ill birds do not respond to treatment. [54 FR 32336, Aug. 7, 1989. Redesignated at 80 FR 13229, Mar. 13, 2015]
