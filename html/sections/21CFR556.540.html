CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 556  /  Sec. 556.540 Progesterone.

(a) [Reserved]

(b) Tolerances. Residues of progesterone are not permitted in excess of the following increments above the concentrations of progesterone naturally present in untreated animals:

(1) Cattle and sheep--

(i) Muscle: 5 parts per billion (ppb).

(ii) Liver: 15 ppb.

(iii) Kidney: 30 ppb.

(iv) Fat: 30 ppb.

(2) [Reserved]

(c) Related conditions of use. See Sec. Sec. 522.1940 and 529.1940 of this chapter. [76 FR 57907, Sept. 19, 2011]
