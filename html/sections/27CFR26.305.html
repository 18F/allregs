CFR /  Title 27  /  Part 26  /  Sec. 26.305 Receipt by consignee.

Proprietors of distilled spirits plants who receive Virgin Islands spirits under this subpart shall follow the requirements in 27 CFR part 19 for spirits received by transfer in bond. However, proprietors are not required to file application on TTB Form 5100.16 to receive Virgin Islands spirits from customs custody. [T.D. ATF-198, 50 FR 8555, Mar. 1, 1985]
