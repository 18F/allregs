CFR /  Title 18  /  Part 34  /  Sec. 34.7 Filing requirements.

Applications must be filed with the Secretary of the Commission in accordance with filing procedures posted on the Commission's Web site at http://www.ferc.gov. If an applicant seeks to protect any portion of an application from public disclosure, the applicant must make its filing in accordance with the Commission's instructions for filing privileged materials and critical energy infrastructure information in this chapter. [Order 737, 75 FR 43403, July 26, 2010, as amended by Order 769, 77 FR 65474, Oct. 29, 2012]
