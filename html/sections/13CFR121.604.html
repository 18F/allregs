CFR /  Title 13  /  Part 121  /  Sec. 121.604 Are 8(a) BD Participants considered small for purposes

A concern which SBA determines to be a small business for the award of a 8(a) BD subcontract will be considered to have met applicable size eligibility requirements of other SBA programs where that assistance directly and primarily relates to the performance of the 8(a) BD subcontract in question. [61 FR 3286, Jan. 31, 1996, as amended at 69 FR 29206, May 21, 2004]
