CFR /  Title 17  /  Part 274  /  Sec. 274.51 Form N-18F-1, for notification of election pursuant to

(a) This form shall be filed with the Commission in triplicate as the notification of election pursuant to Sec. 270.18f-1 of this chapter by a registered open-end investment company to commit itself to pay in cash all redemptions requested by a shareholder of record as provided in said section. [36 FR 11920, June 23, 1971 as amended at 36 FR 20504, Oct. 23, 1971; 39 FR 36003, Oct. 7, 1974; 59 FR 52701, Oct. 19, 1994]
