CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 3425  /  Sec. 3425.1-5 Leasing outside coal production regions.

A lease sale may be held in response to an application under this subpart if the application covers coal deposits which are outside coal production regions identified under Sec. 3400.5 of this title. [47 FR 33141, July 30, 1982]
