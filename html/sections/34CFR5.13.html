CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 5  /  Sec. 5.13 Preservation of agency records.

The Department does not destroy agency records that are the subject of a pending FOIA request, appeal, or lawsuit. (Authority: 5 U.S.C. 552(a), 20 U.S.C. 3474)
