CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 85  /  Sec. 85.2302 Definition of model year.

Model year means the manufacturer's annual production period (as determined under Sec. 85.2304) which includes January 1 of such calendar year, provided, that if the manufacturer has no annual production period, the term ``model year'' shall mean the calendar year.
