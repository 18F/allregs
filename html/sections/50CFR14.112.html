CFR /  Title 50  /  Part 14  /  Sec. 14.112 Other applicable provisions.

In addition to the provisions of Sec. Sec. 14.101-14.111, the requirements of Sec. Sec. 14.121-14.172 applicable for particular groups of animals shall be met for all shipments of wild mammals and birds covered by this part.
