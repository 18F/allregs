CFR /  Title 14  /  Part 11  /  Sec. 11.91 How does FAA inform me of its decision on my petition

The FAA will notify you in writing about its decision on your petition. A copy of this decision is also placed in the public docket. We will include the docket number associated with your petition in our letter to you. [Doc. No. FAA-2005-22982, 71 FR 1485, Jan. 10, 2006]
