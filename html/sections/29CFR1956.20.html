CFR /  Title 29  /  Part 1956  /  Sec. 1956.20 Procedures for submission, approval and rejection.

The procedures contained in subpart C of part 1902 of this chapter shall be applicable to submission, approval, and rejection of State plans submitted under this part, except that the information required in Sec. 1902.20(b)(1)(iii) would not be included in decisions of approval.
