CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 211  /  Sec. 211.13 Payments made after death.

Payments made by an employer with respect to a deceased employee but paid after the calendar year of the employee's death to the employee's survivors or estate are not creditable compensation. [58 FR 45251, Aug. 27, 1993]
