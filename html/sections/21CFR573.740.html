CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 573  /  Sec. 573.740 Odorless light petroleum hydrocarbons.

Odorless light petroleum hydrocarbons complying with Sec. 172.884(a) and (b) of this chapter may be safely used in an amount not in excess of that required as a component of insecticide formulations used in compliance with regulations issued in this part.
