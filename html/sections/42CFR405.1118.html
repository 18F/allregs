CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 405  /  Sec. 405.1118 Obtaining evidence from the MAC.

A party may request and receive a copy of all or part of the record of the ALJ hearing, including the exhibits list, documentary evidence, and a copy of the tape of the oral proceedings. However, the party may be asked to pay the costs of providing these items. If a party requests evidence from the MAC and an opportunity to comment on that evidence, the time beginning with the MAC's receipt of the request for evidence through the expiration of the time granted for the party's response will not be counted toward the 90 calendar day adjudication deadline. [70 FR 11472, Mar. 8, 2005, as amended at 74 FR 65337, Dec. 9, 2009]
