CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 102-33  /  Sec. 102-33.270 What is the process for reporting an excess aircraft?

To report an excess aircraft, you must:

(a) Report electronically to GSA's Federal Disposal System GSAXcess[supreg] (http://gsaxcess.gov). For information on reporting excess property electronically, contact the Federal Acquisition Service (FAS), Pacific Rim Region (Region 9) at (415) 522-2777; and

(b) Submit a Standard Form (SF) 120, Report of Excess Personal Property (see Sec. 102-2.135), to: General Services Administration, Federal Acquisition Service, Pacific Rim Region, 450 Golden Gate Avenue, 4th Floor West, San Francisco, CA, 94102-3434.
