CFR /  Title 18  /  Part 6  /  Sec. 6.5 Annual charges.

Annual charges arising under a license surrendered or terminated shall continue until the effective date set forth in the Commission's order with respect to such surrender or termination. [Order 175, 19 FR 5217, Aug. 18, 1954]

Cross Reference: For annual charges, see part 11 of this chapter.
