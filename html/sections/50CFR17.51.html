CFR /  Title 50  /  Part 17  /  Sec. 17.51 Treatment as endangered or threatened.

(a) Any species listed in Sec. 17.11 or Sec. 17.12, pursuant to Sec. 17.50, shall be treated as Endangered or Threatened, as indicated in the ``Status'' column.

(b) All of the provisions of subparts C (Endangered Wildlife), D (Threatened Wildlife), F (Endangered Plants) or G (Threatened Plants), as appropriate, shall apply to any such species.
