CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 570  /  Sec. 570.508 Public access to program records.

Notwithstanding 2 CFR 200.337, recipients shall provide citizens with reasonable access to records regarding the past use of CDBG funds, consistent with applicable State and local laws regarding privacy and obligations of confidentiality. [53 FR 8058, Mar. 11, 1988, as amended at 80 FR 75938, Dec. 7, 2015]
