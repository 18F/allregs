CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 234  /  Sec. 234.30 General.

When an applicant or an annuitant dies before being paid any annuities that may be due, the total of those annuities become payable to certain survivors in a lump-sum. Refer to Sec. 234.31 through Sec. 234.34 for information about when and to whom each type of unpaid annuity is payable. An application for an unpaid annuity must be filed within two years after the death of the person originally entitled to the annuity. (Approved by the Office of Management and Budget under control numbers 3220-0031 and 3220-0032 and 3220-0042) [51 FR 3036, Jan. 23, 1986, as amended at 52 FR 11017, Apr. 6, 1987]
