CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 21  /  Sec. 21.40 Filing and service of documents.

(a) Except as provided in Sec. 21.32 and in applications subject to the jurisdiction of the CRRA, an applicant shall--

(1) File with the adjudicative officer its application and any related documents; and

(2) Serve on all parties to the adversary adjudication copies of its application and any related documents.

(b)(1) In an application subject to the jurisdiction of the CRRA, the applicant shall--

(1) In an application subject to the jurisdiction of the CRRA, the applicant shall--

(i) File with the CRRA its application and any other related documents; and

(ii) Serve on all parties to the adversary adjudication copies of its application and any related documents.

(2) In applications subject to Sec. 21.40(b)(1), the CRRA shall direct the adjudicative officer to issue an initial decision within 30 days of the completion of the proceedings on the application. The adjudicative officer shall conduct proceedings under the procedures of Sec. Sec. 21.41-21.44. (Authority: 5 U.S.C. 504(a)(2) and (c)(1); 20 U.S.C. 1681; 29 U.S.C. 794; 42 U.S.C. 2000d-1 et seq. and 6101 et seq.)
