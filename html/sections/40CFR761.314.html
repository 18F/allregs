CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 761  /  Sec. 761.314 Chemical analysis of standard wipe test samples.

Perform the chemical analysis of standard wipe test samples in accordance with Sec. 761.272. Report sample results in micrograms per 100 cm\2\.
