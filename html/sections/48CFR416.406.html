CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 416  /  416.406 Contract clauses.

The contracting officer shall insert a clause substantially the same as the clause at 452.216-70, Award Fee, in solicitations and contracts which contemplate the award of cost-plus-award-fee contracts. [61 FR 53646, Oct. 15, 1996. Redesignated at 63 FR 26995, May 15, 1998.]
