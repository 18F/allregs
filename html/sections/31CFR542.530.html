CFR /  Title 31  /  Part 542  /  Sec. 542.530 Transactions incident to importations from Syria

All transactions otherwise prohibited by Sec. 542.207 that are ordinarily incident to an importation into the United States from Syria, directly or indirectly, of goods technology, or services, are authorized, provided the importation is not from or on behalf of, directly or indirectly, a person whose property and interests in property are blocked pursuant to Sec. 542.201(a).

Note to Sec. 542.530: This section does not authorize transactions that are ordinarily incident to an importation that is prohibited pursuant to 542.208 or any transaction prohibited pursuant to 542.209.
