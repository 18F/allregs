CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 850  /  Sec. 850.4 Enforcement.

DOE may take appropriate steps pursuant to part 851 of this chapter to enforce compliance by contractors with this part and any DOE-approved CBDPP. [71 FR 6931, Feb. 9, 2006]
