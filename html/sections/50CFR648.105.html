CFR /  Title 50  /  Part 648  /  Sec. 648.105 Summer flounder recreational fishing season.

Unless otherwise specified pursuant to Sec. 648.107, vessels that are not eligible for a moratorium permit under Sec. 648.4(a)(3), and fishermen subject to the possession limit, may fish for summer flounder from May 1 through September 30. This time period may be adjusted pursuant to the procedures in Sec. 648.102. [76 FR 60629, Sept. 29, 2011]
