CFR /  Title 27  /  Part 479  /  Sec. 479.162 Stamps authorized.

Adhesive stamps of the $5 and $200 denomination, bearing the words ``National Firearms Act,'' have been prepared and only such stamps shall be used for the payment of the transfer tax and for the tax on the making of a firearm. [T.D. ATF-270, 53 FR 10511, Mar. 31, 1988]
