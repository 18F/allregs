CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 172  /  Sec. 172.555 POISON INHALATION HAZARD placard.

(a) Except for size and color, the POISON INHALATION HAZARD placard must be as follows:[GRAPHIC] [TIFF OMITTED] TR22JY97.025

(b) In addition to complying with Sec. 172.519, the background on the POISON INHALATION HAZARD placard and the symbol must be white.
