CFR /  Title 12  /  Part 563  /  Sec. 563.27 Advertising.

No savings association shall use advertising (which includes print or broadcast media, displays or signs, stationery, and all other promotional materials), or make any representation which is inaccurate in any particular or which in any way misrepresents its services, contracts, investments, or financial condition. [54 FR 49552, Nov. 30, 1989, as amended at 58 FR 4313, Jan. 14, 1993]
