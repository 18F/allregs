CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 402  /  Sec. 402.135 Where to send a request.

You may send your request for a record to: The Deputy Executive Director for the Office of Public Disclosure, Office of the General Counsel, Social Security Administration, 6401 Security Boulevard, Baltimore, Maryland 21235. [62 FR 4154, Jan. 29, 1997, as amended at 68 FR 60295, Oct. 22, 2003]
