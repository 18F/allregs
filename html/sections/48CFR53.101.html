CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 53  /  53.101 Requirements for use of forms.

The requirements for use of the forms prescribed or referenced in this part are contained in parts 1 through 52, where the subject matter applicable to each form is addressed. The specific location of each requirement is identified in subpart 53.2. [48 FR 42637, Sept. 19, 1983, as amended at 63 FR 34080, June 22, 1998]
