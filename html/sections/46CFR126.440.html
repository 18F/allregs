CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 126  /  Sec. 126.440 Lifesaving equipment.

At each inspection for certification, the tests and inspections specified by Sec. 91.25-15 of this chapter must occur in the presence of a marine inspector, or as otherwise directed by the cognizant OCMI.
