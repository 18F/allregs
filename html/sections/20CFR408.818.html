CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 408  /  Sec. 408.818 When does SVB entitlement terminate if your benefit

We will terminate your SVB entitlement following 12 consecutive months of benefit suspension for any reason beginning with the first month you were no longer entitled to SVB. We will count the 12-month suspension period from the start of the first month that you are no longer entitled to SVB (see Sec. 408.802(a)). This termination is effective with the first day of the 13th month after the suspension began.
