CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 3273  /  Sec. 3273.15 What must I include in my site license application?

Your site license application must include:

(a) A description of the boundaries of the land applied for, as determined by a certified licensed surveyor. Describe the land by legal subdivision, section, township and range, or by approved protraction surveys, if applicable;

(b) The affected acreage;

(c) The filing fee for a site license application found in the fee schedule in Sec. 3000.12 of this chapter;

(d) A site license bond (see Sec. 3273.19);

(e) The first year's rent, if applicable (see Sec. 3273.18); and

(f) Documentation that the lessee or unit operator accepts the siting of the facility, if the facility operator is neither the lessee nor the unit operator. [72 FR 24400, May 2, 2007, as amended at 72 FR 50887, Sept. 5, 2007]
