CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 101-30  /  Sec. 101-30.001 Applicability.

The provisions of this part are applicable to all Federal agencies. However, they shall apply to the Department of Defense only when so specified within or by the subparts of this part. [36 FR 20292, Oct. 20, 1971]
