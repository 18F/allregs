CFR /  Title 37  /  Part 1  /  Sec. 1.42 Applicant for patent.

(a) The word ``applicant'' when used in this title refers to the inventor or all of the joint inventors, or to the person applying for a patent as provided in Sec. Sec. 1.43, 1.45, or 1.46.

(b) If a person is applying for a patent as provided in Sec. 1.46, the word ``applicant'' refers to the assignee, the person to whom the inventor is under an obligation to assign the invention, or the person who otherwise shows sufficient proprietary interest in the matter, who is applying for a patent under Sec. 1.46 and not the inventor.

(c) If fewer than all joint inventors are applying for a patent as provided in Sec. 1.45, the phrase ``the applicant'' means the joint inventors who are applying for the patent without the omitted inventor(s).

(d) Any person having authority may deliver an application and fees to the Office on behalf of the applicant. However, an oath or declaration, or substitute statement in lieu of an oath or declaration, may be executed only in accordance with Sec. 1.63 or 1.64, a correspondence address may be provided only in accordance with Sec. 1.33(a), and amendments and other papers must be signed in accordance with Sec. 1.33(b).

(e) The Office may require additional information where there is a question concerning ownership or interest in an application, and a showing may be required from the person filing the application that the filing was authorized where such authorization comes into question. [77 FR 48815, Aug. 14, 2012]
