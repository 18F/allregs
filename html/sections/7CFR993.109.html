CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 993  /  Sec. 993.109 Modified definition of non-French prunes.

The definition of non-French prunes set forth in Sec. 993.6 is modified to read as follows: Non-French Prunes means prunes commonly known as Imperial, Sugar, Robe de Sargent, Burton, Standard, Jefferson, Fellenberg, Italian, President, Giant, Hungarian (Gross), and Moyer, produced from such varieties of plums. [38 FR 22887, Aug. 27, 1973]
