CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 97  /  Sec. 97.142 For testing or increase.

An owner who contemplates filing an application and releases for testing or increase, seed of the variety or reproducible plant material of the variety, may label such plant material or containers of the seed or plant material substantially as follows: ``Unauthorized Propagation Prohibited--For Testing (or Increase) Only.'' [58 FR 42435, Aug. 9, 1993, as amended at 60 FR 17190, Apr. 4, 1995]
