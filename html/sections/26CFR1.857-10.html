CFR /  Title 26  /  Part 1  /  Sec. 1.857-10 Information returns.

Nothing in Sec. Sec. 1.857-8 and 1.857-9 shall be construed to relieve a real estate investment trust or its shareholders from the duty of filing information returns required by regulations prescribed under the provisions of subchapter A, chapter 61 of the Code.
