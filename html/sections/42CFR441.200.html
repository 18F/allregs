CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 441  /  Sec. 441.200 Basis and purpose.

This subpart implements section 402 of Pub. L. 97-12, and subsequent laws that appropriate funds for the Medicaid program, including section 204 of Pub. L. 98-619. All of these laws prohibit the use of Federal funds to pay for abortions except when continuation of the pregnancy would endanger the mother's life. [52 FR 47935, Dec. 17, 1987]
