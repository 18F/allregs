CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 153  /  Sec. 153.252 Special requirement for an independent cargo tank.

When Table 1 refers to this section, the cargo tank must be an independent tank that meets Sec. Sec. 38.05-2(d) and 38.05-4(g) of this chapter. (See also Sec. 153.256(b)). [CGD 78-128, 47 FR 21208, May 17, 1982]
