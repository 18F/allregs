CFR /  Title 13  /  Part 120  /  Sec. 120.818 Applicability to existing for-profit CDCs.

Unless expressly provided otherwise in the regulations, any Loan Program Requirement that applies to non-profit CDCs also applies to for-profit CDCs. [79 FR 15649, Mar. 21, 2014]
