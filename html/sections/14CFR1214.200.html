CFR /  Title 14  /  Part 1214  /  Sec. 1214.200 Scope.

This subpart 1214.2 sets forth:

(a) The policy on reimbursement for Shuttle services which are provided by NASA to users (as defined in Sec. 1214.201) under launch services agreements, and

(b) Responsibilities for putting such policy into effect and carrying it out.
