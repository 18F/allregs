CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 62  /  Sec. 62.211 Appeals.

Appeals of adverse decisions under this part, may be made in writing to the Livestock and Seed Program Deputy Administrator at STOP 0249, Room 2092-South, 1400 Independence Avenue, SW., Washington, DC 20250-0249. Appeals must be made within 30 days of receipt of adverse decision.

(a) Procedure for Appeals. Actions under this subparagraph concerning decision of appeals of the Deputy Administrator shall be conducted in accordance with the Rule of Practice Governing Formal Adjudicatory Proceedings Instituted by the Secretary Under Various Statutes set forth at 7 CFR Sec. 1.130 through Sec. 1.151 and the Supplemental Rules of Practice in 7 CFR part 50.
