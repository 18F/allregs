CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 400  /  Sec. 400.205 Federal funding for assistance and services for

Federal funding is available for a State's expenditures for service to unaccompanied minors under Sec. Sec. 400.115 through 400.120 of this part until the minor's status as an unaccompanied minor is terminated as specified by Sec. 400.113.
