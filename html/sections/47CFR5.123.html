CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 5  /  Sec. 5.123 Inspection of stations.

All stations and records of stations in the authorized under this part shall be made available for inspection at any time while the station is in operation or shall be made available for inspection upon reasonable request of an authorized representative of the Commission.

Effective Date Note: At 78 FR 25162, Apr. 29, 2013, Secs. 5.123 was revised. This section contains information collection and recordkeeping requirements and will not become effective until approval has been given by the Office of Management and Budget.
