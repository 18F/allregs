CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 30  /  Sec. 30.38 Application for amendment of licenses and registration

Applications for amendment of a license must be filed in accordance with Sec. 30.32 and must specify the respects in which the licensee desires its license to be amended and the grounds for the amendment. Applications for amendment of sealed source and device registration certificates must be filed in accordance with Sec. 32.210 of this chapter and any other applicable provisions and must specify the respects in which the certificate holder desires its certificate to be amended and the grounds for the amendment. [77 FR 43690, July 25, 2012]
