CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 3910  /  Sec. 3910.22 Lands managed by agencies other than the BLM.

(a) The consent and consultation procedures required by Sec. 3900.61 of this chapter also apply to exploration license applications.

(b) If exploration activities could affect the adjacent lands under the surface management of a Federal agency other than the BLM, the BLM will consult with that agency before issuing an exploration license.
