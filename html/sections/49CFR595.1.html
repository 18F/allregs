CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 595  /  Sec. 595.1 Scope.

This part establishes conditions under which the compliance of motor vehicles and motor vehicle equipment with the Federal motor vehicle safety standards may be made inoperative. [66 FR 12655, Feb. 27, 2001]
