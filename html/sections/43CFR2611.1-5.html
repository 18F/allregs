CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 2611  /  Sec. 2611.1-5 Priority of Carey Act applications.

Properly filed applications under Sec. 2611.1-1 or Sec. 2611.1-3 of this title shall have priority over any subsequently filed agricultural applications for lands within the project boundaries. However, the rejection of a Carey Act application will not preclude subsequent agricultural development under another authority.
