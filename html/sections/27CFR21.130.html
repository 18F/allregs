CFR /  Title 27  /  Part 21  /  Sec. 21.130 Spike lavender oil, natural.

(a) Alcohol content (as borneol). Not less than 30 percent by weight.

(b) Esters (as bornyl acetate). Not less than 1.5 percent by weight.

(c) Refractive index at 20 deg.C. 1.4630 to 1.4680.

(d) Specific gravity at 25 deg./25 deg.C. 0.893 to 0.909.

(e) Odor. Characteristic odor. [T.D. ATF-133, 48 FR 24673, June 2, 1983. Redesignated by T.D. ATF-442, 66 FR 12854, Mar. 1, 2001]
