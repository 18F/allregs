CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 217  /  217.208 Solicitation provisions and contract clauses.

Sealed bid solicitations shall not include provisions for evaluations of options unless the contracting officer determines that there is a reasonable likelihood that the options will be exercised (10 U.S.C. 2305(a)(5)). This limitation also applies to sealed bid solicitations for the contracts excluded by FAR 17.200. [63 FR 11529, Mar. 9, 1998, as amended at 71 FR 27642, May 12, 2006]
