CFR /  Title 19  /  Part 144  /  Sec. 144.12 Contents of entry summary; estimated duties.

The entry summary, Customs Form 7501, shall show the value, classification, and rate of duty as approved by the port director at the time the entry summary is filed. However, no deposit of estimated duties shall be required until the merchandise is withdrawn for consumption. [T.D. 79-221, 44 FR 46828, Aug. 9, 1979, as amended by T.D. 84-129, 49 FR 23168, June 5, 1984]
