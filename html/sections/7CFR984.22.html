CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 984  /  Sec. 984.22 Trade demand.

(a) Inshell. The quantity of merchantable inshell walnuts that the trade will acquire from all handlers during a marketing year for distribution in the United States and its territories.

(b) Shelled. The quantity of merchantable shelled walnuts that the trade will acquire from all handlers during a marketing year for distribution in the United States and its territories. [73 FR 11337, Mar. 3, 2008]
