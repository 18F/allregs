CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 39  /  Sec. 39.11 State or Tribal Organization to retain control of operations.

Neither the Secretary nor any employee of VA shall exercise any supervision or control over the administration, personnel, maintenance, or operation of any State or Tribal veterans cemetery that receives a grant under this program except as prescribed in this part. (Authority: 38 U.S.C. 501, 2408) [77 FR 4474, Jan. 30, 2012] Sec. Sec. 39.12-39.29 [Reserved]
