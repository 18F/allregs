CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 959  /  Sec. 959.115 Planting reports.

Each handler shall furnish every two weeks during the planting season to the committee, on a form provided by the committee, the number of acres of onions planted by the handler or growers for whom the handler packs onions during such period and the location of such plantings. [53 FR 7330, Mar. 8, 1988]
