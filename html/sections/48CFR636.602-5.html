CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 636  /  636.602-5 Short selection processes for contracts not to exceed the

The short selection process described in FAR 36.602-5 is authorized for use for contracts not expected to exceed the simplified acquisition threshold. [59 FR 66766, Dec. 28, 1994, as amended at 60 FR 39663, Aug. 3, 1995]
