CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 51  /  Sec. 51.761 Application of tolerances.

Individual samples are subject to the following limitations, unless otherwise specified in Sec. 51.760. Individual samples shall have not more than one and one-half times a specified tolerance of 10 percent or more, and not more than double a specified tolerance of less than 10 percent: Provided, that at least one decayed or wormy fruit may be permitted in any sample: And provided further, that the averages for the entire lot are within the tolerances specified for the grade. [62 FR 2897, Jan. 21, 1997]
