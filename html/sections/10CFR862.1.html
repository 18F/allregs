CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 862  /  Sec. 862.1 Purpose.

The purpose of this part is to set forth Department of Energy, hereinafter ``DOE'', security policy regarding aircraft and air delivery on nuclear sites under the jurisdiction of DOE pursuant to the Atomic Energy Act of 1954, as amended (42 U.S.C. 2011 et seq.).
