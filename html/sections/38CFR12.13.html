CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 12  /  Sec. 12.13 Posting of notice of the provisions of Pub. L. No. 734, 75th

In order that all persons who bring property on premises of the Department of Veterans Affairs may be advised of the existence of the act of June 25, 1938 (38 U.S.C. 16-16j), and that it affects such property, notice thereof (Form 4-1182), shall be permanently posted in at least one prominent place on the premises of each field facility where persons are likely to see such notice. [13 FR 7130, Nov. 27, 1948]
