CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 389  /  Sec. 389.40 What are the matching requirements?

A grantee must contribute to the cost of a project under this program in an amount satisfactory to the Secretary. The part of the cost to be borne by the grantee is determined by the Secretary at the time of the grant award. (Authority: Secs. 12(c) and 302 of the Act; 29 U.S.C. 711(c) and 774)
