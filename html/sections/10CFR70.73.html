CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 70  /  Sec. 70.73 Renewal of licenses.

Applications for renewal of a license must be filed in accordance with Sec. Sec. 2.109, 70.21, 70.22, 70.33, 70.38, and 70.65 of this chapter. Information contained in previous applications, statements, or reports filed with the Commission under the license may be incorporated by reference, provided that these references are clear and specific.
