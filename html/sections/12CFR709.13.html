CFR /  Title 12  /  Part 709  /  Sec. 709.13 Treatment of swap agreements in liquidation or

The Board has determined that a swap agreement, as defined in the Federal Deposit Insurance Act at 12 U.S.C. 1821(e)(8)(D)(vi), is a qualified financial contract for purposes of the special treatment for qualified financial contracts provided in 12 U.S.C. 1787(c). Any master agreement for any swap agreement, together with all supplements to such master agreement, will be treated as one swap agreement. [68 FR 32356, May 30, 2003]
