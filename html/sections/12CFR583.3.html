CFR /  Title 12  /  Part 583  /  Sec. 583.3 Bank.

The term bank means any national bank, state bank, state-chartered savings bank, cooperative bank, or industrial bank, the deposits of which are insured by the Deposit Insurance Fund. [71 FR 19812, Apr. 18, 2006]
