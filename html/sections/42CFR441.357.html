CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 441  /  Sec. 441.357 Hearing procedures for waiver denials.

The procedures specified in Sec. 430.18 of this subchapter apply to State requests for hearings on denials, renewals, or amendments of waivers for home and community-based services for individuals age 65 or older.
