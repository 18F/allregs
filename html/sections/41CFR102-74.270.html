CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 102-74  /  Sec. 102-74.270 Are vehicles required to display parking permits

When the use of parking space is controlled as in Sec. 102-74.265, all privately owned vehicles other than those authorized to use designated visitor or service areas must display a parking permit. This requirement may be waived in parking facilities where the number of available spaces regularly exceeds the demand for such spaces.
