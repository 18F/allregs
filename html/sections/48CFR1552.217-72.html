As prescribed in 1517.208(c), insert this contract clause in cost-plus-award-fee term contracts when applicable. If only one option period is used, enter ``NA'' in the proper places of the clause. If more than two option periods apply, modify the clause accordingly. Option To Extend the Term of the Contract--Cost-Plus-Award-Fee Contract
