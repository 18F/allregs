CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 3422  /  Sec. 3422.3-3 Unsurveyed lands.

If the land is unsurveyed, the successful bidder shall not be given notice to comply with the requirements of Sec. 3422.4 of this title for lease issuance until the land has been surveyed as provided in Sec. 3471.1-2 of this title.
