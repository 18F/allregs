CFR /  Title 26  /  Part 20  /  Sec. 20.6036-2 Notice of qualification as executor of estate of

In the case of the estate of a decedent dying after December 31, 1970, no special notice of qualification as executor of an estate is required to be filed. The requirement of section 6036 for notification of qualification as executor of an estate shall be satisfied by the filing of the estate tax return required by section 6018 and the regulations thereunder. [T.D. 7238, 37 FR 28721, Dec. 29, 1972]
