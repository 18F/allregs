CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 154  /  Sec. 154.1370 Pressure gauge and vacuum gauge marking.

Each pressure gauge and vacuum gauge under Sec. 154.1335(a) must be marked with the maximum and minimum pressures that are specified on the vessel's certificate for the cargo carried.
