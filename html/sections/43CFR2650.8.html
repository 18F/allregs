CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 2650  /  Sec. 2650.8 Appeals.

Any decision relating to a land selection shall become final unless appealed to the Board of Land Appeals by a person entitled to appeal, under, and in accordance with, subpart E of part 4, 43 CFR. (43 U.S.C. 1601-1624) [40 FR 33175, Aug. 6, 1975]
