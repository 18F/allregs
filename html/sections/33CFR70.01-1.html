CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 70  /  Sec. 70.01-1 General provisions.

No person, excluding the Armed Forces, shall obstruct or interfere with any aid to navigation established and maintained by the Coast Guard, or any private aid to navigation established and maintained in accordance with part 64, 66, 67, or 68 of this subchapter. [CGFR 58-17, 23 FR 3383, May 20, 1958]
