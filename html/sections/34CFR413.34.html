CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 413  /  Sec. 413.34 What activities must be performed during the final

During the fifth year of the award cycle, the National Center or Centers shall develop and remain prepared to implement a contingency plan for completing all substantive work by the end of the eleventh month of that year and transferring all projects, services, and activities to a successor during the twelfth month of that year. (Approved by the Office of Management and Budget under Control No. 1830-0013) (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 2404)
