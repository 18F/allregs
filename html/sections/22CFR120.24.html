CFR /  Title 22  /  Part 120  /  Sec. 120.24 Port Directors.

Port Directors of U.S. Customs and Border Protection means the U.S. Customs and Border Protection Port Directors at the U.S. Customs and Border Protection Ports of Entry (other than the port of New York, New York where their title is the Area Directors). [70 FR 50959, Aug. 29, 2005]
