CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 303  /  Sec. 303.438 Civil action.

Any party aggrieved by the findings and decision issued pursuant to a due process complaint has the right to bring a civil action in State or Federal court under section 639(a)(1) of the Act. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1439(a)(1))
