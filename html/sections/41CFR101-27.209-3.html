CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 101-27  /  Sec. 101-27.209-3 Disposition of unneeded property.

If no transfer is effected and no donation requested, the property shall be assigned for sale, abandonment, or destruction in accordance with part 101-45 of this chapter. [32 FR 6493, Apr. 27, 1967]
