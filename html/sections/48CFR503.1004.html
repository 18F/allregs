CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 503  /  503.1004 Contract clauses.

(a) The FAR threshold for the clause at 52.203-14, Display of Hotline Poster(s), is $5,000,000. However, GSA has exercised the authority provided at FAR 3.1004(b)(1)(i) to establish a lower threshold, $1,000,000, for inclusion of the clause when the contract or order is funded with disaster assistance funds.

(b) The information required to be inserted in the clause at FAR 52.203-14, Display of Hotline Poster(s), is as follows:

(1) Poster: GSA Office of Inspector General ``FRAUDNET HOTLINE''; and

(2) Obtain from: Contracting Officer. [74 FR 51512, Oct. 7, 2009]
