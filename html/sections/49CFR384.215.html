CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 384  /  Sec. 384.215 First offenses.

(a) General rule. The State must disqualify from operating a CMV each person who is convicted, as defined in Sec. 383.5 of this subchapter, in any State or jurisdiction, of a disqualifying offense specified in items (1) through (8) of Table 1 to Sec. 383.51 of this subchapter, for no less than one year.

(b) Special rule for hazardous materials offenses. If the offense under paragraph (a) of this section occurred while the driver was operating a vehicle transporting hazardous materials as defined in Sec. 383.5, the State shall disqualify the person for no less than three years. [59 FR 26039, May 18, 1994, as amended at 67 FR 49762, July 31, 2002; 77 FR 59825, Oct. 1, 2012]
