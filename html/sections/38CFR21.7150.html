CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 21  /  Sec. 21.7150 Pursuit.

Except for a veteran or servicemember seeking tuition assistance top-up or reimbursement for taking an approved licensing or certification test, the veteran's or servicemember's educational assistance depends upon his or her pursuit of a program of education. Verification of this pursuit is accomplished by various certifications. (Authority: 38 U.S.C. 3034(b)) [53 FR 1757, Jan. 22, 1988, as amended at 72 FR 16982, Apr. 5, 2007]
