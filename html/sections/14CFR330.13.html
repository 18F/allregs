CFR /  Title 14  /  Part 330  /  Sec. 330.13 If an air carrier received compensation under the Act

Yes, if, as an air carrier, you previously received compensation under section 101(a)(2) of the Act, you must, in all cases, submit a complete Form 330 (Final) and other documents required under this part. You must do so even if you are not seeking additional compensation. Sec. Sec. 330.15-330.17 [Reserved]
