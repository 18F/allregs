CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 215  /  215.407-2 Make-or-buy programs.

(a) General. See PGI 215.407-2 for guidance on factors to consider when deciding whether to request a make-or-buy plan and for factors to consider when evaluating make-or-buy plan submissions.

(e) Program requirements--(1) Items and work included. The minimum dollar amount is $1.5 million. [63 FR 55040, Oct. 14, 1998, as amended at 75 FR 45073, Aug. 2, 2010; 76 FR 76319, Dec. 7, 2011]
