CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 62  /  Sec. 62.37 Fee prohibition.

Grantees must not charge a fee to very low-income veteran families for providing supportive services that are funded with amounts from a supportive services grant. (Authority: 38 U.S.C. 501, 2044)
