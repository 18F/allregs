CFR /  Title 50  /  Part 665  /  Sec. 665.902 Definitions.

The following definitions are used in this subpart:

Management unit species or MUS means the Mariana Archipelago management unit species as defined in Sec. Sec. 665.401, 665.421, 665.441, and 665.461, and the pelagic management unit species as defined in Sec. 665.800.

Monument means the submerged lands and, where applicable, waters of the Marianas Trench Marine National Monument as defined in Sec. 665.901.

Proclamation means Presidential Proclamation 8335 of January 6, 2009, ``Establishment of the Marianas Trench Marine National Monument.''
