CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 503  /  503.570-1 Policy.

GSA policy precludes contractors from making references to GSA contracts in commercial advertising in a manner that states or implies the Government approves or endorses the product or service or considers it superior to other products or services. The intent of this policy is to prevent the appearance of Government bias toward any product or service. [64 FR 37204, July 9, 1999, as amended at 74 FR 51511, Oct. 7, 2009]
