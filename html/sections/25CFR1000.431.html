CFR /  Title 25  /  Part 1000  /  Sec. 1000.431 Does the Equal Access to Justice Act (EAJA) apply

Yes. EAJA claims against the DOI will be heard under 48 CFR 6101.30, 6101.31 (CBCA) and 43 CFR 4.602, 4.604 through 4.628 (DOI) and under the Equal Access to Justice Act, 5 U.S.C. 504 and 28 U.S.C. 2412. [75 FR 31702, June 4, 2010]
