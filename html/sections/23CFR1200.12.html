CFR /  Title 23  /  Part 1200  /  Sec. 1200.12 Due date for submission.

(a) Except as specified under Sec. 1200.61(a), a State shall submit its Highway Safety Plan electronically to the NHTSA regional office no later than July 1 preceding the fiscal year to which the Highway Safety Plan applies.

(b) Failure to meet this deadline may result in delayed approval and funding of a State's Section 402 grant or disqualification from receiving Section 405 grants.
