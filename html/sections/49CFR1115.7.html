CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 1115  /  Sec. 1115.7 Petitions for judicial review; mailing address.

Petitions for judicial review of final agency orders may be served on the Board pursuant to 28 U.S.C. 2112(a) and be addressed to ``General Counsel, Office of the General Counsel, Surface Transportation Board, Washington, DC 20423.'' [53 FR 20854, June 7, 1988, as amended at 61 FR 52714, Oct. 8, 1996]
