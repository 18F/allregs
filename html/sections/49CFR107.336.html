CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 107  /  Sec. 107.336 Limitation on fines and penalties.

If a State or political subdivision or Indian tribe assesses any fine or penalty determined by the Secretary to be appropriate for a violation concerning a subject listed in Sec. 107.202(a), no additional fine or penalty may be assessed for such violation by any other authority. [Amdt. 107-24, 56 FR 8624, Feb. 28, 1991]
