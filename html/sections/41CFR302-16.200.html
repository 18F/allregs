CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 302-16  /  Sec. 302-16.200 What governing policies must we establish for MEA?

For MEAs, you must establish policies and procedures governing:

(a) Who will determine whether payment for an amount in excess of the flat MEA is appropriate; and

(b) How you will pay a MEA in accordance with Sec. Sec. 302-16.3 and 302-16.4.
