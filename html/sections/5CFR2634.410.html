CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 2634  /  Sec. 2634.410 Dissolution.

Within thirty days of dissolution of a qualified trust, the interested party shall file a report of the dissolution with the Director and a list of assets of the trust at the time of the dissolution, categorized as to value in accordance with Sec. 2634.301(d).
