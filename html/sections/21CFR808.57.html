CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 808  /  Sec. 808.57 Connecticut.

The following Connecticut medical device requirements are enforceable notwithstanding section 521(a) of the act because the Food and Drug Administration has exempted them from preemption under section 521(b) of the act: Connecticut General Statutes, sections 20-403 and 20- 404. [45 FR 67336, Oct. 10, 1980]
