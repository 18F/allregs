CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 68  /  Sec. 68.15 When can an NIH LRP payment obligation be discharged

Any payment obligation incurred under Sec. 68.13 of this part may be discharged in bankruptcy under Title 11 of the United States Code only if such discharge is granted after the expiration of the seven-year period beginning on the first date that payment is required and only if the bankruptcy court finds that a non-discharge of the obligation would be unconscionable.
