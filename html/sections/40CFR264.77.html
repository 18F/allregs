CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 264  /  Sec. 264.77 Additional reports.

In addition to submitting the biennial reports and unmanifested waste reports described in Secs. 264.75 and 264.76, the owner or operator must also report to the Regional Administrator:

(a) Releases, fires, and explosions as specified in Sec. 264.56(j);

(b) Facility closures specified in Sec. 264.115; and

(c) As otherwise required by subparts F, K through N, AA, BB, and CC of this part. [46 FR 2849, Jan. 12, 1981, as amended at 47 FR 32350, July 26, 1982; 48 FR 3982, Jan. 28, 1983; 55 FR 25494, June 21, 1990; 59 FR 62926, Dec. 6, 1994]
