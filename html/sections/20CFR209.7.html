CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 209  /  Sec. 209.7 Employers' supplemental reports of service.

Each employer shall furnish the Board a report of the current year service of each employee who ceases work for the purpose of retiring under the provisions of the Railroad Retirement Act. (Approved by the Office of Management and Budget under control number 3220-0005) [63 FR 32613, June 15, 1998]
