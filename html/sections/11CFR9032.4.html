CFR /  Title 11  /  Part 9032  /  Sec. 9032.4 Contribution.

For purposes of this subchapter, contribution has the same meaning given the term under 52 U.S.C. 30101(8)(A) and 11 CFR part 100, subparts B and C, except as provided at 11 CFR 9034.4(b)(4). [56 FR 35929, July 29, 1991, as amended at 67 FR 78683, Dec. 26, 2002; 79 FR 77851, Dec. 29, 2014]
