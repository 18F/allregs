CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 735  /  Sec. 735.404 Schedule of charges and rates.

(a) A provider authorized under Sec. Sec. 735.401 or 735.402 must furnish FSA with copies of its current schedule of charges and rates for all services as they become effective.

(b) Charges and rates assessed any user by the provider must be in effect for a minimum period of one year.

(c) Providers must furnish FSA and all users a 60-calendar day advance notice of their intent to change any charges and rates.
