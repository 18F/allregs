CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 4100  /  Sec. 4100.3 Field activities.

The Corporation conducts its field activities from district and field offices around the country. District offices provide coordination of field activities in support of local programs within the geographic limits of each district. Field offices within each district provide assistance in the development and support of local programs. A current directory of all district and field offices can be obtained upon request from the Communications Department, Neighborhood Reinvestment Corporation, 1325 G Street NW., Suite 800, Washington, DC 20005. [49 FR 12700, Mar. 30, 1984, as amended at 54 FR 13061, Mar. 30, 1989]
