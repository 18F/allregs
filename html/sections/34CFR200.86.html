CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 200  /  Sec. 200.86 Use of MEP funds in schoolwide projects.

Funds available under part C of Title I of the ESEA may be used in a schoolwide program subject to the requirements of Sec. 200.29(c)(1). (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 6396) [67 FR 71736, Dec. 2, 2002; 68 FR 19152, Apr. 18, 2003]
