CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 244  /  244.303 Extent of review.

(a) Also review the adequacy of rationale documenting commercial item determinations to ensure compliance with the definition of ``commercial item'' in FAR 2.101.

(b) Also review the adequacy of the contractor's counterfeit electronic part detection and avoidance system under clause 252.246-7007, Contractor Counterfeit Electronic Part Detection and Avoidance System. [67 FR 38023, May 31, 2002, as amended at 79 FR 26106, May 6, 2014]
