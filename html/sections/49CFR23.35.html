CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 23  /  Sec. 23.35 What is the personal net worth standard for

The personal net worth standard used in determining eligibility for purposes of this part is $1.32 million. Any individual who has a personal net worth exceeding this amount is not a socially and economically disadvantaged individual for purposes of this part, even if the individual is a member of a group otherwise presumed to be disadvantaged. [70 FR 14508, Mar. 22, 2005, as amended at 77 FR 36931, June 20, 2012]
