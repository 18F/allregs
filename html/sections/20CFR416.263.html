CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 416  /  Sec. 416.263 No additional application needed.

We do not require you to apply for special cash benefits nor is it necessary for you to apply to have the special SSI eligibility status determined. We will make these determinations automatically. [47 FR 15324, Apr. 9, 1982]
