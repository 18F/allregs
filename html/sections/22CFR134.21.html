CFR /  Title 22  /  Part 134  /  Sec. 134.21 Filing and service of documents.

Any application for an award or other pleading or document related to an application shall be filed and served on all parties to the proceeding in the same manner as other pleadings in the proceeding, except as provided in Sec. 134.12(b) for confidential financial information.
