CFR /  Title 27  /  Part 28  /  Sec. 28.218 Disposition of Forms 1582-A (5120.24).

On removal of the wines from the premises, the exporter shall forward one copy of Form 1582-A (5120.24) to the appropriate TTB officer, retain one copy for his files, and deliver the original and remaining copy to the officer to whom the shipment is consigned, or in whose care it is shipped, as required by subpart M of this part. Where the shipment is for delivery for use on aircraft, the copy marked ``Consignee's Copy'', provided for in Sec. 28.214, shall be forwarded to the airline company at the airport. (46 Stat. 690, as amended, 48 Stat. 999, as amended, 72 Stat. 1336; 19 U.S.C. 1309, 81c, 26 U.S.C. 5062) [25 FR 5734, June 23, 1960. Redesignated at 40 FR 16835, Apr. 15, 1975, and amended by T.D.
