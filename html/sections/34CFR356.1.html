CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 356  /  Sec. 356.1 What is the Research Fellowships Program?

The purpose of this program is to build research capacity by providing support to highly qualified individuals, including those who are individuals with disabilities, to perform research on the rehabilitation of individuals with disabilities. (Authority: Sec. 202(d); 29 U.S.C. 761a(d)) [49 FR 24979, June 18, 1984, as amended at 58 FR 49419, Sept. 22, 1993]
