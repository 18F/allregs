CFR /  Title 25  /  Part 83  /  Sec. 83.34 What notice of the proposed finding will OFA provide?

In addition to publishing notice of the proposed finding in the Federal Register, OFA will:

(a) Provide copies of the proposed finding and any supporting reports to the petitioner and those listed in Sec. 83.22(d); and

(b) Publish the proposed finding and reports on the OFA Web site.
