CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 441  /  Sec. 441.12 Inpatient hospital tests.

Except in an emergency situation (see Sec. 440.170(e)(1) of this chapter for definition), FFP is not available in expenditures for inpatient hospital tests unless the tests are specifically ordered by the attending physician or other licensed practitioner, acting within the scope of practice as defined under State law, who is responsible for the diagnosis or treatment of a particular patient's condition. [46 FR 48554, Oct. 1, 1981]
