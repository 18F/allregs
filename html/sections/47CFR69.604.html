CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 69  /  Sec. 69.604 Billing and collection of access charges.

(a) Telephone companies shall bill and collect all access charges except those charges specified in Sec. Sec. 69.116 and 69.117.

(b) All access charges shall be billed monthly. [51 FR 9012, Mar. 17, 1986, as amended at 52 FR 21543, June 8, 1987]
