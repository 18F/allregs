CFR /  Title 31  /  Part 547  /  Sec. 547.508 Authorization of emergency medical services.

The provision of nonscheduled emergency medical services in the United States to persons whose property and interests in property are blocked pursuant to Sec. 547.201(a) is authorized, provided that all receipt of payment for such services must be specifically licensed.
