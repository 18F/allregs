CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 381  /  Sec. 381.1 What is the Protection and Advocacy of Individual Rights program?

This program is designed to support a system in each State to protect the legal and human rights of eligible individuals with disabilities. (Authority: Sec. 509(a) of the Act; 29 U.S.C. 794e(a))
