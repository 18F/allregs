CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 20  /  Sec. 20.114 Data and information submitted pursuant to cooperative

Data and information submitted to the Food and Drug Administration pursuant to a cooperative quality assurance agreement shall be handled in accordance with the rules established in Sec. 20.111.
