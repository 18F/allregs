CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 406  /  Sec. 406.2 Who is eligible for an award?

A State board of vocational education (State board) in the fifty States, Puerto Rico, the District of Columbia, or the Virgin Islands is eligible for an allotment under this program. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 2394a(b))
