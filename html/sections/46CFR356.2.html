CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 356  /  Sec. 356.2 Waivers.

In special circumstances and for good cause shown, we may waive the procedures prescribed in this part, provided the waiver is consistent with the requirements of the AFA and with the intent of this part. [66 FR 45947, Aug. 31, 2001]
