CFR /  Title 17  /  Part 10  /  Sec. 10.36 Commission review of rulings.

Interlocutory review by the Commission of a ruling as to matters within the scope of Sec. 10.33, Sec. 10.34 or Sec. 10.35 may be sought in accordance with the procedures set forth in Sec. 10.101 of these rules without certification by the Administrative Law Judge.
