CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 97  /  Sec. 97.101 Purpose.

This subpart and subparts BB through II set forth the general provisions and the designated representative, permitting, allowance, monitoring, and opt-in provisions for the Federal Clean Air Interstate Rule (CAIR) NOX Annual Trading Program, under section 110 of the Clean Air Act and Sec. 52.35 of this chapter, as a means of mitigating interstate transport of fine particulates and nitrogen oxides.
