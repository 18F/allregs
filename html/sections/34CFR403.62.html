CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 403  /  Sec. 403.62 What administrative provisions apply?

(a) Any project assisted with funds made available for the basic programs must be of sufficient size, scope, and quality to give reasonable promise of meeting the vocational education needs of the students involved in the project.

(b) Each State board receiving financial assistance for the basic programs may consider granting academic credit for vocational education courses that integrate core academic competencies. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 2468e (b) and (d))
