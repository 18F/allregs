CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 62  /  Sec. 62.11480 Identification of Plan-negative declaration.

On June 5, 2001, the Vermont Agency of Natural Resources submitted a letter certifying that there are no existing commercial and industrial solid waste incineration units in the state subject to the emission guidelines under part 60, subpart DDDD of this chapter. [66 FR 63940, Dec. 11, 2001]
