CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 605  /  Sec. 605.31 Application of this subpart.

Subpart D applies to preschool, elementary, secondary, and adult education programs or activities that receive or benefit from Federal financial assistance and to recipients that operate, or that receive Federal financial assistance for the operation of, such programs or activities. [47 FR 8573, Mar. 1, 1982, as amended at 68 FR 51381, Aug. 26, 2003]
