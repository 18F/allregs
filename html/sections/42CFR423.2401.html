CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 423  /  Sec. 423.2401 Definitions.

Non-claims costs means those expenses for administrative services that are not--

(1) Incurred claims (as provided in Sec. 423.2420(b)(2) through (b)(4));

(2) Expenditures on quality improving activities (as provided in Sec. 423.2430);

(3) Licensing and regulatory fees (as provided in Sec. 423.2420(c)(2)(i)); or

(4) State and Federal taxes and assessments (as provided in Sec. 423.2420(c)(2)(ii) and (iii)).
