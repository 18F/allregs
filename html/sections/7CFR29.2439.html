CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 29  /  Sec. 29.2439 Lugs (X Group).

This group consists of leaves that normally grow near the bottom of the stalk. These leaves usually have a blunt tip, tend to roll, and show ground injury. ------------------------------------------------------------------------
