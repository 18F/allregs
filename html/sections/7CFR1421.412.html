CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 1421  /  Sec. 1421.412 Powers of attorney.

DMAs may hold a power of attorney from a producer allowing the DMA to sign MAL and LDP documents for the producer, but DMAs may obtain and hold such powers only in accordance with the requirements of CCC governing such powers. [70 FR 33799, June 10, 2005. Redesignated at 74 FR 15656, Apr. 7, 2009]
