CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 350  /  Sec. 350.303 What are the State and Federal shares of expenses incurred

(a) The FMCSA will reimburse up to 80 percent of the eligible costs incurred in the administration of an approved CVSP.

(b) In-kind contributions are acceptable in meeting the State's matching share if they represent eligible costs as established by 49 CFR part 18 or agency policy.
