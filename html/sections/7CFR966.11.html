CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 966  /  Sec. 966.11 Pack.

Pack means any of the packs of tomatoes as defined and set forth in the United States Standards for Fresh Tomatoes issued by the United States Department of Agriculture (Sec. Sec. 51.1855 to 51.1877 of this title), or any pack of tomatoes recommended by the committee and approved by the Secretary.
