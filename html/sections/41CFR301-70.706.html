CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 301-70  /  Sec. 301-70.706 For what purposes may an employee use the Government

An employee is required to use the Government contractor-issued travel charge card for expenses directly related to official travel. [FTR Amdt. 2010-02, 75 FR 24436, May 5, 2010]
