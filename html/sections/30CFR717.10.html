CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 717  /  Sec. 717.10 Information collection.

The Office of Management and Budget has determined that the information collection requirements contained in 30 CFR part 717 do not require approval under the Paperwork Reduction Act. [59 FR 43420, Aug. 23, 1994]
