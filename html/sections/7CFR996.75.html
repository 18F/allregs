CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 996  /  Sec. 996.75 Effective time.

The provisions of this part, as well as any amendments, shall apply to 2003-04 and subsequent crop year peanuts, to 2002-03 and 2001-02 crop year peanuts not yet inspected, or failing peanuts that have not met disposition standards, and shall continue in force and effect until modified, suspended, or terminated. [68 FR 46924, Aug. 7, 2003]
