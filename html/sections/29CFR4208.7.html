CFR /  Title 29  /  Part 4208  /  Sec. 4208.7 Adjustment of withdrawal liability for subsequent

The liability of an employer for a partial or complete withdrawal from a plan subsequent to a partial withdrawal from that plan in a prior plan year shall be reduced in accordance with part 4206 of this chapter.
