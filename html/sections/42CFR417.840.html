CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 417  /  Sec. 417.840 Administrative review procedures.

The HCPP must apply Sec. 422.568 through Sec. 422.626 of this chapter to--

(a) Organization determinations and fast-track appeals that affect its Medicare enrollees; and

(b) Reconsiderations, hearings, Medicare Appeals Council review, and judicial review of the organization determinations and fast-track appeals specified in paragraph (a) of this section. [75 FR 19803, Apr. 15, 2010]
