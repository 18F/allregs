CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 63  /  Sec. 63.1291 Compliance schedule.

(a) Existing affected sources shall be in compliance with all provisions of this subpart no later than October 8, 2001, with the exception of Sec. 63.1297. Affected sources subject to the requirements of Sec. 63.1297 shall be in compliance with the requirements of this section on or before November 13, 2014.

(b) New or reconstructed affected sources shall be in compliance with all provisions of this subpart upon initial startup.[63 FR 53996, Oct. 7, 1998, as amended at 79 FR 48087, Aug. 15, 2014]
