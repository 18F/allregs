CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 502  /  Sec. 502.136 Enforcement.

In the event of failure to comply with any subpoena or order issued in connection therewith, the Commission may seek enforcement as provided in Sec. 502.210(b). [Rule 136.] [49 FR 44369, Nov. 6, 1984, as amended at 76 FR 10262, Feb. 24, 2011]
