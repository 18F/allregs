CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 56  /  Sec. 56.53 Fees or charges for grading service performed under

Fees or charges to be made to an applicant for grading service which differ from those listed in Sec. Sec. 56.45 through 56.54 shall be provided for by a cooperative agreement.
