CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 871  /  Sec. 871.3 Records.

Determinations made by the Deputy Administrator for Defense Programs pursuant to these rules shall be matters of record. Such authorizations shall be reported to the Administrator of the National Nuclear Security Administration within twenty-four hours after authorization is granted. [42 FR 48332, Sept. 23, 1977, as amended at 71 FR 68734, Nov. 28, 2006]
