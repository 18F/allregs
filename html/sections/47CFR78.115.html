CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 78  /  Sec. 78.115 Modulation limits.

(a) If amplitude modulation is employed, negative modulation peaks shall not exceed 100 percent modulation. [37 FR 3292, Feb. 12, 1972, as amended at 45 FR 78696, Nov. 26, 1980]
