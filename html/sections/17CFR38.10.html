CFR /  Title 17  /  Part 38  /  Sec. 38.10 Reporting of swaps traded on a designated contract market.

With respect to swaps traded on and/or pursuant to the rules of a designated contract market, each designated contract market must maintain and report specified swap data as provided under parts 43 and 45 of this chapter. [77 FR 36700, June 19, 2012]
