CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 711  /  Sec. 711.5 Chemical substances for which information must be reported.

Any chemical substance that is in the Master Inventory File at the beginning of a submission period described in Sec. 711.20, unless the chemical substance is specifically excluded by Sec. 711.6.
