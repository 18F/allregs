CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 136  /  Sec. 136.107 Use of project funds.

(a) A grantee shall only spend funds it receives under this subpart according to the approved application and budget, the regulations of this subpart, the terms and conditions of the award and the applicable cost principles prescribed in subpart Q of 45 CFR part 74.

(b) of the Act may be used as matching shares for any other Federal grant programs which contribute to the purposes for which grants under this section are made.

Note: This provision is excepted from application of 45 CFR 74.53 by section 104(c) of Pub. L. 93-638. [40 FR 53143, Nov. 14, 1975, as amended at 50 FR 1854, Jan. 14, 1985] Sec. 136.108 [Reserved]
