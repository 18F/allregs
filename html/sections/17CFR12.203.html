CFR /  Title 17  /  Part 12  /  Sec. 12.203 Filing of documents; subscription; service.

Except as otherwise specifically provided in these rules, all documents filed in a summary decisional proceeding, including (but not limited to) amended or supplemental pleadings, motions, discovery notices and responses thereto, documents produced or filed pursuant to Sec. 12.34 of these rules, and submissions of proof, shall meet the requirements of Sec. Sec. 12.11 and 12.12 of these rules as to form, and shall be filed and served in accordance with Sec. 12.10 of the Reparation Rules.
