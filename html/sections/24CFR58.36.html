CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 58  /  Sec. 58.36 Environmental assessments.

If a project is not exempt or categorically excluded under Sec. Sec. 58.34 and 58.35, the responsible entity must prepare an EA in accordance with subpart E of this part. If it is evident without preparing an EA that an EIS is required under Sec. 58.37, the responsible entity should proceed directly to an EIS.
