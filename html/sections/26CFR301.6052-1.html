CFR /  Title 26  /  Part 301  /  Sec. 301.6052-1 Information returns and statements regarding payment

For provisions relating to information returns and statements required in connection with the payment of wages in the form of group-term life insurance, see Sec. Sec. 1.6052-1 and 1.6052-2 of this chapter (income tax regulations). [T.D. 7275, 38 FR 11346, May 7, 1973]
