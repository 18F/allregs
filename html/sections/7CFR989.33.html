CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 989  /  Sec. 989.33 Alternate members.

The alternate for a member of the committee shall act in the place and stead of such member (a) during his absence, and (b) in the event of his removal, resignation, disqualification, or death, until a successor for such member's unexpired term has been selected and has qualified. [41 FR 32412, Aug. 3, 1976]
