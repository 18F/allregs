CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 915  /  Sec. 915.11 District.

District means the applicable one of the following described subdivisions of the production area:

(a) District 1 shall include Miami-Dade County.

(b) District 2 shall include all of the production area except Miami-Dade County. [19 FR 3439, June 11, 1954. Redesignated at 26 FR 12751, Dec. 30, 1961, as amended at 73 FR 6837, Feb. 6, 2008]
