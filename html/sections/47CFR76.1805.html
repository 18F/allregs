CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 76  /  Sec. 76.1805 Alternative rate regulation agreements.

Small systems owned by small cable companies must file with the Commission a copy of any operative alternative rate regulation agreement entered into with a local franchising authority pursuant to Sec. 76.934(g), within 30 days after its effective date.
