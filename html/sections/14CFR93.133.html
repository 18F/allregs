CFR /  Title 14  /  Part 93  /  Sec. 93.133 Exceptions.

Except as provided in Sec. 93.130, the provisions of Sec. Sec. 93.123 and 93.125 do not apply to--

(a) The Newark Airport, Newark, NJ;

(b) The Kennedy International Airport, New York, NY, except during the hours from 3 p.m. through 7:59 p.m., local time; and

(c) O'Hare International Airport from 9:15 p.m. to 6:44 a.m., local time. [Doc. No. 24471, 49 FR 8244, Mar. 6, 1984] Subpart L [Reserved]
