CFR /  Title 32  /  Part 1908  /  Sec. 1908.41 Right of further appeal.

A right of further appeal may be available to the Interagency Security Classification Appeals Panel established pursuant to section 5.3 of the Order. Action by that Panel will be the subject of rules to be promulgated by the Information Security Oversight Office. [76 FR 59034, Sept. 23, 2011]
