CFR /  Title 36  /  Part 7  /  Sec. 7.72 Arkansas Post National Memorial.

(a) Launching, beaching, or landing of vessels. Except in emergencies, no vessel shall be launched, beached, or landed from or on lands within the Arkansas Post National Memorial. [35 FR 13206, Aug. 19, 1970]
