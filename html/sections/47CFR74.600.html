CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 74  /  Sec. 74.600 Eligibility for license.

A license for a station in this subpart will be issued only to a television broadcast station, a Class A TV station, a television broadcast network-entity, a low power TV station, or a TV translator station. [ 65 FR 30011, May 10, 2000]
