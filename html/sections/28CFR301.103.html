CFR /  Title 28  /  Part 301  /  Sec. 301.103 Inmate work assignments.

The unit team of each inmate, which ordinarily designates work assignments, or whoever makes work assignments, shall review appropriate medical records, presentence reports, admission summaries, and all other available information prior to the designation of an inmate to a work assignment in an effort to preclude the assignment of an inmate to a work assignment not compatible with the inmate's physical ability or condition. [55 FR 9296, Mar. 12, 1990, as amended at 59 FR 2667, Jan. 18, 1994]
