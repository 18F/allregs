CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 519  /  519.7017 Contract clauses.

(a) The contracting officer shall insert the clause at 552.219-75, GSA Mentor-Protege Program, in all unrestricted solicitations (not set aside) and contracts that exceed the simplified acquisition threshold that offer subcontracting opportunities or in the case of a small business, that can offer developmental assistance to a small business Protege.

(b) The contracting officer shall insert the clause at 552.219-76, Mentor Requirements and Evaluation, in contracts anticipated to exceed the simplified acquisition threshold where the prime contractor has signed a Mentor-Protege Agreement with GSA.
