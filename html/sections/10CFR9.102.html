CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 9  /  Sec. 9.102 General requirement.

Commissioners shall not jointly conduct or dispose of Commission business in Commission meetings other than in accordance with this subpart. Except as provided in Sec. 9.104, every portion of every meeting of the Commission shall be open to public observation.
