CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 1  /  Sec. 1.13 Date of receipt of requests or appeals.

The date of receipt of a request or appeal shall be the date it is received in the agency and office responsible for the administrative processing of FOIA requests or appeals. [65 FR 46339, July 28, 2000]
