CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 301-71  /  Sec. 301-71.212 Should we report late payment fees as wages on a Form W-2?

No, the Internal Revenue Service (IRS) has determined that the late payment fee is in the nature of interest (compensation for the use of money). [FTR Amdt. 90, 65 FR 3058, Jan. 19, 2000]
