CFR /  Title 19  /  Part 210  /  Sec. 210.65 Certification of the record.

When the administrative law judge issues an initial determination concerning temporary relief pursuant to Sec. 210.66(a), he shall also certify to the Commission the record upon which the initial determination is based.
