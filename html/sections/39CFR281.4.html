CFR /  Title 39  /  Part 281  /  Sec. 281.4 Disclaimer.

The Postal Service will not be liable in damages for any loss occasioned by any failure to notify firm mailers in accordance with this part of damage to or destruction of firm mailings. [39 FR 20974, June 17, 1974]
