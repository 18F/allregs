CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 153  /  Sec. 153.1126 Discharge of NLS residue from a slop tank to the

NLS residue in a slop tank may not be discharged into the sea unless--

(a) The ship meets the conditions for discharging the NLS residue from a cargo tank in Sec. 153.1128; and

(b) For Category B NLS residue transferred to the slop tank under Sec. 153.1119(b), the NLS is discharged--

(1) Through an NLS residue discharge system with the flow recording equipment required in Sec. 153.481(b)(2) operating; and

(2) At a rate maintained at or below Q in the following:
