CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 180  /  Sec. 180.1072 Poly-D-glucosamine (chitosan); exemption from the

(a) An exemption from the requirement of a tolerance is established for residues of the biological plant growth regulator poly-D-glucosamine when used as a seed treatment in or on barley, beans, oats, peas, rice, and wheat.

(b) An exemption from the requirement of a tolerance is established for residues of the biological plant growth regulator poly-D-glucosamine when used as a pesticide in the production any raw agricultural commodity. [60 FR 19524, Apr. 19, 1995]
