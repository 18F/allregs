CFR /  Title 11  /  Part 107  /  Sec. 107.2 Registration and reports by host committees and municipal funds.

Each host committee and municipal fund shall register and report in accordance with 11 CFR 9008.51. The reports shall contain the information specified in 11 CFR part 104. [68 FR 47414, Aug. 8, 2003]
