CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 180  /  Sec. 180.1135 Pasteuria penetrans; exemption from the requirement

The biological nematicide Pasteuria penetrans is exempted from the requirement of a tolerance in or on all raw agricultural commodities, except roots and tubers, when used as a nematicide in the production of fruits and vegetables in greenhouses. [59 FR 66741, Dec. 28, 1994]
