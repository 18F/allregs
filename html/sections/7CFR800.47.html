CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 800  /  Sec. 800.47 Withdrawal of request for official services.

An applicant may withdraw a request for official services any time before official personnel release results, either verbally or in writing. See Sec. 800.51 for reimbursement of expenses, if any. (Secs. 8, 9, 10, 13 and 18, Pub. L. 94-582, 90 Stat. 2870, 2875, 2877, 2880, and 2884, 7 U.S.C. 79, 79a, 79b, 84, 87, and 87e) [49 FR 30915, Aug. 2, 1984]
