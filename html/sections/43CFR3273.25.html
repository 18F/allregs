CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 3273  /  Sec. 3273.25 When may I relinquish my site license?

You may request approval to relinquish your site license by sending BLM a written notice requesting relinquishment review and approval. We will not approve the relinquishment until you comply with Sec. 3273.21.
