CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 1600  /  Sec. 1600.32 Treatment accorded transferred funds.

(a) All funds transferred to the TSP pursuant to Secs. 1600.30 and 1600.31 will be treated as employee contributions.

(b) All funds transferred to the TSP pursuant to Secs. 1600.30 and 1600.31 will be invested in accordance with the participant's contribution allocation on file at the time the transfer is completed.

(c) Funds transferred to the TSP pursuant to Secs. 1600.30 and 1600.31 are not subject to the limits on contributions described in Sec. 1600.22. [66 FR 22089, May 2, 2001. Redesignated and amended at 77 FR 26424, May 4, 2012]
