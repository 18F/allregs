CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 3110  /  Sec. 3110.3-1 Duration of lease.

All noncompetitive leases shall be for a primary term of 10 years. [53 FR 22840, June 17, 1988; 53 FR 31958, Aug. 22, 1988]
