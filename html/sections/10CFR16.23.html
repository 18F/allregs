CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 16  /  Sec. 16.23 Interest, penalties, and administrative charges.

Charges may be assessed for interest, penalties, and administrative charges in accordance with the FCCS, 31 CFR Chapter IX, 901.9. [67 FR 57509, Sept. 11, 2002]
