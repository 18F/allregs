CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 725  /  Sec. 725.462 Withdrawal of controversion of issues set for formal

A party may, on the record, withdraw his or her controversion of any or all issues set for hearing. If a party withdraws his or her controversion of all issues, the administrative law judge shall remand the case to the district director for the issuance of an appropriate order.
