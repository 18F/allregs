CFR /  Title 13  /  Part 124  /  Sec. 124.301 What are the ways a business may leave the 8(a) BD program?

A concern participating in the 8(a) BD program may leave the program by any of the following means:

(a) Expiration of the program term established pursuant to Sec. 124.2;

(b) Voluntary withdrawal or voluntary early graduation;

(c) Graduation pursuant to Sec. 124.302;

(d) Early graduation pursuant to the provisions of Secs. 124.302 and 124.304; or

(e) Termination pursuant to the provisions of Secs. 124.303 and 124.304. [76 FR 8258, Feb. 11, 2011]
