CFR /  Title 26  /  Part 53  /  Sec. 53.6695-1 Other assessable penalties with respect to the

(a) of the Code, failure to sign the return under section 6695(b) of the Code, failure to furnish an identification number under section 6695(c) of the Code, failure to retain a copy or list under section 6695(d) of the Code, failure to file a correct information return under section 6695(e) of the Code, and negotiation of a check under section 6695(f) of the Code, in the manner stated in Sec. 1.6695-1 of this chapter.

(b) Effective/applicability date. This section is applicable to returns and claims for refund filed after December 31, 2008. [T.D. 9436, 73 FR 78458, Dec. 22, 2008]
