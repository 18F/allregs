CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 366  /  Sec. 366.26 How does the Secretary evaluate an application?

(a) The Secretary evaluates each application for a grant under this subpart on the basis of the criteria in Sec. 366.27.

(b) The Secretary awards up to 100 points for these criteria.

(c) The maximum possible score for each criterion is indicated in parentheses. (Authority: 29 U.S.C. 796f(b)(3))
