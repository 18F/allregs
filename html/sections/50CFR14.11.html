CFR /  Title 50  /  Part 14  /  Sec. 14.11 General restrictions.

Except as otherwise provided in this part, no person may import or export any wildlife at any place other than a Customs port of entry designated in Sec. 14.12. [45 FR 56673, Aug. 25, 1980; 45 FR 64953, Oct. 1, 1980]
