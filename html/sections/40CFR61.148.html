CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 61  /  Sec. 61.148 Standard for insulating materials.

No owner or operator of a facility may install or reinstall on a facility component any insulating materials that contain commercial asbestos if the materials are either molded and friable or wet-applied and friable after drying. The provisions of this section do not apply to spray-applied insulating materials regulated under Sec. 61.146. [55 FR 48424, Nov. 20, 1990]
