CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 63  /  Sec. 63.7902 What are my inspection and monitoring requirements for containers?

(a) You must inspect each container using Container Level 1 or Container Level 2 controls according to the requirements in Sec. 63.926(a).

(b) If you use Container Level 3 controls, you must meet requirements in paragraphs (b)(1) and (2) of this section, as applicable to your site remediation.

(1) You must perform the verification procedure for each permanent total enclosure annually according to the requirements in Sec. 63.924(c)(1).

(2) You must monitor and inspect each closed vent system and control device according to the requirements in Sec. 63.7927 that apply to you.
