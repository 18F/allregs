CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 90  /  Sec. 90.614 Segments of the 806-824/851-869 MHz band for non-border

The 806-824/851-869 MHz band (``800 MHz band'') will be divided as follows at locations farther then 110 km (68.4 miles) from the U.S./Mexico border and 140 km (87 miles) from the U.S./Canadian border (``non-border areas'')

(a) 800 MHz high density cellular systems--as defined in Sec. 90.7--are prohibited from operating on channels 1-550 in non-border areas.

(b) 800 MHz high density cellular systems--as defined in Sec. 90.7--are permitted to operate on channels 551-830 in non-border areas.
