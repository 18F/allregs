CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 704  /  Sec. 704.9 Where to send reports.

Reports must be submitted by certified mail to the Document Control Office (DCO) (7407M), Office of Pollution Prevention and Toxics (OPPT), Environmental Protection Agency, 1200 Pennsylvania Ave., NW., Washington, DC 20460-0001, ATTN: 8(a) Reporting. [60 FR 34463, July 3, 1995, as amended at 71 FR 33641, June 12, 2006]
