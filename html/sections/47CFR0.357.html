CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 0  /  Sec. 0.357 Record of actions taken.

The official record of all actions taken by the Chief Administrative Law Judge in docketed proceedings pursuant to Sec. 0.351 is contained in the original docket folder, which is maintained by the Reference Information Center of the Consumer and Governmental Affairs Bureau. [64 FR 60722, Nov. 8, 1999, as amended at 67 FR 13221, Mar. 21, 2002]
