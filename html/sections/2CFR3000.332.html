CFR /  Title 2  /  Part 3000  /  Sec. 3000.332 What methods must I use to pass requirements down to

You as a participant in a covered transaction must include a term or condition in any lower-tier covered transaction into which you enter, to require the participant of that transaction to--

(a) Comply with Subpart C of the OMB guidance in 2 CFR part 180; and

(b) Include a similar term or condition in any covered transaction into which it enters at the next lower tier. Subpart D_Responsibilities of Department of Homeland Security Officials
