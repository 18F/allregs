CFR /  Title 31  /  Part 515  /  Sec. 515.334 United States national.

As used in Sec. 515.208, the term United States national means:

(a) Any United States citizen; or

(b) Any other legal entity which is organized under the laws of the United States, or of any State, the District of Columbia, or any commonwealth, territory, or possession of the United States, and which has its principal place of business in the United States. [61 FR 37386, July 18, 1996]
