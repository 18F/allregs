CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 764  /  Sec. 764.202 Eligibility requirements.

The applicant must:

(a) Comply with the general eligibility requirements established at Sec. 764.101 and the FO eligibility requirements of Sec. 764.152; and

(b) Be a beginning farmer or socially disadvantaged farmer. [72 FR 63298, Nov. 8, 2007, as amended at 73 FR 74345, Dec. 8, 2008]
