CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 301  /  Sec. 301.50-2 Regulated articles.

The following are regulated articles:

(a) Pine products (Pinus spp.), as follows: Bark products; Christmas trees; logs and firewood with bark attached; lumber with bark attached; nursery stock; raw pine materials for pine wreaths and garlands; and stumps.

(b) Any article, product, or means of conveyance not covered by paragraph (a) of this section, that presents a risk of spread of the pine shoot beetle and that an inspector notifies the person in possession of it is subject to the restrictions of this subpart. [57 FR 54496, Nov. 19, 1992, as amended at 60 FR 55778, Nov. 3, 1995; 65 FR 51518, Aug. 24, 2000; 71 FR 13925, Mar. 20, 2006; 79 FR 21597, Apr. 17, 2014]
