CFR /  Title 31  /  Part 363  /  Sec. 363.170 What transactions can I conduct in a savings bond that I owner with beneficiary?

The only transaction that you may conduct on a savings bond that you converted on which you are not registered as the owner, either coowner, or owner with beneficiary is to deliver the converted bond to the TreasuryDirect [supreg] account of the registered owner.
