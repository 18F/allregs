CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 75  /  Sec. 75.822 Underground high-voltage longwall cables.

In addition to the high-voltage cable design specifications in Sec. 75.804 of this part, high-voltage cables for use on longwalls may be a type SHD cable with a center ground-check conductor no smaller than a No. 16 AWG stranded conductor. The cables must be MSHA accepted as flame-resistant under part 18 or approved under subpart K of part 7.
