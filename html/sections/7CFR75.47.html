CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 75  /  Sec. 75.47 For certificates.

A charge of $13.00 per certificate will be made for copies of certificates other than those required to be distributed in Sec. 75.23 and for the issuance of a duplicate certificate in accordance with Sec. 75.24 and an appeal certificate in Sec. 75.31.
