CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 585  /  Sec. 585.823 Will BOEM reimburse me for my expenses related to inspections?

Upon request, BOEM will reimburse you for food, quarters, and transportation that you provide for our representatives while they inspect your lease or grant facilities and associated activities. You must send us your reimbursement request within 90 days of the inspection.
