CFR /  Title 31  /  Part 317  /  Sec. 317.0 Purpose and effective date.

The regulations in this part govern the manner in which an organization may qualify and act as an agent for the sale and issue of Series EE and Series I United States Savings Bonds. [63 FR 38041, July 14, 1998]
