CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 456  /  Sec. 456.506 Waiver options for Medicaid agency.

(a) The agency may apply for a waiver at any time it has the procedures referred to under Sec. 456.505(b) in operation at least--

(1) On a demonstration basis; or

(2) In any part of the State.

(b) Any hospital or mental hospital participating under the plan that is not covered by a waiver must continue to meet all the UR plan requirements under subpart C or D of this part. [43 FR 45266, Sept. 29, 1978, as amended at 61 FR 38399, July 24, 1996]
