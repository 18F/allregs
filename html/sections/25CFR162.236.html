CFR /  Title 25  /  Part 162  /  Sec. 162.236 How will a cash bond be administered?

(a) If a cash bond is submitted, we will retain the funds in an account established in the name of the tenant.

(b) We will not pay interest on a cash performance bond.

(c) If the bond is not forfeited under Sec. 162.252(a) of this subpart, we will refund the bond to the tenant upon the expiration or termination of the lease.
