CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 301-74  /  Sec. 301-74.22 When should actual expense reimbursement be authorized

You may authorize actual expenses under Sec. 301-11.300 of this chapter when the applicable lodging rate is inadequate. [FTR Amdt. 2013-01, 78 FR 65212, Oct. 31, 2013]
