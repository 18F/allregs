CFR /  Title 27  /  Part 26  /  Sec. 26.61 General.

Every person filing a bond under this subpart, or consent of surety on such bond, must file it in accordance with the instructions on the form. [T.D. ATF-451, 66 FR 21669, May 1, 2001]
