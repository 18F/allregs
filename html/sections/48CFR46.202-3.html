CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 46  /  46.202-3 Standard inspection requirements.

(a) Standard inspection requirements are contained in the clauses prescribed in 46.302 through 46.308, and in the product and service specifications that are included in solicitations and contracts.

(b) The clauses referred to in (a) above--

(1) Require the contractor to provide and maintain an inspection system that is acceptable to the Government;

(2) Give the Government the right to make inspections and tests while work is in process; and

(3) Require the contractor to keep complete, and make available to the Government, records of its inspection work. [48 FR 42415, Sept. 19, 1983. Redesignated at 60 FR 48249, Sept. 18, 1995; 72 FR 27389, May 15, 2007]
