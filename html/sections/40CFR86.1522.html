CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 86  /  Sec. 86.1522 Carbon monoxide analyzer calibration.

(a) Initial check. (1) Follow good engineering practice for instrument start-up and operation. Adjust the analyzer to optimize performance on the range specified in Sec. 86.1511-84(a)(1).

(1) Follow good engineering practice for instrument start-up and operation. Adjust the analyzer to optimize performance on the range specified in Sec. 86.1511-84(a)(1).

(2) Calibrate the analyzer with the calibration gas specified in Sec. 86.1514-84.

(3) Adjust the electrical span network such that the electrical span point is correct when the analyzer reads the calibration gas correctly.

(4) Determine that the analyzer complies with the specifications in Sec. 86.1511-84.

(b) Periodic check. Follow paragraphs (a) (1), (2), and (3) of this section as specified by Sec. 86.1516-84(b). Adjust or repair the analyzer as necessary. [48 FR 52252, Nov. 16, 1983. Redesignated at 73 FR 37194, June 30, 2008]
