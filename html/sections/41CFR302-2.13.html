CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 302-2  /  Sec. 302-2.13 What is a service agreement?

A service agreement is a written agreement between you and your agency, signed by you and an agency representative, stating that you will remain in the service of the Government for a period of time as specified in Sec. 302-2.14, after you have relocated. A service agreement must also include the duplicate reimbursement disclosure statement specified in Sec. Sec. 302-2.21, 302-2.22, and 302-2.100(g).
