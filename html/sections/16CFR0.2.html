CFR /  Title 16  /  Part 0  /  Sec. 0.2 Official address.

The principal office of the Commission is at Washington, DC. All communications to the Commission should be addressed to the Federal Trade Commission, 600 Pennsylvania Avenue, NW, Washington, DC 20580, unless otherwise specifically directed. The Commission's Web site address is www.ftc.gov. [63 FR 71582, Dec. 29, 1998, as amended at 65 FR 78408, Dec. 15, 2000]
