CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 102-42  /  Sec. 102-42.55 What does the employing agency do with the appraisal?

When an appraisal is necessary under Sec. 102-42.40, the employing agency must include the appraisal with the Standard Form (SF) 120, Report of Excess Personal Property, and send it to GSA in accordance with the requirements of Sec. 102-42.95. By attaching the appraisal, the employing agency is certifying that the value cited is the retail value/appraised value of the item in the United States in U.S. dollars on the date set forth on the appraisal. [74 FR 2396, Jan. 15, 2009]
