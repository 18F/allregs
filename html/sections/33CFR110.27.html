CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 110  /  Sec. 110.27 Lynn Harbor in Broad Sound, Mass.

North of a line bearing 244[deg] from the tower of the Metropolitan District Building, extending from the shore to a point 100 feet from the east limit of the channel; east of a line bearing 358[deg], extending thence to a point 100 feet east of the northeast corner of the turning basin; south of a line bearing 88[deg], extending thence to the shore; and south and west of the shoreline to its intersection with the south boundary. [USCG-2009-0416, 74 FR 27439, June 10, 2009]
