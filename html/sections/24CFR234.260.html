CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 234  /  Sec. 234.260 Assignment of mortgage and certificate by mortgagee.

In addition to the requirements of Secs. 203.350 through 203.353 incorporated by reference, the mortgagee shall certify as to any changes in the plan of apartment ownership including the administration of the property. Any changes shall require FHA approval. [36 FR 24628, Dec. 21, 1971, as amended at 42 FR 29305, June 8, 1977]
