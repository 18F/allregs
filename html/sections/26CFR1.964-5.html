CFR /  Title 26  /  Part 1  /  Sec. 1.964-5 Effective date of subpart F.

Sections 951 through 964 and Secs. 1.951 through 1.964-4 shall apply with respect to taxable years of foreign corporations beginning after December 31, 1962, and to taxable years of United States shareholders within which or with which such taxable years of such corporations end. [T.D. 7120, 36 FR 10862, June 4, 1971]
