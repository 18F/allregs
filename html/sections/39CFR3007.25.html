CFR /  Title 39  /  Part 3007  /  Sec. 3007.25 Use of non-public materials.

Except as pursuant to this part, persons with access to non-public materials under Sec. 3007.24 may not:

(a) Use such materials for purposes other than the purposes for which they are supplied.

(b) Permit anyone who is not allowed access under Sec. 3007.24 to have access to any such materials.
