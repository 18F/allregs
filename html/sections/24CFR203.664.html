CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 203  /  Sec. 203.664 Processing defaulted mortgages on property located on

Before a mortgagee requests that the Secretary accept assignment under Sec. 203.350(b) of a mortgage insured pursuant to section 248 of the National Housing Act (Sec. 203.43h), the mortgagee must submit documents showing that the requirements of Sec. 203.604 have been met.
