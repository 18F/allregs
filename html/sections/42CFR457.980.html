CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 457  /  Sec. 457.980 Verification of enrollment and provider services

The State must establish and maintain systems to identify, report, and verify the accuracy of claims for those enrolled children who meet requirements of section 2105(a) of the Act, where enhanced Federal medical assistance percentage computations apply. [66 FR 2685, Jan. 11, 2001, as amended at 66 FR 33824, June 25, 2001]
