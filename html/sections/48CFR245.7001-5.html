CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 245  /  245.7001-5 DD Form 1641, Disposal Determination/Approval.

Use to record rationale for the following disposal determinations:

(a) Downgrade useable property to scrap.

(b) Abandonment or destruction.

(c) Noncompetitive sale of surplus property.

(d) Other disposal actions. 245.7001-6, DLA Form 1822, End Use Certificate.

Use when directed by the plant clearance officer. [56 FR 36448, July 31, 1991, as amended at 77 FR 52254, Aug. 29, 2012]
