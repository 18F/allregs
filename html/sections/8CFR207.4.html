CFR /  Title 8  /  Part 207  /  Sec. 207.4 Approved application.

Approval of a refugee application by USCIS outside the United States authorizes CBP to admit the applicant conditionally as a refugee upon arrival at the port within four months of the date the refugee application was approved. There is no appeal from a denial of refugee status under this chapter. [76 FR 53783, Aug. 29, 2011]
