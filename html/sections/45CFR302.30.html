CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 302  /  Sec. 302.30 Publicizing the availability of support enforcement services.

Effective October 1, 1985, the State plan shall provide that the State will publicize regularly and frequently the availability of support enforcement services under the plan through public service announcements. Publicity must include information on any application fees which may be imposed for such services and a telephone number or postal address where further information may be obtained. (Approved by the Office of Management and Budget under control number 0960-0385) [50 FR 19647, May 9, 1985, as amended at 51 FR 37731, Oct. 24, 1986]
