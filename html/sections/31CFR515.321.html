CFR /  Title 31  /  Part 515  /  Sec. 515.321 United States; continental United States.

The term United States means the United States and all areas under the jurisdiction or authority thereof, including the Trust Territory of the Pacific Islands. The term continental United States means the States of the United States and the District of Columbia. [49 FR 27144, July 2, 1984]
