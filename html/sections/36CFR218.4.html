CFR /  Title 36  /  Part 218  /  Sec. 218.4 Proposed projects and activities not subject to objection.

Proposed projects and activities are not subject to objection when no timely, specific written comments regarding the proposed project or activity (see Sec. 218.2) are received during any designated opportunity for public comment (see Sec. 218.5(a)). The responsible official must issue a statement in the Record of Decision or Decision Notice that the project or activity was not subject to objection.
