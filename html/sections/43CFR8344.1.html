CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 8344  /  Sec. 8344.1 Permit requirements.

Permits are required for certain types of ORV use and shall be issued in accordance with the special recreation permit procedures under part 2930 of this chapter. [44 FR 34836, June 15, 1979, as amended at 67 FR 61745, Oct. 1, 2002]
