CFR /  Title 22  /  Part 228  /  Sec. 228.18 Long-term leases.

Any commodity obtained under a long-term lease agreement as defined in Sec. 228.01, including motor vehicles, is subject to the source and nationality requirements of this subpart B of this part, including the special procurement rules as set forth in Sec. 228.19.
