CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 315  /  315.209 Solicitation provisions and contract clauses.

(a) The Contracting Officer shall insert paragraph (e) in 352.215-1 in place of paragraph (e) in the provision in FAR 52.215-1, Instructions to Offerors--Competitive Acquisition, in solicitations for competitive, negotiated acquisitions valued at more than the simplified acquisition threshold. This is an authorized FAR deviation.
