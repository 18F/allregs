CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 932  /  Sec. 932.159 Reallocation of handler membership.

Pursuant to Sec. 932.25, handler representation on the Committee is reallocated to provide that the two handlers who handled the largest and second largest total volume of olives during the crop year in which nominations are made and in the preceding crop year shall each be represented by four members and four alternate members. [65 FR 62994, Oct. 20, 2000]
