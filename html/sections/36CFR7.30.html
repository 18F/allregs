CFR /  Title 36  /  Part 7  /  Sec. 7.30 Devils Tower National Monument.

(a) Climbing. Registration with a park ranger is required prior to any climbing above the talus slopes on Devils Tower. The registrant is also required to sign in immediately upon completion of a climb in a manner specified by the registering ranger. [42 FR 20462, Apr. 20, 1977]
