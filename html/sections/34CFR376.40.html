CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 376  /  Sec. 376.40 What are the matching requirements?

The Secretary may pay all or part of the costs of activities funded under this program. If part of the costs is to be paid by a grantee, the amount of grantee participation is specified in the application notice and will not be more than 10 percent of the total cost of the project. (Authority: Secs. 12(c) and 311(b); 29 U.S.C. 711(c) and 777a(b)) [59 FR 8340, Feb. 18, 1994]
