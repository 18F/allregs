CFR /  Title 17  /  Part 274  /  Sec. 274.221 Form N-23c-3, Notification of repurchase offer.

Form N-23c-3 shall be filed with copies of notifications of repurchase offers submitted to the Commission as required under rule 23c-3 (Sec. 270.23c-3 of this chapter). [58 FR 19345, Apr. 14, 1993]

Editorial Note: For Federal Register citations affecting Form N-23c-3, see the List of CFR Sections Affected, which appears in the Finding Aids section of the printed volume and at www.fdsys.gov.
