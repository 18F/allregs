CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 1940  /  Sec. 1940.592 Community facilities grants.

(a).

(b).

(1) The criteria used in the basic formula are:

(i) State's percentage of National rural population--50 percent.

(ii) State's percentage of National rural population with income below the poverty level--50 percent.

(2) Data source for each of these criterion is based on the latest census data available. Each criterion is assigned a specific weight according to its relevance in determining need. The percentage representing each criterion is multiplied by the weight factor and summed to arrive at a State factor (SF). SF (criterion (b)(1)(i) x 50 percent) + (criterion (b)(1)(ii) x 50 percent)

(c). States receiving administrative allocations do not receive formula allocations.

(d) Transition formula. The transition formula for Community Facilities Grants is not used.

(e). States receiving administrative allocations do not receive base allocations.

(f). States participating in the formula base allocation procedures do not receive administrative allocations.

(g).

(h). Funds will be pooled at midyear and yearend. Pooled funds will be placed in the National Office reserve and will be made available administratively.

(j).

(k) Other documentation. Not applicable. [62 FR 16468, Apr. 7, 1997]
