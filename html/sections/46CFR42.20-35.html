CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 42  /  Sec. 42.20-35 Correction for position of deck line.

(a) Where the actual depth to the upper edge of the deck line is greater or less than D, the difference between the depths shall be added to or deducted from the freeboard. [CGFR 68-60, 33 FR 10065, July 12, 1968]
