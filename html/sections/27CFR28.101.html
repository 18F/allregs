CFR /  Title 27  /  Part 28  /  Sec. 28.101 Packages to be stamped.

Each package and authorized bulk conveyance of spirits (including tank cars and tank trucks but not pipelines) withdrawn without payment of tax under the provisions of this subpart shall be marked with the word ``EXPORT'' in accordance with the provisions of 27 CFR part 19 prior to its removal from the bonded premises. (Approved by the Office of Management and Budget under control number 1512-0189) (Sec. 201, Pub. L. 85-859, 72 Stat. 1358, as amended (26 U.S.C. 5205)) [T.D. ATF-198, 50 FR 8560, Mar. 1, 1985; as amended by T.D. ATF-206, 50 FR 23956, June 7, 1985]]
