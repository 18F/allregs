CFR /  Title 19  /  Part 159  /  Sec. 159.44 Special duties on merchandise imported under agreements in

Whenever it appears that imported articles may be subject to the special duties provided for in section 802, Act of September 8, 1916 (15 U.S.C. 73), the port director shall report the matter to the Commissioner of Customs and await instructions with respect to the imposition of such duties.
