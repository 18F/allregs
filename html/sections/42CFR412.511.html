CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 412  /  Sec. 412.511 Reporting and recordkeeping requirements.

A long-term care hospital participating in the prospective payment system under this subpart must meet the requirement of Sec. Sec. 412.22(e)(3) and 412.22(h)(6) to report co-located status, if applicable, and the recordkeeping and cost reporting requirements of Sec. Sec. 413.20 and 413.24 of this subchapter. [71 FR 48140, Aug. 18, 2006]
