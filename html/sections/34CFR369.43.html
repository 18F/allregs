CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 369  /  Sec. 369.43 What are the affirmative action plan requirements affecting

A recipient of Federal assistance must develop and implement an affirmative action plan to employ and advance in employment qualified individuals with disabilities. This plan must provide for specific action steps, timetables, and complaint and enforcement procedures necessary to assure affirmative action. (Authority: Sec. 12(c) of the Act; 29 U.S.C. 711(c)) [46 FR 5417, Jan. 19, 1981, as amended at 53 FR 17144, May 13, 1988; 59 FR 8335, Feb. 18, 1994]
