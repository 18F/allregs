CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 375  /  Sec. 375.105 What are the information collection requirements of

(a) The information collection requirements of this part have been reviewed by the Office of Management and Budget pursuant to the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995 (44 U.S.C. 3501 et seq.) and have been assigned OMB control number 2126-0025.

(b) The information collection requirements are found in the following sections: Sec. Sec. 375.205, 375.207, 375.209, 375.211, 375.213, 375.215, 375.217, 375.303, 375.401, 375.403, 375.405, 375.409, 375.501, 375.503, 375.505, 375.507, 375.515, 375.519, 375.521, 375.605, 375.607, 375.609, 375.803, 375.805, and 375.807. [69 FR 10575, Mar. 5, 2004, as amended at 78 FR 58479, Sept. 24, 2013]
