CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 4  /  Sec. 4.7 Congressional subpoenas directed to the Department or

Notwithstanding the provisions of Sec. Sec. 4.1, 4.2, and 4.3, service of Congressional subpoenas shall be delivered to the staff in the Office of the Assistant Secretary for Legislation, Department of Health and Human Services, 200 Independence Avenue, SW., Washington, DC 20201. [73 FR 48151, Aug. 18, 2008]
