CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 64  /  Sec. 64.1605 Effective date.

The provisions of Sec. Sec. 64.1600 and 64.1602 are effective April 12, 1995. The provisions of Sec. Sec. 64.1601 and 64.1603 are effective December 1, 1995, except Sec. Sec. 64.1601 and 64.1603 do not apply to public payphones and partylines until January 1, 1997. [60 FR 29491, June 5, 1995; 60 FR 54449, Oct. 24, 1995.
