CFR /  Title 19  /  Part 206  /  Sec. 206.41 Applicability of subpart.

This subpart E applies specifically to investigations under section 406(a) or 421(b) or (o) of the Trade Act. For other applicable rules, see subpart A of this part and part 201 of this chapter. [59 FR 5091, Feb. 3, 1994, as amended at 67 FR 8190, Feb. 22, 2002]
