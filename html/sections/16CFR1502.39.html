CFR /  Title 16  /  Part 1502  /  Sec. 1502.39 Examination of record.

Except as provided in Sec. 1502.3, documents in the record will be publicly available. Documents available for examination or copying will be placed on public display in the Office of the Secretary promptly upon receipt in that office.
