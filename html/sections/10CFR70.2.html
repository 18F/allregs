CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 70  /  Sec. 70.2 Scope.

Except as provided in Sec. Sec. 70.11 to 70.13, inclusive, the regulations in this part apply to all persons in the United States. This part also gives notice to all persons who knowingly provide to any licensee, applicant, contractor, or subcontractor, components, equipment, materials, or other goods or services, that relate to a licensee's or applicant's activities subject to this part, that they may be individually subject to NRC enforcement action for violation of Sec. 70.10. [63 FR 1898, Jan. 13, 1998]
