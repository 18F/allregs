CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 302-9  /  Sec. 302-9.12 May I receive an advance of funds for transportation and

Yes, you may receive advance funds in accordance with Sec. 302-2.23 of this chapter and not to exceed the estimated amount of the expenses authorized under this part for transportation and emergency or temporary storage of your POV.
