CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 630  /  Sec. 630.408 Records on the use of sick leave.

An agency must maintain records of the amount of sick leave used by an employee for family care purposes and to make arrangements for or attend the funeral of a family member under Sec. 630.401(a)(3) and (4). The records must be sufficient to ensure that an employee does not exceed the limitations in Sec. 630.401(b) and (c). [71 FR 47695, Aug. 17, 2006. Redesignated at 75 FR 75373, Dec. 3, 2010]
