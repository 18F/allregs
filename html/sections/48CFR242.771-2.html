CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 242  /  242.771-2 Policy.

Defense contractors are encouraged to engage in independent research and development and bid and proposal (IR&D;/B&P;) activities of potential interest to DoD, including activities cited in 231.205-18(c)(iii)(B). [64 FR 8730, Feb. 23, 1999]
