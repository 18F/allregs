CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 2  /  Sec. 2.200 Principal Deputy General Counsel.

Pursuant to Sec. 2.31, the following delegation of authority is made by the General Counsel to the Principal Deputy General Counsel, to be exercised only during the absence or unavailability of the General Counsel: Perform all duties and exercise all powers that are now or which may hereafter be delegated to the General Counsel. [78 FR 40940, July 9, 2013]
