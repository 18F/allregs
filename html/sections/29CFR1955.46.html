CFR /  Title 29  /  Part 1955  /  Sec. 1955.46 Finality for purposes of judicial review.

Only a final decision by the Secretary under Sec. 1955.44 shall be deemed final agency action for purposes of judicial review. A decision of an administrative law judge which becomes final for lack of appeal is not deemed final agency action for purposes of 5 U.S.C. 704.
