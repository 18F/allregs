CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 875  /  Sec. 875.403 May I apply for coverage outside of an open season?

If you are eligible for coverage, you may submit an application at any time outside of an open season. You will be subject to full underwriting requirements. The only exceptions to the full underwriting requirements outside of an open season are described in Sec. 875.206 and Sec. 875.405. [68 FR 5534, Feb. 4, 2003, as amended at 70 FR 30607, May 27, 2005]
