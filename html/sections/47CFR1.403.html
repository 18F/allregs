CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 1  /  Sec. 1.403 Notice and availability.

All petitions for rule making (other than petitions to amend the FM, Television, and Air-Ground Tables of Assignments) meeting the requirements of Sec. 1.401 will be given a file number and, promptly thereafter, a ``Public Notice'' will be issued (by means of a Commission release entitled ``Petitions for Rule Making Filed'') as to the petition, file number, nature of the proposal, and date of filing, Petitions for rule making are available at the Commission's Reference Information Center, 445 12th Street, SW, Washington, DC and may also be available electronically over the Internet at http://www.fcc.gov/. [67 FR 13223, Mar. 21, 2002]
