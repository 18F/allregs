CFR /  Title 15  /  Part 0  /  Sec. 0.735-34 Effective date of supplementary regulations.

Supplementary regulations prescribed pursuant to Sec. 0.735-33, shall become effective upon approval by the issuing officer unless a different date is required by law or a later date is specified therein.
