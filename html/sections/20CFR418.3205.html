CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 418  /  Sec. 418.3205 What makes an application a claim for a subsidy?

We will consider your application a claim for the subsidy if:

(a) You, or someone acting on your behalf as described in Sec. 418.3215, complete an application on a form prescribed by us;

(b) You, or someone acting on your behalf as described in Sec. 418.3215, file the application with us pursuant to Sec. 418.3220; and

(c) You are alive on the first day of the month in which the application is filed.
