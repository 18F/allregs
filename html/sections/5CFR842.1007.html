CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 842  /  Sec. 842.1007 Review of decisions.

(a) The final decision of the agency head denying an individual's request for approval of a position as a primary or secondary customs and border protection officer position made under Sec. 842.1003(a) may be appealed to the Merit Systems Protection Board under procedures prescribed by the Board.

(b) The final decision of the agency head denying an individual coverage while serving in an approved secondary position because of failure to meet the conditions in Sec. 842.1003(b) may be appealed to the Merit Systems Protection Board under procedures prescribed by the Board.
