CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 190  /  Sec. 190.241 Finality.

Except as otherwise provided by Sec. 190.243, an order directing amendment issued under Sec. 190.206, a final order issued under Sec. 190.213, a corrective action order issued under Sec. 190.233, or a safety order issued under Sec. 190.239 is considered final administrative action on that enforcement proceeding. [Amdt. 190-16, 78 FR 58913, Sept. 25, 2013]
