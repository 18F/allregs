CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 1980  /  Sec. 1980.434 Equal opportunity and nondiscrimination requirements.

(See subpart A Sec. 1980.41.)

Administrative

The State Director will assure that equal opportunity and nondiscrimination requirements are met. If there is indication of noncompliance with these requirements, such facts will be reported by the Compliance Reviewing Officer or FmHA or its successor agency under Public Law 103-354 Official in writing to the Administrator, ATTN: Equal Opportunity Officer.
