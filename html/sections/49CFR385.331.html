CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 385  /  Sec. 385.331 What happens if a new entrant operates a CMV after having

A new entrant that operates a CMV in violation of an out-of-service order is subject to the penalty provisions in 49 U.S.C. 521(b)(2)(A) for each offense as adjusted for inflation by 49 CFR part 386, appendix B. [73 FR 76491, Dec. 16, 2008]
