CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 84  /  Sec. 84.122 Breathing resistance test; minimum requirements.

(a) Resistance to airflow will be measured in the facepiece or mouthpiece of a gas mask mounted on a breathing machine both before and after each test conducted in accordance with Sec. Sec. 84.124, 84.125, and 84.126, with air flowing at a continuous rate of 85 liters per minute.
