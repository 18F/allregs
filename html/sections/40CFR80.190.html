CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 80  /  Sec. 80.190 Who must register with EPA under the sulfur program?

(a) Refiners and importers who are registered by EPA under Sec. 80.76 are deemed to be registered for purposes of this subpart.

(b) Refiners and importers subject to the standards in Sec. 80.195 who are not registered by EPA under Sec. 80.76 must provide to EPA the information required by Sec. 80.76 by November 1, 2003, or not later than three months in advance of the first date that such person produces or imports gasoline, whichever is later.

(c) Refiners with any refinery subject to the small refiner standards under Sec. 80.240, or refiners subject to the geographic phase-in area (GPA) standards under Sec. 80.216, who are not registered by EPA under Sec. 80.76 must provide to EPA the information required under Sec. 80.76 by December 31, 2000.

(d) Any refiner who plans to generate credits or allotments under Sec. 80.305 or Sec. 80.275 in any year prior to 2004 who is not registered by EPA under Sec. 80.76 must register under Sec. 80.76 no later than September 30 of the year prior to the first year of credit generation. Any refiner who plans to generate credits in 2000 who is not registered by EPA under Sec. 80.76 must register under Sec. 80.76 no later than May 10, 2000.
