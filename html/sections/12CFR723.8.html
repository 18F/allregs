CFR /  Title 12  /  Part 723  /  Sec. 723.8 How much may one member or a group of associated members

Unless your Regional Director grants a waiver for a higher amount, the aggregate amount of net member business loan balances to any one member or group of associated members must not exceed the greater of:

(a) 15% of the credit union's net worth; or

(b) $100,000. [68 FR 56552, Oct. 1, 2003] Sec. 723.9 [Reserved]
