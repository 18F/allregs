CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 418  /  Sec. 418.1345 Is reopening of an initial or reconsidered determination

We may reopen an initial or reconsidered determination made by us when the conditions for reopening are met as described in Sec. 404.988 of this chapter. We will use the rules in Secs. 404.987 through 404.991a of this chapter when we reopen determinations made by us.
