CFR /  Title 16  /  Part 1018  /  Sec. 1018.43 Comprehensive review.

A comprehensive review of all Commission established or utilized advisory committees shall be made annually in accordance with section 10 of the GSA Circular No. A-63, as amended, and shall be submitted to the GSA Secretariat by November 30 of each year. [41 FR 45882, Oct. 18, 1976, as amended at 46 FR 63249, Dec. 31, 1981]
