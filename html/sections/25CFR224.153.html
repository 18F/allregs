CFR /  Title 25  /  Part 224  /  Sec. 224.153 Must the Secretary notify the tribe of an intent to reassume the authority granted?

If the Secretary determines under Sec. 224.152 that reassumption is necessary to protect the physical trust asset, the Secretary will issue a written notice to the tribal governing body of the Secretary's intent to reassume.
