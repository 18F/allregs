CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 61  /  Sec. 61.68 Special notations.

(a) Any tariff filing made pursuant to an Application for Special Permission, Commission decision or order must contain the following statement:

Issued under authority of (specific reference to the special permission, Commission decision, or order) of the Commission.

(b) When a portion of any tariff publication is issued in order to comply with the Commission order, the following notation must be associated with that portion of the tariff publication:

In compliance with the order of the Federal Communications Commission in -- (a specific citation to the applicable order should be made). [49 FR 40869, Oct. 18, 1984, as amended at 76 FR 43216, July 20, 2011]
