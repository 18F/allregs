CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 295  /  Sec. 295.2 Organizational description.

The description of the central and field organization of FNS is published as a notice in the Federal Register and may be revised from time to time in like manner. Such description contains a listing of FNS headquarters and field organizational units and their functions.
