CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 62  /  Sec. 62.11651 Identification of sources.

The affected facility to which the plan applies is each sewage sludge incineration unit within the Commonwealth of Virginia that commenced construction on or before October 14, 2010. [79 FR 17888, Mar. 31, 2014]
