CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 384  /  Sec. 384.220 Problem Driver Pointer System information.

Before issuing a CLP or CDL to any person, the State must, within the period of time specified in Sec. 384.232, perform the check of the Problem Driver Pointer System in accordance with Sec. 383.73(b)(3)(iii) of this subchapter, and, based on that information, promptly implement the disqualifications, licensing limitations, and/or penalties that are called for in any applicable section(s) of this subpart. [76 FR 26895, May 9, 2011]
