CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 350  /  Sec. 350.65 What level of participation is required of trainees in

Individuals who are receiving training under an Advanced Rehabilitation Research Training Project shall devote at least eighty percent of their time to the activities of the training program during the training period. (Authority: Secs. 202-204; 29 U.S.C. 760-762)
