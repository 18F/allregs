CFR /  Title 17  /  Part 145  /  Sec. 145.8 Fees for records services.

A schedule of fees for record services, including locating, and making records available, and copying, appears in appendix B to this part 145. Copies of the schedule of fees may also be obtained upon request made in person, by telephone or by mail from the FOI, Privacy and Sunshine Acts compliance staff, Office of the Secretariat or at any regional office of the Commission. (7 U.S.C. 4a(j) and 16a as amended by Pub. L. 97-444, 96 Stat. 2294 (1983), and 5 U.S.C. 552, 552a and 552b) [41 FR 16290, Apr. 16, 1976, as amended at 49 FR 12684, Mar. 30, 1984]
