CFR /  Title 25  /  Part 533  /  Sec. 533.1 Requirement for review and approval.

Subject to the Chairman's approval, an Indian tribe may enter into a management contract for the operation of a class II or class III gaming activity.

(a) Such contract shall become effective upon approval by the Chairman.

(b) Contract approval shall be evidenced by a Commission document dated and signed by the Chairman. No other means of approval shall be valid. [58 FR 5829, Jan. 22, 1993, as amended at 74 FR 36935, July 27, 2009]
