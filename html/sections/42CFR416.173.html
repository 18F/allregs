CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 416  /  Sec. 416.173 Publication of revised payment methodologies and

CMS publishes annually, through notice and comment rulemaking in the Federal Register and/or via the Internet on the CMS Web site, the payment methodologies and payment rates for ASC services and designates the covered surgical procedures and covered ancillary services for which CMS will make an ASC payment and other revisions as appropriate. [76 FR 74582, Nov. 30, 2011]
