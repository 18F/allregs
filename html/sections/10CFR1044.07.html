CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 1044  /  Sec. 1044.07 How can you find out if a particular person is authorized

You must contact the Department of Energy Inspector General for help in determining whether a particular person is authorized to receive the classified or unclassified controlled nuclear information you wish to disclose. The Inspector General will contact the Office of Personnel Security as necessary to determine the security access authorization of the person to receive the protected disclosure. [66 FR 4642, Jan. 18, 2001, as amended at 71 FR 68736, Nov. 28, 2006]
