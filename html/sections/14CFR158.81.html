CFR /  Title 14  /  Part 158  /  Sec. 158.81 General.

This subpart contains the procedures for termination of PFCs or loss of Federal airport grant funds for violations of this part or 49 U.S.C. 40117. This subpart does not address the circumstances under which the authority to collect PFCs may be terminated for violations of 49 U.S.C. 47523 through 47528. [Doc. No. FAA-2006-23730, 72 FR 28851, May 23, 2007]
