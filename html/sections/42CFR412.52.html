CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 412  /  Sec. 412.52 Reporting and recordkeeping requirements.

All hospitals participating in the prospective payment systems must meet the recordkeeping and cost reporting requirements of Sec. Sec. 413.20 and 413.24 of this chapter.
