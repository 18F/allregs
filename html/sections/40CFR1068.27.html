CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 1068  /  Sec. 1068.27 May EPA conduct testing with my production engines/equipment?

If we request it, you must make a reasonable number of production- line engines or pieces of production-line equipment available for a reasonable time so we can test or inspect them for compliance with the requirements of this chapter.
