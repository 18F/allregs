CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 49  /  Sec. 49.10411 Permits for general open burning, agricultural burning,

(a) Beginning June 7, 2005, a person must apply for and obtain a permit under Sec. 49.132 Rule for general open burning permits.

(b) Beginning June 7, 2005, a person must apply for and obtain approval of a permit under Sec. 49.133 Rule for agricultural burning permits.

(c) Beginning June 7, 2005, a person must apply for and obtain approval of a permit under Sec. 49.134 Rule for forestry and silvicultural burning permits. Secs. 49.10412-49.10430 [Reserved]
