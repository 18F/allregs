CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 300  /  Sec. 300.193 Request to show cause.

An SEA, LEA or other public agency in receipt of a notice under Sec. 300.192 that seeks an opportunity to show cause why a by-pass should not be implemented must submit a written request for a show cause hearing to the Secretary, within the specified time period in the written notice in Sec. 300.192(b)(2). (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1412(f)(3))
