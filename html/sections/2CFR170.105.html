CFR /  Title 2  /  Part 170  /  Sec. 170.105 Types of awards to which this part applies.

This part applies to an agency's grants, cooperative agreements, loans, and other forms of Federal financial assistance subject to the Transparency Act, as defined in Sec. 170.320.
