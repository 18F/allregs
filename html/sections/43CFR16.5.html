CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 16  /  Sec. 16.5 Bonds.

The applicant shall be required to submit a bond in such amount and in such form as the Secretary may prescribe to secure the faithful performance of the terms of any agreement made. [29 FR 9383, July 9, 1964. Redesignated at 30 FR 9218, July 23, 1965]
