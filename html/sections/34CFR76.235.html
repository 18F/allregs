CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 76  /  Sec. 76.235 The notification of grant award.

(a) To make a grant to a State, the Secretary issues and sends to the State a notification of grant award.

(b) The notification of grant award tells the amount of the grant and provides other information about the grant. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1221e-3 and 3474)
