CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 199  /  Sec. 199.233 Removal from covered function.

Except as provided in Secs. 199.239 through 199.243, no operator shall permit any covered employee to perform covered functions if the employee has engaged in conduct prohibited by Secs. 199.215 through 199.223 or an alcohol misuse rule of another DOT agency.
