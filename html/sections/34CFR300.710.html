CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 300  /  Sec. 300.710 Use of funds under Part B of the Act.

(a) The Secretary of the Interior may reserve five percent of its payment under Sec. 300.707(b) in any fiscal year, or $500,000, whichever is greater, for administrative costs in carrying out the provisions of Secs. 300.707 through 300.709, 300.711, and 300.713 through 300.716.

(b) Payments to the Secretary of the Interior under Sec. 300.712 must be used in accordance with that section. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1411(h)(1)(A))
