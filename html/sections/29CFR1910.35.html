CFR /  Title 29  /  Part 1910  /  Sec. 1910.35 Compliance with alternate exit-route codes.

OSHA will deem an employer demonstrating compliance with the exit-route provisions of NFPA 101, Life Safety Code, 2009 edition, or the exit-route provisions of the International Fire Code, 2009 edition, to be in compliance with the corresponding requirements in Secs. 1910.34, 1910.36, and 1910.37 (incorporated by reference, see section Sec. 1910.6). [76 FR 33606, June 8, 2011]
