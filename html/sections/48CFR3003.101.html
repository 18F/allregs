CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 3003  /  Sec. 3003.101 Standards of conduct. Sec. 3003.101-3 Agency regulations.

The United States Office of Government Ethics has promulgated regulations applicable to the entire Executive Branch that address the conduct matters referenced in (FAR) 48 CFR 3.101-3. See 5 CFR vol. 3, ch. XVI, subch. B. The Department of Homeland Security has also issued Management Directive 0480.1, Ethics/Standards of Conduct. [77 FR 50633, Aug. 22, 2012]
