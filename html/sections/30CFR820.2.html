CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 820  /  Sec. 820.2 Objective.

This part implements subsection 529(a) of the Act, which requires the Secretary to adopt special performance standards for anthracite mines regulated by special environmental protection performance standards of a State as of the date of enactment of the Act. [44 FR 15449, Mar. 13, 1979]
