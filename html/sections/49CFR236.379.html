CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 236  /  Sec. 236.379 Route locking.

Route locking or other type of switch locking shall be tested when placed in service and thereafter when modified, disarranged, or at least once every two years, whichever shall occur first. [49 FR 3385, Jan. 26, 1984]
