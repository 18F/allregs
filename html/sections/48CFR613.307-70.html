CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 613  /  613.307-70 File folders for purchase orders, delivery orders, blanket

Contracting officers shall use Forms DS-1918, Purchase Order File; DS-1919, Delivery Order File; DS-1920, Blanket Purchase Agreement (BPA) File; and DS-3014, Purchase Card Transaction File (Actions Exceeding $3,000 Through $25,000), to record relevant data and document those acquisitions, respectively. [80 FR 6920, Feb. 20, 2015]
