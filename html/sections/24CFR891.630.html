CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 891  /  Sec. 891.630 Denial of admission, termination of tenancy, and

(a) The provisions of part 5, subpart I, of this title apply to Section 202 direct loan projects.

(b) The provisions of part 247 of this title apply to all decisions by a Borrower to terminate the tenancy or modify the lease of a family residing in a unit.

(c) In actions or potential actions to terminate tenancy, the Owner shall follow 24 CFR part 5, subpart L, in all cases where domestic violence, dating violence, stalking, or criminal activity directly related to domestic violence, dating violence, or stalking is involved or claimed to be involved. [66 FR 28798, May 24, 2001, as amended at 73 FR 72343, Nov. 28, 2008; 75 FR 66262, Oct. 27, 2010]
