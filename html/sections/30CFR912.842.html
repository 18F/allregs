CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 912  /  Sec. 912.842 Federal inspections.

(a) Part 842 of this chapter, Federal Inspections, shall apply to all exploration and surface coal mining and reclamation operations.

(b) The Secretary will furnish a copy of each inspection report regarding inspections conducted pursuant to this section to the Office of the Idaho Attornery General upon request.
