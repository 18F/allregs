CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 2530  /  Sec. 2530.60 What is the impact of transferring or receiving a additional education awards?

(a) Impact on Transferring Individual. Pursuant to Sec. 2526.50, an award is considered to be received at the time it becomes available for an individual's use. Transferring all or part of an award does not reduce the aggregate value of education awards the transferring individual is considered to have received.

(b) Impact on Designated Individual. For the purposes of determining the value of the transferred education award under Sec. 2526.50, a designated individual will be considered to have received a value equal to the amount accepted divided by the amount of a full-time award in the year the transferring individual's position was approved.

(c) Result of revocation on award value. If the transferring individual revokes, in whole or in part, a transfer, the value of the education award considered to have been received by the designated individual for purposes of Sec. 2526.50 will be reduced accordingly.
