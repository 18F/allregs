CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 158  /  Sec. 158.250 Standard discharge connection.

Each reception facility that received bilge water containing oily mixtures must have a standard discharge connection that--

(a) Meets Sec. 155.430 of this subchapter; and

(b) Attaches to each hose or pipe that removes bilge water containing oily mixtures from oceangoing ships. [CGD 78-035, 50 FR 36793, Sept. 9, 1985, as amended by USCG-2000-7641, 66 FR 55574, Nov. 2, 2001] Subpart C_Criteria for Certifying That a Port's or Terminal's Facilities

Are Adequate for Receiving NLS Residue

Source: CGD 85-010, 52 FR 7764, Mar. 12, 1987, unless otherwise noted.
