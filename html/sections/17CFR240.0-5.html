CFR /  Title 17  /  Part 240  /  Sec. 240.0-5 Reference to rule by obsolete designation.

Wherever in any rule, form, or instruction book specific reference is made to a rule by number or other designation which is now obsolete, such reference shall be deemed to be made to the corresponding rule or rules in the existing general rules and regulations. [13 FR 8179, Dec. 22, 1948]
