CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 493  /  Sec. 493.1351 General.

This subpart consists of the personnel requirements that must be met by laboratories performing moderate complexity testing, PPM procedures, high complexity testing, or any combination of these tests. [60 FR 20049, Apr. 24, 1995]
