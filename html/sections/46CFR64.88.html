CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 64  /  Sec. 64.88 Plan approval, construction, and inspection of

Plans for the cargo-handling system of a portable tank authorized under subpart 98.30 of this chapter must be approved by the Coast Guard in accordance with the requirements of Sec. 56.01-10 of this subchapter. In addition, the cargo-handling system must be constructed and inspected in accordance with part 56 of this subchapter. [CGD 84-043, 55 FR 37410, Sept. 11, 1990]
