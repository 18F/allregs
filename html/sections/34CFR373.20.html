CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 373  /  Sec. 373.20 What are the matching requirements?

The Secretary may make grants to pay all or part of the cost of activities covered under this program. If the Secretary determines that the grantee is required to pay part of the costs, the amount of grantee participation is specified in the application notice, and the Secretary will not require grantee participation to be more than 10 percent of the total cost of the project. (Authority: 29 U.S.C. 711(c) and 723(a))
