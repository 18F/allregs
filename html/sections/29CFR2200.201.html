CFR /  Title 29  /  Part 2200  /  Sec. 2200.201 Application.

The rules in this subpart will govern proceedings before a Judge in a case chosen for Simplified Proceedings under Sec. 2200.203. [60 FR 41809, Aug. 14, 1995, as amended at 62 FR 14822, Mar. 28, 1997; 62 FR 40934, July 31, 1997; 70 FR 22792, May 3, 2005]
