CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 102-37  /  Sec. 102-37.350 Does coverage under the single audit process in

No, although SASPs are covered under the single audit process in OMB Circular A-133, from time to time the Government Accountability Office (GAO), GSA, or other authorized Federal activities may audit or review the operations of a SASP. GSA will notify the chief executive officer of the State of the reasons for a GSA audit. When requested, you must make available financial records and all other records of the SASP for inspection by representatives of GSA, GAO, or other authorized Federal activities. [67 FR 2584, Jan. 18, 2002, as amended at 71 FR 23868, Apr. 25, 2006]
