CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 3  /  3.807 Civil penalties.

Agencies shall impose and collect civil penalties pursuant to the provisions of the Program Fraud and Civil Remedies Act, 31 U.S.C. 3803 (except subsection (c)), 3804-3808, and 3812, insofar as the provisions therein are not inconsistent with the requirements of this subpart. [55 FR 3190, Jan. 30, 1990, as amended at 67 FR 6120, Feb. 8, 2002]
