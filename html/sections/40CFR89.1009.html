CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 89  /  Sec. 89.1009 What special provisions apply to branded engines?

A manufacturer identifying the name and trademark of another company on the emission control information label, as provided by Sec. 89.110(b)(2), must comply with the provisions of 40 CFR 1039.640. [70 FR 40448, July 13, 2005]
