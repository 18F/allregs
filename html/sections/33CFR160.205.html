CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 160  /  Sec. 160.205 Notices of arrival.

The owner, agent, Master, operator, or person in charge of a vessel must submit notices of arrival consistent with the requirements in this subpart. [USCG-2005-21869, 80 FR 5331, Jan. 30, 2015]

Effective Date Note: By USCG-2005-21869, 80 FR 5331, Jan. 30, 2015, Sec. 160.205 was added. This section contains information collection and recordkeeping requirements and will not become effective until approval has been given by the Office of Management and Budget.
