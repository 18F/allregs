CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 128  /  Sec. 128.311 What is my right of appeal?

Any person directly affected by a decision or action taken by the COTP under this part, may appeal that action or decision to the cognizant District Commander according to the procedures in 46 CFR 1.03-15; the District Commander's decision on appeal may be further appealed to the Commandant according to the procedures in 46 CFR 1.03-25. [CGD 91-012, 61 FR 37654, July 18, 1996. Redesignated by CGD 91-012, 63 FR 53592, Oct. 6, 1998]
