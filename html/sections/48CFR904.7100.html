CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 904  /  904.7100 Scope of subpart.

This subpart implements section 836 of the Fiscal Year 1993 Defense Authorization Act (Pub. L. 102-484) which prohibits the award of a Department of Energy contract under the national security program to an entity controlled by a foreign government if it is necessary for that entity to be given access to information in a proscribed category of information in order to perform the contract. [58 FR 59684, Nov. 10, 1993, as amended at 67 FR 14876, Mar. 28, 2002]
