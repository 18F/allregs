CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 1000  /  Sec. 1000.60 Can HUD prevent improper expenditure of funds already

Yes. In accordance with the standards and remedies contained in Sec. 1000.532 relating to substantial noncompliance, HUD will use its powers under a depository agreement and take such other actions as may be legally necessary to suspend funds disbursed to the recipient until the substantial noncompliance has been remedied. In taking this action, HUD shall comply with all appropriate procedures, appeals, and hearing rights prescribed elsewhere in this part. [77 FR 71524, Dec. 3, 2012]
