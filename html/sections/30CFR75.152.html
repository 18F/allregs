CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 75  /  Sec. 75.152 Tests of air flow; qualified person.

A person is a qualified person within the meaning of the provisions of Subpart D--Ventilation of this part requiring that tests of air flow be made by a qualified person only if he is a certified person under Sec. 75.100 or a person trained and designated by a certified person to perform such tests.
