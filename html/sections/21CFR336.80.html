CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 336  /  Sec. 336.80 Professional labeling.

The labeling provided to health professionals (but not to the general public) may contain the following additional indications.

(a), (b), and (c). ``For the treatment of vertigo of motion sickness.''

(b) For products containing meclizine hydrochloride identified in Sec. 336.10(d). ``For the treatment of vertigo.''
