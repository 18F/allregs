CFR /  Title 12  /  Part 619  /  Sec. 619.9146 Farm Credit institutions.

Except as otherwise defined, the term Farm Credit institutions refers to all institutions chartered and regulated by the Farm Credit Administration as described in section 1.2 of the Act, and to the Funding Corporation. [55 FR 24889, June 19, 1990, as amended at 56 FR 2675, Jan. 24, 1991]
