CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 1840  /  Sec. 1840.1 Service of subpoenas by mail.

In addition to all other methods of authorized service, an Office of Special Counsel subpoena may be served by mailing a copy to the person at his or her residence or place of business by certified or registered mail. [54 FR 47345, Nov. 14, 1989]
