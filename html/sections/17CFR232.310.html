CFR /  Title 17  /  Part 232  /  Sec. 232.310 Marking changed material.

Provisions requiring the marking of changed materials are satisfied in ASCII and HTML documents by inserting the tag before and the tag following a paragraph containing changed material. HTML documents may be marked to show changed materials within paragraphs. Financial statements and notes thereto need not be marked for changed material. [64 FR 27896, May 21, 1999]
