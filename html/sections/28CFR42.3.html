CFR /  Title 28  /  Part 42  /  Sec. 42.3 Responsibility for Department of Justice Equal Opportunity

The Assistant Attorney General for Administration shall be responsible for establishing and implementing the Department of Justice Equal Opportunity Recruitment Program under 5 U.S.C. 7201.
