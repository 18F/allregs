CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 182  /  Sec. 182.200 General.

(a) Propulsion machinery must be suitable in type and design for propulsion requirements of the hull in which it is installed and capable of operating at constant marine load under such requirements without exceeding its designed limitations.

(b) All engines must have at least two means for stopping the engine(s) under any operating conditions. The fuel oil shutoff required at the engine by Sec. 182.455(b)(4) will satisfy one means of stopping the engine.
