CFR /  Title 19  /  Part 12  /  Sec. 12.111 Registration.

All imported pesticides are required to be registered under the provisions of section 3 of the Act, and under the regulations (40 CFR 162.10) promulgated thereunder by the Administrator before being permitted entry into the United States. Devices, although not required to be registered, must not bear any statement, design, or graphic representation that is false or misleading in any particular. [T.D. 75-194, 40 FR 32321, Aug. 1, 1975]
