CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 117  /  Sec. 117.269 Biscayne Bay.

The Venetian Causeway Bridge (East) shall open on signal, except that from 7 a.m. to 7 p.m., Monday through Friday, except Federal holidays, the bridge need only open on the hour and half-hour. [CGD07-06-050, 72 FR 18886, Apr. 16, 2007]

Effective Date Note: By USCG-2014-0719, 80 FR 29946, May 26, 2015, Sec. 117.269 was suspended, effective 7 a.m. on May 26, 2015 to 7 p.m. on Feb. 28, 2016. Sec. 117.T269 Biscayne Bay.

The Venetian Causeway Bridge (East) shall remain closed to navigation. [USCG-2014-0719, 80 FR 29946, May 26, 2015]

Effective Date Note: By USCG-2014-0719, 80 FR 29946, May 26, 2015, Sec. 117.T269 was added, effective 7 a.m. on May 26, 2015 to 7 p.m. on Feb. 28, 2016.
