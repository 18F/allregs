CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 294  /  Sec. 294.111 Custody of records; subpoenas.

(a) The Chief, Plans and Policies Division, Administration Group, OPM, has official custody of OPM records. A subpoena or other judicial order for an official record from OPM should be served on the-- Chief, Plans and Policies Division, Office of Personnel Management, 1900 E Street NW., Washington, DC 20415

(b) See 5 CFR part 297, subpart D--Disclosure of Records, of this title, for the steps other officials should take on receipt of a subpoena or other judicial order for an Office record. [54 FR 25094, June 13, 1989, as amended at 57 FR 32150, July 21, 1992]
