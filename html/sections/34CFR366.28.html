CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 366  /  Sec. 366.28 Under what circumstances may the Secretary award a grant

(a) The Secretary may use funds from the allotment of one State to award a grant to a center located in a bordering State if the Secretary determines that the proposal of the out-of-State center to serve individuals with significant disabilities who reside in the bordering State is consistent with the State plan of the State in which these individuals reside.

(b) An applicant shall submit documentation demonstrating that the arrangements described in paragraph (a) of this section are consistent with the State plan of the State in which the individuals reside. (Approved by the Office of Management and Budget under control number 1820-0018) (Authority: 29 U.S.C. 711(c) and 796f (c) and (d))
