CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 4  /  Sec. 4.475 Service.

Service of notice or other documents required under this subpart shall be governed by Sec. Sec. 4.413 and 4.422. Proof of such service shall be filed in the same office where the notice or document was filed within 15 days after such service, unless filed with the notice or document. [44 FR 41790, July 18, 1979. Redesignated at 68 FR 68770, Dec. 10, 2003]
