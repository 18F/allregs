CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 25  /  Sec. 25.206 Station identification.

The requirement to transmit station identification is waived for all radio stations licensed under this part with the exception of earth stations subject to the requirements of Sec. 25.281. [79 FR 8322, Feb. 12, 2014]
