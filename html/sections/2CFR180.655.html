CFR /  Title 2  /  Part 180  /  Sec. 180.655 How will other Federal awarding agencies know about an

The suspending or debarring official who enters into an administrative agreement with you must report information about the agreement to the designated integrity and performance system within three business days after entering into the agreement. This information is required by section 872 of the Duncan Hunter National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2009 (41 U.S.C. 2313). [80 FR 43308, July 22, 2015]
