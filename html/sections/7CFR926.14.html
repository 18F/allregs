CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 926  /  Sec. 926.14 Broker.

Broker means any person who acts as an agent of the buyer or seller and negotiates the sale or purchase of cranberries or cranberry products.

Effective Date Note: At 71 FR 78046, Dec. 28, 2006, Sec. 926.14 was suspended indefinitely, effective Dec. 29, 2006.
