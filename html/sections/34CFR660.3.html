CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 660  /  Sec. 660.3 What regulations apply?

(a) The regulations in 34 CFR part 655.

(b) The regulations in this part 660. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1121-1125) [58 FR 32577, June 10, 1993]
