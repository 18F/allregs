CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 66  /  Sec. 66.101 Applicability.

The regulations in this subpart apply to National Research Service Awards made by the Secretary to individuals for research and training to undertake research, under section 487 of the Public Health Service Act, as amended (42 U.S.C. 288). [66 FR 29499, May 31, 2001]
