CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 160  /  Sec. 160.044-5 Marking.

(a) Each pump shall be permanently and legibly marked, in letters not less than \1/4\ inch high, either cast or stamped on the body, with the name of the manufacturer, the size for which approved (USCG No. 1, 2 or 3), and the Coast Guard approval number. The tee required by Sec. 160.044-3(f) shall be permanently and legibly marked with the word ``PRIME HERE''.
