CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 80  /  Sec. 80.840 What requirements apply to transmix processors?

Any transmix processor who produces gasoline or gasoline blendstock from transmix, or recovers gasoline or gasoline blendstock from transmix through transmix processing under Sec. 80.84 (c) shall include such gasoline or gasoline blendstock in the baseline and compliance calculations of this subpart to the same extent such gasoline or gasoline blendstock must be included in compliance calculations under subpart D of this part for reformulated gasoline and RBOB, and under subpart E of this part for conventional gasoline, according to the requirements specified in Sec. 80.84(c). [71 FR 31964, June 2, 2006]
