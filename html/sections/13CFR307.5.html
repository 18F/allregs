CFR /  Title 13  /  Part 307  /  Sec. 307.5 Application requirements.

(a) Each application for Economic Adjustment Assistance must:

(1) Include or incorporate by reference (if so approved by EDA) a CEDS, except that a CEDS is not required when applying for a Strategy Grant; and

(2) Explain how the proposed Project meets the criteria set forth in Sec. 307.2.

(b) For a technical assistance Project of significant Regional or national scope under this subpart, EDA may waive the requirement set forth in Sec. 301.2(b) of this chapter that the non-profit organization act in cooperation with officials of a political subdivision of a State.
