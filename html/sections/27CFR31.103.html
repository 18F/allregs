CFR /  Title 27  /  Part 31  /  Sec. 31.103 Formation of a partnership by two dealers.

Where two persons form a partnership after each has registered for a business carried on by himself, the partnership must register as a new dealer to cover the business conducted by the partnership. (26 U.S.C. 5121, 5122, 5124)
