CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 520  /  Sec. 520.816 Epsiprantel.

(a) Specifications. Each tablet contains either 12.5, 25, 50, or 100 milligrams of epsiprantel.

(b) Sponsor. See No. 050604 in Sec. 510.600(c) of this chapter.

(c) Conditions of use--

(1) Dogs--

(i) Amount. 2.5 milligrams per pound of body weight.

(ii) Indications for use. Removal of canine cestodes Dipylidium caninum and Taenia pisiformis.

(2) Cats--

(i) Amount. 1.25 milligrams per pound of body weight.

(ii) Indications for use. Removal of feline cestodes D. caninum and T. taeniaeformis.

(3) Limitations. Federal law restricts this drug to use by or on the order of a licensed veterinarian. [54 FR 50615, Dec. 8, 1989, as amended at 56 FR 50653, Oct. 8, 1991; 60 FR 55659, Nov. 2, 1995; 79 FR 28821, May 20, 2014]
