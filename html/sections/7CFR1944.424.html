CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 1944  /  Sec. 1944.424 Dwelling construction and standards.

All construction will be performed in accordance with subpart A of part 1924 of this chapter. The planned work must meet the building requirements of 7 CFR part 3550 and meet the Development Standards as defined in subpart A of part 1924 of this chapter and in any local codes. Sites and site developments must conform to the requirements of subpart C of part 1924 of this chapter. [55 FR 41833, Oct. 16, 1990, as amended at 67 FR 78328, Dec. 24, 2002]
