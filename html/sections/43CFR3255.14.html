CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 3255  /  Sec. 3255.14 How will BLM administer information concerning other

For information concerning Indian minerals not covered by Sec. 3255.13, BLM will withhold such records as may be withheld under an exemption to the FOIA when it receives a request for information related to tribal or Indian minerals held in trust or subject to restrictions on alienation.
