CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 84  /  Sec. 84.1149 Airflow resistance tests; all dust, fume, and mist

(a) Resistance to airflow will be measured in the facepiece, mouthpiece, hood, or helmet of a dust, fume, or mist respirator mounted on a test fixture with air flowing at a continuous rate of 85 liters per minute, both before and after each test conducted in accordance with Sec. Sec. 84.1144 through 84.1147.
