CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 436  /  Sec. 436.330 Coverage for certain aliens.

If an agency provides Medicaid to the medically needy, it must provide the services necessary for the treatment of an emergency medical condition, as defined in Sec. 440.255(c) of this chapter to those aliens described in Sec. 436.406(c) of this subpart. [55 FR 36820, Sept. 7, 1990]
