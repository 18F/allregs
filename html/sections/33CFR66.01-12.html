CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 66  /  Sec. 66.01-12 May I continue to use the private aid to navigation

If, after March 8, 2004, you modify, replace, or install any light that requires a new application as described in Sec. 66.01-5, you must comply with the rules in this part. [USCG-2000-7466, 68 FR 68239, Dec. 8, 2003]
