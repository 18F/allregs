CFR /  Title 19  /  Part 176  /  Sec. 176.22 Deletion of protest or entry number.

If any protest number or entry number is to be deleted from a schedule of protest numbers or entry numbers attached to or embodied in a statement of agreed facts, a line shall be drawn through the number and the change shall be initialed by the authorized official making and approving the deletion. [T.D. 70-181, 35 FR 13433, Aug. 22, 1970]
