CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 117  /  Sec. 117.165 Lindsey Slough.

The center drawspan of the Hastings Farms Highway Bridge, mile 2.0 between Egbert and Lower Hastings Tracts, must be removed for the passage of vessels if at least 72 hours notice is given to the Hastings Island Land Company office at Rio Vista. [USCG-2001-10881, 71 FR 70309, Dec. 4, 2006]
