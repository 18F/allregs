CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 102-118  /  Sec. 102-118.170 Will GSA continue to maintain a centralized numbering

Yes, GSA will maintain a numbering system for GBLs and GTRs. For commercial TDs, each agency must create a unique numbering system to account for and prevent duplicate numbers. The GSA Audit Division must approve this system. Write to: General Services Administration Transportation Audit Division (QMCA) Crystal Plaza 4, Room 300 2200 Crystal Drive Arlington, VA 22202 www.gsa.gov/transaudits [65 FR 24569, Apr. 26, 2000, as amended at 69 FR 57620, Sept. 24, 2004; 74 FR 30475, June 26, 2009]
