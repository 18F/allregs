CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 156  /  Sec. 156.100 Applicability.

This subpart applies to the transfer of oil or hazardous material on the navigable waters or contiguous zone of the United States to, from, or within each vessel with a capacity of 250 barrels or more; except that, this subpart does not apply to transfer operations within a public vessel. [CGD 86-034, 55 FR 36255, Sept. 4, 1990]
