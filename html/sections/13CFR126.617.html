CFR /  Title 13  /  Part 126  /  Sec. 126.617 Who decides contract disputes arising between a qualified

For purposes of the Disputes Clause of a specific HUBZone contract, the contracting activity will decide disputes arising between a qualified HUBZone SBC and the contracting activity. [69 FR 29426, May 24, 2004]
