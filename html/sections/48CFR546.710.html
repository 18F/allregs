CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 546  /  546.710 Contract clause.

The Contracting officer shall insert the clause at 552.246-77, Additional Contract Warranty Provisions for Supplies of a Noncomplex Nature, when using the clause at 52.246-17 in solicitations and contracts. [74 FR 26108, June 1, 2009]
