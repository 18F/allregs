CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 423  /  Sec. 423.6 Cost-sharing in beneficiary education and

The requirements of section 1857(e)(2) of the Act and Sec. 422.6 of this chapter with regard to the payment of fees established by CMS for cost sharing of enrollment related costs apply to PDP sponsors under Part D.
