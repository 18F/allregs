CFR /  Title 12  /  Part 268  /  Sec. 268.408 Effect of filing a civil action.

Filing a civil action under Sec. Sec. 268.406 or 268.407 shall terminate Commission processing of the appeal. If private suit is filed subsequent to the filing of an appeal, the parties are requested to notify the Commission in writing.
