CFR /  Title 18  /  Part 385  /  Sec. 385.2004 Originals and copies of filings (Rule 2004).

The requirements for making filings under this chapter are posted on the Commission's Web site at http://www.ferc.gov. The requirements cover documents and forms submitted on paper, on electronic media, or via the Commission's electronic filing systems. [Order 737, 75 FR 43405, July 26, 2010]
