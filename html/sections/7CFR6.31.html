CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 6  /  Sec. 6.31 Debarment and suspension.

The provisions in 7 CFR part 3017-- Government-wide Debarment and Suspension (Nonprocurement) and Government Requirements for Drug-Free Workplace (Grants), subparts A through E, apply to this subpart. [80 FR 44254, July 27, 2015]
