CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 62  /  Sec. 62.35-35 Starting systems for internal-combustion engines.

The starting systems for propulsion engines and for prime movers of ships' service generators required to start automatically must meet sections 4-6-5/9.5 and 4-8-2/11.11 of the ABS Steel Vessel Rules (incorporated by reference; see 46 CFR 62.05-1). [USCG-2003-16630, 73 FR 65189, Oct. 31, 2008]
