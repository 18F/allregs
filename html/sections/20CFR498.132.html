CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 498  /  Sec. 498.132 Limitations.

The Office of the Inspector General may initiate a proceeding in accordance with Sec. 498.109(a) to determine whether to impose a penalty and assessment, as applicable--

(a) In cases brought under section 1129 of the Social Security Act, after receiving authorization from the Attorney General pursuant to procedures agreed upon by the Inspector General and the Attorney General; and

(b) Within 6 years from the date on which the violation was committed. [61 FR 18081, Apr. 24, 1996]
