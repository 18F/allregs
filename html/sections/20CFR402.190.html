CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 402  /  Sec. 402.190 Officials who may deny a request for records under FOIA.

Only the Deputy Executive Director for the Office of Public Disclosure, Office of the General Counsel, SSA, or her or his designee is authorized to deny a written request to obtain, inspect, or copy any social security record. [62 FR 4154, Jan. 29, 1997, as amended at 68 FR 60295, Oct. 22, 2003]
