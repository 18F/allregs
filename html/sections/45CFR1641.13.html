CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 1641  /  Sec. 1641.13 Causes for suspension.

The debarring official may suspend an IPA in accordance with the procedures set forth in this part upon adequate evidence that:

(a) A cause for debarment under Sec. 1641.7 may exist;

(b) The IPA has been indicted for or convicted of any offense described in Sec. 1641.7;

(c) The IPA has been found subject to a civil judgment described in Sec. 1641.7(e), whether the judgment is final or not.

(d) The IPA has been suspended from contracting with a Federal agency or entity receiving Federal funds including when the IPA has stipulated to the suspension.
