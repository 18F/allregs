CFR /  Title 37  /  Part 2  /  Sec. 2.155 Notice of publication.

The Office will send the registrant a notice of publication of the mark and of the requirement for filing the affidavit or declaration required by section 8 of the Act. [64 FR 48924, Sept. 8, 1999]
