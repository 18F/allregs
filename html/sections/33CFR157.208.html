CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 157  /  Sec. 157.208 Dedicated Clean Ballast Tanks Operations Manual for foreign

If the owner or operator of a foreign tank vessel meeting Sec. 157.10a(b), Sec. 157.10a(c)(2), Sec. 157.10b(a)(2), or Sec. 157.10c(c)(2) desires a Coast Guard approved Dedicated Clean Ballast Tanks Operations Manual under Sec. 157.210, the owner or operator must submit two copies of a manual that meets Sec. 157.224 to the Commandant (CG-CVC), Attn: Office of Commercial Vessel Compliance, U.S. Coast Guard Stop 7501, 2703 Martin Luther King Jr. Avenue SE., Washington, DC 20593-7501. (Reporting and Recordkeeping requirements approved by the Office of Management and Budget under control number 1625-0036) [CGD 82-28, 50 FR 11629, and 11630, Mar. 22, 1985, as amended by CGD 88-052, 53 FR 25122, July 1, 1988; CGD 96-026, 61 FR 33668, June 28, 1996; USCG-2006-25150, 71 FR 39210, July 12, 2006; USCG-2010-0351, 75 FR 36286, June 25, 2010; USCG-2014-0410, 79 FR 38438, July 7, 2014]
