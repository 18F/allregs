CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 303  /  Sec. 303.35 State.

Except as provided in Sec. 303.732(d)(3) (regarding State allotments under this part), State means each of the 50 States, the Commonwealth of Puerto Rico, the District of Columbia, and the four outlying areas and jurisdictions of Guam, American Samoa, the United States Virgin Islands, and the Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1401(31))
