CFR /  Title 19  /  Part 10  /  Sec. 10.23 Standards, quotas, and visas.

All requirements and restrictions applicable to imported merchandise, such as labeling, radiation standards, flame-retarding properties, quotas, and visas, apply to assembled articles eligible for the exemption in the same manner as they would apply to all other imported merchandise. [T.D. 75-230, 40 FR 43025, Sept. 18, 1975]
