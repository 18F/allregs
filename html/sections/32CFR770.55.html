CFR /  Title 32  /  Part 770  /  Sec. 770.55 Responsibility.

The responsibility for proper identification and control of personnel and vehicle movement on the Portsmouth Naval Shipyard is vested with the Shipyard Security Manager (Code 1700). [49 FR 34003, Aug. 28, 1984, as amended at 65 FR 53593, Sept. 5, 2000]
