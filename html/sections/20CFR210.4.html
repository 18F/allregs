CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 210  /  Sec. 210.4 Year of service.

(a) A year of service is twelve months of reported or deemed service, consecutive or not consecutive. A fraction of a year of service is taken at its actual value.

(b) The term years of service means the total number of years an employee is credited with service as defined in Sec. 210.2 of this part. [49 FR 46731, Nov. 28, 1984, as amended at 53 FR 17183, May 16, 1988]
