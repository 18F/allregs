CFR /  Title 16  /  Part 1512  /  Sec. 1512.13 Requirements for front fork.

The front fork shall be tested for strength by application of at least 39.5 J (350 in-lb) of energy in accordance with the fork test, Sec. 1512.18(k)(1), without visible evidence of fracture. Sidewalk bicycles need not meet this requirement.
