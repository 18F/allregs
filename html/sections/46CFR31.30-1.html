CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 31  /  Sec. 31.30-1 Marine engineering regulations and material

(a) All tank vessels are subject to the regulations contained in parts 50 to 63, inclusive, of subchapter F (Marine Engineering) of this chapter, whenever applicable, except as such regulations are modified by the regulations in this subchapter for tank vessels. [CGFR 68-82, 33 FR 18804, Dec. 18, 1968]
