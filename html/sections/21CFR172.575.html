CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 172  /  Sec. 172.575 Quinine.

Quinine, as the hydrochloride salt or sulfate salt, may be safely used in food in accordance with the following conditions: ------------------------------------------------------------------------

Uses Limitations------------------------------------------------------------------------In carbonated beverages as a flavor..... Not to exceed 83 parts per

million, as quinine. Label

shall bear a prominent

declaration of the presence

of quinine either by the use

of the word ``quinine'' in

the name of the article or

through a separate

declaration.------------------------------------------------------------------------
