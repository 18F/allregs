CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 302-16  /  Sec. 302-16.104 Must I document my miscellaneous expenses to receive

You must show documentation of your miscellaneous expenses only when an amount exceeds that prescribed in Sec. 302-16.102. [FTR Amdt. 98, 66 FR 58196, Nov. 20, 2001, as amended by FTR Amdt. 2011-02, 76 FR 35111, June 16, 2011]
