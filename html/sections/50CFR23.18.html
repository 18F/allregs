CFR /  Title 50  /  Part 23  /  Sec. 23.18 What CITES documents are required to export

Answer the questions in the following decision tree to find the section in this part that applies to the type of CITES document you need to export Appendix-I wildlife. See Sec. 23.20(d) for CITES exemption documents or Sec. 23.92 for specimens that are exempt from the requirements of CITES and do not need CITES documents.
