CFR /  Title 13  /  Part 108  /  Sec. 108.2007 Grant award to SSBICs.

An SSBIC selected for an Operational Assistance grant award will receive a grant award only if, by a date established by SBA, it increases its Regulatory Capital in the specific amount set forth in its application, pursuant to Sec. 108.2004(a), and raises matching resources for the grant in the amount required by Sec. 108.2030(d)(2). [67 FR 68503, Nov. 12, 2002]
