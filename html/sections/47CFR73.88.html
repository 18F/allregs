CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 73  /  Sec. 73.88 Blanketing interference.

The licensee of each broadcast station is required to satisfy all reasonable complaints of blanketing interference within the 1 V/m contour.

Note: For more detailed instructions concerning operational responsibilities of licensees and permittees under this section, see Sec. 73.318 (b), (c) and (d). [28 FR 13574, Dec. 14, 1963, as amended at 56 FR 64859, Dec. 12, 1991]
