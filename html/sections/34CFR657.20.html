CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 657  /  Sec. 657.20 How does the Secretary evaluate an institutional

(a) The Secretary evaluates an institutional application for an allocation of fellowships on the basis of the quality of the applicant's Center or program. The applicant's Center or program is evaluated and approved under the criteria in Sec. 657.21.

(b) The Secretary informs applicants of the maximum possible score for each criterion in the application package or in a notice published in the Federal Register. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1122) [61 FR 50202, Sept. 24, 1996, as amended at 70 FR 13375, Mar. 21, 2005]
