CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 493  /  Sec. 493.1809 Limitation on Medicaid payment.

As provided in section 1902(a)(9)(C) of the Act, payment for laboratory services may be made under the State plan only if those services are furnished by a laboratory that has a CLIA certificate or is licensed by a State whose licensure program has been approved by the Secretary under this part. [57 FR 7237, Feb. 28, 1992; 57 FR 35761, Aug. 11, 1992]
