CFR /  Title 17  /  Part 38  /  Sec. 38.1 Scope.

The provisions of this part 38 shall apply to every board of trade that has been designated or is applying to become designated as a contract market under Sections 5 and 6 of the Act. Provided, however, nothing in this provision affects the eligibility of designated contract markets to operate under the provisions of parts 37 or 49 of this chapter. [71 FR 1964, Jan. 12, 2006, as amended at 77 FR 36697, June 19, 2012]
