CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 411  /  Sec. 411.160 Scope.

This subpart sets forth special rules that apply to individuals who are eligible for, or entitled to, Medicare on the basis of ESRD. (Section 406.13 of this chapter contains the rules for eligibility and entitlement based on ESRD.) [60 FR 45367, Aug. 31, 1995]
