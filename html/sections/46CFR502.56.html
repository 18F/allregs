CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 502  /  Sec. 502.56 Negotiated rulemaking.

The Commission, either upon petition of interested persons or upon its own motion, may establish a negotiated rulemaking committee to negotiate and develop consensus on a proposed rule, if, upon consideration of the criteria of 5 U.S.C. 563, use of such a committee is determined by the Commission to be in the public interest. [Rule 56.] [58 FR 38649, July 19, 1993, as amended at 64 FR 7808, Feb. 17, 1999]
