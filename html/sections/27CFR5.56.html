CFR /  Title 27  /  Part 5  /  Sec. 5.56 Certificates of age and origin.

Distilled spirits imported in bulk for bottling in the United States shall not be removed from the plant where bottled unless the bottler possesses certificates of age and certificates of origin applicable to such spirits which are similar to the certificates required by Sec. 5.52 for like distilled spirits imported in bottles.
