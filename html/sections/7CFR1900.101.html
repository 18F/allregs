CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 1900  /  Sec. 1900.101 General.

This subpart provides Agency policy concerning:

(a) The applicability of Federal rather than State Law in the conduct of Farmers Home Administration (FmHA) or its successor agency under Public Law 103-354 operations, and

(b) The liability of an auctioneer for conversion of personal property mortgaged to FmHA or its successor agency under Public Law 103-354. [44 FR 10979, Feb. 26, 1979, as amended at 45 FR 8934, Feb. 11, 1980]
