CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 800  /  Sec. 800.153 Maintenance and retention of records on official

Agencies and approved scale testing organizations shall maintain complete detailed official inspection work records, copies of official certificates, and equipment testing work records for 5 years. (Approved by the Office of Management and Budget under control number 0580-0011)
