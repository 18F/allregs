CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 245  /  Sec. 245.415 Submission of materials to HUD: Timing of submission.

(a) Initial submission. The mortgagor must submit the materials applicable to the covered action, as specified in Sec. Sec. 245.416 through 245.419, to the local HUD office when the notice required under Sec. 245.410 is served on the tenants.

(b) Subsequent submission. If additional notice under Sec. 245.420(c) is required, the mortgagor must submit to HUD any changes to the materials required under Sec. Sec. 245.416 through 245.419 when the notice required under Sec. 245.420(c) is served on the tenants.
