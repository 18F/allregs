CFR /  Title 31  /  Part 546  /  Sec. 546.303 Effective date.

The term effective date refers to the effective date of the applicable prohibitions and directives contained in this part as follows:

(a) With respect to a person whose property and interests in property are blocked pursuant to Sec. 546.201(a)(1), 12:01 a.m. eastern daylight time, April 27, 2006;

(b) With respect to a person whose property and interests in property are blocked pursuant to Sec. 546.201(a)(2), the earlier of the date of actual or constructive notice of such person's designation.
