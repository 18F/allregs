CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 215  /  Sec. 215.129 Defective cushioning device.

A railroad may not place or continue in service a car if it has a cushioning device that is--

(a) Broken;

(b) Inoperative; or

(c) Missing a part-- unless its sliding components have been effectively immobilized.
