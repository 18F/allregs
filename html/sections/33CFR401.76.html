CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 401  /  Sec. 401.76 In-transit cargo.

Cargo that is carried both upbound and downbound in the course of the same voyage shall be reported in the Seaway Transit Declaration Form, but is deemed to be ballast and not subject to toll assessment. Sec. 401.77 [Reserved]
