CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 953  /  Sec. 953.45 Minimum quantities.

The committee, with the approval of the Secretary, may establish, for any or all portions of the production area, quantities below which shipments shall be free from regulations issued pursuant to: Sec. 953.42, Regulations, Sec. 953.50, Inspection and certification, and Sec. 953.34, Assessments. [33 FR 8504, June 8, 1968]
