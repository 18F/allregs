CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 987  /  Sec. 987.13 Free dates.

Free dates means dates of any variety that are at the time of certification destined for consumption in whole or pitted form in the United States and Canada (and such other countries as the Committee determines are likely to acquire them at prices reasonably comparable with prices received domestically) and which are free to be handled pursuant to any free percentage established by the Secretary in accordance with Sec. 987.44. [43 FR 4251, Feb. 1, 1978]
