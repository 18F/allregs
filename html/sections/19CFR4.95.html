CFR /  Title 19  /  Part 4  /  Sec. 4.95 Records of entry and clearance of vessels.

Permanent records shall be prepared at each customhouse of all entries of vessels on Customs Form 1400 and of all clearances and permits to proceed on Customs Form 1401. Whenever a vessel is diverted, as provided for in Sec. 4.91 (a) or (b), Customs Form 1401 shall be amended to show the new destination. These records shall be open to public inspection. [T.D. 82-224, 47 FR 53727, Nov. 29, 1982]
