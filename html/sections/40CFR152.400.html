CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 152  /  Sec. 152.400 Purpose.

Subpart U prescribes fees to be charged for the pesticide regulatory activities set forth in Sec. 152.403 as performed by the Environmental Protection Agency (as authorized by 31 U.S.C. 9701 and Pub. L. 100-202) and provisions regarding their payment.
