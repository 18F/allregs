CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 605  /  Sec. 605.14 Agreement.

Except as provided in Sec. 605.11 no assistance shall be provided under the Acts unless the applicant and the Administrator shall have first entered into a written agreement that the applicant will not engage in school bus operations exclusively for the transportation of students and school personnel in competition with private school bus operators.
