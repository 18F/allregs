CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 63  /  Sec. 63.11586 Who implements and enforces this subpart?

(a) This subpart can be implemented and enforced by the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (U.S. EPA) or a delegated authority such as your State, local, or Tribal agency. If the U.S. EPA Administrator has delegated authority to your State, local, or Tribal agency, then that agency (the delegated authority), in addition to the U.S. EPA, has the authority to implement and enforce this subpart. You should contact your U.S. EPA Regional Office to find out if implementation and enforcement of this subpart has been delegated.

(b) In delegating implementation and enforcement authority of this subpart to a State, local, or Tribal agency under 40 CFR part 63, subpart E, the following authorities are retained by the Administrator of U.S. EPA:

(1) Approval of alternatives to the requirements in Secs. 63.11579, 63.11580, 63.11581, 63.11582, 63.11583, and 63.11584.

(2) Approval of major changes to test methods under Sec. 63.7(e)(2)(ii) and (f) and as defined in Sec. 63.90.

(3) Approval of major changes to monitoring under Sec. 63.8(f) and as defined in Sec. 63.90.

(4) Approval of major changes to recordkeeping and reporting under Sec. 63.10(f) and as defined in Sec. 63.90.
