CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 62  /  Sec. 62.14425 When must I review the documentation?

(a) You must establish a program for reviewing the information listed in Sec. 62.14424 annually with each HMIWI operator (defined in Sec. 62.14490).

(b) You must conduct your initial review of the information listed in Sec. 62.14424 by [date 6 months after publication of final rule], or prior to assumption of responsibilities affecting HMIWI operation, whichever is later.

(c) You must conduct subsequent reviews of the information listed in Sec. 62.14424 annually. [65 FR 49881, Aug. 15, 2000, as amended at 78 FR 28067, May 13, 2013]
