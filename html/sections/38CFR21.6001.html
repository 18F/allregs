CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 21  /  Sec. 21.6001 Temporary vocational training program for certain

This program provides certain veterans awarded pension with an evaluation and, if feasible, with vocational training, employment assistance and other services to enable them to achieve a vocational goal. (Authority: 38 U.S.C. 1524, Pub. L. 100-687). [55 FR 17271, Apr. 24, 1990]
