CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 356  /  Sec. 356.11 What types of problems may be researched under the

Problems encountered by individuals with disabilities in their daily lives that are due to the presence of a disabling condition, problems associated with the provision of rehabilitation services to individuals with disabilities, and problems connected with the conduct of disability research may be addressed under this program. (Authority: Secs. 202(d), 202(g)(1), 204; 29 U.S.C. 761a(d), 761a(g)(1), 762) [58 FR 49419, Sept. 22, 1993]
