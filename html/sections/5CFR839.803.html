CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 839  /  Sec. 839.803 If I am like the person in the previous question, but of combined retirement deductions and Social Security taxes due for my new retirement coverage, will I get a refund of the excess?

Yes, either OPM or your employer, as appropriate, will issue the payment in accordance with OPM instructions.
