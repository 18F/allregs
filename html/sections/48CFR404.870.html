CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 404  /  404.870 Document numbering system.

The SPE shall issue AGAR Advisories to establish and maintain a numbering system for USDA contracts, modifications, and delivery/task orders. USDA contracting offices shall number contracts, modifications, and orders in accordance with this numbering system. [70 FR 44, Jan. 3, 2005]
