CFR /  Title 50  /  Part 81  /  Sec. 81.12 Contracts.

The State may use its own regulations in obtaining services providing that they adhere to Federal laws and the requirements provided by Office of Management and Budget Circular A-102. The State is the responsible authority without recourse to the Secretary regarding settlement of contractual issues. [40 FR 47509, Oct. 9, 1975, as amended at 44 FR 31581, May 31, 1979]
