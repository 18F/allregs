CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 74  /  Sec. 74.2205 D&C; Green No. 5.

(a) Identity and specifications. The color additive D&C; Green No. 5 shall conform in identity and specifications to the requirements of Sec. 74.1205 (a)(1) and (b)(2).

(b) Uses and restrictions. D&C; Green No. 5 may be safely used for coloring cosmetics generally, including cosmetics intended for use in the area of the eye, in amounts consistent with current good manufacturing practice.

(c) Labeling requirements. The label of the color additive shall conform to the requirements of Sec. 70.25 of this chapter.

(d) Certification. All batches of D&C; Green No. 5 shall be certified in accordance with regulations in part 80 of this chapter. [47 FR 24285, June 4, 1982, as amended at 59 FR 40805, Aug. 10, 1994]
