CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 183  /  Sec. 183.378 Ungrounded systems.

Each ungrounded system must be provided with a suitably sensitive ground detection system located at the respective switchboard that provides continuous indication of circuit status to ground with a provision to momentarily remove the indicating device from the reference ground. [CGD 85-080, 62 FR 51358, Sept. 30, 1997]
