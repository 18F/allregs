CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 3570  /  Sec. 3570.69 Environmental review, intergovernmental review,

All grants awarded under this subpart, including grant-only awards, are subject to the environmental requirements of 7 CFR part 1940, subpart G, to the intergovernmental review requirements of 7 CFR 3015, subpart V and RD Instruction 1970-I, `Intergovernmental Review,' available in any Agency office or on the Agency's Web site. and the public information process in 7 CFR 1942.17(j)(9). [64 FR 32388, June 17, 1999, as amended at 76 FR 80732, Dec. 27, 2011]
