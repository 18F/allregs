CFR /  Title 29  /  Part 500  /  Sec. 500.219 General.

Except as specifically provided in these regulations, the ``Rules of Practice and Procedure for Administrative Hearings Before the Office of Administrative Law Judges'' established by the Secretary at 29 CFR part 18 shall apply to administrative proceedings under MSPA. [48 FR 36741, Aug. 21, 1983. Redesignated at 54 FR 13329, Mar. 31, 1989]
