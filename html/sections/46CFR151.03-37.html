CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 151  /  Sec. 151.03-37 Maximum allowable working pressure.

The maximum allowable working pressure shall be as defined in section VIII of the ASME Boiler and Pressure Vessel Code. [CGFR 70-10, 35 FR 3714, Feb. 25, 1970, as amended by CGD 85-061, 54 FR 50965, Dec. 11, 1989]
