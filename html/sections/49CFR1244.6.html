CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 1244  /  Sec. 1244.6 Retention of files.

(a) Subject railroads which submit waybill sample data by the computerized system instead of the manual system shall retain the underlying hard copy waybills or facsimilies capable of producing legible copies, which shall be complete including inbound references for transit waybills, for a minimum period of four years.

(b) This file of retained waybills shall be maintained in such a manner that the railroads on the computerized system may readily retrieve waybill copies using the waybill identifier code as shown on the submitted computerized waybill record. [46 FR 26784, May 15, 1981. Redesignated at 65 FR 37711, June 16, 2000]
