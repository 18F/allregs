CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 3273  /  Sec. 3273.10 When do I need a site license for a utilization facility?

You must obtain a site license approved by BLM, unless your facility will be located on lands leased as described in Sec. 3273.11. Do not start building or testing your utilization facility on public lands leased for geothermal resources until BLM has approved both your facility construction permit (see Sec. 3272.14) and your site license. The facility operator must apply for the license.
