CFR /  Title 31  /  Part 215  /  Sec. 215.5 In general.

This subpart is the text of the Withholding Agreement between the Secretary and the State, city or county. The terms used in this agreement are defined in Sec. 215.2 of this part. [42 FR 33731, July 1, 1977. Redesignated and amended at 71 FR 2150, Jan. 13, 2006]
