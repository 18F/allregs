CFR /  Title 29  /  Part 98  /  Sec. 98.305 May I enter into a covered transaction with an excluded

(a) You as a participant may not enter into a covered transaction with an excluded person, unless the Department of Labor grants an exception under Sec. 98.120.

(b) You may not enter into any transaction with a person who is disqualified from that transaction, unless you have obtained an exception under the disqualifying statute, Executive order, or regulation.
