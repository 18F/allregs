CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 350  /  Sec. 350.64 What is the required duration of the training in an

A grantee for an Advanced Rehabilitation Research Training Project shall provide training to individuals that is at least one academic year, unless a longer training period is necessary to ensure that each trainee is qualified to conduct independent research upon completion of the course of training. (Authority: Secs. 202-204; 29 U.S.C. 760-762)
