CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 51  /  Sec. 51.713 Bill-and-keep arrangements.

Bill-and-keep arrangements are those in which carriers exchanging telecommunications traffic do not charge each other for specific transport and/or termination functions or services. [76 FR 73856, Nov. 29, 2011]
