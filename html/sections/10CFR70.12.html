CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 70  /  Sec. 70.12 Carriers.

Common and contract carriers, freight forwarders, warehousemen, and the U.S. Postal Service are exempt from the regulations in this part to the extent that they transport special nuclear material in the regular course of carriage for another or storage incident thereto. This exemption does not apply to the storage in transit or transport of material by persons covered by the general license issued under Sec. 70.20a and Sec. 70.20b. [46 FR 12696, Feb. 18, 1981]
