CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 21  /  Sec. 21.224 Prevention of abuse.

Supplies are to be furnished under the most careful checks by the case manager as to what is needed by the veteran to pursue his or her program. Determinations of the supplies needed to enable the veteran to successfully pursue his or her rehabilitation program are made under the provisions of Sec. Sec. 21.210 through 21.222. (Authority: 38 U.S.C. 3104, 3111)
