CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 1  /  Sec. 1.795 Reports regarding interstate rates of return.

Carriers shall file reports regarding interstate rates of return on FCC Form 492 as required by part 65 of this chapter. [52 FR 274, Jan. 5, 1987]
