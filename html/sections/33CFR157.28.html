CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 157  /  Sec. 157.28 Discharges from tank barges exempted from certain design

The person in charge of a tank barge exempted under Sec. 157.08(g) from the requirements in Secs. 157.11, 157.13, 157.15, and 157.23 shall ensure that while the barge is proceeding en route:

(a) Cargo tanks are not ballasted or washed; and

(b) Oil or oily mixtures are not discharged. [CGD 74-32, 40 FR 48283, Oct. 14, 1975, as amended by CGD 76-088b, 48 FR 45721, Oct. 6, 1983; CGD 90-051, 57 FR 36244, Aug. 12, 1992]
