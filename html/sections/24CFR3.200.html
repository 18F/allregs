CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 3  /  Sec. 3.200 Application.

Except as provided in Sec. Sec. 3.205 through 3.235(a), these Title IX regulations apply to every recipient and to each education program or activity operated by such recipient that receives Federal financial assistance.
