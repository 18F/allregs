CFR /  Title 22  /  Part 92  /  Sec. 92.86 Consular responsibility for serving subpoenas.

When directed by the Department of State, officers of the Foreign Service will serve a subpoena issued by a court of the United States on a national or resident of the United States who is in a foreign country unless such action is prohibited by the law of the foreign country. [32 FR 11776, Aug. 16, 1967]
