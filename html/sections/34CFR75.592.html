CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 75  /  Sec. 75.592 Federal evaluation--satisfying requirement for

If a grantee cooperates in a Federal evaluation of a program, the Secretary may determine that the grantee meets the evaluation requirements of the program, including Sec. 75.590. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1221e-3 and 3474)
