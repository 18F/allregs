CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 17  /  Sec. 17.83 Limitations on payment for alcohol and drug dependence

The authority to enter into contracts shall be effective for any fiscal year only to such extent or in such amounts as are provided in appropriation acts, and payments shall not exceed these amounts. (Authority: Pub. L. 96-22, 38 U.S.C. 1720A) [47 FR 57708, Dec. 28, 1982. Redesignated at 61 FR 21965, May 13, 1996]
