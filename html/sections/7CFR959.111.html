CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 959  /  Sec. 959.111 Reapportionment of committee membership.

Pursuant to Sec. 959.25, committee membership is reapportioned among districts as follows:

(a) District 1 (Coastal Bend-Lower Valley): Six producer members and four handler members.

(b) District 2 (Laredo-Winter Garden): Four producer members and three handler members. [55 FR 25962, June 26, 1990]
