CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 91  /  Sec. 91.60-1 Application.

The provisions of this subpart shall apply to all cargo vessels on an international voyage. (See Sec. 91.05-10 of this chapter.) [CGD 95-012, 60 FR 48051, Sept. 18, 1995, as amended by USCG-1999-4976, 65 FR 6503, Feb. 9, 2000]
