CFR /  Title 50  /  Part 665  /  Sec. 665.904 Regulated activities.

(a) Commercial fishing is prohibited in the Islands Unit.

(b) Non-commercial fishing is prohibited in the Islands Unit, except as authorized under permit and pursuant to the procedures and criteria established in Sec. 665.905.

(c) Commercial fishing outside the Islands Unit and non-commercial fishing within the Islands Unit during the same trip is prohibited. [78 FR 33003, June 3, 2013, as amended at 78 FR 39583, July 2, 2013]
