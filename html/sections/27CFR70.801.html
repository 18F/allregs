CFR /  Title 27  /  Part 70  /  Sec. 70.801 Publicity of information.

For information relating to the disclosure of records that is not contained in this Subpart I, see 31 CFR Part 1 and the Appendix of that Part relating to the Bureau of Alcohol, Tobacco and Firearms. Direct further questions to the Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau, Washington, DC 20220, (202) 927-8210. [T.D. ATF-378, 61 FR 29955, June 13, 1996, as amended by T.D. ATF-450, 66 FR 29030, May 29, 2001]
