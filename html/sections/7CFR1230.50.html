CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 1230  /  Sec. 1230.50 Establishment and membership.

There is hereby established a National Pork Board of 15 members consisting of producers representing at least 12 States or importers appointed by the Secretary from nominations submitted pursuant to Sec. 1230.39(d). The Board shall be deemed to be constituted once the Secretary makes the appointments to the Board.
