CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 80  /  Sec. 80.586 What are the record retention requirements for test

Each individual test facility must retain records related to the establishment of accuracy and precision values, all test method documentation, and any quality control testing and analysis under Sec. Sec. 80.582, 80.584 and 80.585, for five years. [69 FR 39188, June 29, 2004] Sec. Sec. 80.587-80.589 [Reserved]
