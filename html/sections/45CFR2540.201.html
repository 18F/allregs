CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 2540  /  Sec. 2540.201 To whom must I apply the National Service Criminal

You must apply the National Service Criminal History Check eligibility criteria to individuals serving in covered positions. A covered position is a position in which the individual receives an education award or a Corporation grant-funded living allowance, stipend, or salary. [77 FR 60932, Oct. 5, 2012]
