CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 989  /  Sec. 989.8 Natural condition raisins.

Natural condition raisins means raisins the production of which includes sun-drying or artificial dehydration but which have not been further processed to a point where they meet any of the conditions for ``packed raisins'', as defined in Sec. 989.9. [25 FR 12813, Dec. 14, 1960, as amended at 42 FR 37201, July 20, 1977]
