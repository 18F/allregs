CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 152  /  Sec. 152.406 Submission of supplementary data.

Applicants may submit data to supplement pending applications without incurring additional charges if the proper fee was paid with submission of the original application and subsequent submissions of supplementary data do not constitute a change in the type of registration action requested. [53 FR 19114, May 26, 1988, as amended at 58 FR 34203, June 23, 1993]
