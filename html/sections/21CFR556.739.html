CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 556  /  Sec. 556.739 Trenbolone.

(a) Acceptable daily intake (ADI). The ADI for total residues of trenbolone is 0.4 microgram per kilogram of body weight per day.

(b) Tolerances. A tolerance for total trenbolone residues in uncooked edible tissues of cattle is not needed. [64 FR 18574, Apr. 15, 1999]
