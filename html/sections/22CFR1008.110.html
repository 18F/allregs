CFR /  Title 22  /  Part 1008  /  Sec. 1008.110 Are any of my Federal assistance awards exempt from this part?

This part does not apply to any award that the Inter-American Foundation President or designee determines that the application of this part would be inconsistent with the international obligations of the United States or the laws or regulations of a foreign government.
