CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 701  /  Sec. 701.45 Forestry Incentives Program (FIP) contracts.

The regulations governing the FIP as of July 31, 2002, and contained in the 7 CFR parts 700 to 899, edition revised as of January 1, 2002, shall continue to apply to FIP contracts in effect as of that date, except as provided in accord with a delegation of the administration of that program and such delegation and actions taken thereunder shall apply to any other FIP matters as may be at issue or in dispute. [69 FR 10302, Mar. 4, 2004, as amended at 75 FR 70088, Nov. 17, 2010]
