CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 380  /  Sec. 380.5 Exception to procedure.

The Maritime Subsidy Board or the Maritime Administrator may dispense with the publication of notice when not inconsistent with applicable laws. Subpart B_Application for Designation of Vessels as American Great Lakes

Vessels

Source: 56 FR 3980, Feb. 1, 1991, unless otherwise noted.
