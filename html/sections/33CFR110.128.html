CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 110  /  Sec. 110.128 Columbia River at Portland, Oreg.

The waters of the Columbia River between Sand Island and Government Island, bounded on the west by pile dike U.S. 5.75 and a line extending true north from the northerly end of the dike to the south shore of Sand Island and bounded on the east by a line bearing 339[deg]15[min] true, from a point on Government Island at latitude 45[deg]35[min]10[sec], longitude 122[deg]32[min]41[sec], to the southerly shore of Sand Island.
