CFR /  Title 50  /  Part 665  /  Sec. 665.604 Prohibitions.

In addition to the general prohibitions specified in Sec. 600.725 of this chapter and Sec. 665.16, it is unlawful for any person to do any of the following:

(a) Fish for PRIA bottomfish MUS using gear prohibited under Sec. 665.605.

(b) Fish for, or retain on board a vessel, PRIA bottomfish MUS in the PRIA without the appropriate permit registered for use with that vessel issued under Sec. 665.13.

(c) Falsify or fail to make or file all reports of PRIA bottomfish MUS landings taken in the PRIA, containing all data in the exact manner, as specified in Sec. 665.14(b).
