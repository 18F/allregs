CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 405  /  Sec. 405.215 Confidential commercial and trade secret information.

To the extent that CMS relies on confidential commercial or trade secret information in any judicial proceeding, CMS will maintain confidentiality of the information in accordance with Federal law. Subpart C_Suspension of Payment, Recovery of Overpayments, and Repayment

of Scholarships and Loans

Authority: Secs. 1102, 1815, 1833, 1842, 1862, 1866, 1870, 1871, 1879 and 1892 of the Social Security Act (42 U.S.C. 1302, 1395g, 1395l, 1395u, 1395y, 1395cc, 1395gg, 1395hh, 1395pp and 1395ccc) and 31 U.S.C. 3711.

Source: 31 FR 13534, Oct. 20, 1966, unless otherwise noted. Redesignated at 42 FR 52826, Sept. 30, 1977.

Editorial Note: Nomenclature changes to subpart C of part 405 appear at 76 FR 5961, Feb. 2, 2011.
