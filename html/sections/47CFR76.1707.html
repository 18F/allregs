CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 76  /  Sec. 76.1707 Leased access.

If a cable operator adopts and enforces a written policy regarding indecent leased access programming pursuant to Sec. 76.701, such a policy will be considered published pursuant to that rule by inclusion of the written policy in the operator's public inspection file.
