CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 264  /  Sec. 264.572 Design and installation of new drip pads.

Owners and operators of new drip pads must ensure that the pads are designed, installed, and operated in accordance with one of the following:

(a)(4)), 264.574 and 264.575 of this subpart, or

(b)), 264.574 and 264.575 of this subpart. [57 FR 61503, Dec. 24, 1992]
