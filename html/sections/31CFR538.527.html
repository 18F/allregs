CFR /  Title 31  /  Part 538  /  Sec. 538.527 Operation of accounts.

The operation of an account in a U.S. financial institution for an individual ordinarily resident in Sudan who is not included within the term ``Government of Sudan,'' as defined in Sec. 538.305, is authorized, provided that transactions processed through the account:

(a) Are of a personal nature and not for use in supporting or operating a business;

(b) Do not involve transfers directly or indirectly to Sudan or for the benefit of individuals ordinarily resident in Sudan unless authorized by Sec. 538.528; and

(c) Are not otherwise prohibited by this part. [70 FR 34062, June 13, 2005]
