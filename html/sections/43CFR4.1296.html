CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 4  /  Sec. 4.1296 Appeals.

Any person aggrieved by a decision concerning the award of costs and expenses in an administrative proceeding under this Act may appeal such award to the Board under procedures set forth in Sec. 4.1271 et seq., unless the Board has made the initial decision concerning such an award.
