CFR /  Title 26  /  Part 1  /  Sec. 1.383-0 Effective date.

(a) The regulations under section 383 (other than the regulations described in paragraph (b) of this section) reflect the amendments made to sections 382 and 383 by the Tax Reform Act of 1986. See Sec. 1.383-1(j) for effective date rules.

(b) Sections 1.383-1A, 1.383-2A, and 1.383-3A do not reflect the amendments made to sections 382 and 383 by the Tax Reform Act of 1986. [T.D. 8352, 56 FR 29434, June 27, 1991]
