CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 331  /  Sec. 331.21 Test modifications.

The formulation or mode of administration of certain products may require a modification of the United States Pharmacopeia 23/National Formulary 18 acid neutralizing capacity test. Any proposed modification and the data to support it shall be submitted as a petition under the rules established in Sec. 10.30 of this chapter. All information submitted will be subject to the disclosure rules in part 20 of this chapter. [61 FR 4823, Feb. 8, 1996]
