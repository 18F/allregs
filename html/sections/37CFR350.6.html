CFR /  Title 37  /  Part 350  /  Sec. 350.6 Construction and waiver.

The regulations of the Copyright Royalty Judges are intended to provide efficient and just administrative proceedings and will be construed to advance these purposes. For purposes of an individual proceeding, the provisions of this subchapter may be suspended or waived, in whole or in part, upon a showing of good cause, to the extent allowable by law. [70 FR 30905, May 31, 2005, as amended at 71 FR 53327, Sept. 11, 2006]
