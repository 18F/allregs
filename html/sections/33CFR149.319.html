CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 149  /  Sec. 149.319 What additional lifejackets must the deepwater port have?

For each person on duty in a location where the lifejacket required by Sec. 149.317 of this part is not readily accessible, an additional lifejacket must be stowed so as to be readily accessible to that location. [USCG-2013-0397, 78 FR 39179, July 1, 2013]
