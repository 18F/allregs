CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 3801  /  Sec. 3801.5 Appeals.

Any person whose request is denied shall have the right to appeal such denial. Appeals shall be in accordance with Sec. 1.6(e) of this title and addressed to the Chairperson, World Agricultural Outlook Board, U.S. Department of Agriculture, Washington, DC 20250.
