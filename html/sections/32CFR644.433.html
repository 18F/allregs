CFR /  Title 32  /  Part 644  /  Sec. 644.433 Surplus disposal to private parties.

General. Sections 644.435(b) through 644.440 cover general procedures for the sale of surplus fee-owned land and easement interests and includes actions to be taken preliminary to proceeding with the appropriate sale procedures set forth in Sec. Sec. 644.540 through 644.557.
