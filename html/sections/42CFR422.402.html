CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 422  /  Sec. 422.402 Federal preemption of State law.

The standards established under this part supersede any State law or regulation (other than State licensing laws or State laws relating to plan solvency) with respect to the MA plans that are offered by MA organizations. [70 FR 4733, Jan. 28, 2005]
