CFR /  Title 27  /  Part 28  /  Sec. 28.105 Report of inspection and tax liability.

When the spirits are ready for shipment, the proprietor shall execute his report of inspection and tax liability on all copies of TTB Form 5100.11. (72 Stat. 1362; 26 U.S.C. 5214) [T.D. ATF-46, 42 FR 44773, Sept. 6, 1977, as amended by T.D. ATF-62, 44 FR 71723, Dec. 11, 1979; T.D. TTB-8, 69 FR 3832, Jan. 27, 2004]
