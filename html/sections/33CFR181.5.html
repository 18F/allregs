CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 181  /  Sec. 181.5 Purpose and applicability.

This subpart prescribes requirements for the certification of boats and associated equipment to which 46 U.S.C. Chapter 43 applies and to which a safety standard prescribed in Part 183 of this chapter applies. [CGD 85-002, 51 FR 37573, Oct. 23, 1986]
