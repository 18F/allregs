CFR /  Title 18  /  Part 381  /  Sec. 381.401 Review of jurisdictional agency determinations.

The fee established for review of a jurisdictional agency determination is $115. The fee must be submitted in accordance with subpart A of this part and Sec. 270.301(c) of this chapter. [Order 616, 65 FR 45872, July 26, 2000]
