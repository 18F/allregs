CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 203  /  Sec. 203.60 Who may apply for royalty relief on a case-by-case basis in

You may apply for royalty relief under Sec. Sec. 203.61(b) and 203.62 for an individual lease, unit or project if you:

(a) Hold a pre-Act lease (as defined in Sec. 203.0) that we have assigned to an authorized field (as defined in Sec. 203.0);

(b) Propose an expansion project (as defined in Sec. 203.0); or

(c) Propose a development project (as defined in Sec. 203.0).
