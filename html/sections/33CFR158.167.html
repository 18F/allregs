CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 158  /  Sec. 158.167 Reporting inadequate reception facilities.

Any person may report to the local Coast Guard COTP that reception facilities required by these regulations or MARPOL 73/78 are inadequate. Reports of inadequate reception facilities may be made orally, in writing or by telephone. [CGD 88-002, 54 FR 18409, Apr. 28, 1989]
