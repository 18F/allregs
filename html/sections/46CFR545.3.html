CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 545  /  Sec. 545.3 Interpretation of Sec. 515.23(b) of this chapter--Payment

A claimant seeking to settle a claim in accordance with Sec. 515.23(b)(1) of this chapter should promptly provide to the financial responsibility provider all documents and information relating to and supporting its claim for the purpose of evaluating the validity and subject matter of the claim. [65 FR 33480, May 24, 2000]
