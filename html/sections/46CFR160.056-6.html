CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 160  /  Sec. 160.056-6 Name plate.

(a) Each rescue boat shall have permanently fitted at the transom a metal name plate, galvanically compatible with the hull material, and bearing information relating to the testing and approval of the prototype boat. Either raised or indented letters shall be used.

(b) The following information shall appear on the name plate:

RescueBoat U.S.C.G. Specification 160.056Prototype approved______________________________________________________

(Date)Approved by OCMI________________________________________________________

(Port)Date of manufacture_____________________________________________________

(Date)Manufacturer's serial No._______________________________________________Manufacturer's name and address_________________________________________
