CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 433  /  Sec. 433.35 Equipment--Federal financial participation.

Claims for Federal financial participation in the cost of equipment under the Medicaid Program are determined in accordance with subpart G of 45 CFR part 95. Requirements concerning the management and disposition of equipment under the Medicaid Program are also prescribed in subpart G of 45 CFR part 95. [47 FR 41564, Sept. 21, 1982]
