CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 364  /  Sec. 364.11 When must the State plan be submitted for approval?

The designated State unit (DSU) shall submit to the Secretary for approval the three-year State plan no later than July 1 of the year preceding the first fiscal year of the three-year period for which the State plan is submitted. (Approved by the Office of Management and Budget under control number 1820-0527) (Authority: 29 U.S.C. 796c(a)(4))
