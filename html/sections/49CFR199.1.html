CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 199  /  Sec. 199.1 Scope.

This part requires operators of pipeline facilities subject to part 192, 193, or 195 of this chapter to test covered employees for the presence of prohibited drugs and alcohol. [Amdt. 199-19, 66 FR 47117, Sept. 11, 2001]
