CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 10  /  Sec. 10.27 Definitions.

For the purpose of Sec. Sec. 10.28 to 10.47, the word Act as used herein refers to the World War Adjusted Compensation Act, as amended; the word Veteran refers to that term as defined in section 2 of title I of said Act; the word Director refers to the Secretary of Veterans Affairs.
