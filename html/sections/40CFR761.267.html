CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 761  /  Sec. 761.267 Sampling non-porous surfaces.

(a) Sample large, nearly flat, non-porous surfaces by dividing the surface into roughly square portions approximately 2 meters on each side. Follow the procedures in Sec. 761.302(a).

(b) It is not necessary to sample small or irregularly shaped surfaces.
