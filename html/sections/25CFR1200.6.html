CFR /  Title 25  /  Part 1200  /  Sec. 1200.6 How could a tribe receive future income directly rather

If a tribe wishes to receive future income directly, the tribe may contact a Fiduciary Trust Officer located at the agency or regional office. [71 FR 15339, Mar. 28, 2006]
