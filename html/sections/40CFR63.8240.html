CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 63  /  Sec. 63.8240 What are my monitoring requirements?

For each by-product hydrogen stream, each end box ventilation system vent, and each mercury thermal recovery unit vent, you must monitor the mercury emissions using the procedures in paragraph (a) or (b) of this section.

(a) You must continuously monitor the mercury concentration using a mercury continuous emissions monitor according to the requirements in Sec. Sec. 63.8242(a) and 63.8244(a); or

(b) You must periodically monitor the mercury emissions according to the requirements in Sec. Sec. 63.8242(b) and 63.8244(b).
