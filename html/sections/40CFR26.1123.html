CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 26  /  Sec. 26.1123 Early termination of research.

The Administrator may require that any project covered by this subpart be terminated or suspended when the Administrator finds that an IRB, investigator, sponsor, or institution has materially failed to comply with the terms of this subpart. Sec. 26.1124 [Reserved]
