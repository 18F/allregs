CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 60  /  Sec. 60.2966 Am I required to apply for and obtain a title V

Yes, if you are subject to this subpart, you are required to apply for and obtain a title V operating permit unless you meet the relevant requirements for an exemption specified in Sec. 60.2887.
