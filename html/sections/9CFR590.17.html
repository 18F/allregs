CFR /  Title 9  /  Part 590  /  Sec. 590.17 Nondiscrimination.

The conduct of all services and the licensing of graders and inspectors under these regulations shall be accomplished without discrimination as to race, color, religion, sex, national origin, age, or disability. [40 FR 20057, May 8, 1975. Redesignated at 42 FR 32514, June 27, 1977. Further redesignated at 46 FR 63203, Dec. 31, 1981, and amended at 60 FR 49169, Sept. 21, 1995]
