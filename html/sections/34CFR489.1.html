CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 489  /  Sec. 489.1 What is the Functional Literacy for State and Local

(a) The Secretary makes grants to eligible entities that elect to establish a demonstration or system-wide functional literacy program for adult prisoners, as described Sec. 489.3.

(b) Grants under this part may be used for establishing, improving, expanding, or carrying out a program, and for developing the plans and submitting the reports required by this part. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1211-2(a), (d)(1))
