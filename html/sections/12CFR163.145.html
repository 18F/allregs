CFR /  Title 12  /  Part 163  /  Sec. 163.145 May I combine my notice or application with other

You may combine the notice or application required under Sec. 163.143 with any other notice or application, if the capital distribution is a part of, or is proposed in connection with, another transaction requiring a notice or application under this chapter. If you submit a combined filing, you must:

(a) State that the related notice or application is intended to serve as a notice or application under this subpart; and

(b) Submit the notice or application in a timely manner.
