CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 1651  /  Sec. 1651.9 Participant's next of kin.

If the account is to be paid to the participant's next of kin under Sec. 1651.2(a)(6), the next of kin of the participant will be determined in accordance with the state law of the participant's domicile at the time of death.
