CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 1503  /  1503.905 Procedures for investigating complaints.

The Assistant Administrator for Administration and Resources Management is designated as the recipient of the written report of findings by the Inspector General. The Assistant Administrator shall ensure that the report of findings is disseminated in accordance with FAR 3.905(c). [61 FR 57337, Nov. 6, 1996]
