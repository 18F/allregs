CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 54  /  Sec. 54.07 Service of notice upon designated Coast Guard official.

The notice and all accompanying documentation must be sent to Commanding Officer, Coast Guard Human Resources Service and Information Center, Federal Building, 444 S.E. Quincy Street, Topeka, KS 66683-3591, telephone 785-339-3595, facsimile 785-339-3788. [CGD 82-109, 48 FR 4285, Jan. 31, 1983, as amended by CGD 88-052, 53 FR 25119, July 1, 1988; CGD 97-023, 62 FR 33362, June 19, 1997; USCG-2001-9286, 66 FR 33639, June 25, 2001]
