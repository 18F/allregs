CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 2  /  Sec. 2.21 How will the bureau respond to requests?

(a) When the bureau informs you of its decision to comply with a request by granting, partially granting, or denying the request, it will do so in writing and in accordance with the deadlines in subpart D of this part. The bureau's written response will include a statement about the services offered by the Office of Government Information Services (OGIS), using standard language that can be found at: http://www.doi.gov/foia/news/guidance/index.cfm.

(b) If the bureau determines that your request will take longer than 10 workdays to process, the bureau immediately will send you a written acknowledgment that includes the request's individualized tracking number and processing track (see Sec. 2.15(e)). The acknowledgement may also include a brief description of the subject of your request.
