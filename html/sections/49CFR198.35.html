CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 198  /  Sec. 198.35 Grants conditioned on adoption of one-call damage

In allocating grants to State agencies under the pipeline safety laws, (49 U.S.C. 60101 et seq.), the Secretary considers whether a State has adopted or is seeking to adopt a one-call damage prevention program in accordance with Sec. 198.37. If a State has not adopted or is not seeking to adopt such program, the State agency may not receive the full reimbursement to which it would otherwise be entitled. [Amdt. 198-2, 61 FR 38403, July 24, 1996]
