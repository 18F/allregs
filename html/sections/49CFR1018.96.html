CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 1018  /  Sec. 1018.96 Stay of offset.

If the debtor timely notifies the Board that the debtor is exercising the right described in Sec. 1018.94(a) of this subpart, any notice to the IRS will be stayed until the issuance of a written decision which sustains or amends its original determination.
