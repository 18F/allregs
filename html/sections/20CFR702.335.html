CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 702  /  Sec. 702.335 Formal hearings; notice.

On a form prescribed for this purpose, the Office of the Chief Administrative Law Judge shall notify the parties (See Sec. 702.333) of the place and time of the formal hearing not less than 30 days in advance thereof. [42 FR 42552, Aug. 23, 1977]
