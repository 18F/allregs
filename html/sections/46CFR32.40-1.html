CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 32  /  Sec. 32.40-1 Application--TB/ALL.

(a) The provisions of this subpart, except Sec. 32.40-60 and Sec. 32.40-65, apply to all tankships of 100 gross tons and over constructed on or after June 15, 1987.

(b) Tankships of less than 100 gross tons and manned tank barges must meet the requirements of Sec. 32.40-60.

(c) Tankships of 100 gross tons and over constructed prior to June 15, 1987, must meet the requirements of Sec. 32.40-65.
