CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 236  /  Sec. 236.1 Plans, where kept.

As required for maintenance, plans shall be kept at all interlockings, automatic signals and controlled points. Plans shall be legible and correct. [49 FR 3382, Jan. 26, 1984]
