CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 1900  /  Sec. 1900.100 Cross-references to employee ethical conduct standards

Officers and employees of the Appalachian Regional Commission Federal Staff are subject to the Standards of Ethical Conduct for Employees of the Executive Branch at 5 CFR part 2635 and the executive branch-wide financial disclosure regulations at 5 CFR part 2634. [60 FR 62702, Dec. 7, 1995; 61 FR 13051, Mar. 26, 1996]
