CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 569  /  Sec. 569.1 Purpose and scope.

This part sets forth the conditions under which regrooved and regroovable tires manufactured or regrooved after the effective date of the regulation may be sold, offered for sale, introduced for sale or delivered for introduction into interstate commerce. [42 FR 21613, Apr. 28, 1977]
