CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 1227  /  Sec. 1227.107 When will the ONRR Director decide whether to

The ONRR Director will decide whether to approve your delegation proposal within 90 days after your delegation proposal is considered complete under Sec. 1227.104. ONRR may extend the 90-day period with your written consent. [62 FR 43084, Aug. 12, 1997, as amended at 75 FR 61087, Oct. 4, 2010]
