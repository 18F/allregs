CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 3281  /  Sec. 3281.13 Is there a format or model a unit operator must use

When proposing a unit agreement, submit to BLM:

(a) The model unit agreement (see Sec. 3286.1);

(b) The model unit agreement with variances noted; or

(c) Any unit agreement format that contains all the terms and conditions BLM requires (see Sec. Sec. 3281.14 and 3281.15).
