CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 192  /  Sec. 192.453 General.

The corrosion control procedures required by Sec. 192.605(b)(2), including those for the design, installation, operation, and maintenance of cathodic protection systems, must be carried out by, or under the direction of, a person qualified in pipeline corrosion control methods. [Amdt. 192-71, 59 FR 6584, Feb. 11, 1994]
