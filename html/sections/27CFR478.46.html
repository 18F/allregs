CFR /  Title 27  /  Part 478  /  Sec. 478.46 Insufficient fee.

If an application is filed with an insufficient fee, the application and any fee submitted will be returned to the applicant. (18 U.S.C. 847 (84 Stat. 959); 18 U.S.C. 926 (82 Stat. 1226)) [T.D. ATF-200, 50 FR 10498, Mar. 15, 1985]
