CFR /  Title 27  /  Part 28  /  Sec. 28.40 Evidence of exportation: distilled spirits and wine.

The exportation of any shipment of distilled spirits or wine may be evidenced by:

(a) A copy of the export bill of lading (Sec. 28.250); or

(b) A copy of the railway express receipt (Sec. 28.251); or

(c) A copy of the air express receipt (Sec. 28.252); or

(d) A copy of the through bill of lading where exportation is to a contiguous foreign country (Sec. 28.250); or

(e) A certificate by the export carrier, as provided for in Sec. 28.253. (72 Stat. 1334, 1335, 1336, 1362, 1380; 26 U.S.C. 5053, 5055, 5062, 5214, 5362) [25 FR 5734, June 23, 1960. Redesignated at 40 FR 16835, Apr. 15, 1975, and amended by T.D. ATF-224, 51 FR 7698, Mar. 5, 1986; T.D. TTB-8, 69 FR 3831, Jan. 27, 2004]
