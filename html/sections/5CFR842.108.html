CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 842  /  Sec. 842.108 Employees covered under the District of Columbia Courts

Employees of the Public Defender Service of the District of Columbia are deemed to be Federal employees for FERS purposes on and after April 1, 1999. [64 FR 15289, Mar. 31, 1999]
