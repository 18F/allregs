CFR /  Title 27  /  Part 21  /  Sec. 21.69 Formula No. 39.

(a) Formula. To every 100 gallons of alcohol add: Nine pounds of sodium salicylate, U.S.P., or salicylic acid, U.S.P.; 1.25 gallons of fluid extract of quassia, N.F. VII; and \1/8\ gallon of tert-butyl alcohol.

(b) Authorized uses. (1) As a solvent: 111. Hair and scalp preparations.112. Bay rum.113. Lotions and creams (hand, face, and body).121. Perfume and perfume tinctures.122. Toilet waters and colognes.

(2) Miscellaneous uses: 812. Product development and pilot plant uses (own use only).
