CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 28  /  Sec. 28.482 United States Cotton Futures Act.

The cotton standards contained in Sec. 28.301 through Sec. 28.603 of this part shall be effective for purposes of the United States Cotton Futures Act (7 U.S.C. 15b) and the regulations thereunder (7 CFR part 27). [45 FR 46783, July 11, 1980]

Official Cotton Standards of the United States for the ColorGrade of

American Pima Cotton

Authority: Secs. 28.501 to 28.510 issued under Sec. 10, 42 Stat. 1519 (7 U.S.C. 61). Interpret or apply Sec. 6, 42 Stat. 1518, as amended (7 U.S.C. 56.)
