CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 2421  /  Sec. 2421.21 Affected by issues raised.

The phrase affected by issues raised, as used in part 2422, should be construed broadly to include parties and other labor organizations, or agencies or activities that have a connection to employees affected by, or questions presented in, a proceeding. [60 FR 67291, Dec. 29, 1995]
