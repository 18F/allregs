CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 153  /  Sec. 153.1122 Discharges of NLS residue from tank washing other

Tank washings that do not result from a prewash and that contain Category A, B, or C NLS residues must be discharged to a reception facility or discharged to the sea under Sec. 153.1126 or Sec. 153.1128 except those tank washings resulting from washing a tank that has been cleaned following a ventilation procedure in the approved Procedures and Arrangements Manual.
