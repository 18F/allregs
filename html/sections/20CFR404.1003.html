CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 404  /  Sec. 404.1003 Employment.

Employment means, generally, any service covered by social security performed by an employee for his or her employer. The rules on who is an employee and who is an employer are contained in Secs. 404.1005 through 404.1010. Section 404.1004 states the general rule on the kinds of work covered as employment. Exceptions to the general rule are contained in Secs. 404.1012 through 404.1038 which explain the kinds of work excluded from employment. All of these rules apply to current work unless otherwise indicated. [45 FR 20075, Mar. 27, 1980, as amended at 61 FR 38365, July 24, 1996]
