CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 370  /  Sec. 370.63 What responsibilities do the SERC and the LEPC have to make

Under this subpart, the SERC or LEPC must make the following information (except for confidential location information discussed in Sec. 370.64(b)) available if a person requests it:

(a) All information obtained from an owner or operator in response to a request under this subpart.

(b) Any requested Tier II information or MSDS otherwise in possession of the SERC or the LEPC.
