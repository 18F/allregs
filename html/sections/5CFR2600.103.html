CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 2600  /  Sec. 2600.103 Office of Government Ethics organization and functions.

OGE's Director is appointed by the President and confirmed by the Senate for a five-year term. Additional information regarding OGE's organization and functions is available on its Web site at www.oge.gov. [80 FR 57072, Sept. 22, 2015]
