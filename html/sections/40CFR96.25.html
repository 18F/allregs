CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 96  /  Sec. 96.25 NOX Budget permit revisions.

(a) For a NOX Budget source with a title V operating permit, except as provided in Sec. 96.23(b), the permitting authority will revise the NOX Budget permit, as necessary, in accordance with the permitting authority's title V operating permits regulations addressing permit revisions.

(b), the permitting authority will revise the NOX Budget permit, as necessary, in accordance with the permitting authority's non-title V permits regulations addressing permit revisions.
