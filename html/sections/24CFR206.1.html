CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 206  /  Sec. 206.1 Purpose.

The purposes of the Home Equity Conversion Mortgage Insurance program are set out in section 255(a) of the National Housing Act, Public Law 73-479, 48 STAT. 1246 (12 U.S.C. 1715z-20) (``NHA''). [61 FR 49032, Sept. 17, 1996]
