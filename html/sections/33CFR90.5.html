CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 90  /  Sec. 90.5 Lights for moored vessels.

A vessel at anchor includes a vessel made fast to one or more mooring buoys or other similar device attached to the ocean floor. Such vessels may be lighted as a vessel at anchor in accordance with Rule 30, or may be lighted on the corners in accordance with 33 CFR 88.13. [CGD 94-011, 63 FR 5732, Feb. 4, 1998]
