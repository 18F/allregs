CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 52  /  52.227-5 Waiver of Indemnity.

As prescribed in 27.201-2(e), insert the following clause:

Waiver of Indemnity (APR 1984)

Any provision or clause of this contract to the contrary notwithstanding, the Government hereby authorizes and consents to the use and manufacture, solely in performing this contract, of any invention covered by the United States patents identified below and waives indemnification by the Contractor with respect to such patents:________________________________________________________________________Contracting Officer identify the patents by number or by other means if more appropriate

(End of clause) [49 FR 12987, Mar. 30, 1984, as amended at 72 FR 63066, Nov. 7, 2007]
