CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 367  /  Sec. 367.20 Under what circumstances does the Secretary award

(a) In the case of a fiscal year for which the amount appropriated under section 753 of the Act is less than $13,000,000, the Secretary awards discretionary grants under this part on a competitive basis to States.

(b) Subparts A, B, C, and E of this part govern the award of competitive grants under this part. (Authority: 29 U.S.C. 796k(b)(1))
