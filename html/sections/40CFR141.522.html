CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 141  /  Sec. 141.522 How does the State determine whether my system's

During an onsite inspection conducted under the provisions of Sec. 141.71(b)(3), the State must determine whether your watershed control program is adequate to limit potential contamination by Cryptosporidium oocysts. The adequacy of the program must be based on the comprehensiveness of the watershed review; the effectiveness of your program to monitor and control detrimental activities occurring in the watershed; and the extent to which your system has maximized land ownership and/or controlled land use within the watershed.
