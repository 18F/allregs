CFR /  Title 12  /  Part 263  /  Sec. 263.16 The Board's right to conduct examination.

Nothing contained in this subpart limits in any manner the right of the Board or any Federal Reserve Bank to conduct any examination, inspection, or visitation of any institution or institution-affiliated party, or the right of the Board or any Federal Reserve Bank to conduct or continue any form of investigation authorized by law. [56 FR 38052, Aug. 9, 1991; 56 FR 60056, Nov. 27, 1991]
