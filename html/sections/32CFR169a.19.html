CFR /  Title 32  /  Part 169a  /  Sec. 169a.19 Study limits.

No DoD funds shall be available to perform any cost study pursuant to the provisions of OMB Circular A-76 if the study being performed exceeds a period of 24 months after initiation of such study with respect to a single function activity or 48 months after initiation of such study for a multi-function activity. [60 FR 67328, Dec. 29, 1995]
