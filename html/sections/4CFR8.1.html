CFR /  Title 4  /  Part 8  /  Sec. 8.1 Applicable law and regulations.

The provisions of subpart G, title 5, United States Code and implementing regulations for the Executive Branch covering compensation for work injuries, retirement, unemployment compensation, life insurance, and health insurance apply to Government Accountability Office employees. [45 FR 68380, Oct. 15, 1980, as amended at 47 FR 56979, Dec. 22, 1982]
