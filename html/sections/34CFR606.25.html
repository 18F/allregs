CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 606  /  Sec. 606.25 What priority does the Secretary use in awarding cooperative

Among applications for cooperative arrangement grants, the Secretary gives priority to proposed cooperative arrangements that are geographically and economically sound, or will benefit the institutions applying for the grant. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1101 et seq.)
