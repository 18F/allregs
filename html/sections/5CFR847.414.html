CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 847  /  Sec. 847.414 Crediting future NAFI service.

An employee who elects CSRS or FERS coverage under Sec. 847.411 will be covered by CSRS or FERS during all periods of future service not excluded from coverage by CSRS or FERS, including any periods of service with a NAFI and service as a reemployed annuitant.
