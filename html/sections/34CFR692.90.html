CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 692  /  Sec. 692.90 What is the Grants for Access and Persistence Program?

The Grants for Access and Persistence (GAP) Program assists States in establishing partnerships to provide eligible students with LEAP Grants under GAP to attend institutions of higher education and to encourage increased participation in early information and intervention, mentoring, or outreach programs. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1070c-3a)
