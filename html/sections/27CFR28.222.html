CFR /  Title 27  /  Part 28  /  Sec. 28.222 Claim, Form 1582-B (5130.6).

Claim for allowance of drawback of internal revenue taxes on beer brewed or produced in the United States shall be prepared on Form 1582-B (5130.6), in quadruplicate, as required by this part. Each Form 1582-B (5130.6) shall be given, by the person initiating the form, a serial number beginning with ``1'' for the first day of January of each year and running consecutively thereafter to December 31, inclusive. (72 Stat. 1335; 26 U.S.C. 5055)
