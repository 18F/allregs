CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 350  /  Sec. 350.11 What are the general requirements for a Field-Initiated

A Field-Initiated Project must--

(a) Further one or more of the purposes in Sec. 350.2; and

(b) Carry out one of the following types of activities:

(1) Research.

(2) Development. (Authority: Sec. 202; 29 U.S.C. 761a)
