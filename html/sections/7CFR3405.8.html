CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 3405  /  Sec. 3405.8 Complementary project proposals.

Institutions may submit proposals that are complementary in nature as defined in Sec. 3405.2(g). Such complementary project proposals may be submitted by the same or by different eligible institutions.
