CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 1421  /  Sec. 1421.13 Special loan deficiency payments.

(a)(1) Eligible producers of unshorn pelts produced from live sheep and hay and silage derived from an eligible loan commodity as provided in Sec. 1421.5 are eligible to request unshorn pelt, hay, and silage quantities for a LDP under subpart C of this part.

(1) Eligible producers of unshorn pelts produced from live sheep and hay and silage derived from an eligible loan commodity as provided in Sec. 1421.5 are eligible to request unshorn pelt, hay, and silage quantities for a LDP under subpart C of this part.

(2) Unshorn pelts, hay, and silage derived from an eligible loan commodity are not eligible to be pledged as collateral to obtain a MAL under subpart B of this part. [71 FR 32424, June 6, 2006, as amended at 74 FR 15654, Apr. 7, 2009; 80 FR 129, Jan. 2, 2015]
