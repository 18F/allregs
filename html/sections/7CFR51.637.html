CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 51  /  Sec. 51.637 Injury.

Injury means any specific defect described in Sec. 51.652, Table IV; or an equally objectionable variation of any one of these defects, any other defect, or any combination of defects, which slightly detracts from the appearance, or the edible or marketing quality of the fruit.
