CFR /  Title 28  /  Part 12  /  Sec. 12.20 Filing of registration statement.

Registration statements shall be filed in duplicate with the National Security Division, Department of Justice, Washington, DC 20530. Filing may be made in person or by mail, and shall be deemed to have taken place upon the receipt thereof by the Division. [Order No. 2865-2007, 72 FR 10068, Mar. 7, 2007]
