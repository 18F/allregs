CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 91  /  Sec. 91.116 Certification procedure--test engine selection.

(a) The manufacturer must select, from each engine family, a test engine of a configuration that the manufacturer deems to be most likely to exceed the Family Emission Limit (FEL).

(b) At the manufacturer's option, the criterion for selecting the worst case engine may be that engine configuration which has the highest weighted brake-specific fuel consumption over the appropriate engine test cycle.

(c) The test engine must be constructed to be representative of production engines.
