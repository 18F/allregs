CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 20  /  Sec. 20.2205 Reports to individuals of exceeding dose limits.

When a licensee is required by Sec. Sec. 20.2203 or 20.2204 to report to the Commission any exposure of an identified occupationally exposed individual, or an identified member of the public, to radiation or radioactive material, the licensee shall also provide the individual a report on his or her exposure data included in the report to Commission. This report must be transmitted no later than the transmittal to the Commission. [72 FR 68059, Dec. 4, 2007]
