CFR /  Title 22  /  Part 124  /  Sec. 124.6 Termination of manufacturing license agreements and

The U.S. party to a manufacturing license or a technical assistance agreement must inform the Directorate of Defense Trade Controls in writing of the impending termination of the agreement not less than 30 days prior to the expiration date of such agreement. [71 FR 20543, Apr. 21, 2006]
