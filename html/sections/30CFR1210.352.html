CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 1210  /  Sec. 1210.352 Special forms and reports.

The ONRR may require submission of additional information on special forms or reports. When special forms or reports other than those referred to in this subpart are necessary, ONRR will give instructions for the filing of such forms or reports. Requests for the submission of such forms will be made in conformity with the requirements of the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1980 and other applicable laws. [56 FR 57286, Nov. 8, 1991. Redesignated at 72 FR 24467, May 2, 2007]
