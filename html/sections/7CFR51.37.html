CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 51  /  Sec. 51.37 Charges for fees, rates, and expenses.

For each carlot of product inspected, a fee or rate determined in accordance with Sec. Sec. 51.38 and 51.39, and expenses determined in accordance with Sec. 51.40, shall be paid by the applicant. [56 FR 55800, Oct. 30, 1991]
