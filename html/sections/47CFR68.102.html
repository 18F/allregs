CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 68  /  Sec. 68.102 Terminal equipment approval requirement.

Terminal equipment must be approved in accordance with the rules and regulations in subpart C of this part, or connected through protective circuitry that is approved in accordance with the rules and regulations in subpart C. [66 FR 7582, Jan. 24, 2001]
