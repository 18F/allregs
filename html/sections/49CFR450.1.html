CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 450  /  Sec. 450.1 Purpose.

This subchapter establishes requirements and procedures for safety approval and periodic examination of cargo containers used in international transport, as defined in the International Safe Container Act. [45 FR 37213, June 2, 1980]
