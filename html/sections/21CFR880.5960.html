CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 880  /  Sec. 880.5960 Lice removal kit.

(a) Identification. The lice removal kit is a comb or comb-like device intended to remove and/or kill lice and nits from head and body hair. It may or may not be battery operated.

(b) Classification. Class I (general controls). The device is exempt from the premarket notification procedures in subpart E of part 807 of this chapter subject to the limitations in Sec. 880.9. [63 FR 59718, Nov. 5, 1998]
