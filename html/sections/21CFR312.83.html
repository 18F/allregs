CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 312  /  Sec. 312.83 Treatment protocols.

If the preliminary analysis of phase 2 test results appears promising, FDA may ask the sponsor to submit a treatment protocol to be reviewed under the procedures and criteria listed in Sec. Sec. 312.305 and 312.320. Such a treatment protocol, if requested and granted, would normally remain in effect while the complete data necessary for a marketing application are being assembled by the sponsor and reviewed by FDA (unless grounds exist for clinical hold of ongoing protocols, as provided in Sec. 312.42(b)(3)(ii)). [53 FR 41523, Oct. 21, 1988, as amended at 76 FR 13880, Mar. 15, 2011]
