CFR /  Title 36  /  Part 13  /  Sec. 13.1188 Where to get charts depicting closed waters.

Closed waters and islands within Glacier Bay as described in Sec. Sec. 13.1174-13.1180 of this subpart are described as depicted on NOAA Chart 17318 GLACIER BAY (4th Ed., Mar. 6/93) available to the public at park offices at Bartlett Cove and Juneau, Alaska.
