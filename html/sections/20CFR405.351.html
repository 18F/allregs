CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 405  /  Sec. 405.351 Closing statements.

You or your representative may present a closing statement to the administrative law judge--

(a) Orally at the end of the hearing,

(b) In writing after the hearing and within a reasonable time period set by the administrative law judge, or

(c) By using both methods under paragraphs (a) and (b).
