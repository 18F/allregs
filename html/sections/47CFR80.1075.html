CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 80  /  Sec. 80.1075 Radio records.

A record must be kept, as required by the Radio Regulations and Sec. 80.409 (a), (b) and (e), of all incidents connected with the radio communication service which appear to be of importance to safety of life at sea.
