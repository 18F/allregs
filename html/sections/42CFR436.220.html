CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 436  /  Sec. 436.220 Individuals who would meet the income and resource

(a) The agency may provide Medicaid to any group or groups of individuals specified under Sec. 436.201(a)(4), (a)(5), and (a)(6) who would meet the income and resource requirements under the State's AFDC plan if their work-related child care costs were paid from their earnings rather than by a State agency as a service expenditure.

(b) The agency may use this option only if the State's AFDC plan deducts work-related child care costs from income to determine the amount of AFDC. [43 FR 45218, Sept. 29, 1978, as amended at 58 FR 4935, Jan. 19, 1993]
