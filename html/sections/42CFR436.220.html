CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 436  /  Sec. 436.220 Individuals who would meet the income and resource

(a)(6) who would meet the income and resource requirements under the State's AFDC plan if their work-related child care costs were paid from their earnings rather than by a State agency as a service expenditure.

(b) The agency may use this option only if the State's AFDC plan deducts work-related child care costs from income to determine the amount of AFDC. [43 FR 45218, Sept. 29, 1978, as amended at 58 FR 4935, Jan. 19, 1993]
