CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 522  /  Sec. 522.1410 Methylprednisolone.

(a) Specifications. Each milliliter of suspension contains 20 or 40 milligrams (mg) of methylprednisolone acetate.

(b) Sponsors. See Nos. 054628 and 054771 in Sec. 510.600(c) of this chapter.

(c) [Reserved]

(d) Conditions of use--

(1) Dogs--

(i) Amount. Administer 2 to 40 mg (up to 120 mg in extremely large breeds or dogs with severe involvement) by intramuscular injection or up to 20 mg by intrasynovial injection.

(ii) Indications for use. For treatment of inflammation and related disorders; treatment of allergic and dermatologic disorders; and as supportive therapy to antibacterial treatment of severe infections.

(iii) Limitations. Federal law restricts this drug to use by or on the order of a licensed veterinarian.

(2) Cats--

(i) Amount. Administer 10 to 20 mg by intramuscular injection.

(ii) Indications for use. For treatment of inflammation and related disorders; treatment of allergic and dermatologic disorders; and as supportive therapy to antibacterial treatment of severe infections.

(iii) Limitations. Federal law restricts this drug to use by or on the order of a licensed veterinarian.

(3) Horses--

(i) Amount. Administer 200 mg by intramuscular injection or 40 to 240 mg by intrasynovial injection.

(ii) Indications for use. For treatment of inflammation and related disorders.

(iii) Limitations. Do not use in horses intended for human consumption. Federal law restricts this drug to use by or on the order of a licensed veterinarian.
