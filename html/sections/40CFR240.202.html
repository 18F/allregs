CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 240  /  Sec. 240.202 Site selection. Sec. 240.202-1 Requirement.

Site selection and utilization shall be consistent with public health and welfare, and air and water quality standards and adaptable to appropriate land-use plans. Sec. 240.202-2 Recommended procedures: Design.

(a) Whenever possible, thermal processing facilities should be located in areas zoned for industrial use and having adequate utilities to serve the facility.

(b) The site should be accessible by permanent roads leading from the public road system.

(c) Environmental factors, climatological conditions, and socioeconomic factors should be given full consideration as selection criteria. Sec. 240.202-3 Recommended procedures: Operations.

Not applicable.
