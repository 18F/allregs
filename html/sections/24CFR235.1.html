CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 235  /  Sec. 235.1 Applicability of regulations.

The regulations regarding eligibility requirements (including eligibility requirements for noncitizens) for homes for lower income families in force before December 8, 1995, will continue to govern the rights and obligations of mortgagors, mortgagees, and the Department of Housing and Urban Development with respect to loans insured under section 235(i) of the National Housing Act. [61 FR 13624, Mar. 27, 1996]
