CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 884  /  Sec. 884.124 Audit.

Where a non-Federal entity (as defined in 2 CFR 200.69) is the eligible owner of a project, or is a contract administrator under Sec. 884.119 or Sec. 884.120, receiving financial assistance under this part, the audit requirements in 2 CFR part 200, subpart F, shall apply. [80 FR 75941, Dec. 7, 2015]
