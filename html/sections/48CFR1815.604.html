CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 1815  /  1815.604 Agency points of contact. (NASA supplements paragraph (a))

(a)(6) Information titled ``Guidance for the Preparation and Submission of Unsolicited Proposals'' is available on the Internet at http://ec.msfc.nasa.gov/hq/library/unSol-Prop.html. A deviation is required for use of any modified or summarized version of the Internet information or for alternate means of general dissemination of unsolicited proposal information. [63 FR 9954, Feb. 27, 1998, as amended at 63 FR 44409, Aug. 19, 1998; 66 FR 53546, Oct. 23, 2001; 69 FR 21764, Apr. 22, 2004]
