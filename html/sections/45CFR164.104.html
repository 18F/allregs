CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 164  /  Sec. 164.104 Applicability.

(a) Except as otherwise provided, the standards, requirements, and implementation specifications adopted under this part apply to the following entities:

(1) A health plan.

(2) A health care clearinghouse.

(3) A health care provider who transmits any health information in electronic form in connection with a transaction covered by this subchapter.

(b) Where provided, the standards, requirements, and implementation specifications adopted under this part apply to a business associate. [68 FR 8375, Feb. 20, 2003, as amended at 78 FR 5692, Jan. 25, 2013]
