CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 426  /  Sec. 426.56 Pretreatment standards for new sources.

Any new source subject to this subpart that introduces process wastewater pollutants into a publicly owned treatment works must comply with 40 CFR part 403. [60 FR 33958, June 29, 1995]
