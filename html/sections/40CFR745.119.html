CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 745  /  Sec. 745.119 Impact on State and local requirements.

Nothing in this subpart shall relieve a seller, lessor, or agent from any responsibility for compliance with State or local laws, ordinances, codes, or regulations governing notice or disclosure of known lead-based paint or lead- based paint hazards. Neither HUD nor EPA assumes any responsibility for ensuring compliance with such State or local requirements. Subparts G-K [Reserved]
