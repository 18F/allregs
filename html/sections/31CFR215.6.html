CFR /  Title 31  /  Part 215  /  Sec. 215.6 Parties.

The parties to this agreement are the Secretary and the State, city or county which has entered into this agreement pursuant to 5 U.S.C. 5516, 5517, or 5520 and Executive Order 11997 (June 22, 1977). [42 FR 33731, July 1, 1977. Redesignated at 71 FR 2150, Jan. 13, 2006]
