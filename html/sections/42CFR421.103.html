CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 421  /  Sec. 421.103 Payment to providers.

Providers are assigned to intermediaries in accordance with Sec. 421.104. As the Medicare Administrative Contractors (MACs) are implemented, providers are reassigned from intermediaries to MACs in accordance with Sec. 412.404 of this chapter. [71 FR 68228, Nov. 24, 2006]
