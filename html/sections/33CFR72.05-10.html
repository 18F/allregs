CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 72  /  Sec. 72.05-10 Free distribution.

Official copies are distributed free of charge to Federal, State, and foreign governments, municipalities, libraries and other public institutions.

Note to Sec. 72.05-10: You may also access Coast Guard Light List data through the Coast Guard Navigation Center's Web site: (http://www.navcen.uscg.gov/?pageName=lightLists). (R.S. 501, as amended, sec. 5, 38 Stat. 75; 44 U.S.C. 82, 84) [CGFR 51-15, 18 FR 13, Jan. 1, 1953, as amended by USCG-2001-10714, 69 FR 24984, May 5, 2004; USCG-2013-0397, 78 FR 39171, July 1, 2013]
