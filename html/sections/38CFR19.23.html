CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 19  /  Sec. 19.23 Applicability of provisions concerning Notice of Disagreement.

(a) Appeals governed by Sec. 20.201(a) of this chapter shall be processed in accordance with Sec. 19.24. Sections 19.26, 19.27 and 19.28 shall not apply to appeals governed by Sec. 20.201(a) of this chapter.

(b) Appeals governed by Sec. 20.201(b) of this chapter shall be processed in accordance with Sec. Sec. 19.26, 19.27, and 19.28. [79 FR 57697, Sept. 25, 2014]
