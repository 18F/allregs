CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 188  /  Sec. 188.10-53 Oceanographic research vessel.

The term oceanographic research vessel means a vessel that the Secretary finds is being employed only in instruction in oceanography or limnology, or both, or only in oceanographic or limnological research, including those studies about the sea such as seismic, gravity meter, and magnetic exploration and other marine geophysical or geological surveys, atmospheric research, and biological research. [CGD 84-069, 61 FR 25312, May 20, 1996]
