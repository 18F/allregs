CFR /  Title 17  /  Part 239  /  Sec. 239.201 Form 2-E, report of sales pursuant to Rule 609 of Regulation E.

This form shall be used for report of sales of securities under Regulation E (Sec. Sec. 230.601-230.610a of this chapter) by a small business investment company described in Rule 602 (Sec. 230.602 of this chapter) as required by Rule 609 of Regulation E (Sec. 230.609 of this chapter).
