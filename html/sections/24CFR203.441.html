CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 203  /  Sec. 203.441 Insurance of loan.

Under compliance with the commitment, or as provided in Sec. 203.255(b) with respect to mortgages processed under the Direct Endorsement program, the Commissioner shall insure the loan evidencing the insurance by the issuance of an insurance certificate which will identify the regulations under which the loan is insured and the date of insurance. [57 FR 58349, Dec. 9, 1992; 58 FR 13537, Mar. 12, 1993]
