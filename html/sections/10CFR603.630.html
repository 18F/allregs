CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 603  /  Sec. 603.630 Use Federally approved indirect cost rates for

In accordance with the general policy in Sec. 603.605, the contracting officer must require a for-profit participant that has federally approved indirect cost rates for its Federal procurement contracts to use those rates to accumulate and report costs under an expenditure-based TIA. This includes both provisional and final rates that are approved up until the time that the TIA is closed out.
