CFR /  Title 14  /  Part 1252  /  Sec. 1252.203 Special benefits for children and the elderly.

If a recipient operating a program or activity provides special benefits to the elderly or to children, such use of age distinctions shall be presumed to be necessary to the normal operation of the program or activity, notwithstanding the provision of Sec. 1252.10. [50 FR 13311, Apr. 4, 1985, as amended at 68 FR 51352, Aug. 26, 2003]
