CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 982  /  Sec. 982.340 Assessment rate.

On and after July 1, 2000, an assessment rate of $0.005 per pound is established for Oregon and Washington hazelnuts. [65 FR 47247, Aug. 2, 2000]
