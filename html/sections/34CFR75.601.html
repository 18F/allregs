CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 75  /  Sec. 75.601 Applicant's assessment of environmental impact.

An applicant shall include with its application its assessment of the impact of the proposed construction on the quality of the environment in accordance with section 102(2)(C) of the National Environmental Policy Act of 1969 and Executive Order 11514 (34 FR 4247). (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1221e-3 and 3474)
