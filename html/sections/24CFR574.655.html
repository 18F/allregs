CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 574  /  Sec. 574.655 Wage rates.

The provisions of the Davis-Bacon Act (40 U.S.C. 276a-276a-5) do not apply to this program, except where funds received under this part are combined with funds from other Federal programs that are subject to the Act. [59 FR 17201, Apr. 11, 1994]
