CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 222  /  Sec. 222.14 What requirements must a local educational agency meet

The Secretary forgives an eligible overpayment, in whole or part as described in Sec. 222.18, if--

(a) An LEA submits to the Department's Impact Aid Program office a written request for forgiveness by the later of--

(1) Thirty days from the LEA's initial receipt of a written notice of the overpayment; or

(2) September 2, 1997;

(b) The LEA submits to the Department's Impact Aid Program office the information and documentation described in Sec. 222.16 by the deadlines described in paragraph (a) of this section, or other time limit established in writing by the Secretary due to lack of availability of the information and documentation; and

(c) The Secretary determines under Sec. 222.17 that--

(1) In the case either of an LEA's or the Department's error, repayment of the LEA's total eligible overpayments will result in an undue financial hardship on the LEA and seriously harm the LEA's educational program; or

(2) In the case of the Department's error, determined on a case-by-case basis, repayment would be manifestly unjust (``manifestly unjust repayment exception''). [62 FR 35413, July 1, 1997]
