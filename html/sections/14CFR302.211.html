CFR /  Title 14  /  Part 302  /  Sec. 302.211 Procedures in certificate cases involving initial

(a) Applicability. This section applies to cases involving certificate authority under sections 41102 and 41103 of the Statute, including applications for new authority, renewals, amendments, modifications, suspensions, and transfers of such certificates, where the issues involve a determination of the applicant's fitness to operate. Where such applications propose the operation of scheduled service in limited entry international markets, the provisions of Sec. 302.212 also apply.

(b) Order establishing further procedures. Within 90 days after a complete application is filed, the DOT decisionmaker will take action as provided in Sec. 302.210.
