CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 790  /  Sec. 790.97 Hearing procedures.

(a) Hearing requests must be submitted using the method specified in Sec. 790.5(b). Such requests must include the applicant's basis for appealing EPA's decision.

(b) If more than one applicant has requested a hearing on similar grounds, all of those appeals will be considered at the same hearing unless confidentiality claims preclude a joint hearing.

(c) EPA will notify each applicant of EPA's decision within 60 days after the hearing. [50 FR 20660, May 17, 1985, as amended at 78 FR 72830, Dec. 4, 2013]
