CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 1217  /  Sec. 1217.250 Audits.

An audit of the lessee's accounts and books may be made annually or at such other times as may be directed by the mining supervisor, by certified public accountants, and at the expense of the lessee. The lessee shall furnish free of cost duplicate copies of such annual or other audits to the mining supervisor, within 30 days after the completion of each auditing. [37 FR 11041, June 1, 1972. Redesignated at 48 FR 35641, Aug. 5, 1983]
