CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 302-16  /  Sec. 302-16.1 What is the purpose of the miscellaneous expenses allowance

The miscellaneous expenses allowance (MEA) is intended to help defray some of the costs incurred due to relocating. (See part 302-10 of this chapter for specific costs normally associated with relocation of a mobile home dwelling that are covered under transportation expenses.) [FTR Amdt. 2011-01, 76 FR 18345, Apr. 1, 2011]
