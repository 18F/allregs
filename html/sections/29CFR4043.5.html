CFR /  Title 29  /  Part 4043  /  Sec. 4043.5 How and where to file.

The PBGC applies the rules in subpart A of part 4000 of this chapter and the instructions to the applicable PBGC reporting form to determine permissible methods of filing with the PBGC under this part. See Sec. 4000.4 of this chapter for information on where to file. [68 FR 61354, Oct. 28, 2003]
