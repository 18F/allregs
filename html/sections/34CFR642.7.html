CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 642  /  Sec. 642.7 How many applications may an eligible applicant submit?

An applicant may submit more than one application for Training grants as long as each application describes a project that addresses a different absolute priority from Sec. 642.24 that is designated in the Federal Register notice inviting applications. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1221e-3) [75 FR 65771, Oct. 26, 2010]
