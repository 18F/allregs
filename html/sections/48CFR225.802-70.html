CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 225  /  225.802-70 Contracts for performance outside the United States and Canada.

Follow the procedures at PGI 225.802-70 when placing a contract requiring performance outside the United States and Canada. Also see subpart 225.3, Contracts Performed Outside the United States. [70 FR 23801, May 5, 2005, as amended at 77 FR 43472, July 24, 2012; 80 FR 36902, June 26, 2015]
