CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 102  /  Sec. 102.73 Approval of benefits.

When the Secretary has determined that benefits will be paid to a requester and has calculated the type and amount of such benefits, he will notify the requester (or his or her representative) in writing. The Secretary will make payments in accordance with Sec. 102.83.
