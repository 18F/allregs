CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 585  /  Sec. 585.638 What must I do upon completion of my commercial operations

(a) Upon completion of your approved activities under your COP, you must initiate the decommissioning process as set forth in subpart I of this part. You must submit your decommissioning application as provided in Sec. Sec. 585.905 and 585.906.

(b) Upon completion of your approved activities under your FERC license, the terms of your FERC license will govern your decommissioning activities. Sec. 585.639 [Reserved] General Activities Plan Requirements For Limited Leases, ROW Grants, and
