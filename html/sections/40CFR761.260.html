CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 761  /  Sec. 761.260 Applicability.

This subpart provides a method for collecting new data for characterizing a PCB remediation waste cleanup site or for assessing the sufficiency of existing site characterization data, as required by Sec. 761.61(a)(2).
