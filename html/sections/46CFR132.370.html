CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 132  /  Sec. 132.370 Added requirements for fixed independent and portable

(a) When carrying fixed independent tanks on deck or portable tanks in compliance with Sec. 125.110 of this subchapter, each vessel must also comply with Sec. Sec. 98.30-37 and 98.30-39 of this chapter.

(b) When carrying portable tanks in compliance with Sec. 125.120 of this subchapter, each vessel must also comply with 49 CFR 176.315.
