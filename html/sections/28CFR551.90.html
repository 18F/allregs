CFR /  Title 28  /  Part 551  /  Sec. 551.90 Policy.

Bureau staff shall not discriminate against inmates on the basis of race, religion, national origin, sex, disability, or political belief. This includes the making of administrative decisions and providing access to work, housing and programs. [63 FR 55774, Oct. 16, 1998]
