CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 35  /  Sec. 35.6025 Deviation from this subpart.

On a case-by-case basis, EPA will consider requests for an official deviation from the non-statutory provisions of this subpart. Refer to the requirements regarding additions and exceptions described in 2 CFR 1500.3. [72 FR 24504, May 2, 2007, as amended at 79 FR 76059, Dec. 19, 2014]
