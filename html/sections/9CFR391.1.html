CFR /  Title 9  /  Part 391  /  Sec. 391.1 Scope and purpose.

Fees shall be charged by the Agency for certain specified inspection services provided on a holiday, on an overtime basis, and/or which are voluntary inspection services. [54 FR 6390, Feb. 10, 1989]
