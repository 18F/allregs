CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 214  /  Sec. 214.109 Disapproval by HUD.

If an application package does not meet all requirements in Sec. 214.103, HUD will provide the agency with the reasons for the denial in writing. Within 30 calendar days of the written notice of denial, the agency may submit a revised application, or appeal HUD's decision in writing to HUD, as provided in Sec. 214.205. If an agency decides to submit a revised application, the agency may consult HUD, to determine the specific actions needed to resolve the deficiencies.
