CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 915  /  Sec. 915.8 Grower.

Grower is synonymous with producer and means any person who produces avocados for market and who has a proprietary interest therein: Provided, That as used in Sec. 915.22 the term grower shall include only those who have a proprietary interest in the production of 10 or more bearing avocado trees. [19 FR 3439, June 11, 1954, as amended at 22 FR 3513, May 21, 1957. Redesignated at 26 FR 12751, Dec. 30, 1961]
