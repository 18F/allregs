CFR /  Title 18  /  Part 1314  /  Sec. 1314.7 Liability of TVA and Reserve Banks.

TVA and the Reserve Banks may rely on the information provided in a transfer message and are not required to verify the information. TVA and the Reserve Banks shall not be liable for any action taken in accordance with the information set out in a transfer message or evidence submitted in support thereof. [62 FR 920, Jan. 7, 1997; 62 FR 4833, Jan. 31, 1997]
