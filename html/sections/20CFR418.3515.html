CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 418  /  Sec. 418.3515 How could you qualify for a subsidy again?

Unless you subsequently qualify as a deemed eligible person (per 42 CFR 423.773(c)), you must file a new application for a subsidy and meet all the requirements in Sec. 418.3101.
