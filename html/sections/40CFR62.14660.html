CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 62  /  Sec. 62.14660 How do I demonstrate initial compliance with the emission

You must conduct an initial performance test, as required under 40 CFR 60.8, to determine compliance with the emission limitations in Table 1 of this subpart and to establish operating limits using the procedure in Sec. 62.14635 or Sec. 62.14640. The initial performance test must be conducted using the test methods listed in table 1 of this subpart and the procedures in Sec. 62.14650.
