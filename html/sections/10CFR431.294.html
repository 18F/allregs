CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 431  /  Sec. 431.294 Uniform test method for the measurement of energy machines.

(a) Scope. This section provides test procedures for measuring, pursuant to EPCA, the energy consumption of refrigerated bottled or canned beverage vending machines.

(b) Testing and Calculations. Determine the daily energy consumption of each covered refrigerated bottled or canned beverage vending machine by conducting the appropriate test procedure set forth in appendix A or B to this subpart. [71 FR 71375, Dec. 8, 2006, as amended at 80 FR 45793, July 31, 2015]
