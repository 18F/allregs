CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 52  /  Sec. 52.2373 Legal authority.

(a) The requirements of Sec. 51.230(f) of this chapter are not met. Vermont does not have the authority to make emissions data available to the public since 10 V.S.A. section 363 would require the data to be held confidential if a source certified that it related to production or sales figures, unique processes, or would tend to affect adversely the competitive position of the owner. [37 FR 10899, May 31, 1972, as amended at 51 FR 40676, Nov. 7, 1986]
