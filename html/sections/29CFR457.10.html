CFR /  Title 29  /  Part 457  /  Sec. 457.10 CSRA; FSA; CAA; LMRDA.

CSRA means the Civil Service Reform Act of 1978; FSA means the Foreign Service Act of 1980; CAA means the Congressional Accountability Act of 1995; LMRDA means the Labor-Management Reporting and Disclosure Act of 1959, as amended. [62 FR 6093, Feb. 10, 1997]
