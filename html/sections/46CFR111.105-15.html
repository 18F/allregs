CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 111  /  Sec. 111.105-15 Additional methods of protection.

Each item of electrical equipment that is--

(a) A powder-filled apparatus must meet IEC 60079-5 (incorporated by reference; see 46 CFR 110.10-1);

(b) An oil-immersed apparatus must meet either IEC 79-6 (incorporated by reference; see 46 CFR 110.10-1) or Article 500.7(I) of NFPA NEC 2002 (incorporated by reference; see 46 CFR 110.10-1);

(c) Type of protection ``e'' must meet IEC 60079-7 (incorporated by reference; see 46 CFR 110.10-1);

(d) Type of protection ``n'' must meet IEC 60079-15 (incorporated by reference; see 46 CFR 110.10-1); and

(e) Type of protection ``m'' must meet IEC 60079-18 (incorporated by reference; see 46 CFR 110.10-1). [USCG-2003-16630, 73 FR 65200, Oct. 31, 2008, as amended by USCG-2013-0671, 78 FR 60153, Sept. 30, 2013]
