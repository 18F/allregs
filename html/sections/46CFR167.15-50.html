CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 167  /  Sec. 167.15-50 Tailshaft examinations.

Tailshaft examinations on nautical school ships must conform with the examination requirements in part 61 of this chapter. [CGD 84-024, 52 FR 39655, Oct. 23, 1987]
