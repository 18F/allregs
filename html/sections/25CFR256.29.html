CFR /  Title 25  /  Part 256  /  Sec. 256.29 Can HIP resources be combined with other available

Yes. HIP resources may be supplemented with other available resources (e.g., in-kind assistance; tribal or housing authority; and any other leveraging mechanism identified in Sec. 256.3(d)) to increase the number of HIP recipients.
