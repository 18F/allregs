CFR /  Title 39  /  Part 3020  /  Sec. 3020.12 Publication of the Mail Classification Schedule.

(a) The Mail Classification Schedule established in accordance with subchapters I, II, and III of chapter 36 of title 39 of the United States Code and this subpart shall appear as Appendix A to this subpart.

(b) Availability of the Mail Classification Schedule. Copies of the Mail Classification Schedule, both current and previous issues, are available during regular business hours for reference and public inspection at the Postal Regulatory Commission's Reading Room located at 901 New York Avenue, NW., Suite 200, Washington, DC 20268-0001. The Mail Classification Schedule, both current and previous issues, also is available on the Internet at http://www.prc.gov.
