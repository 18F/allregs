CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 69  /  Sec. 69.153 Application of other laws.

(a) If a vessel is assigned two gross tonnages under Sec. 69.175(b), the higher gross tonnage is the tonnage used when applying inspection, manning, and load line laws and regulations to the vessel.

(b) Tonnage marks are not to be construed as additional load line marks. Whether or not a tonnage mark is submerged under Sec. 69.171 has no effect on the applicability of load line laws and regulations.
