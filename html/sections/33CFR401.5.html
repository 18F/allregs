CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 401  /  Sec. 401.5 Required equipment.

(a) No vessel shall transit unless it is (1) Propelled by motor power that is adequate in the opinion of an officer; and (2) Marked and equipped in accordance with the requirements of Sec. 401.6 to 401.21.
