CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 384  /  Sec. 384.224 Noncommercial motor vehicle violations.

The State must have and enforce laws and/or regulations applicable to drivers of non-CMVs, as defined in Sec. 383.5 of this subchapter, which meet the minimum requirements of Tables 1 and 2 to Sec. 383.51 of this subchapter. [67 FR 49762, July 31, 2002]
