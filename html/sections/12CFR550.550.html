CFR /  Title 12  /  Part 550  /  Sec. 550.550 May I recover my deposit from State authorities?

Upon issuance of the OTS written notice under Sec. 550.540, you may recover any securities deposited with State authorities, or a Federal Home Loan Bank, under subpart C of this part.
