CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 550  /  Sec. 550.1304 Overtime hourly rates of pay.

(a) For a firefighter who is covered by (i.e., nonexempt from) the overtime provisions of the Fair Labor Standards Act (FLSA), the overtime hourly rate of pay equals 1\1/2\ times the firefighter hourly rate of basic pay for that firefighter, as established under Sec. 550.1303(a) and (b)(2).

(b) For a firefighter who is exempt from the FLSA, the overtime hourly rate is computed as provided in Sec. 550.113(e).

(c) For any firefighter, overtime pay for any pay period is derived by multiplying the applicable overtime hourly rate by all overtime hours within that period.
