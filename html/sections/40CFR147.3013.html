CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 147  /  Sec. 147.3013 Information to be considered for Class I wells.

(a) In addition to the information listed in Sec. 146.14(a) of this chapter, the Director shall consider the following prior to issuing any Class I permit:

(1) Expected pressure changes, native fluid displacement, and direction of movement of the injected fluid; and

(2) Methods to be used for sampling, and for measurement and calculation of flow.

(b) In addition to the information listed in Sec. 146.14(b) of this chapter, the Director shall consider any information required under Sec. 146.14(a) of this chapter (as supplemented by this subpart) that has been gathered during construction.
