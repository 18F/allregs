CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 906  /  Sec. 906.35 Inapplicability of section 18 of the United States

The provisions of section 18 of the 1937 Act (42 U.S.C. 1437p) do not apply to disposition of public housing dwelling units under a homeownership program approved by HUD under this part, or to the sale of a unit to a PRE to operate as public housing and sell to a low-income family within 5 years, under the requirements of Sec. 906.19.
