CFR /  Title 19  /  Part 122  /  Sec. 122.185 Report of loss or theft of Customs access seal.

The loss or theft of an approved Customs access seal must be promptly reported in writing by the employee to the port director. The Customs access seal may be replaced, as provided in Sec. 122.182(f). [T.D. 02-40, 67 FR 48986, July 29, 2002]
