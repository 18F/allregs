CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 14  /  Sec. 14.30 Generally.

(a) The rules in this subpart regarding recordkeeping and enforcement are applicable to all manufacturers and service providers that are subject to the requirements of sections 255, 716, and 718 of the Act and parts 6, 7 and 14 of this chapter.

(b) The requirements set forth in Sec. 14.31 of this subpart shall be effective January 30, 2013.

(c) The requirements set forth in Secs. 14.32 through 14.37 of this subpart shall be effective on October 8, 2013.
