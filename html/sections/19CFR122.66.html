CFR /  Title 19  /  Part 122  /  Sec. 122.66 Clearance or permission to depart denied.

If advance electronic air cargo information is not received as provided in Sec. 192.14 of this chapter, Customs and Border Protection may deny clearance or permission for the aircraft to depart from the United States. [CBP Dec. 03-32, 68 FR 68173, Dec. 5, 2003]
