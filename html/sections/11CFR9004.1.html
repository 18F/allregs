CFR /  Title 11  /  Part 9004  /  Sec. 9004.1 Major parties.

The eligible candidates of each major party in a Presidential election shall be entitled to equal payments under 11 CFR part 9005 in an amount which, in the aggregate, shall not exceed $20,000,000 as adjusted by the Consumer Price Index in the manner described in 11 CFR 110.17(a). [56 FR 35919, July 29, 1991, as amended at 67 FR 78683, Dec. 26, 2002]
