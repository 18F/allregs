CFR /  Title 27  /  Part 20  /  Sec. 20.135 State code numbers.

In showing the permit number on labels as provided in Sec. 20.134(b)(2)(ii), the permittee who distributes the article may substitute the appropriate number shown below for the State abbreviation. For example, permit number SDA-CONN-1234 may be shown on the labels as SDA-07-1234.
