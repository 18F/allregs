CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 59  /  Sec. 59.5 Submissions of information and documents to VA.

All submissions of information and documents required to be presented to VA must be made, unless otherwise specified under this part, to the Director, Capital Asset Management and Support (10NA5), VA Central Office, 810 Vermont Avenue, NW., Washington, DC 20420. (Authority: 38 U.S.C. 101, 501, 1710, 1742, 8105, 8131-8137) [66 FR 33847, June 26, 2001, as amended at 78 FR 21265, Apr. 10, 2013]
