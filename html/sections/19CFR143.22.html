CFR /  Title 19  /  Part 143  /  Sec. 143.22 Formal entry may be required.

The port director may require a formal consumption or appraisement entry for any merchandise if deemed necessary for import admissibility enforcement purposes; revenue protection; or the efficient conduct of customs business. Individual shipments for the same consignee, when such shipments are valued at $2,500 or less, may be consolidated on one such entry. [CBP Dec. 12-19, 77 FR 72720, Dec. 6, 2012]
