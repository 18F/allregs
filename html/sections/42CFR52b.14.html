CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 52b  /  Sec. 52b.14 Other federal laws, regulations, executive orders, and

Other federal laws, regulations, executive orders, and policies apply to grants under this part. These include, but are not necessarily limited to:

(a) Laws.

An Act to Provide for the Preservation of Historical and Archeological Data (and other purposes), as amended (16 U.S.C. 469 et seq.). Architectural Barriers Act of 1968, as amended (42 U.S.C. 4151 et seq.).Earthquake Hazards Reduction Act of 1977, as amended (42 U.S.C. 7701 et seq.).Flood Disaster Protection Act of 1973, section 202, as amended (42 U.S.C. 4106).National Historic Preservation Act, section 106, as amended (16 U.S.C. 470f).Safe Drinking Water Act, as amended (42 U.S.C. 300f et seq.).
