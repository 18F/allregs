CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 1332  /  Sec. 1332.2 Availability of contracts.

Upon request from any member of the public to inspect a contract(s) or agreement(s) described in Sec. 1332.1, at any time between the effective date of such contract(s) or agreement(s) and 15 days prior thereto, the Board will obtain the requested contract(s) or agreement(s) from the U.S. Postal Service and make it (them) available for inspection. [57 FR 23539, June 4, 1992]
