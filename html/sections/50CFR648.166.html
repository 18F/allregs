CFR /  Title 50  /  Part 648  /  Sec. 648.166 Bluefish gear restrictions.

If the MAFMC determines through its annual review or framework adjustment process that gear restrictions are necessary to ensure that the ACL is not exceeded, or to attain other FMP objectives, such measures, subject to the gear other than trawls and gillnets restrictions in Sec. 648.162 regarding specifications, will be enacted through the procedure specified in Sec. 648.162(c) or 648.167. [76 FR 60641, Sept. 29, 2011]
