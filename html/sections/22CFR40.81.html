CFR /  Title 22  /  Part 40  /  Sec. 40.81 Ineligible for citizenship.

An alien will be ineligible to receive an immigrant visa under INA 212(a)(8)(A) if the alien is ineligible for citizenship, including as provided in INA 314 or 315. [64 FR 55418, Oct. 13, 1999]
