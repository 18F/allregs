CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 890  /  Sec. 890.605 Persons confined on effective date.

Benefits may not be limited for persons who, on the effective date of an enrollment under Sec. 890.602, are confined in a hospital or institution. [43 FR 35018, Aug. 8, 1978] Subpart G [Reserved]
