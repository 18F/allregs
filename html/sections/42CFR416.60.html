CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 416  /  Sec. 416.60 General rules.

(a) The services payable under this part are facility services furnished to Medicare beneficiaries, by a participating facility, in connection with covered surgical procedures specified in Sec. 416.65.

(b) The surgical procedures, including all preoperative and post-operative services that are performed by a physician, are covered as physician services under part 410 of this chapter. [56 FR 8844, Mar. 1, 1991]
