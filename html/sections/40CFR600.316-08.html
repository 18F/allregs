CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 600  /  Sec. 600.316-08 Multistage manufacture.

Where more than one person is the manufacturer of a vehicle, the final stage manufacturer (as defined in 49 CFR 529.3) is treated as the vehicle manufacturer for purposes of compliance with this subpart. [76 FR 39566, July 6, 2011]
