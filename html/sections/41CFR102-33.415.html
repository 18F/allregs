CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 102-33  /  Sec. 102-33.415 When may we declassify a Federal aircraft and remove

When an aircraft is lost or destroyed, or is otherwise non-operational and you want to retain it, you may declassify it and remove it from your Federal aircraft inventory. For further details, see Sec. Sec. 102-33.250 and 102-33.420. See Sec. Sec. 102-33.265 and 102-33.270 for reporting excess Federal aircraft.
