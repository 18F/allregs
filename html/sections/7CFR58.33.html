CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 58  /  Sec. 58.33 Who may be licensed.

Any person processing proper qualifications, as determined by an examination for competency, held at such time and in such manner as may be prescribed by the Administrator, may be licensed to perform specified inspection or grading service. Each license issued shall be signed by the Administrator. [53 FR 20278, June 3, 1988]
