CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 453  /  Sec. 453.3 Detention orders and other orders.

(a) The terms of any detention order or other order issued under Sec. 453.1, to the maximum extent practicable, make provisions to avoid loss or damage to cargo.

(b) Written notice of any detention order or other order issued under Sec. 453.1 is given immediately to the terminal operator, stevedore, or other person having actual control over the container involved. Prompt notification is also given to the owner of the container, or his agent. The notification identifies the container involved, its location, and describes the condition which gave rise to the order. [45 FR 37217, June 2, 1980]
