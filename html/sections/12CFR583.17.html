CFR /  Title 12  /  Part 583  /  Sec. 583.17 Qualified thrift lender.

The term qualified thrift lender means a financial institution that meets the appropriate qualified thrift lender test set forth in 12 U.S.C. 1467a(m). [54 FR 49707, Nov. 30, 1989, as amended at 60 FR 66870, Dec. 27, 1995]
