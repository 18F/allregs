CFR /  Title 9  /  Part 590  /  Sec. 590.419 Reuse of containers bearing official identification

The reuse, by any person, of containers bearing official identification is prohibited unless such identification is applicable in all respects to product being packed therein. In such instances, the container and label may be used provided the packaging is accomplished under the supervision of an inspector and the container is in compliance with Sec. 590.504(k).
