CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 1508  /  Sec. 1508.10 Environmental document.

Environmental document includes the documents specified in Sec. 1508.9 (environmental assessment), Sec. 1508.11 (environmental impact statement), Sec. 1508.13 (finding of no significant impact), and Sec. 1508.22 (notice of intent).
