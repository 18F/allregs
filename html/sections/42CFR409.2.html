CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 409  /  Sec. 409.2 Scope.

Subparts A through G of this part describe the benefits available under Medicare Part A and set forth the limitations on those benefits, including certain amounts of payment for which beneficiaries are responsible. [48 FR 12541, Mar. 25, 1983, as amended at 50 FR 33033, Aug. 16, 1985]
