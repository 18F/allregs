CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 77  /  Sec. 77.1300 Explosives and blasting.

(a) No explosives, blasting agent, detonator, or any other related blasting device or material shall be stored, transported, carried, handled, charged, fired, destroyed, or otherwise used, employed or disposed of by any person at a coal mine except in accordance with the provisions of Sec. Sec. 77.1301 through 77.1304, inclusive.

(b) The term ``explosives'' as used in this subpart N includes blasting agents. The standards in this subpart N in which the term ``explosives'' appears are applicable to blasting agents (as well as to other explosives) unless blasting agents are expressly excluded.
