CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 225  /  225.7605 Solicitation provision.

Unless an exception at 225.7603 applies or a waiver has been granted in accordance with 225.7604, use the provision at 252.225-7031, Secondary Arab Boycott of Israel, in all solicitations, including solicitations using FAR part 12 procedures for the acquisition of commercial items. If the solicitation includes the provision at FAR 52.204-7, do not separately list 252.225-7031 in the solicitation. [71 FR 39006, July 11, 2006, as amended at 78 FR 37988, June 25, 2013; 78 FR 40043, July 3, 2013]
