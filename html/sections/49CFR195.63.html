CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 195  /  Sec. 195.63 OMB control number assigned to information collection.

The control numbers assigned by the Office of Management and Budget to the hazardous liquid pipeline information collection pursuant to the Paperwork Reduction Act are 2137-0047, 2137-0601, 2137-0604, 2137-0605, 2137-0618, and 2137-0622. [Amdt. 195-95, 75 FR 72907, Nov. 26, 2010]
