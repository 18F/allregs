CFR /  Title 22  /  Part 12  /  Sec. 12.1 No cognizance taken of complaint.

The Department of State will take no cognizance of a complaint against an employee by an alleged creditor, so far as the complainant is concerned, beyond acknowledging receipt of his communication. (Sec. 4, 63 Stat. 111, as amended; 22 U.S.C. 2658) [22 FR 10789, Dec. 27, 1957]
