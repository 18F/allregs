CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 4  /  Sec. 4.81 Judicial review.

Action taken pursuant to section 602 of the Civil Rights Act of 1964 is subject to judicial review as provided in section 603 of that Act. [40 FR 8778, Mar. 3, 1975]
