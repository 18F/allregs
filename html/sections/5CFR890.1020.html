CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 890  /  Sec. 890.1020 Determining length of debarment based on false,

Debarments under 5 U.S.C. 8902a(c)(4) and (5) and 5 U.S.C. 8902a(d)(1) and (2) shall be for a period of 3 years, subject to adjustment based on the aggravating and mitigating factors listed in Sec. 890.1016.
