CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 28  /  Sec. 28.4 Classing offices.

Classing Offices shall be maintained at points designated by the Administrator. Requests for the review of the classification and/or comparison of cotton performed by Classing Offices may be referred to the Quality Control Section. [52 FR 30881, Aug. 18, 1987] Sec. Sec. 28.5-28.6 [Reserved]
