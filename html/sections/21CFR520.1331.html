CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 520  /  Sec. 520.1331 Meclofenamic acid tablets.

(a) Specifications. Each tablet contains either 10 or 20 milligrams of meclofenamic acid.

(b) Sponsor. See No. 054771 in Sec. 510.600(c) of this chapter.

(c) Conditions of use in dogs--

(1) Amount. 1.1 milligrams per kilogram (0.5 milligram per pound) daily for 5 to 7 days.

(2) Indications for use. For the relief of signs and symptoms of chronic inflammatory disease involving the musculoskeletal system.

(3) Limitations. Federal law restricts this drug to use by or on the order of a licensed veterinarian. [50 FR 43385, Oct. 25, 1985, as amended at 53 FR 23390, June 22, 1988; 78 FR 28824, May 20, 2014]
