CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 405  /  Sec. 405.381 Notice of dismissal of a request for a hearing before

We will mail a written notice of the dismissal of the hearing request to you at your last known address. The notice will tell you that you may ask the Appeals Council to review the dismissal and will explain your right to representation. Your request for review by the Appeals Council must be in writing and must be filed within 60 days after the date that you receive notice of the dismissal.
