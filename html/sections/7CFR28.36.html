CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 28  /  Sec. 28.36 Order of classification.

All samples for which classification requests are pending shall be classified, as far as practicable, in the order in which the samples are delivered for classification. When in the opinion of the Area Director there is a need to deviate from this order of classification, the Area Director shall designate which samples will be given priority in classification. [52 FR 30882, Aug. 18, 1987]
