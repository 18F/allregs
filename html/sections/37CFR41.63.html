CFR /  Title 37  /  Part 41  /  Sec. 41.63 Amendments and affidavits or other evidence after appeal.

(a) Amendments filed after the date of filing an appeal pursuant to Sec. 41.61 canceling claims may be admitted where such cancellation does not affect the scope of any other pending claim in the proceeding.

(b)(1).

(c) Affidavits or other evidence filed after the date of filing an appeal pursuant to Sec. 41.61 will not be admitted except as permitted by reopening prosecution under Sec. 41.77(b)(1).
