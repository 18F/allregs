CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 866  /  Sec. 866.4070 RNA Preanalytical Systems.

(a) Identification. RNA Preanalytical Systems are devices intended to collect, store, and transport patient specimens, and stabilize intracellular RNA from the specimens, for subsequent isolation and purification of the intracellular RNA for RT-PCR used in in vitro molecular diagnostic testing.

(b) Classification. Class II (special controls). The special control is FDA's guidance document entitled ``Class II Special Controls Guidance Document: RNA Preanalytical Systems (RNA Collection, Stabilization and Purification System for RT-PCR Used in Molecular Diagnostic Testing).'' See Sec. 866.1(e) for the availability of this guidance document. [70 FR 49863, Aug. 25, 2005]
