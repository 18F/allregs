CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 97  /  Sec. 97.201 Purpose.

This subpart and subparts BBB through III set forth the general provisions and the designated representative, permitting, allowance, monitoring, and opt-in provisions for the Federal Clean Air Interstate Rule (CAIR) SO2 Trading Program, under section 110 of the Clean Air Act and Sec. 52.36 of this chapter, as a means of mitigating interstate transport of fine particulates and sulfur dioxide.
