CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 843  /  Sec. 843.310 Annuity based on death of an employee.

Except as provided in Sec. 843.312, if an employee dies after completing at least 10 years of service, a current spouse is entitled to an annuity equal to 50 percent of the annuity computed under subpart D of part 842 of this chapter (without reduction for age), with respect to the employee. The annuity is in addition to the benefit described in Sec. 843.309. [52 FR 2074, Jan. 16, 1987, as amended at 52 FR 23014, June 17, 1987]
