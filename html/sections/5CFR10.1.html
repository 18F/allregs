CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 10  /  Sec. 10.1 Definitions.

For purposes of this rule--

(a) 'Agency' means an Executive agency as defined in Rule IX, but does not include a Government corporation or the General Accounting Office; and (b) 'Merit system principles' means the principles for Federal personnel management that are set forth in section 2301(b) of title 5, United States Code.
