CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 845  /  Sec. 845.1 Scope.

This part covers the assessment of civil penalties under section 518 of the Act with respect to cessation orders and notices of violation issued under part 843 (Federal Enforcement), except for the assessment of individual civil penalties under section 518(f), which is covered in part 846. [53 FR 3675, Feb. 8, 1988]
