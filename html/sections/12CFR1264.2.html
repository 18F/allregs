CFR /  Title 12  /  Part 1264  /  Sec. 1264.2 Bank authority to make advances to housing associates.

Subject to the provisions of the Bank Act and part 950 of this title, a Bank may make advances to an entity that is not a member of the Bank if the Bank has certified the entity as a housing associate under the provisions of this part. [65 FR 44426, July 18, 2000, as amended at 75 FR 8240, Feb. 24, 2010]
