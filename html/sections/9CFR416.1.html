CFR /  Title 9  /  Part 416  /  Sec. 416.1 General rules.

Each official establishment must be operated and maintained in a manner sufficient to prevent the creation of insanitary conditions and to ensure that product is not adulterated. [64 FR 56417, Oct. 20, 1999]
