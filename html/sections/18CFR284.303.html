CFR /  Title 18  /  Part 284  /  Sec. 284.303 OCS blanket certificates.

Every OCS pipeline [as that term is defined in Sec. 284.302(b)] is required to provide open-access, nondiscriminatory transportation service pursuant to a blanket transportation certificate issued under subpart G of this part. [Order 559, 58 FR 52663, Oct. 12, 1993]
