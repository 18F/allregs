CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 3604  /  Sec. 3604.27 What rights does a free use permit give me against

Permits that BLM issues under this subpart constitute a superior right to remove the materials in accordance with the permit terms and provisions, as against any claim to or entry of the lands made after the date BLM designated the tract for mineral materials disposal. See Sec. 3602.12.
