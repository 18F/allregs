CFR /  Title 25  /  Part 183  /  Sec. 183.12 How long will it take to receive a decision?

Within 30 days of receiving the information required by Sec. 183.11 we will approve your request if it complies with the Settlement Act and this part. If we disapprove your request we will do so in writing and will provide you with the reasons for disapproval.
