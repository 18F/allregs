CFR /  Title 11  /  Part 111  /  Sec. 111.30 When will subpart B apply?

Subpart B applies to violations of the reporting requirements of 52 U.S.C. 30104(a) committed by political committees and their treasurers that relate to the reporting periods that begin on or after July 14, 2000, and that end on or before the date specified by 52 U.S.C. 30109(a)(4)(C)(v). This subpart, however, does not apply to reports that relate to reporting periods that end between January 1, 2014, and January 21, 2014. [79 FR 3303, Jan. 21, 2014, as amended at 79 FR 77848, Dec. 29, 2014]
