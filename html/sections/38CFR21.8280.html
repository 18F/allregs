CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 21  /  Sec. 21.8280 Effective date of induction into a vocational training program.

Subject to the limitations in Sec. 21.8022, the date an eligible child is inducted into a vocational training program will be the date the child first begins to receive training, services, or assistance under an individualized written plan of vocational rehabilitation. (Authority: 38 U.S.C. 1804(c), (d), 1814)
