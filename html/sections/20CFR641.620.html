CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 641  /  Sec. 641.620 How may an organization apply for pilot, demonstration,

Organizations applying for pilot, demonstration, and evaluation project funding must follow the instructions issued by the Department. Instructions for these unique funding opportunities are published in TEGLs available at http://www.doleta.gov/Seniors.
