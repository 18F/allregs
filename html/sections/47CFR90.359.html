CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 90  /  Sec. 90.359 Field strength limits for EA-licensed LMS systems.

EA-licensed multilateration systems shall limit the field strength of signals transmitted from their base stations to 47 dBuV/m at their EA boundary. [62 FR 52044, Oct. 6, 1997]
