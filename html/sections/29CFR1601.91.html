CFR /  Title 29  /  Part 1601  /  Sec. 1601.91 Request for title VII interpretation or opinion.

Any interested person desiring a written title VII interpretation or opinion from the Commission may make such a request. However, issuance of title VII interpretations or opinions is discretionary. [56 FR 9625, Mar. 7, 1991]
