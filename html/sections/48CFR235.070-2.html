CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 235  /  235.070-2 Indemnification under contracts involving both research and

These contracts may provide for indemnification under the authority of both 10 U.S.C. 2354 and Public Law 85-804. Public Law 85-804 will apply only to work to which 10 U.S.C. 2354 does not apply. Actions under Public Law 85-804 must also comply with FAR 50.104-3. [56 FR 36416, July 31, 1991, as amended at 78 FR 21850, Apr. 12, 2013]
