CFR /  Title 12  /  Part 622  /  Sec. 622.58 Hearing on assessment.

(a) Time and place. An institution or person requesting a hearing will be informed by order of the Board of the time and place set for hearing.

(b) Answer; procedures. The hearing order may require the institution or person requesting the hearing to file an answer as prescribed in Sec. 622.5 of subpart A. The procedures of the Administrative Procedure Act (5 U.S.C. 554-557) and subpart A of these rules will apply to the hearing. [51 FR 21139, June 11, 1986, as amended at 70 FR 12585, Mar. 15, 2005]
