CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 301-11  /  Sec. 301-11.301 Who in my agency can authorize/approve my request for

Any official designated by the head of your agency (see Sec. 301-70.201 for when an agency can issue a blanket actual expense authorization). [FTR Amdt. 70, 63 FR 15961, Apr. 1, 1998, as amended by FTR Amdt. 2011-03, 76 FR 55275, Sept. 7, 2011]
