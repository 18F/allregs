CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 658  /  Sec. 658.426 Complaints against USES.

Complaints alleging that an ETA regional office or the national office of USES has violated JS regulations should be mailed to the Assistant Secretary for Employment and Training, U.S. Department of Labor, Washington, DC 20210. Such complaints should include:

(a) The allegations of wrong-doing, (b) the date of the incident, (c) location of the incident, (d) who the complaint is against, and (e) any other relevant information available to the complainant. The Assistant Secretary or the Regional Administrator as designated shall make a determination and respond to the complainant after investigation of the complaint.
