CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 1210  /  Sec. 1210.51 Who must submit royalty reports?

(a) Any person who pays royalty to ONRR must submit royalty reports to ONRR.

(b) Before you pay or report to ONRR, you must obtain a payor code. To obtain a payor code, refer to the ONRR Minerals Revenue Reporter Handbook for instructions and ONRR contact information (also see Sec. 1210.56 for information on how to obtain a handbook).
