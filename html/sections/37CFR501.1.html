CFR /  Title 37  /  Part 501  /  Sec. 501.1 Purpose.

The purpose of this part is to provide for the administration of a uniform patent policy for the Government with respect to the rights in inventions made by Government employees and to prescribe rules and regulations for implementing and effectuating such policy. [61 FR 40999, Aug. 7, 1996]
