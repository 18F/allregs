CFR /  Title 26  /  Part 403  /  Sec. 403.65 Authority for destruction.

The Commissioner or his delegate is authorized to order the destruction of any coin-operated gaming device as defined in I.R.C. section 4462 upon which a tax is imposed by I.R.C. section 4461, after the expiration of three months from the date of consummation of administrative forfeiture under any provision of I.R.C. (Sec. 7326, 72 Stat. 1429, as amended (26 U.S.C. 7326))
