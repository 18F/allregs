CFR /  Title 37  /  Part 352  /  Sec. 352.3 Final determinations.

Unless a motion for a rehearing is timely filed within 15 days, the determination by the Copyright Royalty Judges pursuant to 17 U.S.C. 803(c) in a proceeding is final when it is issued. [71 FR 53330, Sept. 11, 2006]
