CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 252  /  252.216-7002 Alternate A, Time-and-Materials/Labor-Hour ProposalCompetition.

As prescribed in 216.601(e), substitute the following paragraph (c) for paragraph (c) of the provision at FAR 52.216-29:

Alternate A, Time-and-Materials/Labor-Hour Proposal Requirements--Non-

Commercial Item Acquisition With Adequate Price Competition (FEB 2007)

(c) The offeror must establish fixed hourly rates using separate rates for each category of labor to be performed by each subcontractor and for each category of labor to be performed by the offeror, and for each category of labor to be transferred between divisions, subsidiaries, or affiliates of the offeror under a common control. [71 FR 74471, Dec. 12, 2006]
