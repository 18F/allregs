CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 3278  /  Sec. 3278.11 When I submit confidential, proprietary information, how

When you submit data and information that you believe to be exempt from disclosure under part 2 of this title, you must clearly mark each page that you believe contains confidential information. BLM will keep all data and information confidential to the extent allowed by Sec. 2.13(c) of this title.
