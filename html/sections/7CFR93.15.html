CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 93  /  Sec. 93.15 Fees for analytical testing of oilseeds.

The fee charged for any laboratory analysis for oilseeds shall be obtained from the Laboratory Director for aflatoxin laboratories at the Dothan administrative office as listed in 7 CFR 93.14(a). [65 FR 64318, Oct. 26, 2000]
