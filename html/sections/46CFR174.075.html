CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 174  /  Sec. 174.075 Compartments assumed flooded: general.

The individual flooding of each of the compartments described in Sec. Sec. 174.080 and 174.085 must be assumed for the purpose of determining compliance with Sec. 174.065 (a). Simultaneous flooding of more than one compartment must be assumed only when indicated in Sec. Sec. 174.080 and 174.085.
