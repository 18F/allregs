CFR /  Title 12  /  Part 1006  /  Sec. 1006.5 Public notice of filing.

In connection with any application that has been filed in accordance with the requirements of Sec. Sec. 1006.2 and 1006.3 of this part and following initial review of the application, a notice of such filing shall be published by the Bureau in the Federal Register, and a copy of such application shall be made available for examination by interested persons during business hours at the Bureau of Consumer Financial Protection, 1700 G Street NW., Washington, DC 20006. A period of time shall be allowed from the date of such publication for interested parties to submit written comments to the Bureau regarding that application.
