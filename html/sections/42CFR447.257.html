CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 447  /  Sec. 447.257 FFP: Conditions relating to institutional reimbursement.

FFP is not available for a State's expenditures for hospital inpatient or long-term care facility services that are in excess of the amounts allowable under this subpart. [52 FR 28147, July 28, 1987]
