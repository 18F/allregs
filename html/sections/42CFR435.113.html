CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 435  /  Sec. 435.113 Individuals who are ineligible for AFDC because of

The agency must provide Medicaid to:

(a)(17)(D) of the Act;

(1) Stepparents who are not legally liable for support of stepchildren under a State law of general applicability;

(2) Grandparents;

(3) Legal guardians;

(4) Alien sponsors who are not organizations; and

(5) Siblings.

(b) [Reserved] [58 FR 4926, Jan. 19, 1993, as amended at 59 FR 43052, Aug. 22, 1994]
