CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 101  /  Sec. 101.1 Scope of rules.

The rules of procedure in this part supplement Sec. Sec. 100.9 and 100.10 of this subtitle and govern the practice for hearings, decisions, and administrative review conducted by the Department of Education, pursuant to Title VI of the Civil Rights Act of 1964 (section 602, 78 Stat. 252) and part 100 of this subtitle.
