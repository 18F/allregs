CFR /  Title 27  /  Part 41  /  Sec. 41.73 Notice for cigars.

Before removal subject to internal revenue tax, every package of cigars, except as provided in Sec. 41.75, shall have adequately imprinted on it, or on a label securely affixed to it--

(a) The designation ``cigars'';

(b) The quantity of cigars contained in the package; and

(c) For small cigars, the classification of the product for tax purposes (i.e., either ``small'' or ``little''). (Sec. 202, Pub. L. 85-859, 72 Stat. 1422 (26 U.S.C. 5723)) [T.D. ATF-80, 46 FR 18310, Mar. 24, 1981. Redesignated and amended by T.D. TTB-16, 69 FR 52424, 52425, Aug. 26, 2004]
