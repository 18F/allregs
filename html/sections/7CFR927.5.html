CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 927  /  Sec. 927.5 Size.

Size means the number of pears which can be packed in a 44-pound net weight standard box or container equivalent, or as ``size'' means the greatest transverse diameter of the pear taken at right angles to a line running from the stem to the blossom end, or such other specifications more specifically defined in a regulation issued under this part.
