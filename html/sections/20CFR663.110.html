CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 663  /  Sec. 663.110 What are the eligibility criteria for core services for

To be eligible to receive core services as an adult in the adult and dislocated worker programs, an individual must be 18 years of age or older. To be eligible for the dislocated worker programs, an eligible adult must meet the criteria of Sec. 663.115. Eligibility criteria for intensive and training services are found at Sec. Sec. 663.220 and 663.310.
