CFR /  Title 27  /  Part 20  /  Sec. 20.118 Rubbing alcohol general-use formula.

(a) Rubbing alcohol is an article made with S.D.A. Formula No. 23-H (1) containing 70% ethyl alcohol by volume (2) made in accordance with one of the two formulas prescribed in paragraph (b) of this section, and (3) labeled in accordance with Sec. 20.134(e) of this part.

(b) Either of the following two formulas is approved for manufacturing rubbing alcohol:
