CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 302-7  /  Sec. 302-7.4 Who pays for shipping professional books, papers, and

The agency may pay for shipping PBP&E; as a discretionary item. When authorized, shipping PBP&E; is considered an administrative cost to the agency. However, for ease of administration in calculating this allowance, PBP&E; should be included as part of the HHG shipment, if possible. That is, if the net weight of the HHG plus the PBP&E; is less than 18,000 pounds, the agency should ship the items together and pay for the HHG shipment in one payment. [FTR Amdt. 2011-01, 76 FR 18339, Apr. 1, 2011]
