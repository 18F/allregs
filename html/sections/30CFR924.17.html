CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 924  /  Sec. 924.17 State regulatory program provisions and amendments

The proposed language in section 53-9-55(3), as submitted by Mississippi on May 6, 1997, that allows the commission to promulgate regulations regarding a waiver from the requirement to post a penalty payment bond upon a showing by the operator of an inability to post the bond is disapproved. [63 FR 1362, Jan. 9, 1998]
