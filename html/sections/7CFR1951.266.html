CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 1951  /  Sec. 1951.266 Special requirements for MFH borrowers.

All requirements of 7 CFR part 3560, subpart K must be met prior to graduation and acceptance of the full payment from an MFH borrower. [69 FR 69105, Nov. 26, 2004] Sec. Sec. 1951.267-1951.299 [Reserved]
