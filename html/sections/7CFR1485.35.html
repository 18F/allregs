CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 1485  /  Sec. 1485.35 Suspension, termination, and closeout of agreements.

A program agreement may be suspended or terminated in accordance with the suspension and termination procedures in 2 CFR part 200. If an agreement is terminated, the applicable regulations in 2 CFR part 200 will apply to the closeout of the agreement. [79 FR 75997, Dec. 19, 2014]
