CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 67  /  Sec. 67.257 Requirements for assignments of notices of claim of lien.

An assignment of a notice of claim of lien must:

(a) Be signed by or on behalf of each original claimant or last assignee of record; and

(b) Recite the following:

(1) The name and address of each claimant; and

(2) The name and address of each assignee and the interest in the claim being assigned.
