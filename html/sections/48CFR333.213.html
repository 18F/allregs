CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 333  /  333.213 Obligation to continue performance.

(a) The Contracting Officer shall use the Disputes clause at FAR 52.233-1 without the use of Alternate I. However, if the Contracting Officer determines that the Government's interest would be better served by use of paragraph (i) in Alternate I, the HCA or CCO shall approve its use.
