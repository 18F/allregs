CFR /  Title 29  /  Part 452  /  Sec. 452.2 Application of union constitution and bylaws.

Elections required to be held as provided in title IV are to be conducted in accordance with the validly adopted constitution and bylaws of the labor organizations insofar as they are not inconsistent with the provisions of the Act. [38 FR 18324, July 9, 1973, as amended at 63 FR 33780, June 19, 1998]
