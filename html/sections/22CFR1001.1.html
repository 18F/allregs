CFR /  Title 22  /  Part 1001  /  Sec. 1001.1 Cross-references to employee ethical conduct standards

Directors and other employees of the Inter-American Foundation should refer to the Standards of Ethical Conduct for Employees of the Executive Branch at 5 CFR part 2635, the Inter-American Foundation regulations at 5 CFR part 7301 which supplement the executive branch standards, and the executive branch financial disclosure regulations at 5 CFR part 2634. [59 FR 3772, Jan. 27, 1994]
