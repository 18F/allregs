CFR /  Title 13  /  Part 108  /  Sec. 108.470 SBA approval of merger, consolidation, or reorganization

You may not merge, consolidate, change form of organization (corporation or partnership) or reorganize without SBA's prior written approval. Any such merger or consolidation will be subject to Sec. 108.440.
