CFR /  Title 36  /  Part 272  /  Sec. 272.2 Use of official campaign materials.

Official materials produced for the Woodsy Owl campaign may be used without express approval from the Chief of the Forest Service where such use is solely for the purpose of increasing public knowledge about wise use of the environment and programs which foster maintenance and improvement of environmental quality. [40 FR 12641, Mar. 20, 1975]
