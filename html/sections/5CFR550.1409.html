CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 550  /  Sec. 550.1409 Inapplicability of premium pay and aggregate pay caps.

Accrued compensatory time off under this subpart is not considered in applying the premium pay limitations established under 5 U.S.C. 5547 and 5 CFR 550.105 through 550.107 or the aggregate limitation on pay established under 5 U.S.C. 5307 and 5 CFR part 530, subpart B. Subpart O_Flag Recognition Benefit for Fallen Federal Civilian Employees

Authority: 5 U.S.C. 5570 note; also issued under Sec. 2 of Pub. L. 112-73, 125 Stat.784-785.

Source: 79 FR 53602, Sept. 10, 2014, unless otherwise noted.
