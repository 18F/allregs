CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 97  /  Sec. 97.324 CAIR permit revisions.

Except as provided in Sec. 97.323(b), the permitting authority will revise the CAIR permit, as necessary, in accordance with the permitting authority's title V operating permits regulations or the permitting authority's regulations for other federally enforceable permits as applicable addressing permit revisions.
