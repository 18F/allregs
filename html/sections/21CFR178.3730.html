CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 178  /  Sec. 178.3730 Piperonyl butoxide and pyrethrins as components of

Piperonyl butoxide in combination with pyrethrins may be safely used for insect control on bags that are intended for use in contact with dried feed in compliance with Secs. 561.310 and 561.340 of this chapter, or that are intended for use in contact with dried food in compliance with Secs. 193.60 and 193.390 of this chapter.
