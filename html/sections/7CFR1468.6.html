CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 1468  /  Sec. 1468.6 Practice eligibility provisions.

(a) Practices may be eligible for payment under CFO if the conservation practice specified in the conservation farm plan is determined to be an eligible practice, as determined by the Chief, in accordance with:

(1) 7 CFR part 1410 for land retirement rental payments and practices that are eligible under CRP;

(2) 7 CFR part 1467 for wetland restoration or protection practices that are eligible under WRP; or

(3) 7 CFR part 1466 for conservation practices that are eligible under EQIP.

(b) For practices that are installed on retired land, the CRP cost-share rate for practices must be utilized.
