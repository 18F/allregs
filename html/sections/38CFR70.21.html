CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 70  /  Sec. 70.21 Where to apply.

Claimants for beneficiary travel must submit the information required in Sec. 70.20 to the Chief of the Business Office or other designee at the VA medical facility responsible for the medical care or services being provided and for which travel is required. (Authority: 38 U.S.C. 101, 111, 501, 1701, 1714, 1720, 1728, 1782, 1783, E.O. 11302)
