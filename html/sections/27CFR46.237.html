CFR /  Title 27  /  Part 46  /  Sec. 46.237 Controlled group member.

If the dealer is a member of a controlled group, but has its own employer identification number, the dealer must file a separate floor stocks tax return. The dealer may take the tax credit referred to in Sec. 46.223 if it is apportioned to the dealer as a member of the controlled group.
