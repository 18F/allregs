CFR /  Title 50  /  Part 216  /  Sec. 216.46 U.S. citizens on foreign flag vessels operating under

The MMPA's provisions do not apply to a citizen of the United States who incidentally takes any marine mammal during fishing operations in the ETP which are outside the U.S. exclusive economic zone (as defined in section 3 of the Magnuson-Stevens Fishery Conservation and Management Act (16 U.S.C. 1802)), while employed on a fishing vessel of a harvesting nation that is participating in, and in compliance with, the IDCP. [65 FR 56, Jan. 3, 2000]
