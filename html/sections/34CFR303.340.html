CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 303  /  Sec. 303.340 Individualized family service plan--general.

For each infant or toddler with a disability, the lead agency must ensure the development, review, and implementation of an individualized family service plan or IFSP developed by a multidisciplinary team, which includes the parent, that--

(a) Is consistent with the definition of that term in Sec. 303.20; and

(b) Meets the requirements in Secs. 303.342 through 303.346 of this subpart. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1435(a)(4), 1436) Sec. 303.341 [Reserved]
