CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 250  /  Sec. 250.520 How often do I have to monitor for casing pressure?

You must monitor for casing pressure in your well according to the following table: ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

with a minimum one pressure data

(a) fixed platform wells, monthly, month for each casing.(b) subsea wells, continuously, day for the production casing.(c) hybrid wells, continuously, day for each riser and/or the

production casing.(d) wells operating under a casing pressure daily, day for each casing.

request on a manned fixed platform,

(e) wells operating under a casing pressure weekly, week for each casing.
