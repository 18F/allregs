CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 405  /  Sec. 405.305 Availability of a hearing before an administrative law judge.

You may request a hearing before an administrative law judge if you are dissatisfied with the reconsidered determination on your disability claim or an initial determination subject to a hearing by an administrative law judge under the procedures in this part as a result of Secs. 404.906(b)(4) or 416.1406(b)(4) of this chapter. [76 FR 24808, May 3, 2011]
