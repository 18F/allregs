CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 1351  /  Sec. 1351.13 What are the Federal and non-Federal match requirements

HHS requires a non-Federal share which is equal to at least 10 percent of the Federal funds that will be received under this grant program for any fiscal year. [54 FR 20855, May 15, 1989; 55 FR 5601, Feb. 16, 1990]
