CFR /  Title 29  /  Part 98  /  Sec. 98.430 How do I check to see if a person is excluded or

You check to see if a person is excluded or disqualified in two ways:

(a) You as an agency official must check the EPLS when you take any action listed in Sec. 98.425.

(b) You must review information that a participant gives you, as required by Sec. 98.335, about its status or the status of the principals of a transaction.
