CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 300-70  /  Sec. 300-70.3 How long will we have to respond to the travel survey?

The survey will specify the due date. The head of your agency must appoint a designee at the headquarters level responsible for ensuring that the survey is completed and returned to GSA by the due date. Upon receiving a survey, you must submit the designee's name, address, and telephone number to the Director, Travel Management Policy Division (MTT), Office of Governmentwide Policy, General Services Administration, Washington, DC 20405. [FTR Amdt. 70, 63 FR 15953, Apr. 1, 1998, as amended by FTR Amdt. 2007- 05, 72 FR 61537, Oct. 31, 2007]
