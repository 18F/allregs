CFR /  Title 32  /  Part 1624  /  Sec. 1624.7 Expiration of deferment or exemption.

The Director shall issue an Order to Report for Induction to a registrant who is liable for induction whenever his deferment or exemption expires. [52 FR 24455, July 1, 1987]
