CFR /  Title 29  /  Part 90  /  Sec. 90.34 Notice procedures.

Formal notice of a certification, negative determination, or termination shall be transmitted promptly to the group of workers concerned and to all State workforce agencies concerned whenever such notices are published in the Federal Register.
