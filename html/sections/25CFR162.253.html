CFR /  Title 25  /  Part 162  /  Sec. 162.253 Will BIA's regulations concerning appeal bonds apply to

(a) The appeal bond provisions in Sec. 2.5 of part 2 of this chapter will not apply to appeals from lease cancellation decisions made under Sec. 162.252 of this subpart. Instead, when we decide to cancel an agricultural lease, we may require that the tenant post an appeal bond with an appeal of the cancellation decision. The requirement to post an appeal bond will apply in addition to all of the other requirements in part 2 of this chapter.

(b) An appeal bond should be set in an amount necessary to protect the Indian landowners against financial losses that will likely result from the delay caused by an appeal. Appeal bond requirements will not be separately appealable, but may be contested during the appeal of the lease cancellation decision.
