CFR /  Title 50  /  Part 648  /  Sec. 648.3 Relation to other laws.

(a) The relation of this part to other laws is set forth in Sec. 600.705.

(b) Nothing in these regulations supersedes more restrictive state management measures for any of the species referenced in Sec. 648.1 and, for Atlantic salmon, more restrictive local management measures.
