CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 364  /  Sec. 364.57 What functions and responsibilities may the State delegate?

A DSU may carry out the functions and responsibilities described in Secs. 364.50, 364.51 (subject to 364.43(d)), 364.52, 364.53, and 364.56 or, except as otherwise provided, may delegate these functions and responsibilities to the appropriate service provider with which the DSU subgrants or contracts to provide IL services. (Authority: 29 U.S.C. 711(c), 796c(f) and 796e-2)
