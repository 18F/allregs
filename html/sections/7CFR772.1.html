CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 772  /  Sec. 772.1 Policy.

(a) Purpose. This part contains the Agency's policies and procedures for servicing Minor Program loans which include: Grazing Association loans, Irrigation and Drainage Association loans, and Non-Farm Enterprise and Recreation loans to individuals.

(b) Appeals. The regulations at 7 CFR parts 11 and 780 apply to decisions made under this part.
