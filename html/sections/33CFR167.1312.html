CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 167  /  Sec. 167.1312 In the Strait of Juan de Fuca: Southern lanes.

In the southern lanes of the Strait of Juan de Fuca, the following are established:

(a) A separation zone bounded by a line connecting the following geographical positions: Latitude Longitude

4810.82 N 12325.44 W 4812.38 N 12328.68 W 4812.90 N 12328.68 W 4812.84 N 12327.46 W 4810.99 N 12324.84 W

(b) A traffic lane for northbound traffic between the separation zone and a line connecting the following geographical positions: Latitude Longitude

4811.24 N 12323.82 W 4812.72 N 12325.34 W

(c) A traffic lane for southbound traffic between the separation zone and a line connecting the following geographical positions: Latitude Longitude

4812.94 N 12332.89 W 4809.42 N 12324.24 W
