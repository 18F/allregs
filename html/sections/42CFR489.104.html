CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 489  /  Sec. 489.104 Effective dates.

These provisions apply to services furnished on or after December 1, 1991 payments made under section 1833(a)(1)(A) of the Act on or after December 1, 1991, and contracts effective on or after December 1, 1991.
