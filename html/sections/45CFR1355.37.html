CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 1355  /  Sec. 1355.37 Opportunity for public inspection of review reports

The title IV-E agency must make available for public review and inspection all statewide or Tribal assessments (Sec. 1355.33(b)), report of findings (Sec. 1355.33(e)), and program improvement plans (Sec. 1355.35(a)) developed as a result of a full or partial child and family services review. [77 FR 931, Jan. 6, 2012]
