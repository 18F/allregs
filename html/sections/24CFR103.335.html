CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 103  /  Sec. 103.335 Review of compliance with conciliation agreements.

HUD may, from time to time, review compliance with the terms of any conciliation agreement. Whenever HUD has reasonable cause to believe that a respondent has breached a conciliation agreement, the Assistant Secretary shall refer the matter to the Attorney General with a recommendation for the filing of a civil action under section 814(b)(2) of the Fair Housing Act for the enforcement of the terms of the conciliation agreement. [54 FR 3292, Jan. 23, 1989, as amended at 59 FR 39956, Aug. 5, 1994]
