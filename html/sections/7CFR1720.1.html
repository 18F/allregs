CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 1720  /  Sec. 1720.1 Purpose.

This part prescribes regulations implementing a guarantee program for bonds and notes issued for electrification or telephone purposes authorized by section 313A of the Rural Electrification Act of 1936 (7 U.S.C. 940c-1). [75 FR 42573, July 22, 2010]
