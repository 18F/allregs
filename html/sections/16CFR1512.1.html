CFR /  Title 16  /  Part 1512  /  Sec. 1512.1 Scope.

This part sets forth the requirements for a bicycle as defined in Sec. 1512.2(a) (except a bicycle that is a ``track bicycle'' or a ``one-of-a-kind bicycle'' as defined in Sec. 1512.2 (d) and (e)) which is not a banned article under Sec. 1500.18(a)(12) of this chapter.
