CFR /  Title 9  /  Part 166  /  Sec. 166.8 Vehicles used to transport garbage.

Vehicles used by a licensee to transport untreated garbage, except those that have also been used to treat the garbage so moved, shall not be used for hauling animals or treated garbage until cleaned and disinfected as set forth in Sec. 166.14(c) of this part. [47 FR 49945, Nov. 3, 1982, as amended at 52 FR 4890, Feb. 18, 1987]
