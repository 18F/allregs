CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 356  /  Sec. 356.31 How does the Secretary evaluate an application under

The Secretary awards the following points for each application based on how well the applicant addresses the two criteria in Sec. 356.30: Outstanding (5); Superior (4); Satisfactory (3); Marginal (2); Poor (1). (Authority: Sec. 202(d); 29 U.S.C. 761a(d)) [49 FR 24979, June 18, 1984]
