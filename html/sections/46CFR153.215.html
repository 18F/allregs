CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 153  /  Sec. 153.215 Safety equipment lockers.

Each self-propelled ship must have the following:

(a) Each tankship must have at least two safety equipment lockers.

(b) One safety equipment locker must be adjacent to the emergency shutdown station required by Sec. 153.296(b). This locker must contain one set of the equipment required by Sec. 153.214(a) and two sets of that required by Sec. 153.214(b).

(c) The second safety equipment locker must be adjacent to the second emergency shutdown station required by Sec. 153.296. This locker must contain the remaining equipment required by Sec. 153.214 (a) and (b).

(d) Each safety equipment locker must be marked as described in Sec. 153.955 (c), (d), and (e) with the legend ``SAFETY EQUIPMENT.'' [CGD 73-96, 42 FR 49027, Sept. 26, 1977, as amended by CGD 78-128, 47 FR 21207, May 17, 1982; CGD 81-101, 52 FR 7781, Mar. 12, 1987]
