CFR /  Title 39  /  Part 501  /  Sec. 501.5 Burden of proof standard.

The burden of proof is on the Postal Service in administrative determinations of suspension and revocation under Sec. 501.6 and administrative sanctions under Sec. 501.12. Except as otherwise indicated in those sections, the standard of proof shall be the preponderance-of-evidence standard.
