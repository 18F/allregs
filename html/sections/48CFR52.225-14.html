CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 52  /  52.225-14 Inconsistency between English Version and Translation of Contract.

As prescribed in 25.1103(b), insert the following clause:

Inconsistency Between English Version and Translation of Contract (FEB

2000)

In the event of inconsistency between any terms of this contract and any translation into another language, the English language meaning shall control.

(End of clause) [64 FR 72440, Dec. 27, 1999] 52.225-15--52.225-16 [Reserved]
