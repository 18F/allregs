CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 70  /  Sec. 70.3 License requirements.

No person subject to the regulations in this part shall receive title to, own, acquire, deliver, receive, possess, use, or transfer special nuclear material except as authorized in a license issued by the Commission pursuant to these regulations. [32 FR 2562, Feb. 7, 1967, as amended at 43 FR 6924, Feb. 17, 1978]
