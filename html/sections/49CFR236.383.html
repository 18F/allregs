CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 236  /  Sec. 236.383 Valve locks, valves, and valve magnets.

Valve locks on valves of the non-cut-off type shall be tested at least once every three months, and valves and valve magnets shall be tested at least once every year. [49 FR 3385, Jan. 26, 1984]
