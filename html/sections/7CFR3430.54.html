CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 3430  /  Sec. 3430.54 Indirect costs.

Indirect cost rates for grants and cooperative agreements shall be determined in accordance with 2 CFR part 200, unless superseded by another authority. Use of indirect costs as in-kind matching contributions is subject to Sec. 3430.52(b). [74 FR 45740, Sept. 4, 2009, as amended at 79 FR 76001, Dec. 19, 2014]
