CFR /  Title 50  /  Part 665  /  Sec. 665.645 At-sea observer coverage.

All fishing vessels subject to Sec. Sec. 665.640 through 665.645 and subpart A of this part must carry an observer when requested to do so by the Regional Administrator. Sec. Sec. 665.646-665.659 [Reserved]
