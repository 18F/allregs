CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 430  /  Sec. 430.57 Duration of temporary exemption.

A temporary exemption terminates according to its terms but not later than twenty-four months after the affective date of the rule for which the exemption is allowed. Subpart F [Reserved] Sec. Sec. 430.60-430.75 [Reserved]
