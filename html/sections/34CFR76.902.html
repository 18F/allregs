CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 76  /  Sec. 76.902 Judicial review.

After a hearing by the Secretary, a State is usually entitled-- generally by the statute that required the hearing--to judicial review of the Secretary's decision. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1221e-3, 3474, and 6511(a))
