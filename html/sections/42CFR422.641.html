CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 422  /  Sec. 422.641 Contract determinations.

This subpart establishes the procedures for making and reviewing the following contract determinations:

(a) A determination that an entity is not qualified to enter into a contract with CMS under Part C of title XVIII of the Act.

(b).

(c) A determination to terminate a contract with an MA organization in accordance with Sec. 422.510(a).

(d) A determination that an entity is not qualified to offer a Specialized MA Plan for Special Needs Individuals as defined in Secs. 422.2 and 422.4(a)(1)(iv). [63 FR 35113, June 26, 1998, as amended at 77 FR 22168, Apr. 12, 2012; 80 FR 7962, Feb. 12, 2015]
