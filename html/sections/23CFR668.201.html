CFR /  Title 23  /  Part 668  /  Sec. 668.201 Purpose.

To establish policy, procedures, and program guidance for the administration of emergency relief to Federal agencies for the repair or reconstruction of Federal roads which are found to have suffered serious damage by a natural disaster over a wide area or by catastrophic failure. [43 FR 59485, Dec. 21, 1978]
