CFR /  Title 36  /  Part 1192  /  Sec. 1192.161 Moveable aisle armrests.

A minimum of 50% of aisle seats, including all moveable or removable seats at wheelchair or mobility aide securement locations, shall have an armrest on the aisle side which can be raised, removed, or retracted to permit easy entry or exit. [63 FR 51700, 51702, Sept. 28, 1998]
