CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 966  /  Sec. 966.24 Districts.

For the purpose of determining the basis for selecting committee members the following districts of the production area are hereby initially established. District No. 1. The counties of Broward and Dade in the State of Florida;District No. 2. The counties of Brevard, Glades, Indian River, Martin, Osceola, Okeechobee, Palm Beach, and St. Lucie in the State of Florida;District No. 3. The counties of Charlotte, Collier, Hendry, Lee, and Monroe in the State of Florida; andDistrict No. 4. The counties of De Soto, Hardee, Highlands, Hillsborough, Manatee, Pinellas, Polk, and Sarasota in the State of Florida. [33 FR 8586, June 12, 1968]
