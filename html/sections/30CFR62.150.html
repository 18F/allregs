CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 62  /  Sec. 62.150 Hearing conservation program.

A hearing conservation program established under this part must include:

(a) A system of monitoring under Sec. 62.110 of this part;

(b) The provision and use of hearing protectors under Sec. 62.160 of this part;

(c) Audiometric testing under Sec. Sec. 62.170 through 62.175 of this part;

(d) Training under Sec. 62.180 of this part; and

(e) Recordkeeping under Sec. 62.190 of this part.
