CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 2201  /  Sec. 2201.9 Limitation on the applicability of the definition of

Notwithstanding the definition of Local Television Broadcast Signals provided in Sec. 2201.1 of this part, if an area is being served by either a satellite carrier which rebroadcasts signals of Television Broadcast Stations located in the DMA or a cable television system, and that satellite carrier or cable television system is currently in compliance with the rules administered by the Federal Communications Commission (FCC) as described in part 76 of title 47, Code of Federal Regulations, the group of signals of Television Broadcast Stations located in the DMA being retransmitted by such satellite carrier or cable television system will be considered to meet the definition of Local Television Broadcast Signals for the purposes of the regulation.
