CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 3715  /  Sec. 3715.3-5 What will BLM's notification include?

(a) BLM will include in each determination of concurrence a statement requiring you to continue to comply with Sec. Sec. 3715.2, 3715.2-1 and 3715.5.

(b) BLM will specify in each determination of non-concurrence how the proposed occupancy fails to meet the conditions of Sec. 3715.2, Sec. 3715.2-1 or Sec. 3715.5, and will provide you an opportunity to modify the proposed occupancy or appeal the determination under Sec. 3715.9.
