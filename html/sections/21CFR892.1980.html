CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 892  /  Sec. 892.1980 Radiologic table.

(a) Identification. A radiologic table is a device intended for medical purposes to support a patient during radiologic procedures. The table may be fixed or tilting and may be electrically powered.

(b) Classification. Class II (special controls). The device is exempt from the premarket notification procedures in subpart E of part 807 of this chapter subject to Sec. 892.9. [53 FR 1567, Jan. 20, 1988, as amended at 63 FR 59231, Nov. 3, 1998]
