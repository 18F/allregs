CFR /  Title 19  /  Part 151  /  Sec. 151.64 Extra copy of entry summary.

One extra copy of the entry summary covering wool or hair subject to duty at a rate per clean kilogram shall be filed in addition to the copies otherwise required. [T.D. 93-52, 58 FR 37854, July 14, 1993]
