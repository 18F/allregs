CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 411  /  Sec. 411.130 How will we distribute tickets under the Ticket to

We may send you a ticket if you are eligible to receive one under Sec. 411.125. All Ticket-eligible beneficiaries may receive a Ticket upon request. [77 FR 1864, Jan. 12, 2012]
