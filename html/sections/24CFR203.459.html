CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 203  /  Sec. 203.459 Notice of termination by lender.

No contract of insurance shall be terminated until the lender has given written notice thereof to the Commissioner within 15 calendar days from the occurrence of one of the approved methods of termination set forth in this subpart. [45 FR 31716, May 14, 1980]
