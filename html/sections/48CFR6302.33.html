CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 6302  /  Sec. 6302.33 Motion for reconsideration (Rule 33).

A motion for reconsideration shall set forth specifically the grounds relied upon to sustain the motion and shall be mailed or otherwise furnished within 30 days from the date of receipt of a copy of the Board's decision.
