CFR /  Title 14  /  Part 252  /  Sec. 252.19 Single-entity charters.

On single-entity charters operated pursuant to Sec. Sec. 207.50 or 208.300 of this title, air carriers need not comply with the procedures of this part 252 if such a request is made by the charterer, provided that each passenger on such flights is given notice of the smoking procedures for the flight at the time he or she first makes arrangements to take the flight.
