CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 397  /  Sec. 397.225 Judicial review.

A party to a proceeding under Sec. 397.205(a), Sec. 397.213(a), or Sec. 397.223(a) may seek review by the appropriate district court of the United States of the decision of the Administrator under such proceeding only by filing a petition with such court within 60 days after the final agency decision.
