CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 157  /  Sec. 157.120 Waiver of required documents.

The Coast Guard waives the requirement for the letter under Sec. 157.116(b), if a U.S. tank vessel engages in a voyage, or under Sec. 157.118(b)(2)(ii), if a foreign tank vessel enters the navigable waters of the United States or transfers cargo at a port or place subject to the jurisdiction of the United States, for the purpose of being inspected under Sec. 157.140.
