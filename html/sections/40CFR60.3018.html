CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 60  /  Sec. 60.3018 How do I renew my lapsed operator qualification?

You must renew a lapsed operator qualification by one of the two methods specified in paragraphs (a) and (b) of this section.

(a) For a lapse of less than 3 years, you must complete a standard annual refresher course described in Sec. 60.3017.

(b) For a lapse of 3 years or more, you must repeat the initial qualification requirements in Sec. 60.3016(a).
