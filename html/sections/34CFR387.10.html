CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 387  /  Sec. 387.10 What types of projects are authorized under this program?

The Experimental and Innovative Training Program supports time-limited pilot projects through which new types of rehabilitation workers may be trained or through which innovative methods of training rehabilitation workers may be demonstrated. (Authority: Sec. 302 of the Act; 29 U.S.C. 774) Subpart C [Reserved]
