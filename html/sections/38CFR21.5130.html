CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 21  /  Sec. 21.5130 Payments; educational assistance allowance.

VA will apply the following sections in administering benefits payable under 38 U.S.C. Chapter 32:

(a) Section 21.4131--Commencing dates (except paragraph (d)).

(b) Section 21.4135--Discontinuance dates.

(c) Section 21.4138 (except paragraph (b)--Certifications and release of payments.

(d) Section 21.4146--Assignments of benefits prohibited. (Authority: 38 U.S.C. 3241(a))

(e) Section 21.4136(k) (except paragraph (k)(3))--Mitigating circumstances. (Authority: 38 U.S.C. 3241(a), 3680(a)) [45 FR 31, Jan. 2, 1980, as amended at 46 FR 32024, June 19, 1981; 53 FR 617, Jan. 11, 1988; 55 FR 31582, Aug. 3, 1990; 57 FR 38612, Aug. 26, 1992; 61 FR 29030, June 7, 1996; 63 FR 35836, July 1, 1998; 64 FR 23772, May 4, 1999; 65 FR 5786, Feb. 7, 2000]
