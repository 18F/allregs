CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 408  /  Sec. 408.1021 How does the reconsidered determination affect you?

The reconsidered determination is binding unless--

(a) You request a hearing before an administrative law judge within the stated time period and a decision is made;

(b) The expedited appeals process is used; or

(c) The reconsidered determination is revised.
