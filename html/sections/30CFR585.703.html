CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 585  /  Sec. 585.703 What reports must I submit for project modifications

(a) You must verify and, in a report to us, certify that major repairs and major modifications to the project conform to accepted engineering practices.

(1) A major repair is a corrective action involving structural members affecting the structural integrity of a portion of or all the facility.

(2) A major modification is an alteration involving structural members affecting the structural integrity of a portion of or all the facility.

(b) The report must also identify the location of all records pertaining to the major repairs or major modifications, as required in Sec. 585.714(c).

(c) BOEM may require you to use a CVA for project modifications and repairs. Sec. 585.704 [Reserved]
