CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 257  /  Sec. 257.13 Deadline for making demonstrations.

Existing units that cannot make the demonstration specified in Sec. 257.8(a) pertaining to floodplains by January 1, 1998, must not accept CESQG hazardous waste for disposal.
