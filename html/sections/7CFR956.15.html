CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 956  /  Sec. 956.15 Grade and size.

Grade means any of the officially established grades of onions, including maturity requirements and size means any of the officially established sizes of onions as set forth in the United States standards for grades of onions or amendments thereto, or modifications thereof, or variations based thereon, or States of Washington or Oregon standards of onions or amendments thereto or modifications thereof or variations based thereon, recommended by the committee and approved by the Secretary. [64 FR 4933, Feb. 1, 1999]
