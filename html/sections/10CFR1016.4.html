CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 1016  /  Sec. 1016.4 Communications.

Communications concerning rulemaking, i.e., petition to change part 1016, should be addressed to the Chief Health, Safety and Security Officer, HS-1/Forrestal Building, Office of Health, Safety and Security, U.S. Department of Energy, 1000 Independence Avenue, SW., Washington, DC 20585. All other communications concerning the regulations in this part should be addressed to the cognizant DOE or National Nuclear Security Administration (NNSA) office. [71 FR 68735, Nov. 28, 2006]
