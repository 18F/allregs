CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 2517  /  Sec. 2517.110 Who may apply for a subgrant from a Corporation grantee?

Entities that may apply for a subgrant from a State Commission or grantmaking entity are qualified organizations that have entered into a local partnership with one or more--

(a) Local educational agencies (LEAs);

(b) Other qualified organizations; or

(c) Both.
