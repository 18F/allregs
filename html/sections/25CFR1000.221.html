CFR /  Title 25  /  Part 1000  /  Sec. 1000.221 Can the Secretary grant a waiver of regulations to a

Yes, a Tribe/Consortium may ask the Secretary to grant a waiver of some or all Department of the Interior regulation(s) applicable to a program, in whole or in part, operated by a Tribe/Consortium under an AFA.
