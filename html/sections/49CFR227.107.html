CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 227  /  Sec. 227.107 Hearing conservation program.

(a) Consistent with the requirements of the noise monitoring program required by Sec. 227.103, the railroad shall administer a continuing, effective hearing conservation program, as set forth in Secs. 227.109 through 227.121, for all employees exposed to noise at or above the action level.

(b) For purposes of the hearing conservation program, employee noise exposure shall be computed in accordance with the tables in appendix A of this part, and without regard to any attenuation provided by the use of hearing protectors.
