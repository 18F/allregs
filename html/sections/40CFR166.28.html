CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 166  /  Sec. 166.28 Duration of exemption.

(a) Specific or public health exemptions. EPA shall allow use of a pesticide under a specific or public health exemption for as long a period as is reasonably expected to be necessary but in no case for longer than 1 year.

(b) Quarantine exemption. EPA shall allow use of a pesticide under a quarantine exemption for as long a period as is deemed necessary but in no case for longer than 3 years. Quarantine exemptions may be renewed. Interim reports containing the information specified in Sec. 166.32(b) to the extent available shall be filed annually.
