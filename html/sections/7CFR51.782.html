CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 51  /  Sec. 51.782 Very serious damage.

Very serious damage means any specific defect described in Sec. 51.784, Table I; or an equally objectionable variation of any one of these defects, any other defect, or any combination of defects, which very seriously detracts from the appearance, or the edible or marketing quality of the fruit.
