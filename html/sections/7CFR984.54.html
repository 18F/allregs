CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 984  /  Sec. 984.54 Establishment of obligation.

(a)(5) of the act.

(b) Holding requirements. Each handler shall at all times hold in his possession or under his control in proper storage the kernelweight of certified merchantable walnuts necessary to meet his reserve obligation less:

(1) Any quantity which was disposed of by him pursuant to Sec. 984.56; and

(2) Any quantity for which he is otherwise relieved by the Board of responsibility to so hold walnuts. [41 FR 31543, July 29, 1976]
