CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 111  /  Sec. 111.20-1 General requirements.

Each transformer winding must be resistant to moisture, sea atmosphere, and oil vapor, unless special precautions are taken, such as enclosing the winding in an enclosure with a high degree of ingress protection. [CGD 94-108, 61 FR 28278, June 4, 1996]
