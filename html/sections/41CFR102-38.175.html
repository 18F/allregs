CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 102-38  /  Sec. 102-38.175 How do we find out if a person or entity has been

Refer to the List of Parties Excluded from Federal Procurement and Nonprocurement Programs to ensure you do not solicit from or award contracts to these persons or entities. The list is available through subscription from the U.S. Government Printing Office, or electronically on the Internet at http://epls.arnet.gov. For policies, procedures, and requirements for debarring/suspending a person or entity from the purchase of Federal personal property, follow the procedures in the Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) subpart 9.4 (48 CFR part 9, subpart 9.4). [68 FR 51421, Aug. 26, 2003; 68 FR 53219, Sept. 9, 2003]
