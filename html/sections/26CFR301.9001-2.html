CFR /  Title 26  /  Part 301  /  Sec. 301.9001-2 Definitions.

The terms enumerated in this section are to be defined for the purposes of Sec. Sec. 301.9001-1, 301.9001-2, and 301.9001-3 in the following manner:

(a) ``Barrel'' means 42 United States gallons at 60 degrees Fahrenheit.

(b) ``Oil'' means petroleum, including crude oil or any fraction or residue therefrom, and natural gas condensate, except that the term does not include natural gas.

(c) ``Person'' means an individual, firm, corporation, association, partnership, consortium, joint venture, or governmental entity.

(d) of the Outer Continental Shelf Lands Act Amendments of 1978 (92 Stat. 672) and sec. 7805 of the Internal Revenue Code of 1954 (68A Stat. 917; 26 U.S.C. 7805)) [T.D. 7697, 45 FR 33976, May 21, 1980]
