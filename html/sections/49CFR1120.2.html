CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 1120  /  Sec. 1120.2 Purpose.

In any proceeding requiring the development of platform handling times for distribution of platform expense, carriers may use the results of the national weight formula contained in the Board's study, entitled 1977-1978 Motor Carrier Platform Study, Statement 2S1-79. [47 FR 36184, Aug. 19, 1982. Redesignated at 47 FR 49570, Nov. 1, 1982]
