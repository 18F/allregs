CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 801  /  Sec. 801.3 Applicability of this part to 20 CFR part 802.

Part 802 of title 20, Code of Federal Regulations, contains the rules of practice and procedure of the Board. This part 801, including the definitions and usages contained in Sec. 801.2, is applicable to part 802 of this chapter as appropriate.
