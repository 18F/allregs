CFR /  Title 18  /  Part 385  /  Sec. 385.1010 Certification of the record (Rule 1010).

The presiding officer will certify and file with the Office of the Secretary of the Commission, for the Commission, a copy of the record in the proceeding. [Order 422, 50 FR 21600, May 28, 1985]
