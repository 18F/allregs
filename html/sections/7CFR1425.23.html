CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 1425  /  Sec. 1425.23 Reports.

(a) CMA's must annually provide CCC a report of all commodity deliveries involved in loans and LDP's by FSA farm number for each member.

(b) When requested by CCC, CMA's must report market gains received on behalf of each member. [63 FR 17312, Apr. 9, 1998, as amended at 80 FR 132, Jan. 2, 2015] Sec. 1425.24 [Reserved]
