CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 932  /  Sec. 932.37 Compensation and expenses.

The members of the committee and alternates when acting as members or at the request of the committee or its chairman shall serve without compensation, but shall be reimbursed for necessary expenses, as approved by the committee, incurred by them in the performance of their duties under this part. [47 FR 32908, July 30, 1982]
