CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 0  /  Sec. 0.421 Application forms.

All forms for use in submitting applications for radio authorization, together with instructions and information as to filing such forms, may be obtained at http://www.fcc.gov/forms. For information concerning the forms to be used and filing requirements, see part 1 of this chapter and the appropriate substantive rules. [80 FR 53749, Sept. 8, 2015]
