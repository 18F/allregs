CFR /  Title 18  /  Part 707  /  Sec. 707.10 Scoping.

Scoping will be used to determine the extent of issues to be addressed by the EIS and to identify significant issues related to the proposed action. Scoping will be conducted as described by the CEQ NEPA regulations, Sec. Sec. 1501.7 and 1508.25.
