CFR /  Title 9  /  Part 307  /  Sec. 307.3 Inspectors to furnish and maintain implements in a sanitary

Inspectors shall furnish their own work clothing and implements, such as flashlights and triers, for conducting inspection and shall maintain their implements in sanitary condition as prescribed by Sec. 416.3(a) of this chapter. [64 FR 56415, Oct. 20, 1999]
