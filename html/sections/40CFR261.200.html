CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 261  /  Sec. 261.200 Air emission standards.

The remanufacturer or other person that stores or treats the hazardous secondary material shall manage all hazardous secondary material placed in a tank in accordance with the applicable requirements of subparts AA, BB, and CC of this part. Subparts K-L [Reserved]
