CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 1204  /  Sec. 1204.212 What if I took relief for which I was ineligible?

If you took relief under this subpart for a period for which you were not eligible, you:

(a) May owe additional royalties and late payment interest determined under Sec. 1218.54 from the date your additional payments were due until the date ONRR receives them; and

(b) May be subject to civil penalties.
