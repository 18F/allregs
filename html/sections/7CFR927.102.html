CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 927  /  Sec. 927.102 Order.

Order means Marketing Order No. 927, as amended (Sec. Sec. 927.1 to 927.81), regulating the handling of pears grown in the States of Oregon and Washington. [71 FR 7676, Feb. 14, 2006]
