CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 111  /  Sec. 111.33-5 Installation.

Each semiconductor rectifier system must meet the installation requirements, as appropriate, of--

(a) Sections 10.20.2, 10.20.7, and 10.20.8 of IEEE 45-2002 (incorporated by reference; see 46 CFR 110.10-1); or

(b) IEC 60092-304 (incorporated by reference; see 46 CFR 110.10-1). [CGD 94-108, 61 FR 28279, June 4, 1996, as amended by USCG-2003-16630, 73 FR 65197, Oct. 31, 2008; USCG-2013-0671, 78 FR 60153, Sept. 30, 2013]
