CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 603  /  Sec. 603.1220 Award-specific audit.

An audit of a single TIA, usually done at the cognizant contracting officer's request, to help resolve issues that arise during or after the performance of the RD&D; project. An award-specific audit of an individual award differs from a periodic audit of a participant (as defined in Sec. 603.1295).
