CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 719  /  719.273-11 Solicitation provision and contract clause.

(a) The contracting officer shall insert the provision at (48 CFR) AIDAR 752.219-70 in all unrestricted solicitations exceeding $550,000 ($1,000,000 for construction) that offer subcontracting opportunities.

(b) The contracting officer shall insert the clause at (48 CFR) AIDAR 752.219-71 in all contracts where the prime contractor has signed a Mentor-Protege Agreement with USAID. [72 FR 32543, June 13, 2007, as amended at 79 FR 74988, Dec. 16, 2014]
