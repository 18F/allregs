CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 300  /  Sec. 300.505 Electronic mail.

A parent of a child with a disability may elect to receive notices required by Secs. 300.503, 300.504, and 300.508 by an electronic mail communication, if the public agency makes that option available. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1415(n))
