CFR /  Title 39  /  Part 955  /  Sec. 955.18 Hearings--where and when held.

If there is to be a hearing, it will be held at a time and place prescribed by the Board after consultation with the parties. At the discretion of the Board, hearings may be held in the Board's hearing room in Arlington, Virginia or may be held at another location with due consideration to the just, informal, expeditious and inexpensive resolution of each case. [74 FR 20592, May 5, 2009, as amended at 76 FR 37660, June 28, 2011]
