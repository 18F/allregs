CFR /  Title 31  /  Part 592  /  Sec. 592.302 Effective date.

The term effective date refers to the effective date of the applicable prohibitions and directives contained in this part as follows:

(a) With respect to all provisions of this part except for Sec. 592.301(a)(3), 12:01 a.m., eastern daylight time, July 30, 2003; and

(b) With respect to Sec. 592.301(a)(3), September 23, 2004.
