CFR /  Title 26  /  Part 41  /  Sec. 41.4483-7 Reduction in tax for vehicles registered in a contiguous

(a), a highway motor vehicle that is allowed to operate in a State under a reciprocity agreement is not considered to be registered under the laws of that State.

(b) Contiguous foreign country. The term ``contiguous foreign country'' means Canada or Mexico. [T.D. 8159, 52 FR 33584, Sept. 4, 1987, as amended by T.D. 8177, 53 FR 6626, Mar. 2, 1988]
