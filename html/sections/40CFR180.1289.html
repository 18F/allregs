CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 180  /  Sec. 180.1289 Candida oleophila Strain O; exemption from the

An exemption from the requirement of a tolerance is established for the residues of the microbial pesticide, Candida oleophila Strain O, on apples and pears when applied/used as a post-harvest biofungicide. [74 FR 22464, May 13, 2009]
