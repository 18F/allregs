CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 1245  /  Sec. 1245.2 Reports of railroad employees, service and compensation.

Beginning with the reporting period commencing January 1, 1983, and quarterly thereafter until further ordered, each Class I railroad is required to file a Quarterly Report of Railroad Employees, Service, and Compensation, (Form QRSC). In addition, such carriers shall also file an Annual Report of Railroad Employees, Service, and Compensation, (Form ARSC) for each calendar year. Both reports shall be filed with the Bureau of Accounts, Surface Transportation Board, Washington, DC 20423. The quarterly report shall be filed within thirty days after the end of each calendar quarter. The annual report shall be filed within forty five days after the end of the reporting year. [47 FR 53867, Nov. 30, 1982]
