CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 44  /  44.400 Scope of subpart.

This subpart prescribes the policies limiting the contract clauses a contractor may be required to apply to any subcontractors that are furnishing commercial items or commercial components in accordance with 41 U.S.C. 3307. [76 FR 14565, Mar. 16, 2011, as amended at 79 FR 24213, Apr. 29, 2014]
