CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 149  /  Sec. 149.410 Where must portable and semi-portable fire extinguishers

All portable and semi-portable fire extinguishers described in Table 149.409 of this part must be located in the open so as to be readily seen. [USCG-1998-3884, 71 FR 57651, Sept. 29, 2006, as amended by USCG-2013-0397, 78 FR 39179, July 1, 2013]
