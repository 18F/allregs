CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 9701  /  Sec. 9701.608 Procedures.

An employee against whom an adverse action is proposed is entitled to the following:

(a) A proposal notice under Sec. 9701.609;

(b) An opportunity to reply under Sec. 9701.610; and

(c) A decision notice under Sec. 9701.611.
