CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 556  /  Sec. 556.38 Amoxicillin.

A tolerance of 0.01 part per million is established for negligible residues of amoxicillin in milk and in the uncooked edible tissues of cattle. [49 FR 45422, Nov. 16, 1984]
