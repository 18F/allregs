CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 98  /  Sec. 98.30-14 Cargo pumps.

No person may operate a cargo pump to transfer a product to or from a portable tank unless the pump is installed--

(a) Above deck; or

(b) Below deck, in conformance with subpart 32.60 of this chapter. [CGD 73-172, 39 FR 22954, June 25, 1974. Redesignated by USCG-2011-0088, 78 FR 54785, Sept. 6, 2013]
