CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 17  /  Sec. 17.57 Report of radio transmitting antenna construction, alteration,

The owner of an antenna structure for which an Antenna Structure Registration Number has been obtained must notify the Commission within 5 days of completion of construction (FCC Form 854-R) and/or dismantlement (FCC Form 854). The owner must also notify the Commission within 5 days of any change in structure height or change in ownership information (FCC Form 854). [79 FR 56987, Sept. 24, 2014] Sec. 17.58 [Reserved]
