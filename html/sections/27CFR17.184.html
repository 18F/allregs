CFR /  Title 27  /  Part 17  /  Sec. 17.184 Distilled spirits container marks.

All marks required by Part 19 of this chapter shall remain on containers of taxpaid distilled spirits until the contents are emptied. Whenever such a container is emptied, such marks shall be completely obliterated. (Sec. 454, Pub. L. 98-369, 98 Stat. 820 (26 U.S.C. 5206(d)))
