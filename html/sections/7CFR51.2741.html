CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 51  /  Sec. 51.2741 Unshelled.

Unshelled means a peanut kernel with part or all of the hull (shell) attached.

Subpart_United States Standards for Shelled Virginia Type Peanuts

Source: 24 FR 6182, Aug. 1, 1959; 24 FR 6671, Aug. 18, 1959, unless otherwise noted. Redesignated at 42 FR 32514, June 27, 1977, and further redesignated at 46 FR 63203, Dec. 31, 1981.
