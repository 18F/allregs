CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 1201  /  Sec. 1201.147 Requests for protective orders by persons other than

Requests for protective orders by persons other than the Special Counsel in connection with pending original jurisdiction proceedings are governed by Sec. 1201.55(d) of this part.
