CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 148  /  Sec. 148.248 What happens when a document does not contain all necessary

Any document that does not satisfy the requirements in Secs. 20.303 and 20.304 of this chapter will be returned to the person who submitted it with a statement of the reasons for denial.
