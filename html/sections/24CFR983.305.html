CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 983  /  Sec. 983.305 Rent to owner: effect of rent control and other rent

In addition to the limitation to 110 percent of the FMR in Sec. 983.301(b)(1), the rent reasonableness limit under Sec. Sec. 983.301(b)(2) and 983.303, the rental determination provisions of Sec. 983.301(f), the special limitations for tax credit units under Sec. 983.301(c), and other rent limits under this part, the amount of rent to owner also may be subject to rent control or other limits under local, state, or federal law.
