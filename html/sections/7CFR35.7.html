CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 35  /  Sec. 35.7 Certificate.

Certificate or Certificate of Inspection means any of the official forms of inspection certificate, bearing the statement ``meets Export Grape and Plum Act,'' issued by the Federal or Federal-State Inspection Service in accordance with regulations governing the inspection of fresh fruits, vegetables, and other products (7 CFR part 2851 \1\).---------------------------------------------------------------------------

\1\ Part 2851 was redesignated as part 51 at 46 FR 63203, Dec. 31, 1981.---------------------------------------------------------------------------
