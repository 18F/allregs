CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 365  /  Sec. 365.2 Who is eligible for an award?

Any designated State unit (DSU) identified by the State pursuant to 34 CFR 364.22 is eligible to apply for assistance under this part in accordance with 34 CFR 364.10 and 364.11. (Approved by the Office of Management and Budget under control number 1820-0527) (Authority: 29 U.S.C. 796c(a)(1) and (c) and 796e(a))
