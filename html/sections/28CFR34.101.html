CFR /  Title 28  /  Part 34  /  Sec. 34.101 Exceptions to applicability.

The assistance and procurement contract situations specified in Sec. 34.2 (c), (d), (e), (f), and (g) of subpart A of this part are considered by OJJDP to be outside the scope of the section 262(d) peer review requirement as set forth in this subpart.
