CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 2429  /  Sec. 2429.11 Interlocutory appeals.

Except as set forth in part 2423, the Authority and the General Counsel ordinarily will not consider interlocutory appeals. [62 FR 40923, July 31, 1997]
