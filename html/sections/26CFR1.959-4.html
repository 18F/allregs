CFR /  Title 26  /  Part 1  /  Sec. 1.959-4 Distributions to United States persons not counting

Except as provided in section 960(a)(3) and Sec. 1.960-2, any distribution to a United States person which is excluded from the gross income of such person under section 959(a)(1) and Sec. 1.959-1 shall be treated for purposes of chapter 1 (relating to normal taxes and surtaxes) of subtitle A (relating to income taxes) of the Code as a distribution which is not a dividend. However, see paragraph (b)(1) of Sec. 1.956-1, relating to the dividend limitation on the amount of a controlled foreign corporation's investment of earnings in United States property. [T.D. 7120, 36 FR 10860, June 4, 1971]
