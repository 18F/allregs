CFR /  Title 13  /  Part 120  /  Sec. 120.850 Expiration of Associate Development Company designation.

The designation of Associate Development Company (ADC) will cease to exist on January 1, 2004. After that date, former ADCs may continue to contract with CDCs as Lender Service Providers (see part 103 of this chapter) or to perform other services. [68 FR 57984, Oct. 7, 2003]
