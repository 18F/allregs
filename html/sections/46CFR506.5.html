CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 506  /  Sec. 506.5 Application of increase to violations.

Any increase in a civil monetary penalty under this part shall apply only to violations which occur after the date the increase takes effect.---------------------------------------------------------------------------

\3\ Application of the statutory rounding resulted in no increase to these penalties.---------------------------------------------------------------------------
