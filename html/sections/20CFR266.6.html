CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 266  /  Sec. 266.6 Information to be submitted by a representative

Before the Board selects a representative payee, the Board may request the payee-applicant to provide information concerning the factors listed in Sec. 266.4 of this part. An employee of the Board may also conduct a face-to-face interview with the payee-applicant. (Approved by the Office of Management and Budget under control number 3220-0052)
