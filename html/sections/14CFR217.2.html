CFR /  Title 14  /  Part 217  /  Sec. 217.2 Applicability.

This part applies to foreign air carriers that are authorized by the Department to provide civilian passenger and/or cargo service to or from the United States, whether performed pursuant to a permit or exemption authority. [Doc. No. OST 98-4043, 67 FR 49223, July 30, 2002]
