CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 153  /  Sec. 153.360 Application of risk adjustment to the small group market.

Enrollees in a risk adjustment covered plan must be assigned to the applicable risk pool in the State in which the employer's policy was filed and approved. [78 FR 15528, Mar. 11, 2013]
