CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 1924  /  Sec. 1924.274 Final inspection.

Except for emergency repairs, all repair work must be performed in accordance with subpart A of this part. In all cases, FmHA or its successor agency under Public Law 103-354 will make a final inspection of the repair work performed before final payment is made for the work. Sec. 1924.275 [Reserved]
