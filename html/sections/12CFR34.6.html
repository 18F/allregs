CFR /  Title 12  /  Part 34  /  Sec. 34.6 Applicability of state law to Federal savings associations

In accordance with section 1046 of the Dodd-Frank Wall Street Reform and Consumer Protection Act (12 U.S.C. 25b), Federal savings associations and their subsidiaries shall be subject to the same laws and legal standards, including regulations of the OCC, as are applicable to national banks and their subsidiaries, regarding the preemption of state law. [76 FR 43569, July 21, 2011]
