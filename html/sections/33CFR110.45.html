CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 110  /  Sec. 110.45 Onset Bay, Mass.

Northerly of a line extending from the northernmost point of Onset Island to the easternmost point of Wickets Island; easterly of a line extending from the easternmost point of Wickets Island to the southwest extremity of Point Independence; southerly of the shore line; and westerly of the shore line and of a line bearing due north from the northernmost point of Onset Island. Sec. 110.45a Mattapoisett Harbor, Mattapoisett, Mass.

(a) Area No. 1 beginning at a point on the shore at latitude 41[deg]39[min]23[sec] N., longitude 70[deg]48[min]50[sec] W.; thence 138.5[deg] T. to latitude 41[deg]38[min]45[sec] N., longitude 70[deg]48[min]02[sec] W.; thence 031[deg] T. to latitude 41[deg]39[min]02[sec] N., longitude 70[deg]47[min]48[sec] W.; thence along the shore to the point of beginning.

(b) Area No. 2 beginning at a point on the shore at latitude 41[deg]39[min]24[sec] N., longitude 70[deg]49[min]02[sec] W.; thence 142.5[deg] T. to latitude 41[deg]38[min]10[sec] N., longitude 70[deg]47[min]45[sec] W.; thence 219[deg] T. to latitude 41[deg]37[min]54[sec] N., longitude 70[deg]48[min]02[sec] W.; thence along the shore to the point of beginning.

Note: Administration of the Special Anchorage Area is exercised by the Harbormaster, Town of Mattapoisett pursuant to a local ordinance. The town of Mattapoisett will install and maintain suitable navigational aids to mark the perimeter of the anchorage area. [CGD 83-2R, 49 FR 25445, June 21, 1984; 49 FR 27320, July 3, 1984]
