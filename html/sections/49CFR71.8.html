CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 71  /  Sec. 71.8 Mountain zone.

The fourth zone, the mountain standard time zone, includes that part of the United States that is west of the boundary line between the central and mountain standard time zones described in Sec. 71.7 and east of the boundary line between the mountain and Pacific standard time zones described in Sec. 71.9.
