CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 1823  /  1823.7101 Contract clause.

The contracting officer shall insert the clause at 1852.223-71, Authorization for radio Frequency Use, in solicitations and contracts calling for developing, producing, constructing, testing, or operating a device for which a radio frequency equipment authorization is required. [80 FR 12939, Mar. 12, 2015]
