CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 304  /  Sec. 304.15 Cost allocation.

A State agency in support of its claims under title IV-D of the Social Security Act must have an approved cost allocation plan on file with the Department in accordance with the requirements contained in Subpart E of 45 CFR part 95. Subpart E also sets forth the effect on FFP if the requirements contained in that subpart are not met. [47 FR 17509, Apr. 23, 1982]
