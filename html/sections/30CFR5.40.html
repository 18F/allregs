CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 5  /  Sec. 5.40 Fee administration.

Applicants will be billed for all fees, including actual travel expenses, if any, when processing of the application is completed. Invoices will contain specific payment instructions, including the address to mail payments and authorized methods of payment. [70 FR 46342, Aug. 9, 2005]
