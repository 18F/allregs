CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 397  /  Sec. 397.77 Judicial review of dispute decision.

Any State or Indian tribe adversely affected by the Administrator's decision under Sec. 397.75 of this subpart may seek review by the appropriate district court of the United States under such proceeding only by filing a petition with such court within 90 days after such decision becomes final.
