CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 1  /  Sec. 1.260 Certification of transcript.

After the close of the hearing, the complete transcript of testimony, together with all exhibits, shall be certified as to identity by the presiding officer and filed in the Office of the Secretary. Notice of such certification shall be served on all parties to the proceedings. [71 FR 15618, Mar. 29, 2006]
