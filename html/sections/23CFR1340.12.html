CFR /  Title 23  /  Part 1340  /  Sec. 1340.12 Re-selection of observation sites.

(a) Re-selection of observation sites. States shall re-select observation sites using updated sampling frame data, as described in Sec. 1340.5(a), no less than once every five years.

(b) Re-selection submission deadline. States shall submit updated sampling frame data meeting the requirements of Sec. 1340.5(a) for NHTSA approval no later than March 1 of the re-selection year.
