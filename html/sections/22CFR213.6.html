CFR /  Title 22  /  Part 213  /  Sec. 213.6 Subdivision of claims not authorized.

A claim will not be subdivided to avoid the $100,000 limit on the Agency's authority to compromise, suspend, or terminate a debt. A debtor's liability arising from a particular transaction or contract is a single claim.
