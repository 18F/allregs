CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 334  /  Sec. 334.450 Cape Fear River and tributaries at Sunny Point Army

(a) The area. That portion of Cape Fear River due west of the main ship channel extending from U.S. Coast Guard buoy No. 35 (340203.218" N, 0775628.755" W) at the north approach channel to Sunny Point Army Terminal to U.S. Coast Guard buoy No. 27 (335816.12" N, 0775659.736" W) at the south approach channel to Sunny Point Army Terminal and all waters of its tributaries therein.

(b) Except in cases of extreme emergency, all persons or vessels of any size or rafts other than those authorized by the Commander, Sunny Point Army Terminal, are prohibited from entering this area without prior permission of the enforcing agency.

(c) The regulations in this section shall be enforced by the Commander, Sunny Point Army Terminal, Southport, North Carolina, and such agencies as he/she may designate. [75 FR 53198, Aug. 31, 2010]
