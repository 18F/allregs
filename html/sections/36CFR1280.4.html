CFR /  Title 36  /  Part 1280  /  Sec. 1280.4 What items are subject to inspection by NARA?

NARA may, at its discretion, inspect the personal property in the possession of any NARA contractor, employee, student intern, visitor, volunteer, or other person arriving on, working at, visiting, or departing from NARA property. [71 FR 76167, Dec. 20, 2006]
