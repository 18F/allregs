CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 91  /  Sec. 91.18 Age distinctions contained in HHS regulations.

Any age distinctions contained in a rule or regulation issued by HHS shall be presumed to be necessary to the achievement of a statutory objective of the program or activity to which the rule or regulation applies, notwithstanding the provisions of Sec. 91.13. [47 FR 57858, Dec. 28, 1982, as amended at 70 FR 24322, May 9, 2005]
