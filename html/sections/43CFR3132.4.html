CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 3132  /  Sec. 3132.4 Qualifications.

Submission of a lease bid constitutes certification of compliance with the regulations of this part. Anyone seeking to acquire, or anyone holding, a Federal oil and gas lease or interest therein may be required to submit additional information to show compliance with the regulations of this part. [47 FR 8546, Feb. 26, 1982]
