CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 417  /  Sec. 417.526 Payment for covered services.

Subpart O of this part set forth the principles that CMS follows in determining Medicare payment to an HMO or CMP that has a reasonable cost contract. Subpart P of this part describes the per capita method of Medicare payment to HMOs or CMPs that contract on a risk basis. [50 FR 1346, Jan. 10, 1985; 50 FR 20570, May 17, 1985; as amended at 58 FR 38080, July 15, 1993; 60 FR 46229, Sept. 6, 1995]
