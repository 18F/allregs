CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 929  /  Sec. 929.24 Failure to nominate.

If nominations are not made within the time and in the manner prescribed in Sec. 929.22, the Secretary may, without regard to nominations, select the members and alternate members of the committee on the basis of representation provided for in Sec. Sec. 929.20 and 929.23.
