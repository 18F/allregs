CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 416  /  Sec. 416.435 Change in status involving a couple; ineligibility

Whenever benefits are suspended or terminated for both members of a couple because of ineligibility, no benefits are payable for that month. However, when benefits are suspended or terminated for one member of a couple because of ineligibility for a month, the member who remains eligible assumes the eligibility status of an eligible individual without an eligible spouse for such month and the benefit rate and payment amount will be determined as an eligible individual for the month. [50 FR 48572, Nov. 26, 1985]
