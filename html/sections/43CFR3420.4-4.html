CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 3420  /  Sec. 3420.4-4 Consultation with Indian tribes.

The Secretary shall consult with any Indian tribe which may be affected by the adoption of the proposed regional lease sale schedule. The Secretary shall give the tribe 30 days in which to comment prior to adopting a lease sale schedule. [44 FR 42615, July 19, 1979. Redesignated and amended at 47 FR 33139, July 30, 1982]
