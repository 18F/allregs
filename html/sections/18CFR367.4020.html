CFR /  Title 18  /  Part 367  /  Sec. 367.4020 Account 402, Maintenance expense.

There must be shown under this caption the total amount included in the service company maintenance expense accounts 500 through 598 (Sec. Sec. 367.5000 through 367.5890), 800 through 894 (Sec. Sec. 367.8000 through 367.8810), and 935 (Sec. 367.9350).
