CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 600  /  Sec. 600.100 Purpose.

This subpart implements OMB Circular A-110 and establishes uniform administrative requirements for grants and agreements awarded to institutions of higher education, hospitals, and other non-profit organizations. It also establishes rules governing subawards to institutions of higher education, hospitals, and non-profit organizations (including grants and cooperative agreements administered by State, local and Indian Tribal governments). [59 FR 53266, Oct. 21, 1994, as amended at 68 FR 50650, Aug. 21, 2003]
