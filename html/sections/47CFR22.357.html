CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 22  /  Sec. 22.357 Emission types.

Any authorized station in the Public Mobile Services may transmit emissions of any type(s) that comply with the applicable emission rule, i.e. Sec. 22.359, Sec. 22.861 or Sec. 22.917. [70 FR 19308, Apr. 13, 2005]
