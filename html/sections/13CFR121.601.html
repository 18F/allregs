CFR /  Title 13  /  Part 121  /  Sec. 121.601 What is a small business for purposes of admission to

An applicant must not exceed the size standard corresponding to its primary industry classification in order to qualify for admission to SBA's 8(a) Business Development Program. [69 FR 29205, May 21, 2004]
