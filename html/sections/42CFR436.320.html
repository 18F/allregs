CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 436  /  Sec. 436.320 Medically needy coverage of the aged.

If the agency provides Medicaid to the medically needy, it may provide Medicaid to individuals who--

(a) Are 65 years of age and older, as provided for in Sec. 436.520; and

(b) Meet the income and resource requirements of subpart I of this part. [46 FR 47991, Sept. 30, 1981]
