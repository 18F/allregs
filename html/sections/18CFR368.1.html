CFR /  Title 18  /  Part 368  /  Sec. 368.1 Promulgation.

This part is prescribed and promulgated as the regulations governing the preservation of records by any holding company and by any service company within a holding company system subject to the jurisdiction of the Commission under the Public Utility Holding Company Act of 2005 (42 U.S.C. Sec. Sec. 16451 et seq.).
