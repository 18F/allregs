CFR /  Title 31  /  Part 351  /  Sec. 351.30 What are interest rates and monthly accruals for Series EE original maturity period?

Savings bond rates (defined in Sec. 351.13) apply to earnings during the first semiannual rate period beginning on or after the effective date of the rate. Interest is credited on the first day of each month and compounded semiannually. Interest accrues beginning with the fourth month from the issue date. For example, a bond issued in January has interest first credited on May 1, which represents one month of interest because of the 3-month interest penalty.
