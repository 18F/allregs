CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 852  /  852.228-72 Assisting service-disabled veteran-owned and veteran-owned

As prescribed in 828.106-71, insert the following clause:

Assisting Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Businesses and Veteran-

Owned Small Businesses in Obtaining Bonds

(DEC2009)

Prime contractors are encouraged to assist service-disabled veteran- owned and veteran-owned small business potential subcontractors in obtaining bonding, when required. Mentor firms are encouraged to assist Protege firms under VA's Mentor-Protege Program in obtaining acceptable bid, payment, and performance bonds, when required, as a prime contractor under a solicitation or contract and in obtaining any required bonds under subcontracts. [74 FR 64638, Dec. 8, 2009]
