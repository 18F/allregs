CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 556  /  Sec. 556.310 Haloxon.

A tolerance of 0.1 part per million is established for negligible residues of haloxon (3-chloro-7-hydroxy-4-methyl-coumarin bis(2-chloroethyl) phosphate) in the edible tissues of cattle. [40 FR 13942, Mar. 27, 1975, as amended at 45 FR 10333, Feb. 15, 1980]
