CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 585  /  Sec. 585.421 How long will a suspension be in effect?

A suspension will be in effect for the period specified by BOEM.

(a) BOEM will not approve a suspension request pursuant to Sec. 585.416 for a period longer than 2 years.

(b) If BOEM determines that the circumstances giving rise to a suspension ordered under Sec. 585.417 cannot be resolved within 5 years, the Secretary may initiate cancellation of the lease or grant, as provided in Sec. 585.437. Sec. Sec. 585.422-585.424 [Reserved]
