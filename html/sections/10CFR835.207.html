CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 835  /  Sec. 835.207 Occupational dose limits for minors.

The dose limits for minors occupationally exposed to radiation and/or radioactive materials at a DOE activity are 0.1 rem (0.001 Sv) total effective dose in a year and 10 percent of the occupational dose limits specified at Sec. 835.202(a)(3) and (a)(4). [72 FR 31926, June 8, 2007]
