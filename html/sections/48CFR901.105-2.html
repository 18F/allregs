CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 901  /  901.105-2 Arrangement of regulations.

(a) General. The DEAR is divided into the same parts, subparts, sections, subsections and paragraphs as is the FAR.

(b) Numbering. The numbering illustrations at 48 CFR 1.105-2(b) apply to the DEAR, but the DEAR numbering will be preceded with a 9 or a 90. Material which supplements the FAR will be assigned the numbers 70 and up. [61 FR 41704, Aug. 9, 1996. Redesignated and amended at 76 FR 7689, Feb. 11, 2011]
