CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 2638  /  Sec. 2638.302 Who may request a formal advisory opinion.

Any person (as defined in Sec. 2638.104) may request an opinion with respect to a situation in which that person is directly involved. A designated agency ethics official, representative, or attorney may request an opinion on behalf of the person. Notwithstanding this direct involvement requirement, a designated agency ethics official may always request an opinion concerning a situation about which he or she has knowledge.
