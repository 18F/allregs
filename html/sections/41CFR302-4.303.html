CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 302-4  /  Sec. 302-4.303 For relocation within the continental United States (CONUS), rate specified in Sec. 302-4.300?

No, for a PCS relocation within CONUS involving POV usage, your agency will reimburse you at the standard mileage rate specified in Sec. 302-4.300. [72 FR 35188, June 27, 2007]
