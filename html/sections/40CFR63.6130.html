CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 63  /  Sec. 63.6130 How do I demonstrate initial compliance with the emission

(a) You must demonstrate initial compliance with each emission and operating limitation that applies to you according to Table 4 of this subpart.

(b) You must submit the Notification of Compliance Status containing results of the initial compliance demonstration according to the requirements in Sec. 63.6145(f).
