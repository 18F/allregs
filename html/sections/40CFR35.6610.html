CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 35  /  Sec. 35.6610 Contracts awarded by a contractor.

The recipient must require its contractor to comply with the following provisions in the award of contracts (i.e. subcontracts). (This section does not apply to a supplier's procurement of materials to produce equipment, materials and catalog, off-the-shelf, or manufactured items.)

(a) The requirements referenced in Sec. 35.6020.

(b) The limitations on contract award in Sec. 35.6550(a)(6).

(c) [Reserved]

(d) The requirements regarding specifications in Sec. 35.6555 (a)(6) and (c).

(e) The Federal cost principles in 2 CFR part 200 subpart E.

(f) The prohibited types of contracts in Sec. 35.6575(a).

(g) The cost, price analysis, and profit analysis requirements in Sec. 35.6585.

(h) The applicable provisions in Sec. 35.6595 (b).

(i) The applicable provisions in Sec. 35.6555(b)(2). [72 FR 24504, May 2, 2007, as amended at 73 FR 15922, Mar. 26, 2008; 79 FR 76061, Dec. 19, 2014]
