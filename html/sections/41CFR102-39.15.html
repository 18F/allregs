CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 102-39  /  Sec. 102-39.15 How are the terms ``I'' and ``you'' used in this part?

Use of pronouns ``I'' and ``you'' throughout this part refer to executive agencies. [66 FR 48614, Sept. 21, 2001. Redesignated at 73 FR 50880, Aug. 29, 2008]
