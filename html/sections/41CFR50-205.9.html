CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 50-205  /  Sec. 50-205.9 Inspections by the Department of Labor.

The Director may conduct such inspections as he may find appropriate to assure compliance with the safety and health provisions of the Act or whenever he may find that a safety and health inspection should be carried out along with investigation under other provisions of the Act or the Fair Labor Standards Act of 1938. Whenever an inspection by the Director discloses apparent violations of State safety and health requirements, the Director shall report such disclosures to the State agency. (41 U.S.C. 40; 5 U.S.C. 556) [32 FR 7704, May 26, 1967]
