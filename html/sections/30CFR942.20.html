CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 942  /  Sec. 942.20 Approval of Tennessee reclamation plan for lands and

The Tennessee Reclamation Plan, as submitted on March 24, 1982, is approved. Copies of the approved program are available at: Office of Surface Mining Reclamation and Enforcement, 530 Gay Street, Suite 500, Knoxville, Tennessee 37902 State of Tennessee Department of Conservation, Division of Surface Mining and Reclamation, 305 West Springvale, Knoxville, Tennessee 37917 Office of Surface Mining Reclamation and Enforcement, Administrative Record, Room 5315, 1100 'L' Street, NW, Washington, DC 20240.
