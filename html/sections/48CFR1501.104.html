CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 1501  /  1501.104 Applicability.

The FAR (48 CFR chapter 1) and the EPAAR (48 CFR chapter 15) apply to all EPA acquisitions as defined in part 2 of the FAR, except where expressly excluded. [62 FR 33572, June 20, 1997]
