CFR /  Title 19  /  Part 144  /  Sec. 144.13 Bond requirements.

A bond on Customs Form 301, containing the bond conditions set forth in Sec. 113.62 of this chapter shall be filed in the amount required by the port director to support the entry documentation. [T.D, 84-213, 49 FR 41185, Oct. 19, 1984]
