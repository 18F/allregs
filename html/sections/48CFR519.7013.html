CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 519  /  519.7013 Obligation.

(a) The mentor or Protege may terminate the Agreement in accordance with 519.7010. The mentor will notify the Mentor-Protege Program Manager and the contracting officer, in writing, at least 30 days in advance of the mentor firm's intent to voluntarily withdraw from the Program or to terminate the Agreement, or upon receipt of a Protege's notice to withdraw from the Program.

(b) Mentor and Protege firms will submit a ``Lessons Learned'' evaluation to the GSA Mentor-Protege Program Manager at the conclusion or termination of each Mentor-Protege Agreement or withdrawal from the Mentor-Protege program.
