CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 1900  /  Sec. 1900.5 Assignment of cases.

The State Director may, in writing, assign responsibilities and functions to a different office or staff position within the Rural Development State organizational structure other than that referred to in regulations, provided no benefits, rights, or opportunities of the public are changed. [55 FR 43325, Oct. 29, 1990, as amended at 80 FR 9867, Feb. 24, 2015]
