CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 408  /  Sec. 408.905 Will we withhold or adjust an underpayment to reduce

We will withhold or adjust any underpayment due you to reduce any overpayment to you that we determine for a different period, unless we have waived recovery of the overpayment under the provisions of Secs. 408.910 through 408.914.
