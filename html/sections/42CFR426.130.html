CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 426  /  Sec. 426.130 Party submissions.

Any party submitting material, except for material for which a privilege is asserted, or proprietary data, to the ALJ or the Board after that party's initial challenge must serve the material on all other parties at the same time. Subpart B [Reserved]
