CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 63  /  Sec. 63.63 Deletion of ethylene glycol monobutyl ether from the list

The substance ethylene glycol monobutyl ether (EGBE,2-Butoxyethanol) (CAS Number 111-76-2) is deleted from the list of hazardous air pollutants established by 42 U.S.C. 7412(b)(1). [69 FR 69325, Nov. 29, 2004] Secs. 63.64-63.69 [Reserved]
