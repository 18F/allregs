CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 117  /  Sec. 117.268 Billy's Creek.

The draw of the State Road 80 bridge at Fort Myers need not be opened for the passage of vessels; however, the draw shall be restored to operable condition within 6 months after notification by the District Commander to do so. [CGD 07-98-009, 63 FR 67402, Dec. 7, 1998]
