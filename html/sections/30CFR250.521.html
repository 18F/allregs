CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 250  /  Sec. 250.521 When do I have to perform a casing diagnostic test?

(a) You must perform a casing diagnostic test within 30 days after first observing or imposing casing pressure according to the following table: ------------------------------------------------------------------------

you must perform a casing

(b) You are exempt from performing a diagnostic pressure test for the production casing on a well operating under active gas lift. [76 FR 64462, Oct. 18, 2011. Redesignated at 77 FR 50894, Aug. 22, 2012]
