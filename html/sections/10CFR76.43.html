CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 76  /  Sec. 76.43 Date for decision.

The Director will render a decision on an application within 6 months of the receipt of the application unless the Director alters the date for decisions and publishes notice of the new date in the Federal Register. [62 FR 6670, Feb. 12, 1997]
