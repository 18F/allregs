CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 835  /  Sec. 835.703 Other monitoring records.

The following information shall be documented and maintained:

(a) Results of monitoring for radiation and radioactive material as required by subparts E and L of this part, except for monitoring required by Sec. 835.1102(d);

(b) Results of monitoring used to determine individual occupational dose from external and internal sources;

(c) Results of monitoring for the release and control of material and equipment as required by Sec. 835.1101; and

(d) Results of maintenance and calibration performed on instruments and equipment as required by Sec. 835.401(b). [58 FR 65485, Dec. 14, 1993, as amended at 63 FR 59685, Nov. 4, 1998]
