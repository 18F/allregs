CFR /  Title 22  /  Part 41  /  Sec. 41.106 Processing.

Consular officers must ensure that the Form DS-160 or, alternatively, Form DS-156 is properly and promptly processed in accordance with the applicable regulations and instructions. [73 FR 23069, Apr. 29, 2008]
