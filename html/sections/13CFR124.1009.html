CFR /  Title 13  /  Part 124  /  Sec. 124.1009 Who decides disadvantaged status protests?

In response to a protest challenging the disadvantaged status of a concern, the SBA's AA/BD, or designee, will determine whether the concern is disadvantaged. [76 FR 8264, Feb. 11, 2011]
