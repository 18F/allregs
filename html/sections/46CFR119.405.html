CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 119  /  Sec. 119.405 Fuel restrictions.

The use of a fuel, other than diesel fuel, as an alternative fuel for an internal combustion engine, except gasoline when used as a fuel for outboard motors as allowed by Sec. 119.458 of this part, will be reviewed on a case-by-case basis by the Commandant. [CGD 85-080, 61 FR 922, Jan. 10, 1996, as amended by CGD 97-057, 62 FR 51047, Sept. 30, 1997]
