CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 1  /  Sec. 1.01-90 Commissioned, warrant, and petty officers.

Any commissioned, warrant, or petty officer of the United States Coast Guard may be authorized to carry out the functions delegated to superior officials under Sec. Sec. 1.01-1, 1.01-20, 1.01-30, 1.01-70, and 1.07-80, or redelegated under Sec. 1.01-85, within the jurisdiction of the cognizant official. They will do so under the supervision and general direction of that official. [CGD 91-225, 59 FR 66485, Dec. 27, 1994]
