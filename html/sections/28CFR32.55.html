CFR /  Title 28  /  Part 32  /  Sec. 32.55 Judicial appeal.

(a) Consistent with Sec. 32.8, any approval or denial described in Sec. 32.54(a) shall constitute the final agency determination.

(b) A claimant seeking relief from the denial of his claim may appeal judicially pursuant to the Act, at 42 U.S.C. 3796c-2. [73 FR 76536, Dec. 17, 2008]
