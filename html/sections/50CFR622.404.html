CFR /  Title 50  /  Part 622  /  Sec. 622.404 Prohibited gear and methods.

Also see Sec. 622.9 for additional prohibited gear and methods that apply more broadly to multiple fisheries or in some cases all fisheries.

(b)(4) for the bycatch limits applicable to a vessel that uses or has on board a net or trawl.
