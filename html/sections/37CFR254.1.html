CFR /  Title 37  /  Part 254  /  Sec. 254.1 General.

This part 254 establishes the compulsory license fees for coin-operated phonorecord players beginning on January 1, 1982, in accordance with the provisions of 17 U.S.C. 116. [45 FR 890, Jan. 5, 1981. Redesignated and amended at 59 FR 23993, May 9, 1994]
