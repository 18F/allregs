CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 661  /  Sec. 661.6 Certification requirements for procurement of steel or

If steel, iron, or manufactured products (as defined in Sec. Sec. 661.3 and 661.5 of this part) are being procured, the appropriate certificate as set forth below shall be completed and submitted by each bidder or offeror in accordance with the requirement contained in Sec. 661.13(b) of this part.

Certificate of Compliance with Buy America Requirements

The bidder or offeror hereby certifies that it will comply with the requirements of 49 U.S.C. 5323(j)(1), and the applicable regulations in 49 CFR part 661.
