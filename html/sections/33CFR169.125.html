CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 169  /  Sec. 169.125 What classes of ships are required to make reports?

Each self-propelled ship of 300 gross tons or greater must participate in the reporting systems, except government ships exempted from reporting by regulation V/8-1(c) of SOLAS. However, exempt ships are encouraged to participate in the reporting systems. [USCG-1999-5525, 66 FR 58070, Nov. 20, 2001]
