CFR /  Title 16  /  Part 501  /  Sec. 501.5 Paper table covers, bedsheets, pillowcases.

Table covers, bedsheets, and pillowcases, fabricated from paper, are exempt from the requirements of Sec. 500.12 of this chapter which specifies the expression of measurement of bidimensional commodities: Provided, That such commodities shall clearly present their actual length and width in terms of inches. [35 FR 19077, Dec. 17, 1970]
