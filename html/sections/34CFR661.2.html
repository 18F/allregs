CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 661  /  Sec. 661.2 Who is eligible to apply for a grant under the Business

Under this program the Secretary considers applications from institutions of higher education that have entered into agreements with business enterprises, trade organizations or associations engaged in international economic activity--or a combination or consortium of these enterprises, organizations or associations--for the purposes of pursuing the activities authorized under this program. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1130a)
