CFR /  Title 29  /  Part 541  /  Sec. 541.702 Exempt and nonexempt work.

The term ``exempt work'' means all work described in Sec. Sec. 541.100, 541.101, 541.200, 541.300, 541.301, 541.302, 541.303, 541.304, 541.400 and 541.500, and the activities directly and closely related to such work. All other work is considered ``nonexempt.''
