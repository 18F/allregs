CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 180  /  Sec. 180.570 Isoxadifen-ethyl; tolerances for residues.

(a) General. (1) Tolerances are established for residues of isoxadifen-ethyl (ethyl 5,5-diphenyl-2-isoxazoline-3-carboxylate, (CAS No. 163520-33-0), and its metabolite: 4,5-dihydro-5,5-diphenyl-3-isoxazolecarboxylic acid, when used as an inert ingredient (safener) in or on the following raw agricultural commodities: ------------------------------------------------------------------------

Parts per

Commodity million------------------------------------------------------------------------Corn, field, forage......................................... 0.20Corn, field, grain.......................................... 0.08Corn, field, stover......................................... 0.40Corn, oil................................................... 0.50Corn, pop, grain............................................ 0.04Corn, pop, stover........................................... 0.25Corn, sweet, forage......................................... 0.30Corn, sweet, kernel plus cob with husk removed.............. 0.04Corn, sweet, stover......................................... 0.45------------------------------------------------------------------------
