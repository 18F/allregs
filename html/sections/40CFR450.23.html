CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 450  /  Sec. 450.23 Effluent limitations reflecting the best conventional pollutant

Except as provided in 40 CFR 125.30 through 125.32, any point source subject to this subpart must achieve, at a minimum, the following effluent limitations representing the degree of effluent reduction attainable by application of the best conventional pollutant control technology (BCT). The effluent limitations are described at Sec. 450.21.
