CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 57  /  Sec. 57.114 Surrender of license.

Each license that is canceled, suspended, revoked, or expired shall immediately be surrendered by the licensee to the office of inspection serving the area in which the licensee is located. [69 FR 57167, Sept. 24, 2004]
