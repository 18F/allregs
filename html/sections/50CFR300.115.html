CFR /  Title 50  /  Part 300  /  Sec. 300.115 Appointment of a designated representative.

(a) All holders of permits authorizing fishing in subarea 48.3 must appoint a designated representative in the United States.

(b) The designated representative will be notified of closures under Sec. 300.111 and must transmit this information to the vessel on the grounds.

(c) The designated representative may receive catch reports from the vessel and transmit the reports to NMFS in writing. [61 FR 35550, July 5, 1996. Redesignated at 72 FR 48510, Aug. 23, 2007]
