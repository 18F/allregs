CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 222  /  Sec. 222.60 What are the scope and purpose of this subpart?

The regulations in this subpart implement section 8003(b)(2) of the Act, which provides financial assistance to certain heavily impacted local educational agencies (LEAs). The specific eligibility requirements are detailed in Sec. Sec. 222.62 through 222.66. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 7703(b)(2))
