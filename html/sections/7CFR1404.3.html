CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 1404  /  Sec. 1404.3 Payments which may be assigned.

Except as otherwise provided in this part or in individual program regulations, contracts and agreements entered into by FSA or CCC, any payment due a person from FSA or CCC may be assigned. [54 FR 52883, Dec. 22, 1989, as amended at 56 FR 361, Jan. 4, 1991]
