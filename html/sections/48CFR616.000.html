CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 616  /  616.000 Scope of part.

The contracting officer may use any of the contract types described in FAR part 16 for acquisitions made under simplified acquisition procedures. The contracting officer shall document his/her decision to use a contract type in accordance with the requirements of FAR part 16. [60 FR 39963, Aug. 3, 1995]
