CFR /  Title 39  /  Part 954  /  Sec. 954.25 Public information.

The Librarian of the Postal Service maintains for public inspection in the Library copies of all initial and final Agency decisions. The Recorder of the Postal Service maintains a complete official record of every proceeding. A person may examine a record upon authorization by the Judicial Officer. [36 FR 11567, June 16, 1971, as amended at 62 FR 66998, Dec. 23, 1997]
