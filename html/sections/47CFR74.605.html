CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 74  /  Sec. 74.605 Registration of stationary television pickup receive sites.

Licensees of TV pickup stations in the 6875-7125 MHz and 12700-13200 MHz bands shall register their stationary receive sites using the Commission's Universal Licensing System. [76 FR 59571, Sept. 27, 2011]
