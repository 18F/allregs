CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 70  /  Sec. 70.33 Applications for renewal of licenses.

Applications for renewal of a license should be filed in accordance with Sec. Sec. 70.21 and 70.22. Information contained in previous applications, statements or reports filed with the Commission under the license may be incorporated by reference, provided that such references are clear and specific. [75 FR 73944, Nov. 30, 2010]
