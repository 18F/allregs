CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 201  /  Sec. 201.6 Germination.

The complete record shall include the records of all laboratory tests for germination and hard seed for each lot of seed offered for transportation in whole or in part. The record shall show the kind of seed, lot number, date of test, percentage of germination and hard seeds, and such other information as may be necessary to show the method used. [5 FR 30, Jan. 4, 1940]
