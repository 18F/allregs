CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 7  /  Sec. 7.21 Formal complaints, applicability of Secs. 1.720 through

Formal complaints against a manufacturer or provider, as defined under this subpart, may be filed in the form and in the manner prescribed under Sec. Sec. 1.720 through 1.736 of this chapter. Commission staff may grant waivers of, or exceptions to, particular requirements under Secs. 1.720 through 1.736 for good cause shown; provided, however, that such waiver authority may not be exercised in a manner that relieves, or has the effect of relieving, a complainant of the obligation under Secs. 1.720 and 1.728 of this chapter to allege facts which, if true, are sufficient to constitute a violation or violations of section 255 of the Act or this chapter.
