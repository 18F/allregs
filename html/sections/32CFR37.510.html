CFR /  Title 32  /  Part 37  /  Sec. 37.510 What are my responsibilities for determining that a

Prior to award of a TIA, your responsibilities for determining that the recipient is qualified are the same as those of a grants officer who is awarding a grant or cooperative agreement. Those responsibilities are described in subpart D of 32 CFR part 22. When the recipient is a consortium that is not formally incorporated, you have the additional responsibility described in Sec. 37.515.
