CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 201  /  Sec. 201.27 Name of shipper or consignee.

The full name and address of either the shipper, or consignee, shall appear upon the label except that if the name and address of the shipper are not shown, a code designation identifying the shipper shall be shown. [5 FR 31, Jan. 4, 1940]
