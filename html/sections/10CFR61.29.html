CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 61  /  Sec. 61.29 Post-closure observation and maintenance.

Following completion of closure authorized in Sec. 61.28, the licensee shall observe, monitor, and carry out necessary maintenance and repairs at the disposal site until the license is transferred by the Commission in accordance with Sec. 61.30. Responsibility for the disposal site must be maintained by the licensee for 5 years. A shorter or longer time period for post-closure observation and maintenance may be established and approved as part of the site closure plan, based on site-specific conditions.
