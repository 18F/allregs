CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 1774  /  Sec. 1774.4 Allocation of funds.

The Secretary may use not more than four percent of the total amount of funds made available for a fiscal year for water and waste disposal activities for SEARCH grants. Sec. Sec. 1774.5-1774.6 [Reserved]
