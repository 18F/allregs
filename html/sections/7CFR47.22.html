CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 47  /  Sec. 47.22 Argument before Secretary.

(a) Oral argument. There shall be no right to oral argument other than as provided in Sec. 47.15(h).

(b). Briefs filed in accordance with Sec. 47.19(c) and those filed in support of statements of fact will also be considered by the Secretary. [10 FR 2209, Feb. 27, 1945, as amended at 12 FR 1026, Feb. 13, 1947; 60 FR 8462, Feb. 14, 1995]
