CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 1  /  Sec. 1.47 NRC Regional Offices.

Each Regional Administrator executes established NRC policies and assigned programs relating to inspection, enforcement, licensing, State agreements, State liaison, and emergency response within Regional boundaries set out in Sec. 1.5(b) of this part.
