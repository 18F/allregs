CFR /  Title 28  /  Part 68  /  Sec. 68.56 Judicial review of a final agency order in cases

A person or entity adversely affected by a final agency order may file, within forty-five (45) days after the date of the final agency order, a petition in the United States Court of Appeals for the appropriate circuit for review of the final agency order. Failure to request review by the Chief Administrative Hearing Officer of a final order by an Administrative Law Judge shall not prevent a party from seeking judicial review. [Order No. 2203-99, 64 FR 7083, Feb. 12, 1999]
