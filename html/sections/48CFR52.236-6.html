CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 52  /  52.236-6 Superintendence by the Contractor.

As prescribed in 36.506, insert the following clause:

Superintendence by the Contractor (APR 1984)

At all times during performance of this contract and until the work is completed and accepted, the Contractor shall directly superintend the work or assign and have on the work a competent superintendent who is satisfactory to the Contracting Officer and has authority to act for the Contractor.

(End of clause) [48 FR 42478, Sept. 19, 1983, as amended at 60 FR 34761, July 3, 1995]
