CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 274  /  Sec. 274.6 Division/district pest control programs.

(a) Guides. Referenced technical manuals, and Engineer Circulars issued from time to time, will be used as guides in selecting the type of chemicals and the method of application in the control of vegetation and pests at civil works projects.

(b) Responsibilities and reports (RCS DAEN-CWO-48). Districts will prepare and submit to the Divisions detailed descriptions or their anticipated use of pesticides for review and approval by the appropriate Division.
