CFR /  Title 9  /  Part 114  /  Sec. 114.12 Expiration date required.

Each serial or subserial of biological product prepared in a licensed establishment shall be given an expiration date determined in accordance with the requirements provided in Sec. 114.13 or Sec. 114.14. A licensed biological product shall be considered worthless under the Virus-Serum-Toxin Act subsequent to the expiration date appearing on the label. [41 FR 44687, Oct. 12, 1976]
