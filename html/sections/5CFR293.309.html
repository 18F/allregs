CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 293  /  Sec. 293.309 Reconstruction of lost OPFs.

Agencies will take necessary precautions to safeguard all OPFs. In the event of a lost or destroyed OPF, the current (or last, in the case of a former Federal employee) employing agency shall take the necessary action to reconstruct the essential portions of the OPF as specified in the Guide to Personnel Recordkeeping or other Office instructions. [50 FR 3309, Jan. 24, 1985, as amended at 66 FR 66709, Dec. 27, 2001]
