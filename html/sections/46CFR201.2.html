CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 201  /  Sec. 201.2 Mailing address; hours.

Documents required to be filed in, and correspondence relating to, proceedings governed by the regulations in this part should be addressed to ``Secretary, Maritime Administration, Department of Transportation, Washington, DC 20590.'' The Office of the Secretary, Maritime Administration, including the public document reading room, located in room 7210, 400 Seventh Street, SW., Washington, DC 20590, is open from 8:30 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. [55 FR 12358, Apr. 3, 1990, as amended at 63 FR 9157, Feb. 24, 1998]
