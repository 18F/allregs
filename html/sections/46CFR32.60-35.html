CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 32  /  Sec. 32.60-35 Tank vessels carrying Grade A liquid cargo--TB/ALL.

(a) Grade A liquids having a Reid vapor pressure in excess of 25 pounds per square inch shall be transported in cargo tanks which are independent of the hull.

(b) Barges carrying Grade A liquids having a Reid vapor pressure in excess of 25 pounds per square inch shall be of a Type III barge hull as defined in Sec. 32.63-5(b)(3). [CGFR 70-10, 35 FR 3709, Feb. 25, 1970]
