CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 300  /  Sec. 300.119 Technical assistance and training activities.

Each SEA must carry out activities to ensure that teachers and administrators in all public agencies--

(a) Are fully informed about their responsibilities for implementing Sec. 300.114; and

(b) Are provided with technical assistance and training necessary to assist them in this effort. (Approved by the Office of Management and Budget under control number 1820-0030) (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1412(a)(5))
