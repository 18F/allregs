CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 302-2  /  Sec. 302-2.101 When may we authorize reimbursement for relocation

You may authorize reimbursement for relocation expenses:

(a) When you have determined that an employee's permanent change of station is in the best interest of the Government;

(b) Only after an employee has signed a service agreement to remain in service for the period specified in Sec. 302-2.14; and

(c) When you have determined that the employee's relocation is incident to his/her change of official station. [FTR Amdt. 98, 66 FR 58196, Nov. 20, 2001, as amended by FTR Amdt. 2014- 01, 79 FR 49645, Aug. 21, 2014]
