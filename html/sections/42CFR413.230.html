CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 413  /  Sec. 413.230 Determining the per treatment payment amount.

The per-treatment payment amount is the sum of:

(a) The per treatment base rate established in Sec. 413.220, adjusted for wages as described in Sec. 413.231, and adjusted for facility-level and patient-level characteristics described in Sec. Sec. 413.232 and 413.235 of this part;

(b) Any outlier payment under Sec. 413.237; and

(c) Any training adjustment add-on under Sec. 414.335(b). [75 FR 49200, Aug. 12, 2010]
