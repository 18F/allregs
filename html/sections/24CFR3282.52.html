CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 3282  /  Sec. 3282.52 Address of communications.

Unless otherwise specified, communications shall be addressed to the Administrator, Office of Manufactured Housing Programs, Office of Housing, Department of Housing and Urban Development, 451 7th Street SW., Washington, DC 20410. [78 FR 60199, Oct. 1, 2013]
