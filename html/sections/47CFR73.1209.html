CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 73  /  Sec. 73.1209 References to time.

Unless specifically designated as ``standard (non-advanced)'' or ``advanced,'' all references to time contained in this part, and in license documents and other authorizations issued thereunder shall be understood to mean local time; i.e., the time legally observed in the community. [39 FR 26736, July 23, 1974]
