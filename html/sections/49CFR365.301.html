CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 365  /  Sec. 365.301 Applicable rules.

Generally, all application proceedings are governed by the FMCSA's Rules of Practice at part 386 of this chapter except as designated below.

Effective Date Note: At 78 FR 52647, Aug. 23, 2013, Sec. 365.301 was removed, effective Oct. 23, 2015.
