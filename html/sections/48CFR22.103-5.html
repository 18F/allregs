CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 22  /  22.103-5 Contract clauses.

(a) The contracting officer shall insert the clause 52.222-1, Notice to the Government of Labor Disputes, in solicitations and contracts that involve programs or requirements that have been designated under 22.101- 1(e).

(b) a cost-plus-incentive-fee contract that will provide a swing from the target fee of at least plus or minus 3 percent and a contractor's share of at least 10 percent is contemplated. [48 FR 42258, Sept. 19, 1983, as amended at 71 FR 57367, Sept. 28, 2006]
