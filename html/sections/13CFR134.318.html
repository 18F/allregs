CFR /  Title 13  /  Part 134  /  Sec. 134.318 NAICS appeals.

(a) The regulations at Secs. 121.402, 121.1102, and 121.1103 of this chapter also apply to NAICS code appeals.

(b) Effect of OHA's decision. If OHA grants the appeal (changes the NAICS code), and the contracting officer receives OHA's decision by the date offers are due, the contracting officer must amend the solicitation to reflect the new NAICS code. If the contracting officer receives OHA's decision after the date offers are due, OHA's decision will not apply to the pending procurement, but will apply to future solicitations for the same supplies or services.

(c) Summary dismissal. OHA may summarily dismiss a NAICS appeal either on the Judge's own initiative or on motion by a party. A summary dismissal may be with or without prejudice, and may be issued before the date set for close of record. Grounds for summary dismissal include: premature appeal, withdrawn appeal, settlement, cancellation of the procurement, and contract award. [75 FR 47442, Aug. 6, 2010]
