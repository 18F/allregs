CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 60-30  /  Sec. 60-30.2 Waiver, modification.

Upon notice to all parties, the Administrative Law Judge may, with respect to matters pending before him modify or waive any rule herein upon a determination that no party will be prejudiced and that the ends of justice will be served thereby. [43 FR 49259, Oct. 20, 1978; 43 FR 51401, Nov. 3, 1978]
