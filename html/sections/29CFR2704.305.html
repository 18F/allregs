CFR /  Title 29  /  Part 2704  /  Sec. 2704.305 Settlement.

In the event that counsel for the Secretary and an applicant agree to settle an EAJA claim after an application has been filed with the Commission, the applicant shall timely notify the Commission of the settlement and request dismissal of the application. [63 FR 63177, Nov. 12, 1998]
