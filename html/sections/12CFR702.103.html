CFR /  Title 12  /  Part 702  /  Sec. 702.103 Applicability of the risk-based capital ratio measure.

For purposes of Sec. 702.102, a credit union is defined as ``complex'' and the risk-based capital ratio measure is applicable only if the credit union's quarter-end total assets exceed one hundred million dollars ($100,000,000), as reflected in its most recent Call Report.
