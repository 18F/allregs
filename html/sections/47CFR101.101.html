F--FixedM--MobileTF--Temporary Fixed(1)--Applications for frequencies in the 932.5-935/941.5-944 MHz bands may be filed initially during a one-week period to be announced by public notice.

After these applications have been processed, the Commission will announce by public notice a filing date for remaining frequencies. From this filing

date forward, applications will be processed on a daily first-come, first-served basis.
