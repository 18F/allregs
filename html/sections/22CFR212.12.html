CFR /  Title 22  /  Part 212  /  Sec. 212.12 Effect of nonpublication.

The materials referenced in Sec. 212.11 shall not be binding upon or otherwise adversely affect a person unless either

(a) The materials were in fact published in the Federal Register or

(b) The person otherwise had actual and timely notice of the content of such materials.
