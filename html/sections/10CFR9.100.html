CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 9  /  Sec. 9.100 Scope of subpart.

This subpart prescribes procedures pursuant to which NRC meetings shall be open to public observation pursuant to the provisions of 5 U.S.C. 552b. This subpart does not affect the procedures pursuant to which NRC records are made available to the public for inspection and copying which remain governed by subpart A, except that the exemptions set forth in Sec. 9.104(a) shall govern in the case of any request made pursuant to Sec. 9.23 to copy or inspect the transcripts, recordings, or minutes described in Sec. 9.108. Access to records considered at NRC meetings shall continue to be governed by subpart A of this part. [52 FR 49362, Dec. 31, 1987]
