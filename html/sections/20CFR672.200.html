CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 672  /  Sec. 672.200 How are YouthBuild grants funded and administered?

The Secretary uses funds authorized for appropriation under sec. 173A of the Workforce Investment Act (WIA) to administer YouthBuild as a national program under Title I, Subtitle D of the Act. YouthBuild grants are awarded to eligible entities, as defined in Sec. 672.110, through a competitive selection process described in Sec. 672.205.
