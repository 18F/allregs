CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 1160  /  Sec. 1160.7 Certificate of national interest.

After preliminary review applications for international exhibitions with eligible items under Sec. 1160.4 will be submitted to the Secretary of State or his designee for determination of national interest and issuance of a Certificate of National Interest. [56 FR 49848, Oct. 2, 1991. Redesignated at 60 FR 42465, Aug. 16, 1995, and further redesignated and revised at 73 FR 21056, Apr. 18, 2008]
