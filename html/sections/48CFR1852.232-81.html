CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 1852  /  1852.232-81 Contract funding.

As prescribed in 1832.705-270(b), insert the following clause:

Contract Funding (JUN 1990)

(a) For purposes of payment of cost, exclusive of fee, in accordance with the Limitation of Funds clause, the total amount allotted by the Government to this contract is $______. This allotment is for [Insert applicable item number(s), task(s), or work description] _____ and covers the following estimated period of performance: _____.

(b) An additional amount of $______ is obligated under this contract for payment of fee.

(End of clause) [54 FR 28340, July 5, 1989, as amended at 55 FR 27090, June 29, 1990; 57 FR 40856, Sept. 8, 1992]
