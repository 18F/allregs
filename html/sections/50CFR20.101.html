CFR /  Title 50  /  Part 20  /  Sec. 20.101 Seasons, limits and shooting hours for Puerto Rico

This section provides for the annual hunting of certain doves, pigeons, ducks, coots, gallinules and snipe in Puerto Rico; and for certain doves, pigeons and ducks in the Virgin Islands. In these Commonwealths, the hunting of waterfowl and coots (and other certain species, as applicable) must be with the use of nontoxic shot beginning in the 1991-92 waterfowl season. [53 FR 24290, June 28, 1988]
