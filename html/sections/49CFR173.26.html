CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 173  /  Sec. 173.26 Quantity limitations.

When quantity limitations do not appear in the packaging requirements of this subchapter, the permitted gross weight or capacity authorized for a packaging is as shown in the packaging specification or standard in part 178 or 179, as applicable, of this subchapter. [Amdt. 173-224, 55 FR 52612, Dec. 21, 1990]
