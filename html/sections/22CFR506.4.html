CFR /  Title 22  /  Part 506  /  Sec. 506.4 Annual goals and timetables.

A Board-wide plan for promoting part-time employment opportunities will be developed annually by the Office of Personnel after consultation with the operating elements. This plan will establish annual goals and set deadlines for achieving these goals. [44 FR 63098, Nov. 2, 1979, as amended at 51 FR 11015, Apr. 1, 1986]
