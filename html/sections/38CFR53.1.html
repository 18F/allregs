CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 53  /  Sec. 53.1 Purpose and scope.

In accordance with the provisions of 38 U.S.C. 1744, this part sets forth the mechanism for a State to obtain payments to assist a State Veterans Home (SVH) in the hiring and retention of nurses for the purpose of reducing nursing shortages at that SVH. (Authority: 38 U.S.C. 101, 501, 1744)
