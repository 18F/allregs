CFR /  Title 14  /  Part 95  /  Sec. 95.8001 General.

This subpart prescribes COP's for Federal airways, jet routes, area navigation routes, or other direct routes for which an MEA is designated in this part. Unless otherwise specified the COP is midway between the navigation facilities or way points for straight route segments, or at the intersection of radials or courses forming a dogleg in the case of dogleg route segments. [Doc. No. 10580, 35 FR 14610, Sept. 18, 1970]
