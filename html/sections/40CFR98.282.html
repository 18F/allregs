CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 98  /  Sec. 98.282 GHGs to report.

You must report:

(a) CO2 process emissions from all silicon carbide process units or furnaces combined.

(b) CO2, CH4, and N2O emissions from each stationary combustion unit. You must report these emissions under subpart C of this part (General Stationary Fuel Combustion Sources) by following the requirements of subpart C. [74 FR 56374, Oct. 30, 2009, as amended at 78 FR 71966, Nov. 29, 2013]
