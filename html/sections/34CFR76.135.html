CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 76  /  Sec. 76.135 Are there any requirements for matching funds?

The Secretary waives all requirements for matching funds for those programs that are consolidated by an Insular Area in a consolidated grant application. (Authority: 48 U.S.C. 1469a) [47 FR 17421, Apr. 22, 1982]
