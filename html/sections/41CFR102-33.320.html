CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 102-33  /  Sec. 102-33.320 What must we do if we are unable to perform required

If you are unable to perform the required mutilation of aircraft parts, you must turn the parts in to a Federal or Federally-approved facility for mutilation and proper disposition. Ensure that any contractor follows the provisions of Sec. 102-33.315 for mutilating and disposing of the parts.
