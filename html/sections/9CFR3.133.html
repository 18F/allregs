CFR /  Title 9  /  Part 3  /  Sec. 3.133 Separation.

Animals housed in the same primary enclosure must be compatible. Animals shall not be housed near animals that interfere with their health or cause them discomfort. Sec. Sec. 3.134-3.135 [Reserved]
