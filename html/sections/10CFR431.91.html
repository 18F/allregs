CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 431  /  Sec. 431.91 Purpose and scope.

This subpart specifies test procedures and energy conservation standards for certain commercial air conditioners and heat pumps, pursuant to Part C of Title III of the Energy Policy and Conservation Act, as amended, 42 U.S.C. 6311-6317. [69 FR 61969, Oct. 21, 2004, as amended at 70 FR 60415, Oct. 18, 2005]
