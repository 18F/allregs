CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 180  /  Sec. 180.1149 Inclusion bodies of the multi-nuclear polyhedrosis virus

The microbial pest control agent inclusion bodies of the multi-nuclear polyhedrosis virus of Anagrapha falcifera is exempted from the requirement of a tolerance in or on all raw agricultural commodities when used to control certain lepidopteran pest species. [60 FR 37020, July 19, 1995] Sec. 180.1150 6-Benzyladenine; exemption from the requirement of atolerance.

The biochemical plant regulator 6-benzyladenine (6-BA) is exempt from the requirement of a tolerance in or on apple and pear when applied at a rate of <=182 grams of active ingredient per acre per season, and in or on pistachio when applied at a rate of <=60 grams of active ingredient per acre per season. [72 FR 13179, Mar. 21, 2007]
