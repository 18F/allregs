CFR /  Title 14  /  Part 91  /  Sec. 91.805 Final compliance: Subsonic airplanes.

Except as provided in Sec. Sec. 91.809 and 91.811, on and after January 1, 1985, no person may operate to or from an airport in the United States any subsonic airplane covered by this subpart unless that airplane has been shown to comply with Stage 2 or Stage 3 noise levels under part 36 of this chapter.
