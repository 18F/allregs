CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 230  /  Sec. 230.5 Exception concerning service to a local lodge or division.

In determining whether an annuity is subject to the provisions of this part the Board shall disregard any compensated service rendered after December 31, 1936, to a local lodge or division of a railway-labor-organization employer if the compensation for such service is required to be disregarded under the provisions of Sec. 222.3(f) of this chapter. [Board Order 40-742, 6 FR 298, Jan. 14, 1941. Redesignated at 47 FR 7656, Feb. 22, 1982]
