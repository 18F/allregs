CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 319  /  Sec. 319.37-12 Prohibited articles and articles whose importationarticles.

A restricted article for importation into the United States may not be packed in the same container as an article whose importation into the United States is prohibited by this subpart or in the same container as an article whose importation is not authorized pending pest risk analysis under Sec. 319.37-2a of this subpart. [76 FR 31210, May 27, 2011]
