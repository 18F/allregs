CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 1309  /  Sec. 1309.11 Fee amounts.

(a) For each application for registration or reregistration to manufacture the applicant shall pay an annual fee of $3,047.

(b) For each application for registration or reregistration to distribute, import, or export a List I chemical, the applicant shall pay an annual fee of $1,523. [77 FR 15250, Mar. 15, 2012]
