CFR /  Title 32  /  Part 235  /  Sec. 235.7 Information requirements.

The Chair of the Board shall submit to the PDUSD(P&R;) an annual report documenting the activities, decisions, and membership of the Board. Negative reports are required. The annual report shall be due on October 1st of each year and is not subject to the licensing internal information requirements of DoD 8910.1-M. \2\---------------------------------------------------------------------------

\2\ Copies may be obtained at http://www.dtic.mil/whs/directives/.---------------------------------------------------------------------------
