CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 772  /  Sec. 772.1 Scope and purpose.

This part establishes the requirements and procedures applicable to coal exploration operations on all lands except for Federal lands subject to the requirements of 43 CFR parts 3480-3487. [48 FR 40634, Sept. 8, 1983, as amended at 48 FR 44779, Sept. 30, 1983]
