CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 51  /  Sec. 51.105 Approval of plans.

Revisions of a plan, or any portion thereof, will not be considered part of an applicable plan until such revisions have been approved by the Administrator in accordance with this part. [51 FR 40661, Nov. 7, 1986, as amended at 60 FR 33922, June 29, 1995]
