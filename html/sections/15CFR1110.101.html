CFR /  Title 15  /  Part 1110  /  Sec. 1110.101 Submission of certification.

In order to become certified under the certification program established under this part, a Person must submit a completed certification statement, using the form NTIS FM161 with OMB Control Number 0692-0013, and its accompanying instructions at https://dmf.ntis.gov.
