CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 455  /  Sec. 455.11 Compliance date for pretreatment standards for existing sources

All discharges subject to pretreatment standards for existing sources (PSES) in subparts A and B of this part must comply with the standards no later than September 28, 1993. [61 FR 57549, Nov. 6, 1996]
