CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 56  /  Sec. 56.50-10 Special gauge requirements.

(a) Where pressure-reducing valves are employed, a pressure gauge must be provided on the low-pressure side of the reducing station.

(b) Fuel oil service, fire, cargo and fuel oil transfer and boiler feed pumps must be provided with a pressure gage on the discharge side of the pump. Additional information pertaining to fire pumps is in Sec. 34.10-5 of subchapter D (Tank Vessels), Sec. 76.10-5 of subchapter H (Passenger Vessels), Sec. 95.10-5 of subchapter I (Cargo and Miscellaneous Vessels), and Sec. 108.417 of subchapter IA (Mobile Offshore Drilling Units) of this chapter.
