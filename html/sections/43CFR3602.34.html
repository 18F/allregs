CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 3602  /  Sec. 3602.34 What is the term of a noncompetitive contract?

BLM will not issue a noncompetitive contract for the sale of mineral materials for a term exceeding 5 years, excluding any contract extension under Sec. 3602.27 and any period that BLM may allow for removal of equipment and improvements under Sec. 3601.52.
