CFR /  Title 28  /  Part 45  /  Sec. 45.1 Cross-reference to ethical standards and financial

Employees of the Department of Justice are subject to the executive branch-wide Standards of Ethical Conduct at 5 CFR part 2635, the Department of Justice regulations at 5 CFR part 3801 which supplement the executive branch-wide standards, the executive branch-wide financial disclosure regulations at 5 CFR part 2634 and the executive branch-wide employee responsibilities and conduct regulations at 5 CFR part 735. [61 FR 59815, Nov. 25, 1996]
