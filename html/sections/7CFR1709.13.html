CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 1709  /  Sec. 1709.13 Grant administration.

The authority to approve administrative actions is vested in the Administrator except as otherwise provided in the RUS delegations of authority. Administration of RUS grants is governed by the provisions of this subpart and subpart B of this part, the terms of the grant agreement and, as applicable, the provisions of 2 CFR part 200, as adopted by USDA through 2 CFR part 400. [70 FR 5351, Feb. 2, 2005, as amended at 79 FR 76002, Dec. 19, 2014]
