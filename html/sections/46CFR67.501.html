CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 67  /  Sec. 67.501 Application for Certificate of Documentation.

An application fee is charged for an initial Certificate of Documentation in accordance with subpart K of this part. If application is made for any trade endorsement, the applicable fee under Sec. 67.511 will be charged in addition to the application fee for the Certificate. The application fee does not include the fee under Sec. 67.527 for filing and recording any required bills of sale or instruments in the nature of a bill of sale, or the application fee under Sec. 67.519 for waivers in accordance with Sec. Sec. 67.89 or 67.101.
