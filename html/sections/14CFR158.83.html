CFR /  Title 14  /  Part 158  /  Sec. 158.83 Informal resolution.

The Administrator shall undertake informal resolution with the public agency or any other affected party if, after review under Sec. 158.71, the Administrator cannot determine that PFC revenue is being used for the approved projects in accordance with the terms of the Administrator's approval to impose a PFC for those projects or with 49 U.S.C. 40117. [Doc. No. 26385, 56 FR 24278, May 29, 1991, as amended by Amdt. 158-2, 65 FR 34543, May 30, 2000]
