CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 385  /  Sec. 385.20 Scope.

Only those definitions needed to understand this part will be defined. Generally, terms defined elsewhere in statutes, OMB circulars, and other Federal requirements will not be restated. Special attention is directed to the definitions in the Standard General Provisions of MarAd's grant and cooperative agreements (See Sec. 385.62).
