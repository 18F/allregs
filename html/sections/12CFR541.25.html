CFR /  Title 12  /  Part 541  /  Sec. 541.25 Single-family dwelling.

The term single-family dwelling means a structure designed for residential use by one family, or a unit so designed, whose owner owns, directly or through a non-profit cooperative housing organization, an undivided interest in the underling real estate, including property owned in common with others which contributes to the use and enjoyment of the structure or unit. [69 FR 76602, Dec. 22, 2004]
