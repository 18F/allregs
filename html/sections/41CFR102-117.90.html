CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 102-117  /  Sec. 102-117.90 May I use a U.S. Government bill of lading (GBL) services?

You may use the Government bill of lading (GBL) only for international shipments (including domestic offshore shipments). [75 FR 31393, Aug. 20, 2010]
