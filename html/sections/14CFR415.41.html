CFR /  Title 14  /  Part 415  /  Sec. 415.41 Accident investigation plan.

An applicant must file an accident investigation plan (AIP), that satisfies Sec. 417.111(g) of this chapter, and contains the applicant's procedures for reporting and responding to launch accidents, launch incidents, or other mishaps, as defined by Sec. 401.5 of this chapter. [Doc. No. FAA-2000-7953, 71 FR 50531, Aug. 25, 2006]
