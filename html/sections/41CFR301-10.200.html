CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 301-10  /  Sec. 301-10.200 What types of Government vehicles may my agency

You may be authorized to use:

(a) A Government-furnished automobile in accordance with Sec. 301-10.220;

(b) A Government aircraft in accordance with Sec. Sec. 301-10.260 through 301-10.262 of this part; and

(c) Other type of Government vehicle in accordance with any Government-issued rules governing its use. [FTR Amdt. 70, 63 FR 15955, Apr. 1, 1998; 63 FR 35537, June 30, 1998; Amdt.2015-03, 80 FR 27261, May 13, 2015]
