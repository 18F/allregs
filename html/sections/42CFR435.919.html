CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 435  /  Sec. 435.919 Timely and adequate notice concerning adverse actions.

(a) The agency must give beneficiaries timely and adequate notice of proposed action to terminate, discontinue, or suspend their eligibility or to reduce or discontinue services they may receive under Medicaid.

(b) The notice must meet the requirements of subpart E of part 431 of this subchapter. [44 FR 17937, Mar. 23, 1979, as amended at 45 FR 24887, Apr. 11, 1980; 51 FR 7211, Feb. 28, 1986]
