CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 483  /  Sec. 483.110 Out-of-State arrangements.

(a) Basic rule. The State in which the individual is a State resident (or would be a State resident at the time he or she becomes eligible for Medicaid), as defined in Sec. 435.403 of this chapter, must pay for the PASARR and make the required determinations, in accordance with Sec. 431.52(b).

(b) Agreements. A State may include arrangements for PASARR in its provider agreements with out-of-State facilities or reciprocal interstate agreements. [57 FR 56506, Nov. 30, 1992; 58 FR 25784, Apr. 28, 1993]
