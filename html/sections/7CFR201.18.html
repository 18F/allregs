CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 201  /  Sec. 201.18 Other agricultural seeds (crop seeds).

Agricultural seeds other than those included in the percentage or percentages of kind, variety, or type may be expressed as ``crop seeds'' or ``other crop seeds,'' but the percentage shall include collectively all kinds, varieties, or types not named upon the label. [5 FR 31, Jan. 4, 1940]
