CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 437  /  Sec. 437.24 New source performance standards (NSPS).

Except as provided in Sec. 437.20(b), any new source subject to this subpart must achieve the following performance standards: Standards for oil and grease, pH, TSS, arsenic, cadmium, chromium, cobalt, copper, lead, mercury, tin, zinc, butylbenzyl phthalate, carbazole, n-decane, bis(2-ethylhexyl) phthalate, fluoranthene, and n-octadecane are the same as the corresponding limitation specified in Sec. 437.21. [65 FR 81300, Dec. 22, 2000, as amended at 68 FR 71023, Dec. 22, 2003]
