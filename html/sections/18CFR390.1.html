CFR /  Title 18  /  Part 390  /  Sec. 390.1 Electronic registration.

Any person who wishes to engage in any of the activities listed in Sec. 390.2 must register electronically through the Commission's web site, in compliance with instructions located on the Web site, at http://www.ferc.gov.

Editorial Note: At 68 FR 7416, Feb. 14, 2003, Sec. 390.1 was suspended, effective Jan. 7, 2003.
