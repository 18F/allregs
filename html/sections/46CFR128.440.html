CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 128  /  Sec. 128.440 Bilge systems.

(a) Except as provided by this section, each bilge system must comply with Sec. Sec. 56.50-50 and 56.50-55 of this chapter.

(b) If the steering room, engine room, centerline passageway, forward machinery space, and compartment containing the dry-mud tanks are the only below-deck spaces that must be fitted with bilge suctions, the vessel may be equipped to the standards of Sec. Sec. 56.50-50 and 56.50-57 of this chapter applicable to a dry-cargo vessel of less than 55 meters (180 feet) in length.
