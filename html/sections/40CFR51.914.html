CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 51  /  Sec. 51.914 What new source review requirements apply for 8-hour

The requirements for new source review for the 8-hour ozone standard are located in Sec. 51.165 of this part. [70 FR 71702, Nov. 29, 2005]
