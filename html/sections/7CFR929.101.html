CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 929  /  Sec. 929.101 Minimum exemption.

The requirements of Sec. 929.41 Assessments and Sec. 929.54 Withholding shall not apply to any handler in a fiscal year during which the handler handles notmore than a total of 300 barrels of cranberries. [53 FR 12374, Apr. 14, 1988]
