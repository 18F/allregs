CFR /  Title 44  /  Part 5  /  Sec. 5.29 Effect of failure to make information materials available.

Materials requested to be made available pursuant to Sec. 5.24 that affect a member of the public may be relied upon, used, or cited as precedent by FEMA against any private party only if (a) they have been indexed and either made available or published as required by 5 U.S.C. 552(a)(2), or (b) the private party has actual and timely notice of their terms.
