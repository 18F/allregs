CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 658  /  Sec. 658.423 Handling of other complaints by the Regional

Whenever the regional office receives a JS-related complaint and the appropriate official determines that the nature and scope of the complaint are such that the time required to exhaust the administrative procedures at the State level would adversely affect a significant number of applicants, he/she shall take the complaint and follow up on the complaint as follows: for a complaint against an employer, the regional office shall handle the complaint in a manner consistent with the requirements imposed upon State agencies by Sec. Sec. 658.413 and 658.416 of this part. A hearing shall be offered to the parties once the Regional Administrator makes a determination on the complaint. For a complaint against a State agency, the regional office shall follow procedures established at Sec. 658.702(c).
