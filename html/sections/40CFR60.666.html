CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 60  /  Sec. 60.666 Reconstruction.

For purposes of this subpart ``fixed capital cost of the new components,'' as used in Sec. 60.15, includes the fixed capital cost of all depreciable components which are or will be replaced pursuant to all continuous programs of component replacement which are commenced within any 2-year period following December 30, 1983. For purposes of this paragraph, ``commenced'' means that an owner or operator has undertaken a continuous program of component replacement or that an owner or operator has entered into a contractual obligation to undertake and complete, within a reasonable time, a continuous program of component replacement.
