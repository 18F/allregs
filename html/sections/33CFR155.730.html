CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 155  /  Sec. 155.730 Compliance with transfer procedures.

The vessel operator of each vessel required by Sec. 155.720 to have transfer procedures shall maintain them current and shall require vessel personnel to use the transfer procedures for each transfer operation. [CGD 75-124, 45 FR 7175, Jan. 31, 1980, as amended by CGD 86-034, 55 FR 36254, Sept. 4, 1990]
