CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 404  /  Sec. 404.1618 Notifying claimants of the disability determination.

The State agency will prepare denial notices in accordance with subpart J of this part whenever it makes a disability determination which is fully or partially unfavorable to the claimant. [46 FR 29204, May 29, 1981, as amended at 75 FR 33168, June 11, 2010]
