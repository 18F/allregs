CFR /  Title 36  /  Part 223  /  Sec. 223.218 Consistency with plans, environmental standards, and

The disposal of special forest products from National Forest System lands shall be consistent with applicable land management plans. Each contract, permit, or other authorizing instrument shall include, as appropriate, provisions requiring the person or user to:

(a) Provide fire protection and suppression;

(b) Protect natural resources;

(c) Regenerate harvested species after harvesting operations;

(d) Minimize soil erosion;

(e) Maintain favorable conditions of water flow and quality;

(f) Minimize adverse effects on, protect, or enhance other national forest resources, uses, and improvements; and

(g) Deposit voucher specimens with a curator of a nationally recognized herbarium in North America as identified in the Index Herbariorum when the permit, contract, or other authorizing instrument allows bioprospecting.
