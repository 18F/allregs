CFR /  Title 39  /  Part 3060  /  Sec. 3060.14 Competitive products enterprise statement of allocated

The Postal Service will report the assets and liabilities of the theoretical competitive products enterprise as computed under Sec. Sec. 3060.12 and 3060.13 in the format as prescribed under Sec. 3060.30 for each fiscal year starting with FY 2010.
