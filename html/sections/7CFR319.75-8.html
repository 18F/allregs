CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 319  /  Sec. 319.75-8 Ports of entry.

Any regulated article shall be imported only at a port of entry identified in Sec. 319.37-14 of this part and found by the Administrator and specified on the permit issued pursuant to Sec. 319.75-3 to have a nongovernmental fumigator available at the port to treat such regulated article pursuant to Sec. 319.75-4. It is the responsibility of the importer to arrange with the nongovernmental fumigator for treatment of the article. [46 FR 38334, July 27, 1981, as amended at 72 FR 43523, Aug. 6, 2007; 78 FR 25572, May 2, 2013;79 FR 19811, Apr. 10, 2014]
