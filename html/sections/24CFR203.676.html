CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 203  /  Sec. 203.676 Request for continued occupancy.

An occupant may request permission to continue to occupy the property following conveyance to the Secretary by notifying the HUD Field Office in writing, within 20 days after the date of the mortgagee's notice of pending acquisition. Verification of illness or injury as described in Sec. 203.675(b)(4) shall be submitted within this time period if an occupant seeks to qualify for continued occupancy under the provisions of Sec. 203.674(a). The HUD Field Office will notify the mortgagee in writing that an occupied conveyance has been requested. (Approved by the Office of Management and Budget under control number 2502-0268) [53 FR 875, Jan. 14, 1988, and 53 FR 8626, Mar. 16, 1988, as amended at 58 FR 54246, Oct. 20, 1993]
