CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 76  /  Sec. 76.25-10 Size and arrangement of sprinkler heads and

(a) General.

(1) The system shall be so designed and arranged that the overhead is effectively sprayed and that all portions of the deck are covered.

(2) One-half inch sprinkler heads shall be used. Sprinkler heads shall be so arranged that no portion of the overhead or vertical projection of the deck is more than 7 feet from a sprinkler head.
