CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 11  /  11.604 Solicitation provisions and contract clauses.

(a) Contracting officers shall insert the provision at 52.211-14, Notice of Priority Rating for National Defense, Emergency Preparedness, and Energy Program Use, in solicitations when the contract to be awarded will be a rated order.

(b) Contracting officers shall insert the clause at 52.211-15, Defense Priority and Allocation Requirements, in contracts that are rated orders. [51 FR 19714, May 30, 1986. Redesignated and amended at 60 FR 48241, Sept. 18, 1995; 73 FR 21785, Apr. 22, 2008]
