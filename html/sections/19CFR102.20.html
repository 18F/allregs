(f) Section VI: Chapters 28 through 38------------------------------------------------------------------------

Notes: 1. Chemical reaction origin rule--

Any good of Chapters 28, 29, 31, 32 or 38, except a good of heading3823, that is the product of a chemical reaction shall be considered tobe a good of the country in which the reaction occurred.

A chemical reaction is defined as a process in which chemical bonds inmolecules are broken and new chemical bonds are formed between thefragmented molecules and/or added elements so that one or more of theoriginal bonds no longer link the same chemical element/s or functionalgroup/s.

Notwithstanding any of the line-by-line rules, the ``chemicalreaction'' rule may be applied to any good classified in the abovechapters.
