CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 877  /  Sec. 877.1 Scope.

This part establishes procedures for entry upon lands or property by OSMRE, States, and Indian tribes for reclamation purposes. For certified States or Indian tribes conducting noncoal reclamation projects under the provisions of part 875, the term ``noncoal'' replaces all references to ``coal'' in this part. [80 FR 6446, Feb. 5, 2015]
