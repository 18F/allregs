CFR /  Title 44  /  Part 5  /  Sec. 5.24 Availability of opinions, orders, policies, interpretations,

FEMA will make available for public inspection and copying the material described in 5 U.S.C. 552(a)(2) as enumerated in Sec. 5.25 and an index of those materials as described in Sec. 5.28, at convenient places and times.
