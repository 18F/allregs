CFR /  Title 17  /  Part 249b  /  Sec. 249b.300 FORM NRSRO, application for registration as a section 15E of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 and Sec. 240.17g-1 of this chapter.

This Form shall be used for an initial application for and an application to add a class of credit ratings to, a supplement to an initial application for and an application to add a class of credit ratings to, an update and amendment to an application for, and a withdrawal from a registration as a nationally recognized statistical rating organization pursuant to section 15E of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 (15 U.S.C. 78o-7) and Sec. 240.17g-1 of this chapter. [72 FR 33624, June 18, 2007]

Editorial Note: For Federal Register citations affecting Form NRSRO, see the List of CFR Sections Affected, which appears in the Finding Aids section of the printed volume and at www.fdsys.gov.
