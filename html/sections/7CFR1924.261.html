CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 1924  /  Sec. 1924.261 Handling complaints involving dwellings covered by an

Borrowers with complaints about dwellings covered by an independent or insured home warranty plan will be instructed to first contact the warranty company and follow the complaint resolution process for that company, with the assistance of FmHA or its successor agency under Public Law 103-354, if needed. If the complaint is not resolved in this manner, it will be handled under Sec. 1924.259 of this subpart.
