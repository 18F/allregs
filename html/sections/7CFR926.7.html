CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 926  /  Sec. 926.7 Producer.

Producer is synonymous with grower and means any person who produces cranberries for market and has a proprietary interest therein.

Effective Date Note: At 71 FR 78046, Dec. 28, 2006, Sec. 926.7 was suspended indefinitely, effective Dec. 29, 2006.
