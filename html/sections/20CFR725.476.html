CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 725  /  Sec. 725.476 Issuance of decision and order.

Within 20 days after the official termination of the hearing (see Sec. 725.475), the administrative law judge shall issue a decision and order with respect to the claim making an award to the claimant, rejecting the claim, or taking such other action as is appropriate.
