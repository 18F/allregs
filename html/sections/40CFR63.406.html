CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 63  /  Sec. 63.406 Recordkeeping and reporting requirements.

To demonstrate continuing compliance with Sec. 63.402 of this subpart, the owner or operator of each affected IPCT shall maintain copies of the initial notification and the notification of compliance status as required by Sec. 63.405 of this subpart for a period of at least 5 years onsite.
