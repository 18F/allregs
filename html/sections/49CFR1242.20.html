CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 1242  /  Sec. 1242.20 Highway grade crossings (accounts XX-17-22 and XX-18-22).

Separate running and switching common expenses according to distribution of the running and switching portions only of common expense accounts listed in Sec. 1242.10, Administration--Track (account XX-19-02).
