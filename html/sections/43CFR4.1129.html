CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 4  /  Sec. 4.1129 Certification of record.

Except in expedited review proceedings under Sec. 4.1180, within 5 days after an initial decision has been rendered, the administrative law judge shall certify the official record of the proceedings, including all exhibits, and transmit the official record for filing in the Hearings Division, Office of Hearings and Appeals, Arlington, Va.
