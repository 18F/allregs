CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 63  /  Sec. 63.9505 What are my general requirements for complying with this

(a) You must be in compliance with the emission limitation in this subpart at all times, except during periods of startup, shutdown, or malfunction.

(b) You must always operate and maintain your affected source, including air pollution control and monitoring equipment, according to the provisions in Sec. 63.6(e)(1)(i).

(c) You must develop a written startup, shutdown, and malfunction plan according to the provisions in Sec. 63.6(e)(3). [67 FR 64506, Oct. 18, 2002, as amended at 71 FR 20470, Apr. 20, 2006]
