CFR /  Title 14  /  Part 1206  /  Sec. 1206.900 FOIA offices and electronic libraries.

(a) NASA Headquarters and each NASA Center have a FOIA Electronic Library on the Internet. The Electronic library addresses are located on the NASA FOIA homepage http://www.hq.nasa.gov/office/pao/FOIA/agency/

(b) In addition, a requester may submit a FOIA request electronically. The addresses are located on the NASA FOIA homepage under each Center link.
