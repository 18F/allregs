CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 82  /  Sec. 82.1261 D&C; Orange No. 11.

The color additive D&C; Orange No. 11 shall conform in identity and specifications to the requirements of Sec. 74.1261(a)(1) and (b) of this chapter. D&C; Orange No. 11 is restricted to use in externally applied drugs and cosmetics. [46 FR 18954, Mar. 27, 1981]
