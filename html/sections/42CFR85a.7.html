CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 85a  /  Sec. 85a.7 Imminent dangers.

Whenever, during the course of, or as a result of, an investigation under this part, the NIOSH authorized representatives believe there is a reasonable basis for an allegation of an imminent danger, NIOSH will immediately advise the employer, owner, operator or agent in charge at the place of employment and those employees who appear to be in immediate danger of such allegation and will inform the agencies identified in Sec. 85a.4(a) through (4). [79 FR 2793, Jan. 16, 2014]
