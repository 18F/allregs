CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 52  /  Sec. 52.2824 Review of new sources and modifications.

(a) The requirements of subpart I of this chapter are not met since the Territory of American Samoa failed to submit a plan for review of new or modified indirect sources.

(b) Regulation for review of new or modified indirect sources: The provisions of Sec. 52.22(b) are hereby incorporated by reference and made a part of the applicable implementation plan for the Territory of American Samoa.
