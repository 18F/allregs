CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 21  /  Sec. 21.9510 Claims, VA's duty to assist, and time limits.

The provisions of subpart B of this part apply to claims filed for educational assistance under 38 U.S.C. chapter 33 with respect to VA's responsibilities upon receipt of claim, VA's duty to assist claimants in obtaining evidence, and time limits. (Authority: 38 U.S.C. 3323(c), 5101, 5102, 5103, 5103A)
