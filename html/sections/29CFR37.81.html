CFR /  Title 29  /  Part 37  /  Sec. 37.81 Are there any circumstances under which the Director may

Yes. The Director may extend the 30-day time limit:

(a) If the recipient does not include in its Notice of Final Action the required notice about the complainant's right to file with the Director, as described in Sec. 37.76(b)(5)(ii); or

(b) For other good cause shown.

The complainant has the burden of proving to the Director that the time limit should be extended.
