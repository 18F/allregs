CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 3821  /  Sec. 3821.0-3 Authority.

The authorities for the regulations in this subpart are the Act of April 8, 1948 (62 Stat. 162); Section 314 of the Federal Land Policy and Management Act of 1976 (43 U.S.C. 1744); and 30 U.S.C. 28f-28k, as amended by the Act of November 5, 2001 (115 Stat. 414). [67 FR 38206, June 3, 2002]
