CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 408  /  Sec. 408.22 Increased premiums for late enrollment and for reenrollment.

For an individual who enrolls after expiration of his or her initial enrollment period or reenrolls after termination of a coverage period, the standard monthly premium determined under Sec. 408.20 is increased by ten percent for each full twelve months in the periods specified in Sec. Sec. 408.24 and 408.25.
