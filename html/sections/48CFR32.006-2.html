CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 32  /  32.006-2 Definition.

Remedy coordination official, as used in this section, means the person or entity in the agency who coordinates within that agency the administration of criminal, civil, administrative, and contractual remedies resulting from investigations of fraud or corruption related to procurement activities. (See 10 U.S.C. 2307(i)(10) and 41 U.S.C. 4506(a).) [60 FR 49729, Sept. 26, 1995, as amended at 66 FR 2132, Jan. 10, 2001; 72 FR 46363, Aug. 17, 2007; 79 FR 24211, Apr. 29, 2014]
