CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 708  /  Sec. 708.24 Will there always be a hearing after a report of

(a) No. An employee may withdraw a hearing request after the report of investigation is issued. However, the hearing may be canceled only if all parties agree that they do not want a hearing.

(b) If the hearing is canceled, the Administrative Judge will issue an initial agency decision pursuant to Sec. 708.31 of this subpart.
