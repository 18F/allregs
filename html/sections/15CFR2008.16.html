CFR /  Title 15  /  Part 2008  /  Sec. 2008.16 Security education program.

(a) The Office of the Special Representative for Trade Negotiations will inform agency personnel having access to classified information of all requirements of Executive Order 12065 and ISOO Directive I.

(b) The Director, Office of Management, shall be charged with the implementation of this security education program and shall issue detailed procedures for the use of the agency personnel in fulfilling their day-to-day security responsibilities.
