CFR /  Title 23  /  Part 636  /  Sec. 636.105 Is the FHWA requiring the use of design-build?

No, the FHWA is neither requiring nor promoting the use of the design-build contracting method. The design-build contracting technique is optional. Sec. 636.106 [Reserved]
