CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 441  /  Sec. 441.460 Participant living arrangements.

(a) Self-directed PAS are not available to an individual who resides in a home or property that is owned, operated, or controlled by a PAS provider who is not related to the individual by blood or marriage.

(b) States may specify additional restrictions on a participant's living arrangements if they have been approved by CMS.
