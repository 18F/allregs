CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 207  /  Sec. 207.263 Responsibility for servicing.

After January 10, 1994, servicing of insured mortgages must be performed by a mortgagee which is approved by HUD to service insured mortgages. [57 FR 58350, Dec. 9, 1992]
