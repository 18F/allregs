CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 585  /  Sec. 585.642 How do I submit my GAP?

(a) You must submit one paper copy and one electronic version of your GAP to BOEM at the address listed in Sec. 585.110(a).

(b) If you have a limited lease, you may submit information on any project easement as part of your original GAP submission or as a revision to your GAP. Sec. Sec. 585.643-585.644 [Reserved]
