CFR /  Title 19  /  Part 142  /  Sec. 142.1 Definitions.

For definitions of ``entry'', ``entry summary'', ``submission'', ``filing'', ``presentation'', ``entered for consumption'', ``entered for warehouse'', and ``entered temporarily under bond'', as these terms relate to the entry of merchandise, see Sec. 141.0a of this chapter. (R.S. 251, as amended, secs. 623, as amended, 624, 46 Stat. 759, as amended (19 U.S.C. 66, 1623, 1624)) [T.D. 79-221, 44 FR 46821, Aug. 9, 1979, as amended by T.D. 84-213, 49 FR 41184, Oct. 19, 1984]
