CFR /  Title 32  /  Part 757  /  Sec. 757.20 Receipt and release.

The JAG designee will execute and deliver appropriate releases to third parties who have made full or agreed upon compromised payments. A copy of the release will be kept in the claims file. [72 FR 53430, Sept. 19, 2007]
