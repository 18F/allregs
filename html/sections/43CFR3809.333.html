CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 3809  /  Sec. 3809.333 May I extend my notice, and, if so, how?

Yes, if you wish to conduct operations for 2 additional years after the expiration date of your notice, you must notify BLM in writing on or before the expiration date and meet the financial guarantee requirements of Sec. 3809.503. You may extend your notice more than once.
