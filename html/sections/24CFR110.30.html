CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 110  /  Sec. 110.30 Effect of failure to display poster.

Any person who claims to have been injured by a discriminatory housing practice may file a complaint with the Secretary pursuant to part 105 of this chapter. A failure to display the fair housing poster as required by this part shall be deemed prima facie evidence of a discriminatory housing practice. [37 FR 3429, Feb. 16, 1972]
