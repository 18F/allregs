CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 490  /  Sec. 490.4 General information inquiries.

DOE responses to inquiries with regard to the provisions of this part that are not filed in compliance with Sec. Sec. 490.5 or 490.6 of this part constitute general information and the responses provided shall not be binding on DOE.
