CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 887  /  Sec. 887.13 Grant period.

The grant funding period must not exceed 8 years from the time we approve the grant. You must return any unexpended funds remaining at the end of any grant period to us according to 43 CFR part 12. [73 FR 67647, Nov. 14, 2008]
