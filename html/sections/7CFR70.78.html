CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 70  /  Sec. 70.78 Fees or charges for grading service performed under

Fees or charges to be made to an applicant for any grading service which differ from those listed in Secs. 70.70 through 70.77, shall be provided for by a cooperative agreement.
