CFR /  Title 28  /  Part 31  /  Sec. 31.1 General.

This subpart defines eligibility and sets forth requirements for application for and administration of formula grants to State governments authorized by part B, subpart I, of the Juvenile Justice and Delinquency Prevention Act. [60 FR 28440, May 31, 1995, as amended at 64 FR 19676, Apr. 21, 1999]
