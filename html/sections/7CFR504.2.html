CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 504  /  Sec. 504.2 Fees for deposit and requisition of microbial cultures.

(a) Depositors of microbial cultures must pay a one-time $670 user fee for each culture deposited on or after December 1, 2015.

(b) For cultures deposited on or after December 1, 2015, requestors must pay a $40 user fee for each culture distributed. [80 FR 74966, Dec. 1, 2015]
