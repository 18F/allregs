CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 76  /  Sec. 76.1601 Deletion or repositioning of broadcast signals.

Effective April 2, 1993, a cable operator shall provide written notice to any broadcast television station at least 30 days prior to either deleting from carriage or repositioning that station. Such notification shall also be provided to subscribers of the cable system. [65 FR 53617, Sept. 5, 2000, as amended at 80 FR 11330, Mar. 3, 2015]
