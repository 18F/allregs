CFR /  Title 25  /  Part 36  /  Sec. 36.2 Applicability.

The national criteria for dormitory situations established under subpart G serve as a minimum requirement and are mandatory for all Bureau-operated and Indian-controlled contract schools. [77 FR 30891, May 24, 2012]
