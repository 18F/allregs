CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 153  /  Sec. 153.480 Stripping quantity for Category B and C NLS tanks on

Unless waived under Sec. 153.491, Category B and C NLS cargo tanks on each ship built after June 30, 1986 must have stripping quantities determined under Sec. 153.1604 that are less than--

(a) 0.15 m\3\ if Category B; and

(b) 0.35 m\3\ if Category C.
