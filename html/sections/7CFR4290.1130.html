CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 4290  /  Sec. 4290.1130 Leverage fees payable by RBIC.

(a) Leverage fee. You must pay the Secretary a non-refundable leverage fee for each issuance of a Debenture. The fee is 3 percent of the face amount of the Debenture issued, and will be deducted from the proceeds remitted to you.

(b) Additional charge. You must pay the Secretary an additional annual charge of 1 percent of the outstanding amount of your Debenture.

(c) Other Leverage fees. The Secretary may establish a fee structure for services performed by the Central Registration Agent (CRA). The Secretary will not collect any fee for its guarantee of TCs.
