CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 219  /  219.502-1 Requirements for setting aside acquisitions.

Do not set aside acquisitions for--

(1) Supplies which were developed and financed, in whole or in part, by Canadian sources under the U.S.-Canadian Defense Development Sharing Program; or

(2) Architect-engineer services for military construction or family housing projects of $400,000 or more (10 U.S.C. 2855), including indefinite delivery and indefinite quantity contracts if the value of all anticipated orders is expected to total $350,000 or more. [58 FR 28465, May 13, 1993, as amended at 69 FR 31909, June 8, 2004; 75 FR 45074, Aug. 2, 2010; 80 FR 36904, June 26, 2015]
