CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 213  /  Sec. 213.21 Who presides.

(a) The presiding officer at a hearing shall be the Administrator or his designee.

(b) The designation of the presiding officer shall be in writing. A copy of the designation shall be served on all parties. [39 FR 40850, Nov. 21, 1974]
