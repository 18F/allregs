CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 25  /  Sec. 25.286 Antenna painting and lighting.

The owner of an earth station antenna structure must comply with all applicable painting, marking, and/or lighting requirements in part 17 of this chapter. In the event of default by the owner, the station licensee will be responsible for ensuring that such requirements are met. [79 FR 8326, Feb. 12, 2014]
