CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 350  /  Sec. 350.35 What are the requirements for the composition of an

The majority of a Rehabilitation Engineering Research Center advisory committee's members must be comprised of individuals with disabilities who are users of rehabilitation technology, or their parents, family members, guardians, advocates, or authorized representatives. (Authority: Sec. 204(b)(3)(D); 29 U.S.C. 762(b)(3)(D))
