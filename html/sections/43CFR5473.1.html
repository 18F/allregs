CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 5473  /  Sec. 5473.1 Application.

In order to be considered, written requests for extension shall be delivered to the appropriate BLM office prior to the expiration of the time for cutting and removal. [57 FR 37477, Aug. 19, 1992]
