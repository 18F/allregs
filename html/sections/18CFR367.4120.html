CFR /  Title 18  /  Part 367  /  Sec. 367.4120 Account 412, Cost and expenses of construction or

This account must include expenditures related to the performance of construction or service contracts, under which the service company undertakes projects to construct physical property for associate or non-associate companies (see General Instructions Sec. 367.24, Construction and service contracts for other companies) and the cost of services performed for others not provided for elsewhere.
