CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 0  /  Sec. 0.285 Record of actions taken.

The history card, the station file, and other appropriate files are designated to be the official records of action taken by the Chief of the Media Bureau. The official records of action are maintained in the Reference Information Center in the Consumer and Governmental Affairs Bureau. [67 FR 13220, Mar. 21, 2002]
