CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 117  /  Sec. 117.469 Liberty Bayou.

The draw of the S433 Bridge, mile 2.0, at Slidell, shall open on signal, except that between 7 p.m. to 7 a.m., the draw shall open on signal if at least two hours notice is given. [USCG-2007-0078, 73 FR 12886, Mar. 11, 2008]
