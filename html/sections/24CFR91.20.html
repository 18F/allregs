CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 91  /  Sec. 91.20 Exceptions.

The HUD Field Office may grant a jurisdiction an exception from the submission deadline for plans and reports and from a requirement in the implementation guidelines for good cause, as determined by the field office and reported in writing to HUD Headquarters, unless the requirement is required by statute or regulation. [71 FR 6962, Feb. 9, 2006]
