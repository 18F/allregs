CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 232  /  Sec. 232.7 Bathroom.

Not less than one full bathroom must be provided for every four residents of a board and care home or assisted living facility, and bathroom access from any bedroom or sleeping area must not pass through a public corridor or area. [61 FR 14406, Apr. 1, 1996. Redesignated at 77 FR 55136, Sept. 7, 2012]
