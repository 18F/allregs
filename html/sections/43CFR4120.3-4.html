CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 4120  /  Sec. 4120.3-4 Standards, design and stipulations.

Range improvement permits and cooperative range improvement agreements shall specify the standards, design, construction and maintenance criteria for the range improvements and other additional conditions and stipulations or modifications deemed necessary by the authorized officer. [49 FR 6452, Feb. 21, 1984, as amended at 61 FR 4227, Feb. 5, 1996]
