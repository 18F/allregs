CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 1114  /  Sec. 1114.4 Documents in Board's files.

If a party offers in evidence any matter contained in a report or other document open to public inspection in the files of the Board, such report or other document need not be made available at the hearing. [47 FR 49562, Nov. 1, 1982, as amended at 61 FR 52713, Oct. 8, 1996]
