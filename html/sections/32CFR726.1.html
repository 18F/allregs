CFR /  Title 32  /  Part 726  /  Sec. 726.1 Purpose.

This part explains the procedures for convening competency boards and how to appoint trustees for members of the Naval service who have been determined to be mentally incompetent in accordance with Chapter 11 of Title 37, United States Code. [56 FR 55088, Oct. 24, 1991, as amended at 73 FR 64206, Oct. 29, 2008]
