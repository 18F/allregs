CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 58  /  Sec. 58.234 Buttermilk.

Buttermilk for drying as dry buttermilk or dry buttermilk product shall be fresh and derived from the churning of butter, with or without the addition of harmless lactic culture. No preservative, neutralizing agent or other chemical may be added. Fluid buttermilk, unless cultured, shall be held at 45 F or lower unless processed within 2 hours. [56 FR 33855, July 24, 1991]
