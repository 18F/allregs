CFR /  Title 32  /  Part 37  /  Sec. 37.855 How should I handle protected data?

Prior to releasing or disclosing data marked with a restrictive legend (as described in Sec. 37.850) to third parties, you should require those parties to agree in writing that they will:

(a) Use the data only for governmental purposes; and

(b) Not release or disclose the data without the permission of the licensor (i.e., the recipient).
