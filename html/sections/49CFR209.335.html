CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 209  /  Sec. 209.335 Penalties.

(a) may also be assessed a civil penalty of at least $1,000 and not more than $5,000 per violation.

(b) may be assessed a civil penalty of at least $5,000 and not more than $11,000 per violation.

(c) Each day a violation continues shall constitute a separate offense. [54 FR 42907, Oct. 18, 1989, as amended at 63 FR 11619, Mar. 10, 1998]
