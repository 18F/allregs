CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 872  /  Sec. 872.23 Are there any restrictions on how you may use historic

Yes. You may only use historic coal funds for:

(a) Coal reclamation under Sec. 874.12 of this chapter;

(b) Water supply restoration under Sec. 874.14 of this chapter;

(c) Noncoal reclamation under Sec. 875.12 of this chapter that is requested under section 409(c) of SMCRA;

(d) Deposit into an acid mine drainage abatement and treatment fund under part 876 of this chapter;

(e) Land acquisition under Sec. 879.11 of this chapter; and

(f) Maintenance of the AML inventory under section 403(c) of SMCRA.
