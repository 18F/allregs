CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 180  /  Sec. 180.1259 Reynoutria sachalinensis extract; exemption from the

Residues of the biochemical pesticide Reynoutria sachalinensis extract, when derived from the whole plant extract, are exempt from the requirement of a tolerance in or on all food commodities. [70 FR 55277, Sept. 21, 2005]
