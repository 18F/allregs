CFR /  Title 13  /  Part 127  /  Sec. 127.505 May a non-manufacturer submit an offer on an EDWOSB or

An EDWOSB or WOSB that is a non-manufacturer, as defined in Sec. 121.406(b) of this chapter, may submit an offer on an EDWOSB or WOSB contract for supplies, if it meets the requirements under the non-manufacturer rule set forth in Sec. 121.406(b) of this chapter.
