CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 3835  /  Sec. 3835.15 If I qualify as a small miner, how do I apply for a

You must submit a waiver request complying with Sec. 3835.10 before the assessment year begins for which you wish to obtain a waiver. In addition, you must--

(a) Make a FLPMA filing, in the form of a notice of intent to hold under Sec. Sec. 3835.31 and 3835.33 of this part on or before December 30th immediately following the submission of a waiver request;

(b) Perform your assessment work in the assessment year for which BLM waived the maintenance fee; and

(c) Make a FLPMA filing in the form of an affidavit of assessment work under Sec. Sec. 3835.31 and 3835.33 of this part on or before the December 30th immediately following the close of the assessment year in which you performed assessment work. [68 FR 61064, Oct. 24, 2003; 68 FR 74197, Dec. 23, 2003]
