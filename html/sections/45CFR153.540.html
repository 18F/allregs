CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 153  /  Sec. 153.540 Compliance with risk corridors standards.

HHS or its designee may audit a QHP issuer to assess its compliance with the requirements of this subpart. HHS will conduct an audit in accordance with the procedures set forth in Sec. 158.402(a) through (e) of this subchapter. [79 FR 13836, Mar. 11, 2014]
