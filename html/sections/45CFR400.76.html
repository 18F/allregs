CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 400  /  Sec. 400.76 Criteria for exemption from registration for employmentacceptance of appropriate offers of employment.

States and local resettlement agencies operating a public/private RCA program, as well as States operating a publicly-administered RCA program, may determine what specific exemptions, if any, are appropriate for recipients of a time-limited RCA program in their State. [65 FR 15448, Mar. 22, 2000]
