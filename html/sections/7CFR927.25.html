CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 927  /  Sec. 927.25 Failure to nominate.

In the event nominations are not made pursuant to Sec. Sec. 927.21 and 927.22 on or before June 1 of any year, the Secretary may select members and alternates for members without regard to nominations.
