CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 1000  /  Sec. 1000.152 How is the recipient to use criminal conviction

(a) With regard to adult tenants and applicants for housing assistance, the recipient shall use the criminal conviction information described in Sec. 1000.150 only for applicant screening, lease enforcement, and eviction actions.

(b) With regard to applicants for employment, the recipient shall use the criminal conviction information described in Sec. 1000.150 for the purposes set out in section 208 of NAHASDA.

(c) The criminal conviction information described in Sec. 1000.150 may be disclosed only to any person who has a job-related need for the information and who is an authorized officer, employee, or representative of the recipient or the owner of housing assisted under NAHASDA. [77 FR 71526, Dec. 3, 2012]
