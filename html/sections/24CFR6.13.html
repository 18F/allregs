CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 6  /  Sec. 6.13 Hearings and appeals.

(a) When a Recipient requests an opportunity for a hearing, in accordance with Sec. 6.12(b)(3), the General Counsel will follow the notification procedures set forth in 24 CFR 180.415. The hearing, and any petition for review, will be conducted in accordance with the procedures set forth in 24 CFR part 180.

(b) After a hearing is held and a final agency decision is rendered under 24 CFR part 180, the Recipient may seek judicial review in accordance with section 111(c) of the Act.
