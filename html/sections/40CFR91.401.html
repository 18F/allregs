CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 91  /  Sec. 91.401 Scope; applicability.

(a) This subpart describes the procedures to follow in order to perform exhaust emission tests on new marine gasoline-fueled spark-ignition propulsion engines subject to the provisions of subpart A of this part 91. Provisions specific to raw gas sampling are in Secs. 91.414 through 91.419, provisions specific to constant volume sampling are in Secs. 91.420 through 91.426. All other sections in this subpart apply to both raw gas sampling and constant volume sampling unless indicated otherwise.

(b) Requirements for emission test equipment and calibrating this equipment are found in subpart D of this part.
