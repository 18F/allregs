CFR /  Title 18  /  Part 4  /  Sec. 4.108 Contents of application for exemption from provisions

An application for exemption of a small hydroelectric power project from provisions of Part I of the Act other than the licensing requirement need not be prepared according to any specific format, but must be included as an identified appendix to the related application for license or amendment of license. The application for exemption must list all sections or subsections of Part I of the Act for which exemption is requested. [Order 106, 45 FR 76123, Nov. 18, 1980]
