CFR /  Title 25  /  Part 171  /  Sec. 171.220 What must I do to my farm unit to receive irrigation

You must meet the following requirements for us to provide service:

(a) Put water we deliver to authorized uses;

(b) Make sure your farm ditch has sufficient capacity to carry the water we deliver; and

(c) Properly operate, maintain, and rehabilitate your farm ditch.
