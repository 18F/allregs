CFR /  Title 17  /  Part 201  /  Sec. 201.701 Issuance of order.

At any time following conclusion of the rebuttal period specified in 17 CFR 201.700(b)(4), the Commission may issue an order approving or disapproving the self-regulatory organization's proposed rule change together with a written statement of the reasons therefor. [76 FR 4070, Jan. 24, 2011]
