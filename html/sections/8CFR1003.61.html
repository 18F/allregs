CFR /  Title 8  /  Part 1003  /  Sec. 1003.61 List.

(a) The Chief Immigration Judge shall maintain a current list of organizations and attorneys qualified under this subpart which provide free legal services. This list, which shall be updated not less than quarterly, shall be provided to aliens in immigration proceedings. The Chief Immigration Judge may designate an employee or employees to carry out his or her responsibilities under this subpart. Organizations and attorneys may be included on the list of free legal services providers if they qualify under one of the following categories:

(1) Organizations recognized under Sec. 1292.2 of this chapter that meet the qualifications set forth in Sec. 1003.62(a) and whose representatives, if any, are authorized to practice before the Board and Immigration Courts;

(2) Organizations not recognized under Sec. 1292.2 of this chapter that meet the qualifications set forth in Sec. 1003.62(b);

(3) Bar associations that meet the qualifications set forth in Sec. 1003.62(c); and

(4) Attorneys, as defined in Sec. 1001.1(f) of this chapter, who meet the qualifications set forth in Sec. 1003.62(d).

(b) The listing of an organization qualified under this subpart is not equivalent to recognition under Sec. 292.2 of this chapter.
