CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 222  /  Sec. 222.52 What requirements must a local educational agency meet

To receive a payment under section 8003(d), an eligible LEA shall--

(a) State in its application the number of federally connected children with disabilities it claims for a payment under section 8003(d);

(b) Have in effect written IEPs or IFSPs for all federally connected children with disabilities it claims under section 8003(d); and

(c) Meet the requirements of subparts A and C of the regulations in this part. (Approved by the Office of Management and Budget under control number 1810-0036) (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1400 et seq. and 7703) [60 FR 50778, Sept. 29, 1995, as amended at 80 FR 33166, June 11, 2015]
