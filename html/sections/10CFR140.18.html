CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 140  /  Sec. 140.18 Special provisions applicable to licensees furnishingresources.

In any case where a licensee undertakes to maintain financial protection in the form specified in Sec. 140.14(a)(2) for all or part of the financial protection required by this part, the Commission may require such licensee to file with the Commission such financial information as the Commission determines to be appropriate for the purpose of determining whether the licensee is maintaining financial protection as required by this part. [42 FR 43385, Aug. 29, 1977]
