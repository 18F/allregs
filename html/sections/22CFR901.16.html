CFR /  Title 22  /  Part 901  /  Sec. 901.16 Grievant.

Grievant means anyone who has filed a grievance and who is a member of the Service and is a citizen of the United States, or for the purposes of Sec. 901.18(a)(7) a former member of the Service, or in the case of death of the member, the surviving spouse or, if none, another member of the family.
