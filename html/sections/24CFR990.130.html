CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 990  /  Sec. 990.130 Ineligible units.

(a) Vacant units that do not fall within the definition of Sec. 990.145 or Sec. 990.150 are not eligible for operating subsidy under this part.

(b) Units that are eligible to receive an asset-repositioning fee, as described in Sec. 990.190(h), are not eligible to receive operating subsidy under this subpart.
