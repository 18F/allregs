CFR /  Title 44  /  Part 204  /  Sec. 204.64 Reporting and audit requirements

(a) Reporting. Within 90-days of the Performance Period expiration date, the State will submit a final Financial Status Report, which reports all costs incurred within the incident period and all administrative costs incurred within the performance period; and

(b) Audit.

(1) Audits will be performed, for both the Grantee and the subgrantees, under 2 CFR 200.500-200.520.

(2) FEMA may elect to conduct a program-specific Federal audit on the Fire Management Assistance Grant or a subgrant. [66 FR 57347, Nov. 14, 2001, as amended at 79 FR 63546, Oct. 24, 2014; 79 FR 76085, Dec. 19, 2014]
