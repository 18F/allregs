CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 82  /  Sec. 82.1336 D&C; Red No. 36.

(a) The color additive D&C; Red No. 36 shall conform in identity and specifications to the requirements of Sec. 74.1336 (a)(1) and (b) of this chapter.

(b) All lakes of D&C; Red No. 36 shall be manufactured from previously certified batches of the straight color additive. [53 FR 29031, Aug. 2, 1988]
