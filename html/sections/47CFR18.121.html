CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 18  /  Sec. 18.121 Exemptions.

Non-consumer ultrasonic equipment, and non-consumer magnetic resonance equipment, that is used for medical diagnostic and monitoring applications is subject only to the provisions of Secs. 18.105, 18.109 through 18.119, 18.301 and 18.303 of this part. [59 FR 39472, Aug. 3, 1994; 60 FR 47302, Sept. 12, 1995]
