CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 117  /  Sec. 117.714 Corson Inlet.

The draw of the Corson Inlet Bridge, mile 0.9, at Strathmere, shall open on signal; except that from October 1 through May 15 from 10 p.m. to 6 a.m. and from 6 a.m. to 10 p.m. on December 25 the draw need open only if at least two hours notice is given. [USCG-2007-0026, 73 FR 5749, Jan. 31, 2008]
