CFR /  Title 31  /  Part 515  /  Sec. 515.549 Bank accounts and other property of non-Cuban decedents

Specific licenses may be issued authorizing the administration of the estates of non-Cuban decedents who died in Cuba on or after July 8, 1963, provided that any distribution to a blocked national of Cuba is made by deposit in a blocked account in a domestic bank in the name of the blocked national. [80 FR 2294, Jan. 16, 2015]
