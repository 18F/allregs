CFR /  Title 37  /  Part 1  /  Sec. 1.468 Delays in meeting time limits.

Delays in meeting time limits during international processing of international applications may only be excused as provided in PCT Rule 82. For delays in meeting time limits in a national application, see Sec. 1.137.
