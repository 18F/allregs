CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 5  /  Sec. 5.303 Frequencies.

Licensees may operate in any frequency band, except for the following:

(a) Frequency bands at or below 38.6 GHz that are designated as restricted in Sec. 15.205(a) of this chapter; and

(b) Frequency bands above 38.6 GHz that are listed in footnote US246 of the Table of Frequency Allocations in Sec. 2.106 of this chapter. [79 FR 48691, Aug. 18, 2014]
