CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 35  /  Sec. 35.9000 Applicability.

This subpart codifies policies and procedures for financial assistance awarded by the EPA to State, interstate, and regional water pollution control agencies and entities and other eligible agencies, institutions, organizations, and individuals for pollution abatement and control programs under the National Estuary Program (NEP). These provisions supplement the EPA general assistance regulations in 2 CFR parts 200 and 1500. [54 FR 40804, Oct. 3, 1989, as amended at 79 FR 76061, Dec. 19, 2014]
