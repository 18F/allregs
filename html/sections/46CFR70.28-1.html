CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 70  /  Sec. 70.28-1 Lifesaving appliances and arrangements.

All lifesaving appliances and arrangements on passenger vessels must be in accordance with subchapter W (Lifesaving Appliances and Arrangements) of this chapter. [CGD 84-069, 61 FR 25287, May 20, 1996]
