CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 63  /  Sec. 63.11905 When must I conduct subsequent performance testing and

Following the date of your initial demonstration of compliance in Sec. 63.11900, you must conduct subsequent performance testing and monitoring to demonstrate continuous compliance with your emission limits, operating limits, and work practice standards according to the procedures and frequency in Secs. 63.11910 through 63.11980. If you make a process change as specified in Sec. 63.11896, such that a different emission limit or operating parameter limit applies, you must conduct a performance test according to Sec. 63.11896.
