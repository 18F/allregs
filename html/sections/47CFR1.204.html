CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 1  /  Sec. 1.204 Pleadings; definition.

As used in this subpart, the term pleading means any written notice, motion, petition, request, opposition, reply, brief, proposed findings, exceptions, memorandum of law, or other paper filed with the Commission in a hearing proceeding. It does not include exhibits or documents offered in evidence. See Sec. 1.356. [29 FR 8219, June 30, 1964]
