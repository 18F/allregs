CFR /  Title 12  /  Part 347  /  Sec. 347.112 Restrictions applicable to foreign organizations that

(a) If a bank acquires or retains an equity interest in a foreign organization that acts as a futures commission merchant pursuant to Sec. 347.105(b)(16), the foreign organization may not be a member of an exchange or clearing association that requires members to guarantee or otherwise contract to cover losses suffered by other members unless the:

(1) Foreign organization's liability does not exceed two percent of the bank's Tier 1 capital, or

(2) Bank has obtained the prior approval of the FDIC under Sec. 347.120(d).
