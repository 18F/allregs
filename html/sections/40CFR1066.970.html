CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 1066  /  Sec. 1066.970 Refueling test for liquid fuels.

Except as described in Sec. 1066.975, test vehicles for refueling emissions as described in 40 CFR 86.150-98, 86.151-98, 86.152-98, and 86.154-98. Keep records as described in 40 CFR 86.155-98.
