CFR /  Title 36  /  Part 213  /  Sec. 213.4 Prior rules and regulations superseded.

Except as provided in Sec. 213.3, the rules and regulations heretofore issued for the land utilization projects are hereby superseded as to all such projects administered by the Forest Service, but not as to such project lands administered by other agencies. [27 FR 9217, Sept. 18, 1962]
