CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 581  /  Sec. 581.3 Application.

This standard applies to passenger motor vehicles other than multipurpose passenger vehicles and low-speed vehicles as defined in 49 CFR part 571.3(b). [63 FR 33217, June 17, 1998]
