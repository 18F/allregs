CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 13  /  Sec. 13.5 Licensed commercial radio operator required.

Rules that require FCC station licensees to have certain transmitter operation, maintenance, and repair duties performed by a commercial radio operator are contained in parts 80 and 87 of this chapter. [78 FR 23152, Apr. 18, 2013]
