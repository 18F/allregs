CFR /  Title 28  /  Part 83  /  Sec. 83.105 Does this part apply to me?

(a) Portions of this part apply to you if you are either--

(1) A recipient of an assistance award from the Department of Justice; or

(2) A(n) Department of Justice awarding official. (See definitions of award and recipient in Secs. 83.605 and 83.660, respectively.)

(b) The following table shows the subparts that apply to you: ------------------------------------------------------------------------

(1) A recipient who is not an individual.. A, B and E.(2) A recipient who is an individual...... A, C and E.(3) A(n) Department of Justice awarding A, D and E.
