CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 3560  /  Sec. 3560.579 Servicing off-farm labor housing.

The requirements established in subparts I and J of this part will apply to all off-farm labor housing. Servicing according to subparts I and J of this part shall apply throughout the term of the loan or grant, whichever is longer. Sec. Sec. 3560.580-3560.599 [Reserved]
