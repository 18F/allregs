CFR /  Title 19  /  Part 127  /  Sec. 127.4 General order period defined.

The general order period is that period of time during which general order merchandise, as defined in Sec. 127.1, is not subject to sale. The general order period expires 6 months from the date of importation. [T.D. 79-221, 44 FR 46814, Aug. 9, 1979, as amended by T.D. 98-74, 63 FR 51290, Sept. 25, 1998]
