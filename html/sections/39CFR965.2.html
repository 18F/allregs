CFR /  Title 39  /  Part 965  /  Sec. 965.2 Scope of rules.

The rules in this part shall be applicable to mail dispute cases forwarded to the Judicial Officer pursuant to Postal Operations Manual section 616.21. [76 FR 15219, Mar. 21, 2011]
