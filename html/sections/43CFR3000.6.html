CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 3000  /  Sec. 3000.6 Filing of documents.

All necessary documents shall be filed in the proper BLM office. A document shall be considered filed when it is received in the proper BLM office during regular business hours (see Sec. 1821.2 of this title).
