CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 228  /  Sec. 228.13 Computation of the tier I annuity component of a

The tier I annuity component of a parent's insurance annuity is dependent on whether one or two parents are entitled.

(a) One parent entitled. A parent's tier I annuity component is equal to 82\1/2\ percent of the PIA computed under Sec. 228.10 of this part.

(b) More than one parent entitled. A parent's tier I annuity component is equal to 75 percent of the PIA computed under Sec. 228.10 of this part.

(c) The amounts computed under paragraph (a) or (b) of this section may be adjusted for the family maximum. See Sec. 228.14 of this part.
