CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 366  /  Sec. 366.25 What additional factor does the Secretary use in making a

In selecting from among applicants for a grant under Sec. 366.24 for a new center, the Secretary considers comments regarding the application, if any, by the SILC in the State in which the applicant is located. (Approved by the Office of Management and Budget under control number 1820-0018) (Authority: 29 U.S.C. 796f-1(d)(1))
