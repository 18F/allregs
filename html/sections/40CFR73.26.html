CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 73  /  Sec. 73.26 Conservation and renewable energy reserve.

The Administrator will allocate 300,000 allowances to the Conservation and Renewable Energy Reserve subaccount of the Acid Rain Data System. Allowances from this Reserve will be allocated to units under subpart F of this part. Termination of this Reserve and reallocation of allowances will be made under Sec. 73.80(c). [53 FR 15714, Mar. 23, 1993]
