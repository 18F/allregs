CFR /  Title 9  /  Part 114  /  Sec. 114.15 Disposal of unsatisfactory products and byproducts.

All biological products found to be unsatisfactory for marketing, all biological products which have become worthless subsequent to the expiration date, all refuse, other materials deemed unsatisfactory for production purposes, all carcasses (part or whole) of production or test animals, and any undesirable byproducts of manufacture shall be disposed of as may be required by the Administrator. [41 FR 44687, Oct. 12, 1976, as amended at 56 FR 66784, Dec. 26, 1991]
