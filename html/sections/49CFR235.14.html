CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 235  /  Sec. 235.14 Notice.

The FRA will publish notice of the filing of an application or a request for reconsideration of an application in the Federal Register and a copy of such notice will be available at the U.S. Department of Transportation, Docket Operations (M-30), West Building Ground Floor, Room W12-140, 1200 New Jersey Avenue, SE., Washington, DC 20590, and on the Federal Docket Management System's Web site at http://www.regulations.gov. [64 FR 70195, Dec. 16, 1999, as amended at 74 FR 25174, May 27, 2009]
