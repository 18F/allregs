CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 405  /  Sec. 405.209 Payment for a Category B (Nonexperimental/investigational)

Payment under Medicare for a Category B (Nonexperimental/ investigational) device is based on, and may not exceed, the amount that would have been paid for a currently used device serving the same medical purpose that has been approved or cleared for marketing by the FDA. [78 FR 74809, Dec. 10, 2013]
