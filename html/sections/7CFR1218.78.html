CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 1218  /  Sec. 1218.78 OMB control numbers.

The control number assigned to the information collection requirements by the Office of Management and Budget pursuant to the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995, 44 U.S.C. Chapter 35, is OMB control number 0581-0093, except for the Council nominee background statement form which is assigned OMB control number 0505-001.
