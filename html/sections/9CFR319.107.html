CFR /  Title 9  /  Part 319  /  Sec. 319.107 Bacon.

The weight of cured pork bellies ready for slicing and labeling as ``Bacon'' shall not exceed the weight of the fresh uncured pork bellies. [49 FR 14880, Apr. 13, 1984]
