CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 1427  /  Sec. 1427.20 Handling payments and collections not exceeding $9.99.

Amounts of $9.99 or less will be paid to the producer only at their request. Deficiencies of $9.99 or less, including interest, may be disregarded unless CCC demands in writing that they be paid.
