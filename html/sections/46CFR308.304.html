CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 308  /  Sec. 308.304 Reporting casualties and filing claims.

All casualties occurring after insurance under a binder has attached shall be reported promptly to, and all claim documents filed with, ``Maritime Administration, Attention: Chief, Office of Marine Insurance.'' Sec. 308.305 [Reserved]
