CFR /  Title 17  /  Part 249  /  Sec. 249.480 Form CB, tender offer statement in connection with a

This form is used to report an issuer tender offer conducted in compliance with Sec. 240.13e-4(h)(8) of this chapter and a third-party tender offer conducted in compliance with Sec. 240.14d-1(c) of this chapter. This report also is used by a subject company pursuant to Sec. 240.14e-2(d) of this chapter. [64 FR 61406, Nov. 10, 1999]

Editorial Note: For Federal Register citations affecting Form CB, see the List of CFR Sections Affected, which appears in the Finding Aids section of the printed volume and at www.fdsys.gov.
