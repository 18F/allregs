CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 201  /  Sec. 201.3 Applicability of the regulations.

The regulations in this part may be amended by the Secretary at any time. Such amendment shall not adversely affect the insurance privileges of a lender on any loan that has been made or for which a loan application has been approved before the effective date of the amendment. [61 FR 19796, May 2, 1996]
