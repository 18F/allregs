CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 229  /  Sec. 229.4 Information collection.

(a) The information collection requirements in this part have been reviewed by the Office of Management and Budget pursuant to the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1980, Public Law 96-511, and have been assigned OMB control number 2130-0004.

(b) The information collection requirements are found in the following sections: Secs. 229.9, 229.17, 229.21, 229.23, 229.25, 229.27, 229.29, 229.31, 229.33, 229.55, 229.103, 229.105, 229.113, 229.121, 229.135, and appendix H to part 229. [50 FR 6953, Feb. 19, 1985, as amended at 58 FR 36613, July 8, 1993; 71 FR 63136, Oct. 27, 2006]
