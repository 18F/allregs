CFR /  Title 28  /  Part 4  /  Sec. 4.10 Waiver of oral hearing.

The Commission upon receipt of a statement from the Secretary that he does not object, and in the absence of any request for oral hearing from the others to whom notice has been sent pursuant to Sec. 4.7 may grant an application without receiving oral testimony with respect to it.
