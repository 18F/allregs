CFR /  Title 50  /  Part 35  /  Sec. 35.10 Controlled burning.

Controlled burning will be permitted on wilderness units when such burning will contribute to the maintenance of the wilderness resource and values in the unit; however, any fire in a wilderness area that poses a threat to resources or facilities outside the unit will be controlled and extinguished. [36 FR 25426, Dec. 31, 1971, as amended at 37 FR 12067, June 17, 1972]
