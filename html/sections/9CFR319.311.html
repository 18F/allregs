CFR /  Title 9  /  Part 319  /  Sec. 319.311 Chow mein vegetables with meat, and chop suey vegetables

``Chow Mein Vegetables with Meat'' and ``Chop Suey Vegetables with Meat'' shall contain not less than 12 percent meat computed on the weight of the uncooked fresh meat prior to its inclusion with the other ingredients. Mechanically Separated (Species) may be used in accordance with Sec. 319.6. [35 FR 15597, Oct. 3, 1970; 36 FR 11903, June 23, 1971, as amended at 43 FR 26425, June 20, 1978; 47 FR 28257, June 29, 1982]
