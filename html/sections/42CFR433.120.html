CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 433  /  Sec. 433.120 Procedures for reduction of FFP after reapproval review.

(a) If CMS determines after the reapproval review that the system no longer meets the conditions of reapproval in Sec. 433.119, CMS will reduce FFP for system operations for at least four quarters. However, no system will be subject to reduction of FFP for at least the first four quarters after the quarter in which the system is initially approved as eligible for 75 percent FFP.

(b) CMS will reduce FFP in expenditures for system operations from 75 percent to 50 percent. [50 FR 30848, July 30, 1985, as amended at 54 FR 41974, Oct. 13, 1989; 76 FR 21974, Apr. 19, 2011]
