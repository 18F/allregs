CFR /  Title 19  /  Part 177  /  Sec. 177.24 By whom request is filed.

A request may be filed by an individual or organization listed in Sec. 177.23 or by a duly authorized attorney or agent on behalf of the individual or organization. A request filed by a corporation shall be signed by a corporate officer, and a request filed by a partnership shall be signed by a partner.
