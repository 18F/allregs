CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 49  /  49.602-2 Inventory forms.

Standard Form (SF) 1428, Inventory Disposal Schedule, and SF 1429, Inventory Disposal Schedule--Continuation Sheet, shall be used to support settlement proposals submitted on the forms specified in 49.602-1(b) and (d). [69 FR 17748, Apr. 5, 2004]
