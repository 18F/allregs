CFR /  Title 25  /  Part 83  /  Sec. 83.41 What will the Assistant Secretary consider in his/her review?

(a) AS-IA will consider all the evidence in the administrative record, including any comments and responses on the proposed finding and any the hearing transcript and recommended decision.

(b) AS-IA will not consider comments submitted after the close of the comment period in Sec. 83.35, the response period in Sec. 83.36 or Sec. 83.37, or the hearing election period in Sec. 83.38.
