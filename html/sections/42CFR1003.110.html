CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 1003  /  Sec. 1003.110 Failure to request a hearing.

If the respondent does not request a hearing within the time prescribed by Sec. 1003.109(a), the Inspector General may impose the proposed penalty, assessment, and exclusion, or any less severe penalty, assessment, and suspension. The Inspector General shall notify the respondent by certified mail, return receipt requested, of any penalty, assessment, and exclusion that has been imposed and of the means by which the respondent may satisfy the judgment. The respondent has no right to appeal a penalty, assessment, and exclusion, with respect to which he or she has not requested a hearing. [51 FR 34777, Sept. 30, 1986, as amended at 57 FR 3348, Jan. 29, 1992]
