CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 490  /  Sec. 490.1 What is the Life Skills for State and Local

The Secretary may make grants to eligible entities to assist them in establishing and operating programs designed to reduce recidivism through the development and improvement of life skills necessary for reintegration of adult prisoners into society. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1211-2(e)(1))
