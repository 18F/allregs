CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 21  /  Sec. 21.8380 Additional applicable regulations.

The following regulations are applicable to children in this program in a manner comparable to that provided for veterans under the 38 U.S.C. chapter 31 program: Sec. Sec. 21.380, 21.412, 21.414 (except (c), (d), and (e)), 21.420, and 21.430. (Authority: 38 U.S.C. 1804, 1814, 5112)
