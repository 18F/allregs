CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 8  /  Sec. 8.6 Calculation of time period.

If the last day of a time period specified in Sec. Sec. 8.2 or 8.3 or allowed for filing an application for National Service life insurance or for applying for reinstatement thereof, or paying premiums due thereon, falls on a Saturday, Sunday, or legal holiday, the time period will be extended to include the following workday.
