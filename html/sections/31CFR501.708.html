CFR /  Title 31  /  Part 501  /  Sec. 501.708 Director's finding of no penalty warranted.

If after considering any written response to the Prepenalty Notice submitted pursuant to Sec. 501.707 and any other relevant facts, the Director determines that there was no violation or that the violation does not warrant the imposition of a civil monetary penalty, the Director promptly shall notify the respondent in writing of that determination and that no civil monetary penalty pursuant to this subpart will be imposed.
