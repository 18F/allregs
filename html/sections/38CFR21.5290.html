CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 21  /  Sec. 21.5290 Educational Assistance Pilot Program.

(a) Purpose. The Educational Assistance Pilot Program is designed to encourage enlistments and reenlistments in the Army, Navy, Air Force and Marine Corps. (Authority: Sec. 903, Pub. L. 96-342; 94 Stat. 1115)

(b) Outline of program. This program allows some individuals:

(1) To participate while making contributions at a rate less than that prescribed in Sec. 21.5052(b), and/or

(2) To transfer entitlement allowed in Sec. 21.5071 to a spouse or child. (Authority: Sec. 903, Pub. L. 96-342, 94 Stat. 1115) [47 FR 51747, Nov. 17, 1982]
