CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 724  /  Sec. 724.105 Compliance.

An agency's failure to reimburse the Judgment Fund, to contact FMS within 45 business days after receipt of an FMS notice for reimbursement under Sec. 724.104 will be recorded on an annual basis and posted on the FMS Web site. After an agency meets the requirements of Sec. 724.104, the recording will be eliminated no later than the next annual posting process.
