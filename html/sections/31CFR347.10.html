CFR /  Title 31  /  Part 347  /  Sec. 347.10 Authorized form of registration.

(a) Retirement savings bonds are issued to the designated Roth IRA custodian for Treasury's retirement savings program. No other registrations are permitted.

(b) In the event Treasury designates a successor designated Roth IRA custodian, retirement savings bonds held by the predecessor custodian will be reissued to the successor custodian.
