CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 1250  /  Sec. 1250.62 Submittal of construction plans.

Plans for construction or major reconstruction of sanitation facilities at servicing areas shall be submitted to the Commissioner of Food and Drugs for review of the conformity of the proposed facilities with the requirements of this subpart. [40 FR 5624, Feb. 6, 1975, as amended at 48 FR 11432, Mar. 18, 1983]
