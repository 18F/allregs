CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 200  /  Sec. 200.153 Presentation of claim.

In the event the insured lender is entitled under the contract of mortgage insurance to receive a claim settlement, the mortgagee presents a claim for insurance benefits in accordance with the Secretary's instructions. [61 FR 14404, Apr. 1, 1996]
