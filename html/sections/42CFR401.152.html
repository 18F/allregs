CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 401  /  Sec. 401.152 Court review.

Where the Administrator upon review affirms the denial of a request for records, in whole or in part, the requester may seek court review in the district court of the United States pursuant to 5 U.S.C. 552(a)(4)(B). Subparts C-E [Reserved]
