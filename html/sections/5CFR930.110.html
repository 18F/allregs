CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 930  /  Sec. 930.110 Identification of authorized operators and incidental

Agencies must have procedures to identify employees who are authorized to operate Government-owned or -leased motor vehicles. Such procedures must provide for adequate control of access to vehicles and assure that the other requirements of this subpart are met. [50 FR 34669, Aug. 27, 1985, as amended at 66 FR 66712, Dec. 27, 2001]
