CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 493  /  Sec. 493.1204 Condition: Parasitology.

If the laboratory provides services in the subspecialty of Parasitology, the laboratory must meet the requirements specified in Sec. Sec. 493.1230 through 493.1256, Sec. 493.1264, and Sec. Sec. 493.1281 through 493.1299.
