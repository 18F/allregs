CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 426  /  Sec. 426.516 Role of Medicare Managed Care Organizations (MCOs) and

Medicare MCOs and Medicaid State agencies may participate in the NCD review process only if they meet the amicus participant criteria listed in Secs. 426.510(f)(3) and 426.513.
