CFR /  Title 31  /  Part 223  /  Sec. 223.4 Deposits.

No such company will be granted authority to do business under the provisions of the act referred to in Sec. 223.1 unless it shall have and maintain on deposit with the Insurance Commissioner. or other proper financial officer, of the State in which it is incorporated, or of any other State of the United States, for the protection of claimants, including all its policyholders in the United States, legal investments having a current market value of not less than $100,000. The company shall submit to Treasury with its initial application for a certificate of authority, and annually thereafter, a written statement signed by such State official attesting to the current market value of the deposit (not less than $100,000) and that the legal investments remain on deposit with the State under the terms specified. [36 FR 9630, May 27, 1971, as amended at 79 FR 62000, Oct. 16, 2014]
