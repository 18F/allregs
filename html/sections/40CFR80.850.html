(b) The value of existing toxics standard, TExist, is equal to:

(1) 21.5 percent, for reformulated gasoline and RBOB, combined;

(2) The refinery's or importer's anti-dumping compliance baseline value for exhaust toxics, in mg/mi, per Sec. 80.101(f), for conventional gasoline.
