CFR /  Title 19  /  Part 24  /  Sec. 24.72 Claims; set-off.

When an importer of record or other party has a judgment or other claim allowed by legal authority against the United States, and he is indebted to the United States, either as principal or surety, for an amount which is legally fixed and undisputed, the port director shall set off so much of the judgment or other claim as will equal the amount of the debt due the Government. [T.D. 56388, 30 FR 4671, Apr. 10, 1965]
