CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 416  /  Sec. 416.1031 Confidentiality of information and records.

The State will comply with the confidentiality of information, including the security of systems, and records requirements described in 20 CFR part 401 and pertinent written guidelines (see Sec. 416.1033).
