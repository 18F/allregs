CFR /  Title 31  /  Part 248  /  Sec. 248.1 Introductory.

This part governs the issuance of substitutes for checks of the United States drawn on United States dollar or foreign currency accounts, maintained with designated depositaries in foreign countries or territories or possessions of the United States. Checks of the United States drawn on such depositaries are hereafter referred to as ``depositary checks.'' [54 FR 35647, Aug. 29, 1989]
