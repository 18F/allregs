CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 925  /  Sec. 925.215 Assessment rate.

On and after January 1, 2015, an assessment rate of $0.0250 per 18-pound lug is established for grapes grown in a designated area of southeastern California. [80 FR 27245, May 13, 2015]
