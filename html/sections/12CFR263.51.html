CFR /  Title 12  /  Part 263  /  Sec. 263.51 Definitions.

As used in subparts B through G of this part:

(a) Secretary means the Secretary of the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System;

(b) Member bank means any bank that is a member of the Federal Reserve System.

(c) Institution has the same meaning as that assigned to it in Sec. 263.3(f) of subpart A, and includes any foreign bank with a representative office in the United States. [56 FR 38052, Aug. 9, 1991, as amended at 57 FR 13001, Apr. 15, 1992; 58 FR 6363, Jan. 28, 1993]
