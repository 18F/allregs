CFR /  Title 29  /  Part 500  /  Sec. 500.156 Scope of agreements with Federal agencies.

Every agreement between the Secretary and any other Federal agency under the authority referred to in Sec. 500.155 of this part shall contain terms and conditions mutually agreeable to both parties, and shall contain such delegation of authority as the Secretary deems useful.
