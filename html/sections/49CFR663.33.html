CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 663  /  Sec. 663.33 Description of post-delivery audit.

A post-delivery audit under this part includes--

(a) A post-delivery Buy America certification as described in Sec. 663.35 of this part;

(b) A post-delivery purchaser's requirements certification as described in Sec. 663.37 of this part; and

(c) When appropriate, a manufacturer's Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard self-certification information as described in Sec. 663.41 or Sec. 663.43 of this part.
