CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 1846  /  1846.671 Contract quality assurance on shipments between contractors.

(a) The supplier's commercial shipping document/packing list shall indicate performance of required CQA actions at subcontract level.

The following entries shall be made on the document/packing list:

Required CQA of items has been performed. ________________________________________________________________________ (Signature of Authorized Government Representative) ________________________________________________________________________ (Date) ________________________________________________________________________ (Typed Name and Office)

(b) Distribution for Government purposes shall be one copy each--

(1) With shipment;

(2) For the Government representative at consignee (via mail); and

(3) For the Government representative at consignor.
