CFR /  Title 12  /  Part 1221  /  Sec. 1221.2 Definitions.

* * * * *

Covered swap entity means any regulated entity that is a swap entity or any other entity that FHFA determines.

* * * * *

Regulated entity means any regulated entity as defined in section 1303(20) of the Federal Housing Enterprises Financial Safety and Soundness Act of 1992, as amended (12 U.S.C. 4502(20)).
