CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 193  /  Sec. 193.2705 Construction, installation, inspection, and testing.

(a) Supervisors and other personnel utilized for construction, installation, inspection, or testing must have demonstrated their capability to perform satisfactorily the assigned function by appropriate training in the methods and equipment to be used or related experience and accomplishments.

(b) Each operator must periodically determine whether inspectors performing construction, installation, and testing duties required by this part are satisfactorily performing their assigned functions. [45 FR 9219, Feb. 11, 1980, as amended by Amdt. 193-18, 69 FR 11337, Mar. 10, 2004]
