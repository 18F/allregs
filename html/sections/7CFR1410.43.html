CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 1410  /  Sec. 1410.43 Method of payment.

Except as provided in Sec. 1410.50, payments made by the Deputy Administrator under this part may be made in cash or other methods of payment in accordance with part 1401 of this chapter, unless otherwise specified by the Deputy Administrator. [68 FR 24835, May 8, 2003, as amended at 80 FR 42005, July 16, 2015]
