CFR /  Title 27  /  Part 21  /  Sec. 21.64 Formula No. 37.

(a) Formula. To every 100 gallons of alcohol add: Forty-five fluid onces of eucalyptol, N.F. XII, 30 avoirdupois ounces of thymol, N.F., and 20 avoirdupois ounces of menthol, U.S.P.

(b) Authorized uses. (1) As a solvent: 111. Hair and scalp preparations.112. Bay rum.113. Lotions and creams (hand, face, and body).131. Dentifrices.132. Mouth washes.210. External pharmaceuticals, not U.S.P. or N.F.244. Antispetic solutions, U.S.P. or N.F. 410. Disinfectants, insecticides, fungicides, and other biocides.430. Sterilizing and preserving solutions.470.
