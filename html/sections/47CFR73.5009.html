CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 73  /  Sec. 73.5009 Assignment or transfer of control.

(a) The unjust enrichment provisions found at Sec. 1.2111(b) through (e) of this chapter shall not apply to applicants seeking approval of a transfer of control or assignment of a broadcast construction permit or license within three years of receiving such permit or license by means of competitive bidding.

(b) The ownership disclosure requirements found at Sec. 1.2112(a) of this chapter shall not apply to an applicant seeking consent to assign or transfer control of a broadcast construction permit or license awarded by competitive bidding. [67 FR 45375, July 9, 2002, as amended at 68 FR 43000, July 21, 2003]
