CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 414  /  Sec. 414.40 Applicability; description of the thermoplastic resins

The provisions of this subpart are applicable to the process wastewater discharges resulting from the manufacture of the products classified under SIC 28213 thermoplastic resins including those resins and resin groups listed below.
