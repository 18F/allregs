CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 835  /  Sec. 835.4 Use of reports.

(a) As a testimonial aid and to refresh their memories, Board employees may use copies of the factual accident report they prepared, and may refer to and cite from that report during testimony.

(b) Consistent with section 701(e) of the FA Act and section 304(c) of the Safety Act, a Board employee may not use the Board's accident report for any purpose during his testimony. [55 FR 41541, Oct. 12, 1990, as amended at 63 FR 71607, Dec. 29, 1998]
