CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 78  /  Sec. 78.17 Amendment of applications.

Any application may be amended as a matter of right prior to the adoption date of any final action taken by the Commission with respect to the application. If a petition to deny has been filed, the amendment shall be served on the petitioner. [68 FR 27004, May 19, 2003]
