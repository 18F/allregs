CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 556  /  Sec. 556.346 Laidlomycin.

(a) Acceptable daily intake (ADI). The ADI for total residues of laidlomycin is 7.5 micrograms per kilogram of body weight per day.

(b) Tolerance. The tolerance for parent laidlomycin (the marker residue) in the liver (the target tissue) of cattle is 0.2 part per million (ppm). [68 FR 42590, July 18, 2003]
