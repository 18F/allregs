CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 117  /  Sec. 117.963 Colorado River.

The draw of the highway bridge, mile 10.7 at Wadsworth need open on signal Monday through Friday only, and then only from 8 a.m. to 5 p.m. At least 48 hours notice is required. [CGD8-90-22, 56 FR 488, Jan. 7, 1991]
