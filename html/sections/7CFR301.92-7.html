CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 301  /  Sec. 301.92-7 Availability of inspectors; assembly for inspection.

(a) Any person (other than a person authorized to issue certificates under Sec. 301.92-5(c)) who desires to move a regulated or associated article or non-host nursery stock interstate accompanied by a certificate must notify an inspector \16\ as far in advance of the desired interstate movement as possible, but no less than 48 hours before the desired time of inspection. ---------------------------------------------------------------------------

\16\ See footnote 7 of this subpart. ---------------------------------------------------------------------------

(b) The regulated or associated article or non-host nursery stock must be assembled at the place and in the manner the inspector designates as necessary to comply with this subpart.
