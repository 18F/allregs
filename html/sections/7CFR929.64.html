CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 929  /  Sec. 929.64 Verification of reports and records.

The committee, through its duly authorized agents, during reasonable business hours, shall have access to any handler's premises where applicable records are maintained for the purpose of assuring compliance and checking and verifying records and reports filed by such handler. [70 FR 7645, Feb. 15, 2005]
