CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 183  /  Sec. 183.528 Fuel stop valves.

(a) Each electrically operated fuel stop valve in a fuel line between the fuel tank and the engine must:

(1) Open electrically only when the ignition switch is on; and

(2) Operate manually.

(b) If tested in accordance with the fire test under Sec. 183.590, a fuel stop valve installed in a fuel line system requiring metallic fuel lines or ``USCG Type A1'' hose must not leak fuel. [CGD 74-209, 42 FR 5950, Jan. 31, 1977, as amended by CGD 85-098, 52 FR 19728, May 27, 1987]
