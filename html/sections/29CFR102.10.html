CFR /  Title 29  /  Part 102  /  Sec. 102.10 Where to file.

Except as provided in Sec. 102.33 such charge shall be filed with the regional director for the region in which the alleged unfair labor practice has occurred or is occurring. A charge alleging that an unfair labor practice has occurred or is occurring in two or more regions may be filed with the regional director for any of such regions.
