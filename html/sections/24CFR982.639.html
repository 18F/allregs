CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 982  /  Sec. 982.639 Homeownership option: Administrative fees.

The ongoing administrative fee described in Sec. 982.152(b) is paid to the PHA for each month that homeownership assistance is paid by the PHA on behalf of the family.
