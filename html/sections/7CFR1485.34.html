CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 1485  /  Sec. 1485.34 Noncompliance with an agreement.

If a MAP Participant fails to comply with any term in its program agreement or approval letter, CCC may take one or more of the enforcement actions in 2 CFR part 200 and, if, appropriate, initiate a claim against the MAP Participant, following the procedures set forth in this subpart. CCC may also initiate a claim against a MAP Participant if program income or CCC-provided funds are lost due to an action or omission of the MAP Participant. [60 FR 6363, Feb. 1, 1995, as amended at 79 FR 75997, Dec. 19, 2014]
