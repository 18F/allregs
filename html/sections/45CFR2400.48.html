CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 2400  /  Sec. 2400.48 Fellows' participation in the Summer Institute.

Each fellow is required as part of his or her fellowship to attend the Institute (if it is offered), normally during the summer following the Fellow's commencement of graduate study under a fellowship. [69 FR 11815, Mar. 12, 2004]
