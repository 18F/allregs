CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 583  /  Sec. 583.17 Reporting.

For each model year, manufacturers shall submit to the Administrator 3 copies of the information required by Sec. 583.5(a) to be placed on a label for each carline. The information for each carline shall be submitted not later than the date the first vehicle of the carline is offered for sale to the ultimate purchaser.
