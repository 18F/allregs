CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 528  /  528.311-1 Contract clause.

Use the clause at FAR 52.228-7, Insurance--Liability to Third Persons, in solicitations and contracts, other than those for construction and those for architect-engineer services, when a cost-reimbursement contract is contemplated, unless the head of the contracting activity waives the requirement for use of the clause. [74 FR 17099, Apr. 14, 2009]
