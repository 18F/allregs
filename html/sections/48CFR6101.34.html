CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 6101  /  Sec. 6101.34 Seal of the Board [Rule 34].

The Seal of the Board shall be a circular boss, the outer margin of which shall bear the legend ``Civilian Board of Contract Appeals.'' The Seal shall be the means of authentication of all records, notices, orders, dismissals, opinions, subpoenas, and certificates issued by the Board. Sec. 6101.35-6101.50 [Reserved]
