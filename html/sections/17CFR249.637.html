CFR /  Title 17  /  Part 249  /  Sec. 249.637 Form ATS, information required of alternative trading

This form shall be used by every alternative trading system to file required notices, reports and amendments under Sec. 242.301(b)(2) of this chapter. [63 FR 70933, Dec. 22, 1998]

Editorial Note: For Federal Register citations affecting Form ATS, see the List of CFR Sections Affected, which appears in the Finding Aids section of the printed volume and at www.fdsys.gov.
