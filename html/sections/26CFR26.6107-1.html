CFR /  Title 26  /  Part 26  /  Sec. 26.6107-1 Tax return preparer must furnish copy of return to

(a) In general. A person who is a signing tax return preparer of any return or claim for refund of generation-skipping transfer tax under chapter 13 of subtitle B of the Internal Revenue Code shall furnish a completed copy of the return or claim for refund to the taxpayer, and retain a completed copy or record in the manner stated in Sec. 1.6107-1 of this chapter.

(b) Effective/applicability date. This section is applicable to returns and claims for refund filed after December 31, 2008. [T.D. 9436, 73 FR 78452, Dec. 22, 2008]
