CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 532  /  Sec. 532.509 Pay for Sunday work.

A wage employee whose regular work schedule includes a period of service of up to 8 hours which is not overtime work, a part of which is on Sunday, is entitled to additional pay under the provisions of section 5544 of title 5, United States Code. [76 FR 52539, Aug. 23, 2011]
