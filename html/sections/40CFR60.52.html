CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 60  /  Sec. 60.52 Standard for particulate matter.

(a) On and after the date on which the initial performance test is completed or required to be completed under Sec. 60.8 of this part, whichever date comes first, no owner or operator subject to the provisions of this part shall cause to be discharged into the atmosphere from any affected facility any gases which contain particulate matter in excess of 0.18 g/dscm (0.08 gr/dscf) corrected to 12 percent CO2. [39 FR 20792, June 14, 1974, as amended at 65 FR 61753, Oct. 17, 2000]
