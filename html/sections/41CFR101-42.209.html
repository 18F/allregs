CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 101-42  /  Sec. 101-42.209 Cost of care and handling of hazardous materials and

The special handling requirements associated with many hazardous materials often increase the cost of core and handling of hazardous materials well above the usual costs incurred while holding excess personal property pending disposition. As provided in Sec. 101-43.310- 1, each holding agency shall be responsible for, and bear the cost of, care and handling of excess property pending disposition, including those special costs associated with hazardous materials. Only the cost of transportation and handling incurred incident to the transfer of hazardous materials are borne by the transferee agency if billed by the holding agency in accordance with Sec. 101-43.309-3.
