CFR /  Title 12  /  Part 261b  /  Sec. 261b.13 Fees.

(a) Copies of transcripts, recordings or transcriptions of recordings, or minutes requested pursuant to section Sec. 261b.12(b) of this part will be provided at the cost of 10[cent] per standard page for photocopying or at a cost not to exceed the actual cost of printing, typing, or otherwise preparing such copies.

(b) Documents may be furnished without charge where total charges are less than $2.
