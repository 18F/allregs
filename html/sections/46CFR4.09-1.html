CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 4  /  Sec. 4.09-1 Commandant to designate.

If it appears that it would tend to promote safety of life and property at sea or would be in the public interest, the Commandant may designate a Marine Board of Investigation to conduct an investigation. [CGD 76-170, 45 FR 77441, Nov. 24, 1980]
