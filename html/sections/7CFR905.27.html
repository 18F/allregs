CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 905  /  Sec. 905.27 Failure to nominate.

In the event nominations for a member or alternate member of the committee are not made pursuant to the provisions of Sec. Sec. 905.22 and 905.25, the Secretary may select such member or alternate member without regard to nominations. [22 FR 10734, Dec. 27, 1957. Redesignated at 26 FR 12751, Dec. 30, 1961, as amended at 42 FR 59370, Nov. 17, 1977]
