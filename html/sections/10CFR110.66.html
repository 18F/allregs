CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 110  /  Sec. 110.66 Enforcement hearing.

(a) If the licensee demands a hearing, the Commission will issue an order specifying the time and place.

(b) A hearing pursuant to this subpart will be conducted under the procedures in subpart G of part 2 of this chapter. [43 FR 21641, May 19, 1978, as amended at 75 FR 44093, July 28, 2010]
