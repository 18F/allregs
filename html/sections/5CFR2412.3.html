CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 2412  /  Sec. 2412.3 Notice and publication.

The Authority, the General Counsel, and the Panel will publish in the Federal Register such notices describing systems of records as are required by law. [51 FR 33837, Sept. 23, 1986]
