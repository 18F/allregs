CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 102-117  /  Sec. 102-117.15 To whom does this part apply?

This part applies to all agencies and wholly-owned Government corporations as defined in 5 U.S.C. 101, et seq. and 31 U.S.C. 9101(3), except as otherwise expressly provided. [79 FR 33476, June 11, 2014]
