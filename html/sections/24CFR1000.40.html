CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 1000  /  Sec. 1000.40 Do lead-based paint poisoning prevention requirements

Yes, lead-based paint requirements apply to housing activities assisted under NAHASDA. The applicable requirements for NAHASDA are HUD's regulations at part 35, subparts A, B, H, J, K, M and R of this title, which implement the Lead-Based Paint Poisoning Prevention Act (42 U.S.C. 4822-4846) and the Residential Lead-Based Paint Hazard Reduction Act of 1992 (42 U.S.C. 4851-4856). [64 FR 50230, Sept. 15, 1999; 65 FR 3387, Jan. 21, 2000]
