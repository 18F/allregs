CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 945  /  Sec. 945.54 Minimum quantity exemption.

The committee, with the approval of the Secretary, may establish, for any or all portions of the production area, minimum quantities below which shipments will be free from regulations issued or in effect pursuant to Sec. Sec. 945.40 to 945.65, inclusive, or any combination thereof.
