CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 3  /  Sec. 3.60 Definition of ``living with''.

For the purposes of determining entitlement to pension under 38 U.S.C. 1521, a person shall be considered as living with his or her spouse even though they reside apart unless they are estranged. (Authority: 38 U.S.C. 1521(h)(2)) [44 FR 45935, Aug. 6, 1979]
