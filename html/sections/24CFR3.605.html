CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 3  /  Sec. 3.605 Enforcement procedures.

The investigative, compliance, and enforcement procedural provisions of Title VI of the Civil Rights Act of 1964 (42 U.S.C. 2000d) (``Title VI'') are hereby adopted and applied to these Title IX regulations. These procedures may be found at 24 CFR part 1. [65 FR 52880, Aug. 30, 2000]
