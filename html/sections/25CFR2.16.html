CFR /  Title 25  /  Part 2  /  Sec. 2.16 Extensions of time.

An official to whom an appeal is made may, upon a showing of good cause by a party and with notice to all other parties, extend the period for filing or serving any document; provided, however, that no extension will be granted for filing a notice of appeal under Sec. 2.9 of this part or serve by itself to extend any period specified by law or regulation other than in this part.
