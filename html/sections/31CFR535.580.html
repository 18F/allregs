CFR /  Title 31  /  Part 535  /  Sec. 535.580 Necessary living expenses of relatives of the former

The transfer, payment or withdrawal of property described in Sec. 535.217 is authorized to the extent necessary to pay living expenses of any individual listed in that section. Living expenses for this purpose shall include food, housing, transportation, security and other personal expenses. (Secs. 201-207, 91 Stat. 1626, 50 U.S.C. 1701-1706; E.O. 12170, 44 FR 65729; E.O. 12211, 45 FR 26685; E.O. 12284, 46 FR 7929) [46 FR 14330, Feb. 26, 1981]
