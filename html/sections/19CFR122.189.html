CFR /  Title 19  /  Part 122  /  Sec. 122.189 Bond liability.

Any failure on the part of a principal to comply with the conditions of the bond required under Sec. 122.182(c), including a failure of an employer to comply with any requirement applicable to the employer under this subpart, will constitute a breach of the bond and may result in a claim for liquidated damages under the bond. [T.D. 02-40, 67 FR 48988, July 29, 2002]
