CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 1944  /  Sec. 1944.62 Authorized representative of the applicant.

RHS or its successor agency under Public Law 103-354 will deal only with authorized representatives designated by the applicant. The authorized representatives must have no pecuniary interest in the award of the architectural or construction contracts, the purchase of equipment, or the purchase of the land for the housing site. [58 FR 58643, Nov. 3, 1993, as amended at 61 FR 39851, July 31, 1996]
