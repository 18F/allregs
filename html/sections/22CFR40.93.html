CFR /  Title 22  /  Part 40  /  Sec. 40.93 Aliens unlawfully present after previous immigration

An alien described in INA 212(a)(9)(C)(i) is permanently ineligible for a visa unless the Secretary of Homeland Security consents to the alien's application for readmission not less than 10 years following the alien's last departure from the United States. Such application for readmission shall be made prior to the alien's reembarkation at a place outside the United States. [62 FR 67568, Dec. 29, 1997] Sec. Sec. 40.94-40.99 [Reserved]
