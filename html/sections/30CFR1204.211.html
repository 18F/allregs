CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 1204  /  Sec. 1204.211 When may ONRR rescind relief for a property?

(a) ONRR may retroactively rescind the relief for your property if ONRR determines that your property was not eligible for the relief obtained under this subpart because:

(1) You did not submit a notice or request for relief under Sec. 1204.205;

(2) You submitted erroneous information in the notice or request for relief you provided to ONRR under Sec. 1204.205 or in your royalty or production reports; or

(3) Your property is no longer eligible for relief because production increased, but you failed to provide the notice required under Sec. 1204.209(b).

(b) ONRR may rescind relief for your property if ONRR decides to take royalty in kind.
