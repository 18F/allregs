CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 948  /  Sec. 948.102 Communications.

Unless otherwise provided in Sec. Sec. 948.1 to 948.92, or by specific direction of an area committee, all reports, applications, submittals, requests and communications in connection with the order shall be addressed to the office of the committee for the area in which the potatoes involved are grown.
