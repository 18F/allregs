CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 40  /  Sec. 40.283 How does a certification organization obtain recognition

(a) If you represent a certification organization that wants DOT to authorize its certified drug and alcohol counselors to be added to Sec. 40.281(a)(6), you may submit a written petition to DOT requesting a review of your petition for inclusion.

(b) You must obtain the National Commission for Certifying Agencies (NCCA) accreditation before DOT will act on your petition.

(c) You must also meet the minimum requirements of Appendix E to this part before DOT will act on your petition. [65 FR 79526, Dec. 19, 2000, as amended at 71 FR 49384, Aug. 23, 2006]
