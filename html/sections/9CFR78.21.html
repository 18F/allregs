CFR /  Title 9  /  Part 78  /  Sec. 78.21 Bison steers and spayed heifers.

Bison steers and spayed heifers may be moved interstate without further restriction under this subpart. [51 FR 32580, Sept. 12, 1986, as amended at 78 FR 2070, Jan. 9, 2013]
