CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 89  /  Sec. 89.515 Appeal of hearing decision.

The procedures provided in Sec. 86.1014-84 (t) to (aa) apply for appeals filed with respect to hearings held pursuant to Sec. 89.514. [59 FR 31335, June 17, 1994. Redesignated at 63 FR 56996, Oct. 23, 1998]
