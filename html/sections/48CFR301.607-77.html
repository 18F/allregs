CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 301  /  301.607-77 Governance.

The Departmental ACM, in ASFR/OGAPA/DA, serves as the Departmental FAC-P/PM Program Manager and is responsible for administering the program. To support the overall management of the FAC-P/PM certification program at the OPDIV level, Executive Officers and their HCAs may either use their existing ACM or designate an additional ACM, whose professional background includes program and project management. See Appendix B, Federal Acquisition Certification--Program and Project Managers--Roles and Responsibilities, in the P/PM Handbook for additional information. [74 FR 62398, Nov. 27, 2009. Redesignated at 75 FR 21510, Apr. 26, 2010]
