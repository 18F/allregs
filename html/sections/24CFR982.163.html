CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 982  /  Sec. 982.163 Fraud recoveries.

Under 24 CFR part 792, the PHA may retain a portion of program fraud losses that the PHA recovers from a family or owner by litigation, court-order or a repayment agreement. [60 FR 34695, July 3, 1995; 60 FR 43840, Aug. 23, 1995]
