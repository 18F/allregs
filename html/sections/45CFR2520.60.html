CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 2520  /  Sec. 2520.60 What government-wide requirements apply to staff

You must follow OMB Guidance published at 2 CFR part 200 and Corporation implementing regulations at 2 CFR Chapter XXII. In particular, see 2 CFR 200.442--Fundraising and Investment Management Costs. [79 FR 76077, Dec. 19, 2014]
