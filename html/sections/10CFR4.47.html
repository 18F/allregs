CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 4  /  Sec. 4.47 Noncompliance with Sec. 4.21.

If an applicant fails or refuses to furnish an assurance required under Sec. 4.21 or otherwise fails or refuses to comply with a requirement imposed by or pursuant to that section, Federal financial assistance may be refused in accordance with the procedures of Sec. 4.48. [45 FR 14535, Mar. 6, 1980]
