CFR /  Title 32  /  Part 247  /  Sec. 247.1 Purpose.

This part implements DoD Directive 5122.10 \1\ and implements policy, assigns responsibilities, and prescribes procedures concerning authorized DoD Appropriated Funded (APF) newspapers and magazines, and Civilian Enterprise (CE) newspapers, magazines, guides, and installation maps in support of the DoD Internal Information Program.---------------------------------------------------------------------------

\1\ Copies may be obtained, at cost, from the National Technical Information Service, 5285 Port Royal Road, Springfield, VA 22121.---------------------------------------------------------------------------
