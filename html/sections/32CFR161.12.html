CFR /  Title 32  /  Part 161  /  Sec. 161.12 Benefits for former uniformed services members.

(a) This section describes the benefits for former uniformed services members and their eligible dependents. Former members are eligible to receive retired pay, at age 60, for non-regular service in accordance with 10 U.S.C. chapter 1223, but have been discharged from their respective Service or agency and maintain no military affiliation.

(1) Former Members and their Eligible Dependents. Former members and their dependents are eligible for benefits as shown in Table 6 to part 161.
