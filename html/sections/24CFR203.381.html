CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 203  /  Sec. 203.381 Occupancy of property.

The mortgagee shall certify that the property is vacant and contains no personal property as of the date of filing for record of the deed to the Secretary or that the Secretary has consented to accept the property occupied. [45 FR 59563, Sept. 10, 1980]
