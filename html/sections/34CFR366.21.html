CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 366  /  Sec. 366.21 What are the application requirements for existing eligible

To be eligible for assistance, an eligible agency shall submit--

(a) An application at the time, in the manner, and containing the information that is required;

(b) An assurance that the eligible agency meets the requirements of Sec. 366.2; and

(c) The assurances required by section 725(c) of the Act and subpart F of this part. (Approved by the Office of Management and Budget under control number 1820-0018) (Authority: 29 U.S.C. 796f-1(b))
