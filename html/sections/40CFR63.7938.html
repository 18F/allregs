CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 63  /  Sec. 63.7938 How do I demonstrate continuous compliance with the general standards?

(a) You must demonstrate continuous compliance with the general standards in Sec. Sec. 63.7884 through 63.7887 that apply to your affected sources by meeting the requirements in paragraphs (b) through (d) of this section, as applicable to you.

(b)(1) through (4) of this section, as applicable to your process vents.

(1), you must demonstrate continuous compliance by meeting the requirements in Sec. 63.7893.

(2) to show that the total VOHAP concentration of the remediation material remains less than 10 ppmw.

(3), you must demonstrate continuous compliance by complying with all applicable emissions limitations and work practice standards specified by the applicable subpart.

(4) For each process vent exempted according to Sec. 63.7885(c), you must demonstrate continuous compliance by performing new measurements and preparing new documentation as required in Sec. 63.7885(c)(2) that demonstrates that each exempted process vent stream meets the applicable exemption conditions in Sec. 63.7885(c)(1).

(c)(1) through (6) of this section, as applicable to your remediation material management units.

(1), you must demonstrate continuous compliance by meeting the requirements applicable to the remediation material management unit in Sec. Sec. 63.7898, 63.7903, 63.7908, 63.7913, or 63.7818.

(2) to show that the total VOHAP concentration of the remediation material remains less than 500 ppmw.

(3), you must demonstrate continuous compliance by meeting all applicable emissions limitations and work practice standards specified by the applicable subpart.

(4)(i) and (ii) of this section.

(i) that demonstrates that each unit meets the applicable performance levels.

(ii) Monitoring the biological treatment process conducted in each unit according to the requirements in Sec. 63.7886(4)(i).

(5) For each remediation material management unit used for cleanup of radioactive mixed waste and exempted according to Sec. 63.7886(c), you must demonstrate continuous compliance by meeting all requirements that apply to the remediation material management units under the applicable regulations or directives.

(6) For each remediation material management unit exempted according to Sec. 63.7886(d), you must demonstrate continuous compliance by performing new measurements and preparing new documentation as required in Sec. 63.7886(d)(2) to show that the total annual HAP quantity (based on the HAP listed in Table 1 of this subpart) in the remediation material placed in all of the designated exempted remediation material management units remains less than 1 Mg/yr.

(d) You have demonstrated continuous compliance with the general standards in Sec. 63.7887 that apply to your affected equipment leak sources by meeting the requirements in Sec. 63.7923. [68 FR 58190, Oct. 8, 2003, as amended at 71 FR 69018, Nov. 29, 2006]
