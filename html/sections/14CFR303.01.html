CFR /  Title 14  /  Part 303  /  Sec. 303.01 Purpose.

These regulations set forth the procedures by which applications may be made to the Department of Transportation under sections 412 and 414 of the Federal Aviation Act, as amended (49 U.S.C. 1382 and 1384) and procedures governing proceedings to enforce these provisions. [Amdt. 303-2, 54 FR 33499, Aug. 15, 1989]
