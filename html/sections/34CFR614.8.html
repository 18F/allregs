CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 614  /  Sec. 614.8 What is the significance of the deadline date for applications?

Notwithstanding Sec. 75.102 of this chapter, an application for a grant under this program must be received by the deadline date that will be announced in a separate notice in the Federal Register. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 6832)
