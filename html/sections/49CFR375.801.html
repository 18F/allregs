CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 375  /  Sec. 375.801 What types of charges apply to subpart H?

This subpart applies to all shipments of household goods that:

(a) Entail a balance due freight or expense bill, or

(b) Are transported on an extension of credit basis. [69 FR 10577, Mar. 5, 2004]
