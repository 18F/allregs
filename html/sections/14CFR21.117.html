CFR /  Title 14  /  Part 21  /  Sec. 21.117 Issue of supplemental type certificates.

(a) An applicant is entitled to a supplemental type certificate if the FAA finds that the applicant meets the requirements of Sec. Sec. 21.113 and 21.115.

(b) A supplemental type certificate consists of--

(1) The approval by the FAA of a change in the type design of the product; and

(2) The type certificate previously issued for the product. [Doc. No. 5085, 29 FR 14568, Oct. 24, 1964, as amended by Amdt. 21-92, 74 FR 53387, Oct. 16, 2009]
