CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 648  /  Sec. 648.7 What is the institutional matching contribution?

An institution shall provide, from non-Federal funds, an institutional matching contribution equal to at least 25 percent of the amount of the grant received under this part, for the uses indicated in Sec. 648.63. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1135b, 1135c)
