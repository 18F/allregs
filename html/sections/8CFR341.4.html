CFR /  Title 8  /  Part 341  /  Sec. 341.4 Surrender of immigration documents.

Each claimant shall surrender any immigration identification and permanent resident cards in his or her possession. [30 FR 5472, Apr. 16, 1965, as amended at 63 FR 70316, Dec. 21, 1998]
