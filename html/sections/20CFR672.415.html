CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 672  /  Sec. 672.415 What are the due dates for quarterly reporting?

(a) Quarterly reports are due no later than 45 days after the end of the reporting quarter, unless otherwise specified in the reporting guidance issued under Sec. 672.410; and

(b) A final financial report is required 90 days after the expiration of a funding period or the termination of grant support.
