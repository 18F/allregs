CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 153  /  Sec. 153.365 Liquid overpressurization protection.

(a) Except as noted in paragraph (b) of this section, a containment system requiring closed or restricted gauging must:

(1) Be designed to withstand the maximum pressure that develops during an overfill of the densest cargo endorsed for the containment system; or

(2) Have an overflow control system that meets Sec. 153.408; or

(3) Meet the requirements specified by the Commandant (CG-ENG).

(b) A containment system requiring restricted gauging, except for those cargoes that reference Sec. Sec. 153.525 or 153.527, may be equipped with a spill valve that:

(1) Meets ASTM F 1271 (incorporated by reference, see Sec. 153.4); and

(2) Limits the maximum pressure during liquid overfill at a specified cargo loading rate to that which the containment system is able to withstand (see Sec. Sec. 153.294(b) and 152.977(b)).
