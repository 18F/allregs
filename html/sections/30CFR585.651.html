CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 585  /  Sec. 585.651 When may I construct complex or significant OCS facilities under my GAP?

If you are applying for a project easement, or installing a facility or a combination of facilities on your limited lease deemed by BOEM to be complex or significant, as provided in Sec. 585.648(a)(1), you also must comply with the requirements of subpart G of this part and submit your Safety Management System description required by Sec. 585.810 before construction may begin.
