CFR /  Title 31  /  Part 538  /  Sec. 538.530 Licenses and registrations issued pursuant to Executive

(a) All general licenses issued pursuant to Executive Order 13067 are authorized pursuant to Executive Order 13412 and remain in effect.

(b) All specific licenses and all nongovernmental organization registrations issued pursuant to Executive Order 13067 or this part prior to October 13, 2006, are authorized pursuant to Executive Order 13412 and remain in effect until the expiration date specified in the license or registration or, if no expiration date is specified, June 30, 2008. [72 FR 61516, Oct. 31, 2007]
