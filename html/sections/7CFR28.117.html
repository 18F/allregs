CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 28  /  Sec. 28.117 Fee for new memorandum or certificate.

For each new memorandum or certificate issued in substitution for a prior memorandum or certificate at the request of the holder, thereof, on account of the breaking or splitting of the lot of cotton covered thereby or otherwise for his business convenience, the person requesting such substitution shall pay a fee determined as described in Sec. 28.116. If the memorandum is provided by electronic means, the fee shall be determined using the same provisions. [79 FR 67319, Nov. 13, 2014]
