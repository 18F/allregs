CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 1484  /  Sec. 1484.10 What is the effective date of this part?

This part applies to activities that are conducted in accordance with the Cooperators' FY 2000 and subsequent marketing plan years. The Cooperator Program is administered by personnel of the Foreign Agricultural Service. [64 FR 52630, Sept. 30, 1999. Redesignated and amended at 65 FR 9995, Feb. 25, 2000]
