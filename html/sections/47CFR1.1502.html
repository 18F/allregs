CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 1  /  Sec. 1.1502 When the EAJA applies.

The EAJA applies to any adversary adjudication pending or commenced before the Commission on or after August 5, 1985. The provisions of Sec. 1.1505(b) apply to any adversary adjudications commenced on or after March 29, 1996. [61 FR 39898, July 31, 1996]
