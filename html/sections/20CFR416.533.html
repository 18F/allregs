CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 416  /  Sec. 416.533 Transfer or assignment of benefits.

Except as provided in Sec. 416.525 and subpart S of this part, the Social Security Administration will not certify payment of supplemental security income benefits to a transferee or assignee of a person eligible for such benefits under the Act or of a person qualified for payment under Sec. 416.542. The Social Security Administration shall not certify payment of supplemental security income benefits to any person claiming such payment by virtue of an execution, levy, attachment, garnishment, or other legal process or by virtue of any bankruptcy or insolvency proceeding against or affecting the person eligible for benefits under the Act. [41 FR 20873, May 21, 1976, as amended at 58 FR 52912, Oct. 13, 1993]
