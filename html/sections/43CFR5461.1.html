CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 5461  /  Sec. 5461.1 Payment in advance of cutting or removal.

Except as provided in Sec. Sec. 5451.2 and 5451.4 no part of any timber or other vegetative resources sold may be cut or removed unless advance payment has been made as provided in the contract. [35 FR 9787, June 13, 1970, as amended at 38 FR 6281, Mar. 8, 1973]
