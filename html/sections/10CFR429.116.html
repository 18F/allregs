CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 429  /  Sec. 429.116 Additional certification testing requirements.

Pursuant to Sec. 429.102(b)(2), if DOE determines that independent, third-party testing is necessary to ensure a manufacturer's compliance with the rules of this part, part 430, or part 431, a manufacturer must base its certification of a basic model under subpart B of this part on independent, third-party laboratory testing.
