CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 401  /  Sec. 401.49 Dropping anchor or tying to canal bank.

Except in an emergency, no vessel shall drop anchor in any canal or tie-up to any canal bank unless authorized to do so by the traffic controller. Every anchor shall be suitably rigged for immediate release, holding and efficient retrieval. [78 FR 16182, Mar. 14, 2013]
