CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 168  /  Sec. 168.71 Export pesticide devices.

(a) Each export pesticide device sold or distributed anywhere in the United States must bear labeling that complies with all requirements of this section or collateral labeling in compliance with Sec. 168.66.

(b) The labeling of each export pesticide device must meet all of the prominence and legibility requirements of Sec. 156.10(a)(2) of this chapter.

(c) and Sec. 156.10(a)(3) of this chapter.

(d) The labeling must bear the following information:

(1) The name and address of the producer, meeting the requirements of Sec. 156.10(c) of this chapter;

(2) The producing establishment number, meeting the requirements of Sec. 156.10(f) of this chapter;

(3) The statement ``Not Registered for Use in the United States of America,'' which may be amplified by additional statements describing the reason why the export pesticide device is not registered in the United States, such as ``because pesticide devices are not required to be registered in the United States.''

(e) An export pesticide device is not required to bear an ingredients statement. [78 FR 4077, Jan. 18, 2013, as amended at 79 FR 75754, Dec. 19, 2014]
