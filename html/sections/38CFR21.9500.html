CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 21  /  Sec. 21.9500 Introduction.

An educational assistance program is established for individuals who served on active duty after September 10, 2001. This educational assistance program is effective August 1, 2009. (Authority: Pub. L. 110-252, 122 Stat. 2357, 2378)
