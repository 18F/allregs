CFR /  Title 12  /  Part 10  /  Sec. 10.2 Filing requirements.

(a) A national bank shall use Form MSD-4 (Uniform Application for Municipal Securities Principal or Municipal Securities Representative Associated with a Bank Municipal Securities Dealer) for obtaining the information required by MSRB Rule G-7(b)(i)-(x) from a person identified in Sec. 10.1(b). A national bank receiving a completed MSD-4 form from a person identified in Sec. 10.1(b) must submit this form to the OCC before permitting the person to be associated with it as a municipal securities principal or a municipal securities representative.

(b) A national bank must submit Form MSD-5 (Uniform Termination Notice for Municipal Securities Principal or Municipal Securities Representative Associated with a Bank Municipal Securities Dealer) to the OCC within 30 days of terminating a person's association with the bank as a municipal securities principal or municipal securities representative.

(c) Forms MSD-4 and MSD-5, with instructions, may be obtained by contacting the OCC at 400 7th Street SW., Washington, DC 20219, Attention: Bank Dealer Activities. [63 FR 29094, May 28, 1998, as amended at 63 FR 71343, Dec. 24, 1998; 79 FR 15641, Mar. 21, 2014]
