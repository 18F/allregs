CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 868  /  Sec. 868.309 Information.

Requests for the Rice Inspection Handbook, Equipment Handbook, or for information concerning approved devices and procedures, criteria for approved devices, and requests for approval of devices should be directed to the U.S. Department of Agriculture, Federal Grain Inspection Service, P.O. Box 96454, Washington, DC 20090-6454, or any field office or cooperator. [54 FR 21407, May 18, 1989. Redesignated at 60 FR 16364, Mar. 30, 1995]
