CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 21  /  Sec. 21.9735 Other required reports.

VA will apply the provisions of Sec. 21.7156 to individuals and institutions of higher learning under 38 U.S.C. chapter 33 as those provisions are applied to veterans and educational institutions under 38 U.S.C. chapter 30. (Authority: 38 U.S.C. 3034(a), 3323(a))
