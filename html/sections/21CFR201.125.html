CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 201  /  Sec. 201.125 Drugs for use in teaching, law enforcement, research,

A drug subject to Sec. 201.100 or Sec. 201.105, shall be exempt from section 502(f)(1) of the act if shipped or sold to, or in the possession of, persons regularly and lawfully engaged in instruction in pharmacy, chemistry, or medicine not involving clinical use, or engaged in law enforcement, or in research not involving clinical use, or in chemical analysis, or physical testing, and is to be used only for such instruction, law enforcement, research, analysis, or testing. [41 FR 6911, Feb. 13, 1976]
