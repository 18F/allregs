CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 12  /  Sec. 12.83 Advisory opinions.

Before or during a hearing, a person may, under Sec. 10.85, request the Commissioner for an advisory opinion on whether any regulation or order under consideration in the proceeding applies to a specific situation.
