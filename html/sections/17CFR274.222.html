CFR /  Title 17  /  Part 274  /  Sec. 274.222 Form N-CR, Current report of money market fund material

This form shall be used by registered investment companies that are regulated as money market funds under Sec. 270.2a-7 of this chapter to file current reports pursuant to Sec. 270.30b1-8 of this chapter within the time periods specified in the form. [79 FR 47973, Aug. 14, 2014]

Editorial Note: For Federal Register citations affecting Form N-CR, see the List of CFR Sections Affected, which appears in the Finding Aids section of the printed volume and at www.fdsys.gov.
