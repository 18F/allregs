CFR /  Title 2  /  Part 25  /  Sec. 25.315 Unique entity identifier.

Unique entity identifier has the meaning given in paragraph C.2 of the award term in appendix A to this part. [75 FR 55673, Sept. 14, 2010, as amended at 79 FR 75879, Dec. 19, 2014]
