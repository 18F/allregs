CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 405  /  Sec. 405.357 Notice of right to waiver consideration.

Whenever an initial determination is made that more than the correct amount of payment has been made, notice of the provisions of section 1870(c) of the Act regarding waiver of adjustment or recovery shall be sent to the overpaid individual and to any other individual against whom adjustment or recovery of the overpayment is to be effected (see Sec. 405.358). [61 FR 49271, Sept. 19, 1996]
