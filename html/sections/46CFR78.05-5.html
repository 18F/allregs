CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 78  /  Sec. 78.05-5 Charts and nautical publications.

As appropriate for the intended voyage, all vessels except barges, ferryboats and vessels operating exclusively on rivers, must carry adequate and up-to-date--

(a) Charts;

(b) Sailing directions;

(c) Coast pilots;

(d) Light lists;

(e) Notices to mariners;

(f) Tide tables;

(g) Current tables; and

(h) All other nautical publications necessary. \1\ ---------------------------------------------------------------------------

\1\ For United States vessels in or on the navigable waters of the United States, see 33 CFR 164.33. [CGD 75-074, 42 FR 5963, Jan. 31, 1977]
