CFR /  Title 18  /  Part 385  /  Sec. 385.702 Definitions (Rule 702).

For purposes of this subpart:

(a) Initial decision means any decision rendered by a presiding officer in accordance with Rule 708;

(b) Final decision means any decision referred to in Rule 713. [Order 225, 47 FR 19022, May 3, 1982, as amended by Order 375, 49 FR 21315, May 21, 1984; Order 575, 60 FR 4860, Jan. 25, 1995]
