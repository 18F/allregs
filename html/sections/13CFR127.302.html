CFR /  Title 13  /  Part 127  /  Sec. 127.302 What third-party certifications may a concern use as

In order for a concern to use a certification by another entity as evidence of its status as a qualified EDWOSB or WOSB in support of its representations in SAM (or any successor system) pursuant to Sec. 127.300(b), the concern must have a current, valid certification from:

(a) BD Program participant; or

(b) An entity designated as an SBA-approved certifier on SBA's Web site located at http://www.sba.gov/GC. [75 FR 62282, Oct. 7, 2010, as amended at 78 FR 61147, Oct. 2, 2013]
