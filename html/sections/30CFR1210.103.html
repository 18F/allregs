CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 1210  /  Sec. 1210.103 When are my production reports due?

(a) The ONRR must receive your completed Forms ONRR-4054 and ONRR-4058 by the 15th day of the second month following the month for which you are reporting.

(b) A report is considered received when it is delivered to ONRR by 4 p.m. mountain time at the addresses specified in Sec. 1210.105. Reports received after 4 p.m. mountain time are considered received the following business day.
