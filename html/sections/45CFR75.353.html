CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 75  /  Sec. 75.353 Fixed amount subawards.

With prior written approval from the HHS awarding agency, a pass-through entity may provide subawards based on fixed amounts up to the Simplified Acquisition Threshold, provided that the subawards meet the requirements for fixed amount awards in Sec. 75.201. Sec. Sec. 75.354-75.360 [Reserved]
