CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 1242  /  Sec. 1242.14 Administration--other (account XX-19-06).

Separate common administration--other expenses between freight and passenger services in the same proportion as the common expenses of the following accounts are separated between freight and passenger services: Administration:

Track (XX-19-02)

Bridges and Buildings (XX-19-03)

Signals (XX-19-04)

Communications (XX-19-05)
