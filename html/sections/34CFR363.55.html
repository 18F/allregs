CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 363  /  Sec. 363.55 What are the requirements for successfully rehabilitating

An individual with the most severe disabilities who is receiving supported employment services is considered to be successfully rehabilitated if the individual maintains a supported employment placement for 60 days after making the transition to extended services. (Authority: 29 U.S.C. 711(c))
