CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 110  /  Sec. 110.5 Licensing requirements.

Except as provided under subpart B of this part, no person may export any nuclear equipment or material listed in Sec. 110.8 and Sec. 110.9, or import any nuclear equipment or material listed in Sec. 110.9a, unless authorized by a general or specific license issued under this part. [56 FR 24684, May 31, 1991, as amended at 58 FR 13002, Mar. 9, 1993]
