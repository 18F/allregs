CFR /  Title 19  /  Part 10  /  Sec. 10.878 Packaging and packing materials and containers for retail

Packaging materials and containers in which a good is packaged for retail sale and packing materials and containers for shipment are to be disregarded in determining whether a good qualifies as an originating good under Sec. 10.873 of this subpart and General Note 31, HTSUS, except that the value of such packaging and packing materials and containers may be included in meeting the value-content requirement specified in Sec. 10.873(b) of this subpart.
