CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 585  /  Sec. 585.309 When will BOEM issue a noncompetitive ROW grant or RUE grant?

After completing the review of your grant request, BOEM may offer you a noncompetitive grant.

(a) If you accept the terms and conditions of the grant, then we will issue the grant, and you must comply with all terms and conditions of your grant and all applicable provisions of this part.

(b) If you do not accept the terms and conditions, BOEM will not issue a grant. [76 FR 64623, Oct. 18, 2011, as amended at 79 FR 21623, Apr. 17, 2014]
