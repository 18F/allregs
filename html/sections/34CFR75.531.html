CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 75  /  Sec. 75.531 Limit on total cost of a project.

A grantee shall insure that the total cost to the Federal Government is not more than the amount stated in the notification of grant award. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1221e-3 and 3474)
