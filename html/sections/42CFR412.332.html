CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 412  /  Sec. 412.332 Payment based on the hospital-specific rate.

The payment amount for each discharge (as defined in Sec. 412.4(a)) based on the hospital-specific rate determined under Sec. 412.328 (e) or (f) is determined by multiplying the applicable hospital-specific rate by the DRG weighting factor applicable to the discharge under Sec. 412.60 and the applicable hospital-specific rate percentage for the pertinent cost reporting period under Sec. 412.340.
