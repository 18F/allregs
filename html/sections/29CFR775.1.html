CFR /  Title 29  /  Part 775  /  Sec. 775.1 Advisory interpretations announced by the Administrator.

Advisory interpretations announced by the Administrator serve only to indicate the construction of the law which will guide the Administrator in the performance of his administrative duties unless he is directed otherwise by the authoritative ruling of the courts, or unless he shall subsequently decide that his prior interpretation is incorrect. [11 FR 14099, Dec. 5, 1946]
