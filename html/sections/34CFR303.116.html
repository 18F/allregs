CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 303  /  Sec. 303.116 Public awareness program.

Each system must include a public awareness program that--

(a) Focuses on the early identification of infants and toddlers with disabilities; and

(b) Provides information to parents of infants and toddlers through primary referral sources in accordance with Sec. 303.301. (Approved by Office of Management and Budget under control number 1820-0550) (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1435(a)(6))
