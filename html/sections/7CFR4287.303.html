CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 4287  /  Sec. 4287.303 Exception authority.

The exception authority provisions of this paragraph apply to this subpart instead of those in Sec. 4279.15 of subpart A of part 4279 of this chapter. The Administrator may, with the concurrence of the Secretary of Agriculture, make an exception, on a case-by-case basis, to any requirement or provision of this subpart that is not inconsistent with any authorizing statute or applicable law, if the Administrator determines that application of the requirement or provision would adversely affect the Federal government's interest.
