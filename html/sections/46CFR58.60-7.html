CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 58  /  Sec. 58.60-7 Industrial systems: Piping.

The piping for industrial systems under this subpart must meet ANSI B31.3 (incorporated by reference, see 46 CFR 58.03-1), except that blow out preventor control systems must also meet API RP 53 (incorporated by reference, see 46 CFR 58.03-1). [USCG-2003-16630, 73 FR 65188, Oct. 31, 2008]
