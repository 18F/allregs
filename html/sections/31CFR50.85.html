CFR /  Title 31  /  Part 50  /  Sec. 50.85 Amendment related to settlement approval.

Section 107(a)(6) of the Act, added December 22, 2005, provides that procedures and requirements established by the Secretary under Sec. 50.82 (as in effect on the date of issuance of that section in final form) shall apply to any cause of action described in section 107(a)(1) of the Act. [71 FR 27572, May 11, 2006]
