CFR /  Title 27  /  Part 40  /  Sec. 40.163 Semimonthly tax return periods.

Except as otherwise provided in Sec. 40.164, the periods to be covered by semimonthly tax returns are from the 1st day of each month through the 15th day of that month and from the 16th day of each month through the last day of that month. [T.D. TTB-89, 76 FR 3513, Jan. 20, 2011]
