CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 670  /  Sec. 670.8 Foreign permit exception.

Paragraphs (d) and (e) of Sec. 670.4 shall not apply to transporting, carrying, receiving, or possessing native mammals, native plants, or native birds or to the introduction of non-indigenous animals and plants when conducted by an agency of the United States Government on behalf of a foreign national operating under a permit issued by a foreign government to give effect to the Protocol.
