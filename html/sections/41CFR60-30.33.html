CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 60-30  /  Sec. 60-30.33 Discovery.

(a) Any party may serve requests for admissions in accordance with Sec. 60-30.9 (b) and (c).

(b) Witness lists and hearing exhibits will be exchanged at least 10 days in advance of the hearing.

(c) For good cause shown, and upon motion made in accordance with Sec. 60-30.8, the Administrative Law Judge may allow the taking of depositions. Other discovery will not be permitted.
