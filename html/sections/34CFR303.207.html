CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 303  /  Sec. 303.207 Availability of resources.

Each application must include a description of the procedure used by the State to ensure that resources are made available under this part for all geographic areas within the State. (Approved by Office of Management and Budget under control number 1820-0550) (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1437(a)(7))
