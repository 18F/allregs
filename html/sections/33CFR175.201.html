CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 175  /  Sec. 175.201 Ventilation.

No person may operate a boat built after July 31, 1980, that has a gasoline engine for electrical generation, mechanical power, or propulsion unless it is equipped with an operable ventilation system that meets the requirements of 33 CFR 183.610 (a), (b), (d), (e), and (f) and 183.620(a). [CGD 76-082, 44 FR 73027, Dec. 17, 1979]
