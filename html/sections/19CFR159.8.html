CFR /  Title 19  /  Part 159  /  Sec. 159.8 Allowance for loss, injury, etc.

Allowance in duties for any merchandise which is lost, stolen, destroyed, injured, abandoned, or short-shipped will be made in accordance with the provisions of part 158 of this chapter. [T.D. 73-175, 38 FR 17482, July 2, 1973, as amended by CBP Dec. 11-02, 76 FR 2576, Jan. 14, 2011]
