CFR /  Title 32  /  Part 842  /  Sec. 842.123 Recovery rates in government facilities.

The Federal Register contains the rates set by the Office of Management and Budget, of which judges take judicial notice. HQ USAF/JACC can provide certified copies of the Federal Register upon request. Apply the rates in effect at the time of care to claims.
