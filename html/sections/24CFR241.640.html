CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 241  /  Sec. 241.640 Employment discrimination prohibited.

Any contract or subcontract executed for the performance of constructing the improvements to the project shall provide that there shall be no discrimination against any employee or applicant for employment because of race, color, religion, sex, familial status, disability, age, or national origin. [61 FR 14417, Apr. 1, 1996]
