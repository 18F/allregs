CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 364  /  Sec. 364.25 What are the requirements for a statewide network of

(a) The State plan must include a design for the establishment of a statewide network of centers that comply with the standards and assurances in section 725 (b) and (c) of the Act and subparts F and G of 34 CFR part 366.

(b) The design required by paragraph (a) of this section must identify unserved and underserved areas and must provide an order of priority for serving these areas. (Approved by the Office of Management and Budget under control number 1820-0527) (Authority: 29 U.S.C. 711(c) and 796c(g))
