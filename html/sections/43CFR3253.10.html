CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 3253  /  Sec. 3253.10 Must I share with BLM the data I collect through

(a) For exploration operations on your geothermal lease, you must submit all data you obtain as a result of the operations with a signed notice of completion of exploration operations under Sec. 3253.11, unless we approve a later submission.

(b) For exploration operations on unleased lands or on leased lands where you are not the lessee or unit operator, you are not required to submit data. However, if you want your exploration operations to count toward your diligent exploration expenditure requirement (see Sec. 3210.13), or if you are making significant expenditures to extend your lease (see Sec. 3208.14), you must send BLM the resulting data under the rules of those sections.
