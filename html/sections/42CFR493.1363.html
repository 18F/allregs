CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 493  /  Sec. 493.1363 Standard: PPM testing personnel qualifications.

Each individual performing PPM procedures must--

(a) Possess a current license issued by the State in which the laboratory is located if the licensing is required; and

(b) Meet one of the following requirements:

(1) Be a physician, as defined in Sec. 493.2.

(2) Be a midlevel practitioner, as defined in Sec. 493.2, under the supervision of a physician or in independent practice if authorized by the State in which the laboratory is located.

(3) Be a dentist as defined in Sec. 493.2 of this part.
