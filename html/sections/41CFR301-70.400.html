CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 301-70  /  Sec. 301-70.400 How should we authorize and administer the payment of need?

You should authorize and administer the payment to reasonably accommodate employee(s) with disabilities in accordance with the Rehabilitation Act of 1973, as amended (29 U.S.C. 701-796l) and 5 U.S.C. 3102 and part 301-13 of this chapter. An employee with a special need should be treated the same as an employee with a disability. You must determine that additional travel expenses are necessary to accommodate the employee's needs. [FTR Amdt. 2006-03, 71 FR 24597, Apr. 26, 2006]
