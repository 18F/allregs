CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 1151  /  Sec. 1151.12 Qualified handicapped person.

Qualified handicapped person means:

(a) With respect to employment, a handicapped person who, with reasonable accommodation, can perform the essential functions of the job in question; and

(b) With respect to services, a handicapped person who meets the essential eligibility requirements for the receipt of such services. Sec. Sec. 1151.13-1151.15 [Reserved]
