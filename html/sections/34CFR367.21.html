CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 367  /  Sec. 367.21 How does the Secretary evaluate an application for a

(a) The Secretary evaluates an application for a discretionary grant on the basis of the criteria in Sec. 367.22.

(b) The Secretary awards up to 100 points for these criteria.

(c) The maximum possible score for each criterion is indicated in parentheses. (Authority: 29 U.S.C. 711(c) and 796k(b)(1) and (i)(1))
