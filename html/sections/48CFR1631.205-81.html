CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 1631  /  1631.205-81 Inferred reasonableness.

If the carrier follows the notification and consent requirements of paragraphs (a), (b) and (c) of 1652.244-70, and subsequently obtains the Contracting officer's consent or ratification, then the reasonableness of the subcontract's costs shall be inferred. [70 FR 31382, June 1, 2005]
