CFR /  Title 16  /  Part 301  /  Sec. 301.32 Fur product containing material other than fur.

(a) Where a fur product contains a material other than fur the content of which is required to be disclosed on labels under other statutes administered by the Commission, such information may be set out on the same side of the label and in immediate conjunction with the information required under this Act; as for example: 100% Wool Interlining--100% Recycled Wool Trim--Dyed Muskrat Fur Origin: Canada

or Body: 100% Cotton Lining: 100% Nylon Collar: Dyed Mouton Lamb Fur Origin: Argentina

(b) Information which may be desirable or necessary to fully inform the purchaser of other material content of a fur product may be set out on the same side of the label as used for disclosing the information required under the Act and rules and regulations; as for example: Body--Leather Trim--Dyed Mink [26 FR 3187, Apr. 14, 1961, as amended at 45 FR 44263, July 1, 1980]
