CFR /  Title 12  /  Part 347  /  Sec. 347.213 Establishment or operation of noninsured foreign branch.

(a) A foreign bank may establish or operate a state branch, as provided by state law, without federal deposit insurance whenever:

(1) The branch only accepts initial deposits in an amount equal to the SMDIA or greater; or

(2) The branch meets the criteria set forth in Sec. 347.214 orSec. 347.215.

(b) [Reserved] [70 FR 17560, Apr. 6, 2005, as amended at 74 FR 47718, Sept. 17, 2009]
