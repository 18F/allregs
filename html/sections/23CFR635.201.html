CFR /  Title 23  /  Part 635  /  Sec. 635.201 Purpose.

The purpose of this subpart is to prescribe procedures in accordance with 23 U.S.C. 112(b) for a State transportation department to request approval that highway construction work be performed by some method other than contract awarded by competitive bidding. [48 FR 22912, May 23, 1983]
