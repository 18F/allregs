CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 102  /  Sec. 102.47 Bonito.

``Bonito'' or ``bonito fish'' is the common or usual name of the following food fishes: Cybiosarda elegans (Whitely, 1935)--Leaping bonitoGymnosarda unicolor (Ruppell, 1838)--Dogtooth tunaOrcynopsis unicolor (Geoffroy St. Hilaire, 1817)--Plain bonitoSarda australis (Macleay, 1880)--Australian bonitoSarda chiliensis (Cuvier, 1831)--Eastern Pacific bonitoSarda orientalis (Temminck and Schlegel, 1844)--Striped bonitoSarda sarda (Bloch, 1793)--Atlantic bonito [55 FR 45797, Oct. 31, 1990]
