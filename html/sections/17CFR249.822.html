CFR /  Title 17  /  Part 249  /  Sec. 249.822 Form 19b-7, for electronic filing with respect to Section 19(b)(7)(A) of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934.

This form shall be used by self-regulatory organizations, as defined in section 3(a)(25) of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 (15 U.S.C. 78c(a)(25)), to file electronically proposed rule changes with the Commission pursuant to section 19(b)(7) of the Act (15 U.S.C. 78s(b)(7)) and Sec. 240.19b-7 of this chapter.
