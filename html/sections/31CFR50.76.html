CFR /  Title 31  /  Part 50  /  Sec. 50.76 Insurer responsibility.

For purposes of the collection, reporting and remittance of Federal Terrorism Policy Surcharges to Treasury, an ``insurer,'' as defined in Sec. 50.5(l), shall not include any affiliate of the insurer.
