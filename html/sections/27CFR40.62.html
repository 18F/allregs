CFR /  Title 27  /  Part 40  /  Sec. 40.62 Application for permit.

Every person, before commencing business as a manufacturer of tobacco products as defined in Sec. 40.11, shall make application for, and obtain, the permit provided in Sec. 40.75, covering operations at each proposed factory. Such application shall be made on TTB F 5200.3, in duplicate, to the appropriate TTB officer. All documents required under this part to be furnished with such application shall be made a part thereof. Where the applicant for a permit under this section holds a permit or permits authorizing the production of any tobacco products at premises to be covered by the permit applied for, the applicant shall surrender such permit or permits for cancellation, upon the issuance of the permit applied for. (72 Stat. 1421; 26 U.S.C 5712)
