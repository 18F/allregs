CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 3210  /  Sec. 3210.15 What if I appeal BLM's decision to readjust my lease

If you appeal BLM's decision under Sec. 3210.14(e)(1) to readjust the rentals, royalties, or other terms and conditions of your lease, the decision is effective during the appeal. If you win your appeal and we must change our decision, you will receive a refund or credit for any overpaid rents or royalties.
