CFR /  Title 17  /  Part 31  /  Sec. 31.22 Prohibited trading in leverage contracts.

No futures commission merchant or introducing broker shall offer to enter into, enter into, confirm the execution of, or solicit or accept orders for any leverage contract. [54 FR 41082, Oct. 5, 1989]
