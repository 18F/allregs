CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 57  /  Sec. 57.8525 Main fan maintenance.

Main fans shall be maintained according to either the manufacturer's recommendations or a written periodic schedule adopted by the operator which shall be available at the operation on request of the Secretary or his authorized representative. [50 FR 4082, Jan. 29, 1985, as amended at 60 FR 33723, June 29, 1995]
