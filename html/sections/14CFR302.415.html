CFR /  Title 14  /  Part 302  /  Sec. 302.415 Hearing.

After the issues have been formulated, whether by the pleadings or otherwise, the administrative law judge shall give the parties reasonable written notice of the time and place of the hearings. Except as may be modified by the provisions of this subpart, the procedures in Sec. Sec. 302.17 to 302.38 governing the conduct of oral evidentiary hearings will apply.
