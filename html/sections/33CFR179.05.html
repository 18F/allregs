CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 179  /  Sec. 179.05 Manufacturer discovered defects.

Each manufacturer who is required to furnish a notice of a defect or failure to comply with a standard or regulation under 46 U.S.C. 4310(b), shall furnish that notice within 30 days after the manufacturer discovers or acquires information of the defect or failure to comply. [CGD 93-055, 61 FR 13926, Mar. 28, 1996]
