CFR /  Title 44  /  Part 7  /  Sec. 7.3 Application of this regulation.

No person in the United States shall, on the ground of race, color, or national origin, be excluded from participation in, be denied the benefits of, or be otherwise subjected to discrimination under any program to which this regulation applies. [68 FR 51379, Aug. 26, 2003]
