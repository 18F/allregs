CFR /  Title 31  /  Part 359  /  Sec. 359.48 How are redemption payments made for my redeemed book-entry

We will make payments electronically by direct deposit, using the ACH method, to your designated account at a United States depository financial institution. You may also direct that a payment be used to purchase a certificate of indebtedness to be held in your New Treasury Direct account. [69 FR 50308, Aug. 16, 2004]
