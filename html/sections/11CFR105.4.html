CFR /  Title 11  /  Part 105  /  Sec. 105.4 Place of filing; political committees and other persons

All designations, statements, reports, and notices, as well as any modifications or amendments thereto, required to be filed under 11 CFR parts 101, 102, and 104 by a political committee other than any principal campaign committee or any committee referred to in 11 CFR 105.2 or 105.3, by persons other than political committees making independent expenditures under 11 CFR part 109, and by persons required to report the cost of communications under 11 CFR 104.6, shall be filed in original form with the Federal Election Commission. [45 FR 15116, Mar. 7, 1980, as amended at 61 FR 3550, Feb. 1, 1996]
