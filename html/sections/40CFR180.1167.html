CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 180  /  Sec. 180.1167 Allyl isothiocyanate as a component of food grade oil

The insecticide and repellent Allyl isothiocyanate is exempt from the requirement of a tolerance for residues when used as a component of food grade oil of mustard, in or on all raw agricultural commodities, when applied according to approved labeling. [61 FR 24894, May 17, 1996]
