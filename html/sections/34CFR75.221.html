CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 75  /  Sec. 75.221 Procedures the Department uses under Sec. 75.219(b).

If the special circumstances of Sec. 75.219(b) appear to exist for an application, the Secretary may select the application for funding if:

(a) The Secretary has documentary evidence that the special circumstances of Sec. 75.219(b) exist; and

(b) The Secretary has a statement that explains the circumstances of the mishandling. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1221e-3(a)(1) and 3474) [45 FR 22497, Apr. 3, 1980. Redesignated at 45 FR 77368, Nov. 21, 1980, as amended at 52 FR 27804, July 24, 1987. Redesignated at 60 FR 12096, Mar. 3, 1995]
