CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 512  /  Sec. 512.9 What are the requirements if the information comes from

Where confidentiality is claimed for information obtained by the submitter from a third party, such as a supplier, the submitter is responsible for obtaining from the third party the information that is necessary to comply with Sec. 512.4 of this part, including a certificate in the form set out in appendix A to this Part.
