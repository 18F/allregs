CFR /  Title 18  /  Part 1304  /  Sec. 1304.3 Delegation of authority.

The power to approve or disapprove applications under this part is delegated to the Vice President, Natural Resources, or the designee thereof, subject to appeal to the Chief Executive Officer and discretionary review by a designated committee of the TVA Board, as provided in Sec. 1304.6. The administration of applications is delegated to the Natural Resources staff or the group with functionally equivalent responsibilities. [79 FR 4621, Jan. 29, 2014]
