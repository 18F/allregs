CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 76  /  Sec. 76.610 Operation in the frequency bands 108-137 and 225-400

The provisions of Sec. Sec. 76.605(a)(12), 76.611, 76.612, 76.613, 76.614, 76.616, 76.617, 76.1803 and 76.1804 are applicable to all MVPDs (cable and non-cable) transmitting carriers or other signal components carried at an average power level equal to or greater than 10-4 watts across a 25 kHz bandwidth in any 160 microsecond period, at any point in the cable distribution system in the frequency bands 108-137 and 225-400 MHz for any purpose. Exception: Non-cable MVPDs serving less than 1000 subscribers and less than 1000 units do not have to comply with Sec. 76.1803. [69 FR 57862, Sept. 28, 2004]
