CFR /  Title 9  /  Part 391  /  Sec. 391.5 Laboratory accreditation fees.

(a) The annual fee for the accreditation and maintenance of accreditation provided pursuant to Sec. 439.5 of this chapter shall be $5,000 for the first analyte class, $2,900 for the second analyte class, and $2,100 for each additional analyte class.

(b) Laboratories that request special onsite inspections shall pay FSIS the actual cost of reasonable travel and other expenses necessary to perform the unscheduled or non-routine onsite inspections.
