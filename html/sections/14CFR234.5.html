CFR /  Title 14  /  Part 234  /  Sec. 234.5 Form of reports.

Except where otherwise noted, all reports required by this part shall be filed within 15 days of the end of the month for which data are reported. The reports must be submitted to the Office of Airline Information in a format specified in accounting and reporting directives issued by the Bureau of Transportation Statistics' Assistant Director for Airline Information. [Doc. No. OST-2000-8164, 67 FR 70545, Nov. 25, 2002]
