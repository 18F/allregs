CFR /  Title 17  /  Part 249  /  Sec. 249.25 Form 25, for notification of removal from listing

This form shall be used by registered national securities exchanges and issuers for notification of removal of a class of securities from listing on a national securities exchange and/or withdrawal of registration under section 12(b) of the Act (15 U.S.C. 78l(b)). [70 FR 42469, July 22, 2005]

Editorial Note: For Federal Register citations affecting Form 25, see the List of CFR Sections Affected, which appears in the Finding Aids section of the printed volume and at www.fdsys.gov.
