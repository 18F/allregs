CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 319  /  Sec. 319.24-3 Marking as condition of entry.

Every bag or other container of corn offered for entry shall be plainly marked with such numbers or marks as will make it easily possible to associate the bags or containers with a particular importation. (Approved by the Office of Management and Budget under control number 0579-0049) [24 FR 10788, Dec. 29, 1959, as amended at 48 FR 57466, Dec. 30, 1983] Sec. 319.24-4 [Reserved]
