CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 42  /  Sec. 42.05-65 Surveyor.

The term surveyor means any person designated by the American Bureau of Shipping or other classification society recognized by the Commandant as the person who actually examines the vessel and/or materials associated with such examination, and who ascertains such vessel complies with applicable load line requirements. [CGFR 68-60, 33 FR 10052, July 12, 1968]
