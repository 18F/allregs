CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 164  /  Sec. 164.120-3 Definitions.

In this subpart, the term:

Acceptance means certification by the Coast Guard that a component is suitable for use in the manufacture of Coast Guard-approved lifeboats and rescue boats.

Commandant means Commandant (CG-ENG-4), Attn: Lifesaving and Fire Safety Division, U.S. Coast Guard Stop 7509, 2703 Martin Luther King Jr. Avenue SE., Washington, DC 20593-7509. [USCG-2010-0048, 76 FR 63012, Oct. 11, 2011, as amended by USCG-2013-0671, 78 FR 60162, Sept. 30, 2013]
