CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 566  /  Sec. 566.3 Application.

This part applies to all manufacturers of motor vehicles, and to manufacturers of motor vehicle equipment, other than tires, to which a motor vehicle safety standard applies (hereafter referred to as ``covered equipment''). [36 FR 22063, Nov. 19, 1971]
