CFR /  Title 19  /  Part 12  /  Sec. 12.5 Shipment to other ports.

When imported merchandise, the subject of Sec. 12.1, is shipped to another port for reconditioning or exportation, such shipment shall be under a Customs carrier's manifest, Customs Form 7512, in the same manner as shipments in bond. [T.D. 68-191, 33 FR 11019, Aug. 2, 1968]
