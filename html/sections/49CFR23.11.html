CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 23  /  Sec. 23.11 What compliance and enforcement provisions are used under this part?

The compliance and enforcement provisions of part 26 (Sec. Sec. 26.101 and 26.105 through 26.109) apply to this part in the same way that they apply to FAA recipients and programs under part 26. [70 FR 14508, Mar. 22, 2005, as amended at 72 FR 15616, Apr. 2, 2007]
