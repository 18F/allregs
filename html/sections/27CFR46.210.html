CFR /  Title 27  /  Part 46  /  Sec. 46.210 Articles marked ``not for sale'' or ``complimentary''.

Articles marked ``not for sale'' or ``complimentary'' that are part of a sale (for example, buy two packs and get one pack free) are subject to the floor stocks tax and must be included in the physical or record (book) inventory as provided in Sec. Sec. 46.202 or Sec. 46.203.
