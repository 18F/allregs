CFR /  Title 14  /  Part 16  /  Sec. 16.227 Standard of proof.

The hearing officer shall issue an initial decision or rule in a party's favor only if the decision or ruling is in accordance with law and supported by a preponderance of the reliable, probative, and substantial evidence contained in the record. [Amdt. 16-1, as amended at 78 FR 56147, Sept. 12, 2013]
