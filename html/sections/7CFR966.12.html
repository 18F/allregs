CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 966  /  Sec. 966.12 Maturity.

Maturity means any of the various degrees of ripeness of tomatoes as established by the committee with approval of the Secretary as determined at the time of the inspection, pursuant to Sec. 966.60(a). [34 FR 19186, Dec. 4, 1969]
