CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 234  /  Sec. 234.501 Eligibility requirements.

The requirements set forth in 24 CFR part 200, subpart A, apply to blanket mortgages on condominium projects insured under section 234 of the National Housing Act (12 U.S.C. 1715y), as amended. [61 FR 14406, Apr. 1, 1996]
