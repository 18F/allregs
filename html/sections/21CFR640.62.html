CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 640  /  Sec. 640.62 Medical supervision.

A qualified licensed physician shall be on the premises when donor suitability is being determined, immunizations are being made, whole blood is being collected, and red blood cells are being returned to the donor. [66 FR 1837, Jan. 10, 2001]
