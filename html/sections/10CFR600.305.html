CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 600  /  Sec. 600.305 Debarment and suspension.

Recipients must comply with the nonprocurement debarment and suspension common rule implemented in 2 CFR 180 and 901. This common rule restricts subawards and contracts with certain parties that are debarred, suspended, or otherwise excluded from or ineligible for participation in Federal assistance programs or activities. [68 FR 50650, Aug. 21, 2003, as amended at 74 FR 44278, Aug. 28, 2009]
