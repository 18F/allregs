CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 490  /  Sec. 490.10 How does an eligible entity apply for a grant?

To receive a grant under this part, an eligible entity shall submit an application to the Secretary at such time, in such manner, and containing such information as the Secretary shall require, including, but not limited to, an assurance that the entity will report annually to the Secretary on the participation rate, cost, and effectiveness of the program and any other aspect of the program on which the Secretary may request information. (Approved by the Office of Management and Budget under control number 1830-0512) (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1211-2(e)(2))
