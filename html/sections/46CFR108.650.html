CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 108  /  Sec. 108.650 EPIRBs and SARTs.

Emergency position indicating radiobeacons and search and rescue transponders. Each EPIRB and SART should have the name of the unit plainly marked or painted on its label, except for EPIRBs or SARTs in an inflatable liferaft or permanently installed in a survival craft. [CGD 84-069, 61 FR 25299, May 20, 1996]
