CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 303  /  Sec. 303.200 State application and assurances.

Each application must contain--

(a) The specific State application requirements (including certifications, descriptions, methods, and policies and procedures) required in Secs. 303.201 through 303.212; and

(b) The assurances required in Secs. 303.221 through 303.227. (Approved by Office of Management and Budget under control number 1820-0550) (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1437)
