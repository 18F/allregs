CFR /  Title 27  /  Part 21  /  Sec. 21.161 Weights and specific gravities of specially denatured alcohol.

The weight of one gallon of each formula of specially denatured alcohol at 15.56 [deg]C. (60 [deg]F.) is as listed in this section. The specific gravity of each formula of specially denatured alcohol at 15.56 [deg]C./15.56 [deg]C. (60 [deg]F./60 [deg]F.) in air is as listed in this section. (Weight of 1 gallon of water at 15.56 [deg]C. (60 [deg]F.) is 8.32823 pounds in air.)
