CFR /  Title 36  /  Part 1235  /  Sec. 1235.18 How do agencies transfer records to the National Archives

Agencies transfer records by submitting a signed SF 258, Agreement to Transfer Records to the National Archives of the United States. Each SF 258 must correlate to a specific records series or other aggregation of records, as identified in an item on the SF 115 or cited on the SF 258.
