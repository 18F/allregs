CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 80  /  Sec. 80.810 Who shall register with EPA under the gasoline toxics

(a) Refiners and importers who are registered by EPA under Sec. 80.76 are deemed to be registered for purposes of this subpart.

(b) Refiners and importers subject to the standards in Sec. 80.815 who are not registered by EPA under Sec. 80.76 shall provide to EPA the information required by Sec. 80.76 by October 1, 2001, or not later than three months in advance of the first date that such person produces or imports gasoline, whichever is later.
