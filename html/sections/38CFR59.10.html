CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 59  /  Sec. 59.10 General requirements for a grant.

For a State to obtain a grant under this part and grant funds, its initial application for the grant must be approved under Sec. 59.20, and the project must be ranked sufficiently high on the priority list for the current fiscal year so that funding is available for the project. It must meet the additional application requirements in Sec. 59.60, and it must meet all other requirements under this part for obtaining a grant and grant funds. (Authority: 38 U.S.C. 101, 501, 1710, 1742, 8105, 8131-8137)
