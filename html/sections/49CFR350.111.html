CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 350  /  Sec. 350.111 What constitutes traffic enforcement for the purpose of

Traffic enforcement means enforcement activities of State or local officials, including the stopping of vehicles operating on highways, streets, or roads for moving violations of State or local motor vehicle or traffic laws (e.g., speeding, following too closely, reckless driving, improper lane changes). [72 FR 36769, July 5, 2007]
