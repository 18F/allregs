CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 51-3  /  Sec. 51-3.4 Distribution of orders.

Central nonprofit agencies shall distribute orders from the Government only to nonprofit agencies which the Committee has approved to furnish the specific commodity or service. When the Committee has approved two or more nonprofit agencies to furnish a specific commodity or service, the central nonprofit agency shall distribute orders among those nonprofit agencies in a fair and equitable manner. [56 FR 48979, Sept. 26, 1991; 56 FR 64002, Dec. 6, 1991]
