CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 301  /  Sec. 301.38-8 Costs and charges.

The services of an inspector \4\ during normal business hours, Monday through Friday, 8 a.m. to 4:30 p.m., will be furnished without cost to persons requiring the services. The United States Department of Agriculture will not be responsible for any other costs or charges. [54 FR 32791, Aug. 10, 1989; 54 FR 38494, Sept. 18, 1989]
