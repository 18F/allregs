CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 941  /  Sec. 941.702 Exemption for coal extraction incidental to the

Part 702 of this chapter, Exemption for Coal Extraction Incidental to the Extraction of Other Minerals, shall apply to any person who conducts coal extraction incidental to the extraction of other minerals for purposes of commercial use or sale. [54 FR 52123, Dec. 20, 1989]
