CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 154  /  Sec. 154.453 Failure to meet independent tank type C standards.

If the Commandant (CG-ENG) determines during plan review, that a tank designed as an independent tank type C fails to meet the standards under Sec. 154.450, Sec. 154.451, and 154.452 and cannot be redesigned to meet those standards, the tank may be redesigned as an independent tank type A or B. [CGD 74-289, 44 FR 26009, May 3, 1979, as amended by CGD 82-063b, 48 FR 4782, Feb. 3, 1983; USCG-2014-0688, 79 FR 58284, Sept. 29, 2014]
