CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 304  /  Sec. 304.10 General administrative requirements.

As a condition for Federal financial participation, the provisions of part 74 of this title (with the exception of 45 CFR 74.23, Cost Sharing or Matching and 45 CFR 74.52, Financial Reporting) establishing uniform administrative requirements and cost principles shall apply to all grants made to States under this part. [40 FR 27166, June 26, 1975, as amended at 61 FR 67241, Dec. 20, 1996]
