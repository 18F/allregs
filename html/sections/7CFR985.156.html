CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 985  /  Sec. 985.156 Transfer of excess oil by producers.

(a) Pursuant to Sec. 985.56(a), before December 1 of each marketing year, a producer, following notification of the Committee, may transfer excess oil to another producer to enable that producer to fill a deficiency in that producer's annual allotment.

(b) Pursuant to Sec. 985.56(b), before December 1 of each marketing year, excess oil not used to fill another producer's deficiency shall be delivered to the Committee or its designees for storage. [78 FR 9577, Feb. 11, 2013]
