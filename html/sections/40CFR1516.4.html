CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 1516  /  Sec. 1516.4 Times, places, and requirements for the identification

An individual making a request to the Administrative Officer of the Council pursuant to Sec. 1516.3 shall present the request at the Council's office, 722 Jackson Place NW., Washington, DC 20006, on any business day between the hours of 9 a.m. and 5 p.m. and should be prepared to identify himself by signature. Requests will also be accepted in writing if mailed to the Council's offices and signed by the requester.
