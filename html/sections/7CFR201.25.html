CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 201  /  Sec. 201.25 Contents of the label.

Vegetable seed in packets and in larger containers shall be labeled with the required information in any form that is clearly legible. Any tag used shall be securely attached to the container. The label may contain information in addition to that required by the act, provided such information is not misleading. [5 FR 31, Jan. 4, 1940]
