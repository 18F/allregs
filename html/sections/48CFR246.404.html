CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 246  /  246.404 Government contract quality assurance for acquisitions at

Do not require Government contract quality assurance at source for contracts or delivery orders valued at or below the simplified acquisition threshold unless the criteria at 246.402 have been met. [70 FR 8543, Feb. 22, 2005]
