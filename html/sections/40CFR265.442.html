CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 265  /  Sec. 265.442 Design and installation of new drip pads.

Owners and operators of new drip pads must ensure that the pads are designed, installed, and operated in accordance with one of the following:

(a) All of the applicable requirements of Secs. 265.443 (except Sec. 265.443(a)(4)), 265.444 and 265.445 of this subpart, or

(b) All of the applicable requirements of Secs. 265.443 (except Sec. 265.443(b)), 265.444 and 265.445 of this subpart. [57 FR 61504, Dec. 24, 1992]
