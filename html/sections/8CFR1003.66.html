CFR /  Title 8  /  Part 1003  /  Sec. 1003.66 Changes in information or status.

All providers with a pending application or currently on the List must notify the Director in writing within ten business days if:

(a) The provider's contact information has changed;

(b) Any specific limitations in providing pro bono legal services under Sec. 1003.63(b)(4), (c)(2), or (d)(2) have changed; or

(c) The provider is no longer eligible under Sec. 1003.62. [80 FR 59513, Oct. 1, 2015] Subpart F [Reserved]
