CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 1  /  Sec. 1.421 Further notice of rulemaking.

In any rulemaking proceeding where the Commission deems it warranted, a further notice of proposed rulemaking will be issued with opportunity for parties of record and other interested persons to submit comments in conformity with Secs. 1.415 and 1.419.
