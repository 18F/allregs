CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 1  /  Sec. 1.463 Criminal penalty for violations.

Under 38 U.S.C. 7332(g), any person who violates any provision of this statute or Sec. Sec. 1.460 through 1.499 of this part shall be fined not more than $5,000 in the case of a first offense, and not more than $20,000 for a subsequent offense. (Authority: 38 U.S.C. 7332(g))
