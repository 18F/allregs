CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 408  /  Sec. 408.27 Rounding the monthly premium.

Any monthly premium that is not a multiple of 10 cents is rounded to the nearest multiple of 10 cents, and any odd mulitple of 5 cents is rounded to the next higher multiple of 10 cents. [52 FR 48115, Dec. 18, 1987; 53 FR 4159, Feb. 12, 1988]
