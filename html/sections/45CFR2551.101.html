CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 2551  /  Sec. 2551.101 What rule governs the recruitment and enrollment of Senior Companions without stipends?

Over-income persons, age 55 or over, may be enrolled in SCP projects as non-stipended volunteers in communities where there is no RSVP project or where agreement is reached with the RSVP project that allows for the enrollment of non-stipended volunteers in the SCP project. [64 FR 14115, Mar. 24, 1999, as amended at 74 FR 46508, Sept. 10, 2009]
