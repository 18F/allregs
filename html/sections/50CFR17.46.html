CFR /  Title 50  /  Part 17  /  Sec. 17.46 Special rules--crustaceans.

(a) Madison Cave isopod (Antrolana lira). (1) All provisions of Sec. 17.31 (a) and (b) apply to this species except that it may be taken for scientific purposes without Federal permits issued pursuant to these regulations: Provided, that all other Federal, State, or local laws, regulations, ordinances or other restrictions or limitations have been complied with.
