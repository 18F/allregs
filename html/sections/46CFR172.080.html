CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 172  /  Sec. 172.080 Specific applicability.

This subpart applies to each tank barge that carries a cargo listed in Table 151.05 of this chapter. [CGD 79-023, 48 FR 51040, Nov. 4, 1983, as amended by USCG-2009-0702, 74 FR 49239, Sept. 25, 2009]
