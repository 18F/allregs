CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 890  /  Sec. 890.802 Definition.

In this subpart, a Qualifying court order means a court order acceptable for processing as defined in Sec. 838.103 of this chapter or qualifying court order as defined in Sec. 838.1003 of this chapter. [57 FR 33599, July 29, 1992]
