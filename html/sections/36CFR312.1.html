CFR /  Title 36  /  Part 312  /  Sec. 312.1 Areas covered.

The regulation covered in this part shall be applicable to all water resource project lands under the supervision of the Secretary of the Army not covered in parts 311 and 326, of this title. [29 FR 9710, July 18, 1964]
