CFR /  Title 32  /  Part 552  /  Sec. 552.125 Disposition of confiscated weapons.

Commanders will maintain confiscated weapons in the unit arms room pending final disposition. They will provide written notification of the circumstances or loss or recovery of such weapons and a complete and accurate description of the weapon to Commander, I Corps and Fort Lewis, ATTN: AFZH-PMS-P, Fort Lewis, WA 98433-5000. A copy of this notification will be maintained with the weapon pending final disposition. Subpart J_Control of Firearms, Ammunition and Other Dangerous Weapons on

Fort Gordon

Source: 56 FR 37130, Aug. 2, 1991, unless otherwise noted.
