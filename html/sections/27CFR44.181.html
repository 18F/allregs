CFR /  Title 27  /  Part 44  /  Sec. 44.181 Packages.

All tobacco products and cigarette papers and tubes must, before removal or transfer under this subpart, be put up by the manufacturer in packages that bear the label or notice, tax classification, and mark, as required by this subpart. For purposes of this subpart, the package does not include the cellophane or other transparent exterior wrapping material. [78 FR 38574, June 27, 2013]

Effective Date Note: At 78 FR 38574, June 27, 2013, Sec. 44.181 was revised, effective Aug. 26, 2013 through Aug. 26, 2016.
