CFR /  Title 29  /  Part 403  /  Sec. 403.3 Form of annual financial report--detailed report.

Every labor organization shall, except as expressly provided otherwise in this part, file an annual financial report as required by Sec. 403.2, prepared on United States Department of Labor Form LM-2, ``Labor Organization Annual Report,'' in the detail required by the instructions accompanying the form and constituting a part thereof.

Note: Form LM-2 was revised at 58 FR 67594, December 21, 1993. [28 FR 14383, Dec. 27, 1963, as amended at 62 FR 6092, Feb. 10, 1997]
