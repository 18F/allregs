CFR /  Title 29  /  Part 2700  /  Sec. 2700.51 Hearing dates and sites.

All cases will be assigned a hearing date and site by order of the Judge. In fixing the time and place of the hearing, the Judge shall give due regard to the convenience and necessity of the parties or their representatives and witnesses, the availability of suitable hearing facilities, and other relevant factors. [71 FR 44208, Aug. 4, 2006]
