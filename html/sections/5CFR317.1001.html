CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 317  /  Sec. 317.1001 OPM authority for corrective action.

If OPM finds that an agency has taken an action contrary to law or regulation under this part, it may require the agency to take appropriate corrective action. [54 FR 9761, Mar. 8, 1989]
