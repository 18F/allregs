CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 232  /  232.7102 Contract clause.

Use the clause at 252.232-7010, Levies on Contract Payments, in all solicitations and contracts, including solicitations and contracts using FAR part 12 procedures for the acquisition of commercial items. [71 FR 69492, Dec. 1, 2006, as amended at 78 FR 37989, June 25, 2013]
