CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 194  /  Sec. 194.51 Consideration of protected individual.

Compliance assessments that analyze compliance with Sec. 191.15 of this chapter shall assume that an individual resides at the single geographic point on the surface of the accessible environment where that individual would be expected to receive the highest dose from radionuclide releases from the disposal system.
