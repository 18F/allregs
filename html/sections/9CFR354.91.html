CFR /  Title 9  /  Part 354  /  Sec. 354.91 Information to be furnished to inspectors.

When inspection service is performed within an official plant, the applicant for such inspection shall furnish to the inspector rendering such service such information as may be required for the purposes of Sec. Sec. 354.90 to 354.92. (Approved by the Office of Management and Budget under control number 0583-0036) [41 FR 23702, June 11, 1976, as amended at 47 FR 746, Jan. 7, 1982]
