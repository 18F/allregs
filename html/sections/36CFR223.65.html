CFR /  Title 36  /  Part 223  /  Sec. 223.65 Appraisal of timber for land exchange; right-of-way, or

The value of timber in land exchange or the value of timber required to be cut for occupancy of a right-of-way or other authorized use of National Forest System land for which payment will be made is to be determined by the appraisal methods in Sec. 223.60 of this part.
