CFR /  Title 8  /  Part 1240  /  Sec. 1240.37 Appeals.

Except for temporary exclusions under section 235(c) of the Act, an appeal from a decision of an Immigration Judge under this part may be taken by either party pursuant to Sec. 1003.38 of this chapter.
