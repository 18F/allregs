CFR /  Title 17  /  Part 279  /  Sec. 279.9 Form PF, reporting by investment advisers to private funds.

This form shall be filed pursuant to Rule 204(b)-1 (Sec. 275.204(b)-1 of this chapter) by certain investment advisers registered or required to register under section 203 of the Act (15 U.S.C. 80b-3) that act as an investment adviser to one or more private funds. [76 FR 71175, Nov. 16, 2011]

Editorial Note: For Federal Register citations affecting Form PF, see the List of CFR Sections Affected, which appears in the Finding Aids section of the printed volume and at www.fdsys.gov.
