CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 17  /  Sec. 17.636 Cancellation, waiver, or suspension of obligation.

Cancellation, waiver, or suspension procedures under the VIOMPSP are the same as those procedures for the HPSP under Sec. 17.612. (Authority: 38 U.S.C. 7505(c))
