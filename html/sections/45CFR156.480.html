CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 156  /  Sec. 156.480 Oversight of the administration of the cost-sharing reductions and advance payments of the premium tax credit programs.

(a) Maintenance of records. An issuer that offers a QHP in the individual market through a State Exchange must adhere to, and ensure that any relevant delegated entities and downstream entities adhere to, the standards set forth in Sec. 156.705 concerning maintenance of documents and records, whether paper, electronic, or in other media, by issuers offering QHPs in a Federally-facilitated Exchange, in connection with cost-sharing reductions and advance payments of the premium tax credit.

(b), and 156.460(a) through (c) of this part.

(c) Audits. HHS or its designee may audit an issuer that offers a QHP in the individual market through an Exchange to assess compliance with the requirements of this subpart. [78 FR 65100, Oct. 30, 2013]
