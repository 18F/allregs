CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 524  /  Sec. 524.86 Amitraz.

(a) Specifications. Amitraz liquid contains 19.9 percent amitraz in an organic solvent.

(b) Sponsor. See No. 054771 in Sec. 510.600(c) of this chapter.

(c) Conditions of use--(1) Indications for use. For dogs for the treatment of generalized demodicosis (Demodex canis).

(1) Indications for use. For dogs for the treatment of generalized demodicosis (Demodex canis).

(2) Amount. One 10.6 milliliter bottle per 2 gallons of warm water (250 parts per million) for each treatment, for a total of 3 to 6 treatments, 14 days apart.

(3) Limitations. Federal law restricts this drug to use by or on the order of a licensed veterinarian. [47 FR 18589, Apr. 30, 1982, as amended at 79 FR 10967, Feb. 27, 2014]
