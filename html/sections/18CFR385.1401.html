CFR /  Title 18  /  Part 385  /  Sec. 385.1401 Applicability (Rule 1401).

(a) This subpart applies to oil pipeline proceedings.

(b) If any provision of this subpart is inconsistent with any provision of another subpart of this part, the provision of this subpart governs and the provision of the other subpart is inapplicable to the extent of the inconsistency. [Order 312, 48 FR 29479, June 27, 1983]
