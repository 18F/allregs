CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 380  /  Sec. 380.113 Employer responsibilities.

(a) No motor carrier shall:

(1) Allow, require, permit or authorize an individual to operate an LCV unless he/she meets the requirements in Sec. Sec. 380.203 or 380.205 and has been issued the LCV driver-training certificate described in Sec. 380.401. This provision does not apply to individuals who are eligible for the substitute for driver training provision in Sec. 380.111.

(2) Allow, require, permit, or authorize an individual to operate an LCV which the LCV driver-training certificate, CDL, and CDL endorsement(s) do not authorize the driver to operate. This provision applies to individuals employed by or under contract to the motor carrier.

(b) A motor carrier that employs or has under contract LCV drivers shall provide evidence of the certifications required by Sec. 380.401 or Sec. 380.111 of this part when requested by an authorized FMCSA, State, or local official in the course of a compliance review.
