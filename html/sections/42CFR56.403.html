CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 56  /  Sec. 56.403 Project elements.

A project for the operation of a migrant health entity supported under this subpart must:

(a) Meet all of the requirements of Sec. 56.303 of this part, Provided, That the project will not be required to meet the requirements of paragraphs (c), (h), (i), or (n) of such section if the Secretary finds that meeting any such requirement is not feasible or practical at the time of grant award.

(b) Provide those services enumerated in Sec. 56.102(g)(1) of subpart A of this part which are specified in the grant award.

(c) Meet the requirements of Sec. 56.303 of subpart C of this part by the end of the period of support under section 319(d)(1)(B) of the Act and this subpart, in accordance with the plan submitted under Sec. 56.402(b) of this subpart.
