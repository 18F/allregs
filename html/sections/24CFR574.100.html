CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 574  /  Sec. 574.100 Eligible applicants.

(a) Eligible States and qualifying cities, as defined in Sec. 574.3, qualify for formula allocations under HOPWA.

(b) HUD will notify eligible States and qualifying cities of their formula eligibility and allocation amounts and EMSA service areas annually. [57 FR 61740, Dec. 28, 1992, as amended at 59 FR 17199, Apr. 11, 1994; 60 FR 1917, Jan. 5, 1995]
