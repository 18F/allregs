CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 371  /  Sec. 371.20 What are the application procedures for this program?

In the development of an application, a governing body or consortium is required to consult with the designated State unit or the designated State units of the State or States in which vocational rehabilitation services are to be provided. (Authority: Sec. 130(b) of the Act; 29 U.S.C. 750(b)) [46 FR 5423, Jan. 19, 1981, as amended at 52 FR 30555, Aug. 14, 1987]
