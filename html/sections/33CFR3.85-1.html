CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 3  /  Sec. 3.85-1 Seventeenth district.

(a) The District Office is in Juneau, Alaska.

(b) The Seventeenth Coast Guard District shall comprise the State of Alaska; the ocean area bounded by a line from the Canadian Coast at latitude 54[deg]40[min] N. due west to longitude 140[deg] W.; thence southwesterly to position 40[deg] N., 150[deg] W.; thence due west to position 40[deg] N., 165[deg] E.; thence due north to latitude 43[deg] N.; thence northwesterly to 51[deg] N., 158[deg] E.; thence north and east along the coastline of the continent of Asia to East Cape; thence north to the Arctic Ocean. [CGFR 61-40, 26 FR 10353, Nov. 3, 1961, as amended by CGFR 70-150, 36 FR 912, Jan. 20, 1971]
