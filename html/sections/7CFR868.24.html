CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 868  /  Sec. 868.24 Conditional withholding of service.

(a) Conditional withholding. A cooperator or the Service shall conditionally withhold service when an applicant fails to meet any requirement prescribed in Sec. 868.21.

(b) Procedure for withholding. The cooperator or the Service shall notify the applicant of the reason for the proposal to conditionally withhold service. The applicant will then be afforded reasonable time to take corrective action or to demonstrate that there is no basis for withholding service. If corrective action has not been adequate, the applicant will be notified of the decision to withhold service; and any results of service shall not be released. [53 FR 3722, Feb. 9, 1988. Redesignated and amended at 60 FR 16364, Mar. 30, 1995]
