CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 95  /  Sec. 95.141 Interconnection prohibited.

No station in a GMRS system may be interconnected to the public switched telephone network except as and in accordance with the requirements and restrictions applied to a wireline control link (see Sec. 95.127). [53 FR 47717, Nov. 25, 1988]
