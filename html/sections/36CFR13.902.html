CFR /  Title 36  /  Part 13  /  Sec. 13.902 Subsistence resident zone.

The following communities and areas are included within the resident zone for Denali National Park addition: Cantwell (limited to the area within a 3-mile radius of the Cantwell post office as shown on a map available at the park visitor center), Minchumina, Nikolai, and Telida. [73 FR 67393, Nov. 14, 2008]
