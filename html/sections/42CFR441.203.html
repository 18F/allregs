CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 441  /  Sec. 441.203 Life of the mother would be endangered.

FFP is available in expenditures for an abortion when a physician has found, and certified in writing to the Medicaid agency, that on the basis of his professional judgment, the life of the mother would be endangered if the fetus were carried to term. The certification must contain the name and address of the patient. Sec. Sec. 441.204-441.205 [Reserved]
