CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 585  /  Sec. 585.16 Records.

Each manufacturer shall maintain records of the Vehicle Identification Number of each vehicle for which information is reported under Sec. 585.15(c) until December 31, 2011. Each manufacturer shall maintain records of the Vehicle Identification Number of each vehicle for which information is reported under Sec. 585.15(d)(2) until December 31, 2013.
