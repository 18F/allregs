CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 61  /  Sec. 61.40 General requirement.

Land disposal facilities must be sited, designed, operated, closed, and controlled after closure so that reasonable assurance exists that exposures to humans are within the limits established in the performance objectives in Sec. Sec. 61.41 through 61.44.
