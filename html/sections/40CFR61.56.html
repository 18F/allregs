CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 61  /  Sec. 61.56 Delegation of authority.

(a) In delegating implementation and enforcement authority to a State under section 112(d) of the Act, the authorities contained in paragraph (b) of this section shall be retained by the Administrator and not transferred to a State.

(b) Authorities which will not be delegated to States: Sections 61.53(c)(4) and 61.55(d). The authorities not delegated to States listed are in addition to the authorities in the General Provisions, subpart A of 40 CFR part 61, that will not be delegated to States (Secs. 61.04(b), 61.12(d)(1), and 61.13(h)(1)(ii)). [52 FR 8728, Mar. 19, 1987]
