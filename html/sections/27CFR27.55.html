CFR /  Title 27  /  Part 27  /  Sec. 27.55 Federal Alcohol Administration Act permit.

Under the Federal Alcohol Administration Act and the regulations issued pursuant thereto (27 CFR part 1), any person except an agency of a State or political subdivision thereof, or any officer or employee of any such agency, intending to engage in the business of importing distilled spirits, wines or beer for nonindustrial use is required to procure a permit therefor. (Sec. 3, 49 Stat. 978, as amended; 27 U.S.C. 203) [20 FR 3561, May 21, 1955. Redesignated at 40 FR 16835, Apr. 15, 1975, and amended by T.D. ATF-474, 67 FR 11232, Mar. 13, 2002]
