CFR /  Title 17  /  Part 239  /  Sec. 239.90 Form 1-A, offering statement under Regulation A.

This form shall be used for filing under Regulation A (Sec. Sec. 230.251-230.263 of this chapter). [57 FR 36476, Aug. 13, 1992]

Editorial Note: For Federal Register citations affecting Form 1-A, see the List of CFR Sections Affected, which appears in the Finding Aids section of the printed volume and at www.fdsys.gov.
