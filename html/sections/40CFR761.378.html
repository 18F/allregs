CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 761  /  Sec. 761.378 Decontamination, reuse, and disposal of solvents,

(a) Decontamination. Decontaminate solvents and non-porous surfaces on equipment in accordance with the standards and procedures in Sec. 761.79(b) and (c).

(b) Reuse. A solvent may be reused so long as its PCB concentration is <50 ppm. Decontaminated equipment may be reused in accordance with Sec. 761.30(u). Store solvents and equipment for reuse in accordance with Sec. 761.35.

(c) Disposal. Dispose of all solvents, cleaners, and absorbent materials in accordance with Sec. 761.79(g). Dispose of equipment in accordance with Sec. 761.61(a)(5)(v)(A), or decontaminate in accordance with Sec. 761.79(b) or (c). Store for disposal equipment, solvents, cleaners, and absorbent materials in accordance with Sec. 761.65.
