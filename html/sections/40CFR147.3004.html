CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 147  /  Sec. 147.3004 Duration of rule authorization for existing

Notwithstanding Sec. 144.21(a)(3)(i)(B) of this chapter, authorization by rule for existing Class I and III wells will expire 90 days after the effective date of this UIC program unless a complete permit application has been submitted to the Director.
