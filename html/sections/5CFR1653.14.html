CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 1653  /  Sec. 1653.14 Calculating entitlements.

A qualifying legal process can only require the payment of a specified dollar amount from the TSP. Payment pursuant to a qualifying legal process will be calculated in accordance with Sec. 1653.4(a), (d), (f) and (g).
