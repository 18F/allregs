CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 51  /  Sec. 51.8 Annual reports.

By January 1 of each year, a report shall be submitted, pursuant to section 105(a)(7) of the Act (42 U.S.C. 10805(a)(7)), to the Secretary which is in the format designated by the Secretary.

Effective Date Note: At 62 FR 53564, Oct. 15, 1997, Sec. 51.8 was added. This section contains information collection and recordkeeping requirements and will not become effective until approval has been given by the Office of Management and Budget. Sec. 51.9 [Reserved]
