CFR /  Title 37  /  Part 353  /  Sec. 353.3 Procedure on rehearing.

Upon receipt of a motion for rehearing, the Copyright Royalty Judges will issue an appropriate order. No participant shall file a response to a rehearing motion, unless such response is allowed by order of the Copyright Royalty Judges. [70 FR 30905, May 31, 2005, as amended at 71 FR 53330, Sept. 11, 2006]
