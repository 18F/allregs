CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 633  /  633.102 General.

All communications relative to protests filed with the Government Accountability Office (GAO) shall be coordinated with the Office of the Legal Adviser. [53 FR 26173, July 11, 1988, as amended at 64 FR 43628, Aug. 11, 1999; 72 FR 45696, Aug. 15, 2007]
