CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 11  /  Sec. 11.43 Can interested parties review the results of the

After completing the preliminary application of the NRDAM/CME or NRDAM/GLE, if the authorized official decides to continue with the type A procedure, he or she must issue an Assessment Plan for public comment as described in Sec. 11.32. The Assessment Plan must include the information described in Sec. 11.31, the data inputs and modifications developed under Sec. 11.41, and a summary of the results of the preliminary application. The Assessment Plan must also identify a contact from whom a complete copy of the printout of the preliminary application can be obtained. [61 FR 20612, May 7, 1996]
