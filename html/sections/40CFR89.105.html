CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 89  /  Sec. 89.105 Certificate of conformity.

Every manufacturer of a new nonroad compression-ignition engine must obtain a certificate of conformity covering the engine family, as described in Sec. 89.116. The certificate of conformity must be obtained from the Administrator prior to selling, offering for sale, introducing into commerce, or importing into the United States the new nonroad compression-ignition engine for each model year. [59 FR 31335, June 17, 1994. Redesignated at 63 FR 56995, Oct. 23, 1998]
