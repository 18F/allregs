CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 3809  /  Sec. 3809.580 What happens if I modify my notice or approved plan

(a) If you modify a notice or an approved plan of operations under Sec. 3809.331 or Sec. 3809.431 respectively, and your estimated reclamation cost increases, you must increase the amount of the financial guarantee to cover any estimated additional cost of reclamation and long-term treatment in compliance with Sec. 3809.552.

(b) If you modify a notice or an approved plan of operations under Sec. 3809.331 or Sec. 3809.431 respectively, and your estimated reclamation cost decreases, you may request BLM decrease the amount of the financial guarantee for your operations.
