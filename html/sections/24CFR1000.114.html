CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 1000  /  Sec. 1000.114 How long does HUD have to review and act on a proposal activity?

Whether submitted in the IHP or at any other time, HUD will have 60 calendar days after receiving the proposal to notify the recipient in writing that the proposal to provide assistance to non-low-income families or for model activities is approved or disapproved. If no decision is made by HUD within 60 calendar days of receiving the proposal, the proposal is deemed to have been approved by HUD. [77 FR 71525, Dec. 3, 2012]
