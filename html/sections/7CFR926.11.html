CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 926  /  Sec. 926.11 Processed cranberries or cranberry products.

Processed cranberries or cranberry products means cranberries which have been converted from fresh cranberries into canned, frozen, or dehydrated cranberries or other cranberry products by any commercial process.

Effective Date Note: At 71 FR 78046, Dec. 28, 2006, Sec. 926.11 was suspended indefinitely, effective Dec. 29, 2006.
