CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 308  /  Sec. 308.406 Issuance of policies; terms and conditions.

Upon acceptance of an application, a policy in the form specified in Sec. 308.409 will be issued with endorsements MA-283(A) and MA-283(D), or MA-283(B) and MA-283(D), or MA-283(C), and MA-283(D), as appropriate.
