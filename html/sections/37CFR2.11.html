CFR /  Title 37  /  Part 2  /  Sec. 2.11 Applicants may be represented by an attorney.

Representation before the Office is governed by Sec. 11.14 of this chapter. The Office cannot aid in the selection of an attorney. [73 FR 47685, Aug. 14, 2008] Sec. Sec. 2.12-2.16 [Reserved]
