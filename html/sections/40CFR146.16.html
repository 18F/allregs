CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 146  /  Sec. 146.16 Requirements for new Class I municipal wells in

Prior to commencing injection, any Class I municipal disposal well in one of the counties identified in Sec. 146.15(f) that is not an existing Class I municipal disposal well as defined in Sec. 146.15(b) of this section shall meet all of the requirements for existing wells seeking authorization to inject pursuant to Sec. 146.15. [70 FR 70532, Nov. 22, 2005]
