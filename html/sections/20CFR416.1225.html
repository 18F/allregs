CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 416  /  Sec. 416.1225 An approved plan to achieve self-support; general.

If you are blind or disabled, we will pay you SSI benefits and will not count resources that you use or set aside to use for expenses that we determine to be reasonable and necessary to fulfill an approved plan to achieve self-support. [71 FR 28265, May 16, 2006]
