CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 95  /  Sec. 95.51 Retrieval of classified matter following suspension or

In any case where the access authorization of an individual is suspended or revoked in accordance with the procedures set forth in part 25 of this chapter, or other relevant CSA procedures, the licensee, certificate holder, or other person shall, upon due notice from the Commission of such suspension or revocation, retrieve all classified information possessed by the individual and take the action necessary to preclude that individual having further access to the information. [72 FR 49564, Aug. 28, 2007]
