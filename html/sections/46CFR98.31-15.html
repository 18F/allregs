CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 98  /  Sec. 98.31-15 Operating requirements.

No person may operate a vessel that carries a bulk liquid cargo of NLS unless the vessel--

(a) Has on board a Certificate of Inspection and, if it is a vessel making a foreign voyage, an NLS Certificate endorsed under Sec. 98.31-10 with the name of the NLS cargo;

(b) Discharges no NLS residues to the sea unless the vessel meets--

(1) The equipment requirements in Sec. 98.31-10(a)(2); and

(2) The operating requirements prescribed for oceangoing ships carrying NLSs in Sec. Sec. 153.901, 153.903, 153.909, and 153.1100 through 153.1132 of this chapter.
