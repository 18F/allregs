CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 1206  /  Sec. 1206.254 Quality and quantity measurement standards for

For all leases subject to this subpart, the quantity of coal on which royalty is due shall be measured in short tons (of 2,000 pounds each) by methods prescribed by the BLM. Coal quantity information will be reported on appropriate forms required under 30 CFR part 1210--Forms and Reports. [54 FR 1523, Jan. 13, 1989, as amended at 57 FR 52720, Nov. 5, 1992; 66 FR 45769, Aug. 30, 2001; 73 FR 15891, Mar. 26, 2008; 78 FR 30200, May 22, 2013]
