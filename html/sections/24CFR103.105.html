CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 103  /  Sec. 103.105 Cessation of action on referred complaints.

A referral under Sec. 103.100 does not prohibit the Assistant Secretary from taking appropriate action to review or investigate matters in the complaint that raise issues cognizable under other civil rights authorities applicable to departmental programs (see Sec. 103.5). [54 FR 3292, Jan. 23, 1989, as amended at 61 FR 14379, Apr. 1, 1996]
