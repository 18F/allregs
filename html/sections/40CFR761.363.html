CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 761  /  Sec. 761.363 Applicability.

The double wash/rinse procedure includes two washing steps and two rinsing steps. The two washing and rinsing steps are slightly different depending on whether a contaminated surface was relatively clean before the spill (see Sec. 761.372), or whether the surface was coated or covered with dust, dirt, grime, grease or another absorbent material (see Sec. 761.375).
