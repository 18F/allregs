CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 222  /  Sec. 222.158 What procedures apply to the Secretary's review of

When the Secretary reviews an initial decision, the Secretary--

(a) Notifies the applicant in writing that it may file a written statement or comments; and

(b) Mails to each party written notice of the Secretary's final decision. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 7711(a)) [60 FR 50778, Sept. 29, 1995, as amended at 62 FR 35419, July 1, 1997]
