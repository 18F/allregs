CFR /  Title 18  /  Part 154  /  Sec. 154.307 Joint facilities.

The Statements required by Sec. 154.312 must show all costs (investment, operation, maintenance, depreciation, taxes) that have been allocated to the natural gas operations involved in the subject rate change and are associated with joint facilities. The methods used in making such allocations must be provided.
