CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 712  /  Sec. 712.23 Final decision by DOE Under Secretary.

Within 20 working days of the receipt of the Chief Health, Safety and Security Officer's recommendation, the cognizant Under Secretary, in consultation with the DOE General Counsel should issue a final written decision. A copy of this decision must be sent by certified mail (return receipt requested) to the Manager and to the individual accompanied by a copy of the hearing officer's report and the transcript of the certification review proceedings. [69 FR 3223, Jan. 23, 2004, as amended at 71 FR 68731, Nov. 28, 2006; 76 FR 12273, Mar. 7, 2011]
