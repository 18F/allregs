CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 5501  /  Sec. 5501.112 One-year disqualification of employees of the National

An employee, other than a special Government employee, of the National Institutes of Health who has, within the last year, accepted an award permitted under 5 CFR 2635.204(d) or Sec. 5501.111 shall not participate in any particular matter involving specific parties in which the donor is or represents a party unless authorized to do so under 5 CFR 2635.502(d). [70 FR 5564, Feb. 3, 2005]
