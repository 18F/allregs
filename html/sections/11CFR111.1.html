CFR /  Title 11  /  Part 111  /  Sec. 111.1 Scope (52 U.S.C. 30109).

These regulations provide procedures for processing possible violations of the Federal Election Campaign Act of 1971, as amended (52 U.S.C. 30101, et seq.) and chapters 95 and 96 of the Internal Revenue Code of 1954 (26 U.S.C. 9001, et seq. and 9031 et seq.). [45 FR 15120, Mar. 7, 1980, as amended at 79 FR 77847, Dec. 29, 2014]
