CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 92  /  Sec. 92.901 Purpose and applicability.

The provisions of this subpart identify excluded locomotives (i.e., locomotives not covered by the Act) and allow for the exemption of locomotives and locomotive engines from certain provisions of this part. The applicability of the exclusions is described in Sec. 92.903, and the applicability of the exemption allowances is described in Secs. 92.904 through 92.909.
