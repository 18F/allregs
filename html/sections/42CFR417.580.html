CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 417  /  Sec. 417.580 Basis and scope.

(a), (e), and (g) of the Act that pertain to the amount CMS pays an organization for its Medicare enrollees who are enrolled on a risk basis.

(b) Scope. This subpart sets forth--

(1) Method of payment;

(2) Procedures for determining the HMO's or CMP's payment rate; and

(3) Procedures for determining the additional benefits (and their value) the HMO or CMP must provide to its Medicare enrollees. [50 FR 1346, Jan. 10, 1985; 50 FR 20570, May 17, 1985, as amended at 58 FR 38080, July 15, 1993; 60 FR 46231, Sept. 6, 1995]
