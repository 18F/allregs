CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 364  /  Sec. 364.41 What assurances must be included regarding eligibility?

(a) The State plan must assure that the service provider applies eligibility requirements without regard to age, color, creed, gender, national origin, race, religion, or type of significant disability of the individual applying for IL services.

(b) The State plan must assure that the service provider does not impose any State or local residence requirement that excludes under the plan any individual who is present in the State and who is otherwise eligible for IL services from receiving IL services. (Approved by the Office of Management and Budget under control number 1820-0527) (Authority: 29 U.S.C. 711(c) and 796c(a)(1))
