CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 286  /  Sec. 286.60 Must Tribes obligate all Tribal Family Assistance Grant

No. A Tribe may reserve amounts awarded to it, without fiscal year limitation, to provide assistance, benefits, and services in accordance with the requirements under Sec. 286.35 or Sec. 286.40, if applicable. [74 FR 25163, May 27, 2009]
