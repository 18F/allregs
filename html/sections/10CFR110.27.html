CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 110  /  Sec. 110.27 General license for imports.

(a) Except as provided in paragraphs (b) and (c) of this section, a general license is issued to any person to import byproduct, source, or special nuclear material if the U.S. consignee is authorized to receive and possess the material under the relevant NRC or Agreement State regulations.

(b) The general license in paragraph (a) of this section does not authorize the import of more than 100 kilograms per shipment of source and/or special nuclear material in the form of irradiated fuel.

(c) Paragraph (a) of this section does not authorize the import under a general license of radioactive waste.

(d) A person importing formula quantities of strategic special nuclear material (as defined in Sec. 73.2 of this chapter) under this general license shall provide the notifications required by Sec. 73.27 and Sec. 73.72 of this chapter.

(e) A general license is issued to any person to import the major components of a utilization facility as defined in Sec. 110.2 for end-use at a utilization facility licensed by the Commission.

(f) Importers of radioactive material listed in appendix P to this part must provide the notifications required by Sec. 110.50.
