CFR /  Title 22  /  Part 710  /  Sec. 710.4 Notice.

The President of OPIC or the President's designee shall initiate an administrative disciplinary hearing by providing the former OPIC employee with notice of an intention to institute a proceeding and an opportunity for a hearing. Notice must include:

(a) A statement of allegations and the basis thereof sufficiently detailed to enable the former employee to prepare an adequate defense;

(b) Notification of the right to a hearing; and

(c) An explanation of the method by which a hearing may be requested.
