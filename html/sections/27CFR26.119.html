CFR /  Title 27  /  Part 26  /  Sec. 26.119 Disposition of forms by district director of customs.

Two copies of the Form 487B will be forwarded to the appropriate TTB officer, and one copy of the form will be retained by the district director of customs and be available for inspection by appropriate TTB officers. [ T.D. ATF-451, 66 FR 21669, May 1, 2001]
