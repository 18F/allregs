CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 56  /  Sec. 56.20-5 Marking (modifies 107.2).

Each valve shall bear the manufacturer's name or trademark and reference symbol to indicate the service conditions for which the manufacturer guarantees the valve. The marking shall be in accordance with MSS SP-25 (incorporated by reference; see 46 CFR 56.01-2). [USCG-2003-16630, 73 FR 65176, Oct. 31, 2008]
