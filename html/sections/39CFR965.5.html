CFR /  Title 39  /  Part 965  /  Sec. 965.5 Initial submissions by parties.

Within 15 days after receipt of the Recorder's notice, each party shall file with the Recorder a sworn statement of the facts supporting its claim to receipt of the mail together with a copy of each document on which it relies in making such claim, and any arguments supporting its claim. [76 FR 15219, Mar. 21, 2011]
