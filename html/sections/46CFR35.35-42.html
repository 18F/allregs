CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 35  /  Sec. 35.35-42 Restrictions on vessels alongside a tank vessel loading

(a) No vessel may come alongside or remain alongside a tank vessel in way of its cargo tanks while it is loading or unloading cargo of Grade A, B, or C without permission of the person in charge of the transfer on the tank vessel.

(b) No vessel may come alongside or remain alongside a tank vessel in way of its cargo tanks while it is loading or unloading cargo of Grade A, B, or C unless the conditions then prevailing are acceptable to the persons in charge of cargo-handling on both vessels. [CGD 79-116, 60 FR 17156, Apr. 4, 1995]
