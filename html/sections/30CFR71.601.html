CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 71  /  Sec. 71.601 Drinking water; quality.

(a) Potable water provided in accordance with the provisions of Sec. 71.600 shall meet the applicable minimum health requirements for drinking water established by the State or community in which the mine is located.

(b) Where no such requirements are applicable, the drinking water provided shall conform to the Public Health Service Drinking Water Standards, 42 CFR part 72, subpart J.
