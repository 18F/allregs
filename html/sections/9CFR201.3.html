CFR /  Title 9  /  Part 201  /  Sec. 201.3 Applicability of regulations in this part.

The regulations in this part, when governing or affecting contracts, shall apply to any poultry growing arrangement, swine production contract, or any other livestock or poultry contract entered into, amended, altered, modified, renewed or extended after February 7, 2012. [76 FR 76889, Dec. 9, 2011, as amended at 80 FR 6430, Feb. 5, 2015]
