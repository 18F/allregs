CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 1248  /  Sec. 1248.2 Items to be reported.

(a) The following items are to be reported quarterly and annually by class I railroads:

(1) Average number of miles of road operated in freight service.

(2) For each commodity code used in reporting, except that the number of carloads for commodity code 431, ``Small packaged freight shipments,'' shall be omitted, the following items: Revenue freight originating on respondent's road:

Terminating on line:Number of carloads.Number of tons (2,000 pounds).

Delivered to connecting rail carriers.Number of carloads.Number of tons (2,000 pounds).Revenue freight received from connecting rail carriers:

Terminating on line:Number of carloads.Number of tons (2,000 pounds).

Delivered to connecting rail carriers:Number of carloads.Number of tons (2,000 pounds).

Total revenue freight carried:Number of carloads.Number of tons (2,000 pounds).

Gross freight revenue.
