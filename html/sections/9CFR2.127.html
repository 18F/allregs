CFR /  Title 9  /  Part 2  /  Sec. 2.127 Publication of names of persons subject to the provisions

APHIS will publish lists of persons licensed or registered in accordance with the provisions of this part in the Federal Register. The lists may be obtained upon request from the AC Regional Director. [54 FR 36147, Aug. 31, 1989, as amended at 63 FR 62927, Nov. 10, 1998]
