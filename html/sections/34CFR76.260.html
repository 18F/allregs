CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 76  /  Sec. 76.260 Allotments are made under program statute or

(a) The Secretary allots program funds to a State in accordance with the authorizing statute or implementing regulations for the program.

(b) Any reallotment to other States will be made by the Secretary in accordance with the authorizing statute or implementing regulations for that program. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 3474(a)) [50 FR 29330, July 18, 1985]
