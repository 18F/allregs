CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 192  /  Sec. 192.369 Service lines: Connections to cast iron or ductile

(a) Each service line connected to a cast iron or ductile iron main must be connected by a mechanical clamp, by drilling and tapping the main, or by another method meeting the requirements of Sec. 192.273.

(b) If a threaded tap is being inserted, the requirements of Sec. 192.151 (b) and (c) must also be met.
