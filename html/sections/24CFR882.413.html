CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 882  /  Sec. 882.413 Responsibility of the Family.

(a) A family receiving housing assistance under this Program must fulfill all of its obligations under the Lease and Statement of Family Responsibility.

(b) No family member may engage in drug-related criminal activity or violent criminal activity. Failure of the Family to meet its responsibilities under the Lease, the Statement of Family Responsibility, or this section shall constitute rounds for termination of assistance by the PHA. Should the PHA determine to terminate assistance to the Family, the provisions of Sec. 882.514(f) must be followed. [55 FR 28546, July 11, 1990, as amended at 63 FR 23855, Apr. 30, 1998]
