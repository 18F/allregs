CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 929  /  Sec. 929.14 Marketable quantity.

Marketable quantity means for a crop year the number of pounds of cranberries necessary to meet the total market demand and to provide for an adequate carryover. [33 FR 11640, Aug. 16, 1968]
