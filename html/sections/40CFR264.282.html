CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 264  /  Sec. 264.282 Special requirements for incompatible wastes.

The owner or operator must not place incompatible wastes, or incompatible wastes and materials (see appendix V of this part for examples), in or on the same treatment zone, unless Sec. 264.17(b) is complied with.
