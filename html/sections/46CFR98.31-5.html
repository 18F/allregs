CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 98  /  Sec. 98.31-5 Applicability.

This subpart applies to each offshore supply vessel contracted for, or the keel of which was laid, before March 15, 1996, that is oceangoing as defined in 33 CFR 151.05(j) and that carries noxious liquid substances (NLSs) as defined in Sec. 153.2 of this chapter in bulk, including carriage in portable tanks.
