CFR /  Title 22  /  Part 707  /  Sec. 707.22 Requests to permit access of records to an individual other

(a) Access by an authorized individual. An individual requester who wishes to be accompanied by another individual when reviewing records pertaining to the requester must provide OPIC with a signed, written statement authorizing discussion of the information contained in the records in the presence of the accompanying individual. Both parties will be required to verify their identity under Sec. 707.21(c) before access is granted.

(b) Release to an authorized individual. An individual requester who wishes to have copies of records pertaining to the requester released to another individual must provide OPIC with a written statement authorizing release of the information contained in the records to the other individual. The identity of the individual to whom the record pertains must be verified under Sec. 707.21(c) before release is authorized.

(c) Access or release to parent or guardian. Guardians will be provided access or copies under the provisions of Sec. 707.21.
