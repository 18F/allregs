CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 1710  /  Sec. 1710.120 Construction standards and contracting.

Borrowers shall follow all RUS requirements regarding construction work plans, energy efficiency and conservation program work plans, construction standards, approved materials, construction and related contracts, inspection procedures, and bidding procedures. [57 FR 1053, Jan. 9, 1992, as amended at 78 FR 73366, Dec. 5, 2013]
