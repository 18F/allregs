CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 502  /  Sec. 502.118 Copies of documents for use of the Commission.

(a)-(b)(3) [Reserved]

(4) One copy of each exhibit shall be furnished to the official reporter, to each of the parties present at the hearing and to the Presiding Officer unless he or she directs otherwise. If submitted other than at a hearing, the ``reporter's'' copy of an exhibit shall be furnished to the administrative law judge for later inclusion in the record if and when admitted.

(5) Copies of prepared testimony submitted pursuant to Sec. 502.157 are governed by the requirements for exhibits in paragraph (b)(4) of this section. [Rule 118.] [49 FR 44369, Nov. 6, 1984, as amended at 55 FR 28400, July 11, 1990; 61 FR 66617, Dec. 18, 1996; 64 FR 7809, Feb. 17, 1999; 76 FR 10262, Feb. 24, 2011]
