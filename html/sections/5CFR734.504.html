CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 734  /  Sec. 734.504 Contributions to political action committees through

An employee described in Sec. 734.502(a) may not financially contribute to a political action committee through a voluntary allotment made under Sec. 550.311(b) of this title. [61 FR 35102, July 5, 1996]
