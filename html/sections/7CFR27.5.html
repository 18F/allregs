CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 27  /  Sec. 27.5 Effect of amendments.

Any amendment to this subpart, unless otherwise stated therein, shall apply to all tenders of cotton and settlements therefor made on and after the effective date of such amendment, under basis grade contracts entered into prior, as well as subsequent, to such effective date. [22 FR 10923, Dec. 28, 1957, as amended at 42 FR 40677, Aug. 11, 1977]
