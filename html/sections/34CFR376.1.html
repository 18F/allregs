CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 376  /  Sec. 376.1 What is the program of Special Projects and DemonstrationsDisabilities?

This program is designed to provide job training for youths with disabilities to prepare them for entry into the labor force, including competitive or supported employment. (Authority: Sec. 311(b); 29 U.S.C. 777a(b)) [51 FR 3895, Jan. 30, 1986, as amended at 57 FR 28441, June 24, 1992; 59 FR 8340, Feb. 18, 1994]
