CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 64  /  Sec. 64.1506 Number designation.

Any interstate service described in Sec. 64.1501(a)(1)-(2), and not subject to the exclusions contained in Sec. 64.1501(a)(4), shall be offered only through telephone numbers beginning with a 900 service access code. [59 FR 46770, Sept. 12, 1994]
