CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 59  /  Sec. 59.121 Amendments to application.

Any amendment of an application that changes the scope of the application or changes the cost estimates by 10 percent or more shall be subject to approval in the same manner as an original application. (Authority: 38 U.S.C. 101, 501, 1710, 1742, 8105, 8131-8137)
