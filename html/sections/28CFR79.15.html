CFR /  Title 28  /  Part 79  /  Sec. 79.15 Proof of onset of leukemia more than two years after

The Program will presume that the date of onset was the date of diagnosis as indicated in the medical documentation accepted by the Program as proof of the claimant's leukemia. The date of onset must be more than two years after the date of first exposure as determined under Sec. 79.14(b).
