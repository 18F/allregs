CFR /  Title 17  /  Part 9  /  Sec. 9.21 Record of exchange proceeding.

(a) Filing of record. Within thirty days after service of the notice of appeal, the exchange must file two copies of the record of the exchange proceeding (as defined in Sec. 9.2(i)) with the Proceedings Clerk, and serve a copy on the appellant and any other party to the proceeding, provided that such person has agreed to pay the exchange reasonable fees, as provided in the rules of the exchange, for printing the copy. The record must be bound as a unit, must be chronologically indexed and tabbed, must be certified as correct by a duly authorized official, agent or employee of the exchange, and must contain a certificate of service on the appellant or any other party to the proceeding (or waiver of service for failure to pay costs pursuant to this rule).
