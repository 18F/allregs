CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 1000  /  Sec. 1000.32 May exceptions be made to the conflict of interest

(a) Yes. HUD may make exceptions to the conflict of interest provisions set forth in Sec. 1000.30(b) on a case-by-case basis when it determines that such an exception would further the primary objective of NAHASDA and the effective and efficient implementation of the recipient's program, activity, or project.

(b) A public disclosure of the conflict must be made and a determination that the exception would not violate tribal laws on conflict of interest (or any applicable state laws) must also be made.
