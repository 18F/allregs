CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 303  /  Sec. 303.73 Applications to use the courts of the United States to

The IV-D agency may apply to the Secretary for permission to use a United States district court to enforce a support order of a court of competent jurisdiction against a noncustodial parent who is present in another State if the IV-D agency can furnish evidence in accordance with instructions issued by the office. [61 FR 67241, Dec. 20, 1996, as amended at 68 FR 25304, May 12, 2003]
