CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 21  /  Sec. 21.73 Duration of employment assistance programs.

(a) Duration. Employment assistance may be provided to the veteran for the period necessary to enable the veteran to secure employment in a suitable occupation, and to adjust in the employment. This period shall not exceed 18 months. A veteran may be provided such assistance if he or she is eligible for employment assistance under the provisions of Sec. 21.47 of this part. (Authority: 38 U.S.C. 3105(b))

(b) Employment assistance not charged against Chapter 31 entitlement. The period of employment assistance provided in paragraph (a) of this section is not charged against the months of entitlement under Chapter 31 (see Sec. 21.70). (Authority: 38 U.S.C. 3105(b)) [49 FR 40814, Oct. 18, 1984, as amended at 54 FR 21216, May 17, 1989]
