CFR /  Title 12  /  Part 600  /  Sec. 600.2 Farm Credit Administration.

(a) Background. The Farm Credit Administration is an independent, non-appropriated fund agency in the executive branch of the Federal Government. The FCA Board and employees carry out the FCA's functions, powers, and duties.

(b) Locations. FCA's headquarters address is 1501 Farm Credit Drive, McLean, Virginia 22102-5090. The FCA has the following field offices: 1501 Farm Credit Drive, McLean, VA 22102-5090.2051 Killebrew Drive, Suite 610, Bloomington, Minnesota 55425-1899.511 East Carpenter Freeway, Suite 650, Irving, TX 75062-3930.3131 South Vaughn Way, Suite 250, Aurora, CO 80014-3507.2180 Harvard Street, Suite 300, Sacramento, California 95815-3323.
