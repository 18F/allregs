CFR /  Title 2  /  Part 901  /  Sec. 901.1010 Suspending official (Department of Energy supplement

The suspending official for the Department of Energy, exclusive of NNSA, is the Director, Office of Procurement and Assistance Management, DOE. The suspending official for NNSA is the Director, Office of Acquisition and Supply Management, NNSA. Subpart J [Reserved]
