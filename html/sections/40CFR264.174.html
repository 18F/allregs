CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 264  /  Sec. 264.174 Inspections.

At least weekly, the owner or operator must inspect areas where containers are stored, except for Performance Track member facilities, that may conduct inspections at least once each month, upon approval by the Director. To apply for reduced inspection frequencies, the Performance Track member facility must follow the procedures identified in Sec. 264.15(b)(5) of this part. The owner or operator must look for leaking containers and for deterioration of containers and the containment system caused by corrosion or other factors. [Comment: See Secs. 264.15(c) and 264.171 for remedial action required if deterioration or leaks are detected.] [71 FR 16905, Apr. 4, 2006]
