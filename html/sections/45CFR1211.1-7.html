CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 1211  /  Sec. 1211.1-7 Entitlement to representation.

A volunteer may be accompanied, represented, and advised by a representative of the volunteer's own choice at any stage of the proceeding. The volunteer shall designate his or her representative in writing. A person chosen by the volunteer must be willing to act as representative and have no conflict between his or her position and the subject matter of the grievance.
