CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 302-9  /  Sec. 302-9.601 How many POV's may we authorize for transportation at

Within CONUS, you may authorize transportation of up to two POVs at Government expense, as prescribed in Sec. 302-9.302. For shipments from CONUS to OCONUS, OCONUS to OCONUS, and OCONUS to CONUS, only one POV may be transported at Government expense. [FTR Amdt. 2011-01, 76 FR 18342, Apr. 1, 2011. Redesignated by FTR Amdt. 2011-06, 76 FR 71890, Nov. 21, 2011]
