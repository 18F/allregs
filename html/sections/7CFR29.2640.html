CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 29  /  Sec. 29.2640 Rule 24.

Tobacco that is semifired but which otherwise meets the specifications of a grade shall be treated as a subgrade by placing the special factor ``SF'' after the grademark. This factor does not apply to tobacco designated ``No-G''. [51 FR 40406, Nov. 7, 1986]
