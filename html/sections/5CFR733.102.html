CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 733  /  Sec. 733.102 Exclusion of employees in the Criminal Division and

Employees in the Criminal Division and National Security Division in the Department of Justice (except employees appointed by the President by and with the advice and consent of the Senate) specifically are excluded from coverage under the provisions of this part. [79 FR 25485, May 5, 2014]
