CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 76  /  Sec. 76.591 Federal evaluation--cooperation by a grantee.

A grantee shall cooperate in any evaluation of the program by the Secretary. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1221e-3, 1226c, 1231a, 3474, and 6511(a)) [45 FR 86298, Dec. 30, 1980, as amended at 57 FR 30341, July 8, 1992]
