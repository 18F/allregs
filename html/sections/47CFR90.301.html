CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 90  /  Sec. 90.301 Scope.

This subpart governs the authorization and use of frequencies by land mobile stations in the band 470-512 MHz on a geographically shared basis with Television Broadcast stations. Under this special sharing plan, different frequencies are allocated depending on the geographic urban area involved as fully detailed in the following rule sections. [43 FR 54791, Nov. 22, 1978, as amended at 62 FR 18932, Apr. 17, 1997]
