CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 318  /  Sec. 318.47-2 Articles the movement of which is prohibited

(a) Articles prohibited movement. The movement of seed cotton, cottonseed, and seedy waste, when unfumigated, is prohibited except as provided in Sec. 318.47-3(b)(2).

(b) Articles the movement of which is regulated. Lint; linters; waste; seed cotton; cottonseed; cottonseed hulls, cake, and meal; and bale covers may be moved upon compliance with the conditions prescribed in Sec. 318.47-3.
