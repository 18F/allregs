CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 835  /  Sec. 835.103 Education, training and skills.

Individuals responsible for developing and implementing measures necessary for ensuring compliance with the requirements of this part shall have the appropriate education, training, and skills to discharge these responsibilities. [63 FR 59682, Nov. 4, 1998]
