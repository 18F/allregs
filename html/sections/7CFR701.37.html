CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 701  /  Sec. 701.37 Loss of control of the property during the practice life

In the event of voluntary or involuntary loss of control of the land by the ECP or EFRP cost-share recipient during the practice life-span, if the person or legal entity acquiring control elects not to become a successor to the ECP or EFRP agreement and the practice is not maintained, each participant who received cost-share assistance for the practice may be jointly and severally liable for refunding any ECP or EFRP cost-share assistance related to that practice. The practice life span, for purposes of this section, includes any maintenance period that is essential to its success. [69 FR 10302, Mar. 4, 2004, as amended at 75 FR 70088, Nov. 17, 2010] Sec.
