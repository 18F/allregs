CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 1780  /  Sec. 1780.63 Sewage treatment and bulk water sales contracts.

Owners entering into agreements with private or public parties to treat sewage or supply bulk water shall have written contracts for such service and all such contracts shall be subject to the Agency concurrence. Section 1780.62 should be used as a guide to prepare such contracts. Sec. Sec. 1780.64-1780.66 [Reserved]
