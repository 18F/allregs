CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 2527  /  2527.7001 General.

National Science Foundation policies, procedures, and clauses governing allocation of rights to inventions made under NSF contracts, grants, and cooperative agreements are codified as part 650 of title 45 of the Code of Federal Regulations. [57 FR 34882, Aug. 7, 1992, as amended at 61 FR 51022, Sept. 30, 1996]
