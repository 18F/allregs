CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 417  /  Sec. 417.418 Qualifying condition: Quality assurance program.

(a) Condition. The HMO or CMP must make arrangements for a quality assurance program that meets the requirements of this section.

(b) Standard. An HMO or CMP must have an ongoing quality assurance program that meets the requirements set forth in Sec. 417.106(a). [58 FR 38072, July 15, 1993]
