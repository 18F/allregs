CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 53  /  Sec. 53.4 Kind of service.

Grading service under the regulations shall consist of the determination and certification and other identification, upon request by the applicant, of the class, grade, or other quality of livestock under applicable standards. Class, grade and other quality may be determined under said standards for livestock. Acceptance service under the regulations shall consist of the determination of the conformity of livestock to specifications approved by the Director or Chief and the certification and other identification of such livestock in accordance with specifications, upon request by the applicant. [42 FR 53902, Oct. 4, 1977, as amended at 63 FR 72101, Dec. 31, 1998]
