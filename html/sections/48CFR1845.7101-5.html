CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 1845  /  1845.7101-5 Contractor's privileged financial and business information.

If a transfer of property between contractors involves disclosing costs of a proprietary nature, the contractor shall furnish unit acquisition costs only on copies of shipping documents sent to the shipping and receiving NASA Centers. [65 FR 54816, Sept. 11, 2000, as amended at 66 FR 41806, Aug. 9, 2001]
