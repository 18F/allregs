CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 26  /  Sec. 26.407 Behavioral observation.

While the individuals specified in Sec. 26.4(f) are constructing or directing the construction of safety- or security-related SSCs, licensees and other entities shall ensure that these individuals are subject to behavioral observation, except if the licensee or other entity has implemented a fitness monitoring program under Sec. 26.406. [75 FR 73941, Nov. 30, 2010]
