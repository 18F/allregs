CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 3  /  Sec. 3.856 Change of name of female fiduciary.

If a female fiduciary receiving benefits in such capacity marries or is restored to her former name by divorce decree, her statement setting forth her present name may be accepted. [39 FR 34533, Sept. 26, 1974]
