CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 156  /  Sec. 156.159 Compliance date.

Any pesticide product released for shipment by a registrant after August 16, 2011 must bear a label that complies with Sec. Sec. 156.10(d)(7), 156.10(f), 156.10(i)(2)(ix), 156.140, 156.144, 156.146 and 156.156. [75 FR 62326, Oct. 8, 2010] Subparts I-J [Reserved]
