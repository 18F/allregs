CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 59  /  Sec. 59.1 Purpose.

This part sets forth the mechanism for a State to obtain a grant:

(a) To construct State home facilities (or to acquire facilities to be used as State home facilities) for furnishing domiciliary or nursing home care to veterans, and

(b) To expand, remodel, or alter existing buildings for furnishing domiciliary, nursing home, adult day health, or hospital care to veterans in State homes. (Authority: 38 U.S.C. 101, 501, 1710, 1742, 8105, 8131-8137)
