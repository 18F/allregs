CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 3  /  Sec. 3.359 Determination of service connection for former members

Rating boards will determine whether or not the condition for which treatment is claimed by former members of the Armed Forces of Czechoslovakia or Poland under 38 U.S.C. 109(c) is service connected. This determination will be made using the same criteria that applies to determinations of service connection based on service in the Armed Forces of the United States. [43 FR 4424, Feb. 2, 1978]
