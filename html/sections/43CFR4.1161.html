CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 4  /  Sec. 4.1161 Who may file.

A permittee issued a notice or order by the Secretary pursuant to the provisions of section 521(a)(2) or section 521(a)(3) of the Act or any person having an interest which is or may be adversely affected by a notice or order subject to review under Sec. 4.1160 may file an application for review with the Hearings Division, OHA, 801 North Quincy Street, Arlington, Va. 22203. [43 FR 34386, Aug. 3, 1978, as amended at 67 FR 4368, Jan. 30, 2002]
