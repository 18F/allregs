CFR /  Title 50  /  Part 216  /  Sec. 216.71 Allowable take of fur seals.

Pribilovians may take fur seals on the Pribilof Islands if such taking is

(a) For subsistence uses, and

(b) Not accomplished in a wasteful manner. [51 FR 24840, July 9, 1986. Redesignated at 61 FR 11750, Mar. 22, 1996]
