CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 403  /  Sec. 403.80 Who is eligible for a subgrant or contract?

Eligible recipients and community-based organizations are eligible for an award under the Single Parents, Displaced Homemakers, and Single Pregnant Women Program. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 2335(a)(2), (3); 2335b(1))
