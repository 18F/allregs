CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 21  /  Sec. 21.6380 Additional applicable Chapter 31 regulations.

The following regulations are applicable to veterans pursuing the vocational training under this program in the same manner as they apply to 38 U.S.C. chapter 31: Sec. 21.380, Sec. 21.390, Sec. 21.400, Sec. 21.402, Sec. 21.412, Sec. 21.414 (except (d) and (e)), Sec. 21.420, and Sec. 21.430 (except (a)) of this part. (Authority: 38 U.S.C. 1524)
