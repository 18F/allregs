CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 575  /  Sec. 575.201 Child restraint performance.

The National Highway Traffic Safety Administration has established a program for rating the performance of child restraints. The agency makes the information developed under this rating program available through a variety of means, including postings on its Web site, http://www.nhtsa.dot.gov. [67 FR 67494, Nov. 5, 2002]
