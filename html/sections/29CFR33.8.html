CFR /  Title 29  /  Part 33  /  Sec. 33.8 Program accessibility: Discrimination prohibited.

Except as otherwise provided in Sec. Sec. 33.9 and 33.10 of this part, no qualified individual with handicaps shall, because the Department's facilities are inaccessible to or unusable by individuals with handicaps, be denied the benefits of, be excluded from participation in, or otherwise be subjected to discrimination under any program or activity conducted by the Department. [52 FR 11606, Apr. 9, 1987; 52 FR 23967, June 26, 1987]
