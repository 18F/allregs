CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 21  /  Sec. 21.6242 Resources for provision of medical treatment, care and

(a) General. VA medical centers are the primary resources for the provision of medical treatment, care and services for program participants which may be authorized under the provisions of Sec. 21.6240 of this part. The availability of necessary services in VA facilities shall be ascertained in each case. (Authority: 38 U.S.C. 1524(b)(2))
