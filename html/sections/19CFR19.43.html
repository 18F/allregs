CFR /  Title 19  /  Part 19  /  Sec. 19.43 Filing of application.

The application, listing the containers by marks and numbers, may be filed at the customhouse or with the Customs inspector at the place where the container is unladen, or for merchandise transported in-bond, at the bonded carrier's facility, as designated by the port director. [T.D. 82-135, 47 FR 32416, July 27, 1982]
