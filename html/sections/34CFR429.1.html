CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 429  /  Sec. 429.1 What is the Bilingual Vocational Materials, Methods, and

The Bilingual Vocational Materials, Methods, and Techniques Program provides financial assistance for the development of instructional and curriculum materials, methods, or techniques for bilingual vocational training for individuals with limited English proficiency. (Authority: Sec. 441(c)(1); 20 U.S.C. 2441(c)(1))
