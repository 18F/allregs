CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 86  /  Sec. 86.3 What actions shall an IHE take to comply with the

(a) An IHE shall adopt and implement a drug prevention program as described in Sec. 86.100 to prevent the unlawful possession, use, or distribution of illicit drugs and alcohol by all students and employees on school premises or as part of any of its activities.

(b) An IHE shall provide a written certification that it has adopted and implemented the drug prevention program described in Sec. 86.100. (Approved by the Office of Management and Budget under control number 1880-0522) (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1145g) [55 FR 33581, Aug. 16, 1990, as amended at 61 FR 66225, 66226, Dec. 17, 1996]
