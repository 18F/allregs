CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 361  /  Sec. 361.31 Cooperative agreements with private nonprofit

The State plan must describe the manner in which cooperative agreements with private nonprofit vocational rehabilitation service providers will be established. (Approved by the Office of Management and Budget under control number 1820-0500) (Authority: Sections 101(a)(24)(B); 29 U.S.C. 721(a)(24)(B)) [66 FR 4382, Jan. 17, 2001, as amended at 66 FR 7253, Jan. 22, 2001]
