CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 50  /  Sec. 50.78 Facility information and verification.

(a) In response to a written request by the Commission, each applicant for a construction permit or license and each recipient of a construction permit or a license shall submit facility information, as described in Sec. 75.10 of this chapter, on Form N-71, and associated forms and site information on DOC/NRC Form AP-A and associated forms;

(b) As required by the Additional Protocol, shall submit location information described in Sec. 75.11 of this chapter on DOC/NRC Form AP-1 and associated forms; and

(c) Shall permit verification thereof by the International Atomic Energy Agency (IAEA) and take other action as necessary to implement the US/IAEA Safeguards Agreement, as described in part 75 of this chapter. [73 FR 78605, Dec. 23, 2008]
