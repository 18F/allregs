CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 101-30  /  Sec. 101-30.403-2 Management codes.

For internal use within an agency, alphabetic codes excluding letters ``I'' and ``O'' may be prefixed or suffixed to the national stock number as CM7520-00-123-4567 or 7520-00-123-4567CM, as required for supply management operations. Numeric codes shall not be affixed immediately adjacent to or as a part of the national stock number, nor shall codes be intermingled in the national stock number. [41 FR 11309, Mar. 18, 1976]
