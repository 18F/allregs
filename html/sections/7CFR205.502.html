CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 205  /  Sec. 205.502 Applying for accreditation.

(a) A private or governmental entity seeking accreditation as a certifying agent under this subpart must submit an application for accreditation which contains the applicable information and documents set forth in Sec. Sec. 205.503 through 205.505 and the fees required in Sec. 205.640 to: Program Manager, USDA-AMS-NOP, 1400 Independence Ave. SW., Room 2648 So. Bldg., Ag Stop 0268, Washington, DC 20250-0268.

(b) Following the receipt of the information and documents, the Administrator will determine, pursuant to Sec. 205.506, whether the applicant for accreditation should be accredited as a certifying agent. [65 FR 80637, Dec. 21, 2000, as amended at 80 FR 6429, Feb. 5, 2015]
