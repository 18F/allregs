CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 1253  /  Sec. 1253.30 Retention of records.

Each organization subject to sections 5a or 5b shall retain records or documents relating to its transactions or activities in accordance with part 1220, Preservation of Records, of this chapter. [40 FR 50389, Oct. 29, 1975] PARTS 1260 1269_VALUATION--Table of Contents
