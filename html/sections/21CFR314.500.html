CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 314  /  Sec. 314.500 Scope.

This subpart applies to certain new drug products that have been studied for their safety and effectiveness in treating serious or life-threatening illnesses and that provide meaningful therapeutic benefit to patients over existing treatments (e.g., ability to treat patients unresponsive to, or intolerant of, available therapy, or improved patient response over available therapy). [57 FR 58958, Dec. 11, 1992, as amended at 64 FR 402, Jan. 5, 1999]
