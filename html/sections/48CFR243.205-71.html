CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 243  /  243.205-71 Requests for equitable adjustment.

Use the clause at 252.243-7002, Requests for Equitable Adjustment, in solicitations and contracts, including solicitations and contracts using FAR part 12 procedures for the acquisition of commercial items, that are estimated to exceed the simplified acquisition threshold. [63 FR 17124, Apr. 8, 1998. Redesignated at 66 FR 49865, Oct. 1, 2001; 78 FR 37989, June 25, 2013]
