CFR /  Title 9  /  Part 309  /  Sec. 309.9 Swine erysipelas.

All hogs plainly showing on ante-mortem inspection that they are affected with acute swine erysipelas shall be identified as U.S. Condemned and disposed of in accordance with Sec. 309.13.
