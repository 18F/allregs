CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 5502  /  Sec. 5502.101 General.

The regulations in this part apply to employees of the Department of Health and Human Services and supplement the Executive Branch Financial Disclosure Regulations in 5 CFR part 2634. Any regulation in this part made applicable only to the employees of an HHS component designated as a separate agency under Sec. 5501.102(a) of this chapter shall apply to the employees of that component as defined in Sec. 5501.102(b)(1) of this chapter.
