CFR /  Title 27  /  Part 21  /  Sec. 21.51 Formula No. 25-A.

(a) Formula. To every 100 gallons of alcohol add: A solution composed of 20 pounds of iodine, U.S.P.; 15 pounds of either potassium iodide, U.S.P., or sodium iodide, U.S.P.; and 15 pounds of water.

(b) Authorized uses. (1) As a solvent: 230. Tinctures of iodine.249. Miscellaneous external pharmaceuticals, U.S.P. or N.F.

(1) As a solvent: 230. Tinctures of iodine.249. Miscellaneous external pharmaceuticals, U.S.P. or N.F.

(2) Miscellaneous uses: 812. Product development and pilot plant uses (own use only).
