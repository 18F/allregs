CFR /  Title 17  /  Part 249  /  Sec. 249.1110 Form MSDW, notice of withdrawal from registration as a (17 CFR 240.15Bc3-1).

This form is to be used by a bank or a separately identifiable department or division of a bank (as defined by the Municipal Securities Rulemaking Board) to withdraw from registration with the Securities and Exchange Commission as a municipal securities dealer pursuant to section 15B(c) of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934.

Note: Copies of Form MSDW have been filed with the Office of the Federal Register as part of this document. Copies of Form MSDW may be obtained from the Publications Section, Securities and Exchange Commission, 500 North Capitol Street, Washington, DC 20549. [41 FR 28949, July 14, 1976]
