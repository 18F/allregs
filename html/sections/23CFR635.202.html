CFR /  Title 23  /  Part 635  /  Sec. 635.202 Applicability.

This subpart applies to all Federal-aid and other highway construction projects financed in whole or in part with Federal funds and to be constructed by a State transportation department or a subdivision thereof in pursuant of agreements between any other State transportation department and the Federal Highway Administration (FHWA). [69 FR 7119, Feb. 13, 2004]
