CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 4280  /  Sec. 4280.19 REDG Grants.

Intermediaries receiving Grants must partially finance a Revolving Loan Fund that the Intermediary will operate and administer, by providing supplemental funds of at least 20 percent of the Grant. Grants are subject to 2 CFR parts 200, 400, 415, 417, 418, 421 as applicable. [79 FR 76015, Dec. 19, 2014] Sec. 4280.20 [Reserved]
