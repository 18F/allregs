CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 1710  /  Sec. 1710.3 Form and bulletin revisions.

References in this part to RUS or REA forms or line numbers in RUS or REA forms are based on RUS or REA Form 7 and Form 12 dated December 1992, unless otherwise indicated. These references will apply to corresponding information in future versions of the forms. The terms ``RUS form'', ``RUS standard form'', ``RUS specification'', and ``RUS bulletin'' have the same meanings as the terms ``REA form'', ``REA standard form'', ``REA specification'', and ``REA bulletin'', respectively, unless otherwise indicated. [59 FR 66440, Dec. 27, 1994]
