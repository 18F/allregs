CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 763  /  Sec. 763.121 Does this subpart apply to me?

If you are a State or local government employer and you are not subject to a State asbestos standard that OSHA has approved under section 18 of the Occupational Safety and Health Act or a State asbestos plan that EPA has exempted from the requirements of this subpart under Sec. 763.123, you must follow the requirements of this subpart to protect your employees from occupational exposure to asbestos.
