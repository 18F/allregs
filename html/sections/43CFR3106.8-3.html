CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 3106  /  Sec. 3106.8-3 Corporate merger.

Where a corporate merger affects leases situated in a State where the transfer of property of the dissolving corporation to the surviving corporation is accomplished by operation of law, no transfer of any affected lease interest is required. A notification of the merger shall be furnished with a list, by serial number, of all lease interests affected. Include the processing fee for corporate merger found in the fee schedule in Sec. 3000.12 of this chapter with your notification of a corporate merger. A bond rider or replacement bond conditioned to cover the obligations of all affected corporations may be required by the authorized officer as a prerequisite to recognition of the merger. [53 FR 17355, May 16, 1988, as amended at 70 FR 58874, Oct. 7, 2005]
