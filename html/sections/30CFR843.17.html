CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 843  /  Sec. 843.17 Failure to give notice and lack of information.

No notice of violation, cessation order, show cause order, or order revoking or suspending a permit may be vacated for failure to give the notice to the State regulatory authority required under Sec. 842.11(b)(1)(ii)(B) of this chapter or because it is subsequently determined that the Office did not have information sufficient, under Sec. Sec. 842.11(b)(1) and 842.11(b)(2) of this chapter, to justify an inspection.
