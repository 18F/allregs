CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 868  /  Sec. 868.26 Expenses of the cooperator or the Service.

For any request that has been withdrawn, dismissed, or withheld under Sec. Sec. 868.22, 868.23, or 868.24, respectively, each applicant shall pay expenses incurred by the cooperator or the Service. [53 FR 3722, Feb. 9, 1988. Redesignated and amended at 60 FR 16364, Mar. 30, 1995]
