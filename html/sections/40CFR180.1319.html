CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 180  /  Sec. 180.1319 Banda de Lupinus albus doce (BLAD); exemption from

An exemption from the requirement of a tolerance is established for the residues of Banda de Lupinus albus doce (BLAD), a naturally occurring polypeptide from the catabolism of a seed storage protein ([beta]-conglutin) of sweet lupines (Lupinus albus), in or on all food commodities when applied as a fungicide and used in accordance with label directions and good agricultural practices. [78 FR 17604, Mar. 22, 2013]
