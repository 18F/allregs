CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 102-194  /  Sec. 102-194.5 What is the Standard and Optional Forms Management

The Standard and Optional Forms Management Program is a Governmentwide program that promotes economies and efficiencies through the development, maintenance and use of common forms. The General Services Administration (GSA) provides additional guidance on the Standard and Optional Forms Management Program through an external handbook called Standard and Optional Forms Procedural Handbook. You may obtain a copy of the handbook from: Standard and Optional Forms Management Office General Services Administration (Forms-XR)1800 F Street, NW.; Room 7126Washington, DC 20405-0002(202) 501-0581http://www.gsa.gov/forms
