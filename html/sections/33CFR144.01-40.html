CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 144  /  Sec. 144.01-40 Emergency communications equipment.

On manned platforms means of communication by radio and/or wire telephone shall be provided for contacting the shore or vessels in the vicinity for aid in the event of an emergency. [CGFR 56-4, 21 FR 903, Feb. 9, 1956]
