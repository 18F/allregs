CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 946  /  Sec. 946.104 Reestablishment and reapportionment of committee.

(a) Pursuant to Sec. 946.22, on and after July 1, 2007, the State of Washington Potato Committee consisting of nine members, of whom six shall be producers and three shall be handlers, is hereby reestablished. For each member of the committee there shall be an alternate who shall have the same qualifications as the member.

(b) Pursuant to Sec. 946.22, on and after July 1, 2007, membership representation of the State of Washington Potato Committee shall be reapportioned among the districts of the production area so as to provide that each of the three districts as defined in Sec. 946.103 are represented by two producer members and one handler member and their respective alternates. [72 FR 17795, Apr. 10, 2007]
