CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 185  /  Sec. 185.203 Notice of hazardous conditions.

Whenever there is a hazardous condition, as defined by Sec. 175.400 of this subchapter, on board the vessel, the owner, master, agent, or person in charge shall immediately notify the Captain of the Port of the port of place of destination and the Captain of the Port of the port or place in which the vessel is located of the hazardous condition.
