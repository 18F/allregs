CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 167  /  Sec. 167.3 Geographic coordinates.

Geographic coordinates are defined using North American 1927 Datum (NAD 27) unless indicated otherwise. [CGD 90-039, 59 FR 21937, Apr. 28, 1994]
