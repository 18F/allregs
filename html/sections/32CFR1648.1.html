CFR /  Title 32  /  Part 1648  /  Sec. 1648.1 Authority of local board.

A local board shall consider and determine all claims which it receives in accord with Sec. 1633.2 or Sec. 1648.6 of this chapter. No action shall be taken by the board in the absence of a quorum of its prescribed membership. [52 FR 24458, July 1, 1987]
