CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 201  /  Sec. 201.69 Classes of certified seed.

(a) Classes of certified seed are as follows:

(1) Breeder.

(2) Foundation.

(3) Registered.

(4) Certified. [38 FR 25662, Sept. 14, 1973]
