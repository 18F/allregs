CFR /  Title 13  /  Part 123  /  Sec. 123.19 May I request an increase in the amount of an economic

SBA will consider your request for an increase in the loan amount if you can show that the increase is essential for your business to continue and is based on events occurring after SBA approved your original loan which were beyond your control. For example, delays may have occurred beyond your control which prevent you from resuming your normal business activity in a reasonable time frame. Your request for an increase in the loan amount must be related to the disaster for which the SBA economic injury disaster loan was originally made. [63 FR 15072, Mar. 30, 1998]
