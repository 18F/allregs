CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 701  /  Sec. 701.152 Availability of funding.

Payments under Sec. Sec. 1701.53 through 701.157 are subject to the availability of funds under Public Law 109-148. [71 FR 30265, May 26, 2006. Redesignated and amended at 75 FR 70088, Nov. 17, 2010]
