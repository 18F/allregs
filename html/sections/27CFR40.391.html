CFR /  Title 27  /  Part 40  /  Sec. 40.391 Persons required to qualify.

Every person who manufactures cigarette paper, or makes up cigarette paper into tubes, except for his own personal use or consumption, must first qualify as a manufacturer of cigarette papers and tubes in accordance with the provisions of this subpart. [ATF-467, 66 FR 49532, Sept. 28, 2001]
