CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 303  /  Sec. 303.202 Certification regarding financial responsibility.

Each application must include a certification to the Secretary that the arrangements to establish financial responsibility for the provision of part C services among appropriate public agencies under Sec. 303.511 and the lead agency's contracts with EIS providers regarding financial responsibility for the provision of part C services both meet the requirements in subpart F of this part (Secs. 303.500 through 303.521) and are current as of the date of submission of the certification. (Approved by Office of Management and Budget under control number 1820-0550) (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1437(a)(2))
