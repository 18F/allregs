CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 830  /  Sec. 830.60 Relabeling of a device that is required to bear a unique

If you relabel a device that is required to bear a unique device identifier (UDI), you must:

(a) Assign a new device identifier to the device, and

(b) Keep a record showing the relationship of the prior device identifier to your new device identifier. [78 FR 55825, Sept. 24, 2013]
