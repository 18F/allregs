CFR /  Title 31  /  Part 538  /  Sec. 538.534 Third-country diplomatic and consular funds transfers.

U.S. depository institutions, U.S. registered brokers or dealers in securities, and U.S. registered money transmitters are authorized to process funds transfers for the operating expenses or other official business of third-country diplomatic or consular missions in Sudan, provided such transfers are not by, to, or through a person who is included within the term Government of Sudan, as defined in Sec. 538.305. [76 FR 63198, Oct. 12, 2011]
