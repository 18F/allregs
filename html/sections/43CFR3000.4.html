CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 3000  /  Sec. 3000.4 Appeals.

Except as provided in Sec. Sec. 3101.7-3(b), 3120.1-3, 3165.4, and 3427.2 of this title, any party adversely affected by a decision of the authorized officer made pursuant to the provisions of Group 3000 or Group 3100 of this title shall have a right of appeal pursuant to part 4 of this title. [53 FR 22835, June 17, 1988]
