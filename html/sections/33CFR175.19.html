CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 175  /  Sec. 175.19 Stowage.

(a) No person may use a recreational boat unless each wearable PFD required by Sec. 175.15 is readily accessible.

(b) No person may use a recreational boat unless each throwable PFD required by Sec. 175.15 is immediately available. [USCG-2013-0263, 79 FR 56499, Sept. 22, 2014]
