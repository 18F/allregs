CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 222  /  Sec. 222.114 How does the Assistant Secretary implement the

The Assistant Secretary implements section 8004 of the Act and this subpart through such actions as the Assistant Secretary determines to be appropriate, including the withholding of funds in accordance with Sec. Sec. 222.115-222.122, after affording the affected LEA, parents, and Indian tribe or tribes an opportunity to present their views. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 7704 (d)(2), (e) (8)-(9))
