CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 404  /  404.403 Responsibilities of contracting officers.

When a proposed solicitation is likely to require access to classified information, the contracting officer shall consult with the Information Security Staff, Personnel and Document Security Division, Office of Procurement and Property Management, regarding the procedures that must be followed. [70 FR 44, Jan. 3, 2005]
