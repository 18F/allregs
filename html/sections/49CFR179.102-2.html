CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 179  /  Sec. 179.102-2 Chlorine.

(a) Each tank car used to transport chlorine must comply with all of the following:

(1) Tanks must be fabricated from carbon steel complying with ASTM Specification A 516 (IBR, see Sec. 171.7 of this subchapter), Grade 70, or AAR Specification TC 128, Grade A or B.
