CFR /  Title 27  /  Part 40  /  Sec. 40.257 Processed tobacco.

A manufacturer of tobacco products may be required to obtain authorization from the appropriate TTB officer with regard to the activities involving processed tobacco. See Sec. 40.72. Such manufacturers also must maintain records and may be required to submit reports regarding such activities. See Sec. Sec. 40.182 and 40.202. [T.D. TTB-78, 74 FR 29410, June 22, 2009]
