CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 73  /  Sec. 73.513 Noncommercial educational FM stations operating on

(a). In all other respects, stations operating on Channels 221 through 300 are to be governed by the provisions of this subpart and not subpart B.

(b) When a noncommercial educational applicant is among mutually exclusive applications for an unreserved FM channel, the mutually exclusive applications will be considered pursuant to Subpart I-- Competitive Bidding Procedures and not Subpart K--Application and Selection Procedures On Reserved Noncommercial Educational Channels. [47 FR 30068, July 12, 1982, as amended at 65 FR 36378, June 8, 2000]
