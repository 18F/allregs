CFR /  Title 17  /  Part 249  /  Sec. 249.330 Form N-SAR, annual and semi-annual report of certain

This form shall be used by registered unit investment trusts and small business investment companies for semi-annual or annual reports to be filed pursuant to Sec. 270.30a-1 or Sec. 270.30b1-1 of this chapter in satisfaction of the requirement of section 30(a) of the Investment Company Act of 1940 that every registered investment company must file annually with the Commission such information, documents, and reports as investment companies having securities registered on a national securities exchange are required to file annually pursuant to section 13(a) of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 (15 U.S.C. 78m) and the rules and regulations thereunder. [68 FR 5364, Feb. 3, 2003]
