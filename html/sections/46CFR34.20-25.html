CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 34  /  Sec. 34.20-25 Foam monitor capacity--T/ALL.

The capacity of each foam monitor on ships that have a keel laying date on or after January 1, 1975, must be at least 3 liters per minute per square meter (.073 gallons per minute per square foot) of cargo area protected by that monitor. [CGD 74-127, 41 FR 3846, Jan. 26, 1976]
