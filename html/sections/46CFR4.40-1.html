CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 4  /  Sec. 4.40-1 Purpose.

This subpart prescribes the joint regulations of the National Transportation Safety Board and the Coast Guard for the investigation of marine casualties. [CGD 82-034, 47 FR 45882, Oct. 14, 1982]
