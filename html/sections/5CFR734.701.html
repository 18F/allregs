CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 734  /  Sec. 734.701 General.

In addition to the provisions regulating political activity set forth in subparts A through G of this part, there are a number of statutes and Executive orders that establish standards to which the political activity of an employee, a Federal labor organization, a Federal employee organization, and a multicandidate political committee must conform. The list set forth in Sec. 734.702 references some of the more significant of those statutes. It is not comprehensive and includes only references to statutes of general applicability.
