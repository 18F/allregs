CFR /  Title 32  /  Part 644  /  Sec. 644.323 Neutral language.

Wherever the words ``man'', ``men'', or their related pronouns appear in this subpart, either as words or as parts of words (other than when referring to a specific individual), they have been used for literary purposes and are meant to include both female and male sexes.
