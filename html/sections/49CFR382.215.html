CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 382  /  Sec. 382.215 Controlled substances testing.

No driver shall report for duty, remain on duty or perform a safety- sensitive function, if the driver tests positive or has adulterated or substituted a test specimen for controlled substances. No employer having knowledge that a driver has tested positive or has adulterated or substituted a test specimen for controlled substances shall permit the driver to perform or continue to perform safety-sensitive functions. [66 FR 43103, Aug. 17, 2001, as amended at 77 FR 4483, Jan. 30, 2012]
