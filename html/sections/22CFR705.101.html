CFR /  Title 22  /  Part 705  /  Sec. 705.101 Cross-reference to employee ethical conduct standards and

Employees of the Overseas Private Investment Corporation (OPIC) should refer to the executive branch-wide Standards of Ethical Conduct at 5 CFR part 2635, the OPIC regulation at 5 CFR 4301.101 which supplements the executive branch-wide standards, and the executive branch-wide financial disclosure regulation at 5 CFR part 2634. [58 FR 33320, June 17, 1993]
