CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 447  /  Sec. 447.300 Basis and purpose.

In this subpart, Sec. Sec. 447.302 through 447.325 and 447.361 implement section 1902(a)(30) of the Act, which requires that payments be consistent with efficiency, economy and quality of care. Section 447.371 implements section 1902(a)(15) of the Act, which requires that the State plan provide for payment for rural health clinic services in accordance with regulations prescribed by the Secretary. [72 FR 39239, July 17, 2007]
