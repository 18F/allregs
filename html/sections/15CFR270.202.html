CFR /  Title 15  /  Part 270  /  Sec. 270.202 Coordination with search and rescue efforts.

NIST will coordinate its investigation with any search and rescue or search and recovery efforts being undertaken at the site of the building failure, including FEMA urban search and rescue teams, local emergency management agencies, and local emergency response groups. Upon arrival at a disaster site, the Lead Investigator will identify the lead of the search and rescue operations and will work closely with that person to ensure coordination of efforts. [68 FR 66704, Nov. 28, 2003, as amended at 69 FR 33571, June 16, 2004]
