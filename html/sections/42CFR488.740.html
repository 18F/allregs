CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 488  /  Sec. 488.740 Certification of compliance or noncompliance.

Rules to be followed for certification, documentation of findings, periodic review of compliance and approval, certification of noncompliance, and determining compliance of HHAs are set forth, respectively, in Sec. Sec. 488.12, 488.18, 488.20, 488.24, and 488.26 of this part.
