CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 3  /  Sec. 3.55-25 Sector Humboldt Bay Search and Rescue Mission

The Sector Humboldt Bay office is located in McKinleyville, CA. The boundaries of Sector Humboldt Bay's Search and Rescue Mission Coordinator Zone start in the north by a line bearing 264T from the coastal point of the Oregon-California border (42[deg]00.0[min] N./ 124[deg]13.0[min] W.), on the south by a line bearing 270T from the coastal point of the Mendecino-Sonoma County, CA, border (38[deg]47.0[min] N./123[deg]30.0[min] W.), and on the west by the outermost extent of the exclusive economic zone (EEZ). The inland Area of Responsibility (AOR) includes the entirety of the following California counties: Del Norte, Humboldt, Mendecino, Siskiyou, Trinity, Shasta, Tehama, Glenn, Lake, Colusa, Butte, Plumas, Lassen, and Modoc. [USCG-2013-0397, 78 FR 39170, July 1, 2013]
