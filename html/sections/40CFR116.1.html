CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 116  /  Sec. 116.1 Applicability.

This regulation designates hazardous substances under section 311(b)(2)(A) of the Federal Water Pollution Control Act (the Act). The regulation applies to discharges of substances designated in Table 116.4. [43 FR 10474, Mar. 13, 1978]
