CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 21  /  Sec. 21.9580 Counseling.

An individual may receive counseling from VA before beginning training and during training. VA will apply the provisions of Sec. 21.7100 to beneficiaries under 38 U.S.C. chapter 33 in the same manner as they are applied to individuals under 38 U.S.C. chapter 30. (Authority: 38 U.S.C. 3034(a), 3323(a), 3697A)
