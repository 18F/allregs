CFR /  Title 9  /  Part 381  /  Sec. 381.104 Official export certificates, marks and devices.

The form of certificate described in Sec. 381.106 is an official export certificate, and the mark shown below is the official mark used on outside containers to identify inspected and passed poultry products for export. Devices used by the Department to apply such a mark are official devices.[GRAPHIC] [TIFF OMITTED] TC11SE91.040 [47 FR 29823, July 9, 1982]
