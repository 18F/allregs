CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 250  /  Sec. 250.506 Crew instructions.

Prior to engaging in well-completion operations, crew members shall be instructed in the safety requirements of the operations to be performed, possible hazards to be encountered, and general safety considerations to protect personnel, equipment, and the environment. Date and time of safety meetings shall be recorded and available at the facility for review by BSEE representatives. Sec. Sec. 250.507-250.508 [Reserved]
