CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 1206  /  Sec. 1206.8 Providing information to the public.

Information available to the public under this part will be made available by the Office of the Clerk of the Board, U.S. Merit Systems Protection Board, 1615 M Street, NW., Washington, DC 20419. Individuals or organizations with a special interest in activities of the Board may ask the Office of the Clerk to have them placed on a mailing list for receipt of information available under this part. [54 FR 20367, May 11, 1989, as amended at 65 FR 48886, Aug. 10, 2000]
