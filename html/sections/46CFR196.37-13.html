CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 196  /  Sec. 196.37-13 Fire extinguishing system controls.

The control cabinets or spaces containing valves, manifolds, or controls for the various fire extinguishing systems must be marked in conspicuous red letters at least 2 inches high: ``[CARBON DIOXIDE/CLEAN AGENT/FOAM--as appropriate] FIRE APPARATUS.''. [USCG-2006-24797, 77 FR 33894, June 7, 2012]
