CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 668  /  Sec. 668.123 Collection.

To the extent that the decision of the Secretary sustains the final audit determination or program review determination, subject to the provisions of Sec. 668.24(c)(3), the Department of Education will take steps to collect the debt at issue or otherwise effect the determination that was subject to the request for review. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1094) [59 FR 22453, Apr. 29, 1994]
