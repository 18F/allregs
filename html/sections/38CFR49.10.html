CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 49  /  Sec. 49.10 Purpose.

Sections 49.11 through 49.17 prescribes forms and instructions and other pre-award matters to be used in applying for Federal awards. (Authority: Pub. L. 104-156; 110 Stat. 1396)
