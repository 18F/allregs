CFR /  Title 16  /  Part 423  /  Sec. 423.7 Certain piece goods.

This section applies to certain piece goods.

(a) Manufacturers and importers of certain piece goods must provide care information clearly and conspicuously on the end of each bolt or roll.

(b) Care information must say what regular care is needed for the ordinary use of the product, pursuant to the instructions set forth in Sec. 423.6. Care information on the end of the bolt need only address information applicable to the fabric.
