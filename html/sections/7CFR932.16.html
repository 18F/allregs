CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 932  /  Sec. 932.16 Handle.

Handle means to: (a) Size-grade olives, (b) process olives, or (c) use processed olives in the production of packaged olives, within the production area, or (d) ship packaged olives from the area to any point outside thereof or within the area: Provided, This term shall not include natural condition olives acquired and (1) used for olive oil, salt cured oil coated olives (also variously referred to as ``Greek Olives,'' ``Greek Style Olives,'' or ``Oil Cured Olives''), or Silician Style Olives, or (2) shipped to fresh market outlets. [36 FR 20356, Oct. 21, 1971]
