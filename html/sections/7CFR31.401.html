CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 31  /  Sec. 31.401 Cost of samples for wool grades.

(a) Complete set: $22 each, delivered to any destination within the United States and $24 each, delivered to any destination outside the United States.

(b) Individual sample: $2 each, delivered to any destination within the United States and $2.50 each, delivered to any destination outside the United States.
