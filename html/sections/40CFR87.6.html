CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 87  /  Sec. 87.6 Aircraft safety.

The provisions of this part will be revised if at any time the DOT Secretary determines that an emission standard cannot be met within the specified time without creating a hazard to aircraft safety. [77 FR 36381, June 18, 2012]
