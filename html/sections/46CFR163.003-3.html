CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 163  /  Sec. 163.003-3 ASTM standard.

The following standard of the American Society for Testing and Materials (ASTM) is incorporated by reference into this subpart: ASTM D 1435-94, Standard Practice for Outdoor Weathering of Plastics. You may obtain this standard from the Society at 100 Barr Harbor Drive, West Conshohocken, PA 19428-2959. [USCG-1999-5151, 64 FR 67185, Dec. 1, 1999]
