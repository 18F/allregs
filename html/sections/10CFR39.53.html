CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 39  /  Sec. 39.53 Energy compensation source.

The licensee may use an energy compensation source (ECS) which is contained within a logging tool, or other tool components, only if the ECS contains quantities of licensed material not exceeding 3.7 MBq [100 microcuries].

(a) For well logging applications with a surface casing for protecting fresh water aquifers, use of the ECS is only subject to the requirements of Sec. Sec. 39.35, 39.37 and 39.39.

(b) For well logging applications without a surface casing for protecting fresh water aquifers, use of the ECS is only subject to the requirements of Sec. Sec. 39.15, 39.35, 39.37, 39.39, 39.51, and 39.77. [65 FR 20345, Apr. 17, 2000]
