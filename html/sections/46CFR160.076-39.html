CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 160  /  Sec. 160.076-39 Marking.

Each inflatable PFD must be marked as specified in UL 1180 (incorporated by reference, see Sec. 160.076-11). At a minimum, all labels must include--

(a) Size information, as appropriate;

(b) The Coast Guard approval number;

(c) Manufacturer's contact information;

(d) Model name/number;

(e) Lot number, manufacturer date; and

(f) Any limitations or restrictions on approval or special instructions for use. [USCG-2013-0263, 79 FR 56500, Sept. 22, 2014]
