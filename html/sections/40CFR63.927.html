CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 63  /  Sec. 63.927 Recordkeeping requirements.

(a) Owners and operators that use Container Level 3 controls in accordance with the provisions of Sec. 63.924 of this subpart shall prepare and maintain the following records:

(1) Records for the most recent set of calculations and measurements performed by the owner or operator to verify that the enclosure meets the criteria of a permanent total enclosure as specified in ``Procedure T--Criteria for and Verification of a Permanent or Temporary Total Enclosure'' under 40 CFR 52.741, appendix B.

(2) Records required for the closed-vent system and control device in accordance with the requirements of Sec. 63.693 in 40 CFR part 63, Subpart DD--National Emission Standards for Hazardous Air Pollutants from Off-Site Waste and Recovery Operations.
