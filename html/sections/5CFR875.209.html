CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 875  /  Sec. 875.209 How do I demonstrate that I am eligible to apply for

(a) When you submit your application for coverage, you must make known your status as a member of an eligible group.

(b) If the Carrier finds that you misrepresented your eligibility status, the Carrier has the right to void your coverage and return to you any premiums you paid, without interest. The incontestability provisions in Sec. 875.408 do not apply to this section. [68 FR 5534, Feb. 4, 2003, as amended at 72 FR 12037, Mar. 15, 2007]
