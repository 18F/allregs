CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 102-118  /  Sec. 102-118.25 Does GSA still require my agency to submit its

GSA no longer requires your agency to submit its overall transportation policies for approval. However, as noted in Sec. 102-118.325, agencies must submit their prepayment audit plans for approval. In addition, GSA may from time to time request to examine your agency's transportation policies to verify the correct performance of the prepayment audit of your agency's transportation bills.
