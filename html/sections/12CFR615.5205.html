CFR /  Title 12  /  Part 615  /  Sec. 615.5205 Minimum permanent capital standards.

Each institution shall at all times maintain permanent capital at a level of at least 7 percent of its risk-adjusted asset base. [62 FR 4446, Jan. 30, 1997]
