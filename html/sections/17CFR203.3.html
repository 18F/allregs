CFR /  Title 17  /  Part 203  /  Sec. 203.3 Suspension and disbarment.

The provisions of Sec. 201.102(e) of this chapter (Rule 102(e) of the Commission's rules of practice) are hereby made specifically applicable to all investigations. [29 FR 3620, Mar. 21, 1964, as amended at 60 FR 32823, June 23, 1995]
