CFR /  Title 16  /  Part 801  /  Sec. 801.33 Consummation of an acquisition by acceptance of tendered

The acceptance for payment of any shares tendered in a tender offer is the consummation of an acquisition of those shares within the meaning of the act. [48 FR 34433, July 29, 1983]
