CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 30  /  Sec. 30.01-1 Purpose of regulations.

(a) The rules and regulations in this subchapter are prescribed for all tank vessels in accordance with the intent of the various statutes administered by the Coast Guard and to provide for a correct and uniform administration of the vessel inspection requirements applicable to tank vessels. [CGFR 68-32, 33 FR 5712, Apr. 12, 1968]
