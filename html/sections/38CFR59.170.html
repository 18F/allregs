CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 59  /  Sec. 59.170 Forms.

All forms required by this part are available on the internet at ``http://www.va.gov/forms/'' for VA Forms and at ``http://www.gsa.gov'' for Standard Forms, or at the Veterans Health Administration, Room 789, 810 Vermont Ave., NW., Washington, DC 20420. (Authority: 38 U.S.C. 101, 501, 1710, 1742, 8105, 8131-8137, Section 2, 3, 4, and 4a of the Architectural Barriers Act of 1968, as amended, Pub. L. 90-480, 42 U.S.C. 4151-4157) [73 FR 58880, Oct. 8, 2008]
