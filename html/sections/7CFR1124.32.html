CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 1124  /  Sec. 1124.32 Other reports.

In addition to the reports required pursuant to Sec. Sec. 1124.30 and 1124.31, each handler shall report any information the market administrator deems necessary to verify or establish each handler's obligation under the order.
