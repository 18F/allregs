CFR /  Title 17  /  Part 230  /  Sec. 230.507 Disqualifying provision relating to exemptions under Sec.

(a) No exemption under Sec. 230.505, Sec. 230.505 or Sec. 230.506 shall be available for an issuer if such issuer, any of its predecessors or affiliates have been subject to any order, judgment, or decree of any court of competent jurisdiction temporarily, preliminary or permanently enjoining such person for failure to comply with Sec. 230.503.

(b) Paragraph (a) of this section shall not apply if the Commission determines, upon a showing of good cause, that it is not necessary under the circumstances that the exemption be denied. [54 FR 11374, Mar. 20, 1989]
