CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 1510  /  Sec. 1510.1 Applicability and purpose.

This part prescribes a uniform fee to be paid by passengers of direct air carriers and foreign air carriers in air transportation, foreign air transportation, and intrastate air transportation originating at airports in the United States. [79 FR 35472, June 20, 2014]
