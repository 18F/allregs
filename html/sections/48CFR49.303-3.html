CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 49  /  49.303-3 Audit of settlement proposal.

The TCO shall submit the settlement proposal to the appropriate audit agency for review (see 49.107). However, if the settlement proposal is limited to an adjustment of fee, no referral to the audit agency is required. [48 FR 42447, Sept. 19, 1983. Redesignated at 61 FR 39221, July 26, 1996]
