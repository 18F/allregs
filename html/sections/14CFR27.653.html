CFR /  Title 14  /  Part 27  /  Sec. 27.653 Pressure venting and drainage of rotor blades.

(a) For each rotor blade--

(1) There must be means for venting the internal pressure of the blade;

(2) Drainage holes must be provided for the blade; and

(3) The blade must be designed to prevent water from becoming trapped in it.

(b) Paragraphs (a)(1) and (2) of this section does not apply to sealed rotor blades capable of withstanding the maximum pressure differentials expected in service. [Amdt. 27-2, 33 FR 963, Jan. 26, 1968]
