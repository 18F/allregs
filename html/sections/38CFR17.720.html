CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 17  /  Sec. 17.720 Payments under the grant.

Grantees are to be paid in accordance with the timeframes and manner set forth in the Notice of Fund Availability. (Authority: Sec. 307, Pub. L. 111-163; 38 U.S.C. 501)
