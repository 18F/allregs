CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 3507  /  Sec. 3507.16 Is there a fee or payment required with my application?

Yes. You must submit the first year's rental with your application according to the provisions in Sec. 3504.15 of this part. BLM will also charge a processing fee on a case-by-case basis as described in Sec. 3000.11 of this chapter. [70 FR 58877, Oct. 7, 2005]
