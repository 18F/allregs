CFR /  Title 50  /  Part 402  /  Sec. 402.48 Conference on proposed species or proposed critical habitat.

EPA may employ the procedures described in Sec. 402.10 to confer on any species proposed for listing or any habitat proposed for designation as critical habitat. For the purposes of Sec. 402.10(d), the procedures in Sec. 402.46 are a permissible form of formal consultation.
