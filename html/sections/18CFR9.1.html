CFR /  Title 18  /  Part 9  /  Sec. 9.1 Filing.

Any licensee desiring to transfer a license or rights thereunder granted, and the person, association, corporation, State, or municipality desiring to acquire the same, shall jointly or severally file an application for approval of such transfer and acquisition. Such application shall be verified, shall conform to Sec. 131.20 of this chapter, and shall be filed in accordance with Sec. 4.32 of this chapter. [Order 501, 39 FR 2267, Jan. 18, 1974, as amended by Order 2002, 68 FR 51139, Aug. 25, 2003]
