CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 291  /  Sec. 291.430 Elimination of lead-based paint hazards.

The Lead-Based Paint Poisoning Prevention Act (42 U.S.C. 4821-4846), the Residential Lead-Based Paint Hazard Reduction Act of 1992 (42 U.S.C. 4851-4856), and implementing regulations at part 35, subparts A, B, F, and R of this title, apply to activities covered by this subpart. [64 FR 50225, Sept. 15, 1999, as amended at 69 FR 34275, June 21, 2004]
