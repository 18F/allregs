CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 415  /  Sec. 415.150 Scope.

This subpart sets forth the rules governing payment for the services of physicians in teaching settings and the criteria for determining whether the payments are made as one of the following:

(a) Services to the hospital under the reasonable cost election in Secs. 415.160 through 415.164.

(b) Provider services through the direct GME payment mechanism in Secs. 413.75 through 413.83 of this chapter.

(c) Physician services to beneficiaries under the physician fee schedule as set forth in part 414 of this chapter. [60 FR 63178, Dec. 8, 1995, as amended at 70 FR 47490, Aug. 12, 2005]
