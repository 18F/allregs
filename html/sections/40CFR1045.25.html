CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 1045  /  Sec. 1045.25 How do the requirements related to evaporative emissions

(a) Engine manufacturers must provide the installation instructions required by Sec. 1045.130 to the ultimate purchasers of the engine. These instructions may be combined with the maintenance instructions required by Sec. 1045.125.

(b) Engines sold with attached fuel lines or installed fuel tanks must be covered by the appropriate certificates of conformity issued under 40 CFR part 1060.

(c) Fuel lines intended to be used with new engines and new portable marine fuel tanks must be certified to the applicable requirements of 40 CFR part 1060. Similarly, fuel tanks intended to be used with new enignes must be certified to the applicable requirements of 40 CFR part 1060.

(d) All persons installing engines certified under this part 1045 must follow the certifying manufacturer's emission-related installation instructions (see Sec. 1045.130 and 40 CFR 1068.105).
