CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 302-10  /  Sec. 302-10.202 Am I entitled to any other allowances when I transport

Yes, you are also entitled to the following allowances when you transport your mobile home by POV:

(a) Payment of mileage for use of a POV to transport yourself and/or immediate family member(s) as provided in Sec. 302-4.30 of this chapter; and

(b) Preparation costs as provided in Sec. 302-10.205.
