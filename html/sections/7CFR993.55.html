CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 993  /  Sec. 993.55 Application of salable and reserve percentages after

The salable and reserve percentages established for any crop year shall remain in effect after that crop year until salable and reserve percentages are established for another crop year. After such percentages are established, all reserve obligations shall be adjusted to the newly established percentages. [46 FR 61637, Dec. 18, 1981]

Effective Date Note: At 70 FR 30613, May 27, 2005, Sec. 993.55 was suspended indefinitely.
