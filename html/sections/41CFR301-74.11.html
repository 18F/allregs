CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 301-74  /  Sec. 301-74.11 What must be included in any advertisement or application

Any advertisement or application for attendance at a conference described in Sec. 301-74.9 must include notice of the prohibition against using a non-FEMA approved place of public accommodation for conferences. In addition, any executive agency, as defined in 5 U.S.C. 105, shall notify all non-Federal entities to which it provides Federal funds of this prohibition. [FTR Amdt. 2013-01, 78 FR 65212, Oct. 31, 2013]
