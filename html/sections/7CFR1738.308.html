CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 1738  /  Sec. 1738.308 Full faith and credit of the United States.

Loan guarantees made under this part are supported by the full faith and credit of the United States and are incontestable except for fraud or misrepresentation of which the holder had actual knowledge at the time it became a holder. Sec. Sec. 1738.309-1738.349 [Reserved]
