CFR /  Title 6  /  Part 37  /  Sec. 37.27 Driver's licenses and identification cards issued during

Driver's licenses and identification cards issued to individuals prior to a DHS determination that the State is materially compliant may be renewed or reissued pursuant to current State practices, and will be accepted for official purposes until the validity dates described in Sec. 37.5. [73 FR 5331, Jan. 29, 2008, as amended at 79 FR 77838, Dec. 29, 2014]
