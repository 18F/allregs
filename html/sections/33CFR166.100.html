CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 166  /  Sec. 166.100 Purpose.

The purpose of these regulations is to establish and designate shipping safety fairways and fairway anchorages to provide unobstructed approaches for vessels using U.S. ports. [CGD 81-80a, 48 FR 30110, June 30, 1983]
