CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 222  /  Sec. 222.10 How long must a local educational agency retain records?

An LEA must retain the records described in Sec. 222.9 until the later of--

(a) Three years after the last payment for a fiscal year; or

(b) If the records have been questioned on Federal audit or review, until the question is finally resolved and any necessary adjustments to payments have been made. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1232f, 7702, 7703, 7704, 7706)
