CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 602  /  Sec. 602.38 Contesting the Secretary's final decision to deny, limit,

An agency may contest the Secretary's decision under this part in the Federal courts as a final decision in accordance with applicable Federal law. Unless otherwise directed by the court, a decision of the Secretary to deny, limit, suspend, or terminate the agency's recognition is not stayed during an appeal in the Federal courts. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1099b)
