CFR /  Title 23  /  Part 1  /  Sec. 1.7 Urban area boundaries.

Boundaries of an urban area shall be submitted by the State highway department and be approved by the Administrator prior to the inclusion in a program of any project wholly or partly in such area involving funds authorized for and limited to urban areas. Sec. 1.8 [Reserved]
