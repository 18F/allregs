CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 2  /  Sec. 2.65 Where are records made available?

Records that are required by the FOIA to be made proactively available for public inspection and copying are accessible on the Department's Web site, http://www.doi.gov/foia/libraries.cfm. They may also be available at bureau office locations.
