CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 18b  /  Sec. 18b.30 Notice of hearing or opportunity for hearing.

Proceedings are commenced by mailing a notice of hearing or opportunity for hearing to an affected applicant or recipient, pursuant to Sec. Sec. 18.9 and 18a.5 of this chapter.
