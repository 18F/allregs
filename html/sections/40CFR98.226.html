In addition to the information required by Sec. 98.3(c), each annual report must contain the information specified in paragraphs (a) through (q) of this section.

(a) Nitric Acid train identification number.

(b) Annual process N2O emissions from each nitric acid train (metric tons).

(h) Abatement technologies used (if applicable).

(j) [Reserved]

(l) Number of times in the reporting year that missing data procedures were followed to measure nitric acid production (months).
