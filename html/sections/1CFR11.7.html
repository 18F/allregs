CFR /  Title 1  /  Part 11  /  Sec. 11.7 Federal Register Index.

The annual subscription price for the monthly Federal Register Index, purchased separately, in paper form, is $29. The price excludes postage. The prevailing postal rates will be applied to orders according to the delivery method requested. [69 FR 12783, Mar. 18, 2004]
