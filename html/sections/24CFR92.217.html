CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 92  /  Sec. 92.217 Income targeting: Homeownership.

Each participating jurisdiction must invest HOME funds made available during a fiscal year so that with respect to homeownership assistance, 100 percent of these funds are invested in dwelling units that are occupied by households that qualify as low-income families. [67 FR 61756, Oct. 1, 2002]
