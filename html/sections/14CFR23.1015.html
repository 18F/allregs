CFR /  Title 14  /  Part 23  /  Sec. 23.1015 Oil tank tests.

Each oil tank must be tested under Sec. 23.965, except that--

(a);

(b) For a tank with a nonmetallic liner the test fluid must be oil rather than fuel as specified in Sec. 23.965(d), and the slosh test on a specimen liner must be conducted with the oil at 250 [deg]F.; and

(c) For pressurized tanks used with a turbine engine, the test pressure may not be less than 5 p.s.i. plus the maximum operating pressure of the tank. [Doc. No. 4080, 29 FR 17955, Dec. 18, 1964, as amended by Amdt. 23-15, 39 FR 35460, Oct. 1, 1974]
