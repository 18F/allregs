CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 710  /  Sec. 710.23 Extensions of time by the Manager.

The Manager may, for good cause shown, at the written request of the individual, extend the time for filing a written request for a hearing, and/or the time for filing a written answer to the matters contained in the notification letter. The Manager shall notify the Director, Office of Personnel Security, DOE Headquarters, when such extensions have been approved. [59 FR 35185, July 8, 1994, as amended at 71 FR 68730, Nov. 28, 2006]
