CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 303  /  Sec. 303.733 Reallotment of funds.

If a State (as defined in Sec. 303.35) elects not to receive its allotment, the Secretary reallots those funds among the remaining States (as defined in Sec. 303.732(d)(3)), in accordance with Sec. 303.732(c)(2). (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1443(d))
