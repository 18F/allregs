CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 641  /  Sec. 641.610 How are pilot, demonstration, and evaluation projects

The Department may enter into agreements with States, public agencies, nonprofit private organizations, or private business concerns, as may be necessary, to conduct pilot, demonstration, and evaluation projects.
