CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 228  /  Sec. 228.335 Electronic recordkeeping.

(a) Each railroad shall keep records as required by Sec. 228.323 either--

(1) On paper forms provided by the railroad, or

(2) By electronic means that conform with the requirements of subpart D of this part.

(b) Records required to be kept shall be made available to the Federal Railroad Administration as provided by 49 U.S.C. 20107. Subpart F_Substantive Hours of Service Requirements for Train Employees

Engaged in Commuter or Intercity Rail Passenger Transportation

Source: 76 FR 50397, Aug. 12, 2011, unless otherwise noted.
