CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 223  /  223.7203 Contract clause.

Use the clause at 252.223-7007, Safeguarding Sensitive Conventional Arms, Ammunition, and Explosives, in all solicitations and contracts to which DoD 5100.76-M applies, in accordance with the policy at 223.7201. Complete paragraph (b) of the clause based on information provided by cognizant technical or requirements personnel. [61 FR 7743, Feb. 29, 1996; 61 FR 18195, Apr. 24, 1996]
