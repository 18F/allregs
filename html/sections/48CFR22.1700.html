CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 22  /  22.1700 Scope of subpart.

This subpart prescribes policy for implementing 22 U.S.C. chapter 78 and Executive Order 13627, Strengthening Protections Against Trafficking in Persons in Federal Contracts, dated September 25, 2012. [80 FR 4987, Jan. 29, 2015]
