CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 61  /  Sec. 61.104 Sampling and certification of samples and grades.

The drawing, preparation, and certification of samples of cottonseed, and certification of grades of cottonseed shall be performed in accordance with methods approved from time to time for the purposes by the Director, or his representatives. [22 FR 10948, Dec. 28, 1957, as amended at 58 FR 42413, Aug. 9, 1993]
