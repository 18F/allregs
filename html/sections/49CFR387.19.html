CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 387  /  Sec. 387.19 Electronic filing of surety bonds, trust fund agreements,

(a) Insurers of exempt for-hire motor carriers, as defined in Sec. 390.5 of this subchapter, and private motor carriers that transport hazardous materials in interstate commerce must file certificates of insurance, surety bonds, and other securities and agreements with FMCSA electronically in accordance with the requirements and procedures set forth at Sec. 387.323.

(b) The requirements of this section do not apply to motor carriers excepted under Sec. 387.7(b)(3). [78 FR 52650, Aug. 23, 2013]

Effective Date Note: At 78 FR 52650, Aug. 23, 2013, Sec. 387.19 was added, effective Oct. 23, 2015.
