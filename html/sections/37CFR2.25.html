CFR /  Title 37  /  Part 2  /  Sec. 2.25 Documents not returnable.

Except as provided in Sec. 2.27(e), documents filed in the Office by the applicant or registrant become part of the official record and will not be returned or removed. [73 FR 67768, Nov. 17, 2008]
