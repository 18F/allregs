CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 307  /  Sec. 307.25 Review and certification of computerized support

The Office will review, assess and inspect the planning, design, development, installation, enhancement and operation of computerized support enforcement systems developed under Sec. 307.10, or Sec. 307.11 to determine the extent to which such systems:

(a) Meet the requirements found in Sec. 307.15; and

(b) Can be certified as meeting the requirements described in Sec. 307.10 and in the OCSE guideline entitled ``Automated Systems for Child Support Enforcement: A Guide for States''. [57 FR 47005, Oct. 14, 1992, as amended at 63 FR 44817, Aug. 21, 1998]
