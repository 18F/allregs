CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 69  /  Sec. 69.413 High cost loop support universal service fund expenses.

Beginning April 1, 1989, expenses allocated to the interstate jurisdiction pursuant to Sec. Sec. 54.1310 and 36.641 of this chapter shall be assigned to the Universal Service Fund Element. [79 FR 39193, July 9, 2014]
