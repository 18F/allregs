CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 31  /  Sec. 31.40-30 Safety Management Certificate--T/ALL.

All tankships to which 33 CFR part 96 applies on an international voyage must have a valid Safety Management Certificate and a copy of their company's valid Document of Compliance certificate on board. [CGD 95-073, 62 FR 67514, Dec. 24, 1997]
