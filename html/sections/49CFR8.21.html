CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 8  /  Sec. 8.21 Burden of proof.

For the purpose of determinations to be made under Sec. Sec. 8.13, 8.15, and 8.17, the burden of proof is on the originating Departmental agency to show that continued classification is warranted.
