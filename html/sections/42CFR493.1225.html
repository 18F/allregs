CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 493  /  Sec. 493.1225 Condition: Clinical cytogenetics.

If the laboratory provides services in the specialty of Clinical cytogenetics, the laboratory must meet the requirements specified in Sec. Sec. 493.1230 through 493.1256, Sec. 493.1276, and Sec. Sec. 493.1281 through 493.1299.
