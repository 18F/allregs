CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 429  /  Sec. 429.124 Election of procedures.

(a) In responding to a notice of proposed civil penalty, the respondent may request:

(1) An administrative hearing before an Administrative Law Judge (ALJ) under Sec. 429.126 of this part; or

(2) Elect to have the procedures of Sec. 429.128 apply.

(b) Any election to have the procedures of Sec. 429.128 apply may not be revoked except with the consent of the General Counsel (or delegee).

(c) If the respondent fails to respond to a notice issued under Sec. 429.120 or otherwise fails to indicate its election of procedures, DOE shall refer the civil penalty action to an ALJ for a hearing under Sec. 429.126.
