CFR /  Title 25  /  Part 30  /  Sec. 30.115 Which students' performance data must be included for

The performance data of all students assessed pursuant to Sec. 30.114 must be included for purposes of AYP if the student is enrolled in a Bureau-funded school for a full academic year as defined by the Secretary or by an approved alternative definition of AYP.
