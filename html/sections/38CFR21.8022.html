CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 21  /  Sec. 21.8022 Entry and reentry.

(a) Date of program entry. VA may not enter a child into a vocational training program or provide an evaluation or any training, services, or assistance under this subpart before the date VA first receives an application for a vocational training program filed in accordance with Sec. 21.8014. (Authority: 38 U.S.C. 1151 note, 1804, 1811, 1811 note, 1812, 1814)

(b) Reentry. If an eligible child interrupts or ends pursuit of a vocational training program and VA subsequently allows the child to reenter the program, the date of reentrance will accord with the facts, but may not precede the date VA receives an application for the reentrance. (Authority: 38 U.S.C. 1804, 1814, 1832) [67 FR 72565, Dec. 6, 2002, as amended at 76 FR 4250, Jan. 25, 2011]
