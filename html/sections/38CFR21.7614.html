CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 21  /  Sec. 21.7614 Changes of program.

In determining whether a change of program of education may be approved for the payments of educational assistance, VA will apply Sec. 21.4234 of this part. (Authority: 10 U.S.C. 16136(b), 38 U.S.C. 3691; Pub. L. 98-525, Pub. L. 101-366) (June 1, 1991) [58 FR 50846, Sept. 29, 1993]
