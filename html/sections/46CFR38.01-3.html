CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 38  /  Sec. 38.01-3 Incorporation by reference.

(a). To enforce any edition other than the ones listed in paragraph (b) of this section, notice of change must be published in the Federal Register and the material made available to the public. All approved material is on file at the National Archives and Records Administration (NARA). For information on the availability of this material at NARA, call 202-741- 6030, or go to: http://www.archives.gov/federal_register/ code_of_federal_regulations/ibr_locations.html. All material is available from the sources indicated in paragraph (b) of this section.
