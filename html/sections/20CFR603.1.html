CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 603  /  Sec. 603.1 What are the purpose and scope of this part?

The purpose of this part is to implement the requirements of Federal UC law concerning confidentiality and disclosure of UC information. This part applies to States and State UC agencies, as defined in Sec. 603.2(f) and (g).
