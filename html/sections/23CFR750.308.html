CFR /  Title 23  /  Part 750  /  Sec. 750.308 Reports.

Periodic reports on site acquisitions and actual sign removals shall be submitted on FHWA Form 1424 and as prescribed. \1\---------------------------------------------------------------------------

\1\ Forms are available at FHWA Division Offices located in each State. [39 FR 27436, July 29, 1974, as amended at 41 FR 9321, Mar. 4, 1976]
