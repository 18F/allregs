CFR /  Title 31  /  Part 1020  /  Sec. 1020.200 General.

Banks are subject to the program requirements set forth and cross referenced in this subpart. Banks should also refer to subpart B of part 1010 of this chapter for program requirements contained in that subpart which apply to banks.
