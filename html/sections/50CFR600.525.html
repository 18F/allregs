CFR /  Title 50  /  Part 600  /  Sec. 600.525 Applicability of subpart F to Canadian Albacore Fishing

Fishing by vessels of Canada under the 1981 Treaty Between the Government of the United States of America and the Government of Canada on Pacific Coast Albacore Tuna Vessels and Port Privileges is regulated only under this section and Sec. 600.530 of this subpart F, and is exempt from any other requirements of this subpart F. Regulations governing fishing by U.S. vessels in waters under the fisheries jurisdiction of the Canada more than 12 nautical miles from the baseline from which the territorial sea is measured are found at Secs. 300.170-300.176 of chapter II of this title. [69 FR 31535, June 4, 2004]
