CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 1  /  Sec. 1.725 Cross-complaints and counterclaims.

Cross-complaints seeking any relief within the jurisdiction of the Commission against any carrier that is a party (complainant or defendant) to that proceeding are expressly prohibited. Any claim that might otherwise meet the requirements of a cross-complaint may be filed as a separate complaint in accordance with Secs. 1.720 through 1.736. For purposes of this subpart, the term ``cross-complaint'' shall include counterclaims. [63 FR 1037, Jan. 7, 1998]
