CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 301-10  /  Sec. 301-10.263 What travel authorization documents must I present to

You must present to the aircraft management office that operates the Government aircraft--

(a) A copy of your written travel authorization, including a blanket travel authorization, if applicable, approved in accordance with Sec. 301-10.262; and

(b) Valid picture identification, such as a Government identification card or a state-issued driver's license. [FTR Amdt. 2004-02, 69 FR 34304, June 21, 2004]
