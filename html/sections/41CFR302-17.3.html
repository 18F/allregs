CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 302-17  /  Sec. 302-17.3 What is the Government's objective in reimbursing the

The Government's objective is to reimburse transferred employees for substantially all (not exactly all--see Sec. 302-17.4) of the additional Federal, state, and local income taxes incurred as a result of a relocation, including the taxes on the taxable relocation benefits and the taxes on the reimbursement for taxes.
