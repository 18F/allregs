CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 62  /  Sec. 62.61 Procedural error.

If an applicant would have been selected but for a procedural error committed by VA, VA may select that applicant for funding when sufficient funds become available if there is no material change in the information that would have resulted in the applicant's selection. A new application will not be required for this purpose. (Authority: 38 U.S.C. 501, 2044)
