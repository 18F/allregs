CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 182  /  Sec. 182.70 Substances migrating from cotton and cotton fabrics

Substances migrating to food from cotton and cotton fabrics used in dry food packaging that are generally recognized as safe for their intended use, within the meaning of section 409 of the Act, are as follows: Beef tallow.Carboxymethylcellulose.Coconut oil, refined.Cornstarch.Gelatin.Lard.Lard oil.Oleic acid.Peanut oil.Potato starch.Sodium acetate.Sodium chloride. Sodium silicate.Sodium tripolyphosphate.Soybean oil (hydrogenated).Talc.Tallow (hydrogenated).Tallow flakes.Tapioca starch.Tetrasodium pyrophosphate.Wheat starch.Zinc chloride.
