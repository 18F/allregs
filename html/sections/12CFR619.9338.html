CFR /  Title 12  /  Part 619  /  Sec. 619.9338 Unincorporated business entities.

An Unincorporated Business Entity means a Limited Partnership (LP), Limited Liability Partnership (LLP), Limited Liability Limited Partnership (LLLP), Limited Liability Company (LLC), Business or other Trust Entity (TE), or other business entity established and maintained under State law that is not incorporated under any law or chartered under Federal law. [78 FR 31834, May 28, 2013]
