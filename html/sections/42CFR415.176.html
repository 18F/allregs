CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 415  /  Sec. 415.176 Renal dialysis services.

In the case of renal dialysis services, physicians who are not paid under the physician monthly capitation payment method (as described in Sec. 414.314 of this chapter) must meet the requirements of Secs. 415.170 and 415.172 (concerning physician fee schedule payment for services of teaching physicians).
