CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 264  /  Sec. 264.351 Closure.

At closure the owner or operator must remove all hazardous waste and hazardous waste residues (including, but not limited to, ash, scrubber waters, and scrubber sludges) from the incinerator site. [Comment: At closure, as throughout the operating period, unless the owner or operator can demonstrate, in accordance with Sec. 261.3(d) of this chapter, that the residue removed from the incinerator is not a hazardous waste, the owner or operator becomes a generator of hazardous waste and must manage it in accordance with applicable requirements of parts 262 through 266 of this chapter.] [46 FR 7678, Jan. 23, 1981] Subparts P-R [Reserved]
