CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 652  /  Sec. 652.208 How are core services and intensive services related to

652.207(b)(2)?

Core services and intensive services may be delivered through any of the applicable three methods of service delivery described in Sec. 652.207(b)(2). These methods are:

(a) Self-service;

(b) Facilitated self-help service; and

(c) Staff-assisted service.
