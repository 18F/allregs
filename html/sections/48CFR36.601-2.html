CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 36  /  36.601-2 Competition.

Acquisition of architect-engineer services in accordance with the procedures in this subpart will constitute a competitive procedure. (See 6.102(d)(1).) [56 FR 29128, June 25, 1991]
