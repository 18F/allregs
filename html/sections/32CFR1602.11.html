CFR /  Title 32  /  Part 1602  /  Sec. 1602.11 District appeal board.

A district appeal board or a panel thereof of the Selective Service System is a group of not less than three civilian members appointed by the President to act on cases of registrants in accordance with the provisions of parts 1651 and 1656 of this chapter. [69 FR 20543, Apr. 16, 2004]
