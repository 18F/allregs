CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 668  /  Sec. 668.900 Does WIA provide regulatory and/or statutory

Yes, WIA section 166(h)(3) permits waivers of any statutory or regulatory requirement imposed upon INA grantees (except for the areas cited in Sec. 668.920). Such waivers may include those necessary to facilitate WIA support of long term community development goals.
