CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 431  /  Sec. 431.156 Energy and water conservation standards and effective dates.

(a) Each commercial clothes washer manufactured between January 1, 2007, and January 8, 2013, shall have--

(1) A modified energy factor of at least 1.26; and

(2) A water consumption factor of not more than 9.5.
