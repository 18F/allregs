CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 160  /  Sec. 160.88 Permitted use of words ``turpentine'' and ``rosin.''

The use of the word ``turpentine'' or the word ``rosin'' is not prohibited in the name of an article made, prepared, or processed from spirits of turpentine or rosin, or to indicate the process whereby such article was made or prepared: Provided, That this section shall not apply to any article covered by Sec. 160.87.
