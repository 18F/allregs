CFR /  Title 13  /  Part 107  /  Sec. 107.40 How to read this part 107.

(a) Center Headings. All references in this part to SBA forms, and instructions for their preparation, are to the current issue of such forms. Center headings are descriptive and are used for convenience only. They have no regulatory effect.

(b) Capitalizing defined terms. Terms defined in Sec. 107.50 are capitalized in this part 107.

(c) The pronoun ``you'' as used in this part 107 means a Licensee or license applicant, as appropriate, unless otherwise noted.
