CFR /  Title 32  /  Part 989  /  Sec. 989.12 AF Form 813, Request for Environmental Impact Analysis.

The Air Force uses AF Form 813 to document the need for environmental analysis or for certain CATEX determinations for proposed actions. The form helps narrow and focus the issues to potential environmental impacts. AF Form 813 must be retained with the EA or EIS to record the focusing of environmental issues. [64 FR 38129, July 15, 1999, as amended at 66 FR 16868, Mar. 28, 2001]
