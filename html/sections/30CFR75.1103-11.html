CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 75  /  Sec. 75.1103-11 Tests of fire hydrants and fire hose; record of tests.

Each fire hydrant shall be tested by opening to insure that it is in operating condition, and each fire hose shall be tested, at intervals not exceeding 1 year. A record of these tests shall be maintained at an appropriate location. [37 FR 16546, Aug. 16, 1972]
