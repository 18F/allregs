CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 71  /  Sec. 71.61 Special requirements for Type B packages containing more

A Type B package containing more than 105A2 must be designed so that its undamaged containment system can withstand an external water pressure of 2 MPa (290 psi) for a period of not less than 1 hour without collapse, buckling, or inleakage of water. [69 FR 3795, Jan. 26, 2004]
