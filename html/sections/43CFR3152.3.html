CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 3152  /  Sec. 3152.3 Renewal of exploration permit.

Upon application by the permittee and payment of the filing fee for renewal of exploration permit--Alaska, found in the fee schedule in section 3000.12 of this chapter (except where the exploration operations are to be conducted on a leasehold by or on behalf of the lessee), an exploration permit may be renewed for a period not to exceed one year. [72 FR 50887, Sept. 5, 2007]
