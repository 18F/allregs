CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 69  /  Sec. 69.414 Lifeline assistance expenses.

Expenses allocated to the interstate jurisdiction pursuant to Sec. 36.741 shall be assigned to the Carrier Common Line element until March 31, 1989. Beginning April 1, 1989, such expenses shall be assigned to the Lifeline Assistance element.
