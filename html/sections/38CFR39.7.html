CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 39  /  Sec. 39.7 Line item adjustment to grants.

After a grant has been awarded, upon request from the State or Tribal Organization representative, VA may approve a change in one or more line items (line items are identified in Standard Form 424C) of up to 10 percent (increase or decrease) of the cost of each line item if the change would be within the scope or objective of the project and the aggregate adjustments would not increase the total amount of the grant. (Authority: 38 U.S.C. 501, 2408) [77 FR 4473, Jan. 30, 2012]
