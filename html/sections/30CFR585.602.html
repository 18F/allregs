CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 585  /  Sec. 585.602 What records must I maintain?

Until BOEM releases your financial assurance under Sec. 585.534, you must maintain and provide to BOEM, upon request, all data and information related to compliance with required terms and conditions of your SAP, COP, or GAP. Sec. Sec. 585.603-585.604 [Reserved]
