CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 51  /  Sec. 51.85 Draft environmental impact statement--rulemaking.

Except as the context may otherwise require, procedures and measures similar to those described in Sec. Sec. 51.70, 51.71, 51.72 and 51.73 will be followed in proceedings for rulemaking for which the Commission has determined to prepare an environmental impact statement.
