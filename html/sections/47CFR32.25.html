CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 32  /  Sec. 32.25 Unusual items and contingent liabilities.

Extraordinary items, prior period adjustments, and contingent liabilities may be recorded in the company's books of account without prior Commission approval. [65 FR 16334, Mar. 28, 2000]
