CFR /  Title 19  /  Part 145  /  Sec. 145.31 Importations not over $200 in value.

The port director will pass free of duty and tax, without preparing an entry as provided for in Sec. 145.12, packages containing merchandise having an aggregate fair retail value in the country of shipment of not over $200, subject to the requirements set forth in Secs. 10.151 and 10.153 of this chapter. [T.D. 94-51, 59 FR 30296, June 13, 1994, as amended by CBP Dec. 12-19, 77 FR 72721, Dec. 6, 2012]
