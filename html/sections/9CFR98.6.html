CFR /  Title 9  /  Part 98  /  Sec. 98.6 Ports of entry.

An embryo shall not be imported into the United States unless at a port of entry listed in Sec. 93.303 for horses, Sec. 93.403 for ruminants, or Sec. 93.503 for swine of this chapter. [50 FR 43563, Oct. 25, 1985, as amended at 55 FR 31558, Aug. 2, 1990; 62 FR 56025, Oct. 28, 1997]
