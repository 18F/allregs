CFR /  Title 44  /  Part 6  /  Sec. 6.80 Records available at fee.

The system manager shall provide a copy of a record to a requestor at a fee prescribed in Sec. 6.85 unless the fee is waived under Sec. 6.82. [44 FR 50293, Aug. 27, 1979, as amended at 45 FR 17152, Mar. 18, 1980]
