CFR /  Title 36  /  Part 2  /  Sec. 2.33 Report of injury or damage.

(a) A person involved in an incident resulting in personal injury or property damage exceeding $300, other than an accident reportable under Sec. Sec. 3.4 or 4.4 of this chapter, shall report the incident to the superintendent as soon as possible. This notification does not satisfy reporting requirements imposed by applicable State law.

(b) Failure to report an incident in accordance with paragraph (a) of this section is prohibited. [48 FR 30282, June 30, 1983, as amended at 52 FR 10683, Apr. 2, 1987]
