CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 847  /  Sec. 847.503 Transfers from the CSR Fund.

For elections of NAFI retirement system coverage under Sec. 847.441, the amount under Sec. 847.504 will be transferred from the Fund using the procedures established under Sec. 847.506.
