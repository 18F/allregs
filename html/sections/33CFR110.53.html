CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 110  /  Sec. 110.53 Niantic, Conn.

Beginning on the shoreline at latitude 41[deg]18[min]25.3[sec], longitude 72[deg]12[min]16.3[sec]; thence to latitude 41[deg]18[min]23.3[sec], longitude 72[deg]12[min]11.6[sec]; thence to latitude 41[deg]18[min]50.7[sec], longitude 72[deg]11[min]51.5[sec]; thence to the shoreline at latitude 41[deg]18[min]56.5[sec], longitude 72[deg]12[min]05.6[sec]; thence along the shoreline to the point of beginning.

Note: This area is for public use, principally for vessels used for a recreational purpose. A temporary float or buoy for marking the location of the anchor of a vessel at anchor may be used. Fixed mooring piles or stakes are prohibited. [CGFR 69-24, 34 FR 6480, Apr. 15, 1969]
