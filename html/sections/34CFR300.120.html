CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 300  /  Sec. 300.120 Monitoring activities.

(a) The SEA must carry out activities to ensure that Sec. 300.114 is implemented by each public agency.

(b) If there is evidence that a public agency makes placements that are inconsistent with Sec. 300.114, the SEA must--

(1) Review the public agency's justification for its actions; and

(2) Assist in planning and implementing any necessary corrective action. (Approved by the Office of Management and Budget under control number 1820-0030) (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1412(a)(5))
