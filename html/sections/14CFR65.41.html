CFR /  Title 14  /  Part 65  /  Sec. 65.41 Skill requirements: Facility ratings.

Each applicant for a facility rating at an air traffic control tower must have passed a practical test on each item listed in Sec. 65.37 of this part that is applicable to each operating position at the control tower at which the rating is sought.
