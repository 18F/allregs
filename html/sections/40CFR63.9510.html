CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 63  /  Sec. 63.9510 By what date must I conduct my initial compliance

(a) If you use a solvent recovery system and/or solvent substitution, you must conduct your initial compliance demonstration within 7 calendar days after the compliance date that is specified for your source in Sec. 63.9495.

(b) If you use a control technique other than a solvent recovery system and/or solvent substitution, you must comply with the provisions in Sec. 63.9570.
