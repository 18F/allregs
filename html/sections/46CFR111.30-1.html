CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 111  /  Sec. 111.30-1 Location and installation.

Each switchboard must meet the location and installation requirements in section 8.2 of IEEE 45-2002 or IEC 60092-302 (both incorporated by reference; see 46 CFR 110.10-1), as applicable. [USCG-2003-16630, 73 FR 65196, Oct. 31, 2008]
