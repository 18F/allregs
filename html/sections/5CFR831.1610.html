CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 831  /  Sec. 831.1610 Review of decisions.

(a) The final decision of the agency head issued to an employee as the result of a request for determination filed under Sec. 831.1606 may be appealed to the Merit Systems Protection Board under procedures prescribed by the Board.

(b) The final decision of the agency head denying an individual coverage while serving in an approved secondary position because of failure to meet the conditions in Sec. 831.1604(a) may be appealed to the Merit Systems Protection Board under procedures prescribed by the Board.
