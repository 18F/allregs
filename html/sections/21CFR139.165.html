CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 139  /  Sec. 139.165 Enriched vegetable noodle products.

(a), (g), (h), and (i), and in addition is enriched to meet the requirements prescribed for enriched noodle products by Sec. 139.155 and, except as hereinafter provided, contains a vegetable ingredient in compliance with the requirements prescribed for vegetable noodle products by Sec. 139.160. Because they are apt to impart an egg-yolk color, carrots are not used in enriched vegetable noodle products.

(b), (c), (d), or (e). The blank in each instance is filled in with the name of the vegetable used, as specified in Sec. 139.160(a). [42 FR 14409, Mar. 15, 1977, as amended at 58 FR 2879, Jan. 6, 1993]
