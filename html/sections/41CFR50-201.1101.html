CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 50-201  /  Sec. 50-201.1101 Minimum wages.

Determinations of prevailing minimum wages or changes therein will be published in the Federal Register by the Wage and Hour Division, ESA, of the Department of Labor. [52 FR 6147, Mar. 2, 1987]
