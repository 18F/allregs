CFR /  Title 31  /  Part 50  /  Sec. 50.14 Separate line item.

An insurer is deemed to be in compliance with the requirement of providing disclosure on a ``separate line item in the policy'' under Sec. 50.10(d) if the insurer makes the disclosure:

(a) On the declarations page of the policy;

(b) Elsewhere within the policy itself; or

(c) In any rider or endorsement, or other document that is made a part of the policy. [68 FR 59727, Oct. 17, 2003]
