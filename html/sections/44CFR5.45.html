CFR /  Title 44  /  Part 5  /  Sec. 5.45 Form of payment.

Payment shall be by check or money order payable to the Federal Emergency Management Agency and shall be addressed to the official designated by FEMA in correspondence with the requestor or to the Headquarters FOIA Officer or Regional FOIA Officer, as appropriate. [44 FR 50287, Aug. 27, 1979, as amended at 48 FR 44542, Sept. 29, 1983]
