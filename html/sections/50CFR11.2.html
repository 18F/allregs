CFR /  Title 50  /  Part 11  /  Sec. 11.2 Scope of regulations.

The regulations contained in this part apply only to actions arising under the following laws and regulations issued thereunder: Lacey Act, 18 U.S.C. 43;Lacey Act Amendments of 1981, 16 U.S.C. 3371 et seq.;Bald Eagle Protection Act, 16 U.S.C. 668-668d;Endangered Species Act of 1973, 87 Stat. 884, 16 U.S.C. 1531 et seq.; and Marine Mammal Protection Act of 1972, 16 U.S.C. 1361-1384 and 1401-1407. [47 FR 56860, Dec. 21, 1982]
