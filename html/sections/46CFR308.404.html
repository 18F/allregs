CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 308  /  Sec. 308.404 Application for insurance.

Application for insurance shall be made to ``Maritime Administration, Attention: Chief, Division of Marine Insurance'' at the address in Sec. 308.2(d). The applications shall be signed by all parties to be named as assureds, unless they have filed with the Chief, Division of Marine Insurance, written designations of a broker or brokers to act for them, in which case the applications may be signed by such broker or brokers.
