CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 1940  /  Sec. 1940.969 Forms, exhibits, and subparts.

Forms, exhibits, and subparts of this chapter (all available in any FmHA or its successor agency under Public Law 103-354 office) referenced in this subpart, are for use in establishing a State economic development review panel and for administering the Panel Grant program associated with the panel.
