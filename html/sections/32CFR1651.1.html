CFR /  Title 32  /  Part 1651  /  Sec. 1651.1 Who may appeal to a district appeal board.

(a) The Director of Selective Service may appeal from any determination of a local board when he deems it necessary to assure the fair and equitable administration of the Selective Service Law: Provided, That, no such appeal will be taken after the expiration of the appeal period prescribed in Sec. 1651.2.

(b) The registrant may appeal to a district appeal board the denial of his claim for a judgmental classification by the local board. The registrant may appeal to a district appeal board the denial of his claim for an administrative classification by the local board whenever its decisions is not unanimous. [47 FR 4662, Feb. 1, 1982, as amended at 52 FR 24459, July 1, 1987]
