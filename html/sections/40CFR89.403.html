CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 89  /  Sec. 89.403 Symbols/abbreviations.

(a) The abbreviations in Sec. 86.094-3 or Sec. 89.3 of this chapter apply to this subpart.

(b) The abbreviations in Table 1 in appendix A to subpart D also apply to this subpart. Some abbreviations from Sec. 89.3 have been included for the convenience of the reader.

(c) The symbols in Table 2 in appendix A to subpart D apply to this subpart. [59 FR 31335, June 17, 1994. Redesignated at 63 FR 56996, Oct. 23, 1998]
