CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 236  /  Sec. 236.562 Minimum rail current required.

The minimum rail current required to restore the locomotive equipment of continuous inductive automatic train stop or train control device to normal condition or to obtain a proceed indication of automatic cab signal device (pick-up) shall be in accordance with specifications of the carrier. [49 FR 3387, Jan. 26, 1984]
