CFR /  Title 39  /  Part 954  /  Sec. 954.7 Failure to appeal proposed action.

A ruling of the authorized official shall become final upon failure of the publisher to file a petition in accordance with the requirements of Sec. 954.8(b). [36 FR 11567, June 16, 1971, as amended at 38 FR 17217, June 29, 1973]
