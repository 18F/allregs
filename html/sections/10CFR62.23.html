CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 62  /  Sec. 62.23 Determination for granting temporary emergency access.

(a) The Commission may grant temporary emergency access to an appropriate non-Federal or regional disposal facility or facilities provided that the determination required under Sec. 62.21(a)(1) of this part is made;

(b) The notification procedures under Sec. 62.22 of this part are complied with; and

(c) The temporary emergency access duration will not exceed forty-five (45) days.
