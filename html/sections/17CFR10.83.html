CFR /  Title 17  /  Part 10  /  Sec. 10.83 Oral arguments.

In his discretion the Administrative Law Judge may hear oral arguments by the parties any time before he files his initial decision with the Proceedings Clerk. The argument shall be recorded and transcribed in written form. [41 FR 2511, Jan. 16, 1976, as amended at 60 FR 54802, Oct. 26, 1995]
