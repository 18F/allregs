CFR /  Title 14  /  Part 221  /  Sec. 221.161 Notice of adoption to be filed in former carrier's

At the same time that the adoption notice is issued, posted, and filed pursuant to Sec. 221.160, the adopting carrier shall issue, post and file with the Department a notice in each effective tariff issued by the former carrier providing specific notice of the adoption in a manner authorized by the Office of International Aviation and which shall contain no matter other than that authorized.
