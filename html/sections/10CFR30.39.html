CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 30  /  Sec. 30.39 Commission action on applications to renew or amend.

In considering an application to renew or amend a license or to amend a sealed source or device registration certificate, the Commission will apply the applicable criteria set forth in Sec. 30.33 and parts 32 through 36 and 39 of this chapter. [77 FR 43690, July 25, 2012]
