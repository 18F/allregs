CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 150  /  Sec. 150.518 What are the inspection requirements for work vests

(a) All work vests and immersion suits must be inspected by the owner or operator pursuant to Sec. 150.105 of this part to determine whether they are in serviceable condition.

(b) If a work vest or immersion suit is inspected and is in serviceable condition, then it may remain in service. If not, then it must be removed from the deepwater port.
