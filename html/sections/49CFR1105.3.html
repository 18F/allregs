CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 1105  /  Sec. 1105.3 Information and assistance.

Information and assistance regarding the rules and the Board's environmental and historic review process is available by writing or calling the Section of Environmental Analysis, Surface Transportation Board, 1925 K Street, NW, Washington, DC 20423. [64 FR 53268, Oct. 1, 1999]
