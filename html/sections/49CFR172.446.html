CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 172  /  Sec. 172.446 CLASS 9 label.

(a) Except for size and color, the ``CLASS 9'' (miscellaneous hazardous materials) label must be as follows:[GRAPHIC] [TIFF OMITTED] TR28DE11.007

(b) In addition to complying with Sec. 172.407, the background on the CLASS 9 label must be white with seven black vertical stripes on the top half. The black vertical stripes must be spaced, so that, visually, they appear equal in width to the six white spaces between them. The lower half of the label must be white with the class number ``9'' underlined and centered at the bottom. The solid horizontal line dividing the lower and upper half of the label is optional.

(c) Labels conforming to requirements in place on August 18, 2011 may continue to be used until October 1, 2014. [Amdt. 172-123, 56 FR 66259, Dec. 20, 1991, as amended at 74 FR 2252, Jan. 14, 2009; 76 FR 43528, July 20, 2011; 76 FR 56314, Sept. 13, 2011; 76 FR 81400, Dec. 28, 2011]
