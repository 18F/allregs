CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 1777  /  Sec. 1777.31 Rates.

(a) Applicant loans will bear interest at the rate of 5 percent per annum.

(b) Individual loans will bear interest at the rate of:

(1) Five percent per annum; or

(2) The Federal Financing Bank rate for loans of a similar term at the time of Agency loan approval, whichever is less. Sec. Sec. 1777.32-1777.40 [Reserved]
