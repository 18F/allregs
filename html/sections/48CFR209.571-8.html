CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 209  /  209.571-8 Solicitation provision and contract clause.

(a) Use the provision at 252.209-7008, Notice of Prohibition Relating to Organizational Conflict of Interest--Major Defense Acquisition Program, if the solicitation includes the clause at 252.209-7009, Organizational Conflict of Interest--Major Defense Acquisition Program; and

(b) Use the clause at 252.209-7009, Organizational Conflict of Interest--Major Defense Acquisition Program, in solicitations and contracts for systems engineering and technical assistance for major defense acquisition programs or pre-major defense acquisition programs. [75 FR 81913, Dec. 29, 2010]
