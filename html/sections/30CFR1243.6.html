CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 1243  /  Sec. 1243.6 When must I or another person meet the bonding or

If you must meet the bonding or financial solvency requirements under Sec. 1243.4(a)(1), or if another person is meeting your bonding or financial solvency requirements, then either you or the other person must post a bond or other surety instrument or demonstrate financial solvency within 60 days after you receive the order or the Notice of Order.
