CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 413  /  Sec. 413.92 Costs of surety bonds.

Costs incurred by a provider to obtain a surety bond required by part 489, subpart F of this chapter are not included as allowable costs. [63 FR 310, Jan. 5, 1998]
