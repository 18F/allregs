CFR /  Title 37  /  Part 1  /  Sec. 1.925 Partial refund if request for inter partes

Where inter partes reexamination is not ordered, a refund of a portion of the fee for requesting inter partes reexamination will be made to the requester in accordance with Sec. 1.26(c).
