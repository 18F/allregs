CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 222  /  Sec. 222.119 What is the effect of withholding under this subpart?

(a) The withholding provisions in this subpart apply to all payments that an LEA is otherwise eligible to receive under section 8003 of the Act for any fiscal year.

(b) The Assistant Secretary withholds funds after completion of any administrative proceedings under Sec. Sec. 222.116-222.118 until the LEA documents either compliance or exemption from compliance with the requirements in section 8004 of the Act and this subpart. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 7704 (a), (b), (d)(2), (e) (8)-(9))
