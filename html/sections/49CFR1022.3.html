CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 1022  /  Sec. 1022.3 Civil monetary penalty inflation adjustment.

The Board shall, immediately, and at least once every four years thereafter--

(a) By regulation adjust each civil monetary penalty provided by law within the jurisdiction of the Board by the inflation adjustment described in Sec. 1022.4; and

(b) Publish each such adjustment in the Federal Register.
