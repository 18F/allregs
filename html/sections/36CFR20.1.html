CFR /  Title 36  /  Part 20  /  Sec. 20.1 Definitions.

As used in this part:

(a) Park means Isle Royale National Park.

(b) Permittee includes all persons engaged in commercial fishing from bases in the Park, except those life lessees who were engaged in such occupation at the date of the issuance of their leases. [24 FR 11055, Dec. 30, 1959, as amended at 60 FR 55791, Nov. 3, 1995; 62 FR 30235, June 3, 1997]
