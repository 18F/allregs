CFR /  Title 27  /  Part 28  /  Sec. 28.51 General.

Every person required by this part to file a bond or consent of surety must prepare and execute it on the prescribed form and file it in accordance with its instructions and the procedures of this part. The procedures in parts 19, 24 or 25 of this chapter govern bonds covering distilled spirits plants, bonded wine cellars and breweries, respectively. [T.D. ATF-477, 67 FR 18089, Apr. 15, 2002]
