CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 61  /  Sec. 61.20-21 Extension of examination interval.

The Commandant CG-CVC may authorize extensions of the interval between tailshaft examinations. [CGD 84-024, 52 FR 39652, Oct. 23, 1987, as amended by CGD 95-072, 60 FR 50463, Sept. 29, 1995; CGD 96-041, 61 FR 50728, Sept. 27, 1996; USCG- 2009-0702, 74 FR 49229, Sept. 25, 2009; USCG-2012-0832, 77 FR 59778, Oct. 1, 2012]
