CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 3604  /  Sec. 3604.25 What bond requirements pertain to free use permits?

BLM may require a bond or other security as a guarantee of your faithful compliance with the provisions of your permit and applicable regulations, including reclamation. The type of security must be one of those provided for in Sec. 3602.14(c) of this part.
