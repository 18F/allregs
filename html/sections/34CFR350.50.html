CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 350  /  Sec. 350.50 What is the peer review process for this Program?

(a) The Secretary refers each application for a grant governed by those regulations in this part to a peer review panel established by the Secretary.

(b) Peer review panels review applications on the basis of the applicable selection criteria in Sec. 350.54. (Authority: Sec. 202(e); 29 U.S.C. 761a(e))
