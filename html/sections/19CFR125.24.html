CFR /  Title 19  /  Part 125  /  Sec. 125.24 Failure of designated cartman, lighterman or other

The cartman, lighterman, other bonded carrier, foreign trade zone operator, bonded warehouse proprietor, container station operator or centralized examination station operator designated to convey the merchandise shall be present to take the merchandise when the Customs officer in charge is ready to send it. If the designated vehicle or lighter is not present, after waiting a reasonable time, such officer shall send the merchandise by any available licensed cartman, lighterman, or qualifying bonded carrier. [T.D. 94-81, 59 FR 51496, Oct. 12, 1994]
