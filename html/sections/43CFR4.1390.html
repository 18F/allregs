CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 4  /  Sec. 4.1390 Scope.

Sections 4.1391 through 4.1394 set forth the procedures for obtaining review of an OSM determination under 30 CFR 761.16 that a person does or does not have valid existing rights. [67 FR 61512, Oct. 1, 2002]
