CFR /  Title 28  /  Part 2  /  Sec. 2.58 Prior orders.

Any order of the United States Board of Parole entered prior to May 14, 1976, including, but not limited to, orders granting, denying, rescinding or revoking parole or mandatory release, shall be a valid order of the United States Parole Commission according to the terms stated in the order. [42 FR 39809, Aug. 5, 1977. Redesignated at 44 FR 26551, May 4, 1979]
