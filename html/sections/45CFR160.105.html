CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 160  /  Sec. 160.105 Compliance dates for implementation of new or

Except as otherwise provided, with respect to rules that adopt new standards and implementation specifications or modifications to standards and implementation specifications in this subchapter in accordance with Sec. 160.104 that become effective after January 25, 2013, covered entities and business associates must comply with the applicable new standards and implementation specifications, or modifications to standards and implementation specifications, no later than 180 days from the effective date of any such standards or implementation specifications. [78 FR 5689, Jan. 25, 2013]
