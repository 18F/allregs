CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 702  /  Sec. 702.236 Penalty for failure to report termination of payments.

Any employer failing to notify the district director that the final payment of compensation has been made as required by Sec. 702.235 shall be assessed a civil penalty in the amount of $100.00. Provided, however, that for any violation occurring on or after November 17, 1997 the civil penalty will be $110.00. The district director has the authority and responsibility for assessing a civil penalty under this section. [62 FR 53956, Oct. 17, 1997]
