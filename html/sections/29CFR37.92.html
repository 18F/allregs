CFR /  Title 29  /  Part 37  /  Sec. 37.92 What notice must the Director issue if he or she finds

If the Director determines that there is no reasonable cause to believe that a violation has taken place, he or she must issue a Final Determination under Sec. 37.100. The Final Determination represents the Department's final agency action on the complaint.
