CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 1229  /  Sec. 1229.111 Materials furnished to States necessary to perform delegation.

The ONRR shall provide to the State all reports, files, and supporting materials within its possession necessary to allow the State to effectively carry out the terms of the delegation specified in Sec. 1229.104. [49 FR 40026, Oct. 12, 1984]
