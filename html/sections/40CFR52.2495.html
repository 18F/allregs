CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 52  /  Sec. 52.2495 Voluntary limits on potential to emit

Terms and conditions of regulatory orders issued pursuant to WAC 173-400-091 ``Voluntary limits on emissions'' and in accordance with the provisions of WAC 173-400-091, WAC 173-400-105 ``Records, monitoring, and reporting,'' and WAC 173-400-171 ``Public involvement,'' shall be applicable requirements of the federally-approved Washington SIP and Section 112(l) program for the purposes of section 113 of the Clean Air Act and shall be enforceable by EPA and by any person in the same manner as other requirements of the SIP and Section 112(l) program. Regulatory orders issued pursuant to WAC 173-400-091 are part of the Washington SIP and shall be submitted to EPA Region 10 in accordance with the requirements of Sec. Sec. 51.104(e) and 51.326.
