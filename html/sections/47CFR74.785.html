CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 74  /  Sec. 74.785 Low power TV digital data service pilot project.

Low power TV stations authorized pursuant to the LPTV Digital Data Services Act (Public Law 106-554, 114 Stat. 4577, December 1, 2000) to participate in a digital data service pilot project shall be subject to the provisions of the Commission Order implementing that Act. FCC 01-137, adopted April 19, 2001, as modified by the Commission Order on Reconsideration, FCC 02-40, adopted February 12, 2002. [67 FR 9621, Mar. 4, 2002]
