CFR /  Title 9  /  Part 108  /  Sec. 108.1 Applicability.

Unless otherwise authorized by the Administrator, all buildings, appurtenances, and equipment used in the preparation of biological products shall be in compliance with the regulations in this part. Each land area on which such buildings and appurtenances are located shall be identified by an address which shall appear on the establishment license. [39 FR 16854, May 10, 1974, as amended at 56 FR 66783, Dec. 26, 1991]
