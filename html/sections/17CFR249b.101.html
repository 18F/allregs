CFR /  Title 17  /  Part 249b  /  Sec. 249b.101 Form TA-W, notice of withdrawal from registration as

This form shall be used for withdrawing, pursuant to section 17A of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, the registration of transfer agents registered with the Commission. (Secs. 2, 17, 17A and 23(a); (15 U.S.C. 78b, 78a, 78a-1 and 78w(a))) [42 FR 44984, Sept. 8, 1977]

Editorial Note: For Federal Register citations affecting Form TA-W, see the List of CFR Sections Affected, which appears in the Finding Aids section of the printed volume and at www.fdsys.gov.
