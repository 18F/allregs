CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 442  /  Sec. 442.110 Certification period for ICF/IID with standard-level deficiencies.

Facilities with standard-level deficiencies may be certified under Sec. 442.101 with a condition that the certification will continue if either of the following applies:

(a) The survey agency finds that all deficiencies have been satisfactorily corrected.

(b) The survey agency finds that the facility has made substantial progress in correcting the deficiencies and has a new plan of correction that is acceptable. [79 FR 27153, May 12, 2014]
