CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 414  /  Sec. 414.66 Pretreatment standards for new sources (PSNS).

Except as provided in 40 CFR 403.7 any new source subject to this subpart which introduces pollutants into a publicly owned treatment works must comply with 40 CFR part 403 and achieve discharges in accordance with Sec. 414.111. [58 FR 36892, July 9, 1993]
