CFR /  Title 50  /  Part 622  /  Sec. 622.494 Bag limit.

(a) Applicability. Section 622.11(a) provides the general applicability for bag and possession limits. However, Sec. 622.11(a)(1) notwithstanding, the bag limit of paragraph (b) of this section does not apply to a person who has a valid commercial fishing license issued by Puerto Rico or the U.S. Virgin Islands.

(b) Bag limit. The bag limit for queen conch in or from the Caribbean EEZ is 3 per person or, if more than 4 persons are aboard, 12 per boat.
