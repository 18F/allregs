CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 164  /  Sec. 164.122 Final order and order of suspension.

(a) Final order. Within 7 days of receipt of the record and of the Presiding Officer's recommended findings and conclusions, the Environmental Appeals Board shall issue a final decision and order. Such final order may accept or reject in whole or in part the recommendations of the Presiding Officer.

(b) Order of suspension. No final order of suspension shall be issued unless the Environmental Appeals Board has issued or at the same time issues a notice of its intention to cancel the registration or change the classification of the pesticide. Such notice shall be given as provided in Sec. 164.8. [38 FR 19371, July 20, 1973, as amended at 57 FR 5344, Feb. 13, 1992]
