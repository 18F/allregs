CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 25  /  Sec. 25.251 Special requirements for coordination.

(a) The administrative aspects of the coordination process are set forth in Sec. 101.103 of this chapter in the case of coordination of terrestrial stations with earth stations, and in Sec. 25.203 in the case of coordination of earth stations with terrestrial stations.

(b) The technical aspects of coordination are based on Appendix 7 of the International Telecommunication Union Radio Regulations and certain recommendations of the ITU Radiocommunication Sector (available at the address in Sec. 0.445 of this chapter).
