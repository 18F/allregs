CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 87  /  Sec. 87.17 Scope.

Part 1 of the Commission's rules contains the general rules of practice and procedure applicable to proceedings before the Commission and for the filing of applications for radio station licenses in the aviation services. Specific guidance for each type of radio service license in aviation services is set forth in this part. [63 FR 68957, Dec. 14, 1998]
