CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 180  /  Sec. 180.1322 Bacillus pumilus strain BU F-33; exemption from the

An exemption from the requirement of a tolerance is established for residues of Bacillus pumilus strain BU F-33 in or on all food commodities when applied to elicit induced systemic resistance in plants and used in accordance with label directions and good agricultural practices. [78 FR 35149, June 12, 2013]
