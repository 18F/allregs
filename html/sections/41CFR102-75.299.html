CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 102-75  /  Sec. 102-75.299 What are landholding agencies' responsibilities

Landholding agencies are and remain responsible for all rental/lease payments until the lease expires or is terminated. Landholding agencies are responsible for paying any restoration or other direct costs incurred by the Government associated with termination of a lease, and for paying any demolition and removal costs not offset by the sale of the property. (See also Sec. 102-75.965.)
