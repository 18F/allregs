CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 179  /  Sec. 179.500-4 Thickness of wall.

(a) Minimum thickness of wall of each finished tank shall be such that at a pressure equal to \7/10\ of the marked test pressure of the tank, the calculated fiber stress in psi at inner wall of tank multiplied by 3.0 will not exceed the tensile strength of any specimen taken from the tank and tested as prescribed in Sec. 179.500-7(b). Minimum wall thickness shall be \1/4\ inch.

(b) Calculations to determine the maximum marked test pressure permitted to be marked on the tank shall be made by the formula: P = [10S(D\2\ - d\2\)] / [7(D\2\ + d\2\)] Where: P = Maximum marked test pressure permitted; S = U / 3.0 Where: U = Tensile strength of that specimen which shows the lower tensile

strength of the two specimens taken from the tank and tested

by the operations specified in Sec. 179.500-4(c).
