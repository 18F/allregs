(b) Section 18 emergency exemptions. [Reserved]

(c) Tolerances with regional registrations. A tolerance with regional registration, as defined in Sec. 180.1(m), of 0.1 part per million is established for residues of the plant regulator ethephon [(2- chloroethyl)phosphonic acid] in or on the food commodity sugarcane.
