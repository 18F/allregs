CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 25  /  Sec. 25.404 Submission of down payment and filing of long-form

A high bidder that meets its down payment obligations in a timely manner must, within thirty (30) business days after being notified that it is a high bidder, submit an amendment to its pending application to provide the information required by Sec. 25.144.
