CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 993  /  Sec. 993.108 Non-human consumption outlet.

Non-human consumption outlet means any livestock feeder or manufacturer of inedible syrup, industrial alcohol, animal feed, or other product for non-human use, who has established, to the satisfaction of the committee, that any prunes or prune waste received for a non-human use will be used only within such outlet. [26 FR 8278, Sept. 2, 1961; 26 FR 8483, Sept. 9, 1961]

Effective Date Note: At 70 FR 30613, May 27, 2005, Sec. 993.108 was suspended indefinitely.
