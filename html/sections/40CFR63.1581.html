CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 63  /  Sec. 63.1581 Does the subpart distinguish between different types

Yes, POTW treatment plants are divided into two subcategories. A POTW treatment plant which does not meet the characteristics of an industrial POTW treatment plant belongs in the non-industrial POTW treatment plant subcategory as defined in Sec. 63.1595.
