CFR /  Title 16  /  Part 803  /  Sec. 803.21 Additional information shall be supplied within

All additional information or documentary material requested pursuant to section 7A(e) and Sec. 803.20 (or, if such request is not fully complied with, the information or documentary material submitted and a statement of the reasons for such noncompliance in accordance with Sec. 803.3) shall be supplied within a reasonable time.
