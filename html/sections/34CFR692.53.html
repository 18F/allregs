CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 692  /  Sec. 692.53 What requirements must a State satisfy to receive SLEAP

To receive SLEAP Program funds for any fiscal year, a State must--

(a) Participate in the LEAP Program;

(b) Meet the requirements in Sec. 692.60; and

(c) Have a program that satisfies the requirements in Sec. 692.21(a), (b), (d), (e), (f), (g), (j), and (k). (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1070c-3a) [65 FR 65608, Nov. 1, 2000, as amended at 66 FR 34039, June 26, 2001]
