CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 61  /  Sec. 61.55 Technical assistance reports.

Each technical assistance grantee must submit to VA a quarterly report describing the activities for which the technical assistance grant funds were used, including the type and amount of technical assistance provided and the number of nonprofit community-based groups served. (Authority: 38 U.S.C. 501, 2064) (The Office of Management and Budget has approved the information collection requirements in this section under control number 2900-0554)
