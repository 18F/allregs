CFR /  Title 22  /  Part 1509  /  Sec. 1509.515 Are there any exceptions to those actions?

The ADF President may waive with respect to a particular award, in writing, a suspension of payments under an award, suspension or termination of an award, or suspension or debarment of a recipient if the ADF President determines that such a waiver would be in the public interest. This exception authority cannot be delegated to any other official.
