CFR /  Title 1  /  Part 21  /  Sec. 21.35 OMB control numbers.

To display OMB control numbers in agency regulations, those numbers shall be placed parenthetically at the end of the section or displayed in a table or codified section. [50 FR 12468, Mar. 28, 1985]
