CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 553  /  Sec. 553.21 How can I use self-insurance as OSFR evidence?

(a) If you use self-insurance to satisfy all or part of your obligation to demonstrate OSFR, you must annually pass either a net worth test under Sec. 553.25 or an unencumbered net asset test under Sec. 553.28.

(b) To establish the amount of self-insurance allowed, you must submit evidence of your net worth under Sec. 553.23 or evidence of your unencumbered assets under Sec. 553.26.

(c) You must identify a U.S. agent for service of process.
