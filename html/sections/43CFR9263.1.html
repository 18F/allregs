CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 9263  /  Sec. 9263.1 Operations conducted under the 1872 Mining Law.

See subpart 3809 of this title for law enforcement provisions applicable to operations conducted on public lands under the 1872 Mining Law. [65 FR 70132, Nov. 21, 2000]

Subpart 9264_Range Management Sec. 9264.0-3 Authority.

(a) The provisions of this subpart are issued under section 303(a) of the Federal Land Policy and Management Act of 1976 (43 U.S.C. 1701 et seq.) and section 2 of the Taylor Grazing Act of 1934 (43 U.S.C. 315 et seq.).

(b) The provisions of Sec. 9264.7 of this title are issued under section 8 of the Wild Free-Roaming Horse and Burro Act of 1971 (16 U.S.C. 1331 et seq.).
