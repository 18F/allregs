CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 52  /  Sec. 52.782 Request for 18-month extension.

(a) The requirements of Sec. 51.341 of this chapter are not met since the request for an 18-month extension for submitting that portion of the plan that implements the secondary standards for particulate matter in the Metropolitan Indianapolis Intrastate Region does not show that attainment of the secondary standards will require emission reductions exceeding those which can be achieved through the application of reasonably available control technology.
