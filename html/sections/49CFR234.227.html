CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 234  /  Sec. 234.227 Train detection apparatus.

(a) Train detection apparatus shall be maintained to detect a train or railcar in any part of a train detection circuit, in accordance with the design of the warning system.

(b) If the presence of sand, rust, dirt, grease, or other foreign matter is known to prevent effective shunting, a railroad shall take appropriate action under Sec. 234.105, ``Activation failure,'' to safeguard highway users.
