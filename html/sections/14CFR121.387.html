CFR /  Title 14  /  Part 121  /  Sec. 121.387 Flight engineer.

No certificate holder may operate an airplane for which a type certificate was issued before January 2, 1964, having a maximum certificated takeoff weight of more than 80,000 pounds without a flight crewmember holding a current flight engineer certificate. For each airplane type certificated after January 1, 1964, the requirement for a flight engineer is determined under the type certification requirements of Sec. 25.1523. [Doc. No. 5025, 30 FR 6067, Apr. 29, 1965]
