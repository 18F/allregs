CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 74  /  Sec. 74.2304 FD&C; Red No. 4.

(a) Identity and specifications. The color additive FD&C; Red No. 4 shall conform in identity and specifications to the requirements of Sec. 74.1304(a)(1) and (b).

(b) Uses and restrictions. FD&C; Red No. 4 may be safely used for coloring externally applied cosmetics in amounts consistent with good manufacturing practice.

(c) Labeling. The label of the color additive shall conform to the requirements of Sec. 70.25 of this chapter.

(d) Certification. All batches of FD&C; Red No. 4 shall be certified in accordance with regulations in part 80 of this chapter.
