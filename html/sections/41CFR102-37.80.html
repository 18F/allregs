CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 102-37  /  Sec. 102-37.80 What happens to surplus property that isn't transferred

Surplus property not transferred for donation is generally offered for sale under the provisions of part 102-38 of this chapter. Under the appropriate circumstances (see Sec. 102-36.305 of this chapter), such property might be abandoned or destroyed. [67 FR 2584, Jan. 18, 2002, as amended at 71 FR 23868, Apr. 25, 2006]
