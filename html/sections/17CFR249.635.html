CFR /  Title 17  /  Part 249  /  Sec. 249.635 Form X-17A-19, report by national securities exchanges membership status of any of their members.

This form shall be completed and filed by each national securities exchange or registered national securities association as required by Sec. 240.17a-19 of this chapter within 5 business days of the occurrence of the initiation of the membership of any person or the suspension or termination of the membership of any of its members.
