CFR /  Title 27  /  Part 72  /  Sec. 72.81 Authority for disposal.

Forfeited firearms, aummunition, explosive materials, or contraband cigarettes, not the subject of an allowed petition, may only be disposed of in accordance with the provisions of 26 U.S.C. 5872(b). [T.D. ATF-65, 45 FR 8593, Feb. 8, 1980]
