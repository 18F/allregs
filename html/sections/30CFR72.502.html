CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 72  /  Sec. 72.502 Requirements for nonpermissible light-duty diesel-powered

(a) Each piece of nonpermissible light-duty diesel-powered equipment (as defined by Sec. 75.1908(b) of this chapter), other than generators and compressors, introduced into an underground area of an underground coal mine after May 21, 2001 must emit no more than 5.0 grams per hour of diesel particulate matter.

(b) A piece of nonpermissible light-duty diesel-powered equipment must be deemed to be in compliance with the requirements of paragraph (a) of this section if it utilizes an engine which meets or exceeds the applicable particulate matter emission requirements of the Environmental Protection Administration listed in Table 72.502-1, as follows:
