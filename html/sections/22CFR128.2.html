CFR /  Title 22  /  Part 128  /  Sec. 128.2 Administrative Law Judge.

The Administrative Law Judge referred to in this part is an Administrative Law Judge appointed by the Department of State. The Administrative Law Judge is authorized to exercise the powers and perform the duties provided for in Sec. Sec. 127.7 and 128.3 through 128.16 of this subchapter. [78 FR 52689, Aug. 26, 2013]
