CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 615  /  615.303 Responsibilities.

(a) The Procurement Executive is the agency head for the purposes of FAR 15.303(a). The HCA is delegated authority to appoint someone other than the contracting officer as source selection authority for a particular acquisition. [64 FR 43623, Aug. 11, 1999, as amended at 80 FR 6920, Feb. 9, 2015]
