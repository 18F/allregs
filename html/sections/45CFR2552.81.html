CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 2552  /  Sec. 2552.81 What type of children are eligible to be served?

Foster Grandparents serve only children and youth with special and exceptional needs, or in circumstances that limit their academic, social, or emotional development, who are less than 21 years of age. [74 FR 46509, Sept. 10, 2009]
