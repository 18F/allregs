CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 200  /  Sec. 200.85 Responsibilities of SEAs and operating agencies for

While the specific school improvement requirements of section 1116 of the ESEA do not apply to the MEP, SEAs and local operating agencies receiving MEP funds must use the results of the evaluation carried out under Sec. 200.84 to improve the services provided to migratory children. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 6394)
