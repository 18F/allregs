CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 1  /  Sec. 1.893 Establishing and converting part-time positions.

Position management and other internal reviews may indicate that positions may be either converted from full-time or initially established as part-time positions. Criteria listed in Sec. 1.892 may be used during these reviews. If a decision is made to convert to or to establish a part-time position, regular position management and classification procedures will be followed. (Authority: 5 U.S.C. 3402)
