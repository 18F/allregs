CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 33  /  Sec. 33.42 Judicial review.

Section 3805 of title 31, United States Code, authorizes judicial review by an appropriate United States District Court of a final decision of the Department head imposing penalties or assessments under this part and specifies the procedures for the review. (Authority: 31 U.S.C. 3805)
