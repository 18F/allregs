CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 28  /  28.204-4 Contract clause.

Insert the clause at 52.228-14, Irrevocable Letter of Credit, in solicitations and contracts for services, supplies, or construction, when a bid guarantee, or performance bonds, or performance and payment bonds are required. [61 FR 31653, June 20, 1996]
