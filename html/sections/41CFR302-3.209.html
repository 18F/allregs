CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 302-3  /  Sec. 302-3.209 What is overseas tour renewal travel?

Overseas tour renewal travel refers to travel of you and your immediate family returning to your home in the continental U.S., Alaska, or Hawaii between overseas tours of duty. See Sec. 302-2.222 for travel to an actual place of residence in other than the United States.
