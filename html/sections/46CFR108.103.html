CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 108  /  Sec. 108.103 Equipment not required on a unit.

Each item of lifesaving and firefighting equipment carried on board the unit in addition to equipment of the type required under this subchapter, must--

(a) Be approved; or

(b) Be acceptable to the cognizant OCMI, for use on the unit. [CGD 84-069, 61 FR 25291, May 20, 1996]
