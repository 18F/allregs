CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 21  /  Sec. 21.7659 Reporting fee.

In determining the amount of the reporting fee payable to educational institutions for furnishing required reports, VA will apply the provisions of Sec. 21.4206. (Authority: 10 U.S.C. 16136(b); 38 U.S.C. 3684) [62 FR 55762, Oct. 28, 1997]
