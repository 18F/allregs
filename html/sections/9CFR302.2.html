CFR /  Title 9  /  Part 302  /  Sec. 302.2 Application of requirements in designated States or

Special provisions with respect to establishments and their operations and transactions by any persons in designated States and Territories and with respect to establishments designated as producing adulterated products which clearly endanger public health, and the operators thereof, in any State or Territory appear in part 331 of this subchapter, and apply to such establishments, operations and transactions in lieu of the regulations elsewhere in this subchapter except insofar as such regulations are made applicable by the provisions in part 331 of this subchapter. [35 FR 15556, Oct. 3, 1970, as amended at 51 FR 29909, Aug. 21, 1986]
