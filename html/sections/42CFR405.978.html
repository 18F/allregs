CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 405  /  Sec. 405.978 Effect of a reconsideration.

A reconsideration is binding on all parties, unless--

(a) An ALJ decision is issued in accordance to a request for an ALJ hearing made in accordance with Sec. 405.1014;

(b) A review entity issues a decision in accordance to a request for expedited access to judicial review under Sec. 405.990; or

(c) The reconsideration is revised as a result of a reopening in accordance with Sec. 405.980. [70 FR 11472, Mar. 8, 2005, as amended at 74 FR 65334, Dec. 9, 2009]
