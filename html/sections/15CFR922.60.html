CFR /  Title 15  /  Part 922  /  Sec. 922.60 Boundary.

The Monitor National Marine Sanctuary (Sanctuary) consists of a vertical water column in the Atlantic Ocean one mile in diameter extending from the surface to the seabed, the center of which is at 35[deg]00[min]23[sec] north latitude and 75[deg]24[min]32[sec] west longitude.
