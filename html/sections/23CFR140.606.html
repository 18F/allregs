CFR /  Title 23  /  Part 140  /  Sec. 140.606 Project agreements.

Project Agreements, Form PR-2, shall be prepared and executed. Agreement provision 8 on the reverse side of Form PR-2 \1\ shall apply for bond issue projects.---------------------------------------------------------------------------

\1\ The text of FHWA Form PR-2 is found in 23 CFR part 630, subpart C, appendix A.---------------------------------------------------------------------------
