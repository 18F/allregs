CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 154  /  Sec. 154.540 Quick-closing shut-off valves: Emergency shut-down system.

The quick-closing shut-off valves under Sec. Sec. 154.530, 154.532, and 154.538 must have an emergency shut-down system that:

(a) Closes all the valves;

(b) Is actuated by a single control in at least two locations remote from the quick-closing valves;

(c) Is actuated by a single control in each cargo control station under Sec. 154.320; and

(d) Has fusible elements at each tank dome and cargo loading and discharge manifold that melt between 98 [deg]C (208 [deg]F) and 104 [deg]C (220 [deg]F) and actuate the emergency shut-down system.
