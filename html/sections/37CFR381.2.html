CFR /  Title 37  /  Part 381  /  Sec. 381.2 Definition of public broadcasting entity.

As used in this part, the term public broadcasting entity means a noncommercial educational broadcast station as defined in section 397 of title 47 and any nonprofit institution or organization engaged in the activities described in 17 U.S.C. 118(c). Sec. 381.3 [Reserved]
