CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 0  /  Sec. 0.505 Fees and charges.

(a) The Commission has designated a contractor to make copies of Commission records and offer them for sale (See Sec. 0.465).

(b) An hourly fee is charged for recovery of the direct costs of searching for requested documents (See Sec. 0.466).
