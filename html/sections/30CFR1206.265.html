CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 1206  /  Sec. 1206.265 Value enhancement of marketable coal.

If, prior to use, sale, or other disposition, the lessee enhances the value of coal after the coal has been placed in marketable condition in accordance with Sec. 1206.257(h) of this subpart, the lessee shall notify ONRR that such processing is occurring or will occur. The value of that production shall be determined as follows:

(a) A value established for the feedstock coal in marketable condition by application of the provisions of Sec. 1206.257(c)(2)(i-iv) of this subpart; or,

(b)(2)(v) of this subpart.
