CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 874  /  Sec. 874.5 Definitions.

As used in this Part--

Reclamation plan or State reclamation plan means a plan that a State or Indian tribe submitted and that we approved under section 405 of SMCRA and part 884 of this chapter. [73 FR 67638, Nov. 14, 2008]
