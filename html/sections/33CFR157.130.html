CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 157  /  Sec. 157.130 Crude oil washing with more than one grade of crude oil.

If a tank vessel having a COW system under Sec. 157.10(e), Sec. 157.10a(a)(2), or Sec. 157.10c(b)(2) carries more than one grade of crude oil, the COW system must be capable of washing the cargo tanks with the grades of crude oil that the vessel carries. [CGD 82-28, 50 FR 11627, Mar. 22, 1985]
