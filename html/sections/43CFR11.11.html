CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 11  /  Sec. 11.11 Purpose.

The purpose of this part is to provide standardized and cost- effective procedures for assessing natural resource damages. The results of an assessment performed by a Federal or State natural resource trustee according to these procedures shall be accorded the evidentiary status of a rebuttable presumption as provided in section 107(f)(2)(C) of CERCLA. [53 FR 5171, Feb. 22, 1988]
