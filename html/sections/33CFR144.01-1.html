CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 144  /  Sec. 144.01-1 Life floats.

Each manned platform shall be provided with at least two approved life floats. The life floats shall have sufficient capacity to accommodate all persons present at any one time. [CGFR 56-4, 21 FR 903, Feb. 9, 1956]
