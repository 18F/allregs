CFR /  Title 12  /  Part 261a  /  Sec. 261a.4 Fees.

(a) Copies of records. We will provide you with copies of the records you request under Sec. 261a.5 of this part at the same cost we charge for duplication of records and/or production of computer output under the Board's Rules Regarding Availability of Information, 12 CFR Part 261.

(b) No fee. We will not charge you a fee if:

(1) Your total charges are less than $5, or

(2) You are a Board employee or former employee, or an applicant for employment with the Board, and you request records pertaining to you. Subpart B_Procedures for Requests by Individuals to Whom Record Pertains
