CFR /  Title 50  /  Part 648  /  Sec. 648.127 Scup recreational fishing season.

Fishermen and vessels that are not eligible for a moratorium permit under Sec. 648.4(a)(6), may possess scup year-round, subject to the possession limit specified in Sec. 648.128(a). The recreational fishing season may be adjusted pursuant to the procedures in Sec. 648.122. [78 FR 37480, June 21, 2013]
