CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 2711  /  Sec. 2711.5-3 Notice of conveyance.

The authorized officer shall immediately notify the Governor and the heads of local government of the issuance of conveyance documents for public lands within their respective jurisdiction. [45 FR 39418, June 10, 1980, as amended at 49 FR 29016, July 17, 1984]
