CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 570  /  Sec. 570.488 Displacement, relocation, acquisition, and replacement of housing.

The requirements for States and state recipients with regard to the displacement, relocation, acquisition, and replacement of housing are in Sec. 570.606 and 24 CFR part 42. [61 FR 11477, Mar. 20, 1996]
