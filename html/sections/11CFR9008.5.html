CFR /  Title 11  /  Part 9008  /  Sec. 9008.5 Adjustment of entitlement.

(a) The entitlements established by 11 CFR 9008.4 shall be adjusted on the basis of the Consumer Price Index pursuant to the provisions of 52 U.S.C. 30116(c).

(b)(4) will not be considered private contributions or expenditures counting against the limitation. [59 FR 33616, June 29, 1994, as amended at 79 FR 77851, Dec. 29, 2014]
