CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 25  /  Sec. 25.218 Off-axis EIRP density envelopes for FSS earth stations

(a) This section applies to all applications for Fixed-Satellite Service earth stations transmitting to geostationary space stations in the C band, Ku band, or extended Ku band, except for:

(1) ESV, VMES, and ESAA applications, and

(2) Analog video earth station applications.

(b) Earth station applications subject to this section are eligible for routine processing if they meet the applicable off-axis EIRP density envelope set forth in this section below. The terms ``conventional Ku band'' and ``extended Ku band are defined in Sec. 25.103.
