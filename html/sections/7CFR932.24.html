CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 932  /  Sec. 932.24 Noncanning use.

Noncanning use means the use of olives other than in the production of canned ripe olives, and is the authorized outlet for undersize olives and the limited use size olives which, pursuant to Sec. 932.52(b), are not permitted for limited use in any crop year in which limited use is restricted to less than the available quantity of limited use size olives. [36 FR 20356, Oct. 21, 1971]
