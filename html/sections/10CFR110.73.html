CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 110  /  Sec. 110.73 Availability of NRC records.

(a) Commission records under this part will be made available to the public only in accordance with part 9 of this chapter.

(b) Proprietary information provided under this part may be protected under Part 9 and Sec. 2.390(b), (c), and (d) of this chapter. [43 FR 21641, May 19, 1978, as amended at 69 FR 2281, Jan. 14, 2004]
