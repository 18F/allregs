CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 662  /  Sec. 662.22 How does the J. William Fulbright Foreign Scholarship

(a) The J. William Fulbright Foreign Scholarship Board selects fellows on the basis of the Secretary's recommendations and the information described in Sec. 662.20(e) from binational commissions or United States diplomatic missions.

(b) No applicant for a fellowship may be awarded more than one graduate fellowship under the Fulbright-Hays Act from appropriations for a given fiscal year. (Authority: 22 U.S.C. 2452(b)(6), 2456(a)(1))
