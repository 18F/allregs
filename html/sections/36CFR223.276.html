CFR /  Title 36  /  Part 223  /  Sec. 223.276 Applicability.

This subpart applies to the sale and free use of forest botanical products, as defined in Sec. 223.277, from National Forest System lands, until September 30, 2009, unless the pilot program is extended or made permanent by Congress. The Forest Service shall dispose of forest botanical products in accordance with the procedures set forth in 36 CFR part 223 Subpart G, subject to the requirements of this subpart.
