CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 35  /  Sec. 35.4250 Under what circumstances would EPA terminate my

(a) EPA may terminate your grant if your group materially fails to comply with the terms and conditions of the TAG and the requirements of this subpart.

(b) EPA may also terminate your grant with your group's consent in which case you and EPA must agree upon the termination conditions, including the effective date as 2 CFR 200.339 describes. [65 FR 58858, Oct. 2, 2000, as amended at 79 FR 76059, Dec. 19, 2014]
