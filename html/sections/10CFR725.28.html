CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 725  /  Sec. 725.28 Action on application to renew or amend.

In considering an application by a permittee to review or amend his permit, the Chief Health, Safety and Security Officer will apply the criteria set forth in Sec. 725.15. Failure of an applicant to reply to an DOE request for additional information concerning an application for renewal or amendment within 60 days shall result in a rejection of the application without prejudice to resubmit a properly completed application at a later date. [41 FR 56778, Dec. 30, 1976, as amended at 71 FR 68732, Nov. 28, 2006]
