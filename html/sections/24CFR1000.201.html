CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 1000  /  Sec. 1000.201 How are funds made available under NAHASDA?

Every fiscal year HUD will make grants under the IHBG program to recipients who have submitted to HUD for a tribal program year an IHP in accordance with Sec. 1000.220 to carry out affordable housing activities. [77 FR 71526, Dec. 3, 2012]
