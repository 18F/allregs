CFR /  Title 31  /  Part 542  /  Sec. 542.207 Prohibited exportation, reexportation, sale, or supply

Except as otherwise authorized, the exportation, reexportation, sale, or supply, directly or indirectly, from the United States, or by a United States person, wherever located, of any services to Syria is prohibited.
