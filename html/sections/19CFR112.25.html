CFR /  Title 19  /  Part 112  /  Sec. 112.25 Bonded carriers.

A carrier or freight forwarder who has filed a bond on Customs Form 301 containing the bond conditions set forth in Sec. 113.63 of this chapter may transport merchandise within a port for which the bond provides coverage. [T.D. 94-81, 59 FR 51495, Oct. 12, 1994]
