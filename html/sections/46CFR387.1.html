CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 387  /  Sec. 387.1 Scope.

This part is applicable to Surplus Property that is recommended by the Secretary as being needed for the development or operation of a Port Facility and is appropriate for being assigned to, or that has been assigned to the Secretary for conveyance as provided for in Public Law 103-160 and 40 U.S.C. 471 et seq.
