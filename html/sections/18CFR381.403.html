CFR /  Title 18  /  Part 381  /  Sec. 381.403 Petitions for rate approval pursuant to Sec. 284.123(b)(2).

The fee established for a petition for rate approval pursuant to Sec. 284.123(b)(2) is $12,070. Such fee must be submitted in accordance with subpart A of this part and Sec. 284.123(b)(2). [Order 394, 49 FR 35365, Sept. 7, 1984]

Editorial Note: For Federal Register citations affecting Sec. 381.403, see the List of CFR Sections Affected, which appears in the Finding Aids section of the printed volume and at www.fdsys.gov.

Effective Date Note: At 80 FR 13223, Mar. 13, 2015, Sec. 381.403 was amended by removing ``$12,070'' and adding ``$12,310'' in its place, effective Apr. 13, 2015. Sec. 381.404 [Reserved]
