CFR /  Title 18  /  Part 381  /  Sec. 381.403 Petitions for rate approval pursuant to

The fee established for a petition for rate approval pursuant to Sec. 284.123(b)(2) is $12,430. Such fee must be submitted in accordance with subpart A of this part and Sec. 284.123(b)(2). [Order 394, 49 FR 35365, Sept. 7, 1984]
