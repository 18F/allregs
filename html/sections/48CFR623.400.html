CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 623  /  623.400 Scope of subpart.

The affirmative procurement program is applicable to all domestic acquisitions of items currently designated by an EPA guideline or by future guidelines promulgated by EPA. The requirements of this section are not applicable to acquisitions awarded outside the United States or its possessions. [64 FR 43626, Aug. 11, 1999, as amended at 69 FR 19336, Apr. 13, 2004]
