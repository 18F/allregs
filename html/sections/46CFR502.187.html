CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 502  /  Sec. 502.187 Procedure after filing of memoranda.

An initial, recommended, or tentative decision will be served upon the parties in the same manner as is provided under Sec. 502.225. Thereafter, the procedure will be the same as that in respect to proceedings after formal hearing. [Rule 187.]
