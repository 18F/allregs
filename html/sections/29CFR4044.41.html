CFR /  Title 29  /  Part 4044  /  Sec. 4044.41 General valuation rules.

(a) Valuation of benefits--(1) Trusteed plans. The plan administrator of a plan that has been or will be placed into trusteeship by the PBGC shall value plan benefits in accordance with Secs. 4044.51 through 4044.57.

(1) Trusteed plans. The plan administrator of a plan that has been or will be placed into trusteeship by the PBGC shall value plan benefits in accordance with Secs. 4044.51 through 4044.57.

(2) Non-trusteed plans. The plan administrator of a non-trusteed plan shall value plan benefits in accordance with Secs. 4044.71 through 4044.75. If a plan is unable to satisfy all benefits assigned to priority categories 1 through 4 on the distribution date, the PBGC will place it into trusteeship and the plan administrator shall re-value the benefits in accordance with Secs. 4044.51 through 4044.57.

(b) Valuation of assets. Plan assets shall be valued at their fair market value, based on the method of valuation that most accurately reflects such fair market value. [61 FR 34059, July 1, 1996, as amended at 76 FR 34606, June 14, 2011]
