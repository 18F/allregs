CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 5119  /  Sec. 5119.1001 General.

This subpart implements Pub. L. 100-656, section 722, ``Expanding Small Business Participation in Dredging'' (the Dredging Program). The Program will be conducted through 30 September 1992.
