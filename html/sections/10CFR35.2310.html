CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 35  /  Sec. 35.2310 Records of safety instruction.

A licensee shall maintain a record of safety instructions required by Secs. 35.310, 35.410, and 35.610 for 3 years. The record must include a list of the topics covered, the date of the instruction, the name(s) of the attendee(s), and the name(s) of the individual(s) who provided the instruction.
