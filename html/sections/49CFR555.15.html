CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 555  /  Sec. 555.15 Time period for exemptions.

Subject to Sec. 555.16, each temporary exemption granted by the Administrator under this subpart shall be in effect for a period of three years from the effective date. The Administrator shall identify each exemption by a unique number.
