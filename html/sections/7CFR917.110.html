CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 917  /  Sec. 917.110 Communications.

Unless otherwise prescribed in this subpart, or in the marketing agreement and order, or required by the Control Committee, or a particular commodity committee, all reports, applications, submittals, requests, and communications in connection with the marketing agreement and order shall be addressed as follows: California Tree Fruit Agreement, P.O. Box 968, Reedley, CA, 93654-0968. [63 FR 16041, Apr. 1, 1998, as amended at 71 FR 78041, Dec. 28, 2006]

Effective Date Note: At 76 FR 66605, Oct. 27, 2011, Sec. 917.110 was suspended indefinitely, effective Oct. 28, 2011.
