CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 179  /  Sec. 179.07 Notice given by ``more expeditious means''.

Each manufacturer who gives notice by more expeditious means as provided for in 46 U.S.C. 4310(c)(1)(C), must give such notice in writing. [CGD 93-055, 61 FR 13926, Mar. 28, 1996]
