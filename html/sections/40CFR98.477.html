CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 98  /  Sec. 98.477 Records that must be retained.

(a) You must follow the record retention requirements specified by Sec. 98.3(g). In addition to the records required by Sec. 98.3(g), you must retain quarterly records of CO2 received, including mass flow rate or contents of containers (mass or volumetric) at standard conditions and operating conditions, operating temperature and pressure, and concentration of these streams. You must retain all required records for at least 3 years.

(b) You must complete your monitoring plans, as described in Sec. 98.3(g)(5), by April 1 of the year you begin collecting data.
