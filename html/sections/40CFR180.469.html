CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 180  /  Sec. 180.469 Dichlormid; tolerances for residues.

(a) General. Tolerances are established for residues of dichlormid, including its metabolites and degradates, when used as an inert ingredient (herbicide safener) in pesticide formulations, in or on the commodities in the following table. Compliance with the tolerances is to be determined by measuring only dichlormid (2,2-dichloro-N,N-di-2-propenylacetamide). ------------------------------------------------------------------------
