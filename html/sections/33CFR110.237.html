CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 110  /  Sec. 110.237 Pacific Ocean at Waimea, Hawaii, Naval Anchorage.

(a) The Anchorage grounds. All the waters within a circle having a radius of 300 yards centered at latitude 21[deg]56[min]50.7[sec] N., longitude 159[deg]41[min]22.9[sec] W. (Datum NAD 83)

(b) The regulation. Except in an emergency, no vessel except a Naval vessel may anchor or moor in this anchorage without permission of the Captain of the Port, Honolulu, Hawaii. [CGD 74-187, 41 FR 54176, Dec. 13, 1976, as amended by CGD 96-026, 61 FR 33663, June 28, 1996; USCG-2002-12471, 67 FR 41332, June 18, 2002]
