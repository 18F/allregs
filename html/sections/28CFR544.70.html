CFR /  Title 28  /  Part 544  /  Sec. 544.70 Purpose and scope.

Except as provided for in Sec. 544.71, an inmate confined in a federal institution who does not have a verified General Educational Development (GED) credential or high school diploma is required to attend an adult literacy program for a minimum of 240 instructional hours or until a GED is achieved, whichever occurs first.
