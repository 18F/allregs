CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 2425  /  2425.402 Policy.

(a)(1) It is the Department's policy to determine whether the Trade Agreements Act applies based on the total estimated dollar value of the proposed acquisition before the solicitation is issued, including all line items and options. [57 FR 59790, Dec. 15, 1992, as amended at 64 FR 46097, Aug. 23, 1999]
