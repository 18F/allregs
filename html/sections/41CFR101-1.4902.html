CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 101-1  /  Sec. 101-1.4902 GSA forms.

(a) The GSA forms are illustrated in this section to show their text, format, and arrangement and to provide a ready source of reference. The subsection numbers in this section correspond with the GSA numbers.

(b) GSA forms illustrated in Sec. 101-1.4902 may be obtained by addressing requests to the General Services Administration, National Forms and Publications Center-7 CAR-W, Warehouse 4, Dock No. 1, 501 West Felix Street, Forth Worth, TX 76115. [36 FR 4984, Mar. 16, 1971, as amended at 53 FR 2739, Feb. 1, 1988]
