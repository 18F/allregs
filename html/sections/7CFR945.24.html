CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 945  /  Sec. 945.24 Selection.

Members and alternates of the committee shall be selected by the Secretary on the basis specified in Sec. 945.23 (b) from nominations made pursuant to Sec. 945.25 or from other eligible persons. [60 FR 29727, June 5, 1995]
