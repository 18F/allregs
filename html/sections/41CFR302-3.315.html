CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 302-3  /  Sec. 302-3.315 May I be granted an extension to the time limit for

Yes, your agency may grant you or your immediate family member(s) (in case of your death) an extension to the time limit for beginning your separation travel, for up to two years from your effective date of separation or death, if death occurs before separation. [FTR Amdt. 2011-01, 76 FR 18337, Apr. 1, 2011]
