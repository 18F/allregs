CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 193  /  Sec. 193.2051 Scope.

Each LNG facility designed, constructed, replaced, relocated or significantly altered after March 31, 2000 must be provided with siting requirements in accordance with the requirements of this part and of NFPA 59A (incorporated by reference, see Sec. 193.2013). In the event of a conflict between this part and NFPA-59A-2001, this part prevails.
