CFR /  Title 8  /  Part 233  /  Sec. 233.5 Aliens entering Guam pursuant to section 14 of

A transportation line bringing aliens to Guam under the visa waiver provisions of Sec. 212.1(e) of this chapter shall enter into an agreement on Form I-760. Such agreements shall be negotiated directly by the Service's Headquarters and head offices of the transportation lines. [62 FR 10353, Mar. 6, 1997]
