CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 203  /  Sec. 203.342 Mortgage modification.

If a mortgage is recast pursuant to Sec. 203.616, the principal amount of the mortgage, as modified, shall be considered to be the ``original principal balance of the mortgage'' as that term is used in Sec. 203.401. [62 FR 60129, Nov. 6, 1997]
