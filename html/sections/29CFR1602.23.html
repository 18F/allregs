CFR /  Title 29  /  Part 1602  /  Sec. 1602.23 Penalty for making of willfully false statements on reports.

The making of willfully false statements on Report EEO-3 is a violation of the United States Code, title 18, section 1001, and is punishable by fine or imprisonment as set forth herein. [32 FR 10651, July 20, 1967]
