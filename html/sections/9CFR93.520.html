CFR /  Title 9  /  Part 93  /  Sec. 93.520 Import permit and declaration for swine.

For all swine offered for importation from countries of Central America or of the West Indies, the importer or his or her agent shall present two copies of a declaration as provided in Sec. 93.506. [55 FR 34195, Aug. 2, 1990. Redesignated and amended at 62 FR 56012, 56020, Oct. 28, 1997]

Mexico \9\---------------------------------------------------------------------------

\9\ Importations from Mexico shall be subject to Sec. 93.521, in addition to other sections in this subpart, which are in terms applicable to such importations.---------------------------------------------------------------------------
