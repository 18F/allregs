CFR /  Title 29  /  Part 102  /  Sec. 102.86 Hearing; posthearing procedure.

The method of conducting the hearing and the procedure following the hearing shall be governed, insofar as applicable, by Sec. Sec. 102.63 through 102.68 inclusive. [79 FR 74489, Dec. 15, 2014]
