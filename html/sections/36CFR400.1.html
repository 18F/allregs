CFR /  Title 36  /  Part 400  /  Sec. 400.1 Cross-references to employees' ethical conduct standards,

Employees of the American Battle Monuments Commission are subject to the executive branch-wide standards of ethical conduct and financial disclosure regulations at 5 CFR parts 2634 and 2635 as well as the executive branch-wide employee responsibilities and conduct regulations at 5 CFR part 735. [69 FR 17929, Apr. 6, 2004]
