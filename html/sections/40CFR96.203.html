CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 96  /  Sec. 96.203 Measurements, abbreviations, and acronyms.

Measurements, abbreviations, and acronyms used in this subpart and subparts BBB through III are defined as follows: Btu--British thermal unit CO2--carbon dioxide H2O--water Hg--mercury hr--hour kW--kilowatt electrical kWh--kilowatt hour lb--pound mmBtu--million Btu MWe--megawatt electrical MWh--megawatt hour NOX--nitrogen oxides O2--oxygen ppm--parts per million scfh--standard cubic feet per hour SO2--sulfur dioxide yr--year [71 FR 25387, Apr. 28, 2006]
