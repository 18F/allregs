CFR /  Title 9  /  Part 77  /  Sec. 77.23 Interstate movement from accredited-free States and zones.

Notwithstanding any other provisions of this part, captive cervids that originate in an accredited-free State or zone may be moved interstate in accordance with 9 CFR part 86 and without further restriction under this part. [78 FR 2066, Jan. 9, 2013]
