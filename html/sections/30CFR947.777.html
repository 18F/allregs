CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 947  /  Sec. 947.777 General content requirements for permit applications.

Part 777 of this chapter, General Content Requirements for Permit Applications, shall apply to any person who applies for a permit to conduct surface coal mining and reclamation operations. [52 FR 13817, Apr. 24, 1987]
