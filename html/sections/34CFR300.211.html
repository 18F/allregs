CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 300  /  Sec. 300.211 Information for SEA.

The LEA must provide the SEA with information necessary to enable the SEA to carry out its duties under Part B of the Act, including, with respect to Secs. 300.157 and 300.160, information relating to the performance of children with disabilities participating in programs carried out under Part B of the Act. (Approved by the Office of Management and Budget under control number 1820-0600) (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1413(a)(7))
