CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 892  /  Sec. 892.210 Does premium conversion change the effective date of

No. If you are participating in premium conversion, the effective date of an FEHB enrollment, change in enrollment, or cancellation of enrollment is the same effective date as provided in Sec. 890.301 of this chapter.
