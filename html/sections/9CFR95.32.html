CFR /  Title 9  /  Part 95  /  Sec. 95.32 Hay and straw; requirements for unrestricted entry.

Except as provided in Sec. 95.39, hay or straw shall not be imported except subject to handling and treatment in accordance with Sec. 95.33 after arrival at the port of entry, unless such hay or straw originated in and was shipped directly from a region not declared by the Secretary of Agriculture to be infected with foot-and-mouth disease or rinderpest. [28 FR 5981, June 13, 1963, as amended at 62 FR 56024, Oct. 28, 1997. Redesignated and amended at 78 FR 73003, 73007, Dec. 4, 2013]
