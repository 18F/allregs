CFR /  Title 36  /  Part 1280  /  Sec. 1280.8 May I bring a seeing-eye dog or other assistance animal?

Yes, persons with disabilities may bring guide dogs or other animals used for guidance and assistance onto NARA property. You may not bring any other animals into a NARA facility except for official purposes. [65 FR 34978, June 1, 2000. Redesignated at 71 FR 76167, Dec. 20, 2006]
