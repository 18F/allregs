CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 461  /  Sec. 461.44 How does a State request a waiver of the maintenance

An SEA seeking a waiver of the maintenance of effort requirement in Sec. 461.42 shall--

(a) Submit to the Secretary a request for a waiver; and

(b) Include in the request--

(1) The reason for the request; and

(2) Any additional information the Secretary may require. (Approved by the Office of Management and Budget under control number 1830-0501) (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1209(b)(2))
