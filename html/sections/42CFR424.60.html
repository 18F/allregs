CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 424  /  Sec. 424.60 Scope.

(a) This subpart sets forth provisions applicable to payment after the beneficiary's death and payment to entities that provide coverage complementary to Medicare Part B.

(b) The provisions applicable to payment for services excluded as custodial care or services not reasonable and necessary are set forth in Secs. 405.332 through 405.336 of this chapter. [53 FR 6634, Mar. 2, 1988, as amended at 53 FR 28388, July 28, 1988]
