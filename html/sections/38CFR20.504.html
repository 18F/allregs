CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 20  /  Sec. 20.504 Rule 504. Notices sent to last addresses of record in

Notices in simultaneously contested claims will be forwarded to the last address of record of the parties concerned and such action will constitute sufficient evidence of notice. (Authority: 38 U.S.C. 7105A(b)) Sec. Sec. 20.505-20.599 [Reserved]
