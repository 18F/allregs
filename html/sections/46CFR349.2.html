CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 349  /  Sec. 349.2 Application for certification.

Pursuant to 46 App. U.S.C. 1132, an individual may submit an application to MARAD not later than 45 days after the date the individual completes the period of employment described in Sec. 349.3 of this part.
