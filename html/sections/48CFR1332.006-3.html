CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 1332  /  1332.006-3 Responsibilities.

DOC personnel shall immediately report to the Office of Inspector General any apparent or suspected instances where a contractor's request for advance, partial or progress payments is based on fraud in accordance with DAO 207-10, Inspector General Investigations.
