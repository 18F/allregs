CFR /  Title 28  /  Part 36  /  Sec. 36.603 Preliminary determination.

Upon receipt and review of all information relevant to a request filed by a submitting official for certification of a code, and after consultation with the Architectural and Transportation Barriers Compliance Board, the Assistant Attorney General shall make a preliminary determination of equivalency or a preliminary determination to deny certification. [AG Order No. 3181-2010, 75 FR 56257, Sept. 15, 2010]
