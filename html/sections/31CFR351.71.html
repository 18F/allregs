CFR /  Title 31  /  Part 351  /  Sec. 351.71 How can I find out what my book-entry Series EE savings bonds

(a) Redemption values. You may access redemption values for your book-entry bonds through your New Treasury Direct account.

(b) Redemption penalty. Redemption values shown in your New Treasury Direct account for bonds that are within 5 years from issue date reflect the three-month interest penalty applied to bonds redeemed prior to five years from the issue date. Sec. Sec. 351.72-351.80 [Reserved]
