CFR /  Title 29  /  Part 408  /  Sec. 408.12 Publication of reports required by this part.

Inspection and examination of any report or other document filed as required by this part, and the furnishing by the Office of Labor-Management Standards of copies thereof to any person requesting them, shall be governed by part 70 of this title. [35 FR 2990, Feb. 13, 1970]
