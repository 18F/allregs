CFR /  Title 32  /  Part 720  /  Sec. 720.32 Certificates of full faith and credit.

The Judge Advocate General, the Deputy Judge Advocate General, or any Assistant Judge Advocate General is authorized to execute certificates of full faith and credit certifying the signatures and authority of officers of the Department of the Navy. [38 FR 6021, Mar. 6, 1973]
