CFR /  Title 18  /  Part 225  /  Sec. 225.1 Promulgation.

This part is prescribed and promulgated as the regulations governing the preservation of records by natural gas companies subject to the jurisdiction of the Commission, to the extent and in the manner set forth therein. [Order 617, 65 FR 48160, Aug. 7, 2000]
