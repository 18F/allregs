CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 193  /  Sec. 193.2187 Nonmetallic membrane liner.

A flammable nonmetallic membrane liner may not be used as an inner container in a storage tank. [Amdt. 193-17, 65 FR 10960, Mar. 1, 2000] Secs. 193.2189-193.2233 [Reserved]
