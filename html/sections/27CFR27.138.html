CFR /  Title 27  /  Part 27  /  Sec. 27.138 Transfer record.

The transfer record for imported spirits prescribed in Sec. 27.172 shall show the:

(a) Date prepared;

(b) Serial number of the transfer record, beginning with ``1'' each January 1;

(c) Name and distilled spirits plant number of the proprietor who received the spirits from customs custody;

(d) Country of origin;

(e) Name of foreign producer;

(f) Kind of spirits;

(g) Age, in years, months and days of the spirits;

(h) Proof of the spirits;

(i) Type and number of containers; and

(j) Proof gallons of spirits in the shipment. (Approved by the Office of Management and Budget under control number 1512-0250) [T.D. ATF-198, 50 FR 8558, Mar. 1, 1985]
