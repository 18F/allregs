CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 18a  /  Sec. 18a.5 Delegation to the General Counsel.

The General Counsel is delegated the responsibility, upon receipt of information from the Under Secretary for Benefits, the Chief Medical Director, or the designee of either of them, that compliance cannot be secured by voluntary means, of forwarding to the recipient or other person the notice required by Sec. 18.9(a) of this chapter, and also is delegated the responsibility of representing the agency in all proceedings resulting from such notice. [35 FR 10759, July 2, 1970]
