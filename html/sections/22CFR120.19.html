CFR /  Title 22  /  Part 120  /  Sec. 120.19 Reexport or retransfer.

Reexport or retransfer means the transfer of defense articles or defense services to an end-use, end-user, or destination not previously authorized by license, written approval, or exemption pursuant to this subchapter. [77 FR 16597, Mar. 21, 2012]
