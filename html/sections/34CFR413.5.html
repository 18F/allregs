CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 413  /  Sec. 413.5 What regulations apply?

The following regulations apply to the National Center or Centers:

(a) The regulations in this part 413.

(b) The regulations in 34 CFR part 400. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 2404)
