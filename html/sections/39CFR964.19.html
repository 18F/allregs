CFR /  Title 39  /  Part 964  /  Sec. 964.19 Orders.

If an order is issued by the Judicial Officer which prohibits delivery of mail to a Petitioner it shall be incorporated in the record of the proceeding. The Recorder shall cause notice of the order to be published in the Postal Bulletin and cause the order to be transmitted to such postmasters and other officers and employees of the Postal Service as may be required to place the order into effect. [52 FR 36763, Oct. 1, 1987, as amended at 53 FR 4849, Feb. 18, 1988]
