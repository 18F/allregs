CFR /  Title 26  /  Part 301  /  Sec. 301.6047-1 Information relating to certain trusts and annuity

For provisions relating to the requirement of returns of information regarding certain trusts and annuity and bond purchase plans, see Sec. 1.6047-1 of this chapter (Income Tax Regulations).
