CFR /  Title 26  /  Part 41  /  Sec. 41.0-1 Introduction.

The regulations in this part are designated ``Highway Use Tax Regulations.'' The regulations in this part relate to the tax on the use of certain highway vehicles imposed by section 4481 and to certain associated administrative provisions. [T.D. 8879, 65 FR 17153, Mar. 31, 2000]
