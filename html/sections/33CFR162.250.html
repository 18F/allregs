CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 162  /  Sec. 162.250 Port Alexander, Alaska; speed of vessels.

(a) Definition. The term ``Port Alexander'' includes the entire inlet from its head to its entrance from Chatham Strait.

(b) Speed. The speed of all vessels of 5 tons or more gross, ships register, shall not exceed 3 miles per hour either in entering, leaving, or navigating within Port Alexander, Alaska. [CGD 75-082, 42 FR 51759, Sept. 29, 1977, as amended by CGD 82-039, 47 FR 27266, June 24, 1982]
