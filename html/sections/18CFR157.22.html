CFR /  Title 18  /  Part 157  /  Sec. 157.22 Schedule for final decisions on a request for a Federal

For an application under section 3 or 7 of the Natural Gas Act that requires a Federal authorization--i.e., a permit, special use authorization, certification, opinion, or other approval--from a Federal agency or officer, or State agency or officer acting pursuant to delegated Federal authority, a final decision on a request for a Federal authorization is due no later than 90 days after the Commission issues its final environmental document, unless a schedule is otherwise established by Federal law. [Order 687, 71 FR 62921, Oct. 27, 2006]
