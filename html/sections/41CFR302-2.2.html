CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 302-2  /  Sec. 302-2.2 May I relocate to my new official station before I receive

No, you must have the written TA (paper or electronic) before you relocate to your new official station. [FTR Amdt. 98, 66 FR 58196, Nov. 20, 2001, as amended by FTR Amdt. 2010-07, 75 FR 72968, Nov. 29, 2010]
