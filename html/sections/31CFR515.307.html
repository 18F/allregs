CFR /  Title 31  /  Part 515  /  Sec. 515.307 Unblocked national.

Any person licensed pursuant to Sec. 515.505 as an unblocked national shall, while so licensed, be regarded as a person who is not a national of any designated foreign country. [80 FR 2292, Jan. 16, 2015]
