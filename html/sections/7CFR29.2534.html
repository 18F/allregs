CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 29  /  Sec. 29.2534 Mixed color or variegated (M).

Distinctly different colors of the type mingled together, or any leaf of which 20 percent or more of its surface is off brown, grayish, mottled, or bleached and does not blend with the normal colors of the type or group. (See Rule 16, Sec. 29.2632.) [37 FR 13626, July 12, 1972. Redesignated at 49 FR 16757, Apr. 20, 1984]
