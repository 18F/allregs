CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 1401  /  1401.670 Contracting officers' representatives.

When a CO elects to appoint an individual to act as an authorized representative in the administration of a contract, the CO must notify the contractor of the COR appointment in writing, and provide the name and contact information of the COR. [76 FR 50141, Aug. 12, 2011]
