CFR /  Title 29  /  Part 1952  /  Sec. 1952.291 Developmental schedule.

The following is a summary of the major developmental steps provided by the plan:

(a) Training of enforcement personnel to be completed--July 1, 1974.

(b) Application of the program to State and local employees to take effect--July 1, 1974.

(c) Not less than two industrial hygiene experts shall participate in the program--July 1, 1975.

(d) Proposed amendments to the Nevada Occupational Safety and Health Act to have been adopted and to take effect--July 1, 1975.

(e) System of recordkeeping and reporting fully developed and operational--January 1, 1977.

(f) Program to be fully implemented--January 1, 1977. [39 FR 1009, Jan. 4, 1974. Redesignated at 52 FR 34383, Sept. 11, 1987]
