CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 236  /  236.604 Performance evaluation.

Prepare a separate performance evaluation after actual construction of the project. Ordinarily, the evaluating official should be the person most familiar with the architect-engineer contractor's performance. [76 FR 58155, Sept. 20, 2011]
