CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 795  /  Sec. 795.5 Timing for determining status of persons.

Except as otherwise set forth in this part, the status of individuals or entities as of March 1, or such other date as may be determined and announced by the Administrator shall be the basis on which determinations are made in accordance with this part for the year for which the determination is made. [51 FR 21836, June 16, 1986; 51 FR 36905, Oct. 16, 1986]
