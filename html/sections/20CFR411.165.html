CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 411  /  Sec. 411.165 How does using a ticket under the Ticket to Work program

We periodically review your case to determine if you are still disabled under our rules. However, we will not begin a continuing disability review during the period in which you are using a ticket. Sections 411.170 and 411.171 describe when the period of using a ticket begins and ends. You must meet certain requirements for us to consider you to be using a ticket. [66 FR 67420, Dec. 28, 2001, as amended at 73 FR 29340, May 20, 2008]
