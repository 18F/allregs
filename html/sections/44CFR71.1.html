CFR /  Title 44  /  Part 71  /  Sec. 71.1 Purpose of part.

This part implements section 11 of the Coastal Barrier Resources Act (Pub. L. 97-348) and section 9 of the Coastal Barrier Improvement Act of 1990 (Pub. L. 101-591), as those Acts amend the National Flood Insurance Act of 1968 (42 U.S.C. 4001 et seq.). [48 FR 37039, Aug. 16, 1983, as amended at 57 FR 22661, May 29, 1992]
