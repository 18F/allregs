CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 435  /  Sec. 435.326 Individuals who would be ineligible if they were not

If the agency provides Medicaid to the categorically needy under Sec. 435.212, it may provide it under the same rules to medically needy beneficiaries who are enrolled in MCOs or PCCMs. [67 FR 41095, June 14, 2002]
