CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 385  /  Sec. 385.323 May FMCSA extend the period under Sec. 385.319(c) for apractices?

(a) FMCSA may extend the 60-day period in Sec. 385.319(c)(1) for up to an additional 60 days provided FMCSA determines the new entrant is making a good faith effort to remedy its safety management practices.

(b) FMCSA may extend the 45-day period in Sec. 385.319(c)(2) for up to an additional 10 days if the new entrant has submitted evidence that corrective actions have been taken pursuant to Sec. 385.319(c) and the Agency needs additional time to determine the adequacy of the corrective action. [73 FR 76490, Dec. 16, 2008]
