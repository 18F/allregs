CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 241  /  Sec. 241.1205 Payment of insurance benefits.

All the provisions of Sec. 207.259 of this chapter relating to insurance benefits shall apply to an equity or acquisition loan insured under subpart F of this part. [80 FR 51469, Aug. 25, 2015]
