CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 18  /  Sec. 18.4 Electrical equipment for which approval is issued.

An approval will be issued only for a complete electrical machine or accessory. Only components meeting the requirements of subpart B of this part or those approved under part 7 of this chapter, unless they contain intrinsically safe circuits, shall be included in the assemblies. [57 FR 61209, Dec. 23, 1992]
