CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 953  /  Sec. 953.122 Fiscal period.

The fiscal period, pursuant to Sec. 953.9, which began on April 1, 1976, shall end on May 31, 1977. Thereafter, each fiscal period shall begin on June 1 of each year and end on May 31 of the following year, both dates inclusive. [41 FR 42184, Sept. 27, 1976]
