CFR /  Title 12  /  Part 1081  /  Sec. 1081.300 Public hearings.

All hearings in adjudication proceedings shall be public unless a confidentiality order is entered by the hearing officer pursuant to Sec. 1081.119 or unless otherwise ordered by the Director on the grounds that holding an open hearing would be contrary to the public interest.
