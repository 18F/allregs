CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 101-28  /  Sec. 101-28.203-4 Contact point.

The point within the headquarters of a military service or civilian agency to which requests should be forwarded. Coordination necessary with various organizational elements within a military service or civilian agency shall be accomplished by the contact point. [42 FR 2317, Jan. 11, 1977]
