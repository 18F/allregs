CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 873  /  Sec. 873.11 Applicability.

The provisions of this part apply to funds awarded, as defined in Sec. 872.5 of this chapter, under section 402(g)(6)(A) of SMCRA before its amendment on December 20, 2006, and their use by the States or Indian tribes for coal reclamation purposes after September 30, 1995. [73 FR 67638, Nov. 14, 2008]
