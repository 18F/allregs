CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 710  /  Sec. 710.7 Inspection of registrations.

A copy of the Form FD-2511 filed by the registrant will be available for inspection at the Food and Drug Administration, 5100 Paint Branch Pkwy., College Park, MD 20740. [39 FR 10059, Mar. 15, 1974, as amended at 68 FR 15355, Mar. 31, 2003]
