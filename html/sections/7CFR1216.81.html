CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 1216  /  Sec. 1216.81 Implementation of the Order.

The Order shall not become effective unless:

(a) The Secretary determines that the Order is consistent with and will effectuate the purposes of the Act; and

(b) The Order is approved by a simple majority of the peanut producers as defined in Sec. 1216.21 voting in a referendum who, during a representative period determined by the Secretary, have been engaged in the production of peanuts.
