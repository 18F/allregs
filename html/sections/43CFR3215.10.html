CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 3215  /  Sec. 3215.10 When may BLM collect against my bond?

If you fail to comply with the requirements listed at Sec. 3200.4, we may collect money from the bond to correct your noncompliance. This amount can be as large as the face amount of the bond. Some examples of when we will collect against your bond are when you do not properly or in a timely manner:

(a) Plug and abandon a well;

(b) Reclaim the lease area;

(c) Pay outstanding royalties; or

(d) Pay assessed royalties to compensate for drainage.
