CFR /  Title 11  /  Part 4  /  Sec. 4.3 Scope.

The regulations in this part implement the provisions of the Freedom of Information Act, 5 U.S.C. 552, with respect to the availability of records for inspection and copying. [44 FR 33368, June 8, 1979, as amended at 45 FR 31291, May 13, 1980]
