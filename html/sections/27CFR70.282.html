CFR /  Title 27  /  Part 70  /  Sec. 70.282 Single bond in lieu of multiple bonds.

In the case of bonds required under this part, a single bond will not be accepted in lieu of two or more bonds. (26 U.S.C. 7102) [T.D. ATF-301, 55 FR 47650, Nov. 14, 1990]
