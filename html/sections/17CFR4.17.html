CFR /  Title 17  /  Part 4  /  Sec. 4.17 Severability.

If any provision of this part, or the application thereof to any person or circumstances, is held invalid, such invalidity shall not affect other provisions or application of such provision to other persons or circumstances which can be given effect without the invalid provision or application. [78 FR 52333, Aug. 22, 2013]
