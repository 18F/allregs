CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 117  /  Sec. 117.503 Tensas River.

The draws of the S15 bridge, mile 27.3 at Clayton, and the S128 bridge, mile 61.0 at New Light, shall open on signal if at least 48 hours notice is given. [CGD08-04-034, 69 FR 60556, Oct. 12, 2004]
