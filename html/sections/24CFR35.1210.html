CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 35  /  Sec. 35.1210 Notices and pamphlet.

(a) Notice. In cases where evaluation or paint stabilization is undertaken, the owner shall provide a notice to residents in accordance with Sec. 35.125. A visual assessment alone is not considered an evaluation for purposes of this part.

(b) Lead hazard information pamphlet. The owner shall provide the lead hazard information pamphlet in accordance with Sec. 35.130. [64 FR 50216, Sept. 15, 1999, as amended at 69 FR 34273, June 21, 2004]
