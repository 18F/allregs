CFR /  Title 14  /  Part 1203  /  Sec. 1203.304 Internal effect.

The effect of security protection on program progess and cost and on other functional activities of NASA should be considered. Impeditive effects and added costs inherent in a security classification must be assessed and weighed against the detrimental effects on the national security interests which would result from failure to classify. [44 FR 34913, June 18, 1979, as amended at 78 FR 5118, Jan. 24, 2013]
