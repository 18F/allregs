CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 300  /  Sec. 300.228 State agency eligibility.

Any State agency that desires to receive a subgrant for any fiscal year under Sec. 300.705 must demonstrate to the satisfaction of the SEA that--

(a) All children with disabilities who are participating in programs and projects funded under Part B of the Act receive FAPE, and that those children and their parents are provided all the rights and procedural safeguards described in this part; and

(b) The agency meets the other conditions of this subpart that apply to LEAs. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1413(h))
