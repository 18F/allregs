CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 102-73  /  Sec. 102-73.150 What is the policy for categorical space delegations?

Subject to the limitations cited in Sec. Sec. 102-73.230 through 102-73.240, all Federal agencies are authorized to acquire the types of space listed in Sec. 102-73.155 and, except where otherwise noted, may lease space for terms, including all options, of up to 20 years.
