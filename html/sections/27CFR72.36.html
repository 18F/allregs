CFR /  Title 27  /  Part 72  /  Sec. 72.36 Place of filing.

The petition should be filed in triplicate with the Director or his delegate for the region in which the seizure was made. [T.D. ATF-9, 39 FR 9954, Mar. 15, 1974, as amended by T.D. ATF-183, 49 FR 37061, Sept. 21, 1984]
