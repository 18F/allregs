CFR /  Title 36  /  Part 1120  /  Sec. 1120.13 Effect of nonavailability.

Any material listed in paragraph (a) of this section that is not indexed as required by Sec. 1120.11(e) and Sec. 1120.12, may not be cited, relied on, or used as precedent by the Board to adversely affect any member of the public unless the person against whom it is cited, relied on, or used has had actual and timely notice of the material.
