CFR /  Title 1  /  Part 18  /  Sec. 18.6 Form of certification.

Each copy of each document submitted for filing and publication, except a Presidential document or a duplicate original, must be certified as follows: (Certified to be a true copy of the original) The certification must be signed by a certifying officer designated under Sec. 16.1 of this chapter. [54 FR 9681, Mar. 7, 1989]
