CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 109  /  Sec. 109.555 Propulsion boilers.

The master or person in charge and the engineer in charge shall ensure that--

(a) Steam pressure does not exceed that allowed by the certificate of inspection; and

(b) The safety valves, once set, are not tampered with or made inoperative. [CGD 73-251, 43 FR 56828, Dec. 4, 1978, as amended by CGD 95-028, 62 FR 51208, Sept. 30, 1997]
