CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 180  /  Sec. 180.1250 C8, C10, and C12 fatty acid monoesters of glycerol and

The C8, C10, and C12 straight-chain fatty acid monoesters of glycerol (glycerol monocaprylate, glycerol monocaprate, and glycerol monolaurate) and propylene glycol (propylene glycol monocaprylate, propylene glycol monocaprate, and propylene glycol monolaurate) are exempt from the requirement of a tolerance in or on all food commodities when used in accordance with approved label rates and good agricultural practice. [69 FR 34944, June 23, 2004]
