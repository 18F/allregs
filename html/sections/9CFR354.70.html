CFR /  Title 9  /  Part 354  /  Sec. 354.70 Evidence of label approval.

No inspector shall authorize the use of official identification for any inspected product unless he has on file evidence that such official identification or packaging material bearing such official identification has been approved in accordance with the provisions of Sec. Sec. 354.60 to 354.64.
