CFR /  Title 36  /  Part 14  /  Sec. 14.31 Deviation from approved right-of-way.

No deviation from the location of an approved right-of-way shall be undertaken without the prior written approval of the authorized officer. The authorized officer may require the filing of an amended application in accordance with Sec. 14.20 wherein the authorized officer's judgment the deviation is substantial.
