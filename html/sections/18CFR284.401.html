CFR /  Title 18  /  Part 284  /  Sec. 284.401 Definitions.

Affiliated marketer. For purposes of this subpart, an ``affiliated marketer'' is a person engaged in the ``marketing'' of natural gas that is an ``affiliate'' of an interstate pipeline as those terms are defined in Sec. 161.2 of this chapter. [Order 547, 57 FR 57959, Dec. 8, 1992]
