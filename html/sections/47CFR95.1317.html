CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 95  /  Sec. 95.1317 Grandfathered MURS Stations.

Stations that were licensed under part 90 of the Commission's Rules to operate on MURS frequencies as of November 13, 2000, are granted a license by rule that authorizes continued operations under the terms of such nullified part 90 authorizations, including any rule waivers. [67 FR 63290, Oct. 11, 2002]
