CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 105  /  Sec. 105.01-1 Purpose.

The purpose of the regulations in this part is to provide adequate safety in the transporting and handling of inflammable or combustible cargo in bulk on board certain commercial fishing vessels and tenders. [CGD 95-028, 62 FR 51208, Sept. 30, 1997]
