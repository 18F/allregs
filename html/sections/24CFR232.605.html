CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 232  /  Sec. 232.605 Contract requirements.

The contract between the mortgagor and the general contractor may be in the form of a lump sum contract, a cost plus contract, or different or alternative forms of contract specified by the Commissioner. [80 FR 48028, Aug. 11, 2015]
