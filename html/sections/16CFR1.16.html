CFR /  Title 16  /  Part 1  /  Sec. 1.16 Petition for exemption from trade regulation rule.

Any person to whom a rule would otherwise apply may petition the Commission for an exemption from such rule. The procedures for determining such a petition shall be those of subpart C of these rules. [40 FR 33966, Aug. 13, 1975] Sec. 1.17 [Reserved]
