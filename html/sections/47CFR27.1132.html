CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 27  /  Sec. 27.1132 Protection of incumbent operations in the 2150-2160/62

All AWS licensees, prior to initiating operations from any base or fixed station in the 2110-2180 MHz band, shall follow the provisions of Sec. 27.1255. [79 FR 32414, June 4, 2014]
