CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 302-11  /  Sec. 302-11.5 To be reimbursed for expenses incurred in my residence transactions,

Yes, to be reimbursed for expenses incurred in your residence transactions, you must occupy the residence at the time you are notified of your transfer, unless your transfer is from a foreign area to an official station within the United States other than the one you left when you transferred out of the United States, as specified in Sec. 302-11.2(b).
