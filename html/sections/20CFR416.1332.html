CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 416  /  Sec. 416.1332 Termination of benefit for disabled individual:

Special SSI cash benefits (see Sec. 416.261) will be payable for the period beginning January 1, 1981, and ending June 30, 1987 if you meet eligibility requirements in Sec. 416.262. These requirements apply if you, as a disabled recipient, are no longer eligible for regular SSI benefits because you demonstrate that you are able to engage in SGA. [47 FR 15325, Apr. 9, 1982, as amended at 50 FR 46763, Nov. 13, 1985]
