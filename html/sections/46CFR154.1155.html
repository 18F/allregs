CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 154  /  Sec. 154.1155 Hand hose line: Coverage.

The coverage for the area for a hand hose line under Sec. 154.1150 must not exceed the length of the hand hose line except the coverage for the protection of areas that are inaccessible to personnel must not exceed one-half the projection of the hose at its rated discharge, or 10 m (32.8 ft.), whichever is less.
