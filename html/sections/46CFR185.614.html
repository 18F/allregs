CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 185  /  Sec. 185.614 Portable watertight containers for distress flares and

Portable watertight containers for distress flares and smoke signals shall be of a bright color, and containers shall be clearly marked in legible contrasting letters at least 12.7 millimeters (0.5 inches) high: ``DISTRESS SIGNALS''. [CGD 85-080, 61 FR 1005, Jan. 10, 1996; 61 FR 24465, May 15, 1996]
