CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 147  /  Sec. 147.3014 Construction requirements for Class III wells.

(a) In addition to the requirements of Sec. 146.32(c)(3) of this chapter, radiological characteristics of the formation fluids shall be provided to the Director.

(b) In addition to the requirements of Sec. 146.32(e) of this chapter, the Director may require monitoring wells to be completed into USDWs below the injection zone if those USDWs may be affected by mining operations.
