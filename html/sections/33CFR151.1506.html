CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 151  /  Sec. 151.1506 Restriction of operation.

No vessel subject to the requirements of this subpart may be operated in the Great Lakes or the Hudson River, north of the George Washington Bridge, unless the master of the vessel has certified, in accordance with Sec. 151.1516, that the requirements of this subpart have been met. [CGD 94-003, 59 FR 67634, Dec. 30, 1994]
