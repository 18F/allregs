CFR /  Title 25  /  Part 226  /  Sec. 226.39 What fees must lessee pay to a surface owner for tank

The lessee must pay fees for each tank sited at the rate of $500 per tank, except that:

(a) No payment is due for a tank temporarily set on a well location site for drilling, completing, or testing; and

(b) The sum to be paid for a tank occupying an area more than 2500 square feet will be agreed upon between the surface owner and lessee or, on failure to agree, the same will be determined by arbitration as provided by Sec. 226.41.
