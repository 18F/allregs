CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 413  /  Sec. 413.30 What are the restrictions on the use of funds?

(a) A National Center that performs both research and development activities and dissemination and training activities shall use at least two-thirds of its award for applied research and development.

(b) Not more than 10 percent of each year's budget for a National Center may be used to respond to field-initiated needs unanticipated prior to the annual funding period and that are in the mission of the National Center, but not part of the scope of work of the grant or cooperative agreement. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 2404(a)(3) and (b))
