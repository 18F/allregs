CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 75  /  Sec. 75.525 Conflict of interest: Participation in a project.

(a) A grantee may not permit a person to participate in an administrative decision regarding a project if:

(1) The decision is likely to benefit that person or a member of his or her immediate family; and

(2) The person:

(i) Is a public official; or

(ii) Has a family or business relationship with the grantee.

(b) A grantee may not permit any person participating in the project to use his or her position for a purpose that is--or gives the appearance of being--motivated by a desire for a private financial gain for that person or for others. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1221e-3 and 3474)
