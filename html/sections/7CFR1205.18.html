CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 1205  /  Sec. 1205.18 Producer.

The term producer means any person who shares in a cotton crop, or in the proceeds thereof, as an owner of the farm, cash tenant, landlord of a share tenant, share tenant, or sharecropper, that planted the cotton during the representative period. [67 FR 21169, Apr. 30, 2002]
