CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 3430  /  Sec. 3430.1010 Review criteria.

Panel reviewers conducting merit reviews on proposals submitted by the Centers will be instructed to ensure that proposals adequately address the plan developed in accordance with Sec. 3430.1007 for consideration of the relevance and merit of proposals.
