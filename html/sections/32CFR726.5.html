CFR /  Title 32  /  Part 726  /  Sec. 726.5 Procedures for designation of a trustee.

Upon receipt of a report of a competency board that a member has been found mentally incapable of managing his financial affairs, DFAS-CL(CGA) will initiate action to appoint a trustee, provided no notice of appointment of a committee, guardian, or other legal representative by a State court of competent jurisdiction has been received by DFAS-CL(CGA). [73 FR 64207, Oct. 29, 2008]
