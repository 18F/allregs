CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 401  /  Sec. 401.85 Reporting of impairment or other hazard by vessels intending

The master of any vessel which intends to transit the Seaway shall report to the nearest Seaway Station, prior to entering the Seaway, any of the conditions set out in paragraphs (a) through (d) of Sec. 401.84. (68 Stat. 93-96, 33 U.S.C. 981-990, as amended and secs. 4, 5, 6, 7, 8, 12 and 13 of sec. 2 of Pub. L. 95-474, 92 Stat. 1471) [45 FR 52380, Aug. 7, 1980]
