CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 2520  /  Sec. 2520.45 How much time may an AmeriCorps member spend fundraising?

An AmeriCorps member may spend no more than ten percent of his or her originally agreed-upon term of service, as reflected in the member enrollment in the National Service Trust, performing fundraising activities, as described in Sec. 2520.40. [70 FR 39597, July 8, 2005]
