CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 303  /  303.303 Reporting suspected antitrust violations.

(h) The HCA shall provide a copy of the draft OPDIV report of suspected antitrust violations to the SPE. If the SPE concurs with the draft report, the SPE will provide it to the OGC-GLD for its review. If the OGD-GLD concurs with the draft report, the SPE will provide the signed OGC-approved report to the Attorney General.
