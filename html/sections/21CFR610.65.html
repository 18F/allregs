CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 610  /  Sec. 610.65 Products for export.

Labels on packages or containers of products for export may be adapted to meet specific requirements of the regulations of the country to which the product is to be exported provided that in all such cases the minimum label requirements prescribed in Sec. 610.60 are observed.
