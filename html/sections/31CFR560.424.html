CFR /  Title 31  /  Part 560  /  Sec. 560.424 Payments from blocked accounts to satisfy obligations

Pursuant to Sec. 560.211, no debits may be made to a blocked account to pay obligations to U.S. persons or other persons, except as authorized by or pursuant to this part.

Note to Sec. 560.424: See also Sec. 560.502(f), which provides that no license or other authorization contained in or issued pursuant to this part authorizes transfers of or payments from blocked property or debits to blocked accounts unless the license or other authorization explicitly authorizes the transfer of or payment from blocked property or the debit to a blocked account.
