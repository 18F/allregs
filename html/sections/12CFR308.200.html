CFR /  Title 12  /  Part 308  /  Sec. 308.200 Scope.

The rules and procedures set forth in this subpart apply to banks, insured branches of foreign banks and senior executive officers and directors of banks that are subject to the provisions of section 38 of the Federal Deposit Insurance Act (section 38) (12 U.S.C. 1831o) and subpart B of part 325 of this chapter or subpart H of part 324 of this chapter, as applicable. [78 FR 55470, Sept. 10, 2013]
