CFR /  Title 36  /  Part 3  /  Sec. 3.7 What are the NPS Personal Floatation Device (PFD) requirements?

(a) All requirements in Title 33 CFR part 175 related to PFDs are adopted.

(b) The Superintendent may require that a PFD be worn or carried on designated waters, at designated times and/or during designated water based activities in accordance with Sec. Sec. 1.5 and 1.7 of this chapter.
