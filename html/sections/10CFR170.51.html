CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 170  /  Sec. 170.51 Right to review and appeal of prescribed fees.

All debtors' requests for review of the fees assessed and appeal or disagreement with the prescribed fee (staff hours and contractual) must be submitted in accordance with the provisions of 10 CFR 15.31, ``Disputed Debts,'' of this title. [49 FR 21309, May 21, 1984; 49 FR 24113, June 12, 1984]
