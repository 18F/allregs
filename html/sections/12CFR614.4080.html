CFR /  Title 12  /  Part 614  /  Sec. 614.4080 Loans and chartered territory--banks for cooperatives.

Loans made under title III by banks for cooperatives and agricultural credit banks may be made to eligible domestic parties domiciled within any territory that may be served by Farm Credit institutions under section 1.2 of the Act and to eligible foreign parties without regard to domicile. [55 FR 24882, June 19, 1990]
