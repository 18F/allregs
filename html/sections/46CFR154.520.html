CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 154  /  Sec. 154.520 Piping calculations.

A piping system must be designed to meet the allowable stress values under Sec. 56.07-10 of this chapter and, if the design temperature is - 110 [deg]C (-166 [deg]F) or lower, the stress analysis must be specially approved by the Commandant (CG-ENG) and must include:

(a) Pipe weight loads;

(b) Acceleration loads;

(c) Internal pressure loads;

(d) Thermal loads; and

(e) Loads from the hull. [CGD 74-289, 44 FR 26009, May 3, 1979, as amended by CGD 82-063b, 48 FR 4782, Feb. 3, 1983]
