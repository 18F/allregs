CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 9  /  Sec. 9.85 Fees.

Fees shall not be charged for search or review of records requested under this subpart or for making copies or extracts of records to make them available for review, although fees may be charged for additional copies. Fees established under 31 U.S.C. 483c and 5 U.S.C. 552a(f)(5) shall be charged according to the schedule contained in Sec. 9.35 for actual copies of records disclosed under the Freedom of Information Act from Privacy Act Systems of Records. [70 FR 34309, June 14, 2005]
