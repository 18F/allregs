CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 234  /  Sec. 234.751 Cross-reference.

(a) All of the provisions, except Sec. 207.258(b) of subpart B of this chapter, covering mortgages insured under section 207 of the National Housing Act shall apply to mortgages insured under section 234(d) of such Act.

(b) For the purposes of this subpart, all references in part 207 of this chapter to section 207 of the National Housing Act shall be construed to refer to section 234(d) of the act. [36 FR 24628, Dec. 22, 1971, as amended at 50 FR 38787, Sept. 25, 1985]
