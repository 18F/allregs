CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 74  /  Sec. 74.6 Licensing of broadcast auxiliary and low power auxiliary

Applicants for and licensees of remote pickup broadcast stations, aural broadcast auxiliary stations, television broadcast auxiliary stations, and low power auxiliary stations authorized under subparts D, E, F, and H of this part are subject to the application and procedural rules for wireless telecommunications services contained in part 1, subpart F of this chapter. Applicants for these stations may file either manually or electronically as specified in Sec. 1.913(b) and (d) of this chapter. [68 FR 12761, Mar. 17, 2003]
