CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 3137  /  Sec. 3137.28 What oil and gas resources of committed tracts does the unit agreement include?

A unit agreement includes all oil and gas resources of committed tracts unless BLM approves unit agreement terms to the contrary pursuant to Sec. 3137.50 of this subpart.
