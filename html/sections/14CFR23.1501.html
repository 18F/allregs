CFR /  Title 14  /  Part 23  /  Sec. 23.1501 General.

(a) Each operating limitation specified in Sec. Sec. 23.1505 through 23.1527 and other limitations and information necessary for safe operation must be established.

(b) The operating limitations and other information necessary for safe operation must be made available to the crewmembers as prescribed in Sec. Sec. 23.1541 through 23.1589. [Amdt. 23-21, 43 FR 2319, Jan. 16, 1978]
