CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 3600  /  Sec. 3600.4 Authority to act for the Administrator.

In the absence of the Administrator, the following officials are designated to serve as Acting Administrator in the order indicated: Associate AdministratorDeputy Administrator for Field OperationsDirector, Estimates DivisionDirector, Survey Management DivisionDirector, Systems and Information Division Director, Research Division
