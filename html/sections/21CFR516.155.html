CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 516  /  Sec. 516.155 Labeling of indexed drugs.

(a) The labeling of an indexed drug that is found to be eligible for indexing under Sec. 516.129(c)(7)(i) shall state, prominently and conspicuously: ``NOT APPROVED BY FDA.--Legally marketed as an FDA indexed product. Extra-label use is prohibited.'' ``This product is not to be used in animals intended for use as food for humans or other animals.''

(b) The labeling of an indexed drug that was found to be eligible for indexing for use in an early, non-food life stage of a food-producing minor species animal, under Sec. 516.129(c)(7)(ii), shall state, prominently and conspicuously: ``NOT APPROVED BY FDA.--Legally marketed as an FDA indexed product. Extra-label use is prohibited.''

(c) The labeling of an indexed drug shall contain such other information as may be prescribed in the index listing.
