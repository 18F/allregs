CFR /  Title 25  /  Part 224  /  Sec. 224.59 How will the Director use the results of the application consultation meeting?

The Director will use the information gathered during the application consultation meeting in conjunction with information provided through Sec. Sec. 224.53 and 224.63 to determine the energy resource development capacity of the tribe as detailed in Sec. 224.72.
