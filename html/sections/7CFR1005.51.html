CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 1005  /  Sec. 1005.51 Class I differential, adjustments to Class I prices,

(a) The Class I differential shall be the differential established for Mecklenburg County, North Carolina, which is reported in Sec. 1000.52. The Class I price shall be the price computed pursuant to Sec. 1005.50(a) for Mecklenburg County, North Carolina.
