CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 895  /  Sec. 895.20 General.

The Commissioner may initiate a proceeding to make a device a banned device whenever the Commissioner finds, on the basis of all available data and information, that the device presents substantial deception or an unreasonable and substantial risk of illness or injury that the Commissioner determines cannot be, or has not been, corrected or eliminated by labeling or by a change in labeling, or by a change in advertising if the device is a restricted device. [44 FR 29221, May 18, 1979, as amended at 57 FR 58405, Dec. 10, 1992]
