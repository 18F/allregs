CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 1010  /  Sec. 1010.100 What is the purpose and scope of this part?

(a)(1) of JVA (38 U.S.C. 4215). These regulations fulfill section 605 of the Veterans Benefits, Health Care, and Information Technology Act of 2006, Pub. L. 109-461 (Dec. 22, 2006), which requires the Department to implement priority of service via regulation.

(b) As provided in Sec. 1010.210, this part applies to all qualified job training programs.
