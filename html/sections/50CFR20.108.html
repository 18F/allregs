CFR /  Title 50  /  Part 20  /  Sec. 20.108 Nontoxic shot zones.

Beginning September 1, 1991, the contiguous 48 United States, and the States of Alaska and Hawaii, the Territories of Puerto Rico and the Virgin Islands, and the territorial waters of the United States, are designated for the purpose of Sec. 20.21(j) as nontoxic shot zones for hunting waterfowl, coots and certain other species. ``Certain other species'' refers to those species, other than waterfowl or coots, that are affected by reason of being included in aggregate bags and concurrent seasons. [56 FR 22102, May 13, 1991]
