CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 575  /  Sec. 575.213 Records and reports.

Each agency must keep a record of each determination to pay a relocation incentive and make such records available for review upon OPM's request. [70 FR 25743, May 13, 2005, as amended at 78 FR 49364, Aug. 14, 2013]
