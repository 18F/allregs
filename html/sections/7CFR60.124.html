CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 60  /  Sec. 60.124 Retailer.

Retailer means any person subject to be licensed as a retailer under the Perishable Agricultural Commodities Act of 1930 (7 U.S.C. 499a(b)). [78 FR 31385, May 24, 2013]
