CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 1700  /  Sec. 1700.4 Public comments on proposed rules.

RUS requires that all persons submitting comments to a proposed rule or other document published by the agency in the Federal Register, submit comments as specified in the published notice. Copies of comments submitted are available to the public in conformance with 7 CFR part 1. [71 FR 8435, Feb. 17, 2006] Sec. Sec. 1700.5-1700.24 [Reserved]
