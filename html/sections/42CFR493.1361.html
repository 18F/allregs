CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 493  /  Sec. 493.1361 Condition: Laboratories performing PPM procedures;

The laboratory must have a sufficient number of individuals who meet the qualification requirements of Sec. 493.1363 to perform the functions specified in Sec. 493.1365 for the volume and complexity of testing performed.
