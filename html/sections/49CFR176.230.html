CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 176  /  Sec. 176.230 Stowage of Division 2.1 (flammable gas) materials.

Division 2.1 (flammable gas) materials transported in Specification 106A or 110A multi-unit car tanks must be stowed on deck only, and must be protected from sources of heat. [78 FR 1095, Jan. 7, 2013] Subpart I_Detailed Requirements for Class 3 (Flammable) and Combustible

Liquid Materials
