CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 75  /  Sec. 75.234 The conditions of the grant.

(a) The Secretary makes a grant to an applicant only after determining--

(1) The approved costs; and

(2) Any special conditions.

(b) In awarding a cooperative agreement, the Secretary includes conditions that state the explicit character and extent of anticipated collaboration between the Department and the recipient. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1221e-3 and 3474) [57 FR 30338, July 8, 1992]
