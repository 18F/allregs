CFR /  Title 15  /  Part 315  /  Sec. 315.5 Maintenance and publication of a list of bona fide

The Under Secretary shall maintain and publish from time to time in the Federal Register, a list of the names and addresses of bona fide motor vehicle manufacturers, and the effective dates from each determination. [45 FR 42214, June 23, 1980. Redesignated and amended at 53 FR 52115, Dec. 27, 1988]
