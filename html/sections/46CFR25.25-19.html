CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 25  /  Sec. 25.25-19 Visual distress signals.

Each uninspected passenger vessel must meet the visual distress signal requirements of 33 CFR part 175 applicable to the vessel. [USCG-1999-5040, 67 FR 34776, May 15, 2002]
