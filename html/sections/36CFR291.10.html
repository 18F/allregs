CFR /  Title 36  /  Part 291  /  Sec. 291.10 Collecting.

A paleontological resource may only be collected from National Forest System lands in accordance with the casual collecting provisions in Sec. Sec. 291.11 and 291.12, or in accordance with a permit issued by the Authorized Officer as identified in Sec. 291.13.
