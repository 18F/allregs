CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 101-25  /  Sec. 101-25.110-3 Tires accompanying new motor vehicles.

The tire identifications and recordkeeping regulations issued by the Department of Transportation require each motor vehicle manufacturer or his designee to maintain a record of tires on or in each vehicle shipped by him together with the name and address of the first purchaser. [37 FR 7794, Apr. 20, 1972]
