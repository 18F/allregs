CFR /  Title 27  /  Part 28  /  Sec. 28.266 Release of detained merchandise.

When any merchandise has been detained under the provisions of Sec. 28.265, the district director of customs shall not release such merchandise until he is advised so to do by the appropriate TTB officer. (72 Stat. 1334, 1335, 1336, 1362, 1380; 26 U.S.C. 5053, 5055, 5062, 5214, 5362) [25 FR 5734, June 23, 1960, as amended by T.D. 7006, 34 FR 2251, Feb. 15, 1969.
