CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 60  /  Sec. 60.232 Standard for fluorides.

On and after the date on which the performance test required to be conducted by Sec. 60.8 is completed, no owner or operator subject to the provisions of this subpart shall cause to be discharged into the atmosphere from any affected facility any gases which contain total fluorides in excess of 100 g/megagram (Mg) of equivalent P2O5 feed (0.20 lb/ton). [40 FR 33156, Aug. 6, 1975, as amended at 65 FR 61757, Oct. 17, 2000]
