CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 98  /  Sec. 98.9 Decision if hearing waived.

(a) If the former employee waives or, in accordance with Sec. 98.5(b) of this part, is deemed to have waived a hearing under this part, the examiner shall, after review of the record as it exists, make a decision as to whether the former employee is in violation of 18 U.S.C. 207.

(b) In a decision under paragraph (a) of this section, the requirements of Sec. 98.8 of this part apply.
