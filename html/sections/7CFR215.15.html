CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 215  /  Sec. 215.15 Withholding payments.

In accordance with Departmental regulations at Sec. Sec. 3016.43 and 3019.62 of this title, the State agency shall withhold Program payments in whole or in part, to any school food authority which has failed to comply with the provisions of this part. Program payments shall be withheld until the school food authority takes corrective action satisfactory to the State agency, or gives evidence that such corrective actions will be taken, or until the State agency terminates the grant in accordance with Sec. 215.16. Subsequent to the State agency's acceptance of the corrective actions, payments will be released for any milk served in accordance with the provisions of this part during the period the payments were withheld. [72 FR 61493, Oct. 31, 2007]
