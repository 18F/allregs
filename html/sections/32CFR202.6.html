CFR /  Title 32  /  Part 202  /  Sec. 202.6 Selecting co-chairs.

(a) DoD installation co-chair. The DoD installation co-chair shall be selected by the Installation Commander or equivalent, or in accordance with Military Component-specific guidance.

(b) Community co-chair. The community co-chair shall be selected by the community RAB members. [71 FR 27617, May 12, 2006; 71 FR 30719, May 30, 2006]
