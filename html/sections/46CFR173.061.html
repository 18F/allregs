CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 173  /  Sec. 173.061 Watertight integrity above the margin line.

The watertight integrity of each sailing school vessel above the margin line must comply with the requirements in subpart G of part 171 of this subchapter or Sec. 179.360 in subchapter T of this chapter. [CGD 83-005, 51 FR 925, Jan. 9, 1986, as amended by CGD 85-080, 61 FR 946, Jan. 10, 1996; 61 FR 20556, May 7, 1996]
