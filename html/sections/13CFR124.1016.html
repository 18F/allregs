CFR /  Title 13  /  Part 124  /  Sec. 124.1016 What must a concern do in order to be identified as a

(a) In order to be identified as a SDB in the System for Award Management (SAM) database (or any successor thereto), a concern must certify its SDB status in connection with specific eligibility requirements at least annually.

(b) If a firm identified as a SDB in SAM fails to certify its status within one year of a status certification, the firm will not be listed as a SDB in SAM, unless and until the firm recertifies its SDB status. [78 FR 38819, June 28, 2013]
