CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 300  /  Sec. 300.804 Eligibility.

A State is eligible for a grant under section 619 of the Act if the State--

(a) Is eligible under section 612 of the Act to receive a grant under Part B of the Act; and

(b) Makes FAPE available to all children with disabilities, aged three through five, residing in the State. (Approved by the Office of Management and Budget under control number 1820-0030) (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1419(b)) Sec. 300.805 [Reserved]
