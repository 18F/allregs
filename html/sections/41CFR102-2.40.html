CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 102-2  /  Sec. 102-2.40 May an agency issue implementing and supplementing

Yes, an agency may issue implementing regulations (see Sec. 102-2.50) to expand upon related FMR material and supplementing regulations (see Sec. 102-2.55) to address subject material not covered in the FMR. The Office of the Federal Register assigns chapters in Title 41 of the Code of Federal Regulations for agency publication of implementing and supplementing regulations.
