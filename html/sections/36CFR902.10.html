CFR /  Title 36  /  Part 902  /  Sec. 902.10 Delegation of administration of this part.

Except as provided in subpart H of this part, authority to administer this part is delegated to the Administrative Officer, who shall act upon all requests for access to records which are received by the Corporation from any person citing the Act. [41 FR 43143, Sept. 30, 1976, as amended at 48 FR 17354, Apr. 22, 1983]
