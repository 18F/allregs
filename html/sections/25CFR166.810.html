CFR /  Title 25  /  Part 166  /  Sec. 166.810 How do I redeem my impounded livestock or other property?

You may redeem impounded livestock or other property by submitting proof of ownership and paying all penalties, damages, and costs under Sec. 166.812 of this subpart and completing all corrective actions identified by us under Sec. 166.804 of this subpart.
