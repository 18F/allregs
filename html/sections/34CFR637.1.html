CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 637  /  Sec. 637.1 What is the Minority Science and Engineering Improvement

The Minority Science and Engineering Improvement Program (MSEIP) is designed to effect long-range improvement in science and engineering education at predominantly minority institutions, and to increase the flow of underrepresented ethnic minorities, particularly minority women, into scientific and technological careers. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1067-1067c, 1067g-1067k, 1068, and 1068b, unless otherwise noted) [65 FR 7674, Feb. 15, 2000]
