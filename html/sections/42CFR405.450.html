CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 405  /  Sec. 405.450 Appeals.

(a) A determination by CMS that a physician or practitioner has failed to properly opt-out, failed to maintain opt-out, failed to timely renew opt-out, failed to privately contract, or failed to properly terminate opt-out is an initial determination for purposes of Sec. 498.3(b) of this chapter.

(b) A determination by CMS that no payment can be made to a beneficiary for the services of a physician who has opted-out is an initial determination for purposes of Sec. 405.924. [63 FR 58901, Nov. 2, 1998, as amended at 79 FR 68001, Nov. 13, 2014]
