CFR /  Title 29  /  Part 94  /  Sec. 94.105 Does this part apply to me?

(1) A recipient of an assistance award from the Department of Labor; or

(2) A(n) Department of Labor awarding official. (See definitions of award and recipient in Sec. Sec. 94.605 and 94.660, respectively.)
