CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 403  /  Sec. 403.188 What is a State's responsibility for the cost of services

A State is not required to use non-Federal funds to pay the cost of services and activities that it provides to members of special populations pursuant to Sec. 403.32(a) (18)-(26) or to pay the cost of services and activities that eligible recipients provide to members of special populations pursuant to Sec. Sec. 403.111 (a)(2)(i) and (c)(3), 403.190(b), or 403.193, unless this requirement is imposed by other applicable laws. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 2328)
