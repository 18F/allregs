CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 831  /  Sec. 831.3 Authority of Directors.

The Directors, Office of Aviation Safety, Office of Railroad Safety, Office of Highway Safety, Office of Marine Safety, and Office of Pipeline and Hazardous Materials Safety, subject to the provisions of Sec. 831.2 and part 800 of this chapter, may order an investigation into any accident or incident. [63 FR 71606, Dec. 29, 1998]
