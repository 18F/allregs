CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 21  /  Sec. 21.8410 Delegation of authority.

The Secretary delegates authority for making findings and decisions under 38 U.S.C. 1804 and 1814 and the applicable regulations, precedents, and instructions for the program under this subpart to the Under Secretary for Benefits and to VR&E; supervisory or non-supervisory staff members. (Authority: 38 U.S.C. 512(a), 1804, 1814) Subparts N-O [Reserved]
