CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 415  /  Sec. 415.180 Teaching setting requirements for the interpretation

(a) General rule. Physician fee schedule payment is made for the interpretation of diagnostic radiology and other diagnostic tests if the interpretation is performed or reviewed by a physician other than a resident.

(b) Documentation. Documentation must indicate that the physician personally performed the interpretation or reviewed the resident's interpretation with the resident.
