CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 1  /  Sec. 1.200 Purpose.

This subpart establishes a duty upon and sets forth the mechanism for VA employees to report information about actual or possible criminal violations to appropriate law enforcement entities. (Authority: 5 U.S.C. App. 3, 38 U.S.C. 902) [68 FR 17550, Apr. 10, 2003]
