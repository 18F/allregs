CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 3801  /  Sec. 3801.1 General.

This part is issued in accordance with the regulations of the Secretary of Agriculture in Sec. Sec. 1.1-1.23 of this title and Appendix A thereto, implementing the Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) (5 U.S.C. 552), and governs the availability of records of the World Agricultural Outlook Board (WAOB) to the public.
