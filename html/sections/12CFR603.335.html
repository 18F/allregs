CFR /  Title 12  /  Part 603  /  Sec. 603.335 Agency review of request for amendment of record.

Upon receipt of a request for amendment of a record, the Privacy Act Officer shall:

(a) Correct any portion of a record which the individual making the request believes is not accurate, relevant, timely, or complete and thereafter inform the individual in writing of such correction, or

(b) Inform the individual in writing of refusal to amend the record and of the reasons therefor, and advise that the individual may appeal such determination as provided in Sec. 603.340 of this part. [40 FR 40454, Sept. 2, 1975, as amended at 51 FR 41942, Nov. 20, 1986]
