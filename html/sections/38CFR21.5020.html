CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 21  /  Sec. 21.5020 Post-Vietnam era veterans' educational assistance.

Title 38 U.S.C. Chapter 32 provides for a participatory program for educational assistance benefits to eligible veterans and servicepersons. The intent of the Congress for this program is stated in 38 U.S.C. 3201. (Authority: 38 U.S.C. 3201) [61 FR 29029, June 7, 1996]
