CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 418  /  Sec. 418.1330 Can you request a reconsideration when you believe

If you request a reconsideration solely because you believe that the information that IRS gave us is incorrect, we will dismiss your request for a reconsideration and notify you to obtain proof of a correction from IRS and request a new initial determination (Sec. 418.1335). Our dismissal of your request for reconsideration is not an initial determination subject to further administrative or judicial review.
