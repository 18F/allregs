CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 3415  /  Sec. 3415.605 Content of unsolicited proposals.

(d) Each unsolicited proposal must contain the following certification:

Unsolicited Proposal Certification by Offeror

This is to certify, to the best of my knowledge and belief, that--

a. This proposal has not been prepared under Federal government supervision;

b. The methods and approaches stated in the proposal were developed by this offeror;

c. Any contact with employees of the Department of Education has been within the limits of appropriate advance guidance set forth in FAR 15.604; and

d. No prior commitments were received from Departmental employees regarding acceptance of this proposal. Date:________________________________________________________________________ Organization:________________________________________________________________________ Name:________________________________________________________________________ Title:________________________________________________________________________ (This certification must be signed by a responsible person authorized to enter into contracts on behalf of the organization.)
