CFR /  Title 9  /  Part 319  /  Sec. 319.703 Rendered animal fat or mixture thereof.

``Rendered Animal Fat,'' or any mixture of fats containing edible rendered animal fat, shall contain no added water, except that ``Puff Pastry Shortening'' may contain not more than 10 percent of water. [35 FR 15597, Oct. 3, 1970, as amended at 43 FR 25420, June 13, 1978]
