CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 1112  /  Sec. 1112.11 Authority of officers.

Except to the extent that they apply only to the conduct of a public hearing, the officer assigned to handle a proceeding under the modified procedure shall have the same authority as officers assigned to conduct oral hearings as described in Sec. 1113.3(a) and (b).
