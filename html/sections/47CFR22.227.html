CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 22  /  Sec. 22.227 Petitions to deny and limitations on settlements.

(a) Procedures regarding petitions to deny long-form applications in the paging service will be governed by Sec. 1.939 of this chapter.

(b) The consideration that an individual or an entity will be permitted to receive for agreeing to withdraw an application or petition to deny will be limited by the provisions set forth in Sec. 1.935 of this chapter. [67 FR 45367, July 9, 2002]
