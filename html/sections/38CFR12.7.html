CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 12  /  Sec. 12.7 Cases not applicable to provisions of Sec. Sec. 12.0 to 12.6.

The provisions of Sec. Sec. 12.0 to 12.6 shall be inapplicable to property known to be that of any person dying in or discharged or absent without leave from a Department of Veterans Affairs field facility other than a veteran admitted as such to such field facility. [13 FR 7129, Nov. 27, 1948]
