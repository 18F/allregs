CFR /  Title 32  /  Part 1627  /  Sec. 1627.2 Registration of volunteers.

(a) If a person who is required to be registered but who has failed to register volunteers for induction, he shall be registered.

(b) In registering a volunteer, the area office shall follow the procedure set forth in Sec. 1615.3 of this chapter.
