CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 800  /  Sec. 800.128 Conflicts of interest..

Official personnel cannot perform or participate in performing or issue an official certificate for a reinspection or a review of weighing service if they participated in the original service unless there is only one qualified person available at the time and place of the reinspection or review of weighing. [50 FR 45394, Oct. 31, 1985]
