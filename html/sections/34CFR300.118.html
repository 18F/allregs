CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 300  /  Sec. 300.118 Children in public or private institutions.

Except as provided in Sec. 300.149(d) (regarding agency responsibility for general supervision of some individuals in adult prisons), an SEA must ensure that Sec. 300.114 is effectively implemented, including, if necessary, making arrangements with public and private institutions (such as a memorandum of agreement or special implementation procedures). (Approved by the Office of Management and Budget under control number 1820-0030) (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1412(a)(5)) [71 FR 46753, Aug. 14, 2006, as amended at 72 FR 61306, Oct. 30, 2007]
