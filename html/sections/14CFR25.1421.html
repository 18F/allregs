CFR /  Title 14  /  Part 25  /  Sec. 25.1421 Megaphones.

If a megaphone is installed, a restraining means must be provided that is capable of restraining the megaphone when it is subjected to the ultimate inertia forces specified in Sec. 25.561(b)(3). [Amdt. 25-41, 42 FR 36970, July 18, 1977]
