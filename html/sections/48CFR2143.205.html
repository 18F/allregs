CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 2143  /  2143.205 Contract clause.

The clause at 2152.243-70 shall be inserted in all FEGLI Program contracts in lieu of the clauses in FAR 52.243-1 that are prescribed by FAR 43.205(a).
