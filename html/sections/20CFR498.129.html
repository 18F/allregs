CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 498  /  Sec. 498.129 Notice to other agencies.

As provided in section 1129 of the Social Security Act, when a determination to impose a penalty and assessment, as applicable, with respect to a physician or medical provider becomes final, the Office of the Inspector General will notify the Secretary of the final determination and the reasons therefore. [61 FR 18081, Apr. 24, 1996]
