CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 52  /  Sec. 52.135 Resources.

(a) The requirements of Sec. 51.280 of this chapter are not met because the transportation control plan does not contain a sufficient description of resources available to the State and local agencies and of additional resources needed to carry out the plan during the 5-year period following submittal. [38 FR 16564, June 22, 1973, as amended at 51 FR 40677, Nov. 7, 1986]
