CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 1150  /  Sec. 1150.135 Appointments.

From the nominations made pursuant to Sec. 1150.133, the Secretary shall appoint the members of the Board on the bases of representation provided for in Sec. Sec. 1150.131(b) and 1150.131(c). [76 FR 14789, Mar. 18, 2011]
