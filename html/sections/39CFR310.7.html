CFR /  Title 39  /  Part 310  /  Sec. 310.7 Amendment of regulations.

Amendments of the regulations in this part and in part 320 may be made only in accordance with the rulemaking provisions of the Administrative Procedure Act. [40 FR 23295, May 29, 1975]
