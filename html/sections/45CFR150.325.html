CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 150  /  Sec. 150.325 Settlement authority.

Nothing in Sec. Sec. 150.315 through 150.323 limits the authority of CMS to settle any issue or case described in the notice furnished in accordance with Sec. 150.307 or to compromise on any penalty provided for in Sec. Sec. 150.315 through 150.323.
