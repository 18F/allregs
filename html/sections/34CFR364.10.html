CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 364  /  Sec. 364.10 What are the application requirements?

To receive a grant from a State's allotment of funds under parts B and C of chapter 1 of title VII of the Act and 34 CFR parts 365 and 366, a State shall submit to the Secretary, and obtain approval of, a three- year State plan meeting the requirements in subpart C of this part. (Approved by the Office of Management and Budget under control number 1820-0527) (Authority: 29 U.S.C. 796c(a)(1))
