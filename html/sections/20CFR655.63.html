CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 655  /  Sec. 655.63 Public disclosure.

The Department will maintain an electronic file accessible to the public with information on all employers applying for temporary nonagricultural labor certifications. The database will include such information as the number of workers requested, the date filed, the date decided, and the final disposition. [77 FR 10166, Feb. 21, 2012] Sec.
