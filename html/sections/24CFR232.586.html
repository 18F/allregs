CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 232  /  Sec. 232.586 Minimum principal loan amount.

A mortgagee may not require, as a condition of providing a loan secured by a mortgage insured under this subpart, that the principal amount of the mortgage exceed a minimum amount established by the mortgagee. [53 FR 8885, Mar. 18, 1988]
