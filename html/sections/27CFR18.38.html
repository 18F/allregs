CFR /  Title 27  /  Part 18  /  Sec. 18.38 Permanent discontinuance.

A proprietor who permanently discontinues the business of a concentrate manufacturer shall, after completion of operations, file an application on Form 27-G (5520.3) to cover such discontinuance, giving the date of the discontinuance. (Approved by the Office of Management and Budget under control number 1512-0046) [T.D. ATF-104, 47 FR 23921, June 2, 1982, as amended by T.D. ATF-381, 61 FR 37003, July 16, 1996]
