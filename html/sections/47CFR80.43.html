CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 80  /  Sec. 80.43 Equipment acceptable for licensing.

Transmitters listed in Sec. 80.203 must be authorized for a particular use by the Commission based upon technical requirements contained in subparts E and F of this part, except for transmitters that are used on vessels in the Maritime Security Fleet and are deemed to satisfy all Commission equipment certification requirements pursuant to section 53108(c) of Title 46 of the United States Code. [73 FR 4480, Jan. 25, 2008]
