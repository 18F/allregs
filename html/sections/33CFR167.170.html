CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 167  /  Sec. 167.170 Off Delaware Bay Approach Traffic Separation Scheme: General.

The Off Delaware Bay Approach Traffic Separation Scheme consists of four parts: an Eastern Approach, a Southeastern Approach, a Two-Way Traffic Route, and a Precautionary Area. The specific areas in the Off Delaware Bay Approach Traffic Separation Scheme and Precautionary Area are described in Secs. 167.171 through 167.174. [CGD 97-004, 65 FR 12945, Mar. 10, 2000]
