CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 1640  /  Sec. 1640.6 Methods of providing information.

The TSP will furnish the information described in this part to participants by making it available on the TSP Web site. A participant can request paper copies of that information from the TSP by calling the ThriftLine, submitting a request through the TSP Web site, or by writing to the TSP record keeper. [68 FR 74451, Dec. 23, 2003]
