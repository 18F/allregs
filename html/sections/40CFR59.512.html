CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 59  /  Sec. 59.512 Addresses of EPA regional offices.

All requests (including variance requests), reports, submittals, and other communications to the Administrator pursuant to this regulation shall be submitted to the Regional Office of the EPA which serves the State or territory for the address that is listed on the aerosol coating product in question. These areas are indicated in the following list of EPA Regional Offices. EPA Region I (Connecticut, Maine, Massachusetts, New Hampshire, Rhode
