CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 919  /  Sec. 919.410 May I approve a participant's use of the services of

After entering into a covered transaction with a participant, you as an agency official may not approve a participant's use of an excluded person as a principal under that transaction, unless you obtain an exception under Sec. 919.120.
