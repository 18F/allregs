CFR /  Title 9  /  Part 352  /  Sec. 352.8 Time of inspection in the field and in an official

The official exotic animal establishment on behalf of the applicant shall notify the Regional Director or designee, in advance, of the hours when such inspection is desired. Inspection personnel shall have access at all times to every part of any field ante-mortem inspection area and/or official exotic animal establishment to which they are assigned. [54 FR 1332, Jan. 13, 1989]
