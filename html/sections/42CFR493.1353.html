CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 493  /  Sec. 493.1353 Scope.

In accordance with Sec. 493.19(b), the moderate complexity procedures specified as PPM procedures are considered such only when personally performed by a health care provider during a patient visit in the context of a physical examination. PPM procedures are subject to the personnel requirements in Sec. Sec. 493.1355 through 493.1365.
