CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 108  /  Sec. 108.21 Civil rights/compliance reviewing office compliance

The civil rights/compliance reviewing office shall be responsible for determining whether an applicant's actions are in apparent compliance with its approved AFHM plan, the AFHM regulations, and this part and for determining changes or modifications necessary in the plan after initiation of marketing. [64 FR 44096, Aug. 12, 1999]
