CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 1302  /  Sec. 1302.3 Access to the accounting of disclosures from records.

Rules governing the granting of access to the accounting of disclosures are the same as those for granting access to the records (including verification of identity) outlined in Sec. 1302.2, of this part.
