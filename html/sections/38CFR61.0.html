CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 61  /  Sec. 61.0 Purpose.

This part implements the VA Homeless Providers Grant and Per Diem Program which consists of the following components: capital grants, per diem, special need capital and non-capital grants, and technical assistance grants. (Authority: 38 U.S.C. 501, 2001, 2002, 2011, 2012, 2061, 2064)
