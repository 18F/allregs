CFR /  Title 36  /  Part 902  /  Sec. 902.73 Where to appeal.

An appeal shall be addressed to the Chairman of the Board of Directors, Pennsylvania Avenue Development Corporation, 1331 Pennsylvania Avenue, NW., Suite 1220 North, Washington, DC 20004. [41 FR 43143, Sept. 30, 1976, as amended at 50 FR 45824, Nov. 4, 1985]
