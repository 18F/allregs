CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 642  /  Sec. 642.1 What is the Training Program for Federal TRIO Programs?

The Training Program for Federal TRIO programs, referred to in these regulations as the Training program, provides Federal financial assistance to train the leadership personnel and staff employed in, or preparing for employment in, Federal TRIO program projects. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1070a-17) [75 FR 65771, Oct. 26, 2010]
