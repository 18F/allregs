CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 32  /  32.201 Statutory authority.

10 U.S.C. 2307(f) and 41 U.S.C. 4505 provide that payment for commercial items may be made under such terms and conditions as the head of the agency determines are appropriate or customary in the commercial marketplace and are in the best interest of the United States. [60 FR 49711, Sept. 26, 1995, as amended at 79 FR 24211, Apr. 29, 2014]
