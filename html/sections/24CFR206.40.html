CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 206  /  Sec. 206.40 Disclosure and verification of Social Security and

The mortgagor must meet the requirements for the disclosure and verification of Social Security and Employer Identification Numbers, as provided by part 200, subpart U, of this chapter. [60 FR 42760, Aug. 16, 1995]
