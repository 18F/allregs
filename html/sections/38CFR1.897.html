CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 1  /  Sec. 1.897 Exceptions.

The Secretary of Veterans Affairs, or designees, may except positions from inclusion in this program as necessary to carry out the mission of the Department. (Authority: 5 U.S.C. 3402)
