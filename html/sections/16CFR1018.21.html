CFR /  Title 16  /  Part 1018  /  Sec. 1018.21 Calling of meetings.

Advisory committees shall, as a general rule, meet four times per year, except that, as provided by statute, the Toxicological Advisory Board shall meet not less than two times each year. No advisory committee shall hold a meeting without advance approval of the Chairman or the Commission official designated under Sec. 1018.23(a). Before giving such advance approval, the Chairman or Commission official shall notify the Commission of the date of the proposed meeting. [41 FR 45822, Oct. 18, 1976, as amended at 43 FR 60876, Dec. 29, 1978]
