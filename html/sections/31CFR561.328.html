CFR /  Title 31  /  Part 561  /  Sec. 561.328 Reduce significantly, significantly reduced, and

The terms reduce significantly, significantly reduced, and significant reduction, used with respect to purchases from Iran of petroleum and petroleum products, include a reduction in such purchases in terms of price or volume toward a complete cessation of such purchases. [78 FR 16408, Mar. 15, 2013]
