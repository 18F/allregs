CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 842  /  Sec. 842.202 Definitions.

In this subpart--

Commuting area has the same meaning given that term in Sec. 351.203 of this chapter.

Minimum retirement age means an age based on an individual's year of birth, as follows: ------------------------------------------------------------------------
