CFR /  Title 16  /  Part 1  /  Sec. 1.15 Amendment or repeal of a rule.

(a) Substantive amendment or repeal of a rule. The procedures for substantive amendment to or repeal of a rule are the same as for the issuance thereof.

(b) Nonsubstantive amendment of a rule. The Commission may make a nonsubstantive amendment to a rule by announcing the amendment in the Federal Register. [46 FR 26289, May 12, 1981]
