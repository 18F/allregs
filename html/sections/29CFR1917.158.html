CFR /  Title 29  /  Part 1917  /  Sec. 1917.158 Prohibited operations.

(a) Spray painting and abrasive blasting operations shall not be conducted in the vicinity of cargo handling operations.

(b) Welding and burning operations shall not be conducted in the vicinity of cargo handling operations unless such hot work is part of the cargo operation. Sec. Appendix I to Part 1917--Special Cargo Gear and Container Spreader
