CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 647  /  Sec. 647.1 What is the Ronald E. McNair Postbaccalaureate

The Ronald E. McNair Postbaccalaureate Achievement Program--referred to in these regulations as the McNair program--awards grants to institutions of higher education for projects designed to provide disadvantaged college students with effective preparation for doctoral study. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1070a-15)
