CFR /  Title 14  /  Part 27  /  Sec. 27.673 Primary flight control.

Primary flight controls are those used by the pilot for immediate control of pitch, roll, yaw, and vertical motion of the rotorcraft. [Amdt. 27-21, 49 FR 44434, Nov. 6, 1984]
