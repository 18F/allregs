CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 146  /  Sec. 146.2 Law authorizing these regulations.

The Safe Drinking Water Act, 42 U.S.C. 300f et seq. authorizes these regulations and all other UIC program regulations referenced in 40 CFR part 144. Certain regulations relating to the injection of hazardous waste are also authorized by the Resource Conservation and Recovery Act, 42 U.S.C. 6901 et seq. [58 FR 63898, Dec. 3, 1993]
