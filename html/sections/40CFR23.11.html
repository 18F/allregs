CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 23  /  Sec. 23.11 Holidays.

If the date determined under Secs. 23.2 to 23.10 falls on a Federal holiday, then the time and date of the Administrator's action shall be at 1:00 p.m. eastern time on the next day that is not a Federal holiday.
