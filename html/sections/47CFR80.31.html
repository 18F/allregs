CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 80  /  Sec. 80.31 Cancellation of license.

Wireless telecommunications carriers subject to this part must comply with the discontinuance of service provisions of part 63 of this chapter. [63 FR 68955, Dec. 14, 1998]
