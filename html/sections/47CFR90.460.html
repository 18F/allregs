CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 90  /  Sec. 90.460 Scope.

This subpart sets forth the provisions relating to permissible methods of transmitter control and interconnection (see the definition in Sec. 90.7) of radio systems authorized under this part. [44 FR 67124, Nov. 23, 1979, as amended at 62 FR 18934, Apr. 17, 1997]
