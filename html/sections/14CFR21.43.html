CFR /  Title 14  /  Part 21  /  Sec. 21.43 Location of manufacturing facilities.

Except as provided in Sec. 21.29, the FAA does not issue a type certificate if the manufacturing facilities for the product are located outside of the United States, unless the FAA finds that the location of the manufacturer's facilities places no undue burden on the FAA in administering applicable airworthiness requirements.
