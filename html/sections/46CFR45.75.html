CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 45  /  Sec. 45.75 Intermediate freeboard.

The minimum intermediate freeboard (fI) in inches is obtained by the formula: fI=f(s) + T s(100)/L where: L = length L in feet but not less than 400 feet.
