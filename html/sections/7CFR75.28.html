CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 75  /  Sec. 75.28 When a request for an appeal inspection may be withdrawn.

A request for an appeal inspection may be withdrawn by the applicant at any time before the appeal inspection is performed: Provided, that, the appellant shall pay any expenses incurred in connection with the appeal as provided in Sec. 75.46.
