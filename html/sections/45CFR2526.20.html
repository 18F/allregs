CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 2526  /  Sec. 2526.20 Is an AmeriCorps participant who does not complete an education award?

(a) Compelling personal circumstances. A participant in an approved AmeriCorps position who is released prior to completing an approved term of service for compelling personal circumstances in accordance with Sec. 2522.230(a) is eligible for a pro-rated education award if the participant--

(1) Performed satisfactorily prior to being granted a release for compelling personal circumstances; and

(2) Completed at least 15 percent of the originally-approved term of service.

(b) Release for cause. A participant who is released prior to completing an originally-approved term of service for cause is not eligible for any portion of an education award. [64 FR 37415, July 12, 1999, as amended at 75 FR 51411, Aug. 20, 2010]
