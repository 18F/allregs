CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 207  /  Sec. 207.257 Commissioner's right to require acceleration.

Upon receipt of notice of violation of a covenant, as provided for in Sec. 207.256(b), or otherwise being apprised of the violation of a covenant, the Commissioner reserves the right to require the mortgagee to accelerate payment of the outstanding principal balance due in order to protect the interests of the Commissioner. [76 FR 24371, May 2, 2011]
