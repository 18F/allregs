CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 164  /  Sec. 164.111 Procedure for disposition of motions.

Within 7 days following the service of any motion provided for in Sec. 164.110, any other party to the proceeding may file with the hearing clerk an answer thereto. As soon as practicable thereafter, the Environmental Appeals Board shall announce its decision whether to grant or to deny the motion. Unless the Environmental Appeals Board shall determine otherwise, operation of the order shall not be stayed pending the decision to grant or to deny the motion. In the event that any such motion is granted by the Environmental Appeals Board, the applicable rules of practice, as set out elsewhere herein, shall be followed. [38 FR 19371, July 20, 1973, as amended at 57 FR 5344, Feb. 13, 1992]
