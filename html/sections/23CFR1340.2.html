CFR /  Title 23  /  Part 1340  /  Sec. 1340.2 Applicability.

This part applies to State surveys of seat belt use beginning in calendar year 2013 and continuing annually thereafter. However, a State may elect to conduct its calendar year 2012 seat belt use survey using a survey design approved under this part. [77 FR 20551, Apr. 5, 2012]
