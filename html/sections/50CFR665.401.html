CFR /  Title 50  /  Part 665  /  Sec. 665.401 Definitions.

As used in Sec. Sec. 665.400 through 665.419:

CNMI commercial bottomfish permit means the permit required by Sec. 665.404(a)(2) to engage in commercial fishing for Mariana bottomfish MUS in the CNMI management subarea.

Guam bottomfish permit means the permit required by Sec. 665.404(a)(1) to use a large vessel to fish for, land, or transship Mariana bottomfish MUS shoreward of the outer boundary of the Guam subarea of the Mariana fishery management area.
