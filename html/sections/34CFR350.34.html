CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 350  /  Sec. 350.34 Which Rehabilitation Engineering Research Centers must

A Rehabilitation Engineering Research Center conducting research or demonstration activities that facilitate service delivery systems change must have an advisory committee. (Authority: Sec. 204 (b)(3)(D); 29 U.S.C. 762 (b)(3)(D))
