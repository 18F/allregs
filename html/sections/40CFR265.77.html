CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 265  /  Sec. 265.77 Additional reports.

In addition to submitting the biennial report and unmanifested waste reports described in Secs. 265.75 and 265.76, the owner or operator must also report to the Regional Administrator:

(a) Releases, fires, and explosions as specified in Sec. 265.56(j);

(b) Ground-water contamination and monitoring data as specified in Secs. 265.93 and 265.94; and

(c) Facility closure as specified in Sec. 265.115.

(d) As otherwise required by Subparts AA, BB, and CC of this part. [45 FR 33232, May 19, 1980, as amended at 48 FR 3982, Jan. 28, 1983; 55 FR 25507, June 21, 1990; 59 FR 62935, Dec. 6, 1994]
