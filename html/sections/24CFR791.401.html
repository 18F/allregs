CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 791  /  Sec. 791.401 General.

This subpart D establishes the procedures for allocating budget authority under section 213(d) of the Act for the programs identified in Sec. 791.101. It describes the allocation of budget authority by the appropriate Assistant Secretary to the applicable Program Office Director in the HUD field office, and by the Program Office Director to allocation areas within their jurisdiction. [61 FR 10849, Mar. 15, 1996, as amended at 64 FR 26639, May 14, 1999]
