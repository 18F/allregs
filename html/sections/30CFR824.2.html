CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 824  /  Sec. 824.2 Objectives.

The objectives of this part are to--

(a) Enhance coal recovery;

(b) Reclaim the land to equal or higher postmining use; and

(c) Protect and enhance environmental and other values protected under the Act and this chapter. [44 FR 15452, Mar. 13, 1979]
