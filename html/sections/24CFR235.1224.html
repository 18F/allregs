CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 235  /  Sec. 235.1224 Cross-reference.

(a) Title 24, part 235, subpart C, as modified and supplemented by this subpart, shall constitute the contract between the mortgagee and the Secretary for assistance payments in connection with a mortgage insured under section 235(r) of the National Housing Act.

(b) The sections of subpart C of this part which apply in connection with a mortgage insured under section 235(r) are the following: Sec. 235.301 Definitions.235.340 Time of payments.235.350 Mortgagor's required recertification.235.355 Mortgagor's optional recertification.235.360 Adjustment in assistance payments.235.361 Recovery of assistance payments.235.365 Mortgagee records.235.370 Effect of assignment of mortgage with an assistance payment

contract.235.375 Termination, suspension or reinstatement of the assistance

payments contract.235.499 Effect of amendments. [57 FR 62453, Dec. 30, 1992. Redesignated at 60 FR 56499, Nov. 8, 1995]
