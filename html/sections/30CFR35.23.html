CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 35  /  Sec. 35.23 Performance required for certification.

To qualify as fire-resistant under the regulations of this part, a hydraulic fluid shall meet each performance requirement and stated in Sec. Sec. 35.20(d), 35.21(d), and 35.22(d).
