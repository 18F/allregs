CFR /  Title 25  /  Part 533  /  Sec. 533.7 Void agreements.

Management contracts and changes in persons with a financial interest in or management responsibility for a management contract, that have not been approved by the Chairman in accordance with the requirements of part 531 of this chapter and this part, are void. [74 FR 36936, July 27, 2009]
