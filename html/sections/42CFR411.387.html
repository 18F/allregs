CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 411  /  Sec. 411.387 Parties affected by advisory opinions.

An advisory opinion issued by CMS does not apply in any way to any individual or entity that does not join in the request for the opinion. Individuals or entities other than the requestor(s) may not rely on an advisory opinion. [69 FR 57230, Sept. 24, 2004]
