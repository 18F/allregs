CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 52  /  Sec. 52.1922 Approval status.

With the exceptions set forth in this subpart, the Administrator approves Oklahoma's plan for the attainment and maintenance of the national standards under section 110 of the Clean Air Act. Furthermore, the Administrator finds that the plan satisfies all requirements of Part D, Title 1, of the Clean Air Act as amended in 1977, except as noted below. [45 FR 9741, Feb. 13, 1980, as amended at 61 FR 16062, Apr. 11, 1996]
