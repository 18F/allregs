CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 50  /  Sec. 50.53 Jurisdictional limitations.

No license under this part shall be deemed to have been issued for activities which are not under or within the jurisdiction of the United States. [21 FR 355, Jan. 19, 1956, as amended at 43 FR 6924, Feb. 17, 1978]
