CFR /  Title 29  /  Part 1690  /  Sec. 1690.102 Purpose.

These regulations prescribe the means by which review and consultation shall occur between the Equal Employment Opportunity Commission and other Federal agencies having responsibility for enforcement of Federal statutes, Executive Orders, regulations and policies which require equal employment opportunity without regard to race, color, religion, sex, national origin, age or disability. Subsequent regulations will expand on standards for the coordination of specific matters referenced or alluded to herein. [45 FR 68361, Oct. 14, 1980, as amended at 74 FR 63984, Dec. 7, 2009]
