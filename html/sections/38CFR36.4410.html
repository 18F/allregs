CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 36  /  Sec. 36.4410 Supplementary administrative action.

Subject to statutory limitations and conditions prescribed in title 38, U.S.C., the Secretary may take such action as may be necessary or appropriate to relieve undue prejudice to an eligible individual or a third party contracting or dealing with such eligible individual which might otherwise result. (Authority: 38 U.S.C. 501, 2101(d))
