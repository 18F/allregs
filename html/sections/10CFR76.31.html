CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 76  /  Sec. 76.31 Periodic application requirement.

The Corporation shall periodically apply to the Commission for a certificate of compliance, in accordance with Sec. 76.36, on or before April 15 of the year specified in an existing certificate of compliance as determined by the Commission, but not less frequently than every 5 years. [62 FR 6670, Feb. 12, 1997]
