CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 457  /  Sec. 457.720 State plan requirement: State assurance regarding

A State plan must include an assurance that the State collects data, maintains records, and furnishes reports to the Secretary, at the times and in the standardized format the Secretary may require to enable the Secretary to monitor State program administration and compliance and to evaluate and compare the effectiveness of State plans under Title XXI of the Act. This includes collection of data and reporting as required under Sec. 431.970 of this chapter. [71 FR 51084, Aug. 28, 2006]
