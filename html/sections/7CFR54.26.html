CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 54  /  Sec. 54.26 Application of other regulations to appeal service.

The regulations in Secs. 54.1 through 54.18 and Secs. 54.27 through 54.30 shall apply to appeal service except insofar as they are manifestly inapplicable.
