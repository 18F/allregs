CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 102-42  /  Sec. 102-42.140 How is a sale of a foreign gift or decoration

Foreign gifts and decorations must be offered first through negotiated sales to the employee who has indicated an interest in purchasing the item. The sale price must be the commercially appraised value of the gift. Sales must be conducted and documented in accordance with part 102-38 of this subchapter B. [68 FR 56496, Sept. 4, 2003, as amended at 71 FR 28778, May 18, 2006]
