CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 1493  /  Sec. 1493.270 Certifications.

(a) Exporter's signature. The exporter's signature on documentation submitted to CCC under this subpart, is the exporter's certification that:

(1) There have not been and are no arrangements for any payments in violation of the Foreign Corrupt Practices Act of 1977, as amended, or other U.S. Laws;

(2) All information submitted to CCC is true and correct; and

(3) The exporter is in compliance with this subpart.

(b) False certification. False certifications under this subpart may result in the termination of the facility payment guarantee, suspension or debarment, or civil or criminal action.
