CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 1240  /  Sec. 1240.80 General requirements for water for drinking and

Only potable water shall be provided for drinking and culinary purposes by any operator of a conveyance engaged in interstate traffic, except as provided in Sec. 1250.84(b) of this chapter. Such water shall either have been obtained from watering points approved by the Commissioner of Food and Drugs, or, if treated aboard a conveyance, shall have been subjected to treatment approved by the Commissioner of Food and Drugs. [40 FR 5620, Feb. 6, 1975, as amended at 48 FR 11431, Mar. 18, 1983]
