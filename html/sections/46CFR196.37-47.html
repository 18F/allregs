CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 196  /  Sec. 196.37-47 Portable magazine chests.

(a) Portable magazine chests shall be marked in letters at least 3 inches high:

PORTABLE MAGAZINE CHEST

-- FLAMMABLE --

KEEP LIGHTS AND FIRE AWAY.
