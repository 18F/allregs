CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 709  /  Sec. 709.23 Obtaining consent to a polygraph examination.

DOE may not administer a polygraph examination unless DOE:

(a) Notifies the covered person of the polygraph examination in writing in accordance with Sec. 709.21 of this part; and

(b) Obtains written consent from the covered person prior to the polygraph examination.
