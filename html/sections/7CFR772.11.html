CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 772  /  Sec. 772.11 Transfer and assumption--IMP loans.

Transfers and assumptions for IMP loans are processed in accordance with 7 CFR part 765. Any remaining transferor liability will be serviced in accordance with Sec. 772.9(c) of this subpart. [68 FR 69949, Dec. 16, 2003, as amended at 72 FR 64121, Nov. 15, 2007]
