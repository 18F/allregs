CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 117  /  Sec. 117.323 Outer Clam Bay.

The drawspan of the Outer Clam Bay Boardwalk Drawbridge shall open on signal if at least 30 minutes advance notice is given. [CGD07-06-237, 72 FR 11777, Mar. 14, 2007]
