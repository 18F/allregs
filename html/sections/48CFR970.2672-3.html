CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 970  /  970.2672-3 Contract clause.

The contracting officer shall insert the clause at 970.5226-2, Workforce Restructuring Under Section 3161 of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 1993, in contracts for the management and operation of Department of Energy Defense Nuclear Facilities and, as appropriate, in other contracts that include site management responsibilities at a Department of Energy Defense Nuclear Facility. [65 FR 81009, Dec. 22, 2000, as amended at 75 FR 68221, Nov. 5, 2010]
