CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 917  /  Sec. 917.101 Marketing agreement.

Marketing agreement means Marketing Agreement No. 85 as amended.

Effective Date Note: At 76 FR 66605, Oct. 27, 2011, Sec. 917.101 was suspended indefinitely, effective Oct. 28, 2011.
