CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 850  /  Sec. 850.1 Scope.

This part provides for establishment of a chronic beryllium disease prevention program (CBDPP) that supplements and is deemed an integral part of the worker safety and health program under part 851 of this chapter. [71 FR 6931, Feb. 9, 2006]
