CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 435  /  Sec. 435.322 Medically needy coverage of the blind in States

If the agency provides Medicaid to individuals receiving SSI and elects to cover the medically needy, it may provide Medicaid to blind individuals who meet--

(a) The requirements for blindness, as specified in Sec. Sec. 435.530 and 435.531; and

(b) The income and resource requirements of subpart I of this part. [46 FR 47986, Sept. 30, 1981]
