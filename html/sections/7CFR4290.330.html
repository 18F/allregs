CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 4290  /  Sec. 4290.330 Grant and guarantee issuance fee.

The Applicant must pay to the Secretary an issuance fee for each grant or debenture guarantee of $500. If both a grant and debenture guarantee are issued for the same RBIC, the issuance fee for both is $500. An Applicant must submit this fee in advance, at the time of application submission. [76 FR 80222, Dec. 23, 2011]
