CFR /  Title 22  /  Part 92  /  Sec. 92.13 Form of notarial certificate.

The form of a notarial certificate depends on the nature of the notarial act it attests. (See Sec. Sec. 92.18 to 92.48 for discussions of the various forms of notarial certificates.) Rules pertaining to venue, and signing and sealing, are common to all notarial certificates.
