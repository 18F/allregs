CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 170  /  Sec. 170.140 Operating information for a vessel constructed on or

(a) This section applies to each vessel that is--

(1) Constructed on or after January 1, 2009; and

(2) Issued either a SOLAS Passenger Ship Safety Certificate or a SOLAS Cargo Ship Safety Construction Certificate.

(b) In addition to the information required in Sec. 170.110 of this part, the stability booklet of each vessel to which this section applies must contain the information required by applicable regulations of IMO Res. MSC.216(82) (incorporated by reference, see Sec. 170.015).

(c) As used in SOLAS chapter II-1, Administration means the Commandant, U.S. Coast Guard. [USCG-2007-0030, 75 FR 78084, Dec. 14, 2010]
