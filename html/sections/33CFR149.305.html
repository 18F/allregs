CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 149  /  Sec. 149.305 What are the survival craft requirements for temporary

(a) When temporary personnel are on board a manned deepwater port and the complement exceeds the capacity of the survival craft required under Sec. 149.304 of this part, the deepwater port must have additional liferafts to ensure that the total capacity of the survival craft is not less than 150 percent of the personnel on board at any time.

(b) to Sec. 149.308 of this part, but must comply with the stowage requirements of 46 CFR 108.530(c). [USCG-1998-3884, 71 FR 57651, Sept. 29, 2006, as amended by USCG-2013- 0397, 78 FR 39179, July 1, 2013]
