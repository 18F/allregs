CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 60-50  /  Sec. 60-50.5 Nondiscrimination.

The provisions of this part are not intended and shall not be used to discriminate against any qualified employee or applicant for employment because of race, color, religion, sex, sexual orientation, gender identity or national origin. [79 FR 72995, Dec. 9, 2014]
