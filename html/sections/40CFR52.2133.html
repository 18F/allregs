CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 52  /  Sec. 52.2133 General conformity.

The General Conformity regulations adopted into the South Carolina State Implementation Plan which were submitted on November 8, 1996. South Carolina incorporated by reference regulations 40 CFR part 51, subpart W--determining conformity of General Federal Actions to State or Federal Implementation Plans. [62 FR 32538, June 16, 1997]
