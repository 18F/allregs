CFR /  Title 17  /  Part 230  /  Sec. 230.150 Definition of ``commission or other remuneration'' in

The term commission or other remuneration in section 3(a)(9) of the Act shall not include payments made by the issuer, directly or indirectly, to its security holders in connection with an exchange of securities for outstanding securities, when such payments are part of the terms of the offer of exchange. [2 FR 1076, May 26, 1937]
