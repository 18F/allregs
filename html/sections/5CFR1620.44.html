CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 1620  /  Sec. 1620.44 Restoring forfeited agency automatic (1%) contributions.

If an employee's agency automatic (1%) contributions were forfeited because the employee was not vested when he or she separated to perform military service, the employee must notify the employing agency that a forfeiture occurred. The employing agency will follow the procedure described in Sec. 1620.46(e) to have those funds restored. [64 FR 31057, June 9, 1999, as amended at 67 FR 49526, July 30, 2002]
