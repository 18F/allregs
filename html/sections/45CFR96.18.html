CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 96  /  Sec. 96.18 Participation by faith-based organizations.

The funds provided under this part shall be administered in compliance with the standards set forth in part 87 (Equal Treatment for Faith-based Organizations) of this chapter. [69 FR 42592, July 16, 2004]
