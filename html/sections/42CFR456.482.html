CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 456  /  Sec. 456.482 Medical, psychiatric, and social evaluations.

If a facility provides inpatient psychiatric services to a beneficiary under age 21, the medical, psychiatric, and social evaluations required by Sec. Sec. 456.170, and 456.370 must be made by the team described in Sec. 441.153. [43 FR 45266, Sept. 29, 1978, as amended at 61 FR 38399, July 24, 1996]
