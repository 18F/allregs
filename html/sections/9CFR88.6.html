CFR /  Title 9  /  Part 88  /  Sec. 88.6 Violations and penalties.

(a) The Secretary is authorized to assess civil penalties of up to $5,000 per violation of any of the regulations in this part.

(b) Each equine transported in violation of the regulations of this part will be considered a separate violation. (Approved by the Office of Management and Budget under control number 0579-0160)
