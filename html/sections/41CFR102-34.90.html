CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 102-34  /  Sec. 102-34.90 What motor vehicle identification must we display on

Unless exempted under Sec. 102-34.160, Sec. 102-34.175 or Sec. 102-34.180, Government motor vehicles must display the following identification:

(a) ``For Official Use Only'';

(b) ``U.S. Government''; and

(c) Identification that readily identifies the agency owning the vehicle.
