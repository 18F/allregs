CFR /  Title 17  /  Part 249  /  Sec. 249.1401 Form ABS-EE, for submission of the asset-data file

This Form shall be used by an electronic filer for the submission of information required by Item 1111(h) (Sec. 229.1111(h) of this chapter). [79 FR 57346, Sept. 24, 2014]

Editorial Note: For Federal Register citations affecting Form ABS-EE, see the List of CFR Sections Affected, which appears in the Finding Aids section of the printed volume and at www.fdsys.gov.
