CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 218  /  218.270 Humanitarian or peacekeeping operation.

The term ``humanitarian or peacekeeping operation'' is defined at FAR 2.101. In accordance with 10 U.S.C. 2302(7), when a humanitarian or peacekeeping operation is declared, the simplified acquisition threshold is raised to $300,000 for DoD purchases that are awarded and performed, or purchases that are made, outside the United States in support of that operation. See 202.101. [76 FR 44281, July 25, 2011]
