CFR /  Title 12  /  Part 546  /  Sec. 546.1 Definitions.

The terms used in Sec. Sec. 546.2 and 546.3 shall have the same meaning as set forth in Sec. Sec. 552.13(b) and 563.22(g) of this chapter. [59 FR 44622, Aug. 30, 1994]
