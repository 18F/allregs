CFR /  Title 23  /  Part 658  /  Sec. 658.1 Purpose.

The purpose of this part is to identify a National Network of highways available to vehicles authorized by provisions of the Surface Transportation Assistance Act of 1982 (STAA) as amended, and to prescribe national policies that govern truck and bus size and weight. [59 FR 30419, June 13, 1994]
