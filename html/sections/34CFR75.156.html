CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 75  /  Sec. 75.156 When an applicant under Sec. 75.155 must submit its

(a) Each applicant under a program covered by Sec. 75.155 shall submit a copy of its application to the State on or before the deadline date for submitting its application to the Department.

(b) The applicant shall attach to its application a copy of its letter that requests the State to comment on the application. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1221e-3 and 3474)
