CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 731  /  731.774 Overseas recruitment incentive.

USAID's policies regarding overseas recruitment incentives are set forth in (48 CFR) AIDAR 731.205-70. These policies are also applicable to contracts with a nonprofit organization. [57 FR 5236, Feb. 13, 1992, as amended at 79 FR 74988, Dec. 16, 2014]
