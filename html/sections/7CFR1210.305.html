CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 1210  /  Sec. 1210.305 Watermelon.

Watermelon means all varieties of the Family Curcubitaceae; Genus and Species; Citrullus Lanatus, popularly referred to as watermelon grown by producers in the United States or imported into the United States. [60 FR 10797, Feb. 28, 1995]
