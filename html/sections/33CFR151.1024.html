CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 151  /  Sec. 151.1024 Display of number.

(a) The owner or operator of each vessel under this subpart must ensure that the vessel number stated on the conditional permit issued under Sec. 151.1015 is displayed so that it--

(1) Is clearly legible;

(2) Has a contrasting background;

(3) Is readily visible from either side of the vessel; and

(4) Is in block figures that are at least 18 inches in height.

(b) No person may tamper with or falsify a number required under this section. Subpart C_Ballast Water Management for Control of Nonindigenous Species

in the Great Lakes and Hudson River

Authority: 16 U.S.C. 4711; Department of Homeland Security Delegation No. 0170.1.

Source: CGD 91-066, 58 FR 18334, Apr. 8, 1993, unless otherwise noted.
