CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 133  /  Sec. 133.114 Cheddar cheese for manufacturing.

Cheddar cheese for manufacturing conforms to the definition and standard of identity prescribed for cheddar cheese by Sec. 133.113, except that the milk is not pasteurized, curing is not required, and the provisions of paragraph (b)(3)(iv) of that section do not apply. [48 FR 2743, Jan. 21, 1983]
