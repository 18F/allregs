CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 427  /  427.104 General guidance.

As used in FAR part 27, the agency head or agency head designee is the Senior Procurement Executive, except under FAR 27.306(a) and (b). Under FAR 27.306(a) and (b), the agency head is the Secretary without power of redelegation. [61 FR 53646, Oct. 15, 1996]
