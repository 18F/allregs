CFR /  Title 14  /  Part 1264  /  Sec. 1264.143 Right to administrative offset.

The amount of any penalty or assessment which has become final, or for which a judgment has been entered under Sec. 1264.141 or Sec. 1264.142, or any amount agreed upon in a compromise or settlement under Sec. 1264.145, may be collected by administrative offset under 31 U.S.C. 3716, except that an administrative offset may not be made under this subsection against a refund of an overpayment of Federal taxes, then or later owing by the United States to the defendant.
