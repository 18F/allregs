CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 303  /  Sec. 303.229 Determination by the Secretary that a State is eligible.

If the Secretary determines that a State is eligible to receive a grant under part C of the Act, the Secretary notifies the State of that determination. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1437)
