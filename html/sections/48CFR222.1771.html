CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 222  /  222.1771 Solicitation provision.

Unless the solicitation includes the provision at 252.204-7007, use the provision at 252.222-7007, Representation Regarding Combating Trafficking in Persons, in all solicitations and contracts that exceed the simplified acquisition threshold, including solicitations and contracts using FAR part 12 procedures for the acquisition of commercial items. [80 FR 5001, Jan. 29, 2015]
