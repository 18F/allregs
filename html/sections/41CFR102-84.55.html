CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 102-84  /  Sec. 102-84.55 When are the Annual Real Property Inventory reports

You must prepare the Annual Real Property Inventory information prescribed in Sec. 102-84.50 as of the last day of each fiscal year. This information must be submitted electronically to the General Services Administration, Office of Governmentwide Policy, Office of Real Property (MP), 1800 F Street, NW., Washington, DC 20405, no later than December 15 of each year.
