CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 4120  /  Sec. 4120.3-5 Assignment of range improvements.

The authorized officer shall not approve the transfer of a grazing preference under Sec. 4110.2-3 of this title or approve use by the transferee of existing range improvements, unless the transferee has agreed to compensate the transferor for his/her interest in the authorized improvements within the allotment as of the date of the transfer. [53 FR 10234, Mar. 29, 1988]
