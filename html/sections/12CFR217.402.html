CFR /  Title 12  /  Part 217  /  Sec. 217.402 Identification as a global systemically important BHC.

A bank holding company is a global systemically important BHC if its method 1 score, as calculated under Sec. 217.404, equals or exceeds 130 basis points. Subject to Sec. 217.400(b)(2), a bank holding company must calculate its method 1 score on an annual basis by December 31 of each year.
