CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 523  /  Sec. 523.4 Passenger automobile.

A passenger automobile is any automobile (other than an automobile capable of off-highway operation) manufactured primarily for use in the transportation of not more than 10 individuals. (Sec. 301, Pub. L. 94-163, 80 Stat. 901 (15 U.S.C. 2001)) [42 FR 38362, July 28, 1977]
