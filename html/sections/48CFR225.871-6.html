CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 225  /  225.871-6 Disposal of property.

Dispose of property that is jointly acquired by the members of a cooperative project under the procedures established in the agreement or in a manner consistent with the terms of the agreement, without regard to any laws of the United States applicable to the disposal of property owned by the United States. [70 FR 73155, Dec. 9, 2005]
