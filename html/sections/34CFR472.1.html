CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 472  /  Sec. 472.1 What is the National Workplace Literacy Program?

The National Workplace Literacy Program provides assistance for demonstration projects that teach literacy skills needed in the workplace through exemplary education partnerships between business, industry, or labor organizations and educational organizations. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1211(a)(1))
