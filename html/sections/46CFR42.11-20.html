CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 42  /  Sec. 42.11-20 Application for annual survey.

(a) The owner, master, or agent of a vessel holding a load line certificate shall apply to the assigning and issuing authority who issued the certificate for the annual survey required by Sec. 42.09-40 or the International Convention on Load Lines, 1966. [CGFR 68-60, 33 FR 10058, July 12, 1968]
