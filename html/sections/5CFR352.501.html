CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 352  /  Sec. 352.501 Purpose.

This subpart governs reinstatement authorized by sections 233(d) and 625(b) of the Foreign Assistance Act of 1961, as amended (22 U.S.C. 2193(d) and 22 U.S.C. 235(b)). [36 FR 13897, July 28, 1971]
