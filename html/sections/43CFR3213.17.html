CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 3213  /  Sec. 3213.17 May BLM terminate my lease for reasons other than

BLM may terminate your lease for reasons other than non-payment of rentals, after giving you 30 days written notice, if we determine that you violated the requirements of Sec. 3200.4, including, but not limited to the nonpayment of royalties and fees under 30 CFR parts 206 and 218.
