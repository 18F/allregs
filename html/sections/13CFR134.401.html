CFR /  Title 13  /  Part 134  /  Sec. 134.401 Scope of the rules in this subpart D.

The rules of practice in this subpart D apply to all appeals to OHA from:

(a) Denials of 8(a) BD program admission based solely on a negative finding(s) of social disadvantage, economic disadvantage, ownership or control pursuant to Sec. 124.206 of this title;

(b) Early graduation pursuant to Secs. 124.302 and 124.304;

(c) Termination pursuant to Secs. 124.303 and 124.304;

(d) Denials of requests to issue a waiver pursuant to Sec. 124.515; and

(e) Suspensions pursuant to Sec. 124.305(a).
