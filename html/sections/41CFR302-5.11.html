CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 302-5  /  Sec. 302-5.11 Is there a time limit on the duration of a househunting trip?

A househunting trip should be for a reasonable period, not to exceed 10 calendar days, as authorized by your agency under Sec. 302-5.101(d).
