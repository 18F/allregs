CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 201  /  Sec. 201.23 Name of shipper or consignee.

The full name and address of either the shipper or consignee shall appear upon the label. If the name and address of the shipper are not shown upon the label, a code designation identifying the shipper shall be shown. [5 FR 31, Jan. 4, 1940]
