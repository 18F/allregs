CFR /  Title 31  /  Part 363  /  Sec. 363.179 Does Fiscal Service make any reservations as to the conversion

We may reject any application for conversion or refuse to convert a savings bond in any case or class of cases, if we deem the action to be in the public interest. Our action in any such respect is final. Sec. Sec. 363.180-363.199 [Reserved]
