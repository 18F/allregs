CFR /  Title 25  /  Part 11  /  Sec. 11.110 How are tribal customs affected by this part?

Each Court of Indian Offenses shall apply the customs of the tribe occupying the Indian country over which it has jurisdiction to the extent that they are consistent with the regulations of this part. Sec. 11.112 [Reserved]
