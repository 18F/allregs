CFR /  Title 19  /  Part 125  /  Sec. 125.31 Documents used.

When merchandise is carted or lightered to and received from a bonded store or bonded warehouse, it shall be accompanied by one of the following tickets or documents:

(a) Customs Form 6043--Delivery Ticket.

(b) Customs Form 7501, Entry Summary, annotated ``Permit''.

(c) Customs Form 7512--Transportation Entry and Manifest of Goods Subject to Customs Inspection and Permit. [T.D. 82-204, 47 FR 49375, Nov. 1, 1982, as amended by T.D. 84-129, 49 FR 23167, June 5, 1984; T.D. 95-81, 60 FR 52295, Oct. 6, 1995]
