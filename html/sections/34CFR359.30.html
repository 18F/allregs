CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 359  /  Sec. 359.30 How is peer review conducted under this program?

Peer review is conducted under this program in accordance with 34 CFR 350.30-350.32, using the selection criteria in Sec. 359.31. (Authority: Sec. 202(e); 29 U.S.C. 761a(e))
