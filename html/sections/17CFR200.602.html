CFR /  Title 17  /  Part 200  /  Sec. 200.602 Application.

This regulation (Sec. Sec. 200.601-200.670) applies to all programs or activities conducted by the agency, except for programs or activities conducted outside the United States that do not involve individuals with handicaps in the United States.
