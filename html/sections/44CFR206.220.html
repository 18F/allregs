CFR /  Title 44  /  Part 206  /  Sec. 206.220 General.

This subpart provides policies and procedures for determinations of eligibility of applicants for public assistance, eligibility of work, and eligibility of costs for assistance under sections 402, 403, 406, 407, 418, 419, 421(d), 502, and 503 of the Stafford Act. Assistance under this subpart must also conform to requirements of 44 CFR part 201, Mitigation Planning, and 44 CFR part 206, subparts G--Public Assistance Project Administration, I--Public Assistance Insurance Requirements, J--Coastal Barrier Resources Act, and M--Minimum Standards. Regulations under 44 CFR part 9--Floodplain Management and 44 CFR part 10--Environmental Considerations, also apply to this assistance. [67 FR 8854, Feb. 26, 2002]
