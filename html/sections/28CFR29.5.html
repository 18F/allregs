CFR /  Title 28  /  Part 29  /  Sec. 29.5 Notification of law enforcement officials.

In addition to the actions enumerated in Sec. 29.4(b), as a condition of participating in the program, a State or locality must agree to take reasonable steps to ensure that law enforcement officials under its jurisdiction are familiar with the program and with the conditions under which motor vehicles may be stopped.
