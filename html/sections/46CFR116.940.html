CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 116  /  Sec. 116.940 Guards in vehicle spaces.

On a vessel authorized to carry one or more vehicles, suitable chains, cables, or other barriers must be installed at the end of each vehicle runway. In addition, temporary rails or equivalent protection must be installed in way of each vehicle ramp, in compliance with Sec. 116.900 of this part, when the vessel is underway.
