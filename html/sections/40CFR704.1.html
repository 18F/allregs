CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 704  /  Sec. 704.1 Scope.

(a) of the Toxic Substances Control Act (TSCA) for manufacturers, importers, and processors of chemical substances and mixtures (hereafter collectively referred to as substances) that are identified in subpart B of this part. The reporting and recordkeeping provisions in subpart A of this part apply throughout this part unless revised in any other subpart.

(b) Subpart B of this part sets out chemical-specific reporting and recordkeeping requirements under section 8(a) of TSCA. [53 FR 51715, Dec. 22, 1988, as amended at 60 FR 31920, June 19, 1995]
