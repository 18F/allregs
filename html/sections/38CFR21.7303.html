CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 21  /  Sec. 21.7303 Revision of decisions.

The revision of a decision on which an action was predicated is subject to the following sections:

(a) Clear and unmistakable error, Sec. 3.105(a) of this chapter; and

(b) Difference of opinion, Sec. 3.105(b) of this chapter. (Authority: 38 U.S.C. 511; Pub. L. 98-525)
