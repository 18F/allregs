CFR /  Title 14  /  Part 101  /  Sec. 101.21 Applicability.

(a) This subpart applies to operating unmanned rockets. However, a person operating an unmanned rocket within a restricted area must comply with Sec. 101.25(b)(7)(ii) and with any additional limitations imposed by the using or controlling agency.

(b) A person operating an unmanned rocket other than an amateur rocket as defined in Sec. 1.1 of this chapter must comply with 14 CFR Chapter III. [Doc. No. FAA-2007-27390, 73 FR 73781, Dec. 4, 2008]
