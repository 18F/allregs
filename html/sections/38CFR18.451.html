CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 18  /  Sec. 18.451 Application.

Subpart F applies to health, and other social service programs or activities that receive Federal financial assistance from the Department of Veterans Affairs and to recipients that operate or receive Federal financial assistance for the operation of such programs or activities. [45 FR 63268, Sept. 24, 1980, as amended at 68 FR 51370, Aug. 26, 2003]
