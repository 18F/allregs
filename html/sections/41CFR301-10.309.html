CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 301-10  /  Sec. 301-10.309 What will I be reimbursed if I am authorized to use

You will be reimbursed the applicable POV rate on a mileage basis, plus per diem, not to exceed the total constructive cost of the authorized method of common carrier transportation plus per diem. Your agency must determine the constructive cost of transportation and per diem by common carrier under the rules in Sec. 301-10.310. [FTR Amdt. 2015-03, 80 FR 27260, May 13, 2015]
