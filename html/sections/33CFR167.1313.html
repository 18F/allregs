CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 167  /  Sec. 167.1313 In the Strait of Juan de Fuca: Northern lanes.

In the northern lanes of the Strait of Juan de Fuca, the following are established:

(a) A separation zone bounded by a line connecting the following geographical positions: Latitude Longitude

4821.15 N 12324.83 W4816.16 N 12328.50 W4815.77 N 12327.18 W4820.93 N 12324.26 W

(b) A traffic lane for southbound traffic between the separation zone and a line connecting the following geographical positions: Latitude Longitude

4821.83 N 12325.56 W4816.45 N 12330.42 W

(c) A traffic lane for northbound traffic between the separation zone and a line connecting the following geographical positions: Latitude Longitude

4820.93 N 12323.22 W4815.13 N 12325.62 W
