CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 401  /  Sec. 401.405 Basic rates and charges on the St. Lawrence River and

Except as provided in Sec. 401.420, the following basic rates are payable for all services and assignments performed by U.S. registered pilots in the St. Lawrence River and Lake Ontario.
