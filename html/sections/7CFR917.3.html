CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 917  /  Sec. 917.3 Person.

Person means an individual, partnership, corporation, association, or any other business unit.

Effective Date Note: At 76 FR 66605, Oct. 27, 2011, Sec. 917.3 was suspended indefinitely, effective Oct. 28, 2011.
