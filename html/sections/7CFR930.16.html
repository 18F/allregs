CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 930  /  Sec. 930.16 Sales constituency.

Sales constituency means a common marketing organization or brokerage firm or individual representing a group of handlers and growers. An organization which receives consignments of cherries and does not direct where the consigned cherries are sold is not a sales constituency. [66 FR 35896, July 10, 2001]
