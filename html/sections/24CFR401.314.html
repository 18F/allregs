CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 401  /  Sec. 401.314 Environmental review responsibilities.

HUD will retain all responsibility for environmental review under part 50 of this title. Compliance with part 50 of this title will be completed before any HUD approval of the Restructuring Commitment under Sec. 401.405.
