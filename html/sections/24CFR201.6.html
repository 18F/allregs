CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 201  /  Sec. 201.6 Disclosure and verification of Social Security and

To be eligible for loan insurance under this part, the borrower must meet the requirements for the disclosure and verification of Social Security and Employer Identification Numbers, as provided by part 200, subpart U, of this chapter. (Approved by the Office of Management and Budget under control number 2502-0059) [54 FR 39692, Sept. 27, 1989. Correctly designated at 55 FR 420, Jan. 5, 1990]
