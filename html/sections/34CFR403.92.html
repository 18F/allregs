CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 403  /  Sec. 403.92 Under what circumstances may the age limit under the Sex

The individual appointed under Sec. 403.13(a) may waive the requirement in Sec. 403.91(b) with respect to age limitations if the individual determines (through appropriate research) that the waiver is essential to meet the objectives of Sec. 403.91. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 2335a(b))
