CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 1  /  Sec. 1.672 What will the Forest Service do with a proposed

If any license party proposes an alternative to a preliminary condition under Sec. 1.671(a)(1), the Forest Service must do the following within 60 days after the deadline for filing comments to FERC's NEPA document under 18 CFR 5.25(c):

(a) Analyze the alternative under Sec. 1.673; and

(b) File with FERC:

(1) Any condition that the Forest Service adopts as its modified condition; and

(2) Its analysis of the modified condition and any proposed alternatives under Sec. 1.673(c).
