CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 1610  /  Sec. 1610.5 Minimum Bank loan.

A Bank loan will not be made unless the applicant qualifies for a Bank loan of at least $50,000. [38 FR 17184, June 29, 1973. Redesignated at 58 FR 66252, Dec. 20, 1993]
