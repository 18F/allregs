CFR /  Title 31  /  Part 540  /  Sec. 540.500 Licensing procedures.

For provisions relating to licensing procedures, see part 501, subpart E, of this chapter. Licensing actions taken pursuant to part 501 of this chapter with respect to the prohibitions contained in this part are considered actions taken pursuant to this part. [68 FR 53658, Sept. 11, 2003]
