CFR /  Title 17  /  Part 200  /  Sec. 200.406 Certification by the General Counsel.

For every Commission meeting closed pursuant to Sec. 200.402(a) (1) through (10), the General Counsel of the Commission (or, in his or her absence, the attorney designated by General Counsel pursuant to Sec. 200.21) shall publicly certify that, in his or her opinion, the meeting may be closed to the public and shall state each relevant exemptive provision.
