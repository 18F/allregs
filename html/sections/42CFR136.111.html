CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 136  /  Sec. 136.111 Interest.

Tribes and Tribal organizations shall not be held accountable for interest earned on grant funds, pending disbursement by such organization.

Note: This provision is excepted from application of 45 CFR 74.47(a) by section 106(b) of Pub. L. 93-638. [40 FR 53143, Nov. 14, 1975, as amended at 50 FR 1854, Jan. 14, 1985]
