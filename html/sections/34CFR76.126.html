CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 76  /  Sec. 76.126 What regulations apply to the consolidated grant

The following regulations apply to those programs included in a consolidated grant:

(a) The regulations in Sec. Sec. 76.125 through 76.137; and

(b) The regulations that apply to each specific program included in a consolidated grant for which funds are used. (Authority: 48 U.S.C. 1469a) [47 FR 17421, Apr. 22, 1982]
