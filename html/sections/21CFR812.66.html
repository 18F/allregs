CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 812  /  Sec. 812.66 Significant risk device determinations.

If an IRB determines that an investigation, presented for approval under Sec. 812.2(b)(1)(ii), involves a significant risk device, it shall so notify the investigator and, where appropriate, the sponsor. A sponsor may not begin the investigation except as provided in Sec. 812.30(a). [46 FR 8957, Jan. 27, 1981]
