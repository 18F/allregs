CFR /  Title 19  /  Part 115  /  Sec. 115.55 Termination of approval.

Approval of a road vehicle terminates:

(a) Upon expiration of the certificate of approval; or

(b) Upon a change in the road vehicle by a major repair or alteration of any of the essential features required in Sec. 115.51. Repairs by replacement in kind do not constitute a change of the essential features.
