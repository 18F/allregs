CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 3509  /  Sec. 3509.16 How do I apply for a future interest lease?

No specific form is required. You must file at least one year before the mineral interest vests with the United States or BLM will deny your application. BLM will charge you a processing fee on a case-by-case basis as described in Sec. 3000.11 of this chapter. [64 FR 53536, Oct. 1, 1999, as amended at 70 FR 58878, Oct. 7, 2005]
