CFR /  Title 27  /  Part 71  /  Sec. 71.81 Suspension, revocation, or annulment.

In hearings on the suspension, revocation, or annulment of a permit, the burden of proof is on the Government. [T.D. ATF-199, 50 FR 9197, Mar. 6, 1985]
