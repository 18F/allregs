CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 51  /  Sec. 51.906 Redesignation to nonattainment following initial designations

For any area that is initially designated attainment or unclassifiable for the 8-hour NAAQS and that is subsequently redesignated to nonattainment for the 8-hour ozone NAAQS, any absolute, fixed date applicable in connection with the requirements of this part is extended by a period of time equal to the length of time between the effective date of the initial designation for the 8-hour NAAQS and the effective date of redesignation, except as otherwise provided in this subpart. [70 FR 71700, Nov. 29, 2005]
