CFR /  Title 14  /  Part 1221  /  Sec. 1221.106 Establishment of the NASA Flag.

The NASA Flags for interior and exterior use were created by the NASA Administrator in January 1960. Complete design, size, and color of the NASA interior and exterior flags for manufacturing purposes are detailed in U.S. Army QMG Drawing 5-1-269, revision September 14, 1960. The NASA Flags shall be used as set forth in Sec. 1221.113. [GRAPHIC] [TIFF OMITTED] TC09SE91.012
