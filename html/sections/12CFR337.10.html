CFR /  Title 12  /  Part 337  /  Sec. 337.10 Waiver.

An insured State nonmember bank has the right to petition the Board of Directors of the Corporation for a waiver of this part or any subpart thereof with respect to any particular transaction or series of similar transactions. A waiver may be granted at the discretion of the Board upon a showing of good cause. All such petitions should be filed with the Executive Secretary, Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation, 550 17th Street, NW., Washington, DC 20429. [39 FR 29179, Aug. 14, 1974, as amended at 67 FR 71071, Nov. 29, 2002]
