CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 15  /  Sec. 15.601 General.

The following sections of subparts F, G, and H of this part contain provisions concerning manning of uninspected vessels; Secs. 15.701, 15.705, 15.710, 15.720, 15.730, 15.801, 15.805, 15.810, 15.820, 15.825, 15.840, 15.850, 15.855, 15.905, 15.910, and 15.915.
