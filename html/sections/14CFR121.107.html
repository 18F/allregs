CFR /  Title 14  /  Part 121  /  Sec. 121.107 Dispatch centers.

Each certificate holder conducting domestic or flag operations must show that it has enough dispatch centers, adequate for the operations to be conducted, that are located at points necessary to ensure proper operational control of each flight. [Doc. No. 28154, 61 FR 2610, Jan. 26, 1996]
