CFR /  Title 31  /  Part 595  /  Sec. 595.407 Offshore transactions.

The prohibitions contained in Sec. 595.201 apply to transactions by U.S. persons in locations outside the United States with respect to property which the U.S. person knows, or has reason to know, is held in the name of a specially designated terrorist, or in which the U.S. person knows, or has reason to know, a specially designated terrorist has or has had an interest since the effective date.
