CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 26  /  Sec. 26.404 Observational research not involving greater than

EPA will conduct or fund observational research in which the IRB finds that no greater than minimal risk to children is presented, only if the IRB finds that adequate provisions are made for soliciting the assent of the children and the permission of their parents or guardians, as set forth in Sec. 26.406.
