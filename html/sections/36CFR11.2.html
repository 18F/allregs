CFR /  Title 36  /  Part 11  /  Sec. 11.2 Uses.

The Director may permit the reproduction, manufacture, sale, and use of the ``Arrowhead Symbol'' or the ``Parkscape Symbol'', with or without charge, for uses that will contribute to purposes of education and conservation as they relate to the program of the National Park Service. All other uses are prohibited. [36 FR 16508, Aug. 21, 1971]
