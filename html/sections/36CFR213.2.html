CFR /  Title 36  /  Part 213  /  Sec. 213.2 Authority for Chief, Forest Service, to group, define, and

The Chief, Forest Service, is authorized to group the national grasslands into administrative units, define, change or modify their boundaries, and to provide such specific designations therefor as he finds necessary and desirable for effective and economical administration thereof and for public and official reference thereto. [33 FR 12370, Sept. 4, 1968]
