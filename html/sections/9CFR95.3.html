CFR /  Title 9  /  Part 95  /  Sec. 95.3 Byproducts from diseased animals prohibited.

The importation of any animal byproduct taken or removed from an animal affected with anthrax, foot-and-mouth disease, highly pathogenic avian influenza, Newcastle disease, or rinderpest is prohibited. [28 FR 5981, June 13, 1963, as amended at 79 FR 71007, Dec. 1, 2014]
