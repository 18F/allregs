CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 105-53  /  Sec. 105-53.150 Organization and functions.

Regional offices have been established in 11 cities throughout the United States. Each regional office is headed by a Regional Administrator who reports to the Associate Administrator for Operations and Industry Relations. The geographic composition of each region is shown in Sec. 105-53.151. [54 FR 26742, June 26, 1989]
