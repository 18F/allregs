CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 82  /  Sec. 82.706 FD&C; Yellow No. 6.

(a) The color additive FD&C; Yellow No. 6 shall conform in identity and specifications to the requirements of Sec. 74.706 (a)(1) and (b) of this chapter.

(b) All lakes including current D&C; external and D&C; lakes of FD&C; Yellow No. 6 shall be manufactured from previously certified batches of the straight color additive. [52 FR 21509, June 8, 1987]
