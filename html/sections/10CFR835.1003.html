CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 835  /  Sec. 835.1003 Workplace controls.

During routine operations, the combination of engineered and administrative controls shall provide that:

(a) The anticipated occupational dose to general employees shall not exceed the limits established at Sec. 835.202; and

(b) The ALARA process is utilized for personnel exposures to ionizing radiation. [63 FR 59686, Nov. 4, 1998, as amended at 72 FR 31927, June 8, 2007]
