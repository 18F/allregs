CFR /  Title 25  /  Part 514  /  Sec. 514.3 What is the maximum fee rate?

(a) The rates of fees imposed shall be--

(1) No more than 2.5% of the first $1,500,000 (1st tier), and

(2) No more than 5% of amounts in excess of the first $1,500,000 (2nd tier) of the assessable gross revenues from each gaming operation subject to the jurisdiction of the Commission.

(b) If a tribe has a certificate of self-regulation, the rate of fees imposed shall be no more than .25% of assessable gross revenues from self-regulated class II gaming operations.
