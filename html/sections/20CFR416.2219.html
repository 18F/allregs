CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 416  /  Sec. 416.2219 Method of payment.

Payment to the State VR agencies or alternate participants pursuant to this subpart will be made either by advancement of funds or by payment for services provided (with necessary adjustments for any overpayments and underpayments), as decided by the Commissioner. [55 FR 8458, Mar. 8, 1990]
