CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 17  /  Sec. 17.05-10 Instructions for administration.

The Commandant, United States Coast Guard, will issue such detailed instructions as may be necessary for the administration of the ``United States Coast Guard General Gift Fund'' or for the acceptance, operation, or maintenance of property, real or personal, that may be accepted for the benefit of or in connection with any school, hospital, library, museum, chapel, or other institution or organization under the jurisdiction of the United States Coast Guard subject to the terms and conditions of any particular gift, devise, or bequest. [CGFR 61-36, 26 FR 9321, Oct. 3, 1961]
