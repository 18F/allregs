CFR /  Title 50  /  Part 600  /  Sec. 600.230 Removal.

The Secretary may remove for cause any Secretarially appointed member of a Council in accordance with section 302(b)(6) of the Magnuson-Stevens Act, wherein the Council concerned first recommends removal of that member by not less than two-thirds of the voting members. A recommendation of a Council to remove a member must be made in writing to the Secretary and accompanied by a statement of the reasons upon which the recommendation is based. [61 FR 32540, June 24, 1996, as amended at 63 FR 7073, Feb. 12, 1998]
