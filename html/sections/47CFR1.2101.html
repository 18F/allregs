CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 1  /  Sec. 1.2101 Purpose.

The provisions of Secs. 1.2101 through 1.2114 implement section 309(j) of the Communications Act of 1934, as added by the Omnibus Budget Reconciliation Act of 1993 (Pub. L. 103-66) and subsequent amendments. [79 FR 48528, Aug. 15, 2014]
