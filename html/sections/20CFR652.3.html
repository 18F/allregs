CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 652  /  Sec. 652.3 Basic labor exchange system.

At a minimum, each State shall administer a labor exchange system which has the capacity:

(a) To assist jobseekers in finding employment;

(b) To assist employers in filling jobs;

(c) To facilitate the match between jobseekers and employers;

(d) To participate in a system for clearing labor between the States, including the use of standardized classification systems issued by the Secretary, under section 15 of the Act; and.

(e) To meet the work test requirements of the State unemployment compensation system. [48 FR 50665, Nov. 2, 1983, as amended at 64 FR 18762, Apr. 15, 1999]
