CFR /  Title 22  /  Part 92  /  Sec. 92.15 Signing notarial certificate.

The notarizing officer should sign a notarial certificate on the lower right-hand side. The name and full official title of the notarizing officer should by typed, stamped with a rubber stamp, or printed in ink on two separate lines immediately below his signature. When the notarizing officer is assigned to a Foreign Service post in both a diplomatic and consular capacity, he should use his consular title in the notarial certificate. (See Sec. 92.7.) [22 FR 10858, Dec. 27, 1957, as amended at 60 FR 51723, Oct. 3, 1995]
