CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 17  /  Sec. 17.604 Application for the HPSP.

An applicant for the HPSP must submit an accurate and complete application, including a signed written acceptance agreement. (Authority: 38 U.S.C. 7612(c)(1)(B)) (The Office of Management and Budget has approved the information collection requirements in this section under control number 2900-0793.) [78 FR 51070, Aug. 20, 2013]
