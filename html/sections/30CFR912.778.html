CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 912  /  Sec. 912.778 Permit applications--minimum requirements for legal,

Part 778 of this chapter, Permit Applications--Minimum Requirements for Legal, Financial, Compliance and Related Information, shall apply to any person who applies for a permit to conduct surface coal mining and reclamation operations. [52 FR 13808, Apr. 24, 1987]
