CFR /  Title 27  /  Part 28  /  Sec. 28.212 Persons authorized.

Persons who have qualified under this chapter as proprietors of distilled spirits plants, bonded wine cellars, or taxpaid wine bottling houses, and persons who are wholesale liquor dealers (as defined in Sec. 31.32 of this chapter) and have registered as a wholesale liquor dealer in accordance with part 31 of this chapter, are authorized to remove wines under the provisions of this subpart. (26 U.S.C. 5062) [T.D. TTB-79, 74 FR 37406, July 28, 2009] Sec. 28.213 [Reserved]
