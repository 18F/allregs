CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 886  /  Sec. 886.319 Responsibility for contract administration.

HUD is responsible for administration of the Contract. HUD may contract with another entity for the performance of some or all of its Contract administration functions. [60 FR 11860, Mar. 2, 1995]
