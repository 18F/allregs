CFR /  Title 1  /  Part 16  /  Sec. 16.4 Authorizing duties.

The agency authorizing officer is responsible for furnishing, to the Director of the Federal Register, a current mailing list of officers or employees of the agency who are authorized to receive the Federal Register, the Code of Federal Regulations, and the Weekly Compilation of Presidential Documents for official use. [54 FR 9679, Mar. 7, 1989]
