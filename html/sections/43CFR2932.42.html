CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 2932  /  Sec. 2932.42 How long is my Special Recreation Permit valid?

You may request a permit for a day, season of use, or other time period, up to a maximum of 10 years. BLM will determine the appropriate term on a case-by-case basis. [69 FR 5706, Feb. 6, 2004]
