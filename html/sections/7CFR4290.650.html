CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 4290  /  Sec. 4290.650 Requirement to report portfolio valuations to

You must determine the value of your Loans and Investments in accordance with Sec. 4290.503. You must report such valuations to the Secretary within 90 days of the end of the fiscal year in the case of annual valuations, and within 30 days following the close of other reporting periods. You must report material adverse changes in valuations at least quarterly, within 30 days following the close of the quarter.
