CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 160  /  Sec. 160.133-5 Incorporation by reference.

(a) and 1 CFR part 51. To enforce any edition other than that specified in this section, the Coast Guard must publish notice of change in the Federal Register and the material must be available to the public. All approved material is available for inspection at Coast Guard Headquarters. Contact Commandant (CG-ENG-4), Attn: Lifesaving and Fire Safety Division, U.S. Coast Guard Stop 7509, 2703 Martin Luther King Jr. Avenue, SE., Washington, DC 20593-7509. You may also inspect this material at the National Archives and Records Administration (NARA). For information on the availability of this material at NARA, call 202-741- 6030 or go to http://www.archives.gov/federal--register/code--of-- federal--regulations/ibr--locations.html. You may obtain copies of the material from the sources specified in the following paragraphs.
