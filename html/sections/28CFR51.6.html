CFR /  Title 28  /  Part 51  /  Sec. 51.6 Political subunits.

All political subunits within a covered jurisdiction (e.g., counties, cities, school districts) that have not terminated coverage by obtaining the declaratory judgment described in section 4(a) of the Act are subject to the requirements of section 5. [Order 3262-2011, 76 FR 21243, Apr. 15, 2011]
