CFR /  Title 9  /  Part 317  /  Sec. 317.308 Labeling of meat or meat food products with number

The label of any package of a meat or meat food product that bears a representation as to the number of servings contained in such package shall meet the requirements of Sec. 317.2(h)(10). [58 FR 664, Jan. 6, 1993, as amended at 60 FR 176, Jan. 3, 1995]
