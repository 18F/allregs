CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 605  /  Sec. 605.31 Notification to the respondent.

On receipt of any complaint under Sec. 605.30, or on his own motion if at any time he shall have reason to believe that a violation may have occurred, the Administrator will provide written notification to the grantee concerned (hereinafter called ``the respondent'') that a violation has probably occurred. The Administrator will inform the respondent of the conduct which constitutes a probable violation of the agreement.
