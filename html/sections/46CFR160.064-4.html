CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 160  /  Sec. 160.064-4 Marking.

(a) Labels. Each water safety buoyant device must be marked in accordance with the recognized laboratory's listing and labeling requirements in accordance with Sec. 160.064-3(a). At a minimum, all labels must include--

(1) Size information, as appropriate;

(2) The Coast Guard approval number;

(3) Manufacturer's contact information;

(4) Model name/number;

(5) Lot number, manufacturer date; and

(6) Any limitations or restrictions on approval or special instructions for use.

(b) Durability of marking. Marking must be of a type which will be durable and legible for the expected life of the device. [USCG-2013-0263, 79 FR 56499, Sept. 22, 2014]
