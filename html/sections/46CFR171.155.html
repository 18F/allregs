CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 171  /  Sec. 171.155 Drainage of an open boat.

The deck within the hull of an open boat must drain to the bilge. Overboard drainage of the deck is not permitted. [CGD 85-080, 62 FR 51355, Sept. 30, 1997]
