CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 56  /  Sec. 56.31 Report of grading work.

Reports of grading work performed within official plants shall be forwarded to the Administrator by the grader in a manner as may be specified by the Administrator. [71 FR 42010, July 24, 2006]
