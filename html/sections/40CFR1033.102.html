CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 1033  /  Sec. 1033.102 Transition to the standards of this part.

(a). The Tier 0 and Tier 1 standards of 40 CFR part 92 apply for earlier model years.

(b) Except as specified in Sec. 1033.150(a), the Tier 2 standards of Sec. 1033.101 apply for new locomotives beginning January 1, 2013. The Tier 2 standards of 40 CFR part 92 apply for earlier model years.

(c) The Tier 3 and Tier 4 standards of Sec. 1033.101 apply for the model years specified in that section.
