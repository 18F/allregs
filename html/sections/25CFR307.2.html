CFR /  Title 25  /  Part 307  /  Sec. 307.2 Certificates of genuineness; by whom affixed.

Government certificates of genuineness for Navajo all-wool woven fabrics may be affixed to fabrics meeting the conditions specified in Sec. 307.4 by persons duly authorized to affix such certificates, under license issued by the Indian Arts and Crafts Board.
