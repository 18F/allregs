CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 1206  /  Sec. 1206.160 Operating allowances.

Notwithstanding any other provisions in these regulations, an operating allowance may be used for the purpose of computing payment obligations when specified in the notice of sale and the lease. The allowance amount or formula shall be specified in the notice of sale and in the lease agreement. [61 FR 3804, Feb. 2, 1996]
