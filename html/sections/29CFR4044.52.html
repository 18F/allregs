CFR /  Title 29  /  Part 4044  /  Sec. 4044.52 Valuation of benefits.

The plan administrator shall value all benefits as of the valuation date by--

(a) Using the mortality assumptions prescribed by Sec. 4044.53 and the interest assumptions prescribed in appendix B to this part;

(b) Using interpolation methods, where necessary, at least as accurate as linear interpolation;

(c) Using valuation formulas that accord with generally accepted actuarial principles and practices; and

(d) Adjusting the values to reflect loading expenses in accordance with appendix C to this part. [65 FR 14753, Mar. 17, 2000, as amended at 70 FR 72207, Dec. 2, 2005]
