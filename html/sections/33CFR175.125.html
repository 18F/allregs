CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 175  /  Sec. 175.125 Serviceability.

No person may use a boat unless each signal required by Sec. 175.110 is in serviceable condition and the service life of the signal, if indicated by a date marked on the signal, has not expired. [CGD 93-055, 61 FR 13926, Mar. 28, 1996, as amended by USCG-1999-5832, 64 FR 34715, June 29, 1999]
