CFR /  Title 26  /  Part 601  /  Sec. 601.525 Certification of copies of documents.

The provisions of paragraph (e) of Sec. 601.504 with respect to certification of copies are applicable to a power of attorney or a tax information authorization required to be filed under Sec. 601.522 or Sec. 601.523.
