CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 34  /  Sec. 34.6 Applicability of Foreign Quarantine Regulations.

Aliens arriving at a port of the United States shall be subject to the applicable provisions of 42 CFR part 71, Foreign Quarantine, with respect to examination and quarantine measures. [56 FR 25003, May 31, 1991]
