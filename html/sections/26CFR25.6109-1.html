CFR /  Title 26  /  Part 25  /  Sec. 25.6109-1 Tax return preparers furnishing identifying numbers

(a) In general. Each gift tax return or claim for refund prepared by one or more signing tax return preparers must include the identifying number of the preparer required by Sec. 1.6695-1(b) of this chapter to sign the return or claim for refund in the manner stated in Sec. 1.6109-2 of this chapter.

(b) Effective/applicability date. Paragraph (a) of this section is applicable to returns and claims for refund filed after December 31, 2008. [T.D. 9436, 73 FR 78451, Dec. 22, 2008]
