CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 148  /  Sec. 148.325 How soon after deepwater port decommissioning must the

Within 2 years of deepwater port decommissioning, the licensee must initiate removal procedures. The Commandant (CG-5P) will advise and coordinate with appropriate Federal agencies and the States concerning activities covered by this section. [USCG-2013-0397, 78 FR 39177, July 1, 2013]
