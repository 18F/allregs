CFR /  Title 18  /  Part 46  /  Sec. 46.1 Purpose.

The purpose of this part is to implement section 305(c) of the Federal Power Act, as amended by section 211 of the Public Utility Regulatory Policies Act of 1978. [Order 67, 45 FR 3569, Jan. 18, 1980]
