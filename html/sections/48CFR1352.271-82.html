CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 1352  /  1352.271-82 Department of Labor occupational safety and health

As prescribed in 48 CFR 1371.113, insert the following clause:

Department of Labor Occupational Safety and Health Standards for Ship

Repair (APR 2010)

The contractor, in performance of all work under the contract, shall comply with the requirements of 29 CFR 1910.15. Nothing contained in this contract shall be construed as relieving the contractor from any obligations which it may have for compliance with the aforesaid regulations.

(End of clause) [75 FR 10570, Mar. 8, 2010; 75 FR 14496, Mar. 26, 2010]
