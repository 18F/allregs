CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 710  /  Sec. 710.34 Attorney representation.

In the event the individual is represented by an attorney or other representatives, the individual shall file with the Administrative Judge and DOE Counsel a document designating such attorney or representatives and authorizing one such attorney or representative to receive all correspondence, transcripts, and other documents pertaining to the proceeding under this subpart. [59 FR 35185, July 8, 1994. Redesignated at 66 FR 47065, Sept. 11, 2001]
