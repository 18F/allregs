CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 556  /  Sec. 556.70 Bacitracin.

(a) Acceptable daily intake (ADI). The ADI for total residues of bacitracin is 0.05 milligram per kilogram of body weight per day.

(b) Tolerances. The tolerance for residues of bacitracin from zinc bacitracin or bacitracin methylene disalicylate in uncooked edible tissues of cattle, swine, chickens, turkeys, pheasants, and quail, and in milk and eggs is 0.5 part per million. [65 FR 70791, Nov. 28, 2000]
