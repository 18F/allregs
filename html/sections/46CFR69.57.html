CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 69  /  Sec. 69.57 Gross tonnage.

Gross tonnage (GT) is determined by the following formula GT = K1 V, in which V = total volume of all enclosed spaces in cubic meters and K1 = 0.2 + 0.02 log10 V.
