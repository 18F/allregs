CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 95  /  Sec. 95.7 Time limit for claiming payment for expenditures made

Under the programs listed in Sec. 95.1, we will pay a State for a State agency expenditure made after September 30, 1979, only if the State files a claim with us for that expenditure within 2 years after the calendar quarter in which the State agency made the expenditure. Section 95.19 lists the exceptions to this rule.
