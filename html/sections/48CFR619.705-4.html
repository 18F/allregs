CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 619  /  619.705-4 Reviewing the subcontracting plan.

OSDBU shall review subcontracting plans to determine if small, small disadvantaged, women-owned small, HUBZone small, veteran-owned small, and service-disabled veteran-owned small business concerns are afforded the maximum practicable opportunity to participate as subcontractors. OSDBU shall recommend to the contracting officer changes needed to subcontracting plans found to be deficient. [69 FR 19335, Apr. 13, 2004, as amended at 80 FR 6920, Feb. 9, 2015]
