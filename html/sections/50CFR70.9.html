CFR /  Title 50  /  Part 70  /  Sec. 70.9 Wildlife species management.

The wildlife species management provisions set forth in part 31 of this chapter are equally applicable to national fish hatchery areas, except Sec. 31.15 relating to hunting and fishing, which are treated separately in this part.
