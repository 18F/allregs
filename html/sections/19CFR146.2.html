CFR /  Title 19  /  Part 146  /  Sec. 146.2 Port director as Board representative.

The appropriate port director shall be in charge of the zone as the representative of the Board. [T.D. 86-16, 51 FR 5049, Feb. 11, 1986, as amended by T.D. 99-27, 64 FR 13676, Mar. 22, 1999]
