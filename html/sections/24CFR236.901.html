CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 236  /  Sec. 236.901 Audit.

Where a State or local government receives interest reduction payments under section 236(b) of the National Housing Act or is the mortgagor of a mortgage insured or held by the Commissioner under this part, it shall conduct audits in accordance with HUD audit requirements at 24 CFR part 44. [58 FR 37813, July 13, 1993]
