CFR /  Title 25  /  Part 89  /  Sec. 89.35 Execution in quintuplet.

The contract should be executed in quintuplet, and all copies of it shall be transmitted by the Superintendent to the Area Director. [37 FR 10440, May 23, 1972. Redesignated at 47 FR 13327, Mar. 30, 1982]
