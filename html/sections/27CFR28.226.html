CFR /  Title 27  /  Part 28  /  Sec. 28.226 Removals of beer by agent on behalf of brewer.

Where proper power of attorney authorizing an agent to execute a claim on behalf of the brewer has been filed with the appropriate TTB officer, such agent may, for any of the purposes authorized in Sec. 28.221, remove taxpaid beer from the brewery where produced or from its place of storage elsewhere, and execute the notice and claim on Form 1582-B (5130.6) on behalf of the brewer. On removal of the beer, such agent shall dispose of Form 1582-B in accordance with the applicable procedure set forth in Sec. 28.225. (46 Stat. 690, as amended, 48 Stat. 999, as amended, 72 Stat. 1335; 19 U.S.C. 1309, 81c, 26 U.S.C. 5055) [25 FR 5734, June 23, 1960. Redesignated at 40 FR 16835, Apr. 15, 1975.
