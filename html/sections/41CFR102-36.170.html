CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 102-36  /  Sec. 102-36.170 May we transfer personal property owned by one of our

Property purchased by a non-appropriated fund activity is not federal property. A non-appropriated fund activity has the option of making its privately owned personal property available for transfer to a federal agency, usually with reimbursement. If such reimbursable personal property is not transferred to another federal agency, it may be offered for sale. Such property is not available for donation. [65 FR 31218, May 16, 2000, as amended at 65 FR 33778, May 25, 2000]
