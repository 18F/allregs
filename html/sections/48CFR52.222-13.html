CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 52  /  52.222-13 Compliance With Construction Wage Rate Requirements and

As prescribed in 22.407(a), insert the following clause:

Compliance With Construction Wage Rate Requirements and Related

Regulations (MAY 2014)

All rulings and interpretations of the Construction Wage Rate Requirements and related statutes contained in 29 CFR parts 1, 3, and 5 are hereby incorporated by reference in this contract.

(End of clause) [79 FR 24218, Apr. 29, 2014]
