CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 1290  /  Sec. 1290.103 Who may file an appeal?

(a) If you receive an order that adversely affects you or your lessee, you may appeal that order except as provided under Sec. 1290.104.

(b) If you are a lessee and you receive a Notice of Order, and if you contest the order, you may either appeal the order or join in your designee's appeal under Sec. 1290.106.
