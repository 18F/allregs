CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 108  /  Sec. 108.2 Applicability.

This part applies to any public elementary school, public secondary school, local educational agency, or State educational agency that has a designated open forum or limited public forum and that receives funds made available through the Department. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 7905)
