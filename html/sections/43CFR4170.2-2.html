CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 4170  /  Sec. 4170.2-2 Penal provisions under the Federal Land Policy and

Under section 303(a) of the Federal Land Policy and Management Act of 1976 (43 U.S.C. 1701 et seq.), any person who knowingly and willfully commits an act prohibited under Sec. 4140.1(b) or who knowingly and willfully violates approved special rules and regulations may be brought before a designated U.S. magistrate and is punishable by a fine in accordance with the applicable provisions of Title 18 of the United States Code, or imprisonment for no more than 12 months, or both. [60 FR 9969, Feb. 22, 1995]
