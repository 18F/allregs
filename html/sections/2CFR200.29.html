CFR /  Title 2  /  Part 200  /  Sec. 200.29 Cost sharing or matching.

Cost sharing or matching means the portion of project costs not paid by Federal funds (unless otherwise authorized by Federal statute). See also Sec. 200.306 Cost sharing or matching.
