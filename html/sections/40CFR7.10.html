CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 7  /  Sec. 7.10 Purpose of this part.

This part implements: Title VI of the Civil Rights Act of 1964, as amended; section 504 of the Rehabilitation Act of 1973, as amended; the Age Discrimination Act of 1975, as amended; and section 13 of the Federal Water Pollution Control Act Amendments of 1972, Public Law 92- 500, (collectively, the Acts). [75 FR 31707, June 4, 2010]
