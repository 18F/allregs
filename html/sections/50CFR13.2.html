CFR /  Title 50  /  Part 13  /  Sec. 13.2 Purpose of regulations.

The regulations contained in this part provide uniform rules, conditions, and procedures for the application for and the issuance, denial, suspension, revocation, and general administration of all permits issued pursuant to this subchapter B. [54 FR 38147, Sept. 14, 1989]
