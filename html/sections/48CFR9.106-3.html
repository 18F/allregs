CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 9  /  9.106-3 Interagency preaward surveys.

When the contracting office and the surveying activity are in different agencies, the procedures of this section 9.106 and subpart 42.1 shall be followed along with the regulations of the agency in which the surveying activity is located, except that reasonable special requests by the contracting office shall be accommodated (also see subpart 17.5). [48 FR 42142, Sept. 19, 1983, as amended at 54 FR 20496, May 11, 1989; 55 FR 36795, Sept. 6, 1990; 62 FR 232, Jan. 2, 1997, 75 FR 77735, Dec. 13, 2010]
