CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 17  /  Sec. 17.58 Evacuation of community nursing homes.

When veterans are evacuated from a community nursing home as the result of an emergency, they may be relocated to another facility that meets certain minimum standards, as set forth in 38 CFR 51.59(c)(1). (Authority: 38 U.S.C. 501, 1720) [76 FR 55571, Sept. 8, 2011]
