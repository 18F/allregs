CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 155  /  Sec. 155.775 Maximum cargo level of oil.

(a) For the purposes of this section, ``oil'' has the same meaning as provided in Sec. 151.05 of this chapter.

(b) A cargo tank on a tank vessel may not be filled with oil higher than--

(1) 98.5 percent of the cargo tank volume; or

(2) The level at which the overfill alarm required by Sec. 155.480 is set. [CGD 90-071a, 59 FR 53291, Oct. 21, 1994]
