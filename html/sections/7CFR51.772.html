CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 51  /  Sec. 51.772 Fairly smooth texture.

Fairly smooth texture means that the skin is fairly thin and not coarse for the variety and size of the fruit. ``Fairly thin'' means that the skin thickness does not average more than \1/2\ inch (12.7 mm), on a grapefruit 4\1/8\ inches (104.8 mm) in diameter.
