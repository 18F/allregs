CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 35  /  Sec. 35.4050 Must my group contribute toward the cost of a TAG?

(a) Yes, your group must contribute 20 percent of the total cost of the TAG project unless EPA waives the match under Sec. 35.4055.

(b) Under 2 CFR 200.306, your group may use ``cash'' and/or ``in-kind contributions'' (for example, your board members can count their time toward your matching share) to meet the matching funds requirement. Without specific statutory authority, you may not use Federal funds to meet the required match. [65 FR 58858, Oct. 2, 2000, as amended at 79 FR 76058, Dec. 19, 2014
