CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 54  /  Sec. 54.9 Withdrawal of application or request for service.

An application or a request for service under the regulations may be withdrawn by the applicant at any time before the application is approved or prior to performance of service, upon payment, in accordance with Sec. Sec. 54.27 and 54.28, of any expenses already incurred by the Agricultural Marketing Service in connection therewith.
