CFR /  Title 25  /  Part 900  /  Sec. 900.137 Do all provisions of the other subparts apply to contracts

Yes, except as otherwise provided in this subpart and unless excluded as follows: programmatic reports and data requirements, reassumption, contract review and approval process, contract proposal contents, and Sec. 900.150 (d) and (e) of these regulations.
