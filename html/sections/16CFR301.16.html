CFR /  Title 16  /  Part 301  /  Sec. 301.16 Disclosure of origin of certain furs raised or taken

If the name of any animal set out in the Fur Products Name Guide or term permitted by the regulations to be used in connection therewith connotes foreign origin and such animal is raised or taken in the United States, furs obtained therefrom shall be described in disclosing the required information as having the United States as the country of origin; as for example: Dyed Persian Lamb Fur origin: United States

or Mexican Raccoon Fur origin: United States
