CFR /  Title 27  /  Part 21  /  Sec. 21.132 Toluene.

(a) Distillation range. (For applicable ASTM method, see 1980 Annual Book of ASTM Standards, Part 29, page 569, Standard No. D 362-75 for industrial grade toluene; for incorporation by reference, see Sec. 21.6(b).) When 100 ml of toluene are distilled by this method, not more than 1 ml should distill below 109 [deg]C., and not less than 99 ml below 112 [deg]C.

(b) Boiling point. 110.6 [deg]1 [deg]C.

(c) Odor. Characteristic odor.

(d) Specific gravity at 15.56 [deg]/15.56 [deg]C. 0.869 to 0.873. [T.D. ATF-133, 48 FR 24673, June 2, 1983. Redesignated by T.D. ATF-442, 66 FR 12854, Mar. 1, 2001]
