CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 1532  /  1532.102 Description of contract financing methods.

Progress payments based on a percentage or stage of completion are authorized for use as a payment method under EPA contracts or subcontracts for construction and alteration or repair of buildings, structures, or other real property. [60 FR 38505, July 27, 1995]
