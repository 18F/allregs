CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 404  /  Sec. 404.1263 When fractional part of a cent may be disregarded--for

In paying contributions to a Federal Reserve bank or branch, a State may disregard a fractional part of a cent unless it amounts to one-half cent or more, in which case it shall be increased to one cent. Fractional parts of a cent shall be used in computing the total of contributions.
