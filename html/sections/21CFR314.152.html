CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 314  /  Sec. 314.152 Notice of withdrawal of approval of an application or

If the Food and Drug Administration withdraws approval of an application or abbreviated application for a new drug, FDA will publish a notice in the Federal Register announcing the withdrawal of approval. If the application or abbreviated application was withdrawn for grounds described in Sec. 314.150(a) or Sec. 314.151, the notice will announce the removal of the drug from the list of approved drugs published under section 505(j)(6) of the act and shall satisfy the requirement of Sec. 314.162(b). [57 FR 17994, Apr. 28, 1992]
