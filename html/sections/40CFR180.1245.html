CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 180  /  Sec. 180.1245 Rhamnolipid biosurfactant; exemption from the requirement

An exemption from the requirement of a tolerance is established for residues of rhamnolipid biosurfactant when used in accordance with good agricultural practices as a fungicide in or on all food commodities. [69 FR 16800, Mar. 31, 2004]
