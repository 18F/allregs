CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 408  /  Sec. 408.1040 When can you request a hearing before an administrative

(a) General rules. For purposes of this part, we use the same rules on hearings before an administrative law judge (ALJ) that we use in the title XVI program (see Secs. 416.1429-1416.1440 of this chapter), except as noted in paragraph (b) of this section.

(b) Exceptions. In Sec. 416.1433, the words ``one of our offices'' in paragraph (b) are deemed to read ``any of the offices listed in Sec. 408.1009(b)'' and the reference in the last sentence of Sec. 416.1433(c) to ``Sec. 416.1411'' is deemed to read ``Sec. 408.1011.''
