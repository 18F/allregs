CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 511  /  Sec. 511.201 Coverage of and exclusions from the General Schedule.

This part and chapter 51 of the title 5, United States Code, apply to all positions in the agencies except those specifically excluded by section 5102 of title 5, United States Code. (5 U.S.C. 5102) (5 U.S.C. 1104; Pub. L. 95-454, sec. 3(5)) [44 FR 54693, Sept. 21, 1979]
