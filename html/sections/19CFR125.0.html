CFR /  Title 19  /  Part 125  /  Sec. 125.0 Scope.

This part is concerned with cartage and lighterage of merchandise and the duties and liabilities of cartmen and lightermen, as well as those parties authorized in Sec. 112.2(b) to engage in cartage. Provisions for licensing cartmen and lightermen are in part 112 of this chapter. [T.D. 94-81, 59 FR 51495, Oct. 12, 1994]
