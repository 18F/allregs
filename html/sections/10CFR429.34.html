CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 429  /  Sec. 429.34 Torchieres.

(a) Sampling plan for selection of units for testing.

(1) The requirements of Sec. 429.11 are applicable to torchieres; and

(2) Reserved

(b) Certification reports.

(1) The requirements of Sec. 429.12 are applicable to torchieres; and

(2) Pursuant to Sec. 429.12(b)(13), a certification report shall include the following additional product-specific information: A declaration that the basic model meets the applicable design requirement and the features that have been incorporated into the torchiere to meet the applicable design requirement (e.g., circuit breaker, fuse, ballast).
