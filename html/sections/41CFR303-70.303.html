CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 303-70  /  Sec. 303-70.303 When a immediate family member, residing with the OCONUS, must we furnish mortuary services and/or transportation of the remains?

Yes, you must furnish transportation if requested by the employee. You must follow the guidelines in Sec. 303-70.301 for transportation expenses. You must furnish mortuary services only if the conditions in Sec. 303-70.300 are met.
