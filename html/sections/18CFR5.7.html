CFR /  Title 18  /  Part 5  /  Sec. 5.7 Tribal consultation.

A meeting shall be held no later than 30 days following filing of the notification of intent required by Sec. 5.5 between each Indian tribe likely to be affected by the potential license application and the Commission staff if the affected Indian tribe agrees to such meeting. [Order 2002, 68 FR 51121, Aug. 25, 2003; 68 FR 61742, Oct. 30, 2003]
