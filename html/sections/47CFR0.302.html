CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 0  /  Sec. 0.302 Record of actions taken.

The application and authorization files are designated as the Commission's official records of action of the Chief, Wireline Competition Bureau pursuant to authority delegated to the Chief. The official records of action are maintained in the Reference Information Center in the Consumer and Governmental Affairs Bureau. [67 FR 13221, Mar. 21, 2002] Sec. 0.303 [Reserved]
