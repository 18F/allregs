CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 1104  /  Sec. 1104.8 Objectionable matter.

The Board may order that any redundant, irrelevant, immaterial, impertinent, or scandalous matter be stricken from any document. [47 FR 49554, Nov. 1, 1982, as amended at 61 FR 52711, Oct. 8, 1996] Sec. 1104.9 [Reserved]
