CFR /  Title 37  /  Part 1  /  Sec. 1.435 The description.

(a) The application must meet the requirements as to the content and form of the description set forth in PCT Rules 5, 9, 10, and 11 and sections 204 and 208 of the Administrative Instructions.

(b) In international applications designating the United States the description must contain upon filing an indication of the best mode contemplated by the inventor for carrying out the claimed invention. [43 FR 20466, May 11, 1978, as amended at 63 FR 29618, June 1, 1998]
