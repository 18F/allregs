CFR /  Title 32  /  Part 643  /  Sec. 643.120 Post offices.

Title 10 U.S.C. 4779b, provides that the SA shall assign suitable space for post office purposes at military posts where post offices have been established. Space assignment will be accomplished by arrangement between the postmaster and installation commander.
