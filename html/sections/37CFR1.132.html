CFR /  Title 37  /  Part 1  /  Sec. 1.132 Affidavits or declarations traversing rejections or

When any claim of an application or a patent under reexamination is rejected or objected to, any evidence submitted to traverse the rejection or objection on a basis not otherwise provided for must be by way of an oath or declaration under this section. [65 FR 57057, Sept. 20, 2000]
