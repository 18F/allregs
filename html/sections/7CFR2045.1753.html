CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 2045  /  Sec. 2045.1753 Authority to accept gratuitous services.

(a) State Directors, Director, Personnel Division, and Director, Finance Office, are hereby authorized to accept and utilize gratuitous services offered by the governmental agencies listed in Sec. 2045.1752(a).

(b) An offer received by an FmHA or its successor agency under Public Law 103-354 State or County Office from a source listed in Sec. 2045.1752(b) shall be transmitted to the National Office, Attention: Director, Personnel Division, for decision. The offer will be accompanied by copies of the Articles of Incorporation and By-laws (if the organization is incorporated), a statement that the organization accepts the conditions set forth in the Agreement Form, and evidence that the organization is financially able to meet the required fiscal obligations of the agreement.
