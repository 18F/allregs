CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 27  /  Sec. 27.42 Surrender of certificate.

For good cause any certificate issued under this subpart shall be surrendered to a Marketing Services Office for correction or cancellation. If such certificate be not surrendered upon request it shall nevertheless be invalid under subsection 15b(f) of the Act and this subpart. [48 FR 49211, Oct. 25, 1983]
