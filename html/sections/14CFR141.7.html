CFR /  Title 14  /  Part 141  /  Sec. 141.7 Provisional pilot school certificate.

An applicant that meets the applicable requirements of subparts A, B, and C of this part, but does not meet the recent training activity requirements of Sec. 141.5(d) of this part, may be issued a provisional pilot school certificate with ratings.
