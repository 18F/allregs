CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 200  /  Sec. 200.300 Nondiscrimination and fair housing policy.

Federal Housing Administration programs shall be administered in accordance with:

(a)(2); and

(b) The affirmative fair housing marketing requirements in 24 CFR part 200, subpart M and 24 CFR part 108. [77 FR 5675, Feb. 3, 2012]
