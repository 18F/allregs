CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 157  /  Sec. 157.14 Pump-room bottom protection.

Each oil tanker of 5,000 tons deadweight or more constructed on or after January 1, 2007, must meet the minimum standard of pump room bottom protection required by Annex I, Regulation 22 (incorporated by reference, see Sec. 157.02). [USCG-2010-0194, 80 FR 5938, Feb. 4, 2015]
