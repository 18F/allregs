CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 1910  /  Sec. 1910.101 Preface.

FmHA or its successor agency under Public Law 103-354 Instruction 1910-C (available in any Farmers Home Administration (FmHA) or its successor agency under Public Law 103-354 office) describes the procedure to be used by FMHA in obtaining commercial credit reports. A nonrefundable fee, set forth in Sec. 1910.106(d) of this Instruction will be collected from the applicant, general contractor or dealer contractor who is the subject of the report. This subpart is inapplicable to Farm Service Agency, Farm Loan Programs. [52 FR 6498, Mar. 4, 1987, as amended at 72 FR 64122, Nov. 15, 2007] Sec. Sec. 1910.102-1910.150 [Reserved]
