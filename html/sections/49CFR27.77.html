CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 27  /  Sec. 27.77 Recipients of Essential Air Service subsidies.

Any air carrier receiving Federal financial assistance from the Department of Transportation under the Essential Air Service Program shall, as a condition of receiving such assistance, comply with applicable requirements of this part and applicable section 504 and ACAA rules of the Department of Transportation. [61 FR 56425, Nov. 1, 1996, as amended at 68 FR 51391, Aug. 26, 2003]
