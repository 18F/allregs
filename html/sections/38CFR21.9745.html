CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 21  /  Sec. 21.9745 Reporting fee.

In determining the amount of the reporting fee payable to institutions of higher learning for furnishing required reports, VA will apply the provisions of Sec. 21.4206 in the same manner as they are applied in the administration of 38 U.S.C. chapter 36. (Authority: 38 U.S.C. 3034(a), 3323(a), 3684)
