CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 932  /  Sec. 932.150 Modified minimum quality requirements for canned green

The minimum quality requirements prescribed in Sec. 932.52 (a)(1) of this part are hereby modified with respect to canned green ripe olives so that no requirements shall be applicable with respect to color and blemishes of such olives. [62 FR 1244, Jan. 9, 1997]
