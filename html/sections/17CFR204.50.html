CFR /  Title 17  /  Part 204  /  Sec. 204.50 Purpose.

This subpart establishes procedures for the Commission's referral of past-due legally enforceable debts to the Department of the Treasury's Financial Management Service (FMS) for offset against the income tax refunds of the debtor. [66 FR 54132, Oct. 26, 2001] Sec. 204.51 [Reserved]
