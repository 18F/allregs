CFR /  Title 14  /  Part 93  /  Sec. 93.93 Description of area.

The Los Angeles Special Flight Rules Area is designated as that part of Area A of the Los Angeles Class B airspace area at 3,500 feet above mean sea level (MSL) and at 4,500 feet MSL, beginning at Ballona Creek/Pacific Ocean (lat. 33[deg]57[min]42[sec] N, long. 118[deg]27[min]23[sec] W), then eastbound along Manchester Blvd. to the intersection of Manchester/405 Freeway (lat. 33[deg]57[min]42[sec] N, long. 118[deg]22[min]10[sec] W), then southbound along the 405 Freeway to the intersection of the 405 Freeway/lmperial Highway (lat. 33[deg]55[min]51[sec] N, long. 118[deg]22[min]06[sec] W), then westbound along Imperial Highway to the intersection of Imperial Highway/Pacific Ocean (lat. 33[deg]55[min]51[sec] N, long. 118[deg]26[min]05[sec] W), then northbound along the shoreline to the point of beginning.
