CFR /  Title 14  /  Part 323  /  Sec. 323.8 Exemptions.

Carriers are exempted from paragraphs (a)(1), (a)(3), and (a)(5) of Sec. 323.3 to the extent that those provisions require them to file a notice when terminating or suspending the domestic leg of an international flight (fill-up service). [Doc. No. OST-96-1269, 61 FR 19166, May 1, 1996]
