CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 206  /  Sec. 206.1 Scope.

This part applies to all solid oral dosage form human drug products, including prescription drug products, over-the-counter drug products, biological drug products, and homeopathic drug products, unless otherwise exempted under Sec. 206.7.
