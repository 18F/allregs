CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 169  /  Sec. 169.662 Hazardous locations.

Electrical equipment must not be installed in lockers that are used to store paint, oil, turpentine, or other flammable liquids unless the equipment is explosion-proof or intrinsically safe in accordance with Sec. 111.105-9 or Sec. 111.105-11 of this chapter.
