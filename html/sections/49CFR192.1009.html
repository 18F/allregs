CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 192  /  Sec. 192.1009 What must an operator report when a mechanical fitting

(a) Except as provided in paragraph (b) of this section, each operator of a distribution pipeline system must submit a report on each mechanical fitting failure, excluding any failure that results only in a nonhazardous leak, on a Department of Transportation Form PHMSA F- 7100.1-2. The report(s) must be submitted in accordance with Sec. 191.12.

(b) The mechanical fitting failure reporting requirements in paragraph (a) of this section do not apply to the following:

(1) Master meter operators;

(2) Small LPG operator as defined in Sec. 192.1001; or

(3) LNG facilities. [76 FR 5499, Feb. 1, 2011]
