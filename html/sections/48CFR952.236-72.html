CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 952  /  952.236-72 Nonrefundable fee for plans and specifications.

In accordance with the requirement at 936.202(h) include the following in solicitations for construction:

Nonrefundable Fee for Plans and Specifications (APR 1984)

A fee of $------ is required for the plans and specifications referenced in this solicitation. Send check or money order to ----------. The fee is not refundable. Plans and specifications need not be returned. [49 FR 12042, Mar. 28, 1984; 49 FR 38952, Oct. 2, 1984, as amended at 59 FR 9109, Feb. 25, 1994; 67 FR 14872, Mar. 28, 2002; 74 FR 36370, July 22, 2009]
