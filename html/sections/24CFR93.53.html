CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 93  /  Sec. 93.53 Federal Register notice of formula allocations.

Not later than 60 calendar days after the date that HUD determines the formula amounts under this subpart, HUD will publish a notice in the Federal Register announcing the availability of the allocations to States.
