CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 498  /  Sec. 498.78 Remand by the Administrative Law Judge.

(a) If CMS requests a remand, the ALJ may remand any case properly before him or her to CMS.

(b) The ALJ may remand at any time before notice of hearing decision is mailed. [52 FR 22446, June 12, 1987, as amended at 73 FR 36463, June 27, 2008]
