CFR /  Title 31  /  Part 208  /  Sec. 208.3 Payment by electronic funds transfer.

Subject to Sec. 208.4, and notwithstanding any other provision of law, effective January 2, 1999, all Federal payments made by an agency shall be made by electronic funds transfer.
