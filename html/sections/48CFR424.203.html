CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 424  /  424.203 Policy.

USDA regulations implementing the Freedom of Information Act are found in 7 CFR, Subtitle A, Part 1, Subpart A. Contracting officers shall follow these regulations when responding to requests for information. [70 FR 48, Jan. 3, 2005]
