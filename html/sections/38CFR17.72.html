CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 17  /  Sec. 17.72 Availability of information.

VA standards will be made available to other Federal, State and local agencies charged with the responsibility of licensing, or otherwise regulating or inspecting community residential care facilities. (Authority: 38 U.S.C. 1730) [54 FR 20842, May 15, 1989. Redesignated at 61 FR 21965, May 13, 1996]
