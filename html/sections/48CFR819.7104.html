CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 819  /  819.7104 General policy.

(a) To be eligible, mentors and Proteges must not be listed on the Excluded Parties List System, located at http://www.epls.gov. Mentors will provide appropriate developmental assistance to enhance the capabilities of Proteges to perform as prime contractors and/or subcontractors.

(b) VA reserves the right to limit the number of participants in the program in order to ensure its effective management of the Mentor- Protege Program.
