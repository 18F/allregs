CFR /  Title 39  /  Part 3050  /  Sec. 3050.14 Format of the Postal Service's section 3652 report.

The Postal Service's Cost and Revenue Analysis (CRA) report shall be presented in a format reflecting the classification structure in the Mail Classification Schedule. [79 FR 24336, Apr. 30, 2014]
