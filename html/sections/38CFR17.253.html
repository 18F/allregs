CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 17  /  Sec. 17.253 Applicants for grants.

Applicants for grants generally will be persons authorized to represent a medical school, hospital, or research center which has in effect or has tentatively approved an agreement with the Department of Veterans Affairs to exchange medical information. [33 FR 6011, Apr. 19, 1968. Redesignated at 61 FR 21966, May 13, 1996]
