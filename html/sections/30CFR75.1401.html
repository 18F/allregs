CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 75  /  Sec. 75.1401 Hoists; rated capacities; indicators.

Hoists shall have rated capacities consistent with the loads handled. An accurate and reliable indicator of the position of the cage, platform, skip, bucket, or cars shall be provided. [48 FR 53239, Nov. 25, 1983] Sec. 75.1401-1 Hoists; indicators.

The indicator required by Sec. 75.1401 of this subpart shall be placed so that it is in clear view of the hoisting engineer and shall be checked daily to determine its accuracy. [48 FR 53239, Nov. 25, 1983]
