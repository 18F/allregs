CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 417  /  Sec. 417.161 Compliance with assurances.

Any entity subject to this subpart must comply with the assurances that it provided to CMS, unless compliance is waived under Sec. 417.166. [58 FR 38071, July 15, 1993]
