CFR /  Title 9  /  Part 354  /  Sec. 354.65 Form of inspection mark.

The inspection mark approved for use on inspected and certified edible products shall be contained within a circle and include the following wording: ``Inspected for Wholesomeness by U.S. Department of Agriculture.'' The form and arrangement of such wording shall be as indicated in the example below. The plant number of the official plant shall be set forth if it does not appear on the packaging material.[GRAPHIC] [TIFF OMITTED] TC11SE91.056
