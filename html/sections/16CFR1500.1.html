CFR /  Title 16  /  Part 1500  /  Sec. 1500.1 Scope of subchapter.

Set forth in this subchapter C are the regulations of the Consumer Product Safety Commission issued pursuant to and for the implementation of the Federal Hazardous Substances Act as amended (see Sec. 1500.3(a)(1)).
