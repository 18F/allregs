CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 172  /  Sec. 172.558 CORROSIVE placard.

(a) Except for size and color, the CORROSIVE placard must be as follows: [GRAPHIC] [TIFF OMITTED] TR29SE00.002

(b) In addition to complying with Sec. 172.519, the background color on the CORROSIVE placard must be black in the lower portion with a white triangle in the upper portion. The base of the white triangle must be 38 mm [5 mm (1.5 inches [0.2 inches) above the placard horizontal center line. The text and class number must be white. The symbol and inner border must be black. [Amdt. 172-123, 56 FR 66264, Dec. 20, 1991, as amended at 65 FR 58627, Sept. 29, 2000]
