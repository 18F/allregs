CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 95  /  Sec. 95.812 License term.

(a) The term of each 218-219 MHz service system license is ten years from the date of original grant or renewal.

(b) Licenses for individually licensed CTSs will be issued for a period running concurrently with the license of the associated 218-219 MHz Service system with which it is licensed. [64 FR 59660, Nov. 3, 1999, as amended at 66 FR 9219, Feb. 7, 2001]
