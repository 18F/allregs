CFR /  Title 13  /  Part 123  /  Sec. 123.510 What if you don't use your Military Reservist EIDL funds

If your small business does not use Military Reservist EIDL funds as authorized by Sec. 123.508, then Sec. 123.9 applies (See Sec. 123.9, ``What happens if I don't use loan proceeds for the intended purpose?'').
