CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 75  /  Sec. 75.510 Energized trolley wires; repair.

[Statutory Provisions]

Energized trolley wires may be repaired only by a person trained to perform electrical work and to maintain electrical equipment and the operator of a mine shall require that such person wear approved and tested insulated shoes and wireman's gloves. Sec. 75.510-1 Repair of energized trolley wires; training.

The training referred to in Sec. 75.510 must include training in the repair and maintenance of live trolley wires, and in the hazards involved in making such repairs, and in the limitations of protective clothing used to protect against such hazards.
