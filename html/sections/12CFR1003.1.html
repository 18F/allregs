CFR /  Title 12  /  Part 1003  /  Sec. 1003.1 Authority, purpose, and scope.

* * * * *

(c) Scope. This part applies to financial institutions as defined in Sec. 1003.2(g). This part requires a financial institution to submit data to the appropriate Federal agency for the financial institution as defined in Sec. 1003.5(a)(4), and to disclose certain data to the public, about covered loans for which the financial institution receives applications, or that it originates or purchases, and that are secured by a dwelling located in a State of the United States of America, the District of Columbia, or the Commonwealth of Puerto Rico.
