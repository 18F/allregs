CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 67  /  Sec. 67.37 Association or joint venture.

(a) An association meets citizenship requirements if each of its members is a citizen.

(b) A joint venture meets citizenship requirements if each of its members is a citizen. [USCG-1999-6095, 65 FR 76576, Dec. 7, 2000]
