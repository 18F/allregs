CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 204  /  Sec. 204.3 Responsibilities of the State.

The State agency shall be responsible for assuring that the benefits and services available under titles IV-A, IV-D, and IV-F are furnished in an integrated manner. [57 FR 30425, July 9, 1992]
