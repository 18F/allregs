CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 388  /  Sec. 388.30 What are the matching requirements?

(a) The Secretary may make grants for paying part of the costs of projects under this program. Except as provided for in paragraphs (b) and (c) of this section, the grantee shall provide at least 10 percent of the total costs of the project.

(b) Grantees designated in Sec. 388.21(a)(3) to receive a minimum share of one third of one percent of the sums made available for the fiscal year shall provide at least four percent of the total costs of the project. (Authority: 29 U.S.C. 711(c), 770, and 771a)
