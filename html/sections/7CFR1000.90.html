CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 1000  /  Sec. 1000.90 Dates.

If a date required for a payment contained in a Federal milk order falls on a Saturday, Sunday, or national holiday, such payment will be due on the next day that the market administrator's office is open for public business. Sec. Sec. 1000.91-1000.92 [Reserved]
