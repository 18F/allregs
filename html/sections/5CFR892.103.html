CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 892  /  Sec. 892.103 What can I do if I disagree with my agency's decision

You may use the reconsideration procedure set out at Sec. Sec. 890.104 of this chapter to request an agency to reconsider its initial decision affecting your participation in the premium conversion plan.
