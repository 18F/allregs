CFR /  Title 9  /  Part 325  /  Sec. 325.3 Product transported within the United States as part of

When any shipment of any product is offered to any carrier for transportation within the United States as a part of an export movement, the same certificate shall be required as if the shipment were destined to a point within the United States. Sec. 325.4 [Reserved]
