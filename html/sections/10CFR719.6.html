CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 719  /  Sec. 719.6 Are there any types of legal matters not included in the

Legal matters not covered by this part include:

(a) Matters handled by counsel retained by an insurance carrier, except under retrospective insurance in accordance with Sec. 719.45;

(b) Routine intellectual property law support services; and

(c) Routine workers and unemployment compensation matters.
