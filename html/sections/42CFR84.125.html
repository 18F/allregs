CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 84  /  Sec. 84.125 Particulate tests; canisters containing particulate

Gas mask canisters containing filters for protection against particulates (e.g. dusts, fumes, mists, and smokes) in combination with gases, vapors, or gases and vapors, shall also comply with the requirements as prescribed in Sec. Sec. 84.170 through 84.183, except for the airflow resistance test of Sec. 84.181.
