CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 359  /  Sec. 359.32 What additional factors does the Secretary consider in

In determining which applicants to fund under this program, the Secretary also considers the proposed location of any project in order to achieve, to the extent possible, a geographic distribution of projects. (Authority: Sec. 204(b)(4)(C); 29 U.S.C. 762(b)(4)(C)) [52 FR 30066, Aug. 12, 1987] Secs. 359.33-359.39 [Reserved]
