CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 301  /  Sec. 301.50-7 Assembly and inspection of regulated articles.

(a) Any person (other than a person authorized to issue certificates or limited permits under Sec. 301.50-5(d)), who desires to move a regulated article interstate accompanied by a certificate or limited permit must notify an inspector, \7\ at least 48 hours in advance of the desired interstate movement.---------------------------------------------------------------------------

\7\ See footnote 3 to Sec. 301.50-5(a).---------------------------------------------------------------------------

(b) The regulated article must be assembled at the place and in the manner the inspector designates as necessary to comply with this subpart. (Approved by the Office of Management and Budget under control number 0579-0088) [57 FR 54496, Nov. 19, 1992, as amended at 58 FR 34683, June 29, 1993; 79 FR 21597, Apr. 17, 2014]
