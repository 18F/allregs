CFR /  Title 32  /  Part 728  /  Sec. 728.13 Application for care.

Possession of an ID card (a green colored DD 2 (with letter suffix denoting branch of service), Armed Forces Identification Card; a green colored PHS 1866-1, Identification Card; or a red colored DD 2 Res (Reservists on active duty for training)) alone does not constitute sufficient proof of eligibility. Accordingly, make a DEERS check, per Sec. 728.4(cc), before other than emergency care is rendered to the extent that may be authorized.
