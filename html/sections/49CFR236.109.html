CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 236  /  Sec. 236.109 Time releases, timing relays and timing devices.

Time releases, timing relays and timing devices shall be tested at least once every twelve months. The timing shall be maintained at not less than 90 percent of the predetermined time interval, which shall be shown on the plans or marked on the time release, timing relay, or timing device. [49 FR 3384, Jan. 26, 1984]
