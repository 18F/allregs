CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 154  /  Sec. 154.1320 Response plan submission requirements.

An owner or operator of a facility that handles, stores, or transports other non-petroleum oils shall submit a response plan in accordance with the requirements of this subpart, and with all sections of subpart F of this part, except Secs. 154.1045 and 154.1047, which apply to petroleum oils.
