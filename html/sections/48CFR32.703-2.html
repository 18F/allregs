CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 32  /  32.703-2 Contracts conditioned upon availability of funds.

(a)). This authority may be used only for operation and maintenance and continuing services (e.g., rentals, utilities, and supply items not financed by stock funds) (1) necessary for normal operations and (2) for which Congress previously had consistently appropriated funds, unless specific statutory authority exists permitting applicability to other requirements.

(b)).

(c) Acceptance of supplies or services. The Government shall not accept supplies or services under a contract conditioned upon the availability of funds until the contracting officer has given the contractor notice, to be confirmed in writing, that funds are available. [48 FR 42328, Sept. 19, 1983, as amended at 67 FR 13054, Mar. 20, 2002; 78 FR 37688, June 21, 2013]
