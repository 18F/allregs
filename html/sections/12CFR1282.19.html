CFR /  Title 12  /  Part 1282  /  Sec. 1282.19 Affordability--Rent level definitions--tenant income is

For purposes of determining whether a rental unit is affordable where the income of the family in the dwelling unit is not known to the Enterprise, the affordability of the unit is determined based on unit size as follows:

If an Enterprise makes such efforts but cannot obtain data on the number of bedrooms in particular units, in making the calculations on such units, the units shall be assumed to be efficiencies except as provided in Sec. 1282.15(e)(1).
