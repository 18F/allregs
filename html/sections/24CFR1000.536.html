CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 1000  /  Sec. 1000.536 What happens to NAHASDA grant funds adjusted, reduced,

Such NAHASDA grant funds shall be distributed by HUD in accordance with the next NAHASDA formula allocation. [63 FR 12349, Mar. 12, 1998, as amended at 77 FR 71529, Dec. 3, 2012]
