CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 309  /  Sec. 309.160 How will OCSE determine if Tribal IV-D program funds

OCSE will rely on audits required by OMB Circular A-133, ``Audits of States, Local Governments, and Non-Profit Organizations'' and 45 CFR part 74. The Department has determined that this program is to be audited as a major program in accordance with section 215(c) of the circular. The Department may supplement the required audits through reviews or audits conducted by its own staff.
