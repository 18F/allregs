CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 1033  /  Sec. 1033.401 Applicability.

The requirements of this subpart are applicable to certificate holders for locomotives subject to the provisions of this part. These requirements may also be applied to other manufacturers/remanufacturers as specified in Sec. 1033.1(d).
