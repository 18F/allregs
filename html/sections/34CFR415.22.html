CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 415  /  Sec. 415.22 What additional factors may the Secretary consider?

After evaluating the applications according to the criteria in Sec. 415.21, the Secretary may select applications other than the most highly rated applications if doing so would improve the geographical distribution of projects funded under this program. (Authority: U.S.C. 2413)
