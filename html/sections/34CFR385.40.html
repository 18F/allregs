CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 385  /  Sec. 385.40 What are the requirements pertaining to the membership

If a project funded under 34 CFR parts 386 through 390 or 396 establishes an advisory committee, its membership must include individuals with disabilities or parents, family members, guardians, advocates, or other authorized representatives of the individuals; members of minority groups; trainees; and providers of vocational rehabilitation and independent living rehabilitation services. (Authority: Sec. 12(c) of the Act; 29 U.S.C. 711(c)) [59 FR 8347, Feb. 18, 1994]
