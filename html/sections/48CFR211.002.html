CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 211  /  211.002 Policy.

All defense technology and acquisition programs in DoD are subject to the policies and procedures in DoDD 5000.01, The Defense Acquisition System, and DoDI 5000.02, Operation of the Defense Acquisition System. [71 FR 27641, May 12, 2006, as amended at 76 FR 76319, Dec. 7, 2011]
