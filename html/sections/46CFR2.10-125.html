CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 2  /  Sec. 2.10-125 Fees for examination of foreign tankships.

Each foreign tankship of a country party to the International Convention for the Safety of Life at Sea, 1974 as amended, must pay:

(a) For examination for the issuance of a Certificate of Compliance under Sec. 2.01-6(a)(2)(i) of this part, or examination for the annual endorsement to a Certificate of Compliance, a fee of $1,100.

(b) For examination for the issuance of a Tank Vessel Examination Letter under Sec. 2.01-6(a)(3) of this part, a fee of $1,100. [CGD 91-030, 60 FR 13563, Mar. 13, 1995, as amended by USCG-2010-0759, 75 FR 60001, Sept. 29, 2010]
