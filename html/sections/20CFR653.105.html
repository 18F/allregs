CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 653  /  Sec. 653.105 Job applications at day-haul facilities.

If the State agency is operating a day-haul facility under the exceptional circumstances provisions described in Sec. 653.106(a), a list of JS services shall be distributed and a full application shall be completed whenever an MSFW requests the opportunity to file a full application unless this is impractical at that time. In such cases, a full application shall be taken at the earliest practical time. In all other cases, a list of JS services shall be distributed.
