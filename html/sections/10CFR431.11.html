CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 431  /  Sec. 431.11 Purpose and scope.

This subpart contains energy conservation requirements for electric motors. It contains test procedures that EPCA requires DOE to prescribe, related requirements, energy conservation standards prescribed by EPCA, labeling rules, and compliance procedures. It also identifies materials incorporated by reference in this part. This subpart does not cover ``small electric motors,'' which are addressed in subpart X of this part. [77 FR 26633, May 4, 2012]
