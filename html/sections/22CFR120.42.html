CFR /  Title 22  /  Part 120  /  Sec. 120.42 Subject to the Export Administration Regulations (EAR).

Items ``subject to the EAR'' are those items listed on the Commerce Control List in part 774 of the EAR and all other items that meet the definition of that term in accordance with Sec. 734.3 of the EAR. The EAR is found at 15 CFR parts 730 through 774. [78 FR 22755, Apr. 16, 2013] Sec. 120.43 [Reserved]
