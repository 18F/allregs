CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 302  /  302.7100 HHS standard templates and formats.

HHS has developed standard templates and formats for preparation of various acquisition documents, reports, and plans. The templates and formats, which contain instructions for their completion, may be accessed on the ASFR/OGAPA/DA Internet Web site.
