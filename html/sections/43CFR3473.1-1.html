CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 3473  /  Sec. 3473.1-1 Form of remittance.

All remittances shall be by U.S. currency, postal money order or negotiable instrument payable in U.S. currency and shall be made payable to the Department of the Interior--Bureau of Land Management or the Department of the Interior--Minerals Management Service, as appropriate. In the case of payments made to the Service, such payments may also be made by electronic funds transfer. [49 FR 11638, Mar. 27, 1984]
