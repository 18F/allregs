CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 29  /  Sec. 29.1007 Color symbols.

As applied to flue-cured tobacco, color symbols are L--lemon, F--orange, FR--orange red, R--red, V--greenish, K--variegated, KR--variegated red or scorched, G--green, GR--green red, GK--green variegated (may be scorched), GG--gray green, KL--variegated lemon, KF--variegated orange, KV--variegated greenish, KM--variegated (scorched) mixed, KD--variegated dark red, and LL--whitish-lemon. [48 FR 29670, June 28, 1983]
