CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 0  /  Sec. 0.417 The Annual Reports.

At the end of each fiscal year, the Commission publishes an Annual Report containing general information concerning the Commission and the history of regulation, a summary of developments during the year, and selected industry statistics. [32 FR 10571, July 19, 1967]
