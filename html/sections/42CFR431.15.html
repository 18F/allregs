CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 431  /  Sec. 431.15 Methods of administration.

A State plan must provide for methods of administration that are found by the Secretary to be necessary for the proper and efficient operation of the plan. (Sec. 1902(a)(4) of the Act) [44 FR 17931, Mar. 23, 1979]
