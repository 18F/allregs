CFR /  Title 17  /  Part 274  /  Sec. 274.12 Form N-8B-2, registration statement of unit investment

This form shall be used as the registration statement to be filed, pursuant to section 8(b) of the Investment Company Act of 1940, by unit investment trusts other than separate accounts that are currently issuing securities, including unit investment trusts that are issuers of periodic payment plan certificates. [67 FR 19870, Apr. 23, 2002]
