CFR /  Title 17  /  Part 230  /  Sec. 230.120 Inspection of registration statements.

Except for material contracts or portions thereof accorded confidential treatment pursuant to Sec. 230.406, all registration statements are available for public inspection, during business hours, at the principal office of the Commission in Washington, D.C. Electronic registration statements made through the Electronic Data Gathering, Analysis, and Retrieval system are publicly available through the Commission's Web site (http://www.sec.gov). [61 FR 24654, May 15, 1996]
