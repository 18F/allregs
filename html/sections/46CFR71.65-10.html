CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 71  /  Sec. 71.65-10 Plans required for alterations of existing vessels.

(a) In the event of alterations involving the safety of the vessel, the applicable plans shall be submitted for approval covering the proposed work, except as modified by Sec. 71.55-1(b). The general scope of the plans shall be as noted in Sec. 71.65-5.
