CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 3280  /  Sec. 3280.507 Comfort heat gain.

Information necessary to calculate the home cooling load shall be provided as specified in this part.

(a) Transmission heat gains. Homes complying with this section shall meet the minimum heat loss transmission coefficients specified in Sec. 3280.506(a).
