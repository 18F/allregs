CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 1065  /  Sec. 1065.265 Nonmethane cutter.

(a) Application. You may use a nonmethane cutter to measure CH4 with a FID analyzer. A nonmethane cutter oxidizes all nonmethane hydrocarbons to CO2 and H2O. You may use a nonmethane cutter for raw or diluted exhaust for batch or continuous sampling.

(b) System performance. Determine nonmethane-cutter performance as described in Sec. 1065.365 and use the results to calculate CH4 or NMHC emissions in Sec. 1065.660.

(c) Configuration. Configure the nonmethane cutter with a bypass line if it is needed for the verification described in Sec. 1065.365.

(d) Optimization. You may optimize a nonmethane cutter to maximize the penetration of CH4 and the oxidation of all other hydrocarbons. You may humidify a sample and you may dilute a sample with purified air or oxygen (O2) upstream of the nonmethane cutter to optimize its performance. You must account for any sample humidification and dilution in emission calculations. [70 FR 40516, July 13, 2005, as amended at 73 FR 37300, June 30, 2008; 76 FR 57442, Sept. 15, 2011]
