CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 13  /  Sec. 13.55 Veterans Service Center Manager to select and appoint Department of Veterans Affairs benefits in a fiduciary capacity.

(a) Authority. The Veterans Service Center Manager is authorized to select and appoint (or in the case of a court-appointed fiduciary, to recommend for appointment) the person or legal entity best suited to receive Department of Veterans Affairs benefits in a fiduciary capacity for a beneficiary who is mentally ill (incompetent) or under legal disability by reason of minority or court action, and beneficiary's dependents.

(b) Payees. Authorized payees include:

(1) The beneficiary (Sec. 13.56(c));

(2) The beneficiary under supervision (supervised direct payment) (Sec. 13.56 (a) and (b));

(3) The wife or husband of an incompetent veteran (Sec. 13.57);

(4) The legal custodian of a beneficiary's Department of Veterans Affairs benefits (Sec. 13.58);

(5) A court-appointed fiduciary of a beneficiary (Sec. 13.59);

(6) The chief officer of the institution in which the veteran is receiving care and treatment (Sec. 13.61);

(7) The bonded officer of an Indian reservation (Sec. 13.62);

(8) A custodian-in-fact of the beneficiary (Sec. 13.63);

(9) Dependents of the veteran by an apportioned award (Sec. 13.70).

(c) Certification. The Veterans Service Center Manager's certification is authority to make payments to the designated payee. [40 FR 54247, Nov. 21, 1975]
