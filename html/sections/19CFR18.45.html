CFR /  Title 19  /  Part 18  /  Sec. 18.45 Supervision of exportation.

The provisions of Sec. Sec. 18.41 through 18.44 do not require the director of the port of actual exportation to verify that merchandise moving under cover of a TIR carnet is loaded on board the exporting carrier. [T.D. 71-70, 36 FR 4489, Mar. 6, 1971]
