CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 16  /  Sec. 16.119 Reconsideration and stay of action.

After any final administrative action that is the subject of a hearing under this part, any party may petition the Commissioner for reconsideration of any part or all of the decision or action under Sec. 10.33 or may petition for a stay of the decision or action under Sec. 10.35. [44 FR 22367, Apr. 13, 1979, as amended at 54 FR 9037, Mar. 3, 1989]
