CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 126  /  Sec. 126.260 Temporary Certificate.

If necessary to prevent delay of the OSV, a ``Temporary Certificate of Inspection,'' Form CG-854, containing information listed by Sec. 126.220 may be issued pending the issuance and delivery of the regular Certificate of Inspection. A Temporary Certificate must be carried in the same manner as the regular Certificate.
