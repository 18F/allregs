CFR /  Title 28  /  Part 513  /  Sec. 513.63 Freedom of Information Act requests on behalf of an

A request for records concerning an inmate or former inmate made by an authorized representative of that inmate or former inmate will be treated as in Sec. 513.61, on receipt of the inmate's or former inmate's written authorization. This authorization must be dated within three (3) months of the date of the request letter. Identification data, as listed in 28 CFR 16.41, must be provided.
