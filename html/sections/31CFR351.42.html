CFR /  Title 31  /  Part 351  /  Sec. 351.42 What is the issue date of a definitive series EE savings bond?

The issue date of a definitive bond is the first day of the month in which an authorized issuing agent received payment of the issue price. [76 FR 66855, Oct. 28, 2011]
