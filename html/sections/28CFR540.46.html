CFR /  Title 28  /  Part 540  /  Sec. 540.46 Attorney visits.

Requirements for attorney visits are governed by the provisions on inmate legal activities (see Secs. 543.12 through 543.16 of this chapter). Provisions pertinent to attorney visits for pretrial inmates are contained in Sec. 551.117 of this chapter. [68 FR 10658, Mar. 6, 2003]
