CFR /  Title 25  /  Part 1000  /  Sec. 1000.283 If the Tribe/Consortium or Tribe's/Consortium's employeewhat should the

Tribe/Consortium do?

As part of the notification required by 28 U.S.C. 2679(c), if the Tribe/Consortium or Tribe's/Consortium's employee receives a summons and/or complaint alleging a tort covered by FTCA, the Tribe/Consortium should immediately:

(a) Inform the Assistant Solicitor, Procurement and Patents, Office of the Solicitor, Department of the Interior, Room 6511, 1849 C Street NW., Washington, DC 20240,

(b) Inform the Tribe's/Consortium's tort claims liaison, and

(c) Forward all of the materials identified in Sec. 1000.277(c) to the contacts given in Sec. 1000.283 (a) and (b).
