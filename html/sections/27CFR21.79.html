CFR /  Title 27  /  Part 21  /  Sec. 21.79 Formula No. 44.

(a) Formula. To every 100 gallons of alcohol add: Ten gallons of n-butyl alcohol

(b) Authorized uses. (1) As a solvent: 430. Sterilizing and preserving solutions.

(2) Miscellaneous uses: 812. Product development and pilot plant uses (own use only).
