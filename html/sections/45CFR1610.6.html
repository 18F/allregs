CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 1610  /  Sec. 1610.6 Applicability.

Notwithstanding Sec. 1610.7(a), the prohibitions referred to in Sec. Sec. 1610.2(a)(4) (Criminal proceedings), (a)(5) (Actions challenging criminal convictions), (b)(7) (Aliens) or (b)(11) (Prisoner litigation) of this part will not apply to:

(a) A recipient's or subrecipient's separately funded public defender program or project; or

(b) Criminal or related cases accepted by a recipient or subrecipient pursuant to a court appointment.
