CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 852  /  852.219-72 Evaluation factor for participation in the VA

As prescribed in 819.7115(b), insert the following clause:

Evaluation Factor for Participation in the VA Mentor-Protege Program

(DEC2009)

This solicitation contains an evaluation factor or sub-factor regarding participation in the VA Mentor-Protege Program. In order to receive credit under the evaluation factor or sub-factor, the offeror must provide with its proposal a copy of a signed letter issued by the VA Office of Small and Disadvantaged Business Utilization approving the offeror's Mentor-Protege Agreement.

(End of clause) [74 FR 64638, Dec. 8, 2009]
