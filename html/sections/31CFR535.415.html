CFR /  Title 31  /  Part 535  /  Sec. 535.415 Payment by Iranian entities of obligations to persons

A person receiving payment under Sec. 535.904 may distribute all or part of that payment to anyone: Provided, That any such payment to Iran or an Iranian entity must be to a blocked account in a domestic bank. [44 FR 67617, Nov. 26, 1979]
