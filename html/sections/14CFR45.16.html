CFR /  Title 14  /  Part 45  /  Sec. 45.16 Marking of life-limited parts.

When requested by a person required to comply with Sec. 43.10 of this chapter, the holder of a type certificate or design approval for a life-limited part must provide marking instructions, or must state that the part cannot be practicably marked without compromising its integrity. [Doc. No. FAA-200-8017, 67 FR 2110, Jan. 15, 2002, as amended by Amdt. 45-26, 74 FR 53395, Oct. 16, 2009]
