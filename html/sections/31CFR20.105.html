CFR /  Title 31  /  Part 20  /  Sec. 20.105 Does this part apply to me?

(a) Portions of this part apply to you if you are either--

(1) A recipient of an assistance award from the Department of the Treasury; or

(2) A(n) Department of the Treasury awarding official. (See definitions of award and recipient in Sec. Sec. 20.605 and 20.660, respectively.)

(b) The following table shows the subparts that apply to you: ------------------------------------------------------------------------

(1) A recipient who is not an A, B and E.

individual.(2) A recipient who is an individual... A, C and E.(3) A(n) Department of the Treasury A, D and E.

awarding official.------------------------------------------------------------------------
