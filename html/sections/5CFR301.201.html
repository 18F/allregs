CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 301  /  Sec. 301.201 Appointments of United States citizens recruited overseas.

When there is a shortage of eligible applicants, as defined at Sec. 337.202 of this chapter, resulting from a competitive announcement that is open to applicants in the local overseas area, an agency may give an overseas limited appointment to a United States citizen recruited overseas for a position overseas. [69 FR 33275, June 23, 2004]
