CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 62  /  Sec. 62.14432 When must my waste management plan be completed?

As specified in Secs. 62.14463 and 62.14464, you must submit your waste management plan with your initial report, which is due 60 days after you demonstrate initial compliance with the amended emissions limits, by conducting an initial performance test or submitting the results of previous emissions tests, provided the conditions in Sec. 62.14451(e) are met. [78 FR 28067, May 13, 2013]
