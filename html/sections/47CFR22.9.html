CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 22  /  Sec. 22.9 Operation of certificated signal boosters.

Individuals and non-individuals may operate certificated Consumer Signal Boosters on frequencies regulated under this part provided that such operation complies with all applicable rules under this part and Sec. 20.21 of this chapter. Failure to comply with all applicable rules voids the authority to operate a signal booster. [78 FR 21563, Apr. 11, 2013]
