CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 556  /  Sec. 556.19 Periodic consultation with interested parties.

The Secretary shall provide for periodic consultation with State and local governments, existing and potential oil and gas lessees and permittees, and representatives of other individuals or organizations engaged in any activity in or on the OCS, including those involved in fish and shellfish recovery, and recreational activities. This consultation shall take place primarily through appropriate public notice as described in Sec. Sec. 556.16 and 556.17 and through the OCS Advisory Board and its committees, on a regional and National basis. Meetings of the OCS Advisory Board shall be held on specific issues as required by the Board's charter.
