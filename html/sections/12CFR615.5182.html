CFR /  Title 12  /  Part 615  /  Sec. 615.5182 Interest rate risk management by associations and other

Any association or other Farm Credit System institution other than Farm Credit banks, excluding the Federal Agricultural Mortgage Corporation, with interest rate risk that could lead to significant declines in net income or in the market value of capital must comply with the requirements of Sec. 615.5180. The interest rate risk management program required under Sec. 615.5180 must be commensurate with the level of interest rate risk of the institution. [77 FR 66375, Nov. 5, 2012]
