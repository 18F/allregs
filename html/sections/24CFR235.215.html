CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 235  /  Sec. 235.215 Method of paying insurance benefits.

If the application for insurance benefits is acceptable to the Secretary, the insurance claim shall be paid in cash, unless the mortgagee files a written request with the application for payment in debentures. [59 FR 49817, Sept. 30, 1994]
