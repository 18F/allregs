CFR /  Title 16  /  Part 1460  /  Sec. 1460.1 Scope and application.

In accordance with the Children's Gasoline Burn Prevention Act, portable gasoline containers must comply with the requirements specified in Sec. 1460.3, which are considered to be a consumer product safety rule.
