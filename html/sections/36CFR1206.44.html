CFR /  Title 36  /  Part 1206  /  Sec. 1206.44 Who is eligible for sub-grants?

All organizations located within a State that has an active State historical records board and entities defined in Sec. 1206.54 may be eligible, as determined by the board. [75 FR 66318, Oct. 28, 2010]
