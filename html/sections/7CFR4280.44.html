CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 4280  /  Sec. 4280.44 Limitation on number of loans or Grants to an Intermediary.

Depending on the amount of funds available, Rural Development may publish an announcement limiting an Intermediary to one selected Grant application and two selected loan applications in a fiscal year. Sec. Sec. 4280.45-4280.46 [Reserved]
