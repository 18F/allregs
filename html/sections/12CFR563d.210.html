CFR /  Title 12  /  Part 563d  /  Sec. 563d.210 Form and content of financial statements.

The financial statements required to be contained in filings with the Office under the Act are as set out in the applicable form and Regulation S-X, 17 CFR part 210. Those financial statements, however, shall conform as to form and content to the requirements of Sec. 563c.1 of this chapter.
