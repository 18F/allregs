CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 46  /  Sec. 46.37 Sundays and holidays excluded.

Sundays and holidays shall not be included in the computation of the 5-day period provided by section 7(d) of the Act nor in connection with the periods defined in Sec. 46.43 with exception of paragraph (a) thereof.
