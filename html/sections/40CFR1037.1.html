CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 1037  /  Sec. 1037.1 Applicability

This part contains standards and other regulations applicable to the emission of the air pollutant defined as the aggregate group of six greenhouse gases: carbon dioxide, nitrous oxide, methane, hydrofluorocarbons, perflurocarbons, and sulfur hexafluoride. The regulations in this part 1037 apply for all new heavy-duty vehicles, except as provided in Sec. 1037.5. This includes electric vehicles and vehicles fueled by conventional and alternative fuels.
