CFR /  Title 50  /  Part 11  /  Sec. 11.13 Decision by the Director.

Upon expiration of the period required or granted for filing of a petition for relief, the Director shall proceed to make an assessment of a civil penalty, taking into consideration information available to him and such showing as may have been made by the respondent, either pursuant to Sec. 11.11 or Sec. 11.12, or upon further request of the Director.
