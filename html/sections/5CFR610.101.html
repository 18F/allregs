CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 610  /  Sec. 610.101 Coverage.

This subpart applies to each employee to whom subpart A of part 550 applies and to each employee whose pay is fixed and adjusted from time to time under section 5343 or 5349 of title 5, United States Code, or by a wage board or similar administrative authority serving the same purpose. [42 FR 3297, Jan. 18, 1977]
