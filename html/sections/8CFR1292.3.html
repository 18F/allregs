CFR /  Title 8  /  Part 1292  /  Sec. 1292.3 Professional conduct for practitioners--Rules and procedures.

Attorneys and representatives practicing before the Board, the Immigration Courts, or DHS are subject to the imposition of disciplinary sanctions as provided in 8 CFR part 1003, subpart G, Sec. 1003.101 et seq. See also 8 CFR 292.3 (pertaining to practice before DHS). [77 FR 2015, Jan. 13, 2012]
