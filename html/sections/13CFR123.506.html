CFR /  Title 13  /  Part 123  /  Sec. 123.506 How much can you borrow under the Military Reservist

You can borrow an amount equal to the substantial economic injury you have suffered or are likely to suffer until normal operations resume as a result of the absence of one or more essential employees called to active duty, up to a maximum of $2 million. [73 FR 54675, Sept. 23, 2008]
