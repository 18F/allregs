CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 53  /  Sec. 53.01-5 Scope (modifies HG-100).

(a) of this subchapter gives a breakdown by parts in this subchapter of the regulations governing various types of pressure vessels and boilers.

(b) Modifies HG-100. The requirements of part HG of section IV of the ASME Boiler and Pressure Vessel Code shall be used except as noted otherwise in this part. [USCG-2003-16630, 73 FR 65163, Oct. 31, 2008]
