CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 427  /  Sec. 427.22 What additional factors does the Secretary consider?

(a) After evaluating the applications according to the criteria in Sec. 427.21 and consulting with the appropriate State board established under section 111 of the Act, the Secretary determines whether the most highly rated applications are equitably distributed among populations of individuals with limited English proficiency within the affected State.

(b) The Secretary may select other applications for funding if doing so would improve the--

(1) Equitable distribution of assistance among populations of individuals with limited English proficiency within a State; or

(2) Geographical distribution of projects funded under this program. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 2441(d)(5))
