CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 1045  /  Sec. 1045.8 Procedural exemptions.

(a) Exemptions to the procedural provisions of this part may be granted by the Director of Classification.

(b) A request for an exemption shall be made in writing to the Director of Classification and shall provide all relevant facts, justification, and a proposed alternate procedure. [62 FR 68509, Dec. 31, 1997, as amended at 71 FR 68737, Nov. 28, 2006]
