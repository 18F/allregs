CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 180  /  Sec. 180.1284 Ammonium salts of higher fatty acids (C [bdi8]-Cfrom the requirement of a tolerance.

Ammonium salts of C8-C18 saturated and C8-C12 unsaturated higher fatty acids are exempted from the requirement of a tolerance for residues in or on all food commodities when used in accordance with good agricultural practice. [74 FR 47457, Sept. 16, 2009]
