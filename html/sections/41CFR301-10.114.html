CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 301-10  /  Sec. 301-10.114 What must I do with unused Government Transportation

You must submit any unused GTR(s), unused ticket coupons, unused e-tickets, or refund applications to your agency in accordance with your agency's procedures. [FTR Amdt. 108, 67 FR 57964, Sept. 13, 2002]
