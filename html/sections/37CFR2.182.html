CFR /  Title 37  /  Part 2  /  Sec. 2.182 Time for filing renewal application.

An application for renewal must be filed within one year before the expiration date of the registration, or within the six-month grace period after the expiration date of the registration. If no renewal application is filed within this period, the registration will expire. [64 FR 48926, Sept. 8, 1999]
