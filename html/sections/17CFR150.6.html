CFR /  Title 17  /  Part 150  /  Sec. 150.6 Responsibility of contract markets.

Nothing in this part shall be construed to affect any provisions of the Act relating to manipulation or corners nor to relieve any contract market or its governing board from responsibility under section 5(4) of the Act to prevent manipulation and corners. [52 FR 38923, Oct. 20, 1987, as amended at 59 FR 5528, Feb. 7, 1993]
