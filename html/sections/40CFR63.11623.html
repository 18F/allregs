CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 63  /  Sec. 63.11623 What are the testing requirements?

(a) If you are demonstrating that the cyclone required by Sec. 63.11621(e) is designed to reduce emissions of particulate matter by 95 percent or greater by the performance test option in Sec. 63.11621(e)(1)(iii), you must conduct a test in accordance with paragraph (b) of this section and calculate the percent reduction in accordance with paragraph (c) of this section.

(b) You must use Method 5 in Appendix A to part 60 to determine the particulate matter mass rate at the inlet and outlet of the cyclone. You must conduct at least three runs at the cyclone inlet and three runs at the cyclone outlet. Each run must have a sampling time of at least 60 minutes and a sample volume of at least 0.85 dscm (30 dscf).
