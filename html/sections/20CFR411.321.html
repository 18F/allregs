CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 411  /  Sec. 411.321 Under what conditions will SSA terminate an agreement

We will terminate our agreement with an EN if it does not comply with the requirements under Secs. 411.320, Sec. 411.325, or the conditions in the agreement between SSA and the EN, including minimum performance standards relating to beneficiaries achieving self-supporting employment and leaving the benefit rolls.
