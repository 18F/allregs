CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 987  /  Sec. 987.105 Whole equivalent of pitted dates.

For the purposes of this part, the whole date equivalent weight of pitted dates shall be determined by dividing the weight of the pitted dates by 0.83. [53 FR 39226, Oct. 6, 1988]
