CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 54  /  Sec. 54.671 Resale.

(a) Prohibition on resale. Services purchased pursuant to universal service support mechanisms under this subpart shall not be sold, resold, or transferred in consideration for money or any other thing of value.

(b) Permissible fees. The prohibition on resale set forth in paragraph (a) of this section shall not prohibit a health care provider from charging normal fees for health care services, including instruction related to services purchased with support provided under this subpart. [78 FR 13992, Mar. 1, 2013]
