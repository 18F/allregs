CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 3214  /  Sec. 3214.18 Who will BLM hold liable under the lease and what are

BLM will hold all interest owners in a lease jointly and severally liable for compliance with the requirements of Sec. 3200.4 for obligations that accrue while they hold their interest. Among other things, all interest owners are jointly and severally liable for:

(a) Plugging and abandoning wells;

(b) Reclaiming the surface and other resources;

(c) Compensatory royalties assessed for drainage; and

(d) Rent and royalties due.
