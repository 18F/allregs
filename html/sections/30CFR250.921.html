CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 250  /  Sec. 250.921 How do I analyze my platform for cumulative fatigue?

(a) If you are required to analyze cumulative fatigue on your platform because of the results of an inspection or platform assessment, you must ensure that the safety factors for critical elements listed in Sec. 250.908 are met or exceeded.

(b) If the calculated life of a joint or member does not meet the criteria of Sec. 250.908, you must either mitigate the load, strengthen the joint or member, or develop an increased inspection process.
