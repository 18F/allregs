CFR /  Title 39  /  Part 3030  /  Sec. 3030.11 Service.

Any person filing a complaint must simultaneously serve a copy of the complaint on the Postal Service at the following address: PRCCOMPLAINTS@usps.gov. A complaint is not deemed filed until it is served on the Postal Service. A waiver may be obtained pursuant to Sec. 3001.9(a) of this chapter. [74 FR 54755, Oct. 23, 2009]
