CFR /  Title 23  /  Part 658  /  Sec. 658.7 Applicability.

Except as limited in Sec. 658.17(a) the provisions of this part are applicable to the National Network and reasonable access thereto. However, nothing in this regulation shall be construed to prevent any State from applying any weight and size limits to other highways, except when such limits would deny reasonable access to the National Network.
