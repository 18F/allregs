CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 98  /  Sec. 98.5 Fees and charges.

(a) The fee charged for any single laboratory analysis of meat, meat food products, and MREs, not covered by an agreement with Livestock and Seed Division, is specified in the schedules of charges in paragraph (a) of Sec. 91.37 of this subchapter.

(b) The laboratory analyses of meat, meat food products, and MREs, not covered by a cooperative agreement, shall result in an additional fee, found in Table 7 of Sec. 91.37 of this subchapter, for sample preparation or grinding.

(c) The charge for any requested laboratory analysis of meat, meat food products, and MREs not listed shall be based on the standard hourly rate specified in Sec. 91.37, paragraph (b).
