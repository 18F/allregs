CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 63  /  Sec. 63.748 Standards: Handling and storage of waste.

Except as provided in Sec. 63.741(e), the owner or operator of each facility subject to this subpart that produces a waste that contains HAP shall conduct the handling and transfer of the waste to or from containers, tanks, vats, vessels, and piping systems in such a manner that minimizes spills.
