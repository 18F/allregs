CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 102-33  /  Sec. 102-33.130 If we hire CAS, what are our management

If you hire CAS, you are responsible for--

(a) Establishing agency-specific Flight Program Standards, as defined in Sec. Sec. 102-33.140 through 102-33.185, as applicable, and requiring compliance with these standards in your contracts and agreements;

(b) Accounting for the cost of your aircraft and services hired as CAS;

(c) Accounting for the use of your aircraft hired as CAS; and

(d) Reporting the cost and usage data for your CAS hires (for reporting requirements, see subpart E of this part).
