CFR /  Title 9  /  Part 381  /  Sec. 381.84 Airsacculitis.

Carcasses of poultry with evidence of extensive involvement of the air sacs with airsacculitis or those showing airsacculitis along with systemic changes shall be condemned. Less affected carcasses may be passed for food after complete removal and condemnation of all affected tissues including the exudate. [40 FR 14297, Mar. 31, 1975]
