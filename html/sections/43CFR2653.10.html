CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 2653  /  Sec. 2653.10 Excess selections.

Where land selections by a regional corporation, Native group, any of the four named cities, or a Native pursuant to section 14(h) (1), (2), (3), or (5) exceed the land entitlement, the Bureau of Land Management may request such corporation to indicate its preference among lands selected. [41 FR 14740, Apr. 7, 1976]
