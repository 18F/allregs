CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 658  /  Sec. 658.412 Complaint resolution.

(a) A JS-related complaint is resolved when:

(1) The complainant indicates satisfaction with the outcome, or

(2) The complainant chooses not to elevate the complaint to the next level of review, or

(3) The complainant or the complainant's authorized representative fails to respond within 20 working days or in cases where the complainant is an MSFW, 40 working days of a written request by the appropriate local or State office, or

(4) The complainant exhausts the final level of review, or

(5) A final determination has been made by the enforcement agency to which the complaint was referred.
