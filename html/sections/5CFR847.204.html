CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 847  /  Sec. 847.204 Elections of FERS coverage.

(a) An employee who completes a qualifying move under Sec. 847.202(a), (c) or (e) from an FERS-covered position to an NAFI may elect to continue FERS coverage.

(b) An employee who elects FERS coverage under this section will be covered by FERS during all periods of future service not excluded from coverage by FERS, including any periods of service with a NAFI. [61 FR 41721, Aug. 9, 1996, as amended at 68 FR 2180, Jan. 16, 2003]
