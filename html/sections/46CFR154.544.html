CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 154  /  Sec. 154.544 Quick-closing shut-off valves.

The quick-closing shut-off valve under Sec. Sec. 154.530, 154.532 and 154.538 must:

(a) Be a shut-off valve;

(b) Close from the time of actuation in 30 seconds or less;

(c) Be the fail-closed type; and

(d) Be capable of local manual closing. [CGD 74-289, 44 FR 26009, May 3, 1979, as amended by CGD 77-069, 52 FR 31630, Aug. 21, 1987]
