CFR /  Title 14  /  Part 39  /  Sec. 39.17 What must I do if a change in a product affects my ability

If a change in a product affects your ability to accomplish the actions required by the airworthiness directive in any way, you must request FAA approval of an alternative method of compliance. Unless you can show the change eliminated the unsafe condition, your request should include the specific actions that you propose to address the unsafe condition. Submit your request in the manner described in Sec. 39.19.
