CFR /  Title 6  /  Part 27  /  Sec. 27.100 Purpose.

The purpose of this part is to enhance the security of our Nation by furthering the mission of the Department as provided in 6 U.S.C. Sec. 111(b)(1) and by lowering the risk posed by certain chemical facilities.
