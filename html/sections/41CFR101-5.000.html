CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 101-5  /  Sec. 101-5.000 Scope of part.

This part prescribes the methods by which the General Services Administration provides for establishment of centralized services in Federal buildings or complexes occupied by a number of executive agencies. [56 FR 33873, July 24, 1991]
