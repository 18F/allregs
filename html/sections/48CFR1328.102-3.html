CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 1328  /  1328.102-3 Contract clause.

The contracting officer shall insert the clause at 1352.228-77, Contractor Assurance of Subcontractor Payments, in solicitations and contracts when performance and payment bonds are waived. [80 FR 27267, May 13, 2015]
