CFR /  Title 50  /  Part 27  /  Sec. 27.11 Purpose of regulations.

The regulations in this part 27 govern those acts by the public which are prohibited at all times except as permitted in this part, part 26, and part 25, subpart D--Permits. [42 FR 56954, Oct. 31, 1977]
