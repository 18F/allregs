CFR /  Title 15  /  Part 100  /  Sec. 100.3 Custody.

The seal shall remain in the custody of the Director, Bureau of the Census or such officer or employee of the Bureau as he designates and shall be affixed to all certificates and attestations that may be required from the Bureau. [GRAPHIC] [TIFF OMITTED] TC20SE91.004
