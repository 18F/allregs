CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 301  /  Sec. 301.80-10 Nonliability of the Department.

The U.S. Department of Agriculture disclaims liability for any costs incident to inspections or compliance with the provisions of the quarantine and regulations in this subpart, other than for the services of the inspector. [35 FR 10553, June 30, 1970, as amended at 36 FR 24917, Dec. 24, 1971]
