CFR /  Title 13  /  Part 302  /  Sec. 302.20 Civil rights.

* * * * *

(b) Definitions. (1) For purposes of this section, an ``Other Party'' means an ``other party subject to this part,'' as defined in 15 CFR 8.3(l), and includes an entity which (or which is intended to) creates and/or saves 15 or more permanent jobs as a result of Investment Assistance; provided that such entity also is either specifically named in the application as benefiting from the Project, or is or will be located in an EDA building, port, facility, or industrial, commercial or business park constructed or improved in whole or in part with Investment Assistance prior to EDA's final disbursement of award funds.
