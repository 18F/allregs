CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 778  /  Sec. 778.21 Proof of publication.

A copy of the newspaper advertisements of the application for a permit, significant revision of a permit, or renewal of a permit, or proof of publication of the advertisements which is acceptable to the regulatory authority shall be filed with the regulatory authority and shall be made a part of the application not later than 4 weeks after the last date of publication as required by Sec. 773.6(a)(1) of this chapter. [48 FR 44399, Sept. 28, 1983, as amended at 65 FR 79669, Dec. 19, 2000]
