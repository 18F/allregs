CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 641  /  Sec. 641.230 Must the individual assessment conducted by the SCSEP

One-Stop delivery system be accepted for use by either entity

to determine the individual's need for services in the SCSEP

and adult programs under title I-B of WIA?

Yes, Sec. 502(b)(3) of the OAA provides that an assessment or IEP completed by the SCSEP satisfies any condition for an assessment, service strategy, or IEP completed at the One-Stop and vice-versa. (OAA Sec. 502(b)(3)). These reciprocal arrangements and the contents of the SCSEP IEP and WIA IEP should be negotiated in the MOU.
