CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 52a  /  Sec. 52a.9 Additional conditions.

The Director may, with respect to any grant award, impose additional conditions prior to or at the time of any award when in the Director's judgment the conditions are necessary to assure the carrying out of the purposes of the award, the interests of the public health, or the conservation of grant funds. [61 FR 55110, Oct. 24, 1996]
