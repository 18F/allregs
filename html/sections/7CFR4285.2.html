CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 4285  /  Sec. 4285.2 Cooperative agreement purposes.

Rural Development Administration (RDA) or its successor agency may enter into a cooperative agreement with a State agency to provide funds to the State agency to:

(a) Conduct marketing research related to agricultural cooperatives.

(b) Assist other organizations in conducting marketing research related to agricultural cooperatives.
