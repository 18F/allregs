CFR /  Title 50  /  Part 665  /  Sec. 665.963 Prohibitions.

In addition to the general prohibitions specified in Sec. 600.725 of this chapter, and Sec. 665.15 and subpart B of this part, the following activities are prohibited in the Monument and, thus, unlawful for a person to conduct or cause to be conducted.

(a) Commercial fishing in the Monument.

(b) Non-commercial fishing in the Monument, except as authorized under permit and pursuant to the procedures and criteria established in Sec. 665.965.

(c) Transferring a permit in violation of Sec. 665.965(d).

(d) Commercial fishing outside the Monument and non-commercial fishing within the Monument on the same trip in violation of Sec. 665.964(c).

(e) Fishing within 12 nm of emergent land within the Monument in violation of Sec. 665.964(d). [78 FR 33003, June 3, 2013, as amended at 78 FR 39583, July 2, 2013]
