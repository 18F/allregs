CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 333  /  Sec. 333.103 Definitions.

As used in this subpart:

First aid antibiotic. An antibiotic-containing drug product applied topically to the skin to help prevent infection in minor cuts, scrapes, and burns. [52 FR 47322, Dec. 11, 1987, as amended at 64 FR 403, Jan. 5, 1999]
