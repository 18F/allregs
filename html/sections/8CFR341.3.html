CFR /  Title 8  /  Part 341  /  Sec. 341.3 Depositions.

If satisfied that a witness whose testimony is essential is not available for examination in the United States, the assigned officer may authorize the taking of a deposition abroad by written interrogatories beforea DHS or Department of State official. [30 FR 5472, Apr. 16, 1965, as amended at 76 FR 53804, Aug. 29, 2011]
