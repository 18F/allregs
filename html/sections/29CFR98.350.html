CFR /  Title 29  /  Part 98  /  Sec. 98.350 What must I do if I learn of information required underDepartment of Labor?

At any time after you enter into a covered transaction, you must give immediate written notice to the Department of Labor office with which you entered into the transaction if you learn either that--

(a) You failed to disclose information earlier, as required by Sec. 98.335; or

(b) Due to changed circumstances, you or any of the principals for the transaction now meet any of the criteria in Sec. 98.335.
