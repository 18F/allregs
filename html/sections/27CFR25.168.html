CFR /  Title 27  /  Part 25  /  Sec. 25.168 Employer identification number.

The employer identification number (defined at 26 CFR 301.7701-12) of the taxpayer who has been assigned the number will be shown on each return on Form 5000.24, filed under this part. Failure of the taxpayer to include the employer identification number on Form 5000.24 may result in imposition of the penalty specified in Sec. 70.113 of this chapter. A brewer shall apply for an employer identification number on IRS Form SS-4 as provided in Sec. 25.169. (Pub. L. 87-397, 75 Stat. 828, as amended (26 U.S.C. 6109, 6676)) [T.D. ATF-224, 51 FR 7673, Mar. 5, 1986, as amended by T.D. ATF-301, 55 FR 47605, Nov. 14, 1990; T.D. TTB-79, 74 FR 37405, July 28, 2009]
