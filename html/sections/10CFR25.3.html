CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 25  /  Sec. 25.3 Scope.

The regulations in this part apply to licensees, certificate holders, and others who may require access to classified information related to a license, certificate, an application for a license or certificate, or other activities as the Commission may determine. [70 FR 32227, June 2, 2005]
