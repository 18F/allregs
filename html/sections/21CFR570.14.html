CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 570  /  Sec. 570.14 Indirect food additives resulting from packaging materials

Regulations providing for the use of food packaging materials in parts 174 through 179 of this chapter are incorporated in Subchapter E as applicable to packaging materials used for animal feed and pet food. [42 FR 14091, Mar. 15, 1977]
