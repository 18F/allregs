CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 220  /  Sec. 220.25 Instruction and operational testing of employees.

Each employee who a railroad authorizes to use a radio in connection with a railroad operation, shall be:

(a) Provided with a copy of the railroad's operating rules governing the use of radio communication in a railroad operation;

(b) Instructed in the proper use of radio communication as part of the program of instruction prescribed in Sec. 217.11 of this chapter; and

(c) Periodically tested under the operational testing requirements in Sec. 217.9 of this chapter.
