CFR /  Title 13  /  Part 108  /  Sec. 108.700 Compliance with size standards in part 121 of this

You are permitted to provide financial assistance and management services only to a Small Business. To determine whether an applicant meets the size standards for a Small Business, you may use either the financial size standards in Sec. 121.301(c)(1) of this chapter or the industry standard covering the industry in which the applicant is primarily engaged, as set forth in Sec. 121.301(c)(2) of this chapter.
