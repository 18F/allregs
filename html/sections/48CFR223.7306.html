CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 223  /  223.7306 Contract clause.

Unless an exception in 223.7304 applies, or use has been authorized in accordance with 223.7305, use the clause at 252.223-7008, Prohibition of Hexavalent Chromium, in solicitations and contracts, including solicitations and contracts using FAR part 12 procedures for the acquisition of commercial items, that are for supplies, maintenance and repair services, or construction. . [76 FR 25575, May 5, 2011, as amended at 78 FR 37986, June 25, 2013]
