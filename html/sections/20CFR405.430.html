CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 405  /  Sec. 405.430 Record before the Appeals Council.

Subject to Sec. 405.401(c), the record is closed as of the date of the administrative law judge's decision, and the Appeals Council will base its action on the same evidence that was before the administrative law judge.
