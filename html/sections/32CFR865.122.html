CFR /  Title 32  /  Part 865  /  Sec. 865.122 Summary of statistics for Discharge Review Board.

The Air Force Discharge Review Board shall prepare and provide to the Deputy Assistant Secretary of Defense (Military Personnel and Force Management) DASD(MP&FM;), Office of the ASD(MRA&L;), a semiannual report of discharge review actions in accordance with Sec. 865.125.
