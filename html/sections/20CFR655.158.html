CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 655  /  Sec. 655.158 Duration of positive recruitment.

Except as otherwise noted, the obligation to engage in positive recruitment described in Sec. Sec. 655.150 through 655.154 shall terminate on the date H-2A workers depart for the employer's place of work. Unless the SWA is informed in writing of a different date, the date that is the third day preceding the employer's first date of need will be determined to be the date the H-2A workers departed for the employer's place of business.
