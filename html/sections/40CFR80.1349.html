CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 80  /  Sec. 80.1349 Alternative sampling and testing requirements for

Importers who import conventional gasoline into the United States by truck may comply with the sampling and testing requirements in Sec. 80.101(i)(3) instead of the requirements to sample and test every batch of gasoline under Sec. 80.1347. An importer that uses this approach must meet the 0.62 volume percent benzene standard on a per- gallon basis. [79 FR 23655, Apr. 28, 2014]
