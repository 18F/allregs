CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 883  /  Sec. 883.313 Audit.

Where housing assistance under the Section 8 Program is provided for projects developed or owned by non-Federal entities (as defined in 2 CFR 200.69), the audit requirements in 2 CFR part 200, subpart F, shall apply. [80 FR 75941, Dec. 7, 2015] Subparts D-E [Reserved]
