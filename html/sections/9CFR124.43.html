CFR /  Title 9  /  Part 124  /  Sec. 124.43 Administrative decision.

Within 30 days after completion of the due diligence hearing, the Under Secretary for Marketing and Regulatory Programs, taking into consideration the recommendation of the Administrator, will affirm or revise the determination made under Sec. 124.32. APHIS will publish the due diligence redetermination in the Federal Register, notify PTO of the redetermination, and send copies of the notice to PTO and the requesting party, the applicant, and the petitioner. [59 FR 11369, Feb. 25, 1993, as amended at 64 FR 43045, Aug. 9, 1999]
