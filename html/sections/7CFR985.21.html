CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 985  /  Sec. 985.21 Eligibility.

Each member and alternate member of the Committee shall be, at the time of selection and during the term of office, a producer, or an officer or employee of a producer, in the district for which selected: Provided, That these requirements should not apply to the public member and alternate member.
