CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 357  /  Sec. 357.210 Cholecystokinetic active ingredients.

The active ingredient of the product consists of any of the following when used within the specified concentration and dosage form established for each ingredient:

(a) 50-percent aqueous emulsion of corn oil.

(b) Hydrogenated soybean oil in a suitable, water-dispersible powder. The hydrogenated soybean oil is food-grade, partially hydrogenated with a melting point of 41 to 43.5 [deg]C, an iodine value of 65 to 69, and a fatty acid composition as follows: ------------------------------------------------------------------------
