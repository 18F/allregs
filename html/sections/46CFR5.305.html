CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 5  /  Sec. 5.305 Quashing a subpoena.

Any person subpoenaed to appear to produce evidence at a hearing may request that the subpoena be quashed or modified using the procedures in 33 CFR 20.609. [USCG-1998-3472, 64 FR 28075, May 24, 1999]
