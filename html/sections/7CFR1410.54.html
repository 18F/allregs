CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 1410  /  Sec. 1410.54 Performance based upon advice or action of the Department.

The provisions of Sec. 718.8 of this chapter relating to performance based upon the action or advice of an authorized representative of the Department shall be applicable to this part, and may be considered as a basis to provide relief to persons subject to sanctions under this part to the extent that relief is otherwise required by this part.
