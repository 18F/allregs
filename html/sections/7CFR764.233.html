CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 764  /  Sec. 764.233 Limitations.

(a) The applicant must comply with the general limitations specified in Sec. 764.102 except Sec. 764.102(f), which does not apply to applicants for the CL Program.

(b) The applicant must agree to repay any duplicative financial benefits or assistance to CL.
