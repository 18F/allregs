CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 501  /  Sec. 501.103 Petitions requesting exemptions from or special requirements

The Commissioner of Food and Drugs, either on his own initiative or on behalf of any interested person who has submitted a petition pursuant to part 10 of this chapter may issue a proposal to amend Sec. 501.4 to specify the manner in which an ingredient(s) shall be declared, i.e., by specific or class name, or Sec. 501.100 to exempt an ingredient(s) from the requirements for label declaration. [41 FR 38619, Sept. 10, 1976, as amended at 42 FR 15675, Mar. 22, 1977]
