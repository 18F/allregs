CFR /  Title 28  /  Part 8  /  Sec. 8.8 Commencing the administrative forfeiture proceeding.

An administrative forfeiture proceeding begins when notice is first published in accordance with Sec. 8.9(a), or the first personal written notice is sent in accordance with Sec. 8.9(b), whichever occurs first.
