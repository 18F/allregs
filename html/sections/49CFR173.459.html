CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 173  /  Sec. 173.459 Mixing of fissile material packages with non-fissile

Mixing of fissile material packages with other types of Class 7 (radioactive) materials in any conveyance or storage location is authorized only if the TI of any single package does not exceed 10, the CSI of any single package does not exceed 50, and the provisions of Secs. 173.441 and 173.457 are satisfied. [69 FR 3692, Jan. 26, 2004]
