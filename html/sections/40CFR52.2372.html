CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 52  /  Sec. 52.2372 Approval status.

(a) With the exceptions set forth in this subpart, the Administrator approves Vermont's plan as identified in Sec. 52.2370 for the attainment and maintenance of the national standards under section 110 of the Clean Air Act. Furthermore, the Administrator finds the plans satisfy all requirements of Part D, Title I, of the Clean Air Act, as amended in 1977, except as noted below. In addition, continued satisfaction of the requirements of Part D for the ozone portion of the SIP depends on the adoption and submittal of RACT requirements by July 1, 1980 for the sources covered by CTGs issued between January, 1978 and January, 1979 and adoption and submittal by each subsequent January of additional RACT requirements for sources covered by CTGs issued by the previous January.

(b) [Reserved] [75 FR 82562, Dec. 30, 2010, as amended at 77 FR 60910, Oct. 5, 2012]
