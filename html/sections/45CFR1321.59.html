CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 1321  /  Sec. 1321.59 Submission of an area plan and plan amendments to the

The area agency shall submit the area plan and amendments to the State agency for approval following procedures specified by the State agency in the State policies prescribed by Sec. 1321.11.
