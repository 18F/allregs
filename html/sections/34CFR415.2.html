CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 415  /  Sec. 415.2 Who is eligible for an award?

A private nonprofit organization that is eligible to receive funding under title III of the Job Training Partnership Act (29 U.S.C. 1651 et seq.) is eligible to receive an award under this program. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 2413(d))
