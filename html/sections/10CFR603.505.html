CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 603  /  Sec. 603.505 Program resources.

Program officials can be a source of information for determining the reasonableness of proposed funding (e.g., on labor rates, as discussed in Sec. 603.520) or establishing observable and verifiable technical milestones for payments (see Sec. 603.570).
