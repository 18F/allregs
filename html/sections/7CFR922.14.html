CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 922  /  Sec. 922.14 District.

District means the applicable one of the following described subdivisions of the production area, or such other subdivisions as may be prescribed pursuant to Sec. 922.31(m):

(a) District 1 shall include all counties within the production area not included in District 2.

(b) District 2 shall include the Counties of Yakima, Benton, and Klickitat. [22 FR 3514, May 21, 1957. Redesignated at 26 FR 12751, Dec. 30, 1961, as amended at 27 FR 5188, June 2, 1962]
