CFR /  Title 37  /  Part 255  /  Sec. 255.1 General.

This part 255 adjusts the rates of royalties payable under the compulsory license for making and distributing phonorecords, including digital phonorecord deliveries, embodying nondramatic musical works, under 17 U.S.C. 115. [60 FR 61657, Dec. 1, 1995]
