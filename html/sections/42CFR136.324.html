CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 136  /  Sec. 136.324 Availability of list of recipients.

The Indian Health Service will provide to any persons requesting it a list of the recipients of scholarship grants under this subdivision, including the school attended and tribal affiliation of each recipient. [49 FR 7381, Feb. 29, 1984]
