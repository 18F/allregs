CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 58  /  Sec. 58.60-9 Industrial systems: Design.

Each system under this subpart must be designed and analyzed in accordance with the principles of API RP 14C (incorporated by reference, see 46 CFR 58.03-1). [USCG-2003-16630, 73 FR 65188, Oct. 31, 2008]
