CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 725  /  Sec. 725.426 Applicability of the Tier I exemption.

The Tier I exemption under Sec. 725.424 applies only to a manufacturer or importer of a new microorganism that certifies that the microorganism will be used in all cases in compliance with Sec. Sec. 725.420, 725.421, and 725.422.
