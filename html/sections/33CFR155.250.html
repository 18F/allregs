CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 155  /  Sec. 155.250 Oil fuel tank protection.

Each ship with an aggregate oil fuel capacity of 600 cubic meters or more that is delivered on or after August 1, 2010, must meet the minimum standard of oil fuel tank protection required by Annex I, Regulation 12A (incorporated by reference, see Sec. 155.140). [USCG-2010-0194, 80 FR 5934, Feb. 4, 2015]
