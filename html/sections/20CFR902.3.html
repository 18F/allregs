CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 902  /  Sec. 902.3 Published information.

(a) Federal Register. Pursuant to sections 552 and 553 of title 5 of the United States Code, and subject to the provisions of Sec. 902.5, the Joint Board publishes in the Federal Register for the guidance of the public, in addition to this part, descriptions of its organization and procedures, substantive rules of general applicability, and, as may from time to time be appropriate, statements of general policy, and interpretations of general applicability.

(b) Other published information. From time to time, the Joint Board issues statements to the press relating to its operations.

(c) Obtaining printed information. If not available through the Government Printing Office, printed information released by the Joint Board may be obtained without cost from the Executive Director of the Joint Board (``Executive Director'').
