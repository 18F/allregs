CFR /  Title 37  /  Part 1  /  Sec. 1.323 Certificate of correction of applicant's mistake.

The Office may issue a certificate of correction under the conditions specified in 35 U.S.C. 255 at the request of the patentee or the patentee's assignee, upon payment of the fee set forth in Sec. 1.20(a). If the request relates to a patent involved in an interference or trial before the Patent Trial and Appeal Board, the request must comply with the requirements of this section and be accompanied by a motion under Sec. 41.121(a)(2), Sec. 41.121(a)(3) or Sec. 42.20 of this title. [77 FR 46626, Aug. 6, 2012]
