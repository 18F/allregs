CFR /  Title 13  /  Part 126  /  Sec. 126.603 Does HUBZone certification guarantee receipt of HUBZone

HUBZone certification does not guarantee that a qualified HUBZone SBC will receive HUBZone contracts. Qualified HUBZone SBCs should market their capabilities to appropriate contracting activities in order to increase the prospect that the contracting activity will adopt an acquisition strategy that includes HUBZone contract opportunities. [69 FR 29425, May 24, 2004]
