CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 160  /  Sec. 160.076-15 Suspension or termination of approval.

As provided in Sec. 159.005-15 of this chapter, the Commandant may suspend or terminate the approval of an inflatable PFD design if the manufacturer fails to comply with this subpart or the recognized laboratory's accepted procedures or requirements.
