CFR /  Title 22  /  Part 102  /  Sec. 102.18 Protection of United States citizens involved.

The local Foreign Service post shall follow substantially the procedures prescribed in Sec. Sec. 102.11 to 102.13 in protecting United States citizens (whether alive or dead) involved in a foreign aircraft accident.
