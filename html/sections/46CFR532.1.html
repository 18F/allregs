CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 532  /  Sec. 532.1 Purpose.

The purpose of this part, pursuant to the Commission's statutory authority, is to exempt non-vessel-operating common carriers (NVOCCs) from the tariff rate publication and adherence requirements of the Shipping Act of 1984, as enumerated herein. [78 FR 42888, July 18, 2013]
