CFR /  Title 25  /  Part 15  /  Sec. 15.203 What information must Tribes provide BIA to complete the

Tribes must provide any information that we require or request to complete the probate file. This information may include enrollment and family history data or property title documents that pertain to any pending probate matter, and a copy of Tribal probate orders where they exist. [76 FR 7505, Feb. 10, 2011]
