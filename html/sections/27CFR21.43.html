CFR /  Title 27  /  Part 21  /  Sec. 21.43 Formula No. 18.

(a) Formula. To every 100 gallons of alcohol of not less than 160 proof add: One hundred gallons of vinegar of not less than 90-grain strength or 150 gallons of vinegar of not less than 60-grain strength.

(b) Authorized uses. (1) As a raw material: 511. Vinegar.

(1) As a raw material: 511. Vinegar.

(2) Miscellaneous uses: 812. Product development and pilot plant uses (own use only).
