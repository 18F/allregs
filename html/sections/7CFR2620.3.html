CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 2620  /  Sec. 2620.3 Requests.

(a) Requests for OIG records shall be in writing in accordance with Sec. 1.6(a) of this title and addressed to the Assistant Inspector General for Policy Development and Resources Management (AIG/PD&RM;), Office of Inspector General, U.S. Department of Agriculture, Ag Box 2310, Washington, DC 20250. The above official is hereby delegated authority to make determinations regarding such requests in accordance with Sec. 1.3(a)(3) of this title.

(b) Requests should be reasonably specific in identifying the record requested and should include the name, address, and telephone number of the requester.

(c) Available records may be inspected and copied in the office of the AIG/PD&RM;, from 8 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. local time on regular working days or may be obtained by mail. Copies will be provided upon payment of applicable fees, unless waived or reduced, in accordance with the Department's fee schedule as set forth in appendix A of subpart A of part 1 of this title.
