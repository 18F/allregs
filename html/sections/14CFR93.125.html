CFR /  Title 14  /  Part 93  /  Sec. 93.125 Arrival or departure reservation.

Except between 12 Midnight and 6 a.m. local time, no person may operate an aircraft to or from an airport designated as a high density traffic airport unless he has received, for that operation, an arrival or departure reservation from ATC. [Doc. No. 9974, 37 FR 22794, Oct. 25, 1972]
