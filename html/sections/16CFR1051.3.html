CFR /  Title 16  /  Part 1051  /  Sec. 1051.3 Place of filing.

A petition should be mailed to: Office of the Secretary, Consumer Product Safety Commission, Washington, DC 20207. Persons wishing to file a petition in person may do so in the Office of the Secretary, at 4330 East West Highway, Bethesda, Maryland. [48 FR 57123, Dec. 28, 1983, as amended at 62 FR 46667, Sept. 4, 1997]
