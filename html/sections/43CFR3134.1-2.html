CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 3134  /  Sec. 3134.1-2 Additional bonds.

(a) The authorized officer may require the bonded party to supply additional bonding in accordance with Sec. 3104.5(b) of this chapter.

(b) The holders of any oil and gas lease bond for a lease on the NPR-A shall be permitted to obtain a rider to include the coverage of oil and gas geophysical operations within the boundaries of NPR-A. [46 FR 55497, Nov. 9, 1981, as amended at 53 FR 17358, May 16, 1988; 73 FR 6442, Feb. 4, 2008]
