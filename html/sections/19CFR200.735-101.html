CFR /  Title 19  /  Part 200  /  Sec. 200.735-101 Purpose.

The purpose of the regulations in this part is to maintain the highest standards of honesty, integrity, impartiality, and conduct on the part of all employees of the U.S. International Trade Commission and to maintain public confidence that the business of the Commission is being conducted in accordance with such standards. [31 FR 2593, Feb. 10, 1966, as amended at 46 FR 17542, Mar. 19, 1981]
