CFR /  Title 17  /  Part 249  /  Sec. 249.331 Form N-CSR, certified shareholder report.

This form shall be used by registered management investment companies to file reports pursuant to Sec. 270.30b2-1(a) of this chapter not later than 10 days after the transmission to stockholders of any report that is required to be transmitted to stockholders under Sec. 270.30e-1 of this chapter. [68 FR 5365, Feb. 3, 2003]
