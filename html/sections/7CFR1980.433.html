CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 1980  /  Sec. 1980.433 Flood or mudslide hazard area precautions.

(See subpart A, Sec. 1980.42.)

Administrative

The State Director is responsible for determining if a project is located in a special flood or mudslide hazard area. Refer to subpart B of part 1806 of this chapter [RD Instruction 426.2].
