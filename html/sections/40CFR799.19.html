CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 799  /  Sec. 799.19 Chemical imports and exports.

Persons who export or who intend to export chemical substances or mixtures listed in subpart B, subpart C, or subpart D of this part are subject to the requirements of 40 CFR part 707. [71 FR 66245, Nov. 14, 2006]
