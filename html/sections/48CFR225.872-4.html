CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 225  /  225.872-4 Individual determinations.

If the offer of an end product from a qualifying country source listed in 225.872-1(b), as evaluated, is low or otherwise eligible for award, prepare a determination and findings exempting the acquisition from the Buy American statute and the Balance of Payments Program as inconsistent with the public interest, unless another exception such as the Trade Agreements Act applies. Follow the procedures at PGI 225.872-4. [70 FR 73155, Dec. 9, 2005, as amended at 77 FR 35880, June 15, 2012]
