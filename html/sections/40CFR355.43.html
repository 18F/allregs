CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 355  /  Sec. 355.43 When must I submit the information?

(a) You must provide the required emergency release notification information described under Sec. 355.40(a), immediately.

(b) You must provide the written follow-up emergency notice (or notices, as more information becomes available) described under Sec. 355.40(b), as soon as practicable after the release.
