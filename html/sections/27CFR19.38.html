CFR /  Title 27  /  Part 19  /  Sec. 19.38 Approval of required documents.

Except as otherwise provided in this part, the appropriate TTB officer is authorized to approve all documents, bonds, and consents of surety required by this part. (26 U.S.C. 5171, 5172, 5173, and 5551)
