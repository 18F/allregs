CFR /  Title 16  /  Part 301  /  Sec. 301.10 Use of term ``Broadtail-processed Lamb'' permitted.

The term Broadtail-processed Lamb may be used to describe the skin of a lamb which has been sheared, leaving a moire hair pattern on the pelt having the appearance of the true fur pattern of ``Broadtail Lamb''; as for example: Dyed Broadtail-processed Lamb Fur origin: Argentina
