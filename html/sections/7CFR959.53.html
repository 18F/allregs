CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 959  /  Sec. 959.53 Handling for special purposes.

Regulations in effect pursuant to Sec. Sec. 959.42, 959.52, or 959.60 may be modified, suspended, or terminated to facilitate handling of onions for:

(a) Relief or charity;

(b) Experimental purposes;

(c) Export; and

(d) Other purposes which may be recommended by the committee and approved by the Secretary.
