CFR /  Title 31  /  Part 363  /  Sec. 363.133 What happens to my zero-percent certificate of indebtedness if

Upon the termination of this offering by the Secretary, the zero-percent certificate of indebtedness ceases to roll over; the proceeds will be paid by the ACH method to the bank account at a financial institution that you designated in your TreasuryDirect [supreg] account as your primary bank account.
