CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 29  /  Sec. 29.95 Review or second inspection not an appeal.

A review or investigation made in accordance with Sec. 29.132, or a second inspection, sampling, or weighing made upon the request of an interested party for the purpose of securing new or later information when the correctness of an old certificate or sample is not questioned, shall not be considered an appeal.
