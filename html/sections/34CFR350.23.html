CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 350  /  Sec. 350.23 What restriction exists on Rehabilitation Research and

A host institution with which a Rehabilitation Research and Training Center is affiliated may not collect more than fifteen percent of the total grant award as indirect cost charges, notwithstanding the provisions in 34 CFR 75.562. (Authority: Sec. 204(b)(2)(O); 29 U.S.C. 762(b)(2)(O))
