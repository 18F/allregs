CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 58  /  Sec. 58.01-45 Machinery space, ventilation.

Each machinery space must be ventilated to ensure that, when machinery or boilers are operating at full power in all weather including heavy weather, an adequate supply of air is maintained for the operation of the machinery and for the safety, efficiency, and comfort of the crew. [CGD 83-043, 60 FR 24775, May 10, 1995]
