CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 3163  /  Sec. 3163.4 Failure to pay.

If any person fails to pay an assessment or a civil penalty under Sec. 3163.1 or Sec. 3163.2 of this title after the order making the assessment or penalty becomes a final order, and if such person does not file a petition for judicial review in accordance with this subpart, or, after a court in an action brought under this subpart has entered a final judgment in favor of the Secretary, the court shall have jurisdiction to award the amount assessed plus interest from the date of the expiration of the 90-day period provided by Sec. 3165.4(e) of this title. The Federal Oil and Gas Royalty Management Act requires that any judgment by the court shall include an order to pay. [52 FR 5394, Feb. 20, 1987; 52 FR 10225, Mar. 31, 1987]
