CFR /  Title 12  /  Part 1208  /  Sec. 1208.53 No requirement for duplicate notice.

Where FHFA has previously given a debtor any of the required notice and review opportunities with respect to a particular debt, FHFA is not required to duplicate such notice and review opportunities prior to initiating tax refund offset. Sec. Sec. 1208.54-1208.59 [Reserved]
