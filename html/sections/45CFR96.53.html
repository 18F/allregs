CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 96  /  Sec. 96.53 Length of withholding.

Under the low-income home energy assistance program and community services block grant, the Department may withhold funds until the Department finds that the reason for the withholding has been removed. [64 FR 55857, Oct. 15, 1999]
