CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 423  /  Sec. 423.2335 Beneficiary dispute resolution.

The Part D coverage determination and appeals process as described in Secs. 423.558 through 423.638 applies to beneficiary disputes involving the availability and amount of applicable discounts under the Discount Program.
