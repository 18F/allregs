CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 2400  /  Sec. 2400.56 Payment of stipend.

Payment for tuition, required fees, books, room, and board subject to the limitations in Sec. Sec. 2400.52 through 2400.55 and Sec. Sec. 2400.59 through 2400.60 will be paid via Electronic Funds Transfer to each Fellow at the beginning of each term of enrollment and upon the Fellow's submission of a completed Payment Request Form which includes the current University bulletin of cost information. [69 FR 11815, Mar. 12, 2004]
