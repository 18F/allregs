CFR /  Title 27  /  Part 28  /  Sec. 28.118 Receipt of spirits.

The receipt, gauge, and disposition of the distilled spirits at the distilled spirits plant shall be in accordance with the applicable provisions of subpart Q of part 19 of this chapter.
