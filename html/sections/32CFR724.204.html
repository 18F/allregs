CFR /  Title 32  /  Part 724  /  Sec. 724.204 Eligibility for naval discharge review.

Any former member of the Naval Service, eligible for review under reference (a) or surviving spouse, next of kin or legal representative, shall upon submission of an application be afforded a review of the member's discharge from the Naval Service as provided in Sec. Sec. 724.205 and 724.206. Discharge review may also be initiated on the motion of the NDRB (See Sec. 724.220).
