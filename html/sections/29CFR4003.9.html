CFR /  Title 29  /  Part 4003  /  Sec. 4003.9 Method and date of filing.

(a) Method of filing. The PBGC applies the rules in subpart A of part 4000 of this chapter to determine permissible methods of filing with the PBGC under this part.

(b) Date of filing. The PBGC applies the rules in subpart C of part 4000 of this chapter to determine the date that a submission under this part was filed with the PBGC. [68 FR 61352, Oct. 28, 2003]
