CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 2427  /  2427.470 Contract clause.

The contracting officer shall insert the clause 2452.227-70, Government Information, in all solicitations and contracts when the Government will provide information to the contractor, and/or when the contractor will obtain information on the Government's behalf to perform work required under the contract. The contracting officer shall describe all information to be provided to the contractor in paragraph (d)(1) of the clause. [77 FR 73530, Dec. 10, 2012]
