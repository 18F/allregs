CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 42  /  Sec. 42.05-63 Ship(s) and vessel(s).

The terms ship(s) and vessel(s) are interchangeable or synonymous words, and include every description of watercraft, other than a seaplane on the water, used or capable of being used as a means of transportation on water. [CGFR 68-126, 34 FR 9011, June 5, 1969]
