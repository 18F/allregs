CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 619  /  619.602-1 Referral.

The contracting officer shall transmit to the OSDBU Operations Director concurrently with the submission to the appropriate SBA Regional Office, a copy of the documentation supporting the determination that a small business concern is not responsible, as required by FAR 19.602-1(a). [53 FR 26170, July 11, 1988, as amended at 80 FR 6920, Feb. 9, 2015]
