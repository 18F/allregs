CFR /  Title 27  /  Part 9  /  Sec. 9.0 Scope.

The regulations in this part relate to American viticultural areas created under the authority of the Federal Alcohol Administration Act and referred to in Sec. 4.25(e) of this chapter. [T.D. TTB-90, 76 FR 3500, Jan. 20, 2011]
