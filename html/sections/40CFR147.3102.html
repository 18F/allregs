CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 147  /  Sec. 147.3102 Plugging and abandonment plans.

In lieu of the requirements of Sec. 144.28(c)(1) and (2) (i)-(iii) of this chapter, owners and operators of Class II wells shall comply with the plugging and abandonment provisions of Sec. 147.3108 of this subpart.
