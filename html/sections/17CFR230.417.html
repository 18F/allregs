CFR /  Title 17  /  Part 230  /  Sec. 230.417 Date of financial statements.

Whenever financial statements of any person are required to be furnished as of a date within a specified period prior to the date of filing the registration statement and the last day of such period falls on a Saturday, Sunday, or holiday, such registration statement may be filed on the first business day following the last day of the specified period. [22 FR 2328, Apr. 9, 1957]
