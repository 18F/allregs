CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 304-3  /  Sec. 304-3.4 What payments may my agency or I accept from a non-Federal

You or your agency may accept payments other than cash from a non-Federal source for all of your official travel expenses to attend a meeting of mutual interest, or any portion of those travel expenses mutually agreed upon between your agency and the non-Federal source. You may not accept payments for travel that is not to attend a meeting under this part. However, you may be able to accept payments under other authorities (see Sec. 304-3.19).
