CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 150  /  Sec. 150.21 Transportation of special nuclear material by aircraft.

Except as specifically approved by the Commission no shipment of special nuclear material in excess of 20 grams or 20 curies whichever is less of plutonium or uranium-233 shall be made by a licensee of an Agreement State in passenger aircraft. [38 FR 3039, Feb. 1, 1973]
