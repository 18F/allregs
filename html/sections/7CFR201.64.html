CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 201  /  Sec. 201.64 Pure live seed.

The tolerance for pure live seed shall be determined by applying the respective tolerances to the germination plus the hard seed and the pure seed. [5 FR 35, Jan. 4, 1940. Redesignated at 20 FR 7940, Oct. 21, 1955]
