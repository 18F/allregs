CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 237  /  237.171-2 Definition.

Combatant commander, detainee, and personnel interacting with detainees, as used in this section, are defined in the clause at 252.237-7019, Training for Contractor Personnel Interacting with Detainees. [71 FR 53048, Sept. 8, 2006]
