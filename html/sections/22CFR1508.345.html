CFR /  Title 22  /  Part 1508  /  Sec. 1508.345 What happens if I fail to disclose information required

If we later determine that you failed to disclose information under Sec. 1508.335 that you knew at the time you entered into the covered transaction, we may--

(a) Terminate the transaction for material failure to comply with the terms and conditions of the transaction; or

(b) Pursue any other available remedies, including suspension and debarment.
