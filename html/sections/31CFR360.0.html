CFR /  Title 31  /  Part 360  /  Sec. 360.0 Applicability.

(a) The regulations in this part apply to definitive (paper) Series I savings bonds that have not been converted to book-entry bonds through TreasuryDirect[supreg].

(b) The regulations in 31 CFR part 363 apply to:

(1) book-entry Series I savings bonds that were originally issued as book-entry bonds in TreasuryDirect[supreg]; and

(2) definitive Series I savings bonds that have been converted to book-entry bonds through TreasuryDirect[supreg]. [70 FR 14942, Mar. 23, 2005]
