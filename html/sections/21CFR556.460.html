CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 556  /  Sec. 556.460 Novobiocin.

Tolerances for residues of novobiocin are established at 0.1 part per million in milk from dairy animals and 1 part per million in the uncooked edible tissues of cattle, chickens, turkeys, and ducks. [47 FR 18590, Apr. 30, 1982]
