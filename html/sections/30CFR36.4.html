CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 36  /  Sec. 36.4 Mobile diesel-powered transportation equipment for which

Certificates of approval will be granted for completely assembled mobile diesel-powered transportation equipment only. Subassemblies or components may be granted letters of certification in accordance with Sec. 36.5.
