CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 102-33  /  Sec. 102-33.10 May we request approval to deviate from these rules?

(a) You may request approval to deviate from the rules in this part. See Sec. Sec. 102-2.60 through 102-2.110 of this chapter for guidance on requesting a deviation. In most cases, GSA will respond to your written request within 30 days;

(b) GSA may not grant deviations from the requirements of OMB Circular A-126, ``Improving the Management of Government Aircraft;'' and

(c) You should consult with GSA's Aviation Policy Division before you request a deviation.
