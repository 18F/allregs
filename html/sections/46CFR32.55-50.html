CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 32  /  Sec. 32.55-50 Ventilation of tankships that have a keel laying date

Each tankship that has a keel laying date on or after January 1, 1975, must have deckhouse and superstructure ventilation inlets and outlets and other openings to the exterior arranged to minimize the admission of flammable gas to enclosed spaces that contain a source of ignition. [CGD 74-127, 41 FR 3844, Jan. 26, 1976]
