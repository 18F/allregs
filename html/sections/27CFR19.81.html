CFR /  Title 27  /  Part 19  /  Sec. 19.81 Maintenance of registration file.

The proprietor must maintain the registration documents on the plant premises in a loose-leaf file that is current, complete, and readily available for inspection by the appropriate TTB officer. (26 U.S.C. 5172)
