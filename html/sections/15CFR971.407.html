CFR /  Title 15  /  Part 971  /  Sec. 971.407 Safety at sea.

Before issuing or transferring a commercial recovery permit, the Administrator must find that the commercial recovery proposed in the application will not pose an inordiante threat to the safety of life and property at sea. This finding will be based on the requirements in Sec. 971.205 and subpart G.
