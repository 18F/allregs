CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 384  /  Sec. 384.403 Availability of funds withheld for noncompliance.

Federal-aid highway funds withheld from a State under Sec. 384.401(a) or (b) of this subpart shall not thereafter be available for apportionment to the State. [80 FR 59073, Oct. 1, 2015]
