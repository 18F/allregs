CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 15  /  Sec. 15.65 Referral of a compromise offer.

The NRC may refer a debtor's firm written offer of compromise, which is substantial in amount, to the Civil Division or other appropriate litigating division in DOJ using a CCLR accompanied by supporting data and particulars concerning the debt. [67 FR 30324, May 6, 2002]
