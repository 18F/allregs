CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 411  /  Sec. 411.380 What does a State VR agency do if the State VR agency

A State VR agency can contact the Program Manager (PM) to determine if a person seeking VR services has a ticket and, if so, whether the ticket may be assigned to the State VR agency (see Sec. 411.140) or reassigned to the State VR agency (see Sec. 411.150). (See Sec. 411.115(k) for a definition of the PM.)
