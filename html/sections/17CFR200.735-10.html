CFR /  Title 17  /  Part 200  /  Sec. 200.735-10 Miscellaneous statutory provisions.

Each member and employee is responsible for acquainting himself or herself with the statutory provisions listed in 5 CFR 2635.902 (Related statutes). A violation of any of these provisions is deemed a violation of this subpart M. [75 FR 42277, July 20, 2010]
