CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 723  /  Sec. 723.12 When penalty will be assessed.

(a) The Office shall assess a penalty for each cessation order.

(b) The Office shall assess a penalty for each notice of violation, if the violation is assigned 31 points or more under the point system described in Sec. 723.13.

(c) The Office may assess a penalty for each notice of violation assigned 30 points or less under the point system described in Sec. 723.13. In determining whether to assess a penalty, the Office shall consider the factors listed in Sec. 723.13(b).
