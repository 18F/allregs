CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 483  /  Sec. 483.100 Basis.

The requirements of Sec. Sec. 483.100 through 483.138 governing the State's responsibility for preadmission screening and annual resident review (PASARR) of individuals with mental illness and intellectual disability are based on section 1919(e)(7) of the Act.
