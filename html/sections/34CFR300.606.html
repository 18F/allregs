CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 300  /  Sec. 300.606 Public attention.

Whenever a State receives notice that the Secretary is proposing to take or is taking an enforcement action pursuant to Sec. 300.604, the State must, by means of a public notice, take such actions as may be necessary to notify the public within the State of the pendency of an action pursuant to Sec. 300.604, including, at a minimum, by posting the notice on the SEA's Web site and distributing the notice to the media and through public agencies. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1416(e)(7)) [73 FR 73028, Dec. 1, 2008]
