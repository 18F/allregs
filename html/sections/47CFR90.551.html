CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 90  /  Sec. 90.551 Construction requirements.

Each station authorized under this subpart to operate in the 769-775 MHz and 799-805 MHz frequency bands must be constructed and placed into operation within 12 months from the date of grant of the authorization, except for State channels. However, licensees may request a longer construction period, up to but not exceeding 5 years, pursuant to Sec. 90.155(b). State channels are subject to the build-out requirements in Sec. 90.529. [72 FR 48863, Aug. 24, 2007]
