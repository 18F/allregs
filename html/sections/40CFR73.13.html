CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 73  /  Sec. 73.13 Procedures for submittals.

(a) Address for submittal. All submittals under this subpart shall be made by the designated representative to the Director, Acid Rain Division, (6204J), 1200 Pennsylvania Ave., NW., Washington, DC 20460 and shall meet the requirements specified in 40 CFR 72.21.

(b) Appeals procedures. The designated representative may appeal the decision as to eligibility or allocation of allowances under Sec. Sec. 73.18, 73.19, and 73.20, using the appeals procedures of part 78 of this chapter. [58 FR 15708, Mar. 23, 1993 as amended at 63 FR 51765, Sept. 28, 1998] Sec. Sec. 73.14-73.17 [Reserved]
