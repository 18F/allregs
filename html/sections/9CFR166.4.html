CFR /  Title 9  /  Part 166  /  Sec. 166.4 Storage of garbage.

(a) Untreated garbage at a treatment facility shall be stored in covered and leakproof containers until treated.

(b) Treated garbage shall be transported to a feeding area from the treatment facility only in (1) containers used only for such treated garbage; (2) containers previously used for garbage which have been cleaned and disinfected in accordance with Sec. 166.14 of this part; or (3) containers in which the garbage was treated. [47 FR 49945, Nov. 3, 1982, as amended at 52 FR 4890, Feb. 18, 1987]
