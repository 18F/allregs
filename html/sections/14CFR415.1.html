CFR /  Title 14  /  Part 415  /  Sec. 415.1 Scope.

This part prescribes requirements for obtaining a license to launch a launch vehicle, other than a reusable launch vehicle (RLV), and post-licensing requirements with which a licensee must comply to remain licensed. Requirements for preparing a license application are in part 413 of this subchapter. [Doc. No. FAA-2006-24197, 72 FR 17019, Apr. 6, 2007]
