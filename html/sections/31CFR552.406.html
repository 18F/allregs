CFR /  Title 31  /  Part 552  /  Sec. 552.406 Entities owned by a person whose property and interests

A person whose property and interests in property are blocked pursuant to Sec. 552.201 has an interest in all property and interests in property of an entity in which it owns, directly or indirectly, a 50 percent or greater interest. The property and interests in property of such an entity, therefore, are blocked, and such an entity is a person whose property and interests in property are blocked pursuant to Sec. 552.201, regardless of whether the entity itself is designated pursuant to Executive Order 13611.
