CFR /  Title 29  /  Part 4206  /  Sec. 4206.9 Amount of credit in plans using alternative allocation

A plan that has adopted an alternative method of allocating unfunded vested benefits pursuant to section 4211(c)(5) of ERISA and part 4211 of this chapter shall adopt, by plan amendment, a method of calculating the credit provided by Sec. 4206.3 that is consistent with the rules in Secs. 4206.4 through 4206.8 for plans using the statutory allocation method most similar to the plan's alternative allocation method.
