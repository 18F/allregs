CFR /  Title 14  /  Part 119  /  Sec. 119.8 Safety Management Systems.

(a) Certificate holders authorized to conduct operations under part 121 of this chapter must have a safety management system that meets the requirements of part 5 of this chapter and is acceptable to the Administrator by March 9, 2018.

(b) A person applying to the Administrator for an air carrier certificate or operating certificate to conduct operations under part 121 of this chapter after March 9, 2015, must demonstrate, as part of the application process under Sec. 119.35, that it has an SMS that meets the standards set forth in part 5 of this chapter and is acceptable to the Administrator. [Doc. No. FAA-2009-0671, 80 FR 1328, Jan. 8, 2015]
