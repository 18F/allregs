CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 18  /  Sec. 18.461 Procedures.

The procedural provisions applicable to Title VI of the Civil Rights Act of 1964 apply to this part. These procedures are found in Sec. Sec. 18.6 through 18.11 and part 18b of this chapter.
