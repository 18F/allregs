CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 952  /  952.216-7 Allowable cost and payment.

As prescribed in 916.307(a), when contracting with a commercial organization modify paragraph (a) of the clause at 48 CFR 52.216-7 by adding the phrase ``as supplemented by subpart 931.2 of the Department of Energy Acquisition Regulations (DEAR),'' after 48 CFR subpart 31.2. [74 FR 36369, July 22, 2009, as amended at 76 FR 7694, Feb. 11, 2011]
