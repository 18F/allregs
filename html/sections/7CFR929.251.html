CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 929  /  Sec. 929.251 Marketable quantity and allotment percentage for the

The marketable quantity for the 2001-2002 crop year is set at 4.6 million barrels and the allotment percentage is designated at 65 percent. Fresh and organically grown fruit shall be exempt from the volume regulation provisions of this section. [66 FR 34352, June 27, 2001]
