CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 2003  /  Sec. 2003.4 Officials to receive requests and inquiries.

Officials to receive requests and inquiries for access to, or correction of, records in OIG systems of records are the Privacy Act Officers described in Sec. 2003.2 of this part. Written requests may be addressed to the appropriate Privacy Act Officer at: Office of Inspector General, Department of Housing and Urban Development, Washington, DC 20410. [57 FR 62142, Dec. 29, 1992, as amended at 59 FR 14098, Mar. 25, 1994]
