CFR /  Title 12  /  Part 308  /  Sec. 308.147 Investigations confidential.

Investigations shall be confidential. Information and documents obtained by the FDIC in the course of such investigations shall not be disclosed, except as provided in part 309 of this chapter and as otherwise required by law. [80 FR 5013, Jan. 30, 2015]
