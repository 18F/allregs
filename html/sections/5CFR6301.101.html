CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 6301  /  Sec. 6301.101 General.

In accordance with 5 CFR 2635.105, the regulations in this part apply to employees of the Department of Education and supplement the Standards of Ethical Conduct for Employees of the Executive Branch contained in 5 CFR part 2635. [60 FR 5817, Jan. 30, 1995]
