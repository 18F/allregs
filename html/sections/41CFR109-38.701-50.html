CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 109-38  /  Sec. 109-38.701-50 Authority to sign Standard Form 97, The United States

The Standard Form (SF) 97 shall be signed by an appropriate contracting officer. The Director, Office of Administrative Services and heads of field organizations for their respective organizations may delegate the authority to sign SF 97 to responsible DOE personnel under their jurisdiction.
