CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 64  /  Sec. 64.707 Public dissemination of information by providers of

Providers of operator services shall regularly publish and make available at no cost to inquiring consumers written materials that describe any recent changes in operator services and in the choices available to consumers in that market. [56 FR 18524, Apr. 23, 1991]
