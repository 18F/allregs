CFR /  Title 29  /  Part 779  /  Sec. 779.317 Partial list of establishments lacking ``retail concept.''

There are types of establishments in industries where it is not readily apparent whether a retail concept exists and whether or not the exemption can apply. It, therefore, is not possible to give a complete list of the types of establishments that have no retail concept. It is possible, however, to give a partial list of establishments to which the retail concept does not apply.
