CFR /  Title 25  /  Part 1000  /  Sec. 1000.335 How will retrocession affect the Tribe's/Consortium's

Retrocession does not affect other parts of the AFA or funding agreements with other bureaus. A Tribe/Consortium may request to negotiate for and include retroceded programs in future AFAs or through a self-determination contract.
