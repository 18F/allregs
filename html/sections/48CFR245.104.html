CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 245  /  245.104 Responsibility and liability for Government property.

In addition to the contract types listed at FAR 45.104, contractors are not held liable for loss of Government property under negotiated fixed-price contracts awarded on a basis other than submission of certified cost or pricing data. [76 FR 71826, Nov. 18, 2011]
