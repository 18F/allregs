CFR /  Title 12  /  Part 1238  /  Sec. 1238.5 Required report to FHFA and the FRB of stress test results

(a) Report required for stress tests. On or before February 5 of each year, the Enterprises must report the results of the stress tests required under Sec. 1238.3 to FHFA, and to the FRB, in accordance with paragraph (b) of this section; and on or before April 30 of each year, the Banks must report the results of the stress tests required under Sec. 1238.3 to FHFA, and to the FRB, in accordance with paragraph (b) of this section;

(b) Content of report for annual stress test. Each regulated entity must file a report in the manner and form established by FHFA.

(c) Confidential treatment of information submitted. Reports submitted to FHFA under this part are FHFA property and records (as defined in 12 CFR part 1202 of this chapter). The reports are and include non-public information contained in or related to examination, operating, or condition reports prepared by, on behalf of, or for the use of, FHFA in connection with the performance of the agency's responsibilities regulating or supervising its regulated entities. Disclosure of any reports submitted to FHFA or the information contained in any such report is prohibited unless authorized by this part, legal obligation, or otherwise by the Director of FHFA.
