CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 67  /  Sec. 67.01-10 Delegation of functions.

The Coast Guard District Commander may delegate the authority for performing inspections, enforcement, and administration of regulations to any civilian or military position in the Coast Guard. [USCG-2001-10714, 69 FR 24983, May 5, 2004]
