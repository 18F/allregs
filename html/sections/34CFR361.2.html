CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 361  /  Sec. 361.2 Eligibility for a grant.

Any State that submits to the Secretary a State plan that meets the requirements of section 101(a) of the Act and this part is eligible for a grant under this Program. (Authority: Section 101(a) of the Act; 29 U.S.C. 721(a))
