CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 570  /  Sec. 570.800 Urban renewal regulations.

The regulations governing urban renewal projects and neighborhood development programs in subpart N of this part, that were effective immediately before April 19, 1996, will continue to govern the rights and obligations of recipients and HUD with respect to such projects and programs. [61 FR 11481, Mar. 20, 1996]
