CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 59  /  Sec. 59.207 Test methods.

Each manufacturer or importer subject to the provisions of Sec. 59.203(a) shall demonstrate compliance with the requirements of this subpart through calculation of the VOC content using records of the amounts of constituents used to manufacture the product.
