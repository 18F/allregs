CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 200  /  Sec. 200.34 Identification for restructuring.

(a) If a school continues to fail to make AYP after one full school year of corrective action under Sec. 200.42, the LEA must prepare a restructuring plan for the school and make arrangements to implement the plan.

(b) If the school continues to fail to make AYP, the LEA must implement the restructuring plan no later than the beginning of the school year following the year in which the LEA developed the restructuring plan under paragraph (a) of this section. (Approved by the Office of Management and Budget under control number 1810-0576) (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 6316(b)(8)) [67 FR 71721, Dec. 2, 2002]
