CFR /  Title 14  /  Part 217  /  Sec. 217.7 Certification.

The certification for BTS Form 41 Schedule T-100(f) shall be signed by an officer of the air carrier with the requisite authority over the collection of data and preparation of reports to ensure the validity and accuracy of the reported data. [53 FR 46294, Nov. 16, 1988, as amended at 60 FR 66722, Dec. 26, 1995]
