CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 1210  /  Sec. 1210.362 Influencing government action.

No funds received by the Board under this Plan shall in any manner be used for the purpose of influencing governmental policy or action, except for making recommendations to the Secretary as provided in this subpart. [54 FR 24545, June 8, 1989, as amended at 60 FR 10799, Feb. 28, 1995]
