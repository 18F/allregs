CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 91  /  Sec. 91.405 Housing and homeless needs assessment.

Housing and homeless needs must be described in the consolidated plan in accordance with the provisions of Sec. 91.205 for the entire consortium. In addition to describing these needs for the entire consortium, the consolidated plan may also describe these needs for individual communities that are members of the consortium. (Approved by the Office of Management and Budget under control number 2506-0117)
