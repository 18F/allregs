CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 75  /  Sec. 75.681 Protection of human research subjects.

If a grantee uses a human subject in a research project, the grantee shall protect the person from physical, psychological, or social injury resulting from the project. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1221e-3 and 3474)

Cross Reference: See 34 CFR part 97--Protection of Human Subjects.
