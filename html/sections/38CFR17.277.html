CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 17  /  Sec. 17.277 Third-party liability/medical care cost recovery.

The Center will actively pursue third-party liability/medical care cost recovery in accordance with applicable law. (Authority: 42 U.S.C. 2651; 38 U.S.C. 501, 1781) [63 FR 48102, Sept. 9, 1998, as amended at 79 FR 54616, Sept. 12, 2014]
