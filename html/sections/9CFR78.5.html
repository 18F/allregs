CFR /  Title 9  /  Part 78  /  Sec. 78.5 General restrictions.

Cattle may not be moved interstate except in compliance with this subpart and with 9 CFR part 86. Cattle moved interstate under permit in accordance with this subpart are not required to be accompanied by an interstate certificate of veterinary inspection or owner-shipper statement. [78 FR 2069, Jan. 9, 2013]
