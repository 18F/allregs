CFR /  Title 23  /  Part 636  /  Sec. 636.201 What selection procedures and award criteria may be used?

You should consider using two-phase selection procedures for all design-build projects. However, if you do not believe two-phase selection procedures are appropriate for your project (based on the criteria in Sec. 636.202), you may use a single phase selection procedure or the modified-design-build contracting method.
