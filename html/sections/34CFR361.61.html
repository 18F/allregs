CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 361  /  Sec. 361.61 Limitation on use of funds for construction expenditures.

No more than 10 percent of a State's allotment for any fiscal year under section 110 of the Act may be spent on the construction of facilities for community rehabilitation program purposes. (Authority: Section 101(a)(17)(A) of the Act; 29 U.S.C. 721(a)(17)(A))
