CFR /  Title 25  /  Part 224  /  Sec. 224.73 How will the scope of energy resource development affect

The Secretary's review under Sec. 224.72 of the tribe's capacity to manage and regulate energy resource development under the TERA will include a determination as to each type of energy resource development subject to the TERA for which the tribe seeks to regulate, and each type of regulatory activity the tribe proposes to assume. The Secretary's review of a TERA must be limited to activities specified by its provisions.
