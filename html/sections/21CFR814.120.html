CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 814  /  Sec. 814.120 Temporary suspension of approval of an HDE.

An HDE or HDE supplement may be temporarily suspended for the same reasons and in the same manner as prescribed for PMA's in Sec. 814.47. [63 FR 59221, Nov. 3, 1998]
