CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 431  /  Sec. 431.808 Protection of beneficiary rights.

Any individual performing activities under the MEQC program or the claims processing assessment system specified in this subpart must do so in a manner that is consistent with the provisions of Sec. Sec. 435.902 and 436.901 of this subchapter concerning the rights of beneficiaries.

Medicaid Eligibility Quality Control (MEQC) Program

Source: Sections 431.810 through 431.822 appear at 55 FR 22167, May 31, 1990, unless otherwise noted.
