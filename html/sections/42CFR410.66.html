CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 410  /  Sec. 410.66 Emergency outpatient services furnished by a

Conditions for payment of emergency inpatient services furnished by a nonparticipating U.S. hospital and for services furnished in a foreign country are set forth in subparts G and H of part 424 of this chapter. [71 FR 48136, Aug. 18, 2006]
