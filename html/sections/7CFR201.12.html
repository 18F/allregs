CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 201  /  Sec. 201.12 Name of kind and variety.

The representation of kind or kind and variety shall be confined to the name of the kind or kind and variety determined in accordance with Sec. 201.34. The name shall not have affixed thereto words or terms that create a misleading impression as to the history or characteristics of the kind or variety. [20 FR 7929, Oct. 21, 1955] Sec. 201.12a Lawn and turf seed mixtures.

Seed mixtures intended for lawn and turf purposes shall be designated as a mixture on the label and each seed component shall be listed on the label in the order of predominance. [49 FR 1172, Jan. 10, 1984]
