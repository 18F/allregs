CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 1229  /  Sec. 1229.10 Information collection requirements.

The information collection requirements contained in this part do not require approval by the Office of Management and Budget under 44 U.S.C. 3501 et seq., because there are fewer than 10 respondents annually. Subpart B--Oil and Gas, General [Reserved]
