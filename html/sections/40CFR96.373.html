CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 96  /  Sec. 96.373 Notifications.

The CAIR designated representative for a CAIR NOX Ozone Season unit shall submit written notice to the permitting authority and the Administrator in accordance with Sec. 75.61 of this chapter. [70 FR 25382, May 12, 2005, as amended at 71 FR 25395, Apr. 28, 2006]
