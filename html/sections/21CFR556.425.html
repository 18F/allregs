CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 556  /  Sec. 556.425 Morantel tartrate.

A tolerance of 0.7 part per million is established for N-methyl-1,3-propanediamine (MAPA, marker residue) in the liver (target tissue) of cattle and goats. A tolerance for residues of morantel tartrate in milk is not required. [59 FR 17922, Apr. 15, 1994]
