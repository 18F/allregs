CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 352  /  352.231-70 Precontract costs.

As prescribed in 48 CFR 1331.205-32, insert the following clause:

Precontract Costs (APR 2010)

The contractor is entitled to reimbursement for allowable, allocable, and reasonable costs incurred during the period of -------- to the award date of this contract in an amount not to exceed $--------.

(End of clause) [75 FR 10570, Mar. 8, 2010; 75 FR 14496, Mar. 26, 2010]
