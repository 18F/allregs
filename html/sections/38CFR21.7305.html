CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 21  /  Sec. 21.7305 Conflicting interests.

In administering benefits payable under 38 U.S.C. chapter 30, VA will apply the provisions of Sec. 21.4005. (Authority: 38 U.S.C. 3034, 3036) [62 FR 55761, Oct. 28, 1997]
