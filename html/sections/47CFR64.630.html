CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 64  /  Sec. 64.630 Applicability of change of default TRS provider rules.

Sections 64.630 through 64.636 of this part governing changes in default TRS providers shall apply to any provider of IP Relay or VRS eligible to receive payments from the TRS Fund. [78 FR 40609, July 5, 2013]
