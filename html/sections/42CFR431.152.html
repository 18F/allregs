CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 431  /  Sec. 431.152 State plan requirements.

The State plan must provide for appeals procedures that, as a minimum, satisfy the requirements of Sec. Sec. 431.153 and 431.154. [59 FR 56232, Nov. 10, 1994, as amended at 61 FR 32348, June 24, 1996]
