CFR /  Title 27  /  Part 22  /  Sec. 22.66 Registry of stills.

The provisions of subpart C of part 29 of this chapter are applicable to stills on the premises of a permittee used for distilling. As provided in Sec. 29.55, the listing of a still in the permit application (Form 5150.22), and approval of the application, constitutes registration of the still. (Sec. 201, Pub. L. 85-859, 72 Stat. 1355, as amended (26 U.S.C. 5179)) [T.D. ATF-207, 50 FR 23682, June 5, 1985]
