CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 1  /  Sec. 1.202 Official reporter; transcript.

The Commission will designate from time to time an official reporter for the recording and transcribing of hearing proceedings. The transcript of the testimony taken, or argument had, at any hearing will not be furnished by the Commission, but will be open to inspection under Sec. 0.453(a)(1) of this chapter. Copies of such transcript, if desired, may be obtained from the official reporter upon payment of the charges therefor. (5 U.S.C. 556) [32 FR 20861, Dec. 28, 1967]
