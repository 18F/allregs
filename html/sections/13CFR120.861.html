CFR /  Title 13  /  Part 120  /  Sec. 120.861 Job creation or retention.

A Project must create or retain one Job Opportunity per an amount of 504 loan funding that will be specified by SBA from time to time in a Federal Register notice. Such Job Opportunity average remains in effect until changed by subsequent Federal Register publication. [68 FR 57987, Oct. 7, 2003]
