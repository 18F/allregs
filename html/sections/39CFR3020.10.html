CFR /  Title 39  /  Part 3020  /  Sec. 3020.10 General.

The Mail Classification Schedule shall consist of two parts. Part One shall specify the list of market dominant products and include the explanatory information specified in Sec. 3020.13(a). Part Two shall specify the list of competitive products and include the explanatory information specified in Sec. 3020.13(b).
