CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 95  /  Sec. 95.643 DSRCS-OBU certification.

Sections 95.645 through 95.655 do not apply to certification of DSRCS-OBUs. DSRCS-OBUs must be certified in accordance with subpart L of this part and subpart J of part 2 of this chapter. [69 FR 46446, Aug. 3, 2004]
