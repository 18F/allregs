CFR /  Title 14  /  Part 91  /  Sec. 91.507 Equipment requirements: Over-the-top or night VFR operations.

No person may operate an airplane over-the-top or at night under VFR unless that airplane is equipped with the instruments and equipment required for IFR operations under Sec. 91.205(d) and one electric landing light for night operations. Each required instrument and item of equipment must be in operable condition.
