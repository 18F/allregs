CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 490  /  Sec. 490.603 Prohibited acts.

It is unlawful for any person to violate any provision of sections 501, 503(b), 507, 514 of the Act, or any regulations issued under such sections. [72 FR 12964, Mar. 20, 2007]
