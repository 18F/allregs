CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 200  /  Sec. 200.640 Effect on other requirements.

The requirement for compliance with this part is in addition to, and not in substitution for, any other requirements imposed by or under Executive Order 11063 or the Fair Housing Act. [58 FR 41337, Aug. 3, 1993]
