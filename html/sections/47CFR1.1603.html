CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 1  /  Sec. 1.1603 Conduct of random selection.

The random selection probabilities will be calculated in accordance with the formula set out in rules Secs. 1.1621 through 1.1623. [48 FR 27202, June 13, 1983, as amended at 48 FR 43330, Sept. 23, 1983]
