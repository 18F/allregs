CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 60  /  Sec. 60.82 Standard for sulfur dioxide.

(a) On and after the date on which the performance test required to be conducted by Sec. 60.8 is completed, no owner or operator subject to the provisions of this subpart shall cause to be discharged into the atmosphere from any affected facility any gases which contain sulfur dioxide in excess of 2 kg per metric ton of acid produced (4 lb per ton), the production being expressed as 100 percent H2SO4. [39 FR 20794, June 14, 1974]
