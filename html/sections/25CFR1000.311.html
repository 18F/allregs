CFR /  Title 25  /  Part 1000  /  Sec. 1000.311 How will the Secretary reply to the Tribe's/Consortium's

The Secretary will make a written determination within 10 days of the Tribe's/Consortium's written response as to whether the proposed measures will eliminate the finding of imminent jeopardy.
