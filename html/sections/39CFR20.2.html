CFR /  Title 39  /  Part 20  /  Sec. 20.2 Effective date of the International Mail Manual.

The provisions of the International Mail Manual issued January 26, 2015, are applicable with respect to the international mail services of the Postal Service. [80 FR 13493, Mar. 16, 2015]
