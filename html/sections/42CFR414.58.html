CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 414  /  Sec. 414.58 Payment of charges for physician services to patients

(a) Payment under the physician fee schedule. In addition to the special conditions for payment in Secs. 415.100 through 415.130, and Sec. 415.190 of this chapter, CMS establishes payment for physician services to patients in providers under the physician fee schedule in accordance with Secs. 414.1 through 414.48.

(b) Teaching hospitals. Services furnished by physicians in teaching hospitals may be made on a reasonable cost basis set forth in Sec. 415.162 of this chapter if the hospital exercises the election described in Sec. 415.160 of this chapter. [56 FR 59624, Nov. 25, 1991, as amended at 57 FR 42492, Sept. 15, 1992; 60 FR 63189, Dec. 8, 1995]
