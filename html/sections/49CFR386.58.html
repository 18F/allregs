CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 386  /  Sec. 386.58 Burden of proof.

(a) Enforcement cases. The burden of proof shall be on the Administration in enforcement cases.

(b) Conflict of medical opinion. The burden of proof in cases arising under Sec. 391.47 of this chapter shall be on the party petitioning for review under Sec. 386.13(a).
