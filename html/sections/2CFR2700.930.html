CFR /  Title 2  /  Part 2700  /  Sec. 2700.930 Debarring official (SBA supplement to government-wide

For SBA, the debarring official for financial assistance programs is the Director of the Office of Credit Risk Management; for all other programs, the debarring official is the Associate General Counsel for Procurement Law. [72 FR 39728, July 20, 2007, as amended at 73 FR 43348, July 25, 2008]
