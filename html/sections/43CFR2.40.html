CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 2  /  Sec. 2.40 How will fee amounts be determined?

(a) The bureau will charge the types of fees discussed below unless a waiver of fees is required under Sec. 2.39 of this subpart or has been granted under Sec. 2.45 or Sec. 2.56.

(b) Because the types of fees discussed below already account for the overhead costs associated with a given fee type, the bureau should not add any additional costs to those charges.
