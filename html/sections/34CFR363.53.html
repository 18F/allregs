CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 363  /  Sec. 363.53 What special conditions apply to services and activities

Each grantee shall coordinate the services provided to an individual under this part and under 34 CFR part 361 to ensure that the services are complementary and not duplicative. (Authority: 29 U.S.C. 711(c) and 795p)
