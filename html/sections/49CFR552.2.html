CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 552  /  Sec. 552.2 Purpose.

The purpose of this part is to enable the National Highway Traffic Safety Administration to identify and respond on a timely basis to petitions for rulemaking or defect or noncompliance decisions, and to inform the public of the procedures following in response to such petitions. [60 FR 17267, Apr. 5, 1995]
