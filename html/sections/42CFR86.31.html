CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 86  /  Sec. 86.31 Eligibility; minimum requirements.

In order to be eligible for an award under this subpart an applicant must:

(a) Have been accepted by a public or private institution for the purpose of the activity for which the traineeship is sought.

(b) Be a U.S. citizen, an alien lawfully admitted to the United States for permanent residence or a permanent resident of Guam, American Samoa, or the Trust Territory of the Pacific Islands. [40 FR 29076, July 10, 1975, as amended at 47 FR 53012, Nov. 24, 1982]
