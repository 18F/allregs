CFR /  Title 17  /  Part 269  /  Sec. 269.4 Form T-4, for application for exemption pursuant to

This form shall be filed pursuant to Rule 4c-1 (Sec. 260.4c-1 of this chapter) for applications for exemption filed pursuant to section 304(c) of the Trust Indenture Act of 1939.

Editorial Note: For Federal Register citations affecting Form T-4, see the List of CFR Sections Affected, which appears in the Finding Aids section of the printed volume and at www.fdsys.gov.
