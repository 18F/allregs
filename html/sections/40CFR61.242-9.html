CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 61  /  Sec. 61.242-9 Standards: Surge control vessels and bottoms receivers.

Each surge control vessel or bottoms receiver that is not routed back to the process and that meets the conditions specified in table 1 or table 2 of this subpart shall be equipped with a closed-vent system capable of capturing and transporting any leakage from the vessel back to the process or to a control device as described in Sec. 61.242-11, except as provided in Sec. 61.242-1(c); or comply with the requirements of 40 CFR 63.119(b) or (c). [65 FR 78282, Dec. 14, 2000]
