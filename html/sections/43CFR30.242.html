CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 30  /  Sec. 30.242 When does the judge's decision on a petition for

The decision on a petition for rehearing will become final on the expiration of the 30 days allowed for the filing of a notice of appeal, as provided in this part and Sec. 4.320 of this chapter. [73 FR 67289, Nov. 13, 2008. Redesignated at 76 FR 7507, Feb. 10, 2011]
