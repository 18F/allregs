CFR /  Title 50  /  Part 27  /  Sec. 27.43 Weapons other than firearms.

The use or possession of cross bows, bows and arrows, air guns, spears, gigs, or other weapons on national wildlife refuges is prohibited except as may be authorized under the provision of this subchapter C. [46 FR 47230, Sept. 25, 1981]
