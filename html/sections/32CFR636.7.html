CFR /  Title 32  /  Part 636  /  Sec. 636.7 Extensions of suspensions and revocations.

In addition to the requirements in Sec. 634.17(a) of this subchapter, for each subsequent violation of the suspension period, an additional five years will be added to the suspension period for this installation (see Table 634.46 in Sec. 634.46 of this subchapter).
