CFR /  Title 23  /  Part 1225  /  Sec. 1225.10 Apportionment of withheld funds after compliance.

If a State has not met the requirements of 23 U.S.C. 163 and Sec. 1225.4 of this part by October 1, 2007, the funds withheld under Sec. 1225.8 shall begin to lapse and will no longer be available for apportionment to the State, in accordance with appendix A of this part.
