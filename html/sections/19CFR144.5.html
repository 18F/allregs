CFR /  Title 19  /  Part 144  /  Sec. 144.5 Period of warehousing.

Merchandise must not remain in a bonded warehouse beyond 5 years from the date of importation or such longer period of time as the port director may at his discretion permit upon proper request being filed and good cause shown. [CBP Dec. 09-48, 74 FR 68686, Dec. 29, 2009] Sec. 144.6 [Reserved]
