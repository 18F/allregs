CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 148  /  Sec. 148.128 State flexibility in individual market reforms--

The rules for a State to implement an acceptable alternative mechanism for purposes of guaranteeing the availability of individual health insurance coverage to certain eligible individuals with prior group coverage have been superseded by the requirements of Sec. 147.104 of this subchapter, which set forth Federal requirements for guaranteed availability of coverage in the group and individual markets. [79 FR 30341, May 27, 2014]
