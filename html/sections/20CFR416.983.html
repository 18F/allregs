CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 416  /  Sec. 416.983 How we evaluate statutory blindness.

We will find that you are blind if you are statutorily blind within the meaning of Sec. 416.981. For us to find that you are statutorily blind, it is not necessary--

(a) That your blindness meet the duration requirement; or

(b) That you be unable to do any substantial gainful activity.
