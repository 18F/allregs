CFR /  Title 9  /  Part 95  /  Sec. 95.38 Regulations applicable to products from Territorial

The regulations in this part shall be applicable to all the products and materials specified in this part which are offered for entry into the United States from any place under the jurisdiction of the United States to which the animal-quarantine laws of this country do not apply. [28 FR 5981, June 13, 1963. Redesignated at 78 FR 73003, Dec. 4, 2013]
