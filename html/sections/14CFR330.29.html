CFR /  Title 14  /  Part 330  /  Sec. 330.29 What information must air taxi operators submit on

As an air taxi operator, you must complete Form 330 (Final) in accordance with the requirements in Sec. 330.27. You must also complete pages 2, 5, and 6 (certifying pages 2 and 5) of Form 330-C as shown in appendix C to this part. Explanatory notes are included on that Form.
