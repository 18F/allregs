CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 60  /  Sec. 60.36 Consequence of using an agent.

The delegation of functions to a servicing agency or other party does not relieve a lender or holder of its responsibilities under the HEAL program. [57 FR 28797, June 29, 1992]
