CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 922  /  Sec. 922.4 Production area.

Production area means the counties of Okanogan, Chelan, Kittitas, Yakima, and Klickitat in the State of Washington and all of the counties in Washington lying east thereof. [27 FR 5188, June 2, 1962]
