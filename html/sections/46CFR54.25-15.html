CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 54  /  Sec. 54.25-15 Low temperature operation--high alloy steels

(a)(2).

(b) Materials for pressure vessels with service temperatures below - 320 [deg]F. shall be of the stabilized or low carbon (less than 0.10 percent) austenitic stainless steel type, produced according to the applicable specifications of table UHA-23 of section VIII of the ASME Boiler and Pressure Vessel Code. These materials and their weldments shall be tested for toughness according to the requirements of subpart 54.05 except that the Charpy V-notch testing acceptance criteria will be in accordance with UHT-6(a)(4) and (5) of section VIII of the ASME Boiler and Pressure Vessel Code.''

(c) Except as permitted by Sec. 54.05-30, the allowable stress values used in the design of low temperature pressure vessels may not exceed those given in table UHA-23 of section VIII of the ASME Boiler and Pressure Vessel Code for temperatures of -20 [deg]F. to 100 [deg]F.
