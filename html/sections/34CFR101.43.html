CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 101  /  Sec. 101.43 Reduction of time to file documents.

For good cause, the reviewing authority or the presiding officer, with respect to matters pending before them, may reduce any time limit prescribed by the rules in this part, except as provided by law or in part 100 of this chapter. [45 FR 30931, May 9, 1980, as amended at 79 FR 76095, Dec. 19, 2014]
