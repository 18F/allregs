CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 241  /  Sec. 241.1 Purpose.

This rule gives general instructions on the implementation of section 103(m) of the Water Resources Development Act of 1986, Public Law 99-662, as amended by section 201 of the Water Resources Development Act of 1992, Public Law 102-588, for application to flood control projects. [60 FR 5133, Jan. 26, 1995]
