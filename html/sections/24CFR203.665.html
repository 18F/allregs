CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 203  /  Sec. 203.665 Processing defaulted mortgages on property located

Before a mortgagee requests the Secretary to accept assignment under Sec. 203.350(c) of a mortgage insured pursuant to section 247 of the National Housing Act (Sec. 203.43i), the mortgagee must submit documents showing that the requirements of Sec. 203.604 have been met.
