CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 946  /  Sec. 946.18 Container.

Container means a sack, box, bag, crate, hamper, basket, carton, package, barrel, or any other type of receptacle used in the packing, transportation, sale or other handling of potatoes. [70 FR 41133, July 18, 2005]
