CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 405  /  Sec. 405.331 Submitting evidence to an administrative law judge.

(a) When you submit your request for hearing, you should also submit information or evidence as required by Secs. 404.1512 or 416.912 of this chapter or any summary of the evidence to the administrative law judge. You must submit any written evidence no later than 5 business days before the date of the scheduled hearing. * * *
