CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 27  /  Sec. 27.10 Supervision of cotton inspection, weighing, sampling;

Authorized employees of the Cotton Division will act, when necessary, as supervisors of cotton inspection to supervise the inspection, weighing, and sampling of cotton to be classified and will perform such other duties as may be required of them for the purposes of this subpart. [26 FR 1656, Feb. 25, 1961]
