CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 90  /  Sec. 90.425 CVS calibration frequency.

Calibrate the CVS positive displacement pump or critical flow venturi following initial installation, major maintenance, or as necessary when indicated by the CVS system verification (described in Sec. 90.424(e)).
