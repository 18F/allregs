CFR /  Title 36  /  Part 1256  /  Sec. 1256.102 What fees does NARA charge?

Copies of audiovisual records will only be provided under this subpart upon payment of fees in accordance with 44 U.S.C. 2116(c) and 22 U.S.C. 1461(b)(3). See Sec. 1258.4(b) for additional information.
