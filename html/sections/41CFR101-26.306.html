CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 101-26  /  Sec. 101-26.306 Planned requisitioning for GSA stock items.

In preparing requisitions for GSA stock items, agencies shall follow schedules or cyclical plans for replenishment of stocks so as to reduce the number of repetitive requisitions required while adjusting ordering frequency to comply with the economic order quantity principle. (See Sec. 101-27.102.) [43 FR 22211, May 24, 1978]
