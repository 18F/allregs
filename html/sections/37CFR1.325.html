CFR /  Title 37  /  Part 1  /  Sec. 1.325 Other mistakes not corrected.

Mistakes other than those provided for in Sec. Sec. 1.322, 1.323, 1.324, and not affording legal grounds for reissue or for reexamination, will not be corrected after the date of the patent. (35 U.S.C. 6, Pub. L. 97-247) [48 FR 2714, Jan. 20, 1983]
