CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 30  /  Sec. 30.10-35 Headquarters--TB/ALL.

The term Headquarters means the Commandant (CG-CVC), Attn: Office of Commercial Vessel Compliance, U.S. Coast Guard Stop 7501, 2703 Martin Luther King Jr. Avenue SE., Washington, DC 20593-7501. [CGFR 65-50, 30 FR 16657, Dec. 30, 1965, as amended by CGFR 68-32, 33 FR 5712, Apr. 12, 1968; CGD 88-070, 53 FR 34533, Sept. 7, 1988; USCG-2013-0671, 78 FR 60146, Sept. 30, 2013]
