CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 35  /  Sec. 35.2200 Grant conditions.

In addition to the EPA General Grant Conditions (http://www.epa.gov/ogd/tc.htm), each treatment works grant shall be subject to the conditions under Secs. 35.2202 through 35.2218. [79 FR 76056, Dec. 19, 2014]
