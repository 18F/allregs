CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 4301  /  Sec. 4301.101 Prior approval for outside employment.

Any employee of the Overseas Private Investment Corporation who is interested in engaging in outside employment must first obtain approval from the Designated Agency Ethics Official before engaging in such employment activity. For this purpose, employment has the meaning set forth in Sec. 2635.603(a) of this title. [58 FR 33320, June 17, 1993]
