CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 3284  /  Sec. 3284.10 What must a unit operator do to prevent or compensate

The unit operator must take all necessary measures to prevent or compensate for drainage of geothermal resources from unitized land by wells on land not subject to the unit agreement (see Sec. Sec. 3210.16 and 3210.17 of this chapter).
