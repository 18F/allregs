CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 890  /  Sec. 890.1054 Agencies and entities to be notified of reinstatements.

OPM shall inform the FEHBP carriers, Government agencies and other organizations that were originally notified of a provider's debarment when a provider is reinstated under Sec. 890.1051 or Sec. 890.1052.
