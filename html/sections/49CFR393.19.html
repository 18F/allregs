CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 393  /  Sec. 393.19 Hazard warning signals.

The hazard warning signal operating unit on each commercial motor vehicle shall operate independently of the ignition or equivalent switch, and when activated, cause all turn signals required by Sec. 393.11 to flash simultaneously. [70 FR 48046, Aug. 15, 2005] Sec. 393.20 [Reserved]
