CFR /  Title 29  /  Part 779  /  Sec. 779.20 Person.

As used in the Act (including the definition of ``enterprise'' set forth in Sec. 779.21), ``person'' is defined as meaning ``an individual, partnership, association, corporation, business trust, legal representative, or any organized group of persons.'' (Act, section 3(a).)
