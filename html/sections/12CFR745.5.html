CFR /  Title 12  /  Part 745  /  Sec. 745.5 Accounts held by executors or administrators.

Funds of a decedent held in the name of the decedent or in the name of the executor or administrator of the decedent's estate and deposited in one or more accounts shall be insured up to the SMSIA in the aggregate for all such accounts, separately from the individual accounts of the beneficiaries of the estate or of the executor or administrator. [51 FR 37560, Oct. 23, 1986, as amended at 71 FR 14635, Mar. 23, 2006]
