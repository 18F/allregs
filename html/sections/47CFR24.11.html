CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 24  /  Sec. 24.11 Initial authorization.

(a) An applicant must file a single application for an initial authorization for all markets won and frequency blocks desired.

(b) Blanket licenses are granted for each market and frequency block. Applications for individual sites are not required and will not be accepted. [59 FR 32854, June 24, 1994, as amended at 63 FR 68952, Dec. 14, 1998]
