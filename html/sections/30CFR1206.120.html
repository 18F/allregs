CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 1206  /  Sec. 1206.120 How are operating allowances determined?

BOEMRE may use an operating allowance for the purpose of computing payment obligations when specified in the notice of sale and the lease. BOEM will specify the allowance amount or formula in the notice of sale and in the lease agreement.
