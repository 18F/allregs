CFR /  Title 27  /  Part 40  /  Sec. 40.135 Strengthening bond.

Where the amount of any bond is no longer sufficient under the provisions of Sec. 40.133 or Sec. 40.134, the manufacturer shall immediately file a strengthening bond in an appropriate amount with the same surety as that on the bond already in effect, unless a superseding bond is filed pursuant to Sec. 40.136. Strengthening bonds will not be approved where any notation is made thereon which is intended, or which may be construed, as a release of any former bond, or as limiting the amount of either bond to less than its full amount. (72 Stat. 1421; 26 U.S.C. 5711)
