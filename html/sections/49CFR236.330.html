CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 236  /  Sec. 236.330 Locking dog of switch-and-lock movement.

Locking dog of switch-and-lock movement shall extend through lock rod one-half inch or more in either normal or reverse position. Secs. 236.331-236.333 [Reserved]
