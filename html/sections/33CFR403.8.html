CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 403  /  Sec. 403.8 Proceedings; stay or adjournment. [Rule 8]

The Board may stay proceedings or any part of the proceedings as it thinks fit or may from time to time adjourn any proceedings before it. [Rule 8] [24 FR 9307, Nov. 18, 1959; 24 FR 10445, Dec. 23, 1959. Redesignated at 47 FR 13805, Apr. 1, 1982]
