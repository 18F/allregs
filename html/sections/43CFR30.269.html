CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 30  /  Sec. 30.269 What notice of the hearing will the judge provide?

On receiving a demand for hearing, the judge must:

(a) Set a time and place for the hearing after expiration of the 30- day period fixed for the filing of the demand for hearing as provided in Sec. 30.268; and

(b) Mail a notice of the hearing to all interested parties not less than 20 days in advance of the hearing.
