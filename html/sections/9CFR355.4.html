CFR /  Title 9  /  Part 355  /  Sec. 355.4 Application.

The owner or operator of any plant of the kind specified in Sec. 355.3 may apply to the Administrator for inspection, certification, and identification. In cases of change of ownership or change of location, new applications shall be made. (Approved by the Office of Management and Budget under control number 0583-0036) [23 FR 10107, Dec. 23, 1958. Redesignated at 30 FR 4195, Mar. 31, 1965, and amended at 47 FR 746, Jan. 7, 1982]
