CFR /  Title 25  /  Part 161  /  Sec. 161.710 How can impounded livestock or other property be redeemed?

Impounded livestock or other property may be redeemed by submitting proof of ownership and paying all penalties, damages, and costs under Sec. 161.712 and completing all corrective actions identified by BIA under Sec. 161.704.
