CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 956  /  Sec. 956.16 Pack.

Pack means a quantity of Walla Walla Sweet Onions specified by grade, size, weight, or count, or by type or condition of container, or any combination of these recommended by the committee and approved by the Secretary. [64 FR 4933, Feb. 1, 1999]
