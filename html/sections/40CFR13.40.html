CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 13  /  Sec. 13.40 Stay of offset.

If the debtor timely notifies the EPA that he or she is exercising the right described in Sec. 13.38(a) and timely submits evidence in accordance with Sec. 13.38(b), any notice to the IRS will be stayed until the issuance of a written decision which sustains or amends its original determination.
