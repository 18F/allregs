CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 2  /  Sec. 2.4 Probationary period.

Persons selected from registers of eligibles for career or career-conditional appointment and employees promoted, transferred, or otherwise assigned, for the first time, to supervisory or managerial positions shall be required to serve a probationary period under terms and conditions prescribed by the Office. [45 FR 4337, Jan. 22, 1980]
