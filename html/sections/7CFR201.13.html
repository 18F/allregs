CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 201  /  Sec. 201.13 Lot number or other identification.

The lot number or other identification shall be shown on the label and shall be the same as that used in the records pertaining to the same lot of seed. [5 FR 30, Jan. 4, 1940, as amended at 59 FR 64491, Dec. 14, 1994]
