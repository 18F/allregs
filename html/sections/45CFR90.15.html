CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 90  /  Sec. 90.15 Exceptions to the rules against age discrimination.

A recipient is permitted to take an action otherwise prohibited by Sec. 90.12 which is based on a factor other than age, even though that action may have a disproportionate effect on persons of different ages. An action may be based on a factor other than age only if the factor bears a direct and substantial relationship to the normal operation of the program or activity or to the achievement of a statutory objective.
