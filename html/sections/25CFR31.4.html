CFR /  Title 25  /  Part 31  /  Sec. 31.4 Compulsory attendance.

Compulsory school attendance of Indian children is provided for by law. (60 Stat. 962; 25 U.S.C. 231)

Cross Reference: For penalties for the failure of Indians to send children to school and for contributing to the delinquency of minors, see Sec. 11.424 of this chapter.
