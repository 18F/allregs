CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 452  /  452.237-78 Contracts with Consulting Firms for Services.

As prescribed in 437.270(b), insert a clause substantially as follows:

Contracts with Consulting Firms for Services (FEB 1988)

Offerors are specifically cautioned that any firm(s) receiving a contract award to provide the services described herein will be prohibited from competing for or receiving a follow-on contract to perform ------.*

(End of clause)

* Contracting Officer shall insert the appropriate information.
