CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 429  /  Sec. 429.20 What must an application include?

An application under this part must--

(a) Describe the qualifications of staff responsible for the project; and

(b) Provide that the activities and services for which assistance is sought will be administered by or under the supervision of the applicant. (Approved by the Office of Management and Budget under control number 1830-0013) (Authority: Sec. 441(d)(1), (3); 20 U.S.C. 2441(d)(1), (3))
