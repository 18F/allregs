CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 15  /  Sec. 15.25 Personal interviews.

(a) The NRC may seek an interview with the debtor at the offices of the NRC when--

(1) A matter involved in the claim needs clarification;

(2) Information is needed concerning the debtor's circumstances; or

(3) An agreement for payment might be negotiated.

(b) The NRC shall grant an interview with a debtor upon the debtor's request. The NRC will not reimburse a debtor's interview expenses. [47 FR 7616, Feb. 22, 1982, as amended at 55 FR 32378, Aug. 9, 1990]
