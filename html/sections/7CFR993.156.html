CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 993  /  Sec. 993.156 Application of reserve percentage.

The reserve obligation of each handler shall be determined by applying the reserve percentage to the weight of prunes in each lot, after deducting the weight of prunes in such lot shown as a percentage on the applicable inspection certificate as necessary to be removed therefrom pursuant to Sec. 993.49(c), in such manner as may be prescribed in such reserve control regulation established for the crop year in which such lot is received by a handler from a producer or dehydrator. [30 FR 13311, Oct. 20, 1965]

Effective Date Note: At 70 FR 30613, May 27, 2005, Sec. 993.156 was suspended indefinitely.
