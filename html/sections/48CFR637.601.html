CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 637  /  637.601 General.

It is the Department's policy that all new service contracts be performance-based, with clearly defined deliverables and performance standards. Any deviations from this policy shall be fully justified in writing and approved by the Departmental Advocate for Competition. [69 FR 19337, Apr. 13, 2004, as amended at 80 FR 6923, Feb. 9, 2015]
