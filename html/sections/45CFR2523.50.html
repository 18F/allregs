CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 2523  /  Sec. 2523.50 What types of funds are Federal agencies eligible to

Federal agencies may apply for planning and operating funds subject to the terms established by the Corporation in Sec. 2521.20 of this chapter, except that operating grants will be awarded with the expectation that the Federal agencies will support the proposed programs from their own budgets once the Corporation grant(s) expire.
