CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 812  /  Sec. 812.60 IRB composition, duties, and functions.

An IRB reviewing and approving investigations under this part shall comply with the requirements of part 56 in all respects, including its composition, duties, and functions. [46 FR 8957, Jan. 27, 1981]
