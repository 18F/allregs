CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 413  /  Sec. 413.217 Items and services included in the ESRD prospective payment system.

The following items and services are included in the ESRD prospective payment system effective January 1, 2011:

(a) Renal dialysis services as defined in Sec. 413.171; and

(b) Home dialysis services, support, and equipment as identified in Sec. 410.52 of this chapter. [75 FR 49200, Aug. 12, 2010]
