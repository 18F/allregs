CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 111  /  Sec. 111.33-11 Propulsion systems.

Each power semiconductor rectifier system in a propulsion system must meet sections 4-8-5/5.17.9 and 4-8-5/5.17.10 of ABS Steel Vessel Rules (incorporated by reference; see 46 CFR 110.10-1), except that each one for mobile offshore drilling units must meet the requirements in Part 4, Chapter 3, section 4/3.5.3 of ABS MODU Rules (incorporated by reference; see 46 CFR 110.10-1). [USCG-2003-16630, 73 FR 65197, Oct. 31, 2008]
