CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 1  /  Sec. 1.254 Nature of the hearing; burden of proof.

Any hearing upon an application shall be a full hearing in which the applicant and all other parties in interest shall be permitted to participate but in which both the burden of proceeding with the introduction of evidence upon any issue specified by the Commission, as well as the burden of proof upon all such issues, shall be upon the applicant except as otherwise provided in the order of designation. (Sec. 309, 48 Stat. 1085, as amended; 47 U.S.C. 309)
