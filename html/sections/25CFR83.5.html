CFR /  Title 25  /  Part 83  /  Sec. 83.5 How does a petitioner obtain Federal acknowledgment under this part?

To be acknowledged as a federally recognized Indian tribe under this part, a petitioner must meet the Indian Entity Identification (Sec. 83.11(a)), Governing Document (Sec. 83.11(d)), Descent (Sec. 83.11(e)), Unique Membership (Sec. 83.11(f)), and Congressional Termination (Sec. 83.11(g)) Criteria and must:

(a) Demonstrate previous Federal acknowledgment under Sec. 83.12(a) and meet the criteria in Sec. 83.12(b); or

(b) Meet the Community (Sec. 83.11(b)) and Political Authority (Sec. 83.11(c)) Criteria.
