CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 868  /  Sec. 868.71 Official certificate requirements.

Official certificates shall--

(a) Be on standard printed forms prescribed in the instructions;

(b) Be in English;

(c) Be typewritten or handwritten in ink and be clearly legible;

(d) Show the results of inspection services in a uniform, accurate, and concise manner;

(e) Show the information required by Sec. Sec. 868.70-868.75; and

(f) Show only such other information and statements of fact as are provided in the instructions authorized by the Administrator. [53 FR 3722, Feb. 9, 1988. Redesignated and amended at 60 FR 16364, Mar. 30, 1995]
