CFR /  Title 22  /  Part 513  /  Sec. 513.420 Scope of suspension.

The scope of a suspension is the same as the scope of a debarment (see Sec. 513.325), except that the procedures of Sec. Sec. 513.410 through 513.413 shall be used in imposing a suspension.
