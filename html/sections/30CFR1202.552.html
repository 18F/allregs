CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 1202  /  Sec. 1202.552 How do I determine how much royalty I must pay if my (AFA)?

You must pay royalties each month on production allocated to your lease under the terms of an AFA. To determine the volume and the value of your production, you must follow these three steps:

(a) You must determine the volume of your entitled share of production allocated to your lease under the terms of an AFA. This may include production from more than one AFA.

(b) You must value the production you take using 30 CFR part 1206. If you take more than your entitled share of production, see Sec. 1202.553 for information on how to value this production. If you take less than your entitled share of production, see Sec. 1202.554 for information on how to value production you are entitled to but do not take.
