CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 990  /  Sec. 990.150 Limited vacancies.

(a) Operating subsidy for a limited number of vacancies. HUD will pay operating subsidy for a limited number of vacant units under an ACC. The limited number of vacant units must be equal to or less than 3 percent of the unit months on a project-by-project basis based on the definition of a project under Sec. 990.265 (provided that the number of eligible unit months does not exceed 100 percent of the unit months for a project).
