CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 1699  /  1699.70 Cost accounting standards.

With respect to all experience-rated contracts currently existing under the FEHB Program, the Cost Accounting Standards, found at 48 CFR part 9904, of the Code of Federal Regulations, do not apply. [70 FR 31392, June 1, 2005]
