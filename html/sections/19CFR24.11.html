CFR /  Title 19  /  Part 24  /  Sec. 24.11 Notice to importer or owner of increased or additional

Any increased or additional duties, taxes, fees or interest found due upon liquidation or reliquidation shall be billed to the importer of record, or to the actual owner if the following have been filed with Customs:

(a) A declaration of the actual owner in accordance with section 485(d), Tariff Act of 1930, as amended (19 U.S.C. 1485(d)), and Sec. 141.20 of this chapter; and

(b) A bond on Customs Form 301 in accordance with Sec. 141.20 of this chapter. [T.D. 99-75, 64 FR 56439, Oct. 20, 1999]
