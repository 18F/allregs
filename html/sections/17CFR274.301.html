CFR /  Title 17  /  Part 274  /  Sec. 274.301 Notification of claim of exemption pursuant to Rule

This form shall be filed with the Commission as required by Sec. 270.6e-2 or Sec. 270.6e-3(T) of this chapter by each insurance company with respect to each separate account for which exemption is claimed pursuant to Sec. 270.6e-2 or Sec. 270.6e-3(T). [49 FR 47228, Dec. 3, 1984]
