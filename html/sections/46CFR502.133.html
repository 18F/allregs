CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 502  /  Sec. 502.133 Attendance and mileage fees.

Witnesses summoned by subpoena to a hearing or deposition are entitled to the same fees and mileage that are paid to witnesses in courts of the United States. Fees and mileage shall be paid, upon request, by the party at whose instance the witness appears. [Rule 133.] [64 FR 7809, Feb. 17, 1999, as amended at 76 FR 10262, Feb. 24, 2011]
