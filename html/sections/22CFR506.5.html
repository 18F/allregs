CFR /  Title 22  /  Part 506  /  Sec. 506.5 Review and evaluation.

The part-time career employment program will be reviewed through semiannual reports submitted by the Director, Office of Personnel to the Associate Director for Management. Regular employment reports will be used to determine levels of part-time employment. [44 FR 63098, Nov. 2, 1979, as amended at 51 FR 11016, Apr. 1, 1986]
