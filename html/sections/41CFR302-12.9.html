CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 302-12  /  Sec. 302-12.9 If my agency authorizes me to enter a homesale program,

No, if your agency authorizes you to enter a homesale program, your agency must give you the option to accept or reject an offer from the relocation services company. [FTR Amdt. 98, 66 FR 58196, Nov. 20, 2001. Redesignated at 76 FR 18343, Apr. 1, 2011]
