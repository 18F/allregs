CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 1951  /  Sec. 1951.213 Debt settlement.

Subpart C of part 1956 of this chapter prescribes policies and procedures for debt settlement actions for loans covered under this subpart when it is determined that a debt is eligible for settlement except as provided in Sec. Sec. 1951.216 and 1951.231.
