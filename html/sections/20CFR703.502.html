CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 703  /  Sec. 703.502 Same; employer operating temporarily in another

A district director receiving a report of the issuance of a policy of insurance with the notation authorized by Sec. 703.119, will file such report until he receives from the insured employer named therein a request for certificate of compliance, giving the address of the employer within the compensation district of such district director. Upon receipt of such a request the district director will send the proper certificate of compliance to such employer at such address.

Effective Date Note: At 80 FR 12933, Mar. 12, 2015, Sec. 703.502 was removed and reserved, effective June 10, 2015.
