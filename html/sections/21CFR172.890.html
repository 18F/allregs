CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 172  /  Sec. 172.890 Rice bran wax.

Rice bran wax may be safely used in food in accordance with the following conditions:

(a) It is the refined wax obtained from rice bran and meets the following specifications: Melting point 75 [deg]C to 80 [deg]C.Free fatty acids, maximum 10 percent.Iodine number, maximum 20.Saponification number 75 to 120.
