CFR /  Title 29  /  Part 4003  /  Sec. 4003.53 Where to file.

An appeal or a request for an extension of time to appeal shall be submitted to the Appeals Board. See Sec. 4000.4 of this chapter for additional information on where to file. [61 FR 34012, July 1, 1996, as amended at 68 FR 61352, Oct. 28, 2003; 73 FR 38120, July 3, 2008]
