CFR /  Title 18  /  Part 34  /  Sec. 34.8 Verification.

An application verification shall be signed under oath by an authorized representative of the applicant, who has knowledge of the matters set forth therein and as provided in Sec. 385.2005 of this chapter, and retained at the applicant's business location until the relevant proceeding has been concluded.
