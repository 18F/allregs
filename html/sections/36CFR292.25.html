CFR /  Title 36  /  Part 292  /  Sec. 292.25 Information requirements.

The information required by Sec. 292.24 of this subpart in order for a landowner to obtain a determination of compliance constitutes an information requirement as defined in the Paperwork Reduction Act (44 U.S.C. 3507) and has been approved for use by the Office of Management and Budget and assigned control number 0596-0135.
