CFR /  Title 11  /  Part 9409  /  Sec. 9409.6 Service of subpoenas or requests.

Subpoenas or requests for official records or information or testimony must be served on the General Counsel, U.S. Election Assistance Commission, 1201 New York Avenue, NW., Suite 300, Washington, DC 20005. [73 FR 54271, Sept. 18, 2008, as amended at 75 FR 49814, Aug. 16, 2010]
