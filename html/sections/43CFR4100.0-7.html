CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 4100  /  Sec. 4100.0-7 Cross reference.

The regulations at part 1600 of this chapter govern the development of land use plans; the regulations at part 1780, subpart 1784 of this chapter govern advisory committees; and the regulations at subparts B and E of part 4 of this title govern appeals and hearings. [60 FR 9962, Feb. 22, 1995]
