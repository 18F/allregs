CFR /  Title 13  /  Part 126  /  Sec. 126.702 How can the subcontracting percentage requirements

SBA may change the required subcontracting percentage for a specific industry if the Administrator determines that such action is necessary to reflect conventional industry practices among SBCs that are below the numerical size standard for businesses in that industry group. The procedures for requesting changes in subcontracting percentages are set forth in Sec. 125.6 of this chapter. [69 FR 29427, May 24, 2004]
