CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 227  /  227.7101 Definitions.

(a) As used in this subpart, unless otherwise specifically indicated, the terms ``offeror'' and ``contractor'' include an offeror's or contractor's subcontractors, suppliers, or potential subcontractors or suppliers at any tier.

(b) Other terms used in this subpart are defined in the clause at 252.227-7013, Rights in Technical Data--Noncommercial Items. [56 FR 36389, July 31, 1991, as amended at 60 FR 61598, Nov. 30, 1995]
