CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 215  /  215.371-5 Waiver.

(a) The head of the contracting activity is authorized to waive the requirement at 215.371-2 to resolicit for an additional period of at least 30 days.

(b) This waiver authority cannot be delegated below one level above the contracting officer. [77 FR 39138, June 29, 2012]
