CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 9185  /  Sec. 9185.1-3 Mining claims.

(a) Application for survey. Application for the survey of a mining claim should be filed with the State Director for the State in which the claim is situated.

(b) Mineral surveyors. See Sec. 3861.5-1 for the appointment of mineral surveyors pursuant to section 2334 of the Revised Statutes (30 U.S.C. 39). [35 FR 9798, June 13, 1970, as amended at 38 FR 30001, Oct. 31, 1973]
