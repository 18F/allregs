CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 112  /  Sec. 112.172 How may a State, tribe, or foreign country request a

To request a variance from one or more requirements of this part, the competent authority (i.e., the regulatory authority for food safety) for a State, tribe, or a foreign country must submit a petition under Sec. 10.30 of this chapter.
