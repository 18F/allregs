CFR /  Title 9  /  Part 320  /  Sec. 320.4 Access to and inspection of records, facilities and

Representatives of the Secretary afforded access to a business specified in Sec. 320.1 of this part (see Sec. 300.6(b)(2) of this chapter) also must be afforded any necessary facilities (other than reproduction equipment) for the examination and copying of records and for the examination and sampling of inventory. [69 FR 254, Jan. 5, 2004]
