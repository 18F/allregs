CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 303  /  Sec. 303.221 Expenditure of funds.

The State must ensure that Federal funds made available to the State under section 643 of the Act will be expended in accordance with the provisions of this part, including Secs. 303.500 and 303.501. (Approved by Office of Management and Budget under control number 1820-0550) (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1437(b)(1))
