CFR /  Title 31  /  Part 351  /  Sec. 351.33 What are interest rates and redemption values for Series EE maturity period?

During an extended maturity period the bond will be subject to the terms and conditions in effect when it is issued and will continue to earn interest as described in Sec. 351.30, unless the terms and conditions applicable to an extended maturity period are expressly amended prior to the beginning of such period.
