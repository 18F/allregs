CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 461  /  Sec. 461.14 When are amendments to a State plan required?

(a) General. If an amendment to the State plan is necessary, the SEA shall submit the amendment to the Secretary not later than 90 days prior to the program year of operation to which the amendment applies.

(b) Indicators of program quality. Each SEA shall amend its plan by July 25, 1993, to include the indicators of program quality required by section 331 of the Act and Sec. 461.3(b)(7). Cross-Reference: See 34 CFR 76.140-76.142 Amendments. (Approved by the Office of Management and Budget under control number 1830-0026) (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1207(a))
