CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 52  /  Sec. 52.01-115 Feedwater supply (modifies PG-61).

Boiler feedwater supply must meet the requirements of PG-61 of section I of the ASME Boiler and Pressure Vessel Code (incorporated by reference; see 46 CFR 52.01-1) and Sec. 56.50-30 of this subchapter. [USCG-2003-16630, 73 FR 65161, Oct. 31, 2008]
