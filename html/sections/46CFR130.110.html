CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 130  /  Sec. 130.110 Internal communications on OSVs of less than 100 gross

Each vessel of less than 100 gross tons equipped with an independent auxiliary means of steering, as required by Sec. 130.130(b) of this subpart, must have a fixed means of communication between the pilothouse and the place where the auxiliary means of steering is controlled.
