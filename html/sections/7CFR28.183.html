CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 28  /  Sec. 28.183 Fees and costs; payment.

The provisions of Sec. Sec. 28.115 through 28.126 relating to fees, costs, and method of payment shall apply to services performed with respect to cotton produced outside the continental United States.
