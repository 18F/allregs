CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 1  /  Sec. 1.791 Reports and requests to be filed under part 32 of this chapter.

Reports and requests shall be filed either periodically, upon the happening of specified events, or for specific approval by class A and class B telephone companies in accordance with and subject to the provisions of part 32 of this chapter. [55 FR 30461, July 26, 1990]
