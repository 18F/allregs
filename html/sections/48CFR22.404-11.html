CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 22  /  22.404-11 Wage determination appeals.

The Secretary of Labor has established an Administrative Review Board which decides appeals of final decisions made by the Department of Labor concerning Construction Wage Rate Requirements statute wage determinations. A contracting agency or other interested party may file a petition for review under the procedures in 29 CFR Part 7 if reconsideration by the Administrator has been sought pursuant to 29 CFR 1.8 and denied. [53 FR 4935, Feb. 18, 1988, as amended at 66 FR 53481, Oct. 22, 2001; 79 FR 24204, Apr. 29, 2014]
