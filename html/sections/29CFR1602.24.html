CFR /  Title 29  /  Part 1602  /  Sec. 1602.24 Commission's remedy for failure to file report.

Any person failing or refusing to file Report EEO-3 when required to do so may be compelled to file by order of a U.S. District Court, upon application of the Commission, under authority of section 709(c) of title VII. [37 FR 9220, May 6, 1972]
