CFR /  Title 19  /  Part 207  /  Sec. 207.50 Judicial review.

(a) In general. Persons entitled to judicial review under section 516A of the Act may seek review in the U.S. Court of International Trade.

(b) Transmittal of record. In the event a Commission determination is appealed to the U.S. Court of International Trade under section 516A, a copy of the record in the investigation before the Commission, as such record is defined in Sec. 207.2(f), or a certified list of all items therein, shall be transmitted to the court by the Secretary in accordance with the rules of the court.

(c) Service of process. The Commission's General Counsel shall be the Commission's agent for service of process in cases arising under section 516A of the Act.
