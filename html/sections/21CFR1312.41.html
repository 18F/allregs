CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 1312  /  Sec. 1312.41 Hearings generally.

(a) In any case where the Administrator shall hold a hearing regarding the denial of an application for an import, export or transshipment permit, the procedures for such hearing shall be governed generally by the adjudication procedures set forth in the Administrative Procedure Act (5 U.S.C. 551-559) and specifically by sections 1002 and 1003 of the Act (21 U.S.C. 952 and 953), by Sec. Sec. 1312.42-1312.47, and by the procedures for administrative hearings under the Act set forth in Sec. Sec. 1316.41-1316.67 of this chapter.

(b) [Reserved] [36 FR 23625, Dec. 11, 1971, as amended at 37 FR 15923, Aug. 8, 1972. Redesignated at 38 FR 26609, Sept. 24, 1973]
