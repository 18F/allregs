CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 752  /  752.229-70 Federal, state and local taxes.

For contracts involving performance overseas the clauses prescribed in (48 CFR) FAR 29.401-3 or 29.401-4 may be modified to specify that the taxes referred to are United States taxes. [49 FR 13259, Apr. 3, 1984, as amended at 79 FR 74988, Dec. 16, 2014]
