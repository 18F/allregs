CFR /  Title 12  /  Part 27  /  Sec. 27.1 Scope and OMB control number.

(a) Scope. This part applies to the activities of national banks and their subsidiaries, which make home loans for the purpose of purchasing, construction-permanent financing, or refinancing of residential real property.

(b) OMB control number. The collection of information requirements contained in this part were approved by the Office of Management and Budget under OMB control number 1557-0160. [49 FR 11825, Mar. 28, 1984, as amended at 73 FR 22251, Apr. 24, 2008]
