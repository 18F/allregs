CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 75  /  Sec. 75.65 Opacity reports.

The owner or operator or designated representative shall report excess emissions of opacity recorded under Sec. 75.57(f) to the applicable State or local air pollution control agency. [64 FR 28623, May 26, 1999, as amended at 67 FR 40444, June 12, 2002]
