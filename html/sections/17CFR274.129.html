CFR /  Title 17  /  Part 274  /  Sec. 274.129 Form N-PX, annual report of proxy voting record of

This form shall be used by registered management investment companies, other than small business investment companies registered on Form N-5 (Secs. 239.24 and 274.5 of this chapter), for annual reports to be filed not later than August 31 of each year, containing the company's proxy voting record for the most recent twelve-month period ended June 30, pursuant to section 30 of the Investment Company Act of 1940 and Sec. 270.30b1-4 of this chapter. [68 FR 6584, Feb. 7, 2003]
