CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 155  /  Sec. 155.800 Transfer hose.

Hose used to transfer oil or hazardous material must meet the requirements of Sec. 154.500 of this chapter. [CGD 75-124, 45 FR 7175, Jan. 31, 1980, as amended by CGD 86-034, 55 FR 36255, Sept. 4, 1990]
