CFR /  Title 25  /  Part 141  /  Sec. 141.48 Translation of disclosure statements.

Disclosure required by Secs. 141.46 and 141.47 shall be made in writing regardless of the customer's ability to speak, read, or write the English language. Disclosure to non-English speaking persons shall be translated orally into the appropriate language.
