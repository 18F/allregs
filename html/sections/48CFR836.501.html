CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 836  /  836.501 Performance of work by the contractor.

The contracting officer shall insert the clause at 852.236-72, Performance of work by the contractor, in solicitations and contracts for construction that contain the FAR clause at 52.236-1, Performance of Work by the Contractor. When the solicitations or contracts include a section entitled ``Network Analysis System (NAS),'' the contracting officer must use the clause with its Alternate I.
