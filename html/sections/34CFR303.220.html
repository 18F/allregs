CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 303  /  Sec. 303.220 Assurances satisfactory to the Secretary.

Each application must contain assurances satisfactory to the Secretary that the State has met the requirements in Secs. 303.221 through 303.227. (Approved by Office of Management and Budget under control number 1820-0550) (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1437(b))
