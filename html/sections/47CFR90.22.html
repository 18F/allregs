CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 90  /  Sec. 90.22 Paging operations.

Unless specified elsewhere in this part, paging operations may be authorized in the Public Safety Pool on any frequency except those assigned under the provisions of Sec. 90.20(d)(78). Paging operations on frequencies subject to Sec. 90.20(d)(78) authorized before August 17, 1974, may be continued only if they do not cause harmful interference to regular operations on the same frequencies.
