CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 366  /  Sec. 366.61 What are the compliance indicators?

(a) The compliance indicators establish the activities that a center shall carry out to demonstrate minimum compliance with the evaluation standards in Sec. 366.60.

(b) If a center fails to satisfy any one of the indicators, the center is out of compliance with the evaluation standards. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 796d-1(b))
