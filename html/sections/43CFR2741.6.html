CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 2741  /  Sec. 2741.6 Applications for transfer or change of use.

(a) Applications under the act for permission to add to or change the use specified in a patent or applications to transfer title to a third party shall be filed as prescribed in Sec. 2741.4 of this title.

(b) Applications for transfer of title are subject to the acreage limitations as prescribed in Sec. 2741.7(a) of this title.

(c) Prior to approval of an application filed under this section, the public lands may be reappraised in accordance with Sec. 2741.8 of this title and the beneficiary required to make such payments as are found justified by the reappraisal. [44 FR 43472, July 25, 1979. Redesignated at 51 FR 50300, Dec. 10, 1985, and amended at 50 FR 50301, Dec. 10, 1985]
