CFR /  Title 25  /  Part 90  /  Sec. 90.44 Statement of supervisor.

Following the election a statement is to be prepared by the supervisor pertaining to the conduct of the election and certifying to the correct tabulation of the votes for each candidate. The statement shall also set forth the names of the elected candidates and the office to which each was elected. The statement shall be duly acknowledged before an officer qualified to administer oaths and delivered to the Superintendent of the Osage Agency. [32 FR 10254, July 12, 1967. Redesignated at 47 FR 13327, Mar. 30, 1982]
