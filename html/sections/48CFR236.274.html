CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 236  /  236.274 Restriction on acquisition of steel for use in military

In accordance with section 108 of the Military Construction and Veterans Affairs Appropriations Act, 2009 ( Pub. L. 110-329, Division E) and the same provision in subsequent military construction appropriations acts), do not acquire, or allow a contractor to acquire, steel for any construction project or activity for which American steel producers, fabricators, or manufacturers have been denied the opportunity to compete for such acquisition of steel. [74 FR 2418, Jan. 15, 2009, as amended at 80 FR 15911, Mar. 26, 2015]
