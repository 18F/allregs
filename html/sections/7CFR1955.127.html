CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 1955  /  Sec. 1955.127 Selection and use of contractors to dispose of

Sections 1955.128 through 1955.131 prescribe procedures for contracting for services to facilitate disposal of inventory property. FmHA or its successor agency under Public Law 103-354 Instruction 2024-A (available in any FmHA or its successor agency under Public Law 103-354 office) is applicable for procurement of nonpersonal services. [53 FR 27836, July 25, 1988]
