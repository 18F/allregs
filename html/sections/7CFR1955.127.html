CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 1955  /  Sec. 1955.127 Selection and use of contractors to dispose of inventory

Sections 1955.128 through 1955.131 prescribe procedures for contracting for services to facilitate disposal of inventory property. RD Instruction 2024-A (available in any Rural Development office) is applicable for procurement of nonpersonal services. [53 FR 27836, July 25, 1988]
