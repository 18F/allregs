CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 2526  /  Sec. 2526.25 Is a participant in an approved Summer of Service position term of service eligible to receive a pro-rated education award?

No. An individual released for any reason prior to completing an approved term of service in a Silver Scholar or Summer of Service position is not eligible to receive a pro-rated award. [75 FR 51411, Aug. 20, 2010]
