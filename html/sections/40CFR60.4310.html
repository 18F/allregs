CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 60  /  Sec. 60.4310 What types of operations are exempt from these

(a) Emergency combustion turbines, as defined in Sec. 60.4420(i), are exempt from the nitrogen oxides (NOX) emission limits in Sec. 60.4320.

(b) Stationary combustion turbines engaged by manufacturers in research and development of equipment for both combustion turbine emission control techniques and combustion turbine efficiency improvements are exempt from the NOX emission limits in Sec. 60.4320 on a case-by-case basis as determined by the Administrator.

(c) Stationary combustion turbines at integrated gasification combined cycle electric utility steam generating units that are subject to subpart Da of this part are exempt from this subpart.

(d) Combustion turbine test cells/stands are exempt from this subpart.
