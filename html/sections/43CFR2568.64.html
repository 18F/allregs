CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 2568  /  Sec. 2568.64 Are there different requirements for giving an allotment

No, the estate of the deceased veteran eligible under Sec. 2568.60 must meet the same requirements for a Native allotment as other living Alaska Native veterans. In addition, a deceased veteran must have been a resident of Alaska at the time of death.
