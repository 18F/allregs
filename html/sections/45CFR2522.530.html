CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 2522  /  Sec. 2522.530 May I use the Corporation's program grant funds for

If performance measurement and evaluation costs were approved as part of your grant, you may use your program grant funds to support them, consistent with the level of approved costs for such activities in your grant award. [70 FR 39603, July 8, 2005]
