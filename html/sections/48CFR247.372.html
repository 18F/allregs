CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 247  /  247.372 DD Form 1654, Evaluation of Transportation Cost Factors.

Contracting personnel may use the DD Form 1654 to furnish information to the transportation office for development of cost factors for use by the contracting officer in the evaluation of f.o.b. origin offers. [56 FR 36466, July 31, 1991. Redesignated at 75 FR 51418, Aug. 20, 2010]
