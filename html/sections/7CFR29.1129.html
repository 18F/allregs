CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 29  /  Sec. 29.1129 Rule 23.

Tobacco shall be designated by the grademark ``No-G,'' when it is offtype, semicured, fire-killed, smoked, oxidized over 10 percent, has an odor foreign to the type, or is packed in bales which are not approximately 42 inches wide x 42 inches high x 40 inches long. [65 FR 46086, July 27, 2000]
