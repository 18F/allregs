CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 640  /  Sec. 640.67 Laboratory tests.

Each unit of Source Plasma shall be tested for evidence of infection due to communicable disease agents as required under Sec. 610.40 of this chapter. [66 FR 31165, June 11, 2001]

Effective Date Note: At 80 FR 29905, May 22, 2015, Sec. 640.67 was amended by removing ``communicable disease agents'' and adding in its place ``relevant transfusion-transmitted infections'', effective May 23, 2016.
