CFR /  Title 31  /  Part 363  /  Sec. 363.210 Is there any period of time during which I will be unable

A closed book period will be in effect for four business days prior to the date a marketable security interest and/or redemption payment is made. During the closed book period, you cannot change the registration of the security, change the payment destination of the proceeds, change the view or transaction rights, make transfers, or schedule, edit, or cancel a reinvestment. We will hold transactions requiring submission of a form for processing until the closed book period ends. [75 FR 26091, May 11, 2010, as amended at 75 FR 78901, Dec. 17, 2010] Sec.
