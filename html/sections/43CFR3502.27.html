CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 3502  /  Sec. 3502.27 If I am an individual, what information must I give BLM

If you are an individual, send us a signed statement showing that:

(a) You are a U.S. citizen; and

(b) Your acreage holdings do not exceed the limits in Sec. 3503.37 of this part. This includes your holdings through a corporation, association, or partnership in which you are the beneficial owner of more than 10% of the stock or other instruments of control.
