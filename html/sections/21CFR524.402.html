CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 524  /  Sec. 524.402 Chlorhexidine.

(a) Specifications. Each gram of ointment contains 10 milligrams chlorhexidine acetate.

(b) Sponsors. See Nos. 054771 and 058829 in Sec. 510.600(c) of this chapter.

(c) Conditions of use in dogs, cats, and horses--(1) Indications for use. For use as a topical antiseptic ointment for surface wounds.

(1) Indications for use. For use as a topical antiseptic ointment for surface wounds.

(2) Limitations. Do not use in horses intended for human consumption. [72 FR 265, Jan. 4, 2007, as amended at 79 FR 10967, Feb. 27, 2014]
