CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 850  /  Sec. 850.203 Other elections.

Any other election may be effected in such form as the Director prescribes under Sec. 850.104. Such elections include but are not limited to elections of coverage under CSRS, FERS, FEGLI, FEHB, or RFEHB by individuals entitled to elect such coverage; applications for service credit and applications to make deposit; and elections regarding the withholding of State income tax from annuity payments. [78 FR 68983, Nov. 18, 2013]
