CFR /  Title 31  /  Part 330  /  Sec. 330.0 Purpose.

The regulations in this part establish a procedure under which qualified paying agents may specially endorse definitive United States Savings Bonds of certain series and United States Savings Notes (Freedom Shares), and either redeem the securities so endorsed, or forward them to a TRS Site for redemption, with or without the owner's signature to the request for payment. [77 FR 16169, Mar. 20, 2012]
