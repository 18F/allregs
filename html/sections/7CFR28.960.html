CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 28  /  Sec. 28.960 Confidential information.

No information concerning individual tests under the regulations in this subpart shall be published or communicated in such a way as to disclose to others the identity of the owners of cotton represented by samples submitted for testing, except with the written permission of such owners. [35 FR 8532, June 3, 1970. Redesignated at 46 FR 30075, June 5, 1981]
