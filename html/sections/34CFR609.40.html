CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 609  /  Sec. 609.40 What are the matching requirements?

If an institution receives a grant in excess of $500,000, it must spend non-Federal funds to meet the cost of at least 50 percent of the activities approved in its application. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1063b)
