CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 80  /  Sec. 80.211 What are the requirements for treating imported gasoline

An importer may treat imported gasoline (as defined in Sec. 80.2(c)) as gasoline treated as blendstock, or GTAB, under the provisions of Sec. 80.83 for purposes of compliance with this subpart H. [70 FR 74578, Dec. 15, 2005]
