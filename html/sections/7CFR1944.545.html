CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 1944  /  Sec. 1944.545 Additional grants.

An additional grant may be made to an applicant that has previously received a TSA grant and has achieved or nearly achieved the goals established for the previous grant by submitting a new proposal for TSA funds. The additional grant application will be processed as if it were an initial application. Upon approval, a new grant agreement will be required and the grant will be coded as an initial grant on Form FmHA or its successor agency under Public Law 103-354 440-1. Sec. 1944.546 [Reserved]
