CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 203  /  Sec. 203.443 Insurance premium.

All of the provisions of Sec. Sec. 203.260 through 203.269 \1\ concerning mortgage insurance premiums, apply to loans insured under Sec. 203.50.---------------------------------------------------------------------------

\1\ Section 203.269 was removed at 48 FR 35089, Aug. 3, 1983. [47 FR 30753, July 15, 1982]
