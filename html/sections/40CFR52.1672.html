CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 52  /  Sec. 52.1672 Extensions.

Pursuant to section 186(a)(4) of the Clean Air Act, as amended in 1990, the Regional Administrator hereby extends for one year (until December 31, 1996) the attainment date for the New York-Northern New Jersey-Long Island Consolidated Metropolitan Statistical Carbon Monoxide nonattainment area. [61 FR 56900, Nov. 5, 1996]
