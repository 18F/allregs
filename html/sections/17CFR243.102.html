CFR /  Title 17  /  Part 243  /  Sec. 243.102 No effect on antifraud liability.

No failure to make a public disclosure required solely by Sec. 243.100 shall be deemed to be a violation of Rule 10b-5 (17 CFR 240.10b-5) under the Securities Exchange Act.
