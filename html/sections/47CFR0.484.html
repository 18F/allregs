CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 0  /  Sec. 0.484 Amateur radio operator examinations.

Generally, examinations for amateur radio operation licenses shall be administered at locations and times specified by volunteer examiners. (See Sec. 97.509). When the FCC conducts examinations for amateur radio operator licenses, they shall take place at locations and times designated by the FCC. [58 FR 13021, Mar. 9, 1993]
