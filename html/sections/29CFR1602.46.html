CFR /  Title 29  /  Part 1602  /  Sec. 1602.46 Applicability of State or local law.

The requirements imposed by the Equal Employment Opportunity Commission in these regulations, subparts L and M of this part, supersede any provisions of State or local law which may conflict with them. [38 FR 26720, Sept. 25, 1973]
