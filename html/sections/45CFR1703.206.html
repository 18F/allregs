CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 1703  /  Sec. 1703.206 Providing information to the public.

Individuals or organizations interested in obtaining copies of information available in accordance with Sec. 1703.204 may request same under provisions set forth in Sec. Sec. 1703.402 and 1704.404. Individuals or organizations having a special interest in activities of the Commission may request the Executive Director to the Commissioners to place them on a mailing list for receipt of information available under Sec. 1703.205. The Commission shall provide information to publications whose readers are likely to have a special interest in the work of the Commission.
