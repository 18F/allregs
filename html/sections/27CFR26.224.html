CFR /  Title 27  /  Part 26  /  Sec. 26.224 Filing and disposition of formulas.

Formulas required by this subpart must be submitted, and disposed of, in accordance with the instructions on the prescribed TTB form. The applicant shall maintain copies of approved formulas available for examination by insular agents. [T.D. ATF-451, 66 FR 21670, May 1, 2001]
