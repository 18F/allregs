CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 102-42  /  Sec. 102-42.50 What types of appraisals may my agency consider?

Your agency may allow--

(a) Written commercial appraisals conducted by an appraisal firm or trade organization; and

(b) Retail value appraisals where the value of the gift may be ascertained by reviewing current and reliable non-discounted retail catalogs, retail price lists, or retail Web site valuations. [74 FR 2396, Jan. 15, 2009]
