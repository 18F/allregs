CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 433  /  Sec. 433.302 Scope of subpart.

This subpart sets forth the requirements and procedures under which States have 1 year following discovery of overpayments made to providers for Medicaid services to recover or attempt to recover that amount before the States must refund the Federal share of these overpayments to CMS, with certain exceptions. [77 FR 31511, May 29, 2012]
