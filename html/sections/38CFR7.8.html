CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 7  /  Sec. 7.8 Beneficiary or assignee.

The consent of a beneficiary, assignee, or any other person who may have a right or interest in the proceeds of the policy is not a prerequisite for placing a policy under the protection of the Act. [61 FR 29026, June 7, 1996]
