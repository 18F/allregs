CFR /  Title 9  /  Part 165  /  Sec. 165.1 Availability of information.

The Animal and Plant Health Inspection Service regulations relating to availability of information to the public and disclosure of records under 5 U.S.C. 552, which are set forth in 7 CFR part 370, are incorporated into this subchapter. (5 U.S.C. 552, 559) [32 FR 10915, July 26, 1967, as amended at 36 FR 24928, Dec. 24, 1971]
