CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 102-85  /  Sec. 102-85.70 Are the standard OA terms appropriate for

Yes, most of the standard terms apply; however, the right to cancel upon a 4-month (120 day) notice is not available. See Sec. 102-85.35 for the definition of non-cancelable space.
