CFR /  Title 9  /  Part 91  /  Sec. 91.22 Protection from heat of boilers and engines.

No animals shall be stowed along the alleyways leading to the engine or boiler rooms unless the sides of said engine or boiler rooms are covered by a tongue and groove tight sheathing producing a 3-inch-wide air space except that on ships powered with internal combustion engines this sheathing may not be required at the discretion of the inspector. [42 FR 28990, June 7, 1977. Redesignated at 45 FR 86413, Dec. 31, 1980]
