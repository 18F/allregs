CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 441  /  Sec. 441.308 Hearings procedures for waiver terminations.

The procedures specified in subpart D of part 430 of this chapter are applicable to State requests for hearings on terminations. [50 FR 10028, Mar. 13, 1985. Redesignated at 59 FR 37720, July 25, 1994]
