CFR /  Title 27  /  Part 12  /  Sec. 12.1 Scope.

The regulations in this part relate to foreign names of geographic significance used in the designation of wines which are recognized as nongeneric under 27 CFR 4.24, and include those nongeneric names which the Administrator has found to be distinctive designations of wine, as defined in Sec. 4.24(c)(1) of this chapter.
