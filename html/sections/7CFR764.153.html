CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 764  /  Sec. 764.153 Limitations.

The applicant must:

(a) Comply with the general limitations established at Sec. 764.102;

(b) Have dwellings and other buildings necessary for the planned operation of the farm available for use after the loan is made.
