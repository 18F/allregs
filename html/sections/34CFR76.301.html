CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 76  /  Sec. 76.301 Local educational agency general application.

A local educational agency that applies for a subgrant under a program subject to this part shall have on file with the State a general application that meets the requirements of Section 442 of the General Education Provisions Act. (Approved by the Office of Management and Budget under control number 1880-0513) (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1221e-3, 1232d, and 3474) [52 FR 27804, July 24, 1987, as amended at 53 FR 49143, Dec. 6, 1988; 60 FR 46493, Sept. 6, 1995]
