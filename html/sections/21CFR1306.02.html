CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 1306  /  Sec. 1306.02 Definitions.

Any term contained in this part shall have the definition set forth in section 102 of the Act (21 U.S.C. 802) or part 1300 of this chapter. [62 FR 13964, Mar. 24, 1997]
