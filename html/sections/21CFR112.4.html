CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 112  /  Sec. 112.4 Which farms are subject to the requirements of this part?

(a) Except as provided in paragraph (b) of this section, a farm or farm mixed-type facility with an average annual monetary value of produce (as ``produce'' is defined in Sec. 112.3(c)) sold during the previous 3-year period of more than $25,000 (on a rolling basis), adjusted for inflation using 2011 as the baseline year for calculating the adjustment, is a ``covered farm'' subject to this part. Covered farms subject to this part must comply with all applicable requirements of this part when conducting a covered activity on covered produce.

(b) A farm is not a covered farm if it satisfies the requirements in Sec. 112.5 and we have not withdrawn the farm's exemption in accordance with the requirements of subpart R of this part.
