CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 162  /  Sec. 162.1501 Enrollment and disenrollment in a health plan transaction.

The enrollment and disenrollment in a health plan transaction is the transmission of subscriber enrollment information from the sponsor of the insurance coverage, benefits, or policy, to a health plan to establish or terminate insurance coverage. [74 FR 3327, Jan. 16, 2009]
