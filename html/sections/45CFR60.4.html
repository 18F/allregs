CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 60  /  Sec. 60.4 How information must be reported.

Information must be reported to the NPDB as required under Sec. Sec. 60.7, 60.8, 60.9, 60.10, 60.11, 60.12, 60.13, 60.14, 60.15 and 60.16 in such form and manner as the Secretary may prescribe.
