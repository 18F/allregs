CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 1355  /  Sec. 1355.55 Review and assessment of the system developed with

(a) ACF will, on a continuing basis, review, assess and inspect the planning, design, development, installation and operation of the SACWIS or TACWIS to determine the extent to which such systems:

(1) Meet Sec. 1355.53 of this chapter,

(2) Meet the goals and objectives stated in the approved APD,

(3) Meet the schedule, budget, and other conditions of the approved APD, and

(4) Comply with the automated data processing services and acquisitions procedures and requirements of 45 CFR part 95, subpart F.

(b) [Reserved]

(This requirement has been approved by the Office of Management and Budget under OMB Control Number 0970-0007. In accordance with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1995, an agency may not conduct or sponsor, and a person is not required to respond to, a collection of information unless it displays a currently valid OMB control number.) [58 FR 67946, Dec. 22, 1993, as amended at 77 FR 934, Jan. 6, 2012]
