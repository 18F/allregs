CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 652  /  Sec. 652.100 Services for veterans.

Services for veterans are administered by the Office of the Assistant Secretary for Veterans' Employment and Training (OASVET). OASVET's general regulations are located in chapter IX of this title. [54 FR 39354, Sept. 26, 1989]
