CFR /  Title 18  /  Part 806  /  Sec. 806.13 Submission of application.

Sponsors of projects subject to review and approval of the Commission under Sec. Sec. 806.4, 806.5 or 806.6, or project sponsors seeking renewal of an existing approval of the Commission, shall submit an application and applicable fee to the Commission, in accordance with this subpart. [77 FR 8099, Feb. 14, 2012]
