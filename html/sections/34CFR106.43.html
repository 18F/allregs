CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 106  /  Sec. 106.43 Standards for measuring skill or progress in physical

If use of a single standard of measuring skill or progress in physical education classes has an adverse effect on members of one sex, the recipient shall use appropriate standards that do not have that effect. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1681, 1682) [71 FR 62543, Oct. 25, 2006] Subpart E_Discrimination on the Basis of Sex in Employment in Education
