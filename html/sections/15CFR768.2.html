CFR /  Title 15  /  Part 768  /  Sec. 768.2 Foreign availability described.

(a) and 3(2)(A) of the EAA.)

(b) Types of foreign availability. There are two types of foreign availability:

(1) Foreign availability to a controlled country; and

(2) Foreign availability to a non-controlled country.

Note to paragraph (b) of this section: See Sec. 768.7 of this part for delineation of the foreign availability assessment procedures, and Sec. 768.6 of this part for the criteria used in determining foreign availability.
