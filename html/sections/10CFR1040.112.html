CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 1040  /  Sec. 1040.112 Noncompliance with assurances.

If an applicant fails or refuses to furnish an assurance required under Sec. 1040.4 of subpart A of this part, or otherwise fails or refuses to comply with a requirement imposed by this part, such as Sec. 1040.102(c), subpart G of this part, action to refuse Federal financial assistance shall be taken in accordance with procedures of Sec. 1040.114 of this subpart.
