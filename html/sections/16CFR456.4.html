CFR /  Title 16  /  Part 456  /  Sec. 456.4 Declaration of Commission Intent.

In prohibiting the use of waivers and disclaimers of liability in Sec. 456.2(d), it is not the Commission's intent to impose liability on an ophthalmologist or optometrist for the ophthalmic goods and services dispensed by another seller pursuant to the ophthalmologist's or optometrist's prescription.
