CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 983  /  Sec. 983.57 Substandard pistachios.

The committee shall, with the approval of the Secretary, establish such reporting and disposition procedures as it deems necessary to ensure that pistachios which do not meet aflatoxin and quality requirements are not shipped for human consumption in those markets for which such requirements exist pursuant to Sec. 983.50 and Sec. 983.51. [77 FR 36123, June 18, 2012]
