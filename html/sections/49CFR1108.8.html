CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 1108  /  Sec. 1108.8 Relief.

(a) Relief available. An arbitrator may grant relief in the form of monetary damages to the extent they are available under this part or as agreed to in writing by the parties.

(b) Relief not available. No injunctive relief shall be available in Board arbitration proceedings.
