CFR /  Title 44  /  Part 7  /  Sec. 7.926 Age distinctions contained in FEMA regulations.

Any age distinctions contained in a rule or regulation issued by FEMA shall be presumed to be necessary to the achievement of a statutory objective of the program or activity to which the rule or regulation applies, notwithstanding the provisions of Sec. 7.921. [55 FR 23078, June 6, 1990, as amended at 68 FR 51380, Aug. 26, 2003]
