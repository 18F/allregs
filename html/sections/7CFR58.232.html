CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 58  /  Sec. 58.232 Milk.

Raw milk shall meet the requirements as outlined in Sec. Sec. 58.132 through 58.138 and, unless processed within two hours after being received, it shall be cooled to and held at a temperature of 45 F. or lower until processed.
