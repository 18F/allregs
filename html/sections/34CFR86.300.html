CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 86  /  Sec. 86.300 What constitutes a violation of this part by an IHE?

An IHE violates this part by--

(a) Receiving any form of Federal financial assistance after becoming ineligible to receive that assistance because of failure to submit a certification in accordance with Sec. 86.3(b); or

(b) Violating its certification. Violation of a certification includes failure of an IHE to--

(1) Adopt or implement its drug prevention program; or

(2) Consistently enforce its disciplinary sanctions for violations by students and employees of the standards of conduct adopted by an IHE under Sec. 86.100(a)(1). (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1145g) [55 FR 33581, Aug. 16, 1990, as amended at 61 FR 66226, Dec. 17, 1996]
