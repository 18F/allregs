CFR /  Title 25  /  Part 162  /  Sec. 162.535 What effectiveness, compliance, and enforcement provisions apply to WEELs?

(a) The provisions at Sec. 162.586 apply to WEEL lease documents.

(b) The provisions at Sec. Sec. 162.587 through 162.589 and 162.591 through 162.599 apply to WEELs, except that any references to Sec. 162.590 will apply instead to Sec. 162.536.
