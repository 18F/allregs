CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 57  /  Sec. 57.04-1 Test specimen requirements and definition of ranges

The type and number of specimens that must be tested to qualify an automatic, semiautomatic, or manual procedure specification shall be in accordance with QW 202, QW 210, or QB 202 of the ASME Code as applicable, except as supplemented by Sec. Sec. 57.03-1(b) and 57.03-1(d). [CGD 74-102, 40 FR 27461, June 30, 1975]
