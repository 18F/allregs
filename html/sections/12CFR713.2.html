CFR /  Title 12  /  Part 713  /  Sec. 713.2 What are the responsibilities of a credit union's board

The board of directors of each Federal credit union must at least annually review its fidelity and other insurance coverage to ensure that it is adequate in relation to the potential risks facing the credit union and the minimum requirements set by the Board. [64 FR 28720, May 27, 1999, as amended at 64 FR 57365, Oct. 25, 1999]
