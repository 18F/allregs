CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 17  /  Sec. 17.280 Enforcement procedures.

The compliance and enforcement provisions applicable to title VI of the Civil Rights Act of 1964 apply to this subpart. These procedures are found in 43 CFR part 17, subpart A, Sec. Sec. 17.5-17.11 and 43 CFR part 4, subpart I.
