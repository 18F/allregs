CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 6  /  Sec. 6.8 Representation at Tariff Commission hearings.

The Department of Agriculture shall be represented at all hearings conducted by the Tariff Commission under section 22 by persons designated by the Administrator, assisted by a representative of the Office of the General Counsel. Such representatives shall present the recommendations of the Department of Agriculture, shall submit such information and data in support thereof as are available, and shall exercise the right of examining other witnesses which is granted to the Secretary. [17 FR 8287, Sept. 16, 1952; 20 FR 1830, Mar. 25, 1955]
