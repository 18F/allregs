CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 100  /  Sec. 100.2 Scope.

The siting requirements contained in this part apply to applications for site approval for the purpose of constructing and operating stationary power and testing reactors pursuant to the provisions of part 50 or part 52 of this chapter. [61 FR 65175, Dec. 11, 1996]
