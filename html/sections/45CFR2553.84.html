CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 2553  /  Sec. 2553.84 What happens if a non-Corporation funded sponsor does not

A non-Corporation funded project sponsor's noncompliance with the Memorandum of Agreement may result in suspension or termination of the Corporation's agreement and all benefits specified in Sec. 2553.82.
