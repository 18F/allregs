CFR /  Title 15  /  Part 2  /  Sec. 2.6 Payment of claims.

When an award is made, the file on the case shall be transmitted to the appropriate fiscal office for payment by the Department or for transmittal for payment as prescribed by Sec. 14.10 of the Regulations (28 CFR Part 14). Prior to payment appropriate releases shall be obtained, as provided in said section. [32 FR 3769, Mar. 7, 1967]
