CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 154  /  Sec. 154.440 Allowable stress.

(a) The allowable stresses for an independent tank type A must:

(1) For tank web frames, stringers, or girders of carbon manganese steel or aluminum alloys, meet [sigma]B/2.66 or [sigma]Y/1.33, whichever is less; and

(2) For other materials, be specially approved by the Commandant (CG-ENG).

(b) A greater allowable stress than required in paragraph (a)(1) of this section may be specially approved by the Commandant (CG-ENG) if the equivalent stress ([sigma]c) is calculated from the formula in appendix A of this part.

(c) Tank plating must meet the American Bureau of Shipping's deep tank standards, for an internal pressure head that meets Sec. 154.439(a), published in ``Rules for Building and Classing Steel Vessels'', 1981.
