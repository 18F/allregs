CFR /  Title 8  /  Part 274  /  Sec. 274.2 Delegation of authority.

All powers provided to Fines, Penalties and Forfeitures Officers in 19 CFR parts 162 and 171 are provided to the Chief, Office of Border Patrol or his designees, for purposes of administering seizures and forfeitures made by Border Patrol Officers. [73 FR 9011, Feb. 19, 2008]
