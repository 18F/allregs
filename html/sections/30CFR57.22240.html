CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 57  /  Sec. 57.22240 Actions at 2.0 percent methane (V-A mines).

If methane reaches 2.0 percent in the mine atmosphere, all persons other than competent persons necessary to make ventilation changes shall be withdrawn from affected areas until methane is reduced to less than 1.0 percent. [52 FR 24941, July 1, 1987; 52 FR 27903, July 24, 1987]
