CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 173  /  Sec. 173.290 Trichloroethylene.

Tolerances are established for residues of trichloroethylene resulting from its use as a solvent in the manufacture of foods as follows: ------------------------------------------------------------------------

------------------------------------------------------------------------Decaffeinated ground coffee............... 25 parts per million.Decaffeinated soluble (instant) coffee 10 parts per million.
