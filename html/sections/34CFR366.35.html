CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 366  /  Sec. 366.35 What grants must be made to existing eligible agencies?

In accordance with the order of priorities established in Sec. 366.34(a), an eligible agency may receive a grant under this subpart if the eligible agency meets the applicable requirements in Secs. 366.2, 366.21, and 366.23. (Authority: 29 U.S.C. 796f-2(c))
