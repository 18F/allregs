CFR /  Title 15  /  Part 29  /  Sec. 29.105 Does this part apply to me?

(a) Portions of this part apply to you if you are either--

(1) A recipient of an assistance award from the Department of Commerce; or

(2) A(n) DoC awarding official. (See definitions of award and recipient in Sec. Sec. 29.605 and 29.660, respectively.)
