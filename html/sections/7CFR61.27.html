CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 61  /  Sec. 61.27 Period of license; renewals.

The period for which a license may be issued under the regulations in Secs. 61.25 through 61.42 shall be from the first day of August following receipt of the application, and shall continue for 5 years, ending on the 31st of July in the fifth year. Renewals shall be for 5 years also, beginning with the first day of August and ending on the 31st day of July in the fifth year: Provided, That licenses or renewals issued on and after June 1 of any year shall be for the period ending July 31 of the fifth year following. [59 FR 26411, May 20, 1994]
