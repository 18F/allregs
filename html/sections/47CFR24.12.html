CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 24  /  Sec. 24.12 Eligibility.

Any entity, other than those precluded by section 310 of the Communications Act of 1934, as amended, 47 U.S.C. 310, is eligible to hold a license under this part. [70 FR 61059, Oct. 20, 3005]
