CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 96  /  Sec. 96.33 Referral of cases to the Inspector General.

State or tribal officials who have information indicating the commission or potential commission of fraud or other offenses against the United States involving block grant funds should promptly provide the information to the appropriate Regional Office of Investigations of the Department's Office of the Inspector General. [52 FR 37966, Oct. 13, 1987]
