CFR /  Title 32  /  Part 776  /  Sec. 776.5 Judicial conduct.

To the extent that it does not conflict with statutes, regulations, or this part, the American Bar Association's Code of Judicial Conduct applies to all military and appellate judges and to all other covered USG attorneys performing judicial functions under JAG supervision within the DON.
