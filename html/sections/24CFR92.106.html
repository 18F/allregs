CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 92  /  Sec. 92.106 Continuous designation as a participating jurisdiction.

Once a State or unit of general local government is designated a participating jurisdiction, it remains a participating jurisdiction for subsequent fiscal years and the requirements of Sec. Sec. 92.102 through 92.105 do not apply, unless HUD revokes the designation in accordance with Sec. 92.107.
