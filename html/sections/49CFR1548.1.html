CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 1548  /  Sec. 1548.1 Applicability of this part.

This part prescribes aviation security rules governing each indirect air carrier engaged indirectly in the air transportation of property on aircraft. [67 FR 8382, Feb. 22, 2002, as amended at 71 FR 33255, June 8, 2006]
