CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 130  /  Sec. 130.250 Mooring and towing equipment for OSVs of less than

Each OSV of less than 100 gross tons must be fitted with mooring and towing equipment meeting the applicable requirements for small passenger vessels in Sec. 184.300 of this chapter.
