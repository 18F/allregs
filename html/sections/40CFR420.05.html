CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 420  /  Sec. 420.05 Pretreatment standards compliance date.

The final compliance date for the categorical pretreatment standards set forth in 40 CFR part 420 is July 10, 1985. [48 FR 46943, Oct. 14, 1983]
