CFR /  Title 9  /  Part 146  /  Sec. 146.7 Terminology and classification; general.

The official classification terms defined in Sec. Sec. 146.8 and 146.9 and the various designs illustrative of the official classifications reproduced in Sec. 146.9 may be used only by participants and to describe products that have met all of the specific requirements of such classifications.
