CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 21  /  Sec. 21.348 Leave following completion of a period of training or

(a) Leave following completion of training or rehabilitation services. Leave may not be approved following completion of a period of rehabilitation services described in Sec. 21.340(a).

(b) Postponement of the date of completion of a period of rehabilitation services prohibited. The date of completion of the veteran's program may not be extended for the purpose of allowing the veteran to use leave. (Authority: 38 U.S.C. 3110)
