CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 1336  /  Sec. 1336.30 Eligibility under sections 804 and 805 of the Native

Financial assistance under sections 804 and 805 may be made to public or private agencies including ``for-profit'' organizations. [48 FR 55821, Dec. 15, 1983, as amended at 53 FR 23968, June 24, 1988; 53 FR 28223, July 27, 1988; 54 FR 3452, Jan. 24, 1989; 61 FR 42820, Aug. 19, 1996]
