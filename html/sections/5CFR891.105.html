CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 891  /  Sec. 891.105 Correction of errors.

OPM may order correction of administrative errors at any time upon a showing satisfactory to OPM that it would be against equity and good conscience not to do so. [45 FR 23637, Apr. 8, 1980]
