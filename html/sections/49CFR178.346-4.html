CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 178  /  Sec. 178.346-4 Outlets.

(a) All outlets on each tank must conform to Sec. 178.345-11 and this section.

(b) External self-closing stop-valves are not authorized as an alternative to internal self-closing stop-valves on loading/unloading outlets. [Amdt. 178-89, 54 FR 25029, June 12, 1989. Redesignated by Amdt. 178-112, 61 FR 18934, Apr. 29, 1996]
