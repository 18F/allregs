CFR /  Title 31  /  Part 543  /  Sec. 543.406 Offshore transactions.

The prohibitions in Sec. 543.201 on transactions or dealings involving blocked property apply to transactions by any U.S. person in a location outside the United States with respect to property held in the name of a person whose property and interests in property are blocked pursuant to Sec. 543.201(a), or property in which a person whose property and interests in property are blocked pursuant to Sec. 543.201(a) has or has had an interest since the effective date.
