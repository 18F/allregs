CFR /  Title 27  /  Part 71  /  Sec. 71.63 Suspension, revocation, or annulment proceedings.

If the respondent does not request a hearing within the time specified in Sec. 71.60, and does not file an answer as required in Sec. 71.64, the appropriate TTB officer shall make the initial decision in the case in accordance with Sec. 71.79. [T.D. ATF-244, 51 FR 45763, Dec. 22, 1986, as amended by T.D. ATF-374, 61 FR 29957, June 13, 1996]
