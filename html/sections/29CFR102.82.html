CFR /  Title 29  /  Part 102  /  Sec. 102.82 Transfer, consolidation, and severance.

The provisions of Sec. Sec. 102.33 and 102.72, respecting the filing of a charge or petition with the general counsel and the transfer, consolidation, and severance of proceedings, shall apply to proceedings under this subpart, except that the provisions of Sec. Sec. 102.73 to 102.81, inclusive, shall govern proceedings before the general counsel.
