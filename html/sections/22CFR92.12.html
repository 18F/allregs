CFR /  Title 22  /  Part 92  /  Sec. 92.12 Necessity for certification of notarial acts.

A notarizing officer must execute a written certificate attesting to the performance of a notarial act. This certificate may be inserted on or appended to the notarized document (see Sec. 92.17 regarding the fastening of sheets). The certificate evidences the performance of the notarial act. Failure to execute this certificate renders the notarial act legally ineffective. Each notarial act should be evidenced by a separate certificate; two or more distinct notarial acts should not be attested to by one certificate. [22 FR 10858, Dec. 27, 1957, as amended at 60 FR 51721, Oct. 3, 1995]
