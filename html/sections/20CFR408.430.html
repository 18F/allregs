CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 408  /  Sec. 408.430 When do you need to give us evidence of your other

If you tell us or if we have information indicating that you are receiving other benefit income that could affect your qualification for or the amount of your SVB payments, we will ask you to give us evidence of that income as explained in Sec. 408.432.
