CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 675  /  Sec. 675.49 Procedures and records.

In administering a Work-Colleges program under this subpart, an institution shall comply with the applicable provisions of 34 CFR part 673 and this part 675. (Authority: 42 U.S.C. 2756b) [59 FR 61418, Nov. 30, 1994, as amended at 61 FR 60396, Nov. 27, 1996]
