CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 290  /  Sec. 290.1 Controlled substances.

Any drug that is a controlled substance listed in schedule II, III, IV, or V of the Federal Controlled Substances Act or implementing regulations must be dispensed by prescription only as required by section 503(b)(1) of the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act unless specifically exempted in Sec. 290.2. [67 FR 4906, Feb. 1, 2002]
