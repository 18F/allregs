CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 164  /  Sec. 164.120-15 Procedure for acceptance of material change.

(a) Each change in material from the resin accepted under Sec. 164.120-9 of this subpart must be accepted by the Commandant before being used in any production lifeboat or rescue boat. The manufacturer must submit any such change following the procedures set forth in Sec. 164.120-9 of this subpart, but documentation on items that are unchanged from the resin accepted under Sec. 164.120-9 of this subpart need not be resubmitted.

(b) Determinations of equivalence of materials will be made by the Commandant only.
