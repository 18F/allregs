CFR /  Title 9  /  Part 355  /  Sec. 355.37 Alteration or limitation of statement of certification.

The statement of certification provided for by Sec. 355.32(a)(4) shall not be altered, defaced, imitated, or simulated in any respect or used for the purpose of misrepresentation or deception. [25 FR 1357, Feb. 16, 1960. Redesignated at 30 FR 4195, Mar. 31, 1965]
