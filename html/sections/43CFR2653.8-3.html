CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 2653  /  Sec. 2653.8-3 Appeals.

Appeals from decisions made by the Bureau of Land Management on applications filed pursuant to section 14(h)(5) of the Act shall be made to the Board of Land Appeals in accordance with 43 CFR part 4, subpart E. [41 FR 14740, Apr. 7, 1976]
