CFR /  Title 12  /  Part 370  /  Sec. 370.9 Recordkeeping requirements.

The FDIC will establish procedures, require reports, and require participating entities to provide and preserve any information needed for the operation and supervision of this program. [74 FR 12085, Mar. 23, 2009]
