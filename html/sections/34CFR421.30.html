CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 421  /  Sec. 421.30 What is the cost-sharing requirement?

(a) The Secretary pays no more than 50 percent of the cost of a project.

(b) Each recipient of an award under this part shall provide at least 50 percent of the cost of the business-labor-education technical committees established under the award. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 2416(c))
