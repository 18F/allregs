CFR /  Title 29  /  Part 1955  /  Sec. 1955.45 Effect of appeal of administrative law judge's decision.

An administrative law judge's decision shall be stayed pending a decision on appeal to the Secretary. If there are no exceptions filed to the decisions of the administrative law judge, the administrative law judge's decision shall be published in the Federal Register as a final decision and served upon the parties.
