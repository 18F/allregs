CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 413  /  Sec. 413.04 Standards for integrated facilities.

Pretreatment standards for integrated facilities shall be computed as required by Sec. 403.6(e) of EPA's General Pretreatment Regulations. In cases where electroplating process wastewaters are combined with regulated wastewaters which have 30 days average standards, the corresponding 30 day average standard for the electroplating wastewaters must be used.
