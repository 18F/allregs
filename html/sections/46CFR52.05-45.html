CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 52  /  Sec. 52.05-45 Circumferential joints in pipes, tubes and headers

(a) Circumferential welded joints of pipes, tubes and headers shall be as required by PW-41 of section I of the ASME Boiler and Pressure Vessel Code (incorporated by reference; see 46 CFR 52.01-1) except as noted otherwise in this section.

(b) (Modifies PW-41.1) Circumferential welded joints in pipes, tubes, and headers of pipe material must be nondestructively examined as required by Sec. 56.95-10 of this subchapter and PW-41 of section I of the ASME Boiler and Pressure Vessel Code.

(c) (Modifies PW-41.5) Butt welded connections shall be provided whenever radiography is required by Sec. 56.95-10 of this subchapter for the piping system in which the connection is to be made. When radiography is not required, welded socket or sleeve type joints meeting the requirements of PW-41.5 of section I of the ASME Boiler and Pressure Vessel Code may be provided.
