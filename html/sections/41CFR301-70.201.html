CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 301-70  /  Sec. 301-70.201 May we issue a blanket actual expense authorization

Yes. A blanket authorization regarding actual expense reimbursement may be issued to your employees assigned to perform TDY travel in an area subject to a Presidentially-Declared Disaster. These authorizations must apply to a specific Declaration, and must end on the expiration date of the Declaration, or one year from the date the Declaration is issued, whichever is sooner. A blanket authorization issued under this section shall not apply to any travel performed pursuant to Chapter 302 of this title. [FTR Amdt. 2011-03, 76 FR 55275, Sept. 7, 2011]
