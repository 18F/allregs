CFR /  Title 19  /  Part 206  /  Sec. 206.1 Applicability of part.

Part 206 applies to proceedings of the Commission under sections 201-202, 204, 406, and 421-422 of the Trade Act of 1974, as amended (2251-2252, 2254, 2436, 2451-2451a), sections 301-317 of the North American Free Trade Agreement Implementation Act (19 U.S.C. 3351-3382) (hereinafter NAFTA Implementation Act), and the statutory provisions listed in Sec. 206.31 of this part 206 that implement bilateral safeguard provisions in other free trade agreements into which the United States has entered. [77 FR 37805, June 25, 2012]
