CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 409  /  Sec. 409.40 Basis, purpose, and scope.

This subpart implements sections 1814(a)(2)(C), 1835(a)(2)(A), and 1861(m) of the Act with respect to the requirements that must be met for Medicare payment to be made for home health services furnished to eligible beneficiaries. [59 FR 65493, Dec. 20, 1994]
