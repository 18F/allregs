CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 110  /  Sec. 110.75 Corpus Christi Bay, Tex.

(a) South area. Southward of the southernmost T-head pier at the foot of Cooper Avenue and of a line bearing 156[deg]44[min], 340.6 feet, from the southerly corner of said pier to a point on the rubble breakwater; westward and northward of said breakwater; and eastward of the Corpus Christi sea wall.
