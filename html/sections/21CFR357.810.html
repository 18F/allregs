CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 357  /  Sec. 357.810 Active ingredients for deodorant drug products for

The active ingredient of the product consists of either of the following when used within the dosage limits established for each ingredient in Sec. 357.850(d):

(a) Bismuth subgallate.

(b) Chlorophyllin copper complex.
