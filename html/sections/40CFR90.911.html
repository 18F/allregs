CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 90  /  Sec. 90.911 Submission of exemption requests.

Requests for exemption or further information concerning exemptions and/or the exemption request review procedure should be addressed to: Group Manager, Engine Compliance Programs Group, Engine Programs and Compliance Division (6403J), Environmental Protection Agency, Washington, DC 20460. [64 FR 15252, Mar. 30, 1999]
