CFR /  Title 22  /  Part 62  /  Sec. 62.61 Revocation.

The Department may terminate a sponsor's program designation by revocation for cause as specified in Sec. 62.50. Such sponsor may not apply for a new designation for five (5) years following the effective date of the revocation.
