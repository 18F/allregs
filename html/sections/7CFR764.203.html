CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 764  /  Sec. 764.203 Limitations.

(a) The applicant must:

(1) Comply with the general limitations established at Sec. 764.102; and

(2) Provide a minimum downpayment of 5 percent of the purchase price of the farm.

(b) Downpayment loans will not exceed 45 percent of the lesser of:

(1) The purchase price,

(2) The appraised value of the farm to be acquired, or

(3) $667,000; subject to the direct FO dollar limit specified in 7 CFR 761.8(a)(1)(i).

(c) Financing provided by the Agency and all other creditors must not exceed 95 percent of the purchase price. Financing provided by eligible lenders may be guaranteed by the Agency under part 762 of this chapter.
