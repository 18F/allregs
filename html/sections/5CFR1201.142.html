CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 1201  /  Sec. 1201.142 Actions filed by administrative law judges.

An administrative law judge who alleges a constructive removal or other action by an agency in violation of 5 U.S.C. 7521 may file a complaint with the Board under this subpart. The filing and service requirements of Sec. 1201.137 of this part apply. Such complaints shall be adjudicated in the same manner as agency complaints under this subpart. [77 FR 62370, Oct. 12, 2012]
