CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 1812  /  Sec. 1812.1-1 Regulations governing practice before the Department.

Every individual who wishes to practice before the Department of the Interior, including the Bureau, must comply with the requirements of part 1 of this title. [35 FR 9513, June 13, 1970]
