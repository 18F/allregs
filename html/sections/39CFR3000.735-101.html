CFR /  Title 39  /  Part 3000  /  Sec. 3000.735-101 Cross-reference to employee ethical conduct standards

Employees of the Postal Regulatory Commission (Commission) are subject and should refer to the executive branch-wide Standards of Ethical Conduct at 5 CFR part 2635, the Commission regulation at 5 CFR part 5601 which supplements the executive branch-wide standards, and the executive branch-wide financial disclosure regulation at 5 CFR part 2634. [58 FR 42874, Aug. 12, 1993, as amended at 72 FR 33165, June 15, 2007]
