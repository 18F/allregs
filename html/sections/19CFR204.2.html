CFR /  Title 19  /  Part 204  /  Sec. 204.2 Investigations.

The Commission will make an investigation for the purposes of section 22(a) of the Agricultural Adjustment Act, as amended, only upon request of the President. \2\---------------------------------------------------------------------------

\2\ Applications for investigations for the purposes of section 22 of this Agricultural Adjustment Act, as amended, must be filed with the Secretary of Agriculture (Executive Order 7233). [27 FR 12121, Dec. 7, 1962, as amended at 68 FR 32977, June 3, 2003]
