CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 261  /  Sec. 261.400 Applicability.

The requirements of this subpart apply to those areas of an entity managing hazardous secondary materials excluded under Sec. 261.4(a)(23) and/or (24) where hazardous secondary materials are generated or accumulated on site.

(a) A generator of hazardous secondary material, or an intermediate or reclamation facility operating under a verified recycler variance under Sec. 260.31(d), that accumulates 6000 kg or less of hazardous secondary material at any time must comply with Secs. 261.410 and 261.411.

(b) A generator of hazardous secondary material, or an intermediate or reclamation facility operating under a verified recycler variance under Sec. 260.31(d) that accumulates more than 6000 kg of hazardous secondary material at any time must comply with Secs. 261.410 and 261.420.
