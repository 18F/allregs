CFR /  Title 27  /  Part 19  /  Sec. 19.680 Registration of stills.

The description of stills provided with the application for an alcohol fuel plant permit under this subpart will fulfill the requirement to register a still under Sec. 29.55 of this chapter. (26 U.S.C. 5179, 5181)
