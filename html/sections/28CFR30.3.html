CFR /  Title 28  /  Part 30  /  Sec. 30.3 What programs and activities of the Department are subject to

The Attorney General publishes in the Federal Register a list of the Department's programs and activities that are subject to these regulations and identifies which of these are subject to the requirements of section 204 of the Demonstration Cities and Metropolitan Development Act.
