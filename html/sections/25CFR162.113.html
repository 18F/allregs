CFR /  Title 25  /  Part 162  /  Sec. 162.113 May decisions under this subpart be appealed?

Yes. Except where otherwise provided in this subpart, appeals from decisions by the BIA under this subpart may be taken pursuant to 25 CFR subpart 2. [66 FR 7109, Jan. 22, 2001, as amended at 77 FR 72474, Dec. 5, 2012]
