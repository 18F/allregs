CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 236  /  236.602-1 Selection criteria.

(a) Establish the evaluation criteria before making the public announcement required by FAR 5.205(d) and include the criteria and their relative order of importance in the announcement. Follow the procedures at PGI 236.602-1(a). [69 FR 75000, Dec. 15, 2004, as amended at 71 FR 53044, Sept. 8, 2006]
