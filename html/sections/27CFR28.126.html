CFR /  Title 27  /  Part 28  /  Sec. 28.126 Proprietor's report.

The records of the proprietor of the bonded wine cellar shall reflect the quantity of wine removed without payment of tax under this subpart, and he shall report the quantity of wine so removed on TTB F 5120.17. (72 Stat. 1380; 26 U.S.C. 5362) [25 FR 5734, June 23, 1960. Redesignated at 40 FR 16835, Apr. 15, 1975, and amended by T.D. ATF-299, 55 FR 25033, June 19, 1990; T.D. TTB-8, 69 FR 3833, Jan. 27, 2004]
