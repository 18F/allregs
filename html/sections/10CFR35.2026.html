CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 35  /  Sec. 35.2026 Records of radiation protection program changes.

A licensee shall retain a record of each radiation protection program change made in accordance with Sec. 35.26(a) for 5 years. The record must include a copy of the old and new procedures; the effective date of the change; and the signature of the licensee management that reviewed and approved the change.
