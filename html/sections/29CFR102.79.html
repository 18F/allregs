CFR /  Title 29  /  Part 102  /  Sec. 102.79 Consent-election agreements.

Where a petition has been duly filed, the parties involved may, subject to the approval of the regional director, enter into an agreement governing the method of conducting the election as provided for in Sec. 102.62(a), insofar as applicable.
