CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 64  /  Sec. 64.609 Enforcement of related customer premises equipment rules.

Enforcement of Sec. Sec. 64.607 and 64.608 is delegated to those state public utility or public service commissions which adopt those sections and provide for their enforcement.
