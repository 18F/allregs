CFR /  Title 29  /  Part 500  /  Sec. 500.56 Replacement of Certificate of Registration or Farm Labor

If a Certificate of Registration or a Farm Labor Contractor Employee Certificate is lost or destroyed, a duplicate certificate may be obtained by the submission to the regional office that issued it or to any regional office of the Wage and Hour Division, Employment Standards Administration, of a written statement explaining its loss or destruction, indicating where the original application was filed and requesting that a duplicate be issued.
