CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 426  /  Sec. 426.515 CMS' role in making the NCD record available.

CMS will provide a copy of the NCD record (as described in Sec. 426.518) to the Board and all parties to the NCD review within 30 days of the receipt of the Board's order.
