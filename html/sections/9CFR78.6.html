CFR /  Title 9  /  Part 78  /  Sec. 78.6 Steers and spayed heifers.

Steers and spayed heifers may be moved interstate in accordance with 9 CFR part 86 and without further restriction under this subpart. [78 FR 2069, Jan. 9, 2013]
