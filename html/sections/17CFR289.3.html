CFR /  Title 17  /  Part 289  /  Sec. 289.3 Reports with respect to proposed distribution of primary

The IFC shall file with the Commission, on or prior to the date on which it sells any of its primary obligations in connection with a distribution of such obligations in the United States, a report containing the information and documents specified in Schedule A of this part. The term ``sell'' as used in this section and in Schedule A of this Part means a completed sale, or a firm commitment to sell to an underwriter.
