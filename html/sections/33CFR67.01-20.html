CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 67  /  Sec. 67.01-20 Prescribing lines of demarcation.

The District Commander sends recommendations for establishing or changing lines of demarcation to the Commandant. For the purposes of this part, when the Commandant approves of additions to or changes in prescribed lines of demarcation, such additions or changes will be published in the Federal Register and will become effective on the date specified in that publication. [USCG-2001-10714, 69 FR 24983, May 5, 2004]
