CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 73  /  Sec. 73.3545 Application for permit to deliver programs to foreign

Application under section 325(c) of the Communications Act for authority to locate, use, or maintain a broadcast studio in connection with a foreign station consistently received in the United States, should be made on FCC Form 308, ``Application for Permit to Deliver Programs to Foreign Broadcast Stations.'' An informal application may be used by applicants holding an AM, FM or TV broadcast station license or construction permit. Informal applications must, however, contain a description of the nature and character of the programming proposed, together with other information requested on Page 4 of Form 308. [44 FR 38497, July 2, 1979, as amended at 58 FR 51250, Oct. 1, 1993]
