CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 970  /  970.5001-4 Contract clause.

When use of the clause at 48 CFR 52.250-1, Indemnification Under Public Law 85-804, Alternate 1 is appropriate, the contracting officer may substitute the words ``Obligation of funds'' for the words ``Limitation of Cost or Limitation of Funds.'' [65 FR 81009, Dec. 22, 2000. Redesignated and amended at 74 FR 36374, July 22, 2009]
