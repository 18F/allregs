CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 51  /  Sec. 51.2555 General.

(a) Compliance with the provisions of these standards shall not excuse failure to comply with provisions of applicable Federal or State laws.

(b) These standards are applicable to raw, roasted, salted or salted/roasted pistachio kernels. [55 FR 28747, July 13, 1990, as amended at 68 FR 50685, Aug. 22, 2003]
