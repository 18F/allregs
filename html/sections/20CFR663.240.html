CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 663  /  Sec. 663.240 Are there particular intensive services an individual 134(d)(4)(A)(i)?

(a) Yes, at a minimum, an individual must receive at least one intensive service, such as development of an individual employment plan with a case manager or individual counseling and career planning, before the individual may receive training services.

(b) The case file must contain a determination of need for training services under Sec. 663.310, as identified in the individual employment plan, comprehensive assessment, or through any other intensive service received.
