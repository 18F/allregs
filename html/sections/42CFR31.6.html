CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 31  /  Sec. 31.6 Personnel absent without leave.

No member of any of the services enumerated in Sec. 31.2 shall be entitled when absent without leave to medical relief except at a medical relief station or by a designated physician or designated dentist. (Sec. 326, 58 Stat. 697, as amended; 42 U.S.C. 253)
