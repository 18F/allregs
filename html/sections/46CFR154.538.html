CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 154  /  Sec. 154.538 Cargo transfer connection.

A cargo transfer connection must have a:

(a) Remotely controlled quick-closing shut-off valve that meets Sec. Sec. 154.540 and 154.544; or

(b) Blank flange.
