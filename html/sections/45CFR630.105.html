CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 630  /  Sec. 630.105 Does this part apply to me?

(a) Portions of this part apply to you if you are either--

(1) A recipient of an assistance award from the National Science Foundation; or

(2) A(n) National Science Foundation awarding official. (See definitions of award and recipient in Sec. Sec. 630.605 and 630.660, respectively.)
