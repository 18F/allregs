CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 2540  /  Sec. 2540.200 What does ``you'' mean in this section?

As used in this section, ``you'' means a Corporation grantee or other entity subject to Corporation grant provisions. Unless the context otherwise requires, this includes, but is not limited to, recipients of federal financial assistance under grant programs defined in Sec. 2510.20 of this chapter as well as projects under the Senior Companion Program, the Foster Grandparent Program, and RSVP. [77 FR 60932, Oct. 5, 2012]
