CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 161  /  Sec. 161.17 Definitions.

As used in this subpart:

Center means a Vessel Traffic Center or Vessel Movement Center.

Published means available in a widely-distributed and publicly available medium (e.g., VTS User's Manual, ferry schedule, Notice to Mariners). [USCG-2003-14757, 68 FR 39366, July 1, 2003]
