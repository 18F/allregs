CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 1355  /  Sec. 1355.54 Submittal of advance planning documents.

The title IV-E agency must submit an APD for a statewide automated child welfare information system, signed by the appropriate official, in accordance with procedures specified by 45 CFR part 95, subpart F. [77 FR 934, Jan. 6, 2012]
