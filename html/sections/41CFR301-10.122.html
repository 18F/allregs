CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 301-10  /  Sec. 301-10.122 What class of airline accommodations must I use?

For official business travel, both domestic and international, you must use coach-class accommodations, except as provided under Sec. Sec. 301-10.123 and 301-10.124.
