CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 906  /  Sec. 906.235 Assessment rate.

On and after August 1, 2014, an assessment rate of $0.11 per 7/10-bushel carton or equivalent is established for oranges and grapefruit grown in the Lower Rio Grande Valley in Texas. [79 FR 47553, Aug. 14, 2014]
