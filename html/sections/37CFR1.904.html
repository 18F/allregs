CFR /  Title 37  /  Part 1  /  Sec. 1.904 Notice of inter partes reexamination in Official Gazette.

A notice of the filing of an inter partes reexamination request will be published in the Official Gazette. The notice published in the Official Gazette under Sec. 1.11(c) will be considered to be constructive notice of the inter partes reexamination proceeding and inter partes reexamination will proceed.
