CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 109-45  /  Sec. 109-45.309 Special classes of property. Sec. 109-45.309-2.50 Hazardous property.

Hazardous property shall be made available for sale only after the review and certification requirements of Sec. 109-43.307-2.50 of this subpart have been met.
