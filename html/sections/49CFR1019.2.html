CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 1019  /  Sec. 1019.2 Interpretation and advisory service.

(a) The Board's Executive Counsel shall be the Board's Designated Agency Ethics Official (DAEO).

(b) By June 30 of each year, the DAEO shall report to the Board on the operation of the Board's ethics program with any recommendations that the DAEO deems advisable. [58 FR 42027, Aug. 6, 1993, as amended at 64 FR 53267, Oct. 1, 1999]
