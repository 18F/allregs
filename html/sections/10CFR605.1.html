CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 605  /  Sec. 605.1 Purpose and scope.

This part sets forth the policies and procedures applicable to the award and administration of grants and cooperative agreements by the DOE Office of Science (SC) for basic and applied research, educational and/or training activities, conferences and related activities. [57 FR 40583, Sept. 3, 1992, as amended at 79 FR 76046, Dec. 19, 2014]
