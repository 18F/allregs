CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 28  /  Sec. 28.955 Disposition of samples.

The remnants of samples accumulated in the making of tests under the regulations in this subpart shall become the property of the Government unless the applicant requests that such remnants be returned. Returns will be at the applicant's expense. [46 FR 30073, June 5, 1981; 46 FR 51593, Oct. 21, 1981]
