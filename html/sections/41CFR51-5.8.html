CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 51-5  /  Sec. 51-5.8 Violations by entities of the Government.

Any alleged violations of the JWOD Act or these regulations by entities of the Government shall be investigated by the Committee, which shall notify the entity and afford it an opportunity to submit a statement. [56 FR 48981, Sept. 26, 1991, as amended at 59 FR 59343, Nov. 16, 1994]
