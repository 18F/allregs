CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 105  /  Sec. 105.20-5 Piping systems.

(a) Piping shall be copper, nickel copper, or copper nickel having a minimum wall thickness of 0.035; except that seamless steel pipe or tubing which provides equivalent safety may be used for diesel cargo systems.

(b) Valves shall be of a suitable nonferrous metallic Union Bonnet type with ground seats except that steel or nodular iron may be used in cargo systems utilizing steel pipe or tubing.

(c) Aluminum or aluminum alloy valves and fittings are prohibited for use in cargo lines. [CGFR 69-53, 34 FR 11265, July 4, 1969, as amended by USCG-2014-0688, 79 FR 58283, Sept. 29, 2014]
