CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 86  /  Sec. 86.11 Application.

Except as provided in this subpart, this part 86 applies to every recipient and to the education program or activity operated by such recipient which receives Federal financial assistance. (Secs. 901, 902, Education Amendments of 1972, 86 Stat. 373, 374; 20 U.S.C. 1681, 1682) [40 FR 24137, June 4, 1975, as amended at 70 FR 24321, May 9, 2005]
