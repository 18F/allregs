CFR /  Title 17  /  Part 39  /  Sec. 39.23 Antitrust considerations.

Unless necessary or appropriate to achieve the purposes of the Act, a derivatives clearing organization shall not adopt any rule or take any action that results in any unreasonable restraint of trade, or impose any material anticompetitive burden. Sec. Sec. 39.24-39.26 [Reserved]
