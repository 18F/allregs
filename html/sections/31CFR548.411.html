CFR /  Title 31  /  Part 548  /  Sec. 548.411 Entities owned by a person whose property and interests in

A person whose property and interests in property are blocked pursuant to Sec. 548.201(a) has an interest in all property and interests in property of an entity in which it owns, directly or indirectly, a 50 percent or greater interest. The property and interests in property of such an entity, therefore, are blocked, and such an entity is a person whose property and interests in property are blocked pursuant to Sec. 548.201(a), regardless of whether the entity itself is listed in the Annex to Executive Order 13405 or designated pursuant to Sec. 548.201(a).
