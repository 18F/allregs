CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 570  /  570.801 Standard forms.

Use Standard Form 2, U.S. Government Lease for Real Property, to award leases unless the contracting officer uses GSA Form 3626 (see 570.802). [76 FR 30854, May 27, 2011]
