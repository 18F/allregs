CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 1533  /  1533.103 Protests to the Agency.

Protests to the Agency are processed pursuant to the requirements of FAR 33.103. Contracting Officers must include in every solicitation the provision at 1552.233-70, Notice of Filing Requirements for Agency Protests. [64 FR 17110, Apr. 8, 1999]
