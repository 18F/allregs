CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 982  /  Sec. 982.446 Inspection documentation.

Pursuant to Sec. 982.46(b), handlers are required to use the following identification on bags and cartons of 25 pounds or larger capacity which contain certified hazelnuts:

(a) The words ``This Produce Inspected and Certified Per Federal Marketing Order No. 982'' shall be contained within an outline of the combined States of Oregon and Washington; and

(b) This identification shall be printed on the upper right quarter of the printed side of a bag; or

(c) This identification shall be printed on the upper right quarter of one of the side panels of a carton. [54 FR 46720, Nov. 7, 1989]
