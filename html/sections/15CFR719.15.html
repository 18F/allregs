CFR /  Title 15  /  Part 719  /  Sec. 719.15 Procedural stipulations.

Unless otherwise ordered and subject to Sec. 719.16 of the CWCR, a written stipulation agreed to by all parties and filed with the ALJ will modify the procedures established by this part.
