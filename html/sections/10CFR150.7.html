CFR /  Title 10  /  Part 150  /  Sec. 150.7 Persons in offshore waters not exempt.

Persons in offshore waters are not exempt from the Commission's licensing and regulatory requirements with respect to byproduct, source, and special nuclear materials. [46 FR 44152, Sept. 3, 1981]
