CFR /  Title 9  /  Part 201  /  Sec. 201.70 Restriction or limitation of competition between packers

Each packer and dealer engaged in purchasing livestock, in person or through employed buyers, shall conduct his buying operations in competition with, and independently of, other packers and dealers similarly engaged. [24 FR 3183, Apr. 24, 1959]
