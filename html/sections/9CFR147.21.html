CFR /  Title 9  /  Part 147  /  Sec. 147.21 Sanitation procedures.

Sanitation must be maintained in a manner approved by the Administrator. Approved procedures for maintaining sanitation are listed in the NPIP Program Standards, as defined in Sec. 147.51. Sanitation procedures may also be approved by the Administrator in accordance with Sec. 147.53(d)(2).
