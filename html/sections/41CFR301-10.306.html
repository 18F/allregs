CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 301-10  /  Sec. 301-10.306 What will I be reimbursed if authorized to use a POV carrier terminal, or from my residence directly to a common carrier terminal?

If determined advantageous to the Government, you will be reimbursed on a mileage basis plus other allowable costs for round-trip travel on the beginning and/or ending of travel between the points involved. [FTR Amdt. 70, 63 FR 15955, Apr. 1, 1998, as amended by FTR. Amdt. 2005-05, 70 FR 61047, Oct. 20, 2005]
