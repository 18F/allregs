CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 90  /  Sec. 90.275 Selection and assignment of frequencies in the

Applicants must specify the frequencies in which the proposed system will operate pursuant to a recommendation by a frequency coordinator certified for the pool in which the requested frequency is assigned. [62 FR 18932, Apr. 17, 1997]
