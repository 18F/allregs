CFR /  Title 48  /  Part 1552  /  1552.216-75 Base fee and award fee proposal.

As prescribed in 1516.405(b), insert the following clause:

Base Fee and Award Fee Proposal (FEB 1999)

For the purpose of this solicitation, offerors shall propose a combination of base fee and award fee. Base fee shall not exceed 3% of the estimated cost, excluding fee, and the award fee shall not be less than __% of the total estimated cost, excluding fee. The combined percentages of base and award fee shall not exceed __% of the total estimated cost, excluding fee.

(End of clause) [64 FR 3876, Jan. 26, 1999]
