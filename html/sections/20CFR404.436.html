CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 404  /  Sec. 404.436 Excess earnings; months to which excess earnings cannot

Under the annual earnings test, excess earnings (as described in Sec. 404.430) are not charged to any month in which an individual is deemed not entitled to a benefit. A beneficiary (i.e., the insured individual or any person entitled or deemed entitled on the individual's earnings record) is deemed not entitled to a benefit for a month if he is subject to a deduction for that month because of:

(a) Engaging in noncovered remunerative activity outside the United States (as described in Secs. 404.417 and 404.418); or

(b) Failure to have a child in his or her care (as described in Sec. 404.421). [32 FR 19159, Dec. 20, 1967, as amended at 38 FR 9429, Apr. 16, 1973; 38 FR 17716, July 3, 1973; 43 FR 8133, Feb. 28, 1978; 68 FR 40123, July 7, 2003]
