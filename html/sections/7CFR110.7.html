CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 110  /  Sec. 110.7 Penalties.

Any certified applicator who violates 7 U.S.C. 136i-1(a), (b), or (c) or this part shall be subject to a civil penalty of not more than the amount specified in section Sec. 3.91(b)(1)(i)(A) of this title in the case of the first offense, and in the case of subsequent offenses, be subject to a civil penalty of not less than the amount specified in Sec. 3.91(b)(1)(i)(B) of this title for each violation, except that the civil penalty shall be less than the amount specified in Sec. 3.91(b)(1)(i)(B) of this title if the Administrator determines that the certified applicator made a good faith effort to comply with 7 U.S.C. 136i-1(a), (b), and (c) and this part. [70 FR 29579, May 24, 2005]
