CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 1620  /  Sec. 1620.14 Payment to the record keeper.

(a)) is responsible for transmitting employer and employee contributions to the TSP record keeper.

(b) and (c), respectively) is responsible for transmitting employer and employee contributions to the employee's Federal agency of record. Employee contributions will be deducted from the employee's actual pay. The employee's agency of record is responsible for transmitting the employer and employee's contributions to the TSP record keeper in accordance with Board procedures. The employee's election form (TSP-1) will be filed in the employee's official personnel folder or other similar file maintained by the employing authority.
