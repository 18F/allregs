CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 128  /  Sec. 128.320 Exhaust systems.

No diesel-engine exhaust system need meet the material requirements in Sec. 58.10-5(d)(1)(i) of this chapter if the installation is certified as required by Sec. 128.220(c) of this part.
