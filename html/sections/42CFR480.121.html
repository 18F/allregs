CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 480  /  Sec. 480.121 Optional disclosure of nonconfidential information.

A QIO may, on its own initiative, subject to the notification requirements in Sec. 480.105, furnish the information available under Sec. 480.120 to any person, agency, or organization. [50 FR 15359, Apr. 17, 1985. Redesignated at 64 FR 66279, Nov. 24, 1999, as amended at 69 FR 49267, Aug. 11, 2004]
