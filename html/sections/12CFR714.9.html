CFR /  Title 12  /  Part 714  /  Sec. 714.9 Are indirect leasing arrangements subject to the purchase

Your indirect leasing arrangements are not subject to the eligible obligation limit if they satisfy the provisions of Sec. 701.23(b)(3)(iv) that require that you make the final underwriting decision and that the lease contract is assigned to you very soon after it is signed by the member and the dealer or leasing company.
