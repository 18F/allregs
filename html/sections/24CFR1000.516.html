CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 1000  /  Sec. 1000.516 What reporting period is covered by the annual

For the first annual performance report to be submitted under NAHASDA, the period to be covered is October 1, 1997, through September 30, 1998. This first report must be submitted by January 31, 1999. Subsequent annual performance reports must cover the period that coincides with the recipient's program year. [64 FR 3015, Jan. 20, 1999]
