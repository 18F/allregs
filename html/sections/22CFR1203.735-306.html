CFR /  Title 22  /  Part 1203  /  Sec. 1203.735-306 Miscellaneous statutory provisions.

Each special Government employee shall become acquainted with each statute that relates to the employee's ethical and other conduct as a special Government employee of an agency and of the Government. The attention of special Government employees is directed to the statutes listed in Sec. 1203.735-216.
