CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 422  /  Sec. 422.303 Interest, late payment penalties, and administrative

We may charge the debtor with interest, late payment penalties, and our costs of collection on delinquent debts covered by this subpart when authorized by our regulations issued in accordance with the Federal Claims Collection Standards (31 CFR 901.9). [71 FR 38070, July 5, 2006]
