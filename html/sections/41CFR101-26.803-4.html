CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 101-26  /  Sec. 101-26.803-4 Adjustments.

GSA and DOD will adjust billings resulting from over or under charges, or discrepancies or deficiencies in shipments, or material on a bill submitted under the provisions of this subpart 101-26.8 and the GSA publication referenced in Sec. 101-26.803-1.
