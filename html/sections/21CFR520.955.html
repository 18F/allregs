CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 520  /  Sec. 520.955 Florfenicol.

(a) Specifications. Each milliliter (mL) contains 23 milligrams (mg) florfenicol.

(b) Sponsor. See Nos. 000061 and 058198 in Sec. 510.600(c) of this chapter.

(c) Related tolerances. See Sec. 556.283 of this chapter.

(d) Conditions of use in swine--(1) Amount. Administer in drinking water ad libitum at 400 mg per gallon (100 parts per million (ppm)) for 5 consecutive days.

(1) Amount. Administer in drinking water ad libitum at 400 mg per gallon (100 parts per million (ppm)) for 5 consecutive days.

(2) Indications for use. For the treatment of swine respiratory disease (SRD) associated with Actinobacillus pleuropneumoniae, Pasteurella multocida, Salmonella choleraesuis and Streptococcus suis.

(3) Limitations. Do not slaughter within 16 days of last treatment. Federal law restricts this drug to use by or on the order of a licensed veterinarian. [67 FR 78357, Dec. 24, 2002, as amended at 72 FR 262, Jan. 4, 2007; 78 FR 52854, Aug. 27, 2013]
