CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 4290  /  Sec. 4290.640 Requirement to file portfolio financing reports with

For each Financing you make (excluding guarantees), you must submit a Portfolio Financing Report on SBA Form 1031 or other USDA-approved form(s) within 30 days of the closing date. [76 FR 80223, Dec. 23, 2011]
