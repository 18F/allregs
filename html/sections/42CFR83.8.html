CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 83  /  Sec. 83.8 How is a petition submitted?

The petitioner(s) must send a petition in writing to NIOSH. A petition must provide identifying and contact information on the petitioner(s) and information to justify the petition, as specified under Sec. 83.9. Detailed instructions for preparing and submitting a petition, including an optional petition form, are available from NIOSH through direct request (1-800-35- NIOSH) or on the Internet at www.cdc.gov/niosh/ocas.
