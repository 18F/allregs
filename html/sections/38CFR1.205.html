CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 1  /  Sec. 1.205 Notification to the Attorney General or United States

VA police and/or the OIG, whichever has primary responsibility within VA for investigation of the offense in question, will be responsible for notifying the appropriate United States Attorney's Office, pursuant to 28 U.S.C. 535. (Authority: 5 U.S.C. App. 3, 38 U.S.C. 902) [68 FR 17550, Apr. 10, 2003]
