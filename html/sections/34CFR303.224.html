CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 303  /  Sec. 303.224 Reports and records.

The State must ensure that it will--

(a) Make reports in the form and containing the information that the Secretary may require; and

(b) Keep records and afford access to those records as the Secretary may find necessary to ensure compliance with the requirements of this part, the correctness and verification of reports, and the proper disbursement of funds provided under this part. (Approved by Office of Management and Budget under control number 1820-0550) (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1437(b)(4))
