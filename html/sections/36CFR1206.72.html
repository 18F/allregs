CFR /  Title 36  /  Part 1206  /  Sec. 1206.72 Where can I find the regulatory requirements that apply

(a) In addition to this Part 1206, NARA has issued other regulations that apply to NHPRC grants in 36 CFR parts 1202, 1208, 1211, 1212 and 2 CFR part 2600 (which incorporates OMB's Uniform Administrative Requirements, Cost Principles, and Audit Requirements for Federal Awards guidance at 2 CFR part 200).

(b) The Commission provides additional policy guidance related to Title VI of the Civil Rights Act of 1964, regarding persons with limited English proficiency, at http://www.archives.gov/nhprc/ and from the NHPRC staff. [79 FR 76080, Dec. 19, 2014]
