CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 469  /  Sec. 469.30 Applicability.

(a) The provisions of this subpart are applicable to discharges resulting from the manufacture of cathode ray tubes.

(b) The compliance deadline for PSES shall be no later than July 14, 1986. [48 FR 55704, Dec. 14, 1983; 49 FR 1056, Jan. 9, 1984]
