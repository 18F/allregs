CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 90  /  Sec. 90.10-20 Liftboat.

Liftboat means an offshore supply vessel with moveable legs capable of raising its hull above the surface of the sea. [CGD 82-004a, 55 FR 2525, Jan. 25, 1990, as amended by USCG-2014-0688, 79 FR 58282, Sept. 29, 2014]
