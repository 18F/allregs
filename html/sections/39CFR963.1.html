CFR /  Title 39  /  Part 963  /  Sec. 963.1 Authority for the rules.

These rules of practice are issued by the Judicial Officer of the U.S. Postal Service pursuant to authority delegated by the Postmaster General (39 CFR 226.2(e)(1)). [52 FR 18912, May 20, 1987, as amended at 59 FR 10751, Mar. 8, 1994]
