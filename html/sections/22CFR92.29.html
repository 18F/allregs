CFR /  Title 22  /  Part 92  /  Sec. 92.29 Oath or affirmation to affidavit.

Affidavits made before notarizing officers must be sworn to or affirmed (see Sec. 92.23(d)). [22 FR 10858, Dec. 27, 1957, as amended at 60 FR 51723, Oct. 3, 1995]
