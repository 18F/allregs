CFR /  Title 50  /  Part 270  /  Sec. 270.20 Payment of assessments.

Persons subject to an assessment would be required to pay the assessment on or before the date due, unless they have demanded a refund or filed a petition of objection with NMFS under Sec. 270.21. However, persons who have demanded a refund under Sec. 270.22 or filed a petition of objection under Sec. 270.21 may submit proof of these actions in leu of payment. In the case of a petition of objection, NMFs will inform the Council and the petitioner of its finding at which time petitioner must pay the revised assessment if applicable.
