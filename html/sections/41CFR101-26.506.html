CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 101-26  /  Sec. 101-26.506 Interior planning and design services.

In addition to the assistance provided in the selection of furniture and furnishings as specified in Sec. 101-26.505-7, the GSA Public Buildings Service, through facilities located in each region, will assist Federal activities within the United States, the Commonwealth of Puerto Rico, and the Virgin Islands in various phases of interior planning and design. These services will be provided either directly or through commercial sources. (For services involving space layout, see Sec. 101-17.400.) [41 FR 42953, Sept. 29, 1976]
