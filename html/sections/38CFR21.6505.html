CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 21  /  Sec. 21.6505 Participation in the temporary program.

Participation in this temporary program of trial work periods and vocational rehabilitation is limited to qualified veterans. (Authority: 38 U.S.C. 1163(a)(2)(A)). [55 FR 17272, Apr. 24, 1990]
