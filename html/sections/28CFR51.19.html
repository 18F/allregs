CFR /  Title 28  /  Part 51  /  Sec. 51.19 Request for notification concerning voting litigation.

A jurisdiction subject to the preclearance requirements of section 5 that becomes involved in any litigation concerning voting is requested to notify the Chief, Voting Section, Civil Rights Division, at the addresses, telefacsimile number, or email address specified in Sec. 51.24. Such notification will not be considered a submission under section 5. [Order 3262-2011, 76 FR 21244, Apr. 15, 2011]
