CFR /  Title 9  /  Part 2  /  Sec. 2.2 Acknowledgement of regulations and standards.

(a) Application for initial license. APHIS will supply a copy of the applicable regulations and standards to the applicant with each request for a license application. The applicant shall acknowledge receipt of the regulations and standards and agree to comply with them by signing the application form before a license will be issued.

(b) Application for license renewal. APHIS will renew a license after the applicant certifies by signing the application form that, to the best of the applicant's knowledge and belief, he or she is in compliance with the regulations and standards and agrees to continue to comply with the regulations and standards. APHIS will supply a copy of the applicable regulations and standards to the applicant upon request. (Approved by the Office of Management and Budget under control number 0579-0254) [60 FR 13895, Mar. 15, 1995, as amended at 69 FR 42100, July 14, 2004]
