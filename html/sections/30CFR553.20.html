CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 553  /  Sec. 553.20 What methods may I use to demonstrate OSFR?

As the designated applicant, you may satisfy your OSFR requirements by using one or a combination of the following methods to demonstrate OSFR:

(a) Self-insurance under Sec. Sec. 553.21 through 553.28;

(b) Insurance under Sec. 553.29;

(c) An indemnity under Sec. 553.30;

(d) A surety bond under Sec. 553.31; or

(e) An alternative method the Director approves under Sec. 553.32.
