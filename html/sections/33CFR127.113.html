CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 127  /  Sec. 127.113 Warning signs.

(a) The marine transfer area for LNG must have warning signs that--

(1) Meet paragraph (b) of this section;

(2) Can be seen from the shore and the water; and

(3) Have the following text: WarningDangerous CargoNo VisitorsNo SmokingNo Open Lights

(b) Each letter in the words on the sign must be--

(1) Block style;

(2) Black on a white background; and

(3) 7.6 centimeters (3 inches) high. [CGD 78-038, 53 FR 3376, Feb. 7, 1988, as amended by CGD 88-049, 60 FR 39796, Aug. 3, 1995]
