CFR /  Title 25  /  Part 224  /  Sec. 224.74 When must the Secretary approve or disapprove a final proposed TERA?

The Secretary must approve or disapprove a final proposed TERA or a revised final proposed TERA within 270 days of the Director's receipt of a complete application for a TERA. With the consent of the tribe, or as provided in Sec. 224.62(b), the Secretary may extend the period for a decision.
