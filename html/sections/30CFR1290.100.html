CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 1290  /  Sec. 1290.100 What is the purpose of this part?

This part tells you how to appeal Office of Natural Resources Revenue (ONRR) or delegated State orders concerning reporting to the Minerals Revenue Management (MRM) and the payment of royalties and other payments due under leases subject to this part. [71 FR 51752, Aug. 31, 2006, as amended at 78 FR 30206, May 22, 2013]
