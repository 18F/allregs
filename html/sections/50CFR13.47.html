CFR /  Title 50  /  Part 13  /  Sec. 13.47 Inspection requirement.

Any person holding a permit under this subchapter B shall allow the Director's agent to enter his premises at any reasonable hour to inspect any wildlife or plant held or to inspect, audit, or copy any permits, books, or records required to be kept by regulations of this subchapter B. [39 FR 1161, Jan. 4, 1974, as amended at 42 FR 32377, June 24, 1977]
