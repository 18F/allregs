CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 241  /  Sec. 241.260 Definitions.

All of the definitions contained in Sec. 241.1 shall apply to this subpart. In addition, the term contract of insurance, as used in this subpart, means the agreement evidenced by endorsement of the credit instrument by the Commissioner or his duly authorized representative, and includes the provisions of this subpart and of the National Housing Act.
