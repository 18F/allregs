CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 661  /  Sec. 661.30 How does the Secretary evaluate an application?

The Secretary evaluates an application for a grant under this program on the basis of the criteria in Sec. 661.31. The Secretary informs applicants of the maximum possible score for each criterion in the application package or in a notice published in the Federal Register. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1130a) [70 FR 13376, Mar. 21, 2005]
