CFR /  Title 12  /  Part 390  /  Sec. 390.190 What regulations govern the fiduciary operations

A State savings association must conduct its fiduciary operations in accordance with applicable State law, and must exercise its fiduciary powers in a safe and sound manner. Subpart K [Reserved]
