CFR /  Title 50  /  Part 660  /  Sec. 660.311 Open access fishery--definitions.

General definitions for the Pacific Coast groundfish fisheries are defined at Sec. 660.11, subpart C. The definitions in this subpart are specific to the open access fishery covered in this subpart and are in addition to those specified at Sec. 660.11, subpart C.

Closely tended for the purposes of this subpart means that a vessel is within visual sighting distance or within 0.25 nm (463 m) of the gear as determined by electronic navigational equipment.
