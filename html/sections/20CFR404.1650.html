CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 404  /  Sec. 404.1650 Action we will take if a State agency does not meet

If a State agency does not meet two of the three established threshold levels (one of which must be performance accuracy) for two or more consecutive calendar quarters, we will notify the State agency in writing that it is not meeting the standards. Following our notification, we will provide the State agency appropriate performance support described in Secs. 404.1660, 404.1661 and 404.1662 for a period of up to 12 months. [56 FR 11020, Mar. 14, 1991]
