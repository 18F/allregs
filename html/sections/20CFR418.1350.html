CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 418  /  Sec. 418.1350 What are the rules for review of a reconsidered

You may request a hearing before an OMHA administrative law judge consistent with HHS' regulations at 42 CFR part 405. You may seek further review of the administrative law judge's decision by requesting MAC review and judicial review in accordance with HHS' regulations. [78 FR 57260, Sept. 18, 2013]
