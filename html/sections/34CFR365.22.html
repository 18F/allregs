CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 365  /  Sec. 365.22 What additional IL services may the State provide?

In addition to the IL core services that the State may provide pursuant to Sec. 365.21(a) with funds received under part B of chapter 1 of title VII of the Act, the State also may use funds received under part B of chapter 1 of title VII of the Act to provide other IL services defined in 34 CFR 364.4 (Independent living services). (Authority: 29 U.S.C. 796e-2(1))
