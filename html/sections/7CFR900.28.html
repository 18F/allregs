CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 900  /  Sec. 900.28 Deadline for issuance of recommended decisions or tentative

In a Federal milk marketing agreement or order amendatory proceeding, USDA shall issue a recommended decision under Sec. 900.12 or, when applicable, a tentative final decision, not later than 90 days after the deadline for submission of proposed findings and conclusions, and written arguments or briefs.
