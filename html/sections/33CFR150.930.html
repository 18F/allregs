CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 150  /  Sec. 150.930 What datum is used for the geographic coordinates in this

The geographic coordinates used in this subpart have been revised to enable plotting using the North American Datum of 1983 (NAD 83) and no longer require the use of any further conversion factors for correction. [USCG-2007-27887, 72 FR 45903, Aug. 16, 2007]
