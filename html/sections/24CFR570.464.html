CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 570  /  Sec. 570.464 Project closeout.

HUD will advise the recipient to initiate closeout procedures when HUD determines, in consultation with the recipient, that there are not impediments to closeout. Closeout shall be carried out in accordance with Sec. 570.509 and applicable HUD guidelines. [53 FR 8058, Mar. 11, 1988]
