CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 20  /  Sec. 20.710 Rule 710. Witnesses at hearings.

The testimony of witnesses, including appellants, will be heard. All testimony must be given under oath or affirmation. Oath or affirmation is not required for the sole purpose of presenting contentions and argument. (Authority: 38 U.S.C. 7102, 7105(a), 7107) [61 FR 29028, June 7, 1996]
