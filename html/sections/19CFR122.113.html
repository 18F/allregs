CFR /  Title 19  /  Part 122  /  Sec. 122.113 Form for transit air cargo manifest procedures.

A manifest on Customs Form 7509 is required for transit air cargo, as provided in Sec. 122.48(c) of this part. The words ``Transportation Entry and Transit Air Cargo Manifest'' shall be printed, stamped or marked on the form and on all copies of the form required for transit air cargo movement.
