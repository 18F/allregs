CFR /  Title 37  /  Part 253  /  Sec. 253.2 Definition of public broadcasting entity.

As used in this part, the term public broadcasting entity means a noncommercial educational broadcast station as defined in section 397 of title 47 and any nonprofit institution or organization engaged in the activities described in 17 U.S.C. 118(d)(2). Sec. 253.3 [Reserved]
