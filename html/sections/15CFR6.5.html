CFR /  Title 15  /  Part 6  /  Sec. 6.5 Effective date of adjustments.

The adjustments made by Sec. 6.4 of this part, of the penalties there specified, are effective on December 7, 2012, and said penalties, as thus adjusted by the adjustments made by Sec. 6.4 of this part, shall apply only to violations occurring after December 7, 2012, and before the effective date of any future inflation adjustment thereto made subsequent to December 7, 2012 as provided in Sec. 6.6 of this part. [77 FR 72917, Dec. 7, 2012]
