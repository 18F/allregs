CFR /  Title 22  /  Part 92  /  Sec. 92.42 Certification of copies of foreign records relating to land titles.

In certifying documents of the kind described in title 28, section 1742, of the United States Code, diplomatic and consular officers of the United States will conform to the Federal procedures for authenticating foreign public documents (Sec. 92.39), unless otherwise instructed in a specific case.
