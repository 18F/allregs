CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 417  /  Sec. 417.836 Written description of administrative review procedures.

Each HCPP is responsible for ensuring that all Medicare enrollees are informed in writing of the administrative review procedures that are available to them. [59 FR 59943, Nov. 21, 1994]
