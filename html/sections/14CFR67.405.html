CFR /  Title 14  /  Part 67  /  Sec. 67.405 Medical examinations: Who may perform?

(a) First-class. Any aviation medical examiner who is specifically designated for the purpose may perform examinations for the first-class medical certificate.

(b) Second- and third-class. Any aviation medical examiner may perform examinations for the second-or third-class medical certificate. [Doc. No. FAA-2007-27812, 73 FR 43066, July 24, 2008]
