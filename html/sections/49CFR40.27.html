CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 40  /  Sec. 40.27 May an employer require an employee to sign a consent

No, as an employer, you must not require an employee to sign a consent, release, waiver of liability, or indemnification agreement with respect to any part of the drug or alcohol testing process covered by this part (including, but not limited to, collections, laboratory testing, MRO and SAP services). [66 FR 41950, Aug. 9, 2001]
