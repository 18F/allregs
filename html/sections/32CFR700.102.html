CFR /  Title 32  /  Part 700  /  Sec. 700.102 Statutory authority for issuance of United States Navy

Title 10, United States Code, section 6011, provides that United States Navy Regulations shall be issued by the Secretary of the Navy. Regulations issued under this authority are permanent regulations of general applicability, as opposed to regulations issued by the Secretary under Sec. 700.104.
