CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 52  /  Sec. 52.10 Per diem based on recognition and certification.

VA will pay per diem to a State for providing adult day health care to eligible veterans in a facility if the Under Secretary for Health recognizes the facility as a State home based on a current certification that the facility management meet the standards of subpart D of this part. (Authority: 38 U.S.C. 101, 501, 1741-1743)
