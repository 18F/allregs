CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 57  /  Sec. 57.05-5 Low temperature application.

For low temperature application, each welder shall demonstrate his ability to weld satisfactorily in accordance with procedures qualified in accordance with Sec. 57.03-1(b). Manual welding shall be qualified in the position prescribed by the procedure.
