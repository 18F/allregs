CFR /  Title 30  /  Part 761  /  Sec. 761.12 Exception for existing operations.

The prohibitions and limitations of Sec. 761.11 do not apply to:

(a) Surface coal mining operations for which a valid permit, issued under Subchapter G of this chapter or an approved State regulatory program, exists when the land comes under the protection of Sec. 761.11. This exception applies only to lands within the permit area as it exists when the land comes under the protection of Sec. 761.11.

(b) With respect to operations subject to Subchapter B of this chapter, lands upon which validly authorized surface coal mining operations exist when the land comes under the protection of 30 U.S.C. 1272(e) or Sec. 761.11. [64 FR 70833, Dec. 17, 1999]
