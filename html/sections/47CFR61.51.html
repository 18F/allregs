CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 61  /  Sec. 61.51 Scope.

The rules in this subpart apply to tariffs filed by issuing carriers, with the exception of the informational tariffs filed pursuant to 47 U.S.C. 226(h)(1)(A), unless otherwise noted. [76 FR 43215, July 20, 2011]
