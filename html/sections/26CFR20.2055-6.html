CFR /  Title 26  /  Part 20  /  Sec. 20.2055-6 Disallowance of double deduction in the case of

No deduction is allowed from the decedent's gross estate under section 2055 for property with respect to which a deduction is allowed by reason of section 2056(b)(7). See section 2056(b)(9) and Sec. 20.2056(b)-9. [T.D. 8522, 59 FR 9647, Mar. 1, 1994]
