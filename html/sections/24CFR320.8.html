CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 320  /  Sec. 320.8 Excess Yield Securities.

(a) Definition. Excess Yield Securities are securities backed by the excess servicing income relating to mortgages underlying previously issued Ginnie Mae mortgage-backed securities.

(b) GNMA guaranty. The Association guarantees the timely payment of interest as provided by the terms of the security. [71 FR 32389, June 5, 2006]
