CFR /  Title 29  /  Part 1919  /  Sec. 1919.51 Provisions respecting application for accreditation,

The provisions of Sec. Sec. 1919.3, 1919.4, 1919.5, 1919.7, 1919.8, and 1919.9 shall govern accreditation to certificate shore-based material handling devices to the extent applicable. (Section 1919.51 contains a collection of information which has been approved by the Office of Management and Budget under OMB Control No. 1218-0003) [39 FR 22096, June 19, 1974, as amended at 61 FR 5509, Feb. 13, 1996]
