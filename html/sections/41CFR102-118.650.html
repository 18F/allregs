CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 102-118  /  Sec. 102-118.650 Can a TSP request a review of a settlement action

Yes, a TSP desiring a review of a settlement action taken by the Administrator of General Services may request a review by the Civilian Board of Contract Appeals (CBCA) or file a claim with the United States Court of Federal Claims (28 U.S.C. 1491). [65 FR 24569, Apr. 26, 2000, as amended at 74 FR 30476, June 26, 2009]
