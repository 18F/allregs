CFR /  Title 20  /  Part 210  /  Sec. 210.2 Definition of service.

Service means a period of time for which an employee receives payment from a railroad employer for the performance of work; or a period of time for which an employee receives compensation which is paid for time lost as an employee; or a period of time credited to an employee for creditable military service as defined in part 212 of this chapter. Service shall also include deemed months of service as provided under Sec. 210.3(b) of this chapter and any month in which an employee is credited with compensation under Sec. 211.12 of this chapter based on benefits paid under title VII of the Regional Rail Reorganization Act of 1973. [53 FR 17182, May 16, 1988]
