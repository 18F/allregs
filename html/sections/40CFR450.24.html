CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 450  /  Sec. 450.24 New source performance standards reflecting the best available

Any new source subject to this subpart must achieve, at a minimum, the following new source performance standards representing the degree of effluent reduction attainable by application of the best available demonstrated control technology (NSPS): The standards are described at Sec. 450.22.
