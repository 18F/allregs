CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 157  /  Sec. 157.146 Similar tank design: Inspections on U.S. tank vessels.

(a) If a U.S. tank vessel has tanks similar in dimensions and internal structure, the owner or operator may submit a written request to the Officer in Charge, Marine Inspection, of the zone in which the COW system is inspected, for only one of those tanks to be inspected under Sec. 157.140(a)(1).

(b) Only one tank of a group of tanks similar in dimensions and internal structure is inspected under Sec. 157.140(a)(1), if the Officer in Charge, Marine Inspection, accepts the request submitted under paragraph (a) of this section.
