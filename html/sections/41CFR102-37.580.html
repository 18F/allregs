CFR /  Title 41  /  Part 102-37  /  Sec. 102-37.580 Who is responsible for costs associated with the

The recipient public body is responsible for paying the disposal costs incident to the donation, such as packing, preparation for shipment, demilitarization (as defined in Sec. 102-36.40 of this chapter), loading, and transportation to its site.
