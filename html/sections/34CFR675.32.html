CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 675  /  Sec. 675.32 Program description.

An institution may expend up to the lesser of $50,000 or 10 percent of its FWS allocation and reallocation for an award year to establish or expand a program under which the institution, separately or in combination with other eligible institutions, locates and develops jobs, including community service jobs, for currently enrolled students. (Authority: 42 U.S.C. 2756) [59 FR 61417, Nov. 30, 1994]
