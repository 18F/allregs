CFR /  Title 27  /  Part 18  /  Sec. 18.18 Execution under penalties of perjury.

When a form or other document called for under this part is required to be executed under penalties of perjury, it will be so executed, as defined in Sec. 18.11, and signed by an authorized person. (Act of August 16, 1954, Pub. L. 591, Chapter 736, 68A Stat. 749 (26 U.S.C. 6065))
