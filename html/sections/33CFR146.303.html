CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 146  /  Sec. 146.303 Notice and written report of casualties.

The owner, operator, or person in charge of a vessel engaged in OCS activities shall ensure that the notice of casualty requirements of Sec. 146.30 and the written report requirements of Sec. 146.35 are complied with whenever a casualty involving the vessel occurs which results in:

(a) Death;

(b) Injury to 5 or more persons in a single incident; or

(c) Injury causing any person to be incapacitated for more than 72 hours. (Approved by the Office of Management and Budget under control number 1625-0001) [CGD 78-160, 47 FR 9383, Mar. 4, 1982, as amended by USCG-2006-25150, 71 FR 39209, July 12, 2006]
