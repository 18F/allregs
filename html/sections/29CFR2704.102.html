CFR /  Title 29  /  Part 2704  /  Sec. 2704.102 Applicability.

Section 2704.105(a) applies to adversary adjudications before the Commission pending or commenced on or after August 5, 1984. Section 2704.105(b) applies to adversary adjudications commenced on or after March 29, 1996. [63 FR 63175, Nov. 12, 1998]
