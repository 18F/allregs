CFR /  Title 21  /  Part 314  /  Sec. 314.99 Other responsibilities of an applicant of an abbreviated

(a) An applicant shall comply with the requirements of Sec. 314.65 regarding withdrawal by the applicant of an unapproved abbreviated application and Sec. 314.72 regarding a change in ownership of an abbreviated application.

(b) An applicant may ask FDA to waive under this section any requirement that applies to the applicant under Sec. Sec. 314.92 through 314.99. The applicant shall comply with the requirements for a waiver under Sec. 314.90.
