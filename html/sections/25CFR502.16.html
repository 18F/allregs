CFR /  Title 25  /  Part 502  /  Sec. 502.16 Net revenues.

Net revenues means gross gaming revenues of an Indian gaming operation less--

(a) Amounts paid out as, or paid for, prizes; and

(b) Total gaming-related operating expenses, including all those expenses of the gaming operation commonly known as operating expenses and non-operating expenses consistent with professional accounting pronouncements, excluding management fees. [74 FR 36932, July 27, 2009]
