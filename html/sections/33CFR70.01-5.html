CFR /  Title 33  /  Part 70  /  Sec. 70.01-5 Penalty.

Any person violating the provisions of this section shall be deemed guilty of a misdemeanor and be subject to a fine not exceeding the sum of $500 for each offense, and each day during which such violation shall continue shall be considered a new offense. [CGFR 52-15, 18 FR 12, Jan. 1, 1953]
