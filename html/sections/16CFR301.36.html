CFR /  Title 16  /  Part 301  /  Sec. 301.36 Sectional fur products.

(a) Where a fur product is composed of two or more sections containing different animal furs the required information with respect to each section shall be separately set forth in labeling, invoicing or advertising; as for example: Dyed RabbitFur origin: FranceTrimming: Dyed Mouton-processed LambFur origin: Argentina

or Body: Dyed KolinskyFur origin: RussiaTail: Dyed MinkFur origin: Canada

(b) The provisions of this section shall not be interpreted so as to require the disclosure of very small amounts of different animal furs added to complete a fur product or skin such as the ears, snoot, or under part of the jaw.
