CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 220  /  Sec. 220.1 Scope.

This part prescribes minimum requirements governing the use of wireless communications in connection with railroad operations. In addition, this part sets forth prohibitions, restrictions, and requirements that apply to the use of personal and railroad-supplied cellular telephones and other electronic devices. So long as these minimum requirements are met, railroads may adopt additional or more stringent requirements. [75 FR 59601, Sept. 27, 2010] Sec. 220.2 [Reserved]
