CFR /  Title 24  /  Part 200  /  Sec. 200.37 Preventing crime in federally assisted housing.

See part 5, subparts I and J of this title, for provisions concerning preventing crime in federally assisted housing, including programs administered under section 236 and under sections 221(d)(3) and 221(d)(5) of the National Housing Act. [66 FR 28797, May 24, 2001]
