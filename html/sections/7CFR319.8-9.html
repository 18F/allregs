CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 319  /  Sec. 319.8-9 Hull fiber and gin trash.

(a) Entry of hull fiber will be authorized under the same conditions as are applicable to waste under this subpart.

(b) Gin trash may be imported only under the provisions of Sec. 319.8-20. [24 FR 10788, Dec. 29, 1959, as amended at 27 FR 5390, June 7, 1962]
