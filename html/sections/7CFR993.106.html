CFR /  Title 7  /  Part 993  /  Sec. 993.106 In-line inspection.

In-line inspection means inspection of prunes where samples are drawn from a flow of prunes prior to packaging.

Effective Date Note: At 70 FR 30613, May 27, 2005, Sec. 993.106 was suspended indefinitely.
