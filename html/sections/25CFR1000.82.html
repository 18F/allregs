CFR /  Title 25  /  Part 1000  /  Sec. 1000.82 What types of provisions must be included in a BIA AFA?

Each AFA must specify the programs and it must also specify the applicable funding:

(a) Retained by BIA for ``inherently Federal functions'' identified as ``residuals'' (See Sec. 1000.94);

(b) Transferred or to be transferred to the Tribe/Consortium (See Sec. 1000.91); and

(c) Retained by BIA to carry out functions that the Tribe/Consortium could have assumed but elected to leave with BIA. (See Sec. 1000.101).
