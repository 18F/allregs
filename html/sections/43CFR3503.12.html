CFR /  Title 43  /  Part 3503  /  Sec. 3503.12 For what areas may I receive a sulphur permit

You may get a sulphur permit or lease for public domain lands in the States of Louisiana and New Mexico or for Federal acquired lands nationwide, subject to the exceptions listed in Sec. Sec. 3503.10 and 3503.11 of this part.
