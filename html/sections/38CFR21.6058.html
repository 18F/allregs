CFR /  Title 38  /  Part 21  /  Sec. 21.6058 Consequences of evaluation.

(a) Eligible veteran may choose to participate. If VA finds, based on the evaluation, that achievement of a vocational goal by the veteran is reasonably feasible, the veteran shall be offered and may elect to pursue a vocational training program. If the veteran elects to pursue such a program, the program shall be designed in consultation with the veteran in order to meet the veteran's individual needs, and shall be set forth in an Individualized Written Rehabilitation Plan (IWRP) under the provisions of Sec. 21.84 of this part or an Individualized Employment Assistance Plan (IEAP) under Sec. 21.88 of this part. (Authority: 38 U.S.C. 1524(b)(1))
