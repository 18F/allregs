CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 188  /  Sec. 188.10-65 Seagoing barge.

A seagoing barge is a nonself-propelled vessel of at least 100 gross tons making voyages beyond the Boundary Line (as defined in 46 CFR part 7). [CGD 95-028, 62 FR 51219, Sept. 30, 1997]
