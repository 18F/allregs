CFR /  Title 47  /  Part 69  /  Sec. 69.701 Application of rules in this subpart.

The rules in this subpart apply to all incumbent LECs subject to price cap regulation, as defined in Sec. 61.3(x) of this chapter, seeking pricing flexibility on the basis of the development of competition in parts of its service area.
