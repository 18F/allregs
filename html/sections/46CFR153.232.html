CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 153  /  Sec. 153.232 Type III system.

A type III containment system must be in either a type I, II, or III hull. The requirements for type I, II, and III hulls are in subpart F of part 172 of this chapter. [CGD 79-023, 48 FR 51009, Nov. 4, 1983]
