CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 700  /  Sec. 700.49 Failure to remit fees.

EPA will not consider a section 5 notice to be complete unless the appropriate certification under Sec. 700.45(e) is included and until the appropriate remittance under Sec. 700.45(b) has been sent to EPA as provided in Sec. 700.45(e) and received by EPA. EPA will notify the submitter that the section 5 notice is incomplete in accordance with Sec. Sec. 720.65(c) and 725.33 of this chapter. [62 FR 17932, Apr. 11, 1997]
