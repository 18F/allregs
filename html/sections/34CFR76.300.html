CFR /  Title 34  /  Part 76  /  Sec. 76.300 Contact the State for procedures to follow.

An applicant for a subgrant can find out the procedures it must follow by contacting the State agency that administers the program. (Authority: 20 U.S.C. 1221e-3 and 3474)

Cross Reference: See subparts E and G of this part for the general responsibilities of the State regarding applications for subgrants.
