CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 156  /  Sec. 156.806 Notice of non-compliance.

If HHS learns of a potential violation described in Sec. 156.805 or if a State informs HHS of a potential violation, prior to imposing any CMPs, HHS must provide a written notice to the issuer, to include the following:

(a) Describe the potential violation.

(b) Provide 30 days from the date of the notice for the QHP issuer to respond and to provide additional information to refute an alleged violation.

(c) State that a civil money penalty may be assessed if the allegations are not, as determined by HHS, refuted. [79 FR 30351, May 27, 2014]
