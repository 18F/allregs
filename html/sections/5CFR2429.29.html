CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 2429  /  Sec. 2429.29 Content of filings.

With one exception, if you file any document with the Authority or the Office of Administrative Law Judges in a proceeding covered by this subchapter--including any briefs that you upload into the FLRA's eFiling system as attachments--and that document exceeds 10 double-spaced pages in length, then you must ensure that the document includes a table of contents. The one exception is that, if you use the fillable forms in the FLRA's eFiling system, then you are not required to submit a table of contents to accompany the fillable forms. [77 FR 26437, May 4, 2012]
