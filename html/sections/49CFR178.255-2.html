CFR /  Title 49  /  Part 178  /  Sec. 178.255-2 Material.

(a) Material used in the tank must be steel of good weldable quality and conform with the requirements in Sections V, VIII, and IX of the ASME Code (IBR, see Sec. 171.7 of this subchapter).
