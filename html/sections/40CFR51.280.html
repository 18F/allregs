CFR /  Title 40  /  Part 51  /  Sec. 51.280 Resources.

Each plan must include a description of the resources available to the State and local agencies at the date of submission of the plan and of any additional resources needed to carry out the plan during the 5-year period following its submission. The description must include projections of the extent to which resources will be acquired at 1-, 3-, and 5-year intervals. [51 FR 40674, Nov. 7, 1986]
