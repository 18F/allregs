CFR /  Title 5  /  Part 511  /  Sec. 511.202 Authority of agency.

Subject to the provisions of subpart F of this part and Sec. 511.203, an agency may determine whether a position is subject to, or is excluded from, chapter 51 of title 5, United States Code, by section 5102(c) (7) and (8) thereof.
