CFR /  Title 46  /  Part 1  /  Sec. 1.03-50 Appeals from decisions or actions of the Great

Any person directly affected by a decision or action of the Great Lakes Pilotage Division (CG-WWM-2), may make a formal appeal of that decision or action to Commandant (CG-5P), in accordance with the procedures contained in Sec. 1.03-15 of this subpart. [USCG-2003-15137, 68 FR 37093, June 23, 2003, as amended by USCG-2008-0906, 73 FR 56507, Sept. 29, 2008; USCG-2013-0671, 78 FR 60144, Sept. 30, 2013]
