CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 2553  /  Sec. 2553.83 What financial obligation does the Corporation incur for

Entry into a Memorandum of Agreement with, or issuance of an NGA to a sponsor of a non-Corporation funded project does not create a financial obligation on the part of the Corporation for any costs associated with the project.
