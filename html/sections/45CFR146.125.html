CFR /  Title 45  /  Part 146  /  Sec. 146.125 Applicability dates.

Section 144.103, Sec. Sec. 146.111 through 146.119, 146.143, and 146.145 are applicable for plan years beginning on or after July 1, 2005. Until the applicability date for this regulation, plans and issuers are required to continue to comply with the corresponding sections of 45 CFR parts 144 and 146, contained in the 45 CFR, parts 1 to 199, edition revised as of October 1, 2004. [69 FR 78797, Dec. 30, 2004; 70 FR 21147, Apr. 25, 2005]
