CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 456  /  Sec. 456.100 Scope.

Sections 456.101 through 456.145 of this subpart prescribe requirements for a written utilization review (UR) plan for each hospital providing Medicaid services. Sections 456.105 and 456.106 prescribe administrative requirements: Sec. Sec. 456.111 through 456.113 prescribe informational requirements; Sec. Sec. 456.121 through 456.129 prescribe requirements for admission review; Sec. Sec. 456.131 through 456.137 prescribe requirements for continued stay review; and Sec. Sec. 456.141 through 456.145 prescribe requirements for medical care evaluation studies.
