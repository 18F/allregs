CFR /  Title 42  /  Part 488  /  Sec. 488.6 Providers or suppliers that participate in the Medicaid

A provider or supplier that has been granted ``deemed status'' by CMS by virtue of its accreditation from a CMS-approved accreditation program is eligible to participate in the Medicaid program if they are not required under Medicaid regulations to comply with any requirements other than Medicare participation requirements. [80 FR 29837, May 22, 2015]
